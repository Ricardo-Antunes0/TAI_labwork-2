Tha Dogg Pound is an American hip hop group from Long Beach, California. The group was formed in 1991, by Dr. Dre and Ice Cube.  The group's debut album, "Tha Dogg Pound", was released in 1992 on Death Row Records. The album peaked at number two on the Billboard 200 chart and has been certified platinum. The album's lead single, "How Much a Dollar Cost", became a hit, peaking at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and staying there for four weeks. The song also reached number one on the Billboard R&B chart and number two on the Billboard Hot Rap Songs chart.
