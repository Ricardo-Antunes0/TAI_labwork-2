Shingle Creek Crossing is a regional shopping center located in the Town of Hampstead, New York. Opened in 1998, the center is anchored by Sears and Kmart stores.  Shingle Creek Crossing is located in the Town of Hampstead, New York, about north of the City of Rochester. The center is on U.S. Route 20 and County Route 524, about northwest of the Rochester city center.  The mall has a mix of stores, including a Kmart store, a Sears store, a Macy's store, and a JCPenney store. There is also a food court with several restaurants, including a Subway restaurant and a Starbucks coffee shop. The mall has a movie theater with an IMAX screen.
