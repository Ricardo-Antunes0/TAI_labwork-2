Scaachi Koul (born February 7, 1991) is a Canadian comedian and actor. He is known for his stand-up comedy and roles on the television series "Trailer Park Boys" and "The Ron James Show".  Koul was born in Regina, Saskatchewan, to Punjabi parents who are originally from India. At the age of 10, he moved with his family to Toronto, Ontario.  Koul began his comedy career in 2009 when he won the Canadian Comedy Award for Best Newcomer. In 2011, he starred in the television series "Trailer Park Boys". He has also appeared on "The Ron James Show", "The Mindy Project", and "Broad City".  In 2018, Koul released his first comedy album, "I Feel Pretty", which was nominated for a Canadian Comedy Award for Best Comedy Album.
