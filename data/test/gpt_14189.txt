Hurley is a surname of Irish and Scottish origin. The Irish form of the name is Ó hUrláin meaning descendant of Ó hUrláin. The Scottish form of the name is MacHurlie.   The surname Hurley was first found in County Cork (Irish: Corcaigh), where they held a family seat from very early times. Some of the first recorded bearers of the name were: Phelim Hurley, who was documented in the 1296 Book of Leinster; and Tadhg Hurley, who was documented in the 1486 Poll Tax Rolls of County Cork.
