Crystal (also known as Crystal City) is a Statutory Town in El Paso County, Colorado, United States. The population was 1,287 at the 2010 census.  Crystal is located at (36.103572, -106.780671).  According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of , all of it land.  As of the census of 2000, there were 1,331 people, 495 households, and 356 families residing in the town. The population density was 1,120.2 people per square mile (435.0/km²). There were 538 housing units at an average density of 498.5 per square mile (190.3/km²). The racial makeup of the town was 98.73% White, 0.28% African American, 0.08% Native American, 0.31% Asian, and 0.62% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 2.18% of the population.  There were 495 households out of which 36.4% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 61.4% were married couples living together, 8.3% had a female householder with no husband present, and 25.9% were non-families. 22.6% of all households were made up of individuals and 9.5% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.
