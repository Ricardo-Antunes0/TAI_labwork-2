Anthony Cruz (born March 9, 1972), better known by his stage name AZ, is an American rapper from Los Angeles, California. He is a member of the West Coast hip hop group Cypress Hill and has released several solo albums and collaborations.  AZ first came to prominence in the early 1990s as a member of the rap group Cypress Hill. The group released their debut album in 1991 and achieved mainstream success with the singles "Insane in the Brain" and "How I Could Just Kill a Man". AZ's solo debut, Operation Ivy, was released in 1997 and established him as one of the most successful West Coast rappers. His subsequent albums, including 2001's The Revolver and 2003's The Grand Hustle, were also well received by critics. AZ has collaborated with several other artists, including Snoop Dogg and Kanye West.
