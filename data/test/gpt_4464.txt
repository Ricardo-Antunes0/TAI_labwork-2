Fabric 47 is a 2009 album by American post-hardcore band Thursday. The album was released on June 10, 2009 through Epitaph Records.  The album has been described as post-hardcore, but with a "dramatic" and "emotional" sound.  The song "A Single Step" was used in the 2010 film "The A-Team".  In a review for "Alternative Press", Stephen Thomas Erlewine said that the album is "tight, energetic, and intense" and that it "shows how deftly Thursday can balance dramatic intensity with catchy hooks."
