Pterostylis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved pterostylis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.  Pterostylis microphylla is a terrestrial orchid endemic to New South Wales and Victoria. It has a single thin leaf, about long and wide, and a single white or creamy-white flower with green stripes on the sepals and petals. The flower is borne on a thin, wiry stem tall.
