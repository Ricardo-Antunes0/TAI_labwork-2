Karel Geraerts (; born 5 January 1982) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a centre back for Scottish Premiership club Rangers.  Karel Geraerts was born in Turnhout, Belgium on 5 January 1982. He began his career with local side Jupiler League side KFC Turnhout, where he made his senior debut in 2001. He had a brief spell at FC Twente before joining Premier League side Middlesbrough in 2003. Geraerts was part of the Middlesbrough team that won promotion to the Premier League in 2004. He made 42 appearances in all competitions for the "Boro" before joining Tottenham Hotspur in 2006.  Geraerts spent two seasons with Tottenham, making 42 appearances in all competitions. He then joined Blackburn Rovers in 2008, where he made 54 appearances in all competitions before signing for Sunderland on 31 January 2011. He made 54 appearances for Sunderland before joining Rangers on 13 July 2013.
