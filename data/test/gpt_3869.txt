Eternal Law is a 2012 ITV fantasy drama series which follows the journey of two young lawyers, Tom and Hester, who are reincarnated as ancient gods - Zeus and Athena.  Eternal Law is a unique and ambitious series that promises an entertaining and thought-provoking experience for viewers. The series is set in a mythical world and follows the journey of two young lawyers, Tom and Hester, who are reincarnated as ancient gods - Zeus and Athena.   Zeus and Athena are forced to work together to save their world from destruction. As they travel through time, they learn about the eternal law, which is the source of all life. They also have to face challenges such as fighting evil and saving the world from destruction.
