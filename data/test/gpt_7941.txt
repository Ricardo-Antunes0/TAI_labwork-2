Großbottwar (or Grossbottwar) is a town in the district of Göttingen, in Lower Saxony, Germany. It is situated on the banks of the River Weser, about 10 km southwest of Göttingen. The town has a population of about 20,000.  The town's economy is based on agriculture and its main exports are pork and potatoes. Großbottwar is also the home of the Göttingen University of Technology (TU), one of the most prestigious universities in Germany.
