T. V. Chandran (born 23 November 1950) is an Indian film director and screenwriter who has worked in the Tamil film industry. He directed the 1989 Tamil film Enna Enna Thyagam and the 1992 Tamil film Aaranya Kaandam.  Chandran was born on 23 November 1950 in a Tamil Hindu family in Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu. He completed his schooling from S.V.M. Arts and Science School, Tiruchirapalli and later graduated from Madras University with a degree in English Literature.  Chandran started his career as a journalist before turning to cinema in the early 1980s. He made his directorial debut with the 1989 Tamil film Enna Enna Thyagam, which became a commercial success. Chandran followed up this success with the 1992 Tamil film Aaranya Kaandam, which also became a critical and commercial success.
