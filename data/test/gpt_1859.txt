The Northern Professional Championship was a professional golf tournament played annually in Canada.  The Northern Professional Championship was first played in 1988 at the Thunderbird Country Club in Abbotsford, British Columbia. The event was discontinued after the 2006 edition.  The Northern Professional Championship was revived in 2007 as a part of the PGA Tour Canada's Canadian Tour. The event was played at the Goldeyes Resort and Casino in Whitehorse, Yukon. The event was discontinued after the 2011 edition.
