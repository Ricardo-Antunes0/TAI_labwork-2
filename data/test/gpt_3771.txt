HMAS LST 3022 was a Mark 3 Landing Ship, Tank (LST) of the Royal Australian Navy (RAN). She was built in the United States of America and commissioned into the RAN in 1963. LST 3022 was decommissioned in 1996 and sold for breaking in 2000.  HMAS LST 3022 was built in the United States of America and commissioned into the RAN in 1963.
