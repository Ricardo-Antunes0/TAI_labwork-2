The 2011 Rally México was the second round of the 2011 World Rally Championship. The rally took place over 25 stages, beginning on January 10 and ending on January 15.  The rally was won by Sébastien Ogier and teammate Jari-Matti Latvala, their second consecutive victory in the WRC. Toyota secured their fourth consecutive Manufacturers' Championship with Jari-Matti Latvala finishing second overall.
