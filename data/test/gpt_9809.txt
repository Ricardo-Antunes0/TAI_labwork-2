Courtenay was the son of Sir George Courtenay, of Ford, Devon, and Lady Mary Somerset, daughter of Henry Somerset, 3rd Duke of Beaufort. He was educated at Eton and Christ Church, Oxford. In 1687 he succeeded to his father's estates.   Courtenay was elected Member of Parliament for Devon in 1701 and 1702. He was elected MP for Tavistock in 1708. He died at the age of 63.   Courtenay married Dorothy Sidney, daughter of Sir Francis Sidney, in 1692. They had one son and two daughters.
