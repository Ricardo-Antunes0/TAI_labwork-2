Michael Wayne Atha (born December 30, 1979), better known by his stage name Yelawolf, is an American rapper and songwriter. He is best known for his single "Runnin' (Lose My Mind)", which reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).  Yelawolf was born in Gadsden, Alabama, and began rapping at the age of 12. He was first signed to Shady Records in 2002 and released his debut album, Love Story, in 2005. The album peaked at number 58 on the US Billboard 200 chart. He released his second album, Trunk Muzik, in 2009, which peaked at number 31 on the US Billboard 200 chart. His third album, Radioactive, was released in 2013 and peaked at number eight on the US Billboard 200 chart. Yelawolf has released three singles from Radioactive: "Dirt Nap", "Best Friend", and "Crazy".
