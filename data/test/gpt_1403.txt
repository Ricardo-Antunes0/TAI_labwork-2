Prof. RNDr. Jiří Horáček, DrSc. (* February 2, 1945 in Šumperk) is a Czechoslovakian historian and professor at Charles University in Prague.  Horáček graduated from the University of Prague in 1969 with a degree in history. He earned his doctorate in 1975, and became a professor at Charles University two years later. Horáček specializes in medieval Czech history, and has written extensively on the Hussite movement, the Reformation, and the Bohemian National Revival. He is also a published poet.
