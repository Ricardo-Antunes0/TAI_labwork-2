Boston Spa Academy (formerly Boston Spa Comprehensive School) is a secondary school in Boston Spa, North Yorkshire, England. It has a current enrolment of around 1,500 pupils.  The school was founded in 1898 as Boston Spa Comprehensive School by the Reverend Canon Charles Huddart and Miss Alice Garton Huddart. The school moved to its present site in 1927. In 2002 the school became a foundation school and changed its name to Boston Spa Academy.  The school has an extensive range of facilities, including a gym, two libraries, a dance studio, five music rooms, a technology suite, three sports halls and a performing arts centre. The school also has a well-equipped culinary unit with an oven and two hobbs.  Since 2007 the headteacher has been Michelle Whitehead.
