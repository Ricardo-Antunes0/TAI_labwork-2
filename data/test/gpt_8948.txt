The Mill at Anselma (a.k.a. Lightfoot Mill) is a historic grist mill located at Anselma in Schoharie County, New York. It was built about 1801 and is a 1-1/2 story, Federal style frame building on a stone foundation. It has a gable roof and features a front porch with two square posts and balustrade. The mill operated until the early 1920s.  The Mill at Anselma was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
