American singer-songwriter Sufjan Stevens has released twelve studio albums, one live album, one compilation album, thirty-seven singles, and forty-eight music videos. His debut album, Seven Swans, was released in 2000 and peaked at number eighty-one on the "Billboard" 200. His most recent album, Carrie & Lowell, was released in 2015 and peaked at number two on the "Billboard" 200.  Stevens has won three Grammy Awards—one for Best New Artist in 2002, one for Best Americana Album in 2005, and one for Best Spoken Word Album in 2014—and six American Music Awards—one for Favorite Male Artist in 2002, three for Favorite Pop/Rock Artist in 2003, 2004, and 2006, and one for Favorite Adult Contemporary Artist in 2016. He has also been nominated for a Brit Award and a Golden Globe Award.
