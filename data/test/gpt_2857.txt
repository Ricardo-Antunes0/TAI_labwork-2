Mingyi Nyo (; also spelled Minkyi-nyo; ; also known as the "Queen of Snacks") is a Burmese actress and restaurateur. She is best known for her role in the 2012 Burmese film, "The Lady", for which she won the National Film Award for Best Actress.  Mingyi Nyo was born on 12 December 1988 in the town of Monywa, Sagaing Region, Myanmar. She is the eldest daughter of seven siblings. Mingyi Nyo started her acting career in 2008 with a minor role in the film, "Insein". She then starred in the 2009 film, "Hluttaw Kyaw Zin", which won her an award at the 2009 Myanmar Film Festival. In 2012, she starred in the Burmese film, "The Lady", which won her the National Film Award for Best Actress.  Mingyi Nyo has also appeared in several television commercials and short films. In 2014, she opened her own restaurant, Mingyi Nyo's Kitchen, in Yangon.
