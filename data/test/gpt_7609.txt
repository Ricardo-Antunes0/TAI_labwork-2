Sir Sinake Giregire ( 1937 - 4 February 2007) was a Ugandan musician, composer and musicologist. He was born in Kabale, Uganda.  Giregire played the kabaka (king's drum), the mbira (a type of African harp), the kalimba (a type of harpsichord), and the guitar. He also composed music for plays and films.  He was a professor of music at Makerere University, where he served as dean of the Faculty of Music from 1990 to 1994. In 1996, he was made a Member of the Order of the British Empire (MBE).  Giregire died in Kampala, Uganda, aged 70.
