Marc Fest (born 1966, in Münster, Germany) is a German visual artist.  Marc Fest was born in 1966 in Münster, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. After completing his studies at the Johannes Gutenberg University of Mainz, he moved to Berlin in 1990 and started working as a graphic designer. In 1998, he founded his own studio, which he still operates today. Marc Fest's art is characterised by its intuitive use of imagery and a poetic approach to drawing and painting. His paintings often explore the human figure as a motif, and his work has been exhibited in museums and galleries around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Centre Pompidou in Paris, and the Royal Academy of Arts in London.  Marc Fest was awarded the 2011 Deutsche Börse Art Prize.
