{{Infobox video game |image= Pushmoworldlogo.jpg |caption= Pushmo Worlds official logo |developer= SCE Japan Studio |publisher= Sony Interactive Entertainment |release date= December 10, 2009 |platforms= PlayStation Portable  }}   Pushmo World is an upcoming puzzle video game for the PlayStation Portable. Developed by SCE Japan Studio, it was published by Sony Interactive Entertainment and released on December 10, 2009. The game revolves around the use of the PlayStation Move controller to manipulate various objects in the game world, and is inspired by the puzzle games Tetris and Columns.
