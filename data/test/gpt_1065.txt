Lake Menéndez (Lago Menéndez) is a large freshwater lake in the Andes of northwestern Chile. The lake has an area of about 1,460 km² and is the second largest in Chile.   The lake is located in the Aconcagua National Park and is fed by the Aconcagua River and several smaller streams. The average depth of the lake is 460 m and its maximum depth is 1,723 m. The lake has a surface area of 1,460 km² and a volume of about 8,000 km³.
