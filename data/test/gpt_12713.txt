Shibu Soren (born 11 January 1944) is an Indian film actor and producer. He has appeared in over 250 films in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu and Kannada language films. Soren has received three National Film Awards, two Filmfare Awards South and two Filmfare Awards North.  Shibu Soren was born on 11 January 1944 in a middle-class family in Bangalore, Karnataka, India to Madhavan and Saraswathi Soren. His father was a lawyer who later became the Deputy Commissioner of Bangalore.   Soren completed his schooling from Saint Joseph's School, Bangalore and then studied at the University of California, Berkeley where he graduated with a degree in political science. While studying at Berkeley, he started working as a stage hand for the American Shakespeare Company before making his film debut in 1969 in the Tamil film "Pulan" with Jayalalithaa.   Soren is married to Sarojini and they have two daughters, Twinkle Khanna and Divya Dutta.
