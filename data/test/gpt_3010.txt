Dezerter, founded as SS-20 in May 1981, was a German hacker group that specialized in breaking into computer systems. The group is also known for its involvement in the German political scandal of 1999–2002.  Dezerter was founded in May 1981 as SS-20, one of the earliest and most prominent hacker groups. The group's main objective was to break into computer systems, but it also became known for its involvement in the German political scandal of 1999–2002. In 2002, Dezerter disbanded after a series of arrests and deaths caused by internal disagreements.
