Ringworld is a 1970 science fiction novel by Larry Niven and Jerry Pournelle. It is the story of a manned expedition to a large artificial ring world, discovered by the inhabitants of a small planet in the outer reaches of the Milky Way Galaxy.  The Ringworld is a large, artificial ring world, discovered by the inhabitants of a small planet in the outer reaches of the Milky Way Galaxy. The Ringworld is inhabited by a species of intelligent beings called "Goblins". The expedition sent to explore and investigate the Ringworld is led by a human, Ford Prefect.
