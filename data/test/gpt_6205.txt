Supadio International Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Internasional Supadio, abbreviated as BNS Supadio) is an airport serving the city of Supadio, West Java, Indonesia. The airport is located near the village of Situbondo and is operated by PT Angkasa Pura I, a state-owned enterprise under the supervision of the Ministry of Transportation.  Supadio International Airport has a single runway that can handle aircraft with a wingspan up to 45 meters. In 2012, the airport handled 2,259 passengers and 6,656 metric tonnes of cargo. In 2016, the airport handled 8,133 passengers and 18,050 metric tonnes of cargo.
