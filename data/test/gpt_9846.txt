Kepler-10c is an exoplanet orbiting the G-type main-sequence star Kepler-10. The planet has a mass of 1.15 Earths and is at a distance of 0.057 AU from the star, which is slightly larger than the Earth-Sun distance. The planet was discovered by the Kepler space telescope in February 2015.  Kepler-10c is one of the first planets to be discovered using the radial velocity method. It was also one of the first planets to be found that was not part of a multi-planet system.
