Schiano was born in 1755 in Brescia, Lombardy, to a family of painters. He enrolled at the Academy of Fine Arts of Brescia in 1771 and won first prize in 1774. He painted mainly altarpieces and religious frescoes for churches and monasteries in his home region, but he also travelled to Milan, Venice, Padua and Rome. He died in Brescia in 1777.
