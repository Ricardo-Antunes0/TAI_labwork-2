The Fiji Meteorological Service (FMS) is a government agency responsible for providing weather and climate information to the Fiji Islands. The FMS was established in 1953 and is headquartered in Suva.  The FMS is a member of the World Meteorological Organization (WMO), the Regional Specialized Meteorological Centre for Oceania (RSMC Oceania), and the International Hydrographic Organization (IHO). The FMS provides forecasts and warnings for Fiji, as well as forecasts for the South Pacific region. It also issues guidelines for safe coastal navigation, forecasts tropical cyclones in the South Pacific, and provides data on climate, water resources, and coastal hazards.
