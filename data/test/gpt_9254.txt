Donald Hunsberger (born August 2, 1932 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania) is an American composer and conductor.  Donald Hunsberger was born in 1932 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He studied composition with Milton Babbitt at the University of California, Berkeley, and conducting with Leonard Bernstein at the Tanglewood Music Center. Hunsberger has worked extensively as a composer and conductor. He has composed music for film and television, as well as for chamber and symphony orchestra. His works have been performed around the world. Hunsberger is currently the conductor of the Boston Symphony Orchestra.
