Bhajarangi is a 2013 Indian Kannada-language fantasy romantic drama film directed by Somnath Bharti and written by Bhagawan and R. Phalguni. The film stars Rishabh Bachchan, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Prithviraj Sukumaran, and Dhruv Samant in lead roles.  The film is based on the Hindu epic Mahabharata and tells the story of two young lovers, Rishi (Rishabh Bachchan) and Draupadi (Samantha Ruth Prabhu), who are forced to part ways due to an arranged marriage. Twenty years later, they are reunited when Rishi comes to visit Draupadi's village.
