KTOY (104.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Grand Junction, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by Educational Media Foundation.  KTOY first went on the air in 1978 as KGJZ-FM. The station was owned by the Grand Junction Broadcasting Company. In 1980, the station changed its call letters to KTOY and adopted a contemporary Christian music format. The station was purchased by Educational Media Foundation in 2006.
