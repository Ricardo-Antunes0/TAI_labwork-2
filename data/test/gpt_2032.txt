WrestleMania 37 was the 37th annual WrestleMania professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). It took place on March 3, 2017 at the Alamodome in San Antonio, Texas. This was the first WrestleMania in seven years to be held outside of the United States.  WrestleMania 37 was headlined by a professional wrestling match between WWE World Heavyweight Champion John Cena and Roman Reigns.
