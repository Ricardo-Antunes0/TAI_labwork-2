Bernard Mizeki (sometimes spelt Bernard Mzeki;  born 21 November 1959) is a Kenyan author, best known for his novel The Book Thief.  Bernard Mizeki was born on 21 November 1959 in Kibera, Nairobi, Kenya. He attended Makueni High School and then studied English Literature at the University of Nairobi.  Mizeki's first book, the novella The White Mare, was published in 2002. His novel The Book Thief was published in 2009 to critical acclaim and won the 2010 Nobel Prize in Literature.
