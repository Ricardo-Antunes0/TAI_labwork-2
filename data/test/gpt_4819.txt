One Way Down is a post-hardcore rock band from Seattle, Washington. Formed in 2006, the band consists of vocalist and guitarist Travis Morrison, drummer Brian Cook, bassist Dan Donegan, and guitarists Kurt Ballou and Nate Mendel.  One Way Down's sound has been described as "a bruising blend of metalcore, hardcore punk and post-hardcore", with a heavy emphasis on melodic vocals and catchy melodies. They have released two full-length albums, "One Way Down" (2009) and "Southern Hospitality" (2013), both to critical acclaim. The band has toured extensively throughout the United States and Europe, playing alongside such notable acts as Thursday, At The Drive-In, The Devil Wears Prada, Bad Religion and Underoath.
