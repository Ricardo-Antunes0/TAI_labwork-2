CSAB, Inc., formerly called the Computing Sciences Association of Bulgaria, is a professional organization for information technology professionals in Bulgaria.  The CSAB was founded in 1992 as the Computing Sciences Association of Bulgaria. It changed its name to CSAB, Inc. in 1997. The organization's mission is to promote the interests of information technology professionals in Bulgaria. The CSAB also provides resources and support to its members and promotes the use of information technology in business and government.
