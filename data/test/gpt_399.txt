HD 172051 (86 G. Sagittarii) is a F-type giant star located about 370 light years away in the constellation Sagittarius. With a stellar classification of F2, it is a red supergiant star with the mass of about 150 Suns and the luminosity of about 330,000 times that of the Sun. It is also among the most rapidly rotating stars known, with a rotational velocity of about 220 kilometers per second.
