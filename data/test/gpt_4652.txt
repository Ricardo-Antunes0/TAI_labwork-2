Flanagan's compositions have been performed by the Philadelphia Orchestra, the Buffalo Philharmonic Orchestra, the New York Philharmonic, the National Symphony Orchestra, and many other ensembles. He has been awarded commissions by the American Academy of Arts and Letters, the Cleveland Institute of Music, and the Berliner Festspiele.  Flanagan studied at Yale University with Ives and Darius Milhaud. He later taught at Cornell University, where he served as Chair of the Music Department from 1968 to 1978.
