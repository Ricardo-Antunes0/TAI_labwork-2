Athamanthia is an Eastern Palearctic genus of skippers, containing the following species:  Athamanthia annulipes  Athamanthia bicolor  Athamanthia dorsalis  Athamanthia polymorpha  Athamanthia viridigaster   All of these skippers are small, with a wingspan of about 2.5 cm. They have a black body and wings, with yellow or green markings on the wings. They fly in spring and summer, feeding on nectar and insects.
