Seaboard Corporation is a diverse multinational agribusiness company. It is headquartered in Houston, Texas with operations in the United States, Canada, Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay. The company was founded in 1886 and has been in the agricultural products industry since its inception.  Today Seaboard Corporation is one of the world's leading producers of corn, cotton, soybeans, wheat and other crops. The company also manufactures livestock feed, poultry products, and agrochemicals. Seaboard Corporation employs approximately 8,000 people worldwide and generates annual sales of more than $5 billion.
