WOW Worship: Yellow is the 4th installment of the WOW Worship series. This album was released on October 4th, 2017, and features 14 worship songs written by Scott Craddock, Jonny Diaz, and Lexie Stone.  WOW Worship: Yellow is the perfect album for any worship leader looking to add some excitement and energy to their services. From the opening track "Come Alive" to the final anthem "We Sing", this album will help you bring your congregation to their feet in praise. Whether you're looking for inspiration or simply want to add some new worship songs to your repertoire, WOW Worship: Yellow is a must-have album.
