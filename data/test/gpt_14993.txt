Mississippi Highway 584 (MS 584) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Mississippi. It runs from Interstate 20 east to the Tennessee state line, where it becomes Tennessee Highway 66.  The highway was designated on July 1, 1958, and runs from I-20 east to the Tennessee state line. It is a part of the National Highway System from its western terminus to I-20 and from there to the Tennessee state line. MS 584 is also part of the Mississippi Heritage Trail.
