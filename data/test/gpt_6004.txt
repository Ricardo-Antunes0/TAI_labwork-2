In 1962, he joined the faculty at the University of California, San Francisco (UCSF), where he served as professor and chair of the biochemistry department until his retirement in 1987.  Isabekyan was a member of numerous scientific organizations, including the American Academy of Arts and Sciences, National Academy of Sciences, and European Molecular Biology Organization. He was also a fellow of the American Association for the Advancement of Science and the American Physical Society.  Isabekyan died on September 21, 1995 at the age of 80 after a long battle with cancer. He was buried at Holy Cross Cemetery in Colma, California.
