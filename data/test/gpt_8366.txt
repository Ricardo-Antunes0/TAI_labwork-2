Microfinance Focus is an English-language microfinance international directory.  Microfinance Focus is an online directory of microfinance institutions (MFIs) and microfinance programmes. The directory includes profiles of MFIs, their services and products, as well as contact information for key executives.  The Microfinance Focus directory was launched in 2006 and is currently updated with information on 1,500 MFIs from more than 60 countries. The directory is free to access and includes a search engine that allows users to find MFIs by country, type of institution, or service area.  Microfinance Focus is a joint project of the World Bank and the Inter-American Development Bank.
