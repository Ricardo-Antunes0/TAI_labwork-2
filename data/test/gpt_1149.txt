Dana Katherine Scully, MD, is a fictional character and the protagonist of the American science fiction-drama television series "The X-Files". She is portrayed by Gillian Anderson, who won a Golden Globe Award and a Primetime Emmy Award for her performance.  Dana Scully is a medical doctor and FBI special agent who works on the X-Files, a government-run paranormal investigation unit. She is highly intelligent and skilled in both science and law enforcement, often using her skills to investigate cases that defy explanation. She also has a strong sense of moral responsibility, which she frequently puts before her own safety.
