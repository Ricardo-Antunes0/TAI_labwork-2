He played with the Montreal Amateur Athletic Association (MAAA) club from 1895 to 1897 and then with the Montreal Wanderers of the National Hockey Association (NHA) from 1898 to 1901. He also played for the Quebec Bulldogs of the Eastern Canada Amateur Hockey Association (ECAHA) in 1902. Spittal was a member of the MAAA's championship team in 1896.  Spittal won a silver medal at the 1900 World Ice Hockey Championships with Montreal. He retired from playing in 1901 to become a team manager with the Wanderers. He also coached the club from 1907 to 1909 and 1912 to 1913. He was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1937.
