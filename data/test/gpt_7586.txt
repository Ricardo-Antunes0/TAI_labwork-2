KXTQ-CD, virtual channel 46 (UHF digital channel 36), is an American digital television channel that is licensed to El Paso, Texas. The channel is owned by the Hubbard Broadcasting subsidiary of the Tribune Media Company. The KXTQ-CD license was announced on May 10, 2009, as part of the virtual channel reallocation process following the shutdown of analog television service in the El Paso market. The channel began broadcasting on September 1, 2009.
