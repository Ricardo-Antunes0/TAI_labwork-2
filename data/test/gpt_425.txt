Joseph Bliss (1853 – 12 December 1939) was an Irish painter and etcher.  Bliss was born in Dublin, the son of an artist, John Bliss. He studied at the Dublin Metropolitan School of Art under William Orpen from 1872 to 1874 and at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Brussels, Belgium, under Jean-Léon Gérôme from 1876 to 1879. Back in Dublin, he exhibited with the Royal Hibernian Academy from 1878 to 1899.  He died in Dublin.
