The Ruoff Music Center (originally Deer Creek Music Center) is an outdoor amphitheater located in Noblesville, Indiana. Opened in 1988, the venue has a seating capacity of 13,000 people.  The Ruoff Music Center is home to the Indiana Pacers of the NBA, and the Indy Symphony Orchestra. The venue also hosts a variety of concerts and festivals throughout the year. In 2002, the Ruoff Music Center was designated a National Historic Landmark.
