Peter Nicholas Price (born 19 February 1942) is a Welsh Labour Party politician who has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for Clwyd South since 1997.  Born in Rhuddlan, Price was educated at Rhuddlan County School and the University of Wales, Aberystwyth. He joined the Labour Party in 1963 and became an active trade unionist. He was general secretary of the National Union of Mineworkers from 1978 to 1984, and was elected to Westminster as MP for Clwyd South at the 1983 general election.  Price served as Parliamentary Private Secretary to Hugh Gaitskell from 1984 to 1987, and to Neil Kinnock from 1987 to 1992. He served as Minister of State for Trade and Industry from 1992 to 1993, and Minister for Science, Technology and Industrial Strategy from 1993 to 1997.  Price retired from Parliament at the 2005 general election, but returned to Parliament in 2010 as MP for Flintshire East. He has been Parliamentary Private Secretary to Ed Miliband since September 2010.
