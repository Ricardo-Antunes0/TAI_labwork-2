Liu Peng (; born September 1951) is a Chinese politician who is currently serving as the Political Commissar of the PRC Central Military Commission.  Liu Peng was born in September 1951 in Huai'an, Jiangsu province. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1975. Liu served in a variety of posts within the party, including secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee, secretary of the Central Organization Department, and deputy commander of the Nanjing Military Region. He was elevated to the Politburo Standing Committee in 2007 and became a member of the Secretariat at the same time. In March 2013, he was appointed Political Commissar of the Central Military Commission, a position he continues to hold.
