Football Manager 2021 (officially abbreviated as FM21) is a football management video game developed by Sports Interactive and published by Sega. It is the eleventh installment in the Football Manager series, and was released on PC, macOS, and Linux on 7 October 2021.  Football Manager 2021 features a new artificial intelligence system called "Tactical Intelligence" which allows the game to adapt to player's behaviour in order to improve the team's performance. The game also introduces a new "Franchise Mode", which allows players to manage a club from its inception through to international success.   FM21 was generally well-received by critics, who praised its overhauled artificial intelligence system as well as the addition of new features.
