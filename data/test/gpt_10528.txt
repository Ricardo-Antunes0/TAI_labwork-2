Jonathan "Jacques" or "Jock" Boyer (born October 22, 1949) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player. He played his entire National Hockey League career with the Montreal Canadiens.  Boyer was drafted by the Canadiens in the second round, 28th overall, of the 1970 NHL Entry Draft. He played his first NHL game on October 15, 1970 against the Toronto Maple Leafs. Boyer was a member of the Canadiens team that won the Stanley Cup in 1975. He played his final game on April 6, 1978 against the Philadelphia Flyers.
