Parennefer is an ancient Egyptian noble and one of the most important figures in the reign of Pharaoh Akhenaten. He served as the chief adviser to Akhenaten and was also the father of Queen Nefertiti. Parennefer played a significant role in the religious reforms of Akhenaten and is credited with helping to create one of the most distinctive periods in Egyptian history. Despite his prominence during Akhenaten's reign, little is known about Parennefer's life prior to his involvement with the king.
