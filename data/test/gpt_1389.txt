The zebra shark (Stegostoma tigrinum) is a species of shark in the family Carcharhinidae. It is found in the eastern central and western Indian Ocean, the Gulf of Aden, the Red Sea, and the western Atlantic Ocean. Its natural habitat is open seas. The zebra shark is a medium-sized shark with a long snout and a broad, triangular fin. This species can reach a length of 1.8 m and a weight of 130 kg.
