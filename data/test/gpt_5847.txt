David Vann was born October 19, 1966, in Los Angeles, California. He is an American writer and filmmaker.  Vann's first novel, The Tin Drum (1991), was awarded the National Book Award for Fiction. His second novel, The Coming of the Fireflies (1994), was also awarded the National Book Award for Fiction. His third novel, The Road to Memphis (1998), was awarded the Pulitzer Prize for Fiction.  Vann's films include The Revenant (2015), Glory (1989), and Heaven's Gate (1980).
