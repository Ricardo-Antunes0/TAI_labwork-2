Philip Mustard (born 8 October 1982) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship club Burton Albion.  Born in Sheffield, Mustard began his career with his local side, Sheffield Wednesday, before moving to Newcastle United in 2003. He spent four years at the club, making over 150 appearances, before joining Blackburn Rovers in 2007. Mustard spent three seasons in Lancashire, helping the club to promotion to the Premier League in 2009. He subsequently moved to Cardiff City, where he played for two years before joining Burton Albion in 2013.
