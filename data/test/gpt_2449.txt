"The Marine Biologist" is the 78th episode of the American television series M*A*S*H. It originally aired on CBS on February 20, 1983.  It is the fourth episode of the show's eighth season and was written by Larry Gelbart and directed by Gene Reynolds.  Hawkeye has to leave for a few days to go to a medical conference, so he assigns one of the nurses, Margaret, to help him care for the camp. However, Margaret proves to be inept and ends up making many mistakes. Radar tries to teach her but it's not long before she is making even more mistakes. Ultimately, it is Klinger who teaches her how to be a marine biologist.
