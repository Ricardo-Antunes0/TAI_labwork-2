Demon's Crest, known in Japan as Demon's Souls, is an action role-playing video game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in Japan on March 14, 2010, and in North America on November 18, 2010.  Demon's Crest is a game where players explore the environment and fight enemies to gain experience, items, and magic to help them on their way. By killing bosses, players can access new areas. The game has a threefold story: the player begins by fighting their way out of the prison-city of Forlorn Hope before traveling to other locations to complete quests. In the end, they face off against the demon king.  The game received mixed reviews from critics. Some praised the graphics and combat while others found the game too difficult or criticized its short length.
