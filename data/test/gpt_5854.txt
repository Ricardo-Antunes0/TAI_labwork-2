The Ecuadorian cacique (Cacicus sclateri) is a small bird of the family Thraupidae. It is found in western Ecuador and far north-western Peru.  The Ecuadorian cacique is a small, brightly coloured bird with a long tail. The male is brighter than the female and has a more showy crest. It is a shy bird and is difficult to spot. It feeds mainly on insects, but can also take small prey. It nests in trees, but may also nest on the ground.
