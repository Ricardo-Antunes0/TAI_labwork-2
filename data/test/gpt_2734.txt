Kaydean is a British-American record and TV producer, songwriter, and musician.  Kaydean was born in the United Kingdom but moved to the United States as a child. She began her music career as a songwriter and record producer in the 1990s, working with artists such as Michael Jackson, Whitney Houston, and Celine Dion. Kaydean has also released two solo albums: "Introducing Kaydean" (2002) and "The Journey" (2009).  In 2006, Kaydean founded her own record label, Kaydean Records. The label has released albums by artists including Lisa Stansfield, Celine Dion, and Boyzone.  Kaydean has been involved in various charity projects over the years, including work with ChildLine and the Teenage Cancer Trust. In 2013, she was awarded an OBE for her services to music.
