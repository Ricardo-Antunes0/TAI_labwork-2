The Kallerup Stone, designated as runic inscription 1, is a runestone located in Kallerup, Denmark. It is one of the Rundata stones.  The inscription is in runic script and consists of eight runic lines, each consisting of two runes. The inscription is dated to the late 8th century.  The inscription is about a man named Hrólfr, who was killed in battle. The text reads:  "Here lies Hrólfr, son of Beinir. He died in battle by the edge of the meadow by the river."
