Wison Group (Chinese: 惠生集团) is an engineering, procurement, construction, and management company headquartered in Beijing, China. It is the largest construction company in China.  The company was founded in 1988 by He Wenbin and Wang Weiping. It has since grown to become one of the largest construction companies in China, with a portfolio of projects across a range of sectors including residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure. In 2017, the company was ranked as the 35th largest private enterprise in China by Forbes magazine.  Wison Group has a strong presence in the Beijing metropolitan area, with operations across 11 districts. The company has a workforce of over 50,000 employees.
