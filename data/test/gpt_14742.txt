Signature are English dance duo Suleman Mirza and Rebekah Welham, who have collaborated since 2003. Welham is a classically trained dancer and choreographer, and Mirza is a contemporary dancer and actor. The duo's work has been featured in various television programmes and films, including "Strictly Come Dancing" and "The Fault in Our Stars".
