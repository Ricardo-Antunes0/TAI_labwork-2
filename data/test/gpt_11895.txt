The Mauritanian is a 2021 legal drama film directed by Ousmane Sembene and written by Sembene and Abderrahmane Sissako. The film stars Modou Keita, Aïssa Maïga, and Souleymane Cissé.  The Mauritanian is set in a small town in the Sahara desert during the early 1970s. It tells the story of a young boy, Béji, who is growing up in this isolated community and striving to find his place in a world that is constantly changing around him. Béji is torn between his traditional Muslim upbringing and the new freedoms that he is discovering as a teenager. He struggles to find his footing in a society that is both oppressive and volatile. The Mauritanian is a timely and piercing exploration of identity, culture, and conflict.
