High Crime  is a 1973 Italian-Spanish crime film directed by Antonio Margheriti and starring Tomas Milian, Ornella Muti and Aldo Fabrizi. The film's plot follows a group of criminals who are forced to rob a bank in order to fund their heroin addiction.  High Crime was released in the United States as Organized Crime.  The film has been praised for its cinematography and its performances by its cast. However, it has been criticized for its slow pacing and its derivative plot.
