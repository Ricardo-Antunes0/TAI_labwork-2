Cindy Vela (born January 4, 1979) is an American actress, singer and model.  Vela was born in San Antonio, Texas. She began her career as a model and has appeared in campaigns for shoe companies such as Nike and Adidas. In 2002, she made her acting debut in the independent film Sideways. She has since appeared in films such as Cold Mountain (2003), The Village (2004), The Motorcycle Diaries (2004), Foolproof (2009) and Safe House (2012). In 2006, she released her debut album, entitled Cindy Vela.
