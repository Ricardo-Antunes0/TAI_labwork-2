Mantas Jankavičius (born 20 May 1980) is a Lithuanian professional basketball player. He is a member of the Lithuania national basketball team and competed in the 2008 Summer Olympics.  Jankavičius has played for BC Lietuvos rytas, CSKA Moscow, Lokomotiv Kuban, Real Madrid, and Panathinaikos. He has also played in the Spanish Liga for Unicaja Málaga and CB Granada. In June 2017, he signed with Olympiacos Piraeus of the Greek Basket League.  Jankavičius was born in Kaunas, Lithuania and started playing basketball at a young age. After playing for BC Lietuvos rytas for four seasons, he moved to CSKA Moscow for the 2000–01 season. He then signed with Lokomotiv Kuban for two seasons before moving to Real Madrid in 2003. In 2007, he joined Spanish Liga club Unicaja Málaga where he spent two seasons. He then moved to CB Granada where he averaged 10 points and 5 rebounds per game during the 2009–10 season.  In June 2017, Jankavičius signed with Olympiacos Piraeus of the Greek Basket League. On 12 October 2017, he made his debut in a loss against AEK Athens scoring 11 points off the bench.
