Michael Smith, usually referred to as Mikey Smith, (born on January 5, 1984) is an American professional wrestler and actor. He is signed to WWE, where he performs on the SmackDown brand under the ring name Mikey Thunder.  Smith is best known for his time in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA), where he was a one-time World Heavyweight Champion, and Ring Of Honor (ROH), where he is a one-time World Champion. Smith has also appeared in Japan for Pro Wrestling Noah and New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW).  Born and raised in Memphis, Tennessee, Smith began wrestling at the age of 10. After high school, he attended Memphis State University, where he played football. Smith decided to drop out of college to pursue a career in professional wrestling.  Smith made his professional wrestling debut in 2002 under the ring name "Maniac" Mikey Thunder. He spent the next few years performing for various independent promotions across the United States. In 2006, Smith signed with Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) and made his debut on the company's main roster that year. He quickly became a fan favorite, mainly due to his hardcorestyle wrestling.  In 2007, Smith won the TNA World Heavyweight Championship from then-champion Kurt Angle.
