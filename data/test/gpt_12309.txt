The 101st Division Sustainment Brigade is a subordinate unit of the 101st Airborne Division stationed at Fort Campbell, Kentucky. The brigade is a subordinate unit of the 101st Airborne Division Combat Aviation Brigade and provides logistical support to the division's units. The brigade is composed of approximately 2,400 Soldiers and civilians.  The 101st Division Sustainment Brigade was first activated on 10 October 1965 in Ohio as the 1st Infantry Division Support Brigade. The brigade was reactivated on 1 February 1993 in Louisiana as the 3rd Infantry Division Support Brigade, and was redesignated as the 101st Division Sustainment Brigade on 9 July 2004. The brigade has seen action in Operation Iraqi Freedom, Operation Enduring Freedom, and Operation Inherent Resolve. The brigade has been awarded a Meritorious Unit Commendation, a Joint Meritorious Unit Award, a Army Superior Unit Award, and two Army Distinguished Unit Awards.
