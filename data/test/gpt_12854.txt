Queens Hotel is a luxury hotel in Southsea, Hampshire, England. It is located on the south coast opposite Portsmouth Harbour. The hotel has an indoor pool and spa, a fitness centre, a tennis court and a cricket ground on site.   The Queens Hotel was built in 1888 on the site of an earlier inn. It was rebuilt in 1923 and refurbished in 2007. The hotel has been managed by the Hilton family since 1901.
