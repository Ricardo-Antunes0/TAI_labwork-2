"Bedroom Floor" is a song by English rock band Radiohead. It was written by Radiohead frontman Thom Yorke and recorded for the band's seventh album, "In Rainbows" (2007).  The song features a simple guitar riff and drum beat, with Yorke singing about being "on the bedroom floor". The song was released as the album's lead single on 10 October 2007, and reached number one on the UK Singles Chart. It also peaked at number two on the US "Billboard" Hot 100, becoming Radiohead's highest-charting single in the United States.  "Bedroom Floor" has been frequently cited as one of Radiohead's most popular and well-known songs, and has been performed live at several concerts throughout their career.
