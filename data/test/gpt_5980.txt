Some Nights is the second and most recent studio album by American singer-songwriter and record producer Halsey. It was released on September 3, 2018, by Astralwerks and Interscope Records. The album was preceded by the single "Stranger" which was released on August 10, 2018.  Some Nights is a pop-rock album that spans twelve tracks. It was produced by Halsey and Greg Kurstin with additional production from Mike Elizondo and Beau Youngberg. The album's lyrical content revolves around love, heartbreak, and self-reflection.  Some Nights has received generally positive reviews from music critics who commended its musicality and Halsey's vocals. At the 59th Annual Grammy Awards, Some Nights won the Best Pop Vocal Album award.   Halsey began recording Some Nights in early 2018 at various studios in Los Angeles. The album's lead single, "Stranger", was released on August 10, 2018. The song became a commercial success, reaching number one on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 and becoming Halsey's first number-one hit in the United States. The song has been certified quadruple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). Halsey also won Best Pop Solo Performance at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards for "Stranger".
