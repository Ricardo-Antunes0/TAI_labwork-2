The canton of Saint-Galmier is a French administrative division located in the department of Haute-Savoie. Its capital is the town of Saint-Galmier. The canton was created at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015.  Saint-Galmier is located in the eastern part of the department of Haute-Savoie, in the heart of the Alps. The canton is subdivided into 3 communes: Saint-Galmier, Val D'Isère and La Grave.  The canton of Saint-Galmier has a population of 10,936 inhabitants (2015). The majority of the population is made up of people who live in Saint-Galmier (8,506 inhabitants), followed by Val D'Isère (1,820 inhabitants) and La Grave (1,016 inhabitants).
