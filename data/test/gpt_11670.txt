Steven McGeady is a former Intel executive who served as the company's president and chief operating officer from 2003 to 2009. McGeady was responsible for Intel's product lines including its processors, chipsets, and graphics products.  McGeady is a co-founder of the Silicon Valley Leadership Group, a business improvement association for technology companies in Northern California. He also serves on the boards of directors for eBay and Juniper Networks. McGeady earned his bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Stanford University in 1984.
