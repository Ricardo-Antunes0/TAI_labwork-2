Jade Ell is a Swedish singer / songwriter. She has released one album, "Weightless" in 2014.  Jade Ell was born in Stockholm, Sweden in 1992. She started her music career as a singer and songwriter in 2010, when she released her debut album "Weightless". The album was produced by Stargate and featured the singles "High" and "Shine". In 2014, she released her second album "Good Days".
