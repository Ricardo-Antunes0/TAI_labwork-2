George Darko (born 12 January 1951 in London, England) is a British composer and musician.  George Darko was born in London on 12 January 1951. He grew up in Rugby, Warwickshire, and studied at the Royal Academy of Music, where he was awarded a Bachelor of Music degree in 1974.  Darko began his musical career as a guitarist and keyboard player with the progressive rock group Ambrosia. He also played with the groups Glass Hammer and Marillion before founding his own group, George Darko's Unit, in 1985. The Unit released two albums before disbanding in 1987.
