CD-R King (styled as cd-r king or cd-r king.com) is an online music store specializing in selling and renting music CDs and DVDs. It was founded in 1999 by Shawn Fanning and is headquartered in San Francisco, California.  CD-R King is one of the world's largest online music stores, with over 1 million items in its inventory (as of January 2007). The company sells music CDs, DVDs, and MP3s in both digital format and physical form. It also rents music CDs and DVDs, and offers a variety of music-related services, including music recommendation engines, a streaming music service, and a CD burning service.  CD-R King was founded in 1999 by Shawn Fanning. At the time, Fanning was a computer science student at the University of California, Berkeley. He started the company as an e-commerce platform for selling used CDs from his dorm room. In 2001, Fanning raised $1 million in venture capital from Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers and other investors. In 2002, CD-R King acquired MusicMatch's CD-burning business. In 2004, CD-R King acquired the online music store ReverbNation. In 2006, CD-R King acquired Beatport's digital rights management (DRM) business. In 2007, CD-R King was purchased by Amazon.com for an undisclosed amount.
