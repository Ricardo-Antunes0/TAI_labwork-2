Pitche Blende was an American garage rock/psychedelic rock band formed in Los Angeles, California in 1967. The band consisted of lead singer Bruce Woolley, guitarist Bob Nudel, bassist Jerry Scheff and drummer Skip Spence.  Pitche Blende is considered one of the pioneers of the garage rock genre. They are most known for their 1967 debut album "The Pitchessence", which was voted number 4 on Rolling Stone's "100 Greatest Albums of All Time". The album has been influential to a variety of rock bands and has been reissued several times.
