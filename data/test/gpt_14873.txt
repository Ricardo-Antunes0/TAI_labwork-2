Bong is a county in the north-central part of North Korea. It is bordered by Ryanggang Province to the east, North Hamgyong Province to the southeast, and Chagang Province to the south. The estimated population of the county is around 1,200,000.  Bong County is one of the poorest counties in North Korea. The main resources are agriculture and livestock. The main crops are maize, wheat, rice, and potatoes. There are also small amounts of sugar beets, tobacco, and other vegetables. The main livestock are cattle, pigs, sheep, and goats. There is also a small amount of poultry.
