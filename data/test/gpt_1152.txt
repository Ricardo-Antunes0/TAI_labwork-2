Ann Kirschner is an American entrepreneur, educator, and author. She is the founder and CEO of Ann Kirschner Enterprises, a global management consulting firm.  Kirschner was born in Worcester, Massachusetts, in 1963. She earned her undergraduate degree from Wellesley College in 1985 and her MBA from Harvard Business School in 1988.  Kirschner founded Ann Kirschner Enterprises in 1990. The company provides consulting services to businesses around the world. Kirschner has written two books, "The Lean Start-Up" (2011) and "Pulse: The New Science of Personal Leadership" (2013). She has also appeared on various television shows, including "The Oprah Winfrey Show", "60 Minutes", and "Charlie Rose".
