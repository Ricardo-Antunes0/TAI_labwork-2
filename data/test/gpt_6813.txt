Nathaniel Tan (born 1980) is a Malaysian-born Singaporean artist.  Tan was born in Malaysia in 1980, and moved to Singapore with his family when he was two years old. He attended the National Junior College of Arts and completed his tertiary education at the National University of Singapore, graduating with a BFA in Fine Arts in 2005.  Tan's art practice spans painting, installation, and performance. His early works were figurative, but he has since developed a more experimental approach, incorporating elements of abstract and conceptual art.
