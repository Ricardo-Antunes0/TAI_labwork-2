Teatro O Bando is a Portuguese professional stage theatre company. It was founded in 1996 by director Rui Álvares and actor Nuno Lopes.   The company has a permanent home at the Teatro da Trindade in Lisbon, Portugal, and has toured nationally and internationally since its inception. The company has received a number of awards, including the Prémio de Teatro de Lisboa in 1998, the Prémio Nacional de Artes e Letras in 2002, and the Prémio da Associação Portuguesa de Teatro in 2006.
