Slavko Ziherl (September 23, 1945January 21, 2012) was a Croatian composer, conductor and musicologist.  Ziherl was born in Zagreb, Croatia. He studied composition with Stjepan Å utej and conducting with Vinko Globokar at the Zagreb Academy of Music. He completed his studies in 1973 with the award of a diploma summa cum laude. From 1973 to 1987 he was conductor of the Croatian Radio Symphony Orchestra, and from 1987 to 1995 he was principal conductor of the Croatian National Opera. In 1995 he became professor of composition at the Academy of Music in Zagreb.  Ziherl's compositions include operas, ballets, chamber music, and choral works. He has also written extensively on Croatian music theory and history. His most famous work is the opera "Ognjenka" (1991), which has been staged worldwide.
