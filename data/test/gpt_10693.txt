She married a printer, Edward Honey, in 1839 and had two sons. Honey wrote her first novel, The Lancashire Witches, in 1851. It was followed by The Governess (1852), The Village (1853), and other novels. Her short stories were collected in A Choice Collection of Tales (1868) and Stories from the Village (1871). Honey also wrote poems and plays. She died in 1888 in London.
