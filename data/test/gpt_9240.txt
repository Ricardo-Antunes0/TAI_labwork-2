"A Boy" (, Sonyeoniyeo) is a song by South Korean singer and songwriter BoA. It was released as the lead single from her seventh studio album, "Crying Out" (2018).  The song was written by BoA, Kim Eunji, Cho Young-chan and Lee Soo-man, with production credits shared by the latter three. The song is about a young man who is in love with someone who doesn't love him back.  "A Boy" is an electropop song that lasts for three minutes and fifty-eight seconds. It is composed in the key of G major and BoA's vocals span from the low notes of F to the high notes of C#. Lyrically, the song talks about a young man who loves someone who doesn't love him back.  "A Boy" was first revealed as the lead single from BoA's seventh studio album, "Crying Out", on November 14th, 2017. The song was written by BoA, Kim Eunji, Cho Young-chan and Lee Soo-man with production credits shared by the latter three. The song was recorded in six days at Sohyang Recording Studio in Gangwon Province and it was mixed at The Hit Factory in New York City.
