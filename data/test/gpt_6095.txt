An-Nāziʻāt (, “Those Who Pull Out”, in English also known as the Nazi Party, National Socialist German Workers’ Party, Nationalsozialistische Deutsche Arbeiterpartei, or Nazism),[1] also known as the Al-Qaeda of the Arab World,[2] is an Islamist political party and paramilitary organization founded in Egypt in July 1941 by Hassan al-Banna.  The Al-Nusra Front is a Syrian rebel group affiliated with al-Qaeda.
