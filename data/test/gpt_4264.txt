Igor, or sometimes Ygor, is a stock character in Russian folklore and popular culture. He is typically depicted as a mischievous and cowardly man, but can also be honorable and brave.  Igor is one of the most popular and well-known Russian folkloric characters. He is usually depicted as a clumsy, cowardly man, but can also be brave and honorable. Igor is usually the butt of jokes, but can also be very helpful and good-natured.
