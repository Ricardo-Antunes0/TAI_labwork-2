Terror Against Terror (Hebrew: Terror Neged Terror, TNT) is an Israeli paramilitary organization which opposes terrorism and seeks to eradicate it. It was founded in 1996 by Israeli security officials.  Terror Against Terror is a pro-active organization that seeks to prevent terrorist attacks before they occur. The organization maintains a database of known terrorist organizations and their planned targets, and provides information to the Israeli security forces in order to prevent terrorist attacks.  Terror Against Terror also works to rehabilitate terrorist prisoners, providing them with education and job opportunities. The organization has received criticism for its close ties to the Israeli government, but it has also been praised for its efforts to combat terrorism.
