Operational transformation (OT) is a technology for managing and transforming the business operations of an organization. OT can be used in a wide range of industries, from manufacturing to service companies.   OT is a process that helps an organization become more efficient and effective by changing how it does its work. OT can involve changes in organizational structure, processes, and technology. It can also involve change management and communication.  OT can help an organization improve its performance by making its processes more streamlined and efficient. It can also help reduce waste and improve quality of products or services. OT can also help an organization reduce costs by automating tasks or improving management of resources.  OT is often used to improve the efficiency of large organizations such as factories and service companies. However, it can also be used to improve the efficiency of small businesses, such as restaurants and boutiques.
