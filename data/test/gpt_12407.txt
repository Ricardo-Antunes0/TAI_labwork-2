Kanche  is a 2015 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film written and directed by Krishna Vamsi. It stars Naga Chaitanya, Rakul Preet Singh, and Soundarya Rajesh in the lead roles. The film is produced by S. S. Rajamouli under his production house Sri Satya Sai Films.  Kanche is a story of two teenagers who fall in love and are torn apart by circumstances beyond their control. The story is set against the backdrop of rural Andhra Pradesh and the search for identity and meaning in life.
