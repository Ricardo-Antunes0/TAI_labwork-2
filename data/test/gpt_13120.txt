Kailasanathar Temple in Srivaikuntam, a village in the Tiruchirappalli district of Tamil Nadu, India, is a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Shiva. The presiding deity is Kailasanathar, also known as Kailasan, and is considered to be the most important of the 108 Vaishnava temples in Tamil Nadu. The temple complex is located on a hilltop and includes a large gateway tower and several shrines. The temple is open daily from 6:00 am to 10:00 pm.     The temple is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Tamil Nadu and receives around two million visitors annually. The temple is also a major centre of pilgrimage for Shaivaites and Vaishnavites.
