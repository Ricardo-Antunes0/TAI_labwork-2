Han Sun-hwa (born October 6, 1990) is a South Korean actress. In 2012, she made her debut in the film Thoroughbred.   Han Sun-hwa was born on October 6, 1990, in Seoul, South Korea. She is the eldest of three children born to a police officer father and a homemaker mother. Han began her acting career in 2012 with the film Thoroughbred, which was released to generally positive reviews. She has since appeared in a variety of television series and films, most notably the popular zombie movie Train to Busan, where she played the role of a survivalist high school student.
