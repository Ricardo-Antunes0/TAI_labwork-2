The Green-Rainbow Party (GRP) is the Massachusetts affiliate of the Green Party of the United States. The party was founded in 2000 and has fielded candidates for office in Massachusetts since 2002.  The GRP seeks to promote environmentalism, social justice, and peace through grassroots activism and electoral participation. The party's platform emphasizes a $15/hour minimum wage, universal health care, public education, and a federal prohibition on nuclear weapons.  The Green-Rainbow Party is one of two statewide Green parties in the United States, the other being the Alaska Green Party. As of 2012, the party had registered members in Massachusetts representing 0.2% of the population.
