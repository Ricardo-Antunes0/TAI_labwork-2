Henry Manuel Andrade (born April 17, 1962) is an American actor, director, and producer. He has appeared in such films as The Crow, Dead Man Walking, The Machinist, and Sin City.  Andrade was born in Santa Monica, California, the son of actress Patti LuPone and actor/director Henry Andrade. He is of Portuguese descent. He has two sisters, actress Christine Ebersole and singer/songwriter Katharine Andrade.  Andrade first came to prominence in the early 1990s with his roles in The Crow and Dead Man Walking, both directed by Peter Jackson. These roles earned him widespread critical acclaim and Saturn Award nominations for Best Actor. He went on to appear in such films as The Machinist, Sin City, and The Town. In 2009, he directed and starred in the drama film Rules Don't Apply.
