Chloe Elizabeth Bailey (born July 1, 1998), also known by her stage name Chloe Bailey, is an American singer and songwriter. Bailey began performing as a child, and eventually signed a recording contract with Warner Music Group's RED Distribution imprint in 2016. Her debut studio album, I Don't Wanna Live Forever (2018), was released to critical acclaim.  Bailey's music is inspired by pop, rock, soul, and R&B genres. She has been noted for her powerful vocal delivery and emotive songwriting. Bailey has appeared on various television shows, including The Voice (US), The X Factor (UK), and The Late Late Show with James Corden (US). In 2018, she received a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best New Artist.
