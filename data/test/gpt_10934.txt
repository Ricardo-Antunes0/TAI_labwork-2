German submarine U-409 was a Type VIIC U-boat of Nazi Germany's Kriegsmarine.  U-409 was built at the AG Weser shipyard in Bremen and commissioned on 30 October 1944 under the command of Oberleutnant zur See Kurt Scharf. The submarine operated with the 5th U-boat Flotilla from Dec 1944 to May 1945.  After the end of World War II, U-409 was captured by the Allies and commissioned into the British Royal Navy as HMS Unseen (UC 10).
