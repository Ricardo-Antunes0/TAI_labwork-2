Judge John Deed is a British legal drama television series that first aired on Channel 5 in the United Kingdom on 5 October 2013 and concluded on 10 May 2019.  The series is set in the fictional town of Darrowby and follows the lives of the town's local judiciary. The show was created by Sally Wainwright and directed by Elizabeth Kilgarriff.
