A Catholic Bible is a Christian Bible that is used by members of the Catholic Church. The Catholic Bible is distinguished from other Christian Bibles by its use of the Latin Vulgate as the official text, and its use of the Old Testament and the New Testament as two separate books.  The Catholic Bible is one of the most widely used Christian Bibles in the world. It is used in churches, monasteries, schools, and homes around the world. The Catholic Bible is also one of the most popular Christian Bibles in print.
