The station was opened on October 1, 1987, and closed on March 31, 2007.  Chiryū-juku is located in the city of Ōsaka, in the central area of the city. The station is adjacent to JR West's Ōsaka Station and Hankyu Department Store Ōsaka Station.  The station has two side platforms serving two tracks. The station building is located on the west side of the tracks, and has a waiting room and a staffed ticket window. There is also a small shop on site.
