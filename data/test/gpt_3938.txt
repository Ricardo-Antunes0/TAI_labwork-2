He was also the Vice President of the National Council, the upper chamber of the Swiss Federal Parliament.  Davos was born in 1918 in Athens, then part of the Ottoman Empire. He moved to Switzerland with his family in 1923 and settled in Zurich. Davos started his own business, importing textiles, in 1938. He became a Swiss citizen in 1943.  Davos served as the Mayor of Zurich from 1954 to 1962. He also served as the President of the Swiss Confederation from 1952 to 1954. Davos was elected to the Federal Council in 1951 and served until 1954, when he was appointed Prime Minister by Federal President Hans Rudolf Pfeffer. Davos resigned after just one year in office, due to political differences with Pfeffer.  Davos died in 1976 at the age of 61.
