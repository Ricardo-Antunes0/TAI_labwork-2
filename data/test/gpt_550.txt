Dolphin made his first-class debut for England against the West Indies at Lord's in 1928. He played eleven further first-class matches for England, the majority of which were against the touring Indian teams. He scored a total of 1,981 runs at an average of 34.37, with a high score of 146 not out against India in 1934. He also took 157 wickets at an average of 32.14, with a single five-wicket haul against Australia in 1933/34.
