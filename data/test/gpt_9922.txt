Leonid Alexandrovich Kolobov (Russian: Леонид Александрович Колобов; born 26 March 1945) is a Russian chess Grandmaster. Kolobov has won many international tournaments, including the World Chess Championship 1984.  Kolobov was born in Moscow, USSR, in 1945. He became a chess Grandmaster in 1975.
