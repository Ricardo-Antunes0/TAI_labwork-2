From 1965 to 1972 he was an animation director at Toei Company, where he worked on several well-known titles such as "Golgo 13", "The Castle of Cagliostro", and "Sherlock Jr.". He also directed several episodes of "Robot Carnival". In 1973, he founded his own animation studio, Tatsuo Productions.  Okamoto is most famous for his work on "Astro Boy". In 1979, he won the Japan Academy Prize for Best Animated Film for his work on the film.
