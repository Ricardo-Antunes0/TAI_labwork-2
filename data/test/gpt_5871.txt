Life is the debut studio album by American hip hop group Sigma. It was released on July 7, 1999, by Ruff Ryders Entertainment and Jive Records. The album features production from The Neptunes, The Hitmen, Pharrell Williams and Chad Hugo.  Life received generally mixed reviews from critics. It debuted at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart, selling 161,000 copies in its first week. However, the album's sales decreased over time and it was eventually certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). Life has been certified platinum in Canada and multi-platinum in other countries.  The album spawned two singles: "Get Up" and "Made You Look". "Get Up" reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and became Sigma's first number-one single on the chart. "Made You Look" peaked at number two on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and became their second consecutive top ten single on the chart.  Life was nominated for Best Rap Album at the 2000 Grammy Awards but lost to Dr. Dre's 2001 album2001. Life was also nominated for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group at the 2000 Grammy Awards but lost to LL Cool J's song "I'm Bad".
