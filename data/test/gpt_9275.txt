Front and Center is a television series that premiered on the Nickelodeon cable network in the United States on September 25, 2017. The show is produced by Paramount Pictures and Nickelodeon.  The series follows the lives of five friends, who are all actors, as they experience the highs and lows of working in Hollywood. The show is set in the fictional Backlot Studios, which is a rundown but thriving Hollywood studio.  The series was created by Francie Calfo and is executive produced by Dan Schneider and Suzanne Martin.
