Lucy Jumeyi Ogbadu (born 25 September 1953) is a Nigerian actress, film producer and director.  Lucy Jumeyi Ogbadu was born in Lagos, Nigeria on 25 September 1953. She attended the University of Ibadan, where she earned a degree in English Literature. Ogbadu began her acting career in the late 1970s, appearing in several television series and films. She achieved greater acclaim for her roles in the films "Bad Boys" (1996) and "The Wedding Party" (1998). Ogbadu has also produced and directed several films, including the critically acclaimed "The Wedding Party 2" (2005).
