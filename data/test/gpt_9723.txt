"Gifted" is a song by American rapper Eminem from his eighth studio album Kamikaze. It was released on August 11, 2018, as the album's lead single.  Eminem had been working on the song since early 2018, and revealed it during an interview with Zane Lowe on Beats 1. "Gifted" is a dark and introspective song, featuring guest appearances from Royce da 5'9", Big Sean, and Pharrell. The track speaks to Eminem's struggles with addiction and his feelings of depression, and has been described as a "dark and emotional" track.  The song received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its lyrical content and Eminem's vocals. It debuted at number one on the US Billboard Hot 100, becoming his ninth number one single on the chart. It also became his seventh number one single in Australia, his eighth in Canada, and his ninth in the UK. "Gifted" was certified Platinum in the United Kingdom for sales of over 300,000 copies.
