Warren Row is a village in Berkshire, England. It is situated east of Reading, and south of Newbury. The A4 intersects the village midway between its two main streets, Warren Road to the north and High Street to the south.  The village has a population of around 1,000 people. There is a pub, a post office, a shop, a primary school and a church. The village also has a cricket ground and a football pitch.
