Vestal High School is a public high school in Vestal, New York, United States. It is the only high school in the Vestal Central School District.  Vestal High School was opened in 1891 as a two-year school. In 1927, it became a four-year institution. In 1974, it was upgraded to a six-year school. In 2000, it became a seven-year school. In 2007, the school received its current name.  Vestal High School has a total of 1,600 students and employs 69 full-time and part-time staff members. The school offers more than 60 courses and programs, including those in business, engineering, health sciences, mathematics and science. Vestal High School is accredited by the New York State Department of Education and the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business.
