Rich Brightman (born April 17, 1990) is an American professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Brightman played college hockey with the University of Minnesota-Duluth Bulldogs. After graduating from college, he signed a three-year entry level contract with the Edmonton Oilers on May 2, 2012. Brightman made his NHL debut on October 5, 2013 against the Vancouver Canucks.  On July 1, 2016, Brightman left the Oilers as a free agent to sign a two-year contract with HC Sochi of the KHL.
