A garniture is a number or collection of objects, typically decorative, used in the setting of a particular type of object or structure.  Garniture is the term used for a collection of objects, typically decorative, used in the setting of a particular type of object or structure. In the 18th century, it was fashionable for wealthy aristocrats to surround themselves with a variety of fine furnishings, including paintings, sculptures, tapestries, and furniture. Today, garniture is often used to enhance the appearance of a room without requiring any structural changes.
