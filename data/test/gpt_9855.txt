Dark 7 White is a Hindi-language Indian movie directed by Vikas Bahl and produced by Aditya Chopra under the banner of Yash Raj Films. The film stars Shahid Kapoor, Ranveer Singh, Alia Bhatt and Diljit Dosanjh in lead roles.  The story is set in the backdrop of the 1989 Mumbai riots, and revolves around two brothers - one white and one black - who are pitted against each other in a race to save their families.
