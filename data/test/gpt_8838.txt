The Hmong Archives, formerly known as Hmong History Archives, is a repository of Hmong-language historical materials in the United States. It was established in 1984 by Hmong scholars in order to preserve and promote the history of the Hmong people. The archive contains manuscripts, photographs, and other historical artifacts related to the Hmong people and their culture.  The Hmong Archives is located in St. Paul, Minnesota. The archive is open to the public free of charge.
