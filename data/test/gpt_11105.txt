23rd Street Grounds, also known as State Street Grounds and later as New State Street Grounds, was a baseball stadium in Bloomington, Illinois. It was the home of the Bloomington Bluejays baseball team from 1891 to 1952. The ballpark was located on the northeast side of State Street, just east of the intersection with New State Street.  The ballpark was built in 1891 and originally called 23rd Street Grounds. It was renamed State Street Grounds in 1916, and New State Street Grounds in 1948. The Bluejays played their last game at the stadium on June 3, 1952. The ballpark was demolished in 1957.
