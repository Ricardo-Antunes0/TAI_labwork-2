Dayalan is a 1941 Indian, Tamil-language film directed by M. S. R. Das and produced by A. P. Srinivasan. It stars Kannada actor S. V. Ramakrishna and Tollywood actress Savitri in the lead roles. The film has music composed by C. R. Subbu.  The story is about a woman, Dayalan, who is in love with a man, Venni, but he marries another woman, Parvathy. Dayalan then decides to commit suicide but is saved by Venni who subsequently re-marries Parvathy.
