Dr. Afif bin Bahardin (born 13 May 1969 in Muscat) is a Omani physician and politician. He is the Minister of Health of Oman.   He was educated at Sultan Qaboos University, graduating with a medical degree in 1993. He has worked as a doctor in Muscat since then.  In 2001 he was appointed Deputy Director General of the National Hospital in Muscat, a post he held until 2003. That year he was also appointed Director General of the National Institute of Health, remaining in that position until 2007.  From 2007 to 2011 he served as Minister of State for Planning, Development and Investment. From 2011 to 2013 he served as Minister of State for Interior.   In 2013 he was appointed Minister of Health, a post he still holds.
