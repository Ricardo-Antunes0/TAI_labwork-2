Tiiu Kera (born July 21, 1945) is an American artist who uses traditional techniques and materials in her art, often working with natural dyes and minerals.  Tiiu Kera was born in Honolulu, Hawaii on July 21, 1945. She is of Samoan descent. Kera studied art at the University of Utah and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston. She has exhibited her work throughout the United States, as well as in Guam, Tonga, and New Zealand. In 2007, she was awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship.
