Roman Józef Wojtusiak (28 December 1906-5 December 1981) was a Polish mathematician who made important contributions to the theory of calculus of variations, the theory of elasticity, mathematical analysis and probability.  Wojtusiak was born in Łódź, Poland in 1906. He studied at the University of Warsaw, where he obtained his doctorate in 1930. He then worked at the University of Krakow, where he became a professor in 1945. He retired in 1974.
