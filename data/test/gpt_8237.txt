Masao (written: 正雄, 正夫, 正生, 正男, 正郎, 正哉, 正智) is a Japanese name. Notable people with the name include:   1) Masao Akio (born 1966), Japanese politician and Managing Director of the Tokyo-based private equity firm Sentinel Capital Partners 2) Masao Furusawa (1915-1993), Japanese composer 3) Masao Nakamura (born 1960), Japanese composer 4) Masao Tsurumi (born 1966), Japanese singer-songwriter 5) Masao Yamanaka (born 1962), Japanese basketball player
