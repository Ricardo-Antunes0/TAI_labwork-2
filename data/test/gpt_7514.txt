Kevin E. Lowe is a finance executive who served as president and CEO of the NHL's Anaheim Ducks from 2006 to 2015. Lowe was previously the general manager of the Colorado Avalanche from 2001 to 2006.  Lowe played lacrosse for Johns Hopkins University and was a three-time All American. He then played professional lacrosse for the Boston Blazers, Nashville Venom, and Philadelphia Wings. Lowe served as the goalie coach of the United States national lacrosse team from 2007 to 2012, and was an assistant coach for Team USA at the 2016 World Lacrosse Championship.
