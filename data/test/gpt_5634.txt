Fulvalene (bicyclopentadienylidene) is the member of the fulvalene family of aromatic hydrocarbons, which consists of molecules with a five-member ring system. Fulvalene is the simplest fulvalene molecule, consisting of a single carbon atom bonded to four hydrogen atoms. Fulvalene is a colorless, odorless, and tasteless compound with a melting point of −113 °C.
