Acatech (styled acatech), founded in 2002 and headquartered in Lyon, is a French technology company specialising in the development and marketing of software as a service (SaaS) and cloud computing solutions.  Acatech has a portfolio of software applications that provide customers with a range of services such as customer relationship management (CRM), enterprise resource planning (ERP) and supply chain management (SCM). The company's flagship product, Acatech CRM, is a customerrelationship management solution that allows businesses to manage their customer relationships across channels, including email, web, phone and social media.   Acatech's cloud computing solutions allow businesses to access its software applications from anywhere in the world. These solutions include Acatech Cloud, which provides customers with access to Acatech's CRM, ERP and SCM applications; Acatech On Demand, which allows businesses to access Acatech's CRM, ERP and SCM applications on a pay-as-you-go basis; and Acatech Cloud for Businesses, which provides businesses with access to Acatech's full range of cloud computing services.
