Transparent City  is a romance novel by American author Jennifer L. Armentrout. It is the first book in the Lux series.  Lux is a city where the citizens can see through to the other side, where everyone has their secrets and no one is safe. When Dex, a street-wise vagrant, is caught in a robbery and taken captive, he has no choice but to enter Lux and become its newest citizen. Dex quickly befriends Aiden, an aristocrat who is hiding a secret, and Ria, a rebellious artist who is struggling to find her place in Lux. As they all try to navigate the dangerous and confusing cityscape, they must fight to keep their secrets hidden or risk exposing themselves and their deepest desires to the public eye.
