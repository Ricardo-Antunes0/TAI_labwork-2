Madison Kyle Bumgarner (born August 1, 1989), nicknamed "Bumgarner", is an American professional baseball pitcher for the San Francisco Giants of Major League Baseball (MLB). He has also played for the Kansas City Royals and the Fresno Grizzlies.  Bumgarner made his MLB debut with the Giants in 2010 and has become one of the most dominant pitchers in the league. He led the Giants to the 2014 World Series championship, becoming only the fourth player in MLB history to win a championship in his rookie season. Bumgarner has also won two Cy Young Awards, in 2013 and 2016.
