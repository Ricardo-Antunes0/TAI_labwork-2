Ricardo Arap Bethke Galdames (born 12 March 1990) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays for UD Las Palmas, as a midfielder.  Ricardo Arap Bethke was born in Zaragoza, Aragon, on 12 March 1990. After starting out with local Zaragoza CF, he joined Real Madrid's youth ranks in 2006, aged only 14. He made his senior debut with the B-team in the Segunda División B on 10 August 2009, coming on as a second-half substitute in a 2–1 home win against CD Lugo.  Bethke made his first team debut on 20 October 2009, starting and playing the entire 1–1 away draw against Racing Santander for the Copa del Rey. On 31 January 2010 he signed a new contract with Madrid until 2013.  On 15 July 2011, Bethke was loaned to Racing Santander for one year, with the option to make the move permanent at the end of the season. He made his La Liga debut on 23 August 2011, starting and playing the entire 2–0 home win against Valencia CF; he scored his first goal in Spanish top flight football on 4 February 2012, netting the winning goal in a 3–2 away victory over Real Betis.
