Hans Mathisen (born 27 July 1967 in Trondheim) is a Norwegian author and journalist.  Mathisen was educated at the University of Oslo, where he studied journalism. He has worked as a journalist for the national newspapers "Aftenposten" and "Dagbladet", and for the magazines "Nettavisen" and "Skriver". He has also written books about Norwegian politics and culture.  Mathisen is married to the writer Mari Skar and they have two children.
