Jamie Harrison (born 19 November 1990) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship club Nottingham Forest.  Born in Stoke-on-Trent, Harrison started his career with hometown club Stoke City, making his first-team debut in 2007. He spent time on loan at Northampton Town, where he made 36 appearances and scored one goal, and at Rotherham United, where he made 59 appearances and scored six goals. He signed for Forest in January 2014.  Harrison has represented England at U21 level.
