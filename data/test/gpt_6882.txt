The Ripon Spa Baths are a grade II* listed building in Ripon, North Yorkshire, England.  The Ripon Spa Baths are a Grade II listed building in Ripon, North Yorkshire, England. The baths were built in 1868-9 and are on the north side of the Market Place. They were designed by T.H. Wyatt and are faced with ashlar dressings and a tiled roof. The baths consist of an octagonal central pool with a domed ceiling, flanked by two smaller pools on either side. There is also a Turkish bath and a cold plunge pool.
