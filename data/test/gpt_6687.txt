Anti-Japan War Online  is a Chinese-produced website that provides extensive information about the Anti-Japan War of 1937-1945. The website was created by a team of volunteers and is updated regularly.  The Anti-Japan War was a conflict fought between the Republic of China (ROC) and the Empire of Japan from 1937 to 1945. The conflict began when the Republic of China (ROC) attempted to resist Japanese aggression in China, and eventually led to World War II. Over 3 million people were killed in the war, making it one of the deadliest in history.  The website provides detailed information about the war, including historical background, events, and battles. It also includes information about Japanese atrocities during the war, including the Nanjing Massacre and Tokyo War Crimes Trial. The website is an excellent resource for students and historians interested in learning more about the Anti-Japan War.
