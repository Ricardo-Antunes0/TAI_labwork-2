Kattinakere (ಕಟ್ಟಿನಕೆರೆ) also called B Sagadde is a village in Belgaum district in the southern state of Karnataka, India.  The village is located in the Yellapur taluk of Belgaum district and is around 95 kilometers from the district headquarters, Belgaum. The nearest town is Koppala.  The village has a population of around 2500 people, with a majority of the population being Hindu Brahmins. There are also a few Muslim families in the village. The main occupation of the people is farming. Kattinakere has a primary school and a government hospital. There are also a few temples in the village. The most famous temple is the Someshwara temple.
