The name Bertha has been used for at least four storms in the Atlantic Ocean. The first Bertha was a Category 1 hurricane which made landfall on the Yucatan Peninsula on September 16, 1961. The second Bertha was a Category 3 hurricane which made landfall in southeastern Texas on September 25, 1985. The third Bertha was a Category 2 hurricane which made landfall in southeast Louisiana on October 10, 2003. The fourth and most recent Bertha was a Category 1 hurricane which made landfall in the Florida Keys on October 8, 2008.
