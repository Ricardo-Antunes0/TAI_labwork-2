Digital Photography Review, also known as DPReview, is a website and magazine devoted to digital photography. The website was founded by editor-in-chief David Pogue in 1997, and since then it has become one of the most highly respected resources for digital photography advice and news.   DPReview offers comprehensive coverage of digital photography technology, photography tips, product reviews, firmware updates, digital imaging seminars, and much more. The site is updated daily with news, reviews, features, and tutorials covering everything from basic photography to advanced techniques.   DPReview also publishes a monthly magazine, called "Digital Photography Review". The magazine contains comprehensive coverage of digital photography technology, along with tips and tricks for improving your photography skills.
