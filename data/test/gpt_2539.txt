She attended the Art Institute of Chicago, where she studied with Milton Avery and Robert Henri.  Wood's illustrations appeared in magazines and books throughout her career, including "The New Yorker", "The Saturday Evening Post", "Reader's Digest", "Life", and "The New York Times". She also created covers for books by J.D. Salinger and E.L. James.  Wood died of a heart attack in 1993 in Greenwich Village, New York City at the age of 80.
