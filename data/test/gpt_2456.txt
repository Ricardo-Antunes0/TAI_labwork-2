Psyanotic Is the debut album of American post-metal band Sigh, released on August 5, 2009 through Century Media Records. The album was produced by Jason Suecof and recorded at Woodland Sound in Nashville, Tennessee.  Psyanotic is an experimental post-metal album. It is the debut album of American post-metal band Sigh. It was produced by Jason Suecof and recorded at Woodland Sound in Nashville, Tennessee. The album has a shoegaze and post-rock influence, and lyrically, it explores the theme of mental illness.  The album has been well received by critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 76, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  Psyanotic was nominated for the 2010 Grammy Awards for Best Metal Performance.
