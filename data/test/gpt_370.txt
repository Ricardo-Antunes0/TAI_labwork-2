Aatadista is a 2008 Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy film written and directed by K. S. Ravikumar. The film features Akkineni Nagarjuna and Trisha Krishnan in the lead roles, with music composed by G. V. Prakash Kumar. The film was released on 7 September 2008 to mixed reviews, but became a commercially successful venture.  Rajesh (Nagarjuna) is an unemployed youth who lives with his father (Girish Karnad) and sister (Trisha Krishnan). Rajesh falls in love with Radha (Trisha Krishnan), but she is already married to Gopi (Akkineni Nagarjuna). Rajesh's friends help him find a job, but he is fired from every one of them for being unprofessional. Eventually, Rajesh lands a job as a driver for a wealthy man named Raghu (Sumanth Raghavan). Radha also starts working as a maid for Raghu, and they eventually start seeing each other. However, Gopi finds out about their relationship and tries to ruin it.
