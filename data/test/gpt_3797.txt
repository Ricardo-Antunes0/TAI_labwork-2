The Automatic Hate is a 2015 American black metal / doom metal band from St. Cloud, Minnesota. The band is composed of vocalist Mike Hranica, guitarists Tyler Smith and Joshua Eichelberger, drummer Ben Koller, and bassist Dustin Koller.  The Automatic Hate was formed in the summer of 2014 by vocalist Mike Hranica (formerly of the hardcore band Conflict), guitarists Tyler Smith and Joshua Eichelberger (both formerly of the hardcore band Terror) and drummer Ben Koller (formerly of the hardcore band Unbroken). After playing their first show together in early 2015, the band entered the studio to record their debut album, "The Black Crown", which was released on August 25th, 2015 through Prosthetic Records.
