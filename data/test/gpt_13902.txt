Vaea Naufahu Anitoni (born 20 September 1970) is a New Zealand politician who is currently the Deputy Leader of the Green Party.  Anitoni was born in Palmerston North, New Zealand on 20 September 1970. She has a background in environmental studies and has worked as an environmental consultant. Anitoni first became involved in politics as a member of the Earth First! movement, where she campaigned for the protection of endangered wildlife. She joined the Green Party in 2003 and has since been a key figure in its policy development.  Anitoni was first elected to Parliament as the Member of Parliament for Mana in the 2011 general election, having won the seat by just over 1,000 votes. She was re-elected in the 2014 general election with a substantially increased majority. In addition to her role as Deputy Leader of the Green Party, Anitoni also serves on several parliamentary committees.
