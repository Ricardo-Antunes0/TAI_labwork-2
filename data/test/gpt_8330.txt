Bakemono no e (化物之繪, "Illustrations of Supernatural Creatures") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Mitsuhiro Ito. The manga follows the story of a high school student named Koyomi Araragi who is possessed by a Bakemono, a type of monster that is said to be extinct.  The series has been published in the Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since 2009, and has been collected into fifteen volumes. A spin-off manga, titled Kemono Friends (動物仲間, "Animal Companions"), was launched in 2013 and also written and illustrated by Ito.
