Bachelors At War is a reality show following twenty-two single men as they compete for the love of one woman.   The show is produced by Bachelor Nation Productions and stars former Bachelor contestants from The Bachelor, The Bachelorette, and other reality TV shows.   The show is filmed in Paradise, California.   The premise of the show is that the men are all competing for the love of one woman. During the course of the show, the men will date multiple women and compete for her heart.   At the end of the show, one man will be chosen to be the winner and he will be given a marriage proposal from the woman he loves.
