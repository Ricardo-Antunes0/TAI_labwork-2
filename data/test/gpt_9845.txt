"Searching" is the 150th episode of the ABC television series, Desperate Housewives. It was originally broadcast on April 25, 2008.  In the episode, Bree is desperate to find her missing daughter and hires Tom to help her. Carlos is also looking for his daughter and hires Bree's husband, Mike, to help him. Meanwhile, Karen is trying to find out who killed her ex-boyfriend.  The episode was written by Terri Miller and directed by Beth McCarthy-Miller.
