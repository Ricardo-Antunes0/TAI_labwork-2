Live! Bootleg is a double live album by the American rock band Cheap Trick. It was recorded on December 12 and 13, 2002 at the Oakland-Alameda County Coliseum in Oakland, California and released on November 22, 2003. The album was produced by Rick Rubin and mixed by Chris Lord-Alge.  The album's title is a reference to the bootleg recording industry.  Cheap Trick released their twelfth studio album, "Live!
