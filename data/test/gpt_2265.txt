Carly Wilson (born 8 July 1982) is an English singer and songwriter. She rose to fame in 2002 with the release of her debut single "Your Song". The song became a worldwide hit, and led to Wilson's signing with EMI Records. Her debut album, "Carly", was released in 2003 and peaked at number two on the UK Albums Chart. The album spawned three further UK top 10 singles: "Since U Been Gone", "Superstar" and "Never Really Over". Wilson's second album, "So Pure", was released in 2005 and reached number one in the UK. The album spawned two UK top 5 singles: "Saving My Life for You" and "If You Were Mine". Wilson's third album, "The Greatest", was released in 2007 and became her first UK number one album. The album spawned two UK top 5 singles: "Make You Feel My Love" and "I Believe in You". In 2009, Wilson released her fourth album, "Take the Crown". The album reached number one in the UK, spawning two top 5 singles: "Good Days" and "The One". In 2013, she released her fifth studio album, "Honeymoon". The album reached number one in the UK, spawning two top 5 singles: "You're Not Alone" and "Love Me Like You Do".   Wilson has sold over 20 million records worldwide.
