Pearson Education is a British-owned education publishing company. It was founded in 1878 as the Pearson School Book Company by brothers Arthur and Charles Pearson. The company became a public company in 1924 and was acquired by the Thomson Corporation in 1968. In 2013, Thomson sold Pearson Education to the Blackstone Group.  Pearson Education publishes textbooks, instructional materials, and software for higher education. It has a major presence in the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, and Brazil. Pearson Education's products are used by millions of students worldwide.
