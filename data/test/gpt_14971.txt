Hackfalls Arboretum is an arboretum in New Haven, Connecticut, United States. It is operated by the Yale University Botanic Garden.   The arboretum was founded in 1898 as the Hackfalls Laboratory, and was first located on the grounds of the Yale University School of Forestry. It moved to its current location in 1966. The arboretum has a collection of over 10,000 plants, including specimens from around the world.
