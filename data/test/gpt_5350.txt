Ellie Harrison (born 1979) is a British artist, who creates paintings, drawings and sculptures.  Harrison was born in 1979 in London, England. She studied at the Slade School of Fine Art, University of London, graduating with a BFA in 2003. She subsequently moved to Berlin, where she continued her studies at the Weimar Academy of Arts.  Harrison's art is based on figurative and abstract models, depicting people and animals in naturalistic or fantastical settings. She has exhibited her work internationally, including at the Venice Biennale (2009), the Tate Modern (2010), and the National Gallery of Victoria (2013). In 2017 she had her first solo exhibition at the Walker Art Gallery in Liverpool.
