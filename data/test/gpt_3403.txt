Victoria Leeds is a shopping and leisure complex in Leeds, West Yorkshire, England. It is situated on the north side of the city centre, just off junction 30 of the M62.  Victoria Leeds opened on 20 October 1999, and was built on the site of the former Leeds General Infirmary. The complex comprises a shopping mall, a cinema, a hotel and a casino. The shopping mall has an area of and includes outlets such as Dorothy Perkins, Next and John Lewis. The cinema has a capacity of 1,265 seats and is operated by Showcase Cinemas. The hotel has 220 rooms and is operated by Premier Inn. The casino has a capacity of 450 players and is operated by Grosvenor Casinos.
