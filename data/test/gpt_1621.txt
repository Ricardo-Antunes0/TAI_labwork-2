Shum Gora (: "Noise Hill") is a mountain in the Tian Shan mountain range, located in Xinjiang, China. It is located between the towns of Hotan and Kashgar. It has an elevation of 4,839 meters (15,521 feet).  Shum Gora is a part of the Tianshan mountain range and is located in the Hotan prefecture-level city. The mountain is famous for its splendid views of the Karakoram and Pamir ranges. The name "Shum Gora" means "Noise Hill" in the local Hotan dialect.  The first recorded ascent of Shum Gora was made by Wang Zhongxian in November 1935.
