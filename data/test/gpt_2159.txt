Kittim was a settlement in present-day Larnaca, Cyprus. It was located on the eastern coast of the island, between Larnaca and Limassol. It was abandoned in the early 20th century.  Kittim was a small village located on the eastern coast of Cyprus, between Larnaca and Limassol. It was abandoned in the early 20th century, when the population moved to larger towns. Today, only a few ancient ruins remain.
