"Compass" is the third episode of the first season of the post-apocalyptic drama Falling Skies.  In this episode, the survivors of the crash landing on Earth find themselves in a new and dangerous world, where they must use all their skills to survive. Tom Mason (Noah Wyle) and his team of survivors find themselves in the middle of a battle between the United States military and an unknown foe.  Meanwhile, Hal (guest star Max Charles) struggles with his new position as leader of the group, while Anne (guest star Halle Berry) comes up with a plan to get them out of their current predicament.
