Thomas Allen Gipson III (born January 11, 1986) is an American professional basketball player for the San Antonio Spurs of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball at the University of Kansas.  Gipson was born in Lawrence, Kansas, to Thomas Allen Gipson II and Toni Hargrave. His father played college football at the University of Oklahoma. Gipson attended Bishop Miege High School in Wichita, Kansas. As a sophomore, he averaged 16.1 points, 10 rebounds, three assists and two blocks per game while leading Bishop Miege to a 29-5 record and a Class 4A state championship. As a junior, he averaged 21.8 points, 10.3 rebounds, 4.7 assists and 2.9 blocks per game while leading Bishop Miege to a 34-2 record and a Class 4A state semifinal appearance. He was also named Kansas Mr. Basketball as a junior and senior.
