Ledbury Signal Box is a typical Great Western Railway signal box located at Ledbury in Gloucestershire, England. The box was opened on 1 October 1907 and closed for traffic on 31 December 1988.  The signal box is a brick building with a tiled roof and is in the Edwardian style. It has a single room with an open platform at one end. The front of the building has a hipped roof, while the rear has a gable roof. The box is currently used as a museum.
