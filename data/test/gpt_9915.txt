Carnie Wilson: Unstapled is an American reality television series that premiered on the Bravo network on June 12, 2018. The series follows Wilson, the stepmother of singer Ariana Grande, as she deals with Grande's touring and music career.  The show has been met with mixed reviews from television critics. On the review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes, the series has a 56% rating, based on 9 reviews.
