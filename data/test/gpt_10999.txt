The MV Dmitry Donskoy was a 5,061-ton German cargo ship that was sunk on 9 May 1941 by the British submarine HMS Seraph in the Atlantic Ocean. The ship had been travelling from Brazil to Liverpool when it was sunk. Of the crew of 57, only four men survived.
