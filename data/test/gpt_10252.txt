SII may be an acronym of: Chartered Institute of IT, Software and Systems Security, or  Society for Information Innovation.   This article discusses the Society for Information Innovation (SII). SII is a nonprofit international organization that specializes in the field of information security. SII was founded in 2002 and has since grown to have more than 1,000 members from around the world.   SII's mission is to educate and inspire individuals and organizations to build secure systems and protect their data. The society provides resources and support for its members to help them achieve these goals. SII also provides opportunities for its members to share their knowledge and experiences with others in the field.   The society offers a variety of membership benefits, including access to its online resources, member-only events, and quarterly publications. In addition, SII participates in several international conferences each year to share its latest research and developments with its members.
