Alta Via 1 is a 150-kilometre-long high-level cross-country skiing trail in the Dolomites, Italy. It was inaugurated on 14 December 2006. The trail runs from the town of Sella Ronda in the east to the village of San Martino in the west, following the valleys of the rivers Sella and Piave.  The trail is open to skiing from early December to late April. It can be reached by car or by bus from either Trento or Verona.
