Dillo Day is an annual all-day music festival in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Held at the city's Downsview Park, it typically features a wide variety of music genres, from punk to hip hop and electronica.  The festival began in 1997 as a one-day event called Dillo Fest, and was organized by Toronto DJs Jeff Mills and John Morales. It was renamed Dillo Day in 2008, in honor of the eponymous character from the video game series Super Mario Bros., which coincided with the release of Super Mario Galaxy. The festival features live music from a variety of artists, as well as food and merchandise vendors.
