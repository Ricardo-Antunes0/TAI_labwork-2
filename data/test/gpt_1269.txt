Deryneia (Greek: Δερύνεια; Turkish: Derinya) is a coastal town in the Pella regional unit, Thessaly, Greece. It is part of the municipality of Pella. The town is situated on the east coast of the Gulf of Corinth. Deryneia is also a municipal unit. The municipal unit has an area of 104.593 km2 and a population of 6,851 (2011).
