Donovan in Concert is the sixth album by the British singer Donovan, released in 1969 on the Philips label.  Donovan in Concert is a 1969 album by British singer Donovan, his sixth on the Philips label. It reached No. 6 in the UK Albums Chart. The album was recorded between February and April 1969 at the Royal Albert Hall in London and features performances by Donovan and his then-current backing band, the Sunshine Boys.  The album was produced by Donovan and Roy Thomas Baker. The cover photograph was taken by Bryanston Street photographer David Bailey.
