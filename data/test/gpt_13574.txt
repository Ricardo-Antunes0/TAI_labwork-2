Ronnie Aaron Killings (born January 19, 1972) is an American rapper, actor, and former professional wrestler who is currently signed to WWE under the ring name R-Truth.  Killings was born in Houston, Texas, and is of African American and Native American descent. He began rapping in the early 1990s and started his own record label, Truth World Records, in 1996. He has released three solo albums: "The Truth" (2000), "Killings 2" (2003), and "Truth 2.0" (2010). He also appeared on the albums "One Nation Underground" (1999) and "The Greatest Hits" (2006) by the Wu-Tang Clan.  Killings began wrestling in 1995 and won the NWA Texas Heavyweight Championship in 1998. He was also a one-time NWA World Heavyweight Champion. After leaving wrestling in 2003, Killings returned to rap full-time.
