Persicaria maculosa (syn. Polygonum persicaria) is an annual or biennial plant in the knotweed family, Polygonaceae. It is native to Europe and the Near East.  P. maculosa is a densely branched, erect, yellow-green herb up to 100 cm tall, with opposite leaves and often numerous small yellow flowers. The fruit is a dry capsule which opens explosively to release small black seeds.
