Wonder is an emotion comparable to surprise, awe, and amazement. It is a feeling of being in awe of something extraordinary, novel, or inexplicable.  Wonder is a feeling of being in awe of something extraordinary, novel, or inexplicable. It can be seen as a form of astonishment or admiration, and often induces feelings of curiosity and excitement. It can be induced by anything from the sight of a beautiful sunset to the discovery of a new scientific theory.  Wonder can be both fleeting and long-lasting, depending on the circumstance and individual. For some, it may only last for a few seconds after witnessing something unexpected, while for others it may linger for hours or even days. In either case, the experience is universally unique and unforgettable.
