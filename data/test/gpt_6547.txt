The Wanted is the first extended play by British boy band The Wanted. It was released on 14 October 2012 and contains the singles "Glad You Came" and "I Want You".  The Wanted was formed in 2009 by Max George, Tom Parker, Nathan Sykes and then-band member Siva Kaneswaran. The group signed to Syco Music and released their debut studio album, Wanted (2011). The album charted in the UK at number one, and spawned the singles "Glad You Came" and "I Want You". In 2012, The Wanted released their first extended play, The Wanted (EP). The EP contains the singles "Glad You Came" and "I Want You".
