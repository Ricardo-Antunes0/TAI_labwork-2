James Allenby-Kirk or simply James Kirk (born January 8, 1984) is a British professional racing cyclist who rides for UCI Professional Continental team IAM Cycling. Kirk was born in London, England.  Kirk's first major result as a professional cyclist came at the 2006 Tour of Britain where he won a stage. He followed this up with a second-place finish in the 2007 Tour of Britain. In 2008, Kirk competed in his first Tour de France and finished in 154th place overall.
