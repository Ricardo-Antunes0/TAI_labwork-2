In the Russian Empire, there were Polish National Districts (called in Russian "полрайоны", "polraiony"). They were administrative divisions of the Russian Empire, established on October 26, 1849. They were initially based on the former Polish voivodeships. In 1915, these districts were replaced by provinces.
