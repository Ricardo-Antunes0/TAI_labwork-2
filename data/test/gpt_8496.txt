Aidan Salakhova (, born March 25, 1964) is a retired Bulgarian football defender who played in the Bulgaria national team. He was a participant at the 1990 World Cup and the 1996 UEFA European Championship.  Aidan Salahova was born in Sofia on March 25, 1964. He started his career at his local club CSKA Sofia, and made his debut for the Bulgaria national team in 1983. Salahova played at the 1990 World Cup and the 1996 UEFA European Championship.
