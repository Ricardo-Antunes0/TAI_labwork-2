 Yates Mill is a fully restored, circa 1810, flour mill located in Hudson, New York. It is operated as a tourist attraction by the Hudson Mills Historic Association.  The mill was built by Samuel Yates, and is one of only four remaining mills in the Hudson Valley. It was designated a National Historic Landmark in 1966.  The mill is open seasonally from April to October, and tours are available on weekends and holidays. The mill is also used for events and weddings.
