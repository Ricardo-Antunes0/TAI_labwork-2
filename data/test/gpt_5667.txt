Graham Warwick Charles House (born 4 September 1990) is an English cricketer who has played for Derbyshire County Cricket Club since the start of the 2010 season. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-fast bowler.   House made his first-class debut for Derbyshire against Nottinghamshire in 2010. He made his List A debut for Derbyshire against Lancashire in the 2010 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy. House has also played for the England under-19s and England Lions.
