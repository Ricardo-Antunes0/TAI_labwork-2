The First Western Army was created in 1859 by the United States Army to serve in the American Civil War. It was originally headquartered in St. Louis, Missouri, and was eventually divided into the Western Theater and the Eastern Theater. The Western Theater encompassed most of the American west, while the Eastern Theater encompassed most of the eastern United States.
