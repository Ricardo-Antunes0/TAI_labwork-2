Allianoi was an ancient spa settlement which is located in the municipality of Riva del Garda, Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol, Italy. It is located on the right bank of the Garda River, 8 km southeast of Trento. The settlement was first mentioned in a document from 1115 AD and it was part of the possessions of the Bishopric of Brixen. In 1375 it came under the control of the Counts of Tyrol. In 1564 it was plundered by Ottoman forces. The settlement was abandoned in the early 19th century. Today Allianoi is a tourist destination and a popular spa resort.
