She followed this with roles in several made-for-TV movies and guest spots on TV shows such as "Cheers" and "Murphy Brown".  In 1993, Vail made her feature film debut in the romantic comedy "Two Girls and a Guy". She followed this with roles in the independent films "The Safe House" (1995) and "The Last Supper" (1995), both of which garnered her critical acclaim. In 1998, she starred alongside Johnny Depp in the adventure film "Pirates of Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl". The following year she co-starred in the horror film "Scream 2", which was a commercial success.
