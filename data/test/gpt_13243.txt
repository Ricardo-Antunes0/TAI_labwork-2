It is also known as Kanjira Cotton. Kurkar is obtained from the kuri or karkari palm. The length of the thread can range from 30 to 70 yards and the gauge can be anywhere from 10 to 16 deniers per yard. The most popular colors used to dye Kurkar are blue and green, but other colors including black, brown, and yellow are also seen on occasion.
