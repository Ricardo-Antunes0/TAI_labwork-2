Dr Christoph Müller (born. 20 December 1950) is a German diplomat. He has been the Federal Minister for Economic Cooperation and Development (BMZ) of Germany since 2013, and was previously the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Germany from 2005 to 2009.  Christoph Müller was born in 1950 in Wuppertal, North Rhine-Westphalia. He studied law at the University of Tuebingen, where he graduated with a degree in 1975. He then worked as a lawyer until 1990, when he joined the German Foreign Ministry.  Christoph Müller has served as the Federal Minister for Economic Cooperation and Development (BMZ) of Germany since 2013. Previously, he was the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Germany from 2005 to 2009. During his time in this role, he has helped to improve relations between Germany and a number of countries, including Russia and China. He has also played an important role in helping to revive the European Union.
