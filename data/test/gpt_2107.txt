Extreme Aggression is the fourth studio album by American thrash metal band Megadeth, released on October 27, 1995. The album was produced by Dave Mustaine and recorded at the band's home studio in Rancho Santa Margarita, California.  The album marked a departure from the band's thrash metal roots into a more aggressive style, which was influenced by the hardcore punk movement.
