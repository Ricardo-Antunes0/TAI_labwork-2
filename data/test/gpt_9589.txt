Chai Liyuan (; born September 29, 1966) is a retired Chinese professional basketball player who competed in the 1992 Barcelona Olympics.  Liyuan played for the Beijing Ducks in the Chinese Basketball Association (CBA). He also played for the Shanghai Sharks of the NBA and won a championship with them in 1998. He was drafted by the Golden State Warriors in the second round of the 1988 NBA Draft, but he never played for them.
