The Leveller was a British political magazine, first published in 1765. It was a radical voice of the Poor Law Reform movement, and was instrumental in the passage of the New Poor Law.  The Leveller was a magazine that advocated for social and economic reform. First published in 1765, it was one of the earliest publications to advocate for the implementation of a new system of Poor Laws. The magazine played an important role in the passage of the New Poor Law, which replaced the Old Poor Law.
