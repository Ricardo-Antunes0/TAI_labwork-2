Ulambayar Byambajav (; 24 November 1984 â€“ 1 October 2014), also known as Byambajav Ulambayar, was a Mongolian professional boxer. He was the former WBA World lightweight champion and IBF International lightweight champion.  Ulambayar was born in 1984 in the city of Darkhan, Mongolia, and started boxing at the age of 13. He won his first national title in 2002 and eventually won the WBA World lightweight championship in 2006, defeating Ricky Burns by a majority decision. Ulambayar also captured the IBF International lightweight title in 2007, beating Ruslan Provodnikov by a unanimous decision.  Ulambayar retired in January 2013 with a record of 36 wins (24 by knockout), five losses and one draw.  Ulambayar died on 1 October 2014 due to injuries sustained in a car crash near his home in Darkhan. At the time of his death, he was 37 years old.
