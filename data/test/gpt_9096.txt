PoxNora: Battlefield of the Immortals is a free-to-play online action role-playing video game developed by Chinese game developer NetEase. It was announced on September 25, 2017, and is currently in closed beta.  The game is set in a world of swords and sorcery, with players taking on the role of one of several classes of characters, each with their own abilities and weapons, in order to fight their way through hordes of enemies to reach the final boss.
