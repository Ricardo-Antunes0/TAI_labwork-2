Lewis Archer is a fictional character from the British television series Midsomer Murders. He was created by the writer and producer John Nathan-Turner, and first appeared in the fourth episode of the show's first season, "The Killer at the End of the Line".  Lewis Archer is a fastidious, methodical and intelligent detective with the Barnaby team. He has a great sense of observation and he is able to rapidly assess a situation and formulate a plan of action. Lewis is also highly organised, able to keep track of vast amounts of information and investigate complex cases with ease. He is also confident in his abilities and never backs down from a challenge. Lewis is a highly ethical officer and he always puts the safety of his colleagues first.
