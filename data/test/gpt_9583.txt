The Architectural Woodwork Institute (AWI), founded in 1961, is the premier professional organization for architects, engineers, and other professionals involved in the design and construction of wood structures. AWI's membership includes designers, builders, inspectors, educators, and researchers from all areas of wood architecture.  The mission of AWI is to advance the understanding, application, and performance of wood architecture through education, research, and outreach. AWI provides its members with a forum for exchanging ideas and sharing experiences; provides resources for training and certification; and promotes the use of wood in architecture through publications, exhibitions, and events.  AWI is a not-for-profit organization registered in the state of Illinois. The Institute's headquarters are located in Chicago, Illinois.
