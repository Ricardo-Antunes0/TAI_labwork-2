Doshin the Giant is a Nintendo god game for the Game Boy Advance. The game was developed by Intelligent Systems, and published by Nintendo in Japan on November 6, 2003 and in North America on October 21, 2004.  The objective of the game is to help Doshin defeat the evil giant, Kaguya. Along the way, players must traverse a variety of environments, including a castle and a forest, and collect items to power up Doshin.  Doshin the Giant received mixed reviews from critics. Some appreciated the graphics and humor of the game, while others found it repetitive and dull. The game has sold over one million copies worldwide.
