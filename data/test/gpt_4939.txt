Hateship, Loveship is an American drama film directed by Kenneth Lonergan and written by Lonergan and his brother Paul. The film stars John Gallagher Jr. and Emma Roberts.  The film tells the story of a young couple, Hugh (John Gallagher Jr.) and Althea (Emma Roberts), who are struggling to deal with the aftermath of a break-up. Hugh is angry and resentful, while Althea is trying to rebuild her life. They are both attracted to each other but are unable to let go of their past hurts.
