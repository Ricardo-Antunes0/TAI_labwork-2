Tutter Ellen Margrethe Givskov (born 30 April 1978) is a Danish actress, singer and television presenter.  Born in Copenhagen, Givskov started her career as a child actress, appearing in several Danish TV shows and films. She made her breakthrough as the title character in the Danish series "Tutter" (2002–2006), for which she was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Television Series Drama. She has since starred in several successful Danish films, including "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" (2009), "The Girl Who Played with Fire" (2010), and "The Hunt" (2012).  In 2013, she starred in the American film "The Wolf of Wall Street", for which she was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Motion Picture Drama. She has since starred in the British thriller "Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children" (2015), the French thriller "The Dinner" (2015), and the American action comedy film "Sicario" (2015). In 2017, she starred in the Danish comedy-drama film "Going In Style".
