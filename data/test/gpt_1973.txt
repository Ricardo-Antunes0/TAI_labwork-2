Sabrina Marie Le Beauf (born March 21, 1989) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Carly Shay in the CW series "Gossip Girl" and Jenna Maroney in the TV series "New Girl".  Le Beauf was born in Brooklyn, New York to parents of French descent. She has a younger sister, actress Talia Le Beauf. She began her acting career at the age of six, appearing in commercials and playing minor roles in television shows. Le Beauf made her feature film debut in 2009, starring as Jackie in the independent film "This Is Our Youth". She subsequently starred in the 2010 independent films "The Runaways" and "The Lookout".  In 2011, Le Beauf was cast as Carly Shay on the CW series "Gossip Girl". The show ran for six seasons from 2012 to 2017. In 2018, she starred as Jenna Maroney on the Fox comedy-drama series "New Girl".
