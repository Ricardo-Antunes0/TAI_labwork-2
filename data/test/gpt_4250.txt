The Print Center is a nonprofit gallery and education center located in the heart of Old City Philadelphia.  The Print Center provides an exhibition and workshop space for emerging and mid-career artists, as well as a library and archives for the study of printmaking and the history of printing in America. The center also offers classes, lectures, and workshops to the public on a variety of printmaking topics.  The Print Center is open to the public Tuesday through Saturday from 11:00am to 5:00pm, and Sunday from 1:00pm to 4:00pm. Admission is free.
