Aslan was a highly regarded British role-playing fanzine published between 1978 and 1981. The magazine was published by the Aslan Group, which also produced the fanzine's associated mailing list and convention programme. The fanzine focussed on fantasy role-playing games, with articles on rule systems, gaming tables and campaigns. The magazine had a circulation of around 1,000 copies.
