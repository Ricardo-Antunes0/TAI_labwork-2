Jesse Hogan (born 12 February 1995) is an Australian professional rugby league footballer who plays as a centre for the Melbourne Storm in the National Rugby League.  Hogan was born and raised in Melbourne, Victoria. He is of Irish and Indigenous Australian descent. Hogan played his junior rugby league for the Melbourne Sharks before being signed by the Melbourne Storm. In 2013, Hogan made his debut for the Melbourne Storm against the New Zealand Warriors. Hogan played 25 games for the Storm in 2014, before being named captain of their NYC team. In 2016, Hogan was named captain of the Storm's NRL side.  In February 2017, Hogan was selected to play for Australia in the 2017 Rugby League World Cup. He played two games for Australia in the World Cup, scoring a try against New Zealand.
