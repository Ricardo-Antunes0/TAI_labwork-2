Don Loren Harper is a Los Angeles-based artist and writer whose work has appeared in various magazines and anthologies, as well as several books. Harper received his BFA from the University of California, Davis in 1976.  Harper's paintings are often lush and atmospheric, characterized by their use of bright colors and subtle brush strokes. His writing often explores the complexities of human relationships, both personal and societal. Harper has exhibited his work throughout the United States and in Europe, most notably at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art.
