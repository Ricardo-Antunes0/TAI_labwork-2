JMKO is the R&B, Soul, and Pop music critic for The Guardian newspaper.   Miko Manguba is an award-winning music critic, journalist and broadcaster. She has worked as a music journalist for the Guardian newspaper since 2003, and has also been a regular presenter on BBC Radio 1's The Ticket since 2006. Manguba has also written for publications including NME, Time Out London, The Observer and Spin. In 2010, she won a Brit Award for Best Music Journalist.
