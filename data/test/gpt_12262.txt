The Final World Tour (also referred to as the Slayer Farewell Tour) was a world tour by American heavy metal band Slayer. The tour commenced on July 20, 2016 in Lisbon, Portugal and ended on December 3, 2016 in Mexico City, Mexico.  Slayer guitarist Kerry King announced the tour in June 2016, with the first leg consisting of shows in Europe. The tour was met with mixed reviews from fans and critics.  The final show of the tour was filmed and recorded for a live album and DVD and released on December 3, 2016.
