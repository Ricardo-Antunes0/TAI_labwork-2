John James Teeling (born January 1946) is an Irish whiskey producer and distiller. In 1984, he founded John Teeling Distillery in Clonakilty, County Cork, Ireland. The distillery has since been bought by Pernod Ricard and is now part of the Bacardi-Martini portfolio.  John Teeling is one of the leading Irish whiskey producers, and his distillery is one of the oldest stills in operation in Ireland. He was born and raised in Clonakilty on the west coast of Cork, and began working at the distillery in 1968. He took over as head distiller in 1984, and has since produced a range of Irish whiskies that have won a number of awards. In 2007, he was awarded an honorary doctorate from University College Cork.
