Damaris Keith Johnson (born November 22, 1989) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Betty Cooper on the Fox series The CW series Riverdale, and as Monet Mazur on ABC's Station 19.  Damaris Johnson was born in Charlotte, North Carolina, to Keith and Sherry Johnson. She has a younger brother named Anthony. She is of African American and Cherokee heritage. Johnson attended Myers Park High School in Charlotte, North Carolina, where she was a member of the theater and choir programs.  Johnson's first major role was as Betty Cooper on the Fox series The CW series Riverdale, which aired from 2016 to 2019. In 2019, she starred as Monet Mazur on ABC's Station 19.
