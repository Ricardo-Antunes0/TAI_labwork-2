Massey Sahib is a 1985 Hindi drama film directed by Ramesh Sippy and produced by Ramesh Sippy Productions. The film stars Sanjay Dutt, Juhi Chawla and Shah Rukh Khan in lead roles.  Massey Sahib is a heart-warming story of love, sacrifice and redemption. Set in the days of the British Raj in Punjab, the film tells the story of two young lovers, Arjun (Sanjay Dutt) and Geeta (Juhi Chawla), who are torn apart by the events of 1947. Years later, Arjun is a convicted terrorist and Geeta has married another man. When their son is killed in a terrorist attack, Arjun is released from prison and sets out to find Geeta and make amends for his past.
