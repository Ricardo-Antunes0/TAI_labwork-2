Işık Doğudan Yükselir  (1995) is Sezen Aksu's second poetry book. It is a collection of poems that deal with the speaker's experiences with love, loss, and longing.  The title of the book, Işık Doğudan Yükselir, means "Light Comes From the East" in Turkish. The collection takes its inspiration from a Turkish proverb which says, "The heart of the woman is in the east." The speaker addresses various topics such as love, loss, longing, and heartache in these poems.  Sezen Aksu is a Turkish poet who lives in Istanbul. She has published two poetry books: Işık Doğudan Yükselir (1995) and Birazdan Gelen (2002).
