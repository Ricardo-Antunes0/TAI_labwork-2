Wynyard Cricket Club (WCC) is a cricket club located in Wynyard, Tasmania. The club was founded in 1875 and has since played in the Tasmanian Premier Cricket competition.   The club has a long and proud history of cricketing excellence. Wynyard has won the TSC premiership on six occasions (1982–1983, 1984–1985, 1988–1989, 1990–1991, 1992–1993), as well as the Southern Premier Cricket Championship on four occasions (1981, 1982, 1984, 1985). The club also made it to the final of the prestigious Australian National Club Championship in 2007 but were narrowly defeated by Sydney Cricket Club.   The club fields teams in senior men's and women's cricket competitions. The men's team have been successful in both the TSC premiership and Southern Premier Cricket Championship competitions. The women's team have also enjoyed some success in these competitions, winning the TSC premiership in 2000/01 and 2001/02 as well as reaching the final of the Australian National Club Championship in 2007.
