Cheetah-licious Christmas is  a Christmas album by American singer and songwriter Sheryl Crow. It was released on November 11, 2009, by A&M Records.  The album was preceded by the single "Winter Wonderland" in December 2008. The album was produced by Crow and her husband, David Lindley.  "Cheetah-licious Christmas" debuted at number one on the "Billboard" 200 chart, selling 302,000 copies in its first week. As of November 2013, the album has sold 1,017,000 copies in the United States.  The album's second single, "All I Wanna Do for Christmas Is Watch You Dance", became a top ten hit on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and topped the Adult Contemporary chart in December 2009. The third single from the album, "O Holy Night", reached number two on the Hot 100 chart and topped the Adult Contemporary chart. The fourth and final single from the album, "Christmas Time (Is Here Again)", reached number ten on the Hot 100 chart.  In support of the album, Crow embarked on a concert tour, which began on April 30, 2010 in Stroudsburg, Pennsylvania and ended on December 20, 2010 in West Palm Beach, Florida.
