Christopher Paul Anthony DiStefano (born August 26, 1984) is an American comedian. Primarily a stand-up comedian, DiStefano began his career in entertainment on MTV and MTV2's shows Guy Code and Girl Code. His first hour-long stand-up special Chris DiStefano: Size 38 Waist was released in 2019 by Comedy Central. He co-hosted the podcast History Hyenas with Yannis Pappas and currently hosts Hey Babe with Sal Vulcano along with Chris DiStefano Presents: Chrissy Chaos. Early life DiStefano, who is mostly of German descent (and also has some Italian and Irish ancestry), grew up in the Ridgewood neighborhood of Queens and attended Archbishop Molloy High School. He then attended St. Joseph's College where he studied psychology and played basketball; he graduated in 2006. DiStefano earned a doctorate in physical therapy from New York Institute of Technology in 2010. He went on to practice pediatric physical therapy until 2013 when he began pursuing his career in comedy full-time.
