Cushman was born in Des Moines, Iowa, on 16 January 1924. He received his BS from the University of Iowa in 1944 and his PhD from Princeton University in 1949, under the supervision of John von Neumann. He taught at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill from 1949 until his retirement in 1989.  Cushman was a fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences (1954), the Institute of Mathematical Sciences (1966), and the American Mathematical Society (1974). He died on 11 October 1998 in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.
