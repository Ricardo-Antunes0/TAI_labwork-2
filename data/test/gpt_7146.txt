That's Tough was an American documentary television series that aired on the Discovery Channel from 2009 to 2012. The show was created and produced by Michael J. Fox, who also served as an executive producer.  The show centered around the lives of people with physical disabilities, chronic illnesses, and psychological challenges. Each episode featured the testimony of a different person with a unique story, and offered viewers a glimpse into the everyday challenges they face.  That's Tough was praised for its candidness and its willingness to address difficult topics head-on. Critics noted that the series could have been more comprehensive in its coverage, but overall found it to be an insightful and compelling watch.
