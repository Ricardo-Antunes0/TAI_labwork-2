Alberto Giraldo Jaramillo PSS (October 7, 1934 – December 25, 2009) was a Colombian prelate of the Roman Catholic Church.  Jaramillo was ordained a priest on May 21, 1957. He was appointed auxiliary bishop of the Archdiocese of Bogotá on January 12, 1982 and consecrated on February 3 of that year. Jaramillo served as bishop of the Bogotá archdiocese until his retirement on October 7, 2001.  Jaramillo died in Bogotá at the age of 78 after a long illness.
