Xavier Tavera Castro (born August 05, 1971) is a Spanish-American actor and voice actor who has worked in both English and Spanish language dubbing.  Xavier Tavera Castro was born in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain on August 5, 1971. He grew up in the United States, where he started his professional acting career in 1992. He is best known for his roles as Hector Salamanca on the television series Breaking Bad and Dr. Carlos Villanueva on the medical drama ER. He also voiced characters in the English versions of the anime series Death Note and Naruto.
