The Plastiscines are a French all-female rock band formed in 1993. The band's lineup has changed over the years, but it has been consistently composed of sisters.      The band is known for its energetic and theatrical performances, as well as its eclectic mix of rock and pop songs.  Since its formation, the Plastiscines have released five albums and toured extensively throughout France and Belgium. In 2006, they released their first album in English, entitled "The Stage Is Your Screen". In 2011, they released their sixth album, "Dancing On My Own". In 2013, they released their seventh album, "World Without You", which was nominated for a Victoires de la Musique award.
