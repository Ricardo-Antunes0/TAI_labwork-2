Somebody is an American true-crime podcast, hosted by Preet Bharara and produced by New York Times journalist Sarah Koenig.  The show, which debuted on October 5, 2018, is an investigative journalism podcast about crime. It is produced by the New York Times, and is the first podcast produced by the newspaper in more than a decade.  The show has been met with critical acclaim, winning numerous awards, including a Peabody Award.
