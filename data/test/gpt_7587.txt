"Exit Event" is the series finale of the American television series "Breaking Bad". The episode aired on September 29, 2013, and was written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Bryan Cranston.  The series finale follows the events of the previous eight episodes, which concluded with the revelation that Walter White (Bryan Cranston) had cancer and intended to use his meth production to finance his treatment. Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul) exposes White's scheme to his former boss, Hank Schrader (Dean Norris), and the DEA sets up a sting operation to capture him. In the end, White is arrested as he tries to flee Albuquerque with his family in a RV.  "Exit Event" was generally well-received by critics, who praised its emotional impact and concluded that it was an appropriate end to an acclaimed television series.
