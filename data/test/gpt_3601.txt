Meadowvale is a large suburban district located in the north-west of Mississauga, Ontario, Canada. It is bordered by Burnhamthorpe to the east, Britannia to the south, and Erin Mills to the west.  Meadowvale is an affluent suburb of Mississauga and one of the largest in Peel Region. It has a population of over 140,000 people. It has a mix of single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments. There are many parks and recreational facilities in the district, including four golf courses. The district is home to several large corporations, including Loblaw Companies Ltd., Mattel Canada Inc., and Ford Motor Company of Canada Ltd.
