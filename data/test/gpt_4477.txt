Harry Hargreaves was born on 9 February 1922 in Manchester. He attended Manchester Grammar School and then the Royal College of Art.  Hargreaves began his career as a cartoonist with the children's comic 'The Beano' in 1950. He remained with the magazine until his retirement in 1994. During this time he created some of the comic strip's most famous characters, including Dennis the Menace, Minnie the Minx and The Bash Street Kids.  Hargreaves died on 12 November 2006 in Ilkley. He was 83 years old.
