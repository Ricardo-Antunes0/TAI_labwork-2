The Hamburg University Archive is a central depository for the collected works of the University of Hamburg. The archives are located at the University of Hamburg, in the former premises of the Philosophical Faculty. The archive contains manuscripts, printed works, photographs, and other items relating to the history of the university and its faculty.  The Hamburg University Archive was founded in 1874 as a part of the University of Hamburg. From 1874 to 1945, the archive was located in various locations within the university. After World War II, the archive was relocated to its current location in 1951. The archive is open to researchers and students.
