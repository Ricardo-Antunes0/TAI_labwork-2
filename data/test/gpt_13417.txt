"Graduate" is a song by American rock band the White Stripes. The song was released as the lead single from their fifth studio album, Elephant, on June 3, 2014.  The song was written by Jack White and Meg White, and produced by them both with help from Brendan O'Brien. "Graduate" is a blues rock song with a tempo of 108 beats per minute. The song has been described as having a "fuzzy electric guitar riff" and a "swelling chorus".  "Graduate" was well received by music critics, who praised its groove and melody. It charted in several countries, reaching the top 10 in Austria, Canada, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Sweden and the United Kingdom. The song has been nominated for three Grammy Awards: Best Rock Song, Best Rock Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal and Best Hard Rock Performance.  In November 2014, White announced that he had begun working on an album of covers titled "Covers". The first cover to be released from the album was "Graduate" on December 2.
