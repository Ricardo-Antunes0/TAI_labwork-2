Stefanos Tsitsipas (, ; born 12 August 1995) is a professional tennis player from Greece.  Tsitsipas has won two ATP World Tour singles titles, the 2018 Australian Open and the 2019 Indian Wells Masters. He also reached the semifinals of the 2017 US Open, where he was defeated by Novak Djokovic. He has also won one ATP World Tour doubles title, the 2017 Australian Open with Alexander Zverev.  Tsitsipas made his professional debut in 2012. His biggest year to date came in 2017, when he reached the semifinals of the US Open and won two ATP World Tour singles titles in Australia and Indian Wells.
