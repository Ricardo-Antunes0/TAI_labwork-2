Aisha Alfa is a Canadian comedian and actress who is known for her work on the CBC comedy series, Schitt's Creek.  Aisha Alfa was born in Toronto, Ontario, to Egyptian parents. Her father is a physician and her mother is a homemaker. Alfa attended Catholic school before dropping out to pursue a career in comedy. She began performing stand-up at open mic nights in Toronto, and eventually landed a role on the CBC comedy series, Schitt's Creek. Alfa has since appeared in several television and film roles, including the films The House with a Clock in Its Walls and Girls Trip.
