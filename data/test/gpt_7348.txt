The Himalayan (a.k.a. Himalayan Persian, or Colourpoint) is a breed of domestic cat originating from the mountains of north-west India and Nepal. In the United Kingdom, it is informally known as the Snowy. The Himalayan is a large, solidly built cat with a long, slender body and short, thick fur. It has a pronounced ruff around its neck and two prominent white socks on its hind legs.
