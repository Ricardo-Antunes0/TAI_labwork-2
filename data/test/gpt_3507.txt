Again (also known as Again '02) is the second studio album by American recording artist Jewel. It was released on September 24, 2002, by J Records. The album was produced by Jewel and her then-husband, musician Neil Zaza.  Again debuted at number one on the U.S. "Billboard" 200 and has since sold over two million copies in the United States. It also reached number one in Canada and the UK, as well as number two in Australia and Switzerland. The album received five Grammy nominations, including Album of the Year, but lost to Eminem's 2002 album, The Marshall Mathers LP2.  Again spawned four singles: "Again", "Beautiful Life", "Only You", and "Who Will Save You Now?". The ballad "Only You" became a worldwide hit, topping the charts in fifteen countries.
