ANAPROF 2002 is the 2002 season of the Association Nationale de Rugby Ã  XV. The campaign was won by Toulouse who claimed their first title since 1972.  The 2002 season of the ANAPROF was contested by thirteen teams, divided into two pools of seven. The competition was played in a round-robin format with each team playing each of the other teams in its pool once.  Toulouse were the defending champions, having won the previous season's competition.   The top four teams from each pool advanced to the semi-finals, played as a two-legged affair. The winners of these semi-finals then advanced to the final, played over three legs.
