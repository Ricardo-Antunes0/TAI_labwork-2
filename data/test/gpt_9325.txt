Robert E. Wittes was Physician-in-Chief of Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center from 1984 to 1992.   Robert E. Wittes is a physician-in-chief at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center and a senior fellow at the Hoover Institution. He is also a professor of health law at the New York University School of Law and the author of numerous books, including Peaceful Cities, Just War, The Politics of Non-Violence, and The Future of War.
