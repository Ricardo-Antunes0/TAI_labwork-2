Ramya Krishnan (born 15 September 1970), also credited as Ramya, is an Indian film actress and model who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. Krishnan made her acting debut in the 1990 Tamil film Azhagi. She has since appeared in a number of commercially successful films, notably turning in critically acclaimed performances in films such as Siruthai (1996), Mynaa (2005), and Rajinikanth-starrer Enthiran (2010).
