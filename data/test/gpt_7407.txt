Brihadratha, belonging to the Ikshvaku race, was the first king of the Pandyan dynasty. He was born to the king Purusottama and Queen Vashni of the Kuru dynasty. Brihadratha succeeded Purusottama upon the latter's death in battle, and reigned for twenty-two years. He was a great warrior and expansionist, and under his rule, the Pandyan kingdom reached its greatest extent. Brihadratha is best known for his conquest of Kalinga, which solidified the power of the Pandyan dynasty. He was also known for his generosity and benevolence, and is revered as a great king in Tamil history.
