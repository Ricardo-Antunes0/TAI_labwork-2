Adaptive Standing Tennis is a modality of tennis that allows players with mobility limitations to play the same game as their able-bodied counterparts. By adjusting their stance and movement, adaptive standing tennis players can generate power and control while playing the ball.  Adaptive standing tennis is a growing sport with potential to improve the lives of people with mobility limitations. This modality of tennis allows players to generate power and control while playing the ball, which can help them play at their best. Adaptive standing tennis is also a fun sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and abilities.
