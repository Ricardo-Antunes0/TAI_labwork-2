The Confidant  (Traditional Chinese: 大太監; jyutping: daat6aam4; pinyin: Dàtāi Máo) is a 1997 Hong Kong action thriller film directed and written by Johnnie To. It stars Chow Yun-fat and Tony Leung Chiu-wai.  The film tells the story of a hitman, played by Chow Yun-fat, who is forced to retire after the death of his mentor. He starts to become disillusioned with the world, until he is unexpectedly given a new mission.
