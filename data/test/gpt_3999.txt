Maya Tawan ( is a 2013 Thai romantic drama film directed by Apichatpong Weerasethakul. It was screened in the Contemporary World Cinema section of the 2013 Toronto International Film Festival.  Set in the era of the Bangkok Bankruptcy, Maya Tawan tells the story of a young woman who flees her arranged marriage and falls in love with a wandering sculptor. When her family forces her to return home, she must choose between fidelity to her husband and following her heart.
