Allison Jaime "A.J." Mleczko Griswold is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is best known as the frontman for the metalcore band As I Lay Dying.  Mleczko was born in Arlington, Virginia, on August 10, 1984. He started playing guitar at the age of 12 and formed As I Lay Dying in 2002 with fellow students from his high school. The band released its debut album, Awake, in May 2003 and its follow-up, The New Order, in October 2005. In 2007, Mleczko left As I Lay Dying to focus on his solo career.  Mleczko's first solo album, The Great Destroyer, was released in 2009. He released his second album, Have You Seen My Son?, in 2013 and his third album, Roots of Emptiness, in 2017.
