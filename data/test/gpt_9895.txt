The Programme for Belize is a private, non-profit organization that provides education, health and economic development services in the country.   The Programme for Belize was founded in 1984 by a group of business and civic leaders. The organization's goal is to improve the quality of life for Belizeans through education, health and economic development services. The Programme operates out of six regional headquarters, and employs more than 1,000 people in Belize. The organisation provides services to more than 230,000 people each year.
