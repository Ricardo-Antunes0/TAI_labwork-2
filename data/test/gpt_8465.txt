Getter Robo is a manga series created by Go Nagai and published by Shogakukan. The story follows the adventures of the titular robot, Getter Robo, and his human partner, Dr. Kaneda. The series has been adapted into an anime television series, which aired from 1982 to 1985 and was later remade in 2001. A film adaptation of the series was released in 2004.
