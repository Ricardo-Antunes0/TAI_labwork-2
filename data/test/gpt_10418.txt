Vipin Handa (born October 28, 1958) is an Indian-born American theoretical physicist and professor of physics at the University of California, Berkeley.  Handa was born in Mumbai, India on October 28, 1958. He received his Bachelor of Science degree in physics from the University of Bombay in 1980 and his Doctor of Philosophy degree from the University of California, Berkeley in 1984.  Handa has been a professor of physics at the University of California, Berkeley since 1984. He is a fellow of the American Physical Society and the American Association for the Advancement of Science. He also holds a professorship at Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. Handa is currently a member of the National Academy of Sciences and the Royal Society of London. In 2006, he was awarded the prestigious Dirac Medal by the Institute of Physics.
