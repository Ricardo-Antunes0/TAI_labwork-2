Marcel Boekhoorn (; born 30 October 1959) is a Dutch sculptor and artist. He is considered one of the most important contemporary sculptors in the world.  Marcel Boekhoorn was born in the Netherlands in 1959. After studying at the Academy of Fine Arts in Amsterdam, he started working as an artist in the early 1980s. He has since exhibited his work around the world, including at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Tate Gallery in London, and the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum in New York. Boekhoorn has been awarded numerous prizes and awards, including the prestigious Turner Prize in 1997.
