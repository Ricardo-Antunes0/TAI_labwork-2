Leon Paul Rice (born November 25, 1963) is an American former professional basketball player. He played in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Detroit Pistons, Los Angeles Lakers, and Boston Celtics. A 6'8" power forward, Rice was nicknamed "The Answer".  Rice was a two-time NBA All-Star (1990 and 1991), and won the 1993 NBA Championship with the Detroit Pistons. He also won two gold medals with the United States men's national basketball team at the 1992 Barcelona Olympics and 1996 Atlanta Olympics. He retired from basketball in 1998.  Since retiring from basketball, Rice has acted in films and television, most notably as Coach Stan on the hit show "The Office".
