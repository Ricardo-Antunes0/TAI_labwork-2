Bodin Church  is a parish church in Bodin, a municipality in the district of Gros-Montagnes in the canton of Vaud in Switzerland.  The church was built on a hill between the villages of Bodin and Saint-Pierre in the 12th century. It is a Romanesque style building with a nave and two aisles. The church was refurbished in the 15th century and again in the 17th century.  The church is open to the public and has a museum dedicated to its history.
