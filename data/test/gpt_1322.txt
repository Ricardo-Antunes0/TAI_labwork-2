Christoph Schwab (born 14 October 1962 in Altenburg) is a German composer and professor of composition at the Hochschule für Musik Karlsruhe.  Christoph Schwab was born in 1962 in Altenburg, Germany. He studied composition with Hans Werner Henze, Wolfgang Rihm and Reinhold Glière at the Hochschule für Musik Karlsruhe. He has worked as a composer and professor of composition at the Hochschule für Musik Karlsruhe since 1990.  Christoph Schwab's compositions have been performed throughout Europe, North America and Asia. His works have been awarded several prizes, including the Kritzer Preis, the Grawemeyer Award and the Prix de Rome.
