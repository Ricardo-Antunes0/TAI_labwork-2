Truelove's Gutter is the sixth studio album by American rock band Stone Temple Pilots. It was released on October 12, 2017, through Atlantic Records.  The album was produced by the band's longtime collaborator Brendan O'Brien and features the band's first new material in six years. The album has generally received positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's return to their heavier roots and O'Brien's production.  Stone Temple Pilots formed in 1985 in San Diego, California. The band's original lineup consisted of Scott Weiland (vocals, lead guitar), Robert Truelove (bass), Dean DeLeo (guitar), and Eric Kretzschmar (drums). After releasing two independent EPs, the band signed to Atlantic Records in 1990 and released their debut album, Core. The album was well-received by critics and sold over a million copies worldwide.  After Core's success, the band released two more albums with Atlantic:1992's Purple and 1993's Shangri-La Dee Da. Both albums were met with mixed reviews from music critics, but still sold well. In 1996, Weiland left the band and was replaced by former Guns N' Roses drummer Steven Adler. The band released two more albums with Adler before breaking up in 2003.  Weiland reformed Stone Temple Pilots in 2013 with original members DeLeo, Kretzschmar, and Truelove.
