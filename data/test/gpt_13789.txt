Li Yuchao (; born 1963) is a Chinese author who has written novels and short stories.  Li was born in 1963 in the city of Kunming, Yunnan province. She completed her undergraduate studies at the Southwest University of Political Science and Law in 1986, and then moved to Beijing to continue her studies at the China Academy of Social Sciences.  Li has been a prolific writer, publishing over twenty novels and short stories since 1987. Her work has been translated into several languages, including English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Dutch, Finnish, Swedish and Japanese.  Li was awarded the prestigious Mao Dun Prize in 2011 for her novel "The Book Thief".
