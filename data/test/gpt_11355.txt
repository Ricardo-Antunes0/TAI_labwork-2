Laoennea renouardi is one of two species of orchid endemic to the highlands of Laos. It is found at elevations of 1,800 to 4,900 meters. The plant grows on trees and rocks in moist montane forests.  Laoennea renouardi is a small, fragile orchid with white flowers and a greenish brown stem. The flowers are arranged in a spike and have a curved lip. They are pollinated by bats and the nectar is used by them to feed their young.
