Janine Boyd (born February 5, 1971) is an American actress and singer who is known for her roles in the television series Beverly Hills, 90210, and Chicago Hope. She also had a recurring role on the TV series Outsiders.  Boyd was born in Santa Monica, California to parents Herbert Boyd, an actor and singer, and Sally Boyd (n√©e Miano), an actress. She has two older sisters, Joanne and Laurie. After high school, Boyd attended the University of Southern California, where she studied theater arts. In 1992, she moved to New York City to pursue an acting career.  Boyd's first major role was in the television series Beverly Hills, 90210 as Kelly Taylor from 1991 to 1995. After 90210 ended, she guest starred on several TV shows, including The X-Files, NYPD Blue, and ER. In 2003, she landed a recurring role on the TV series Outsiders as Deputy Sheriff Tanya Lewis. From 2007 to 2009, she starred as Fiona Dunbar on the NBC series Chicago Hope. After Chicago Hope ended, Boyd had a recurring role in the CBS series Vegas from 2014 to 2015.  Since 2017, Boyd has starred as Valorie Dean on the CW series Dynasty.
