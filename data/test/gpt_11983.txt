Luna Park Sydney is a heritage-listed amusement park at Homebush Bay in the Inner West of Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. It was designed by Walter Burley Griffin and built from 1912 to 1913. It is also known as Luna Park Amusement Park, Sydney Luna Park and Luna Park Sydney. The property is owned by the Sydney Parks and Wildlife Service. It was added to the New South Wales State Heritage Register on 2 April 1999.
