Abinadi Meza (born 1977 in Austin, Texas) is an American painter and sculptor.  Abinadi Meza was born in 1977 in Austin, Texas. He studied fine art at the University of Texas at Austin and at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago.  Meza's work is based on the principle that form should be revealed through its interaction with light and space. He often uses natural materials, such as earth, stone, and wood, to create sculptures and paintings that explore the relationships between structure and surface. His work has been exhibited in galleries in the United States and abroad, including the Museum of Fine Arts Houston, the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago, and the Guggenheim Museum Bilbao.
