The Caretaker Gazette is a bi-monthly newsletter that covers news and events related to the caretaking profession. It is produced by the National Caretaker Association (NCA), the professional organization for caretakers.  The NCA is a nonprofit membership organization that represents the interests of caretakers in the United States. The NCA was founded in 1944 and has more than 1,000 members. The NCA provides education and resources to its members and works to improve the caretaking profession.  The Caretaker Gazette is published bimonthly and is available online and in print. The newsletter is distributed to NCA members, other interested parties, and the media.
