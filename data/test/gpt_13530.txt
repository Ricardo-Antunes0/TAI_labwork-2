MedPro Group, formerly known as Medical Protective, is a global provider of medical security and risk management services. With more than 7,000 employees in more than 50 countries, MedPro Group offers a full range of solutions to protect health care workers, patients, and facilities.   The company's flagship product is the MedPro Medical Security System, which provides comprehensive protection for patients, health-care providers, and facilities. The system includes a broad array of products and services designed to protect people and assets from risks associated with medical incidents. MedPro Group also offers a variety of risk management solutions, including incident response, safety and security assessment, threat assessment, and compliance monitoring.  MedPro Group is headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, and has operations in the United States, Canada, Europe, Asia, South America, and the Middle East. The company was founded in 1984 and is privately held by a consortium of leading health-care organizations.
