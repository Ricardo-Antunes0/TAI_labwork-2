Wu Weian (; born September 1, 1981) is a Chinese actor, singer and model.  Wu Weian was born in Changsha, Hunan Province on September 1, 1981. He is a graduate of the Hunan Academy of Arts. Wu has starred in several television series and films, most notably "The Legend of the Condor Heroes" (2008), "The Face Reader" (2009), "My Beloved Concubine" (2011), "Dragon Blade" (2013), and "The Mermaid" (2015).
