Javor Gardev  is a Bulgarian stage and film actor. He has appeared in more than sixty films and television shows since 1988.  Javor Gardev was born in Varna, Bulgaria on October 4, 1969. He began his acting career in 1988, appearing in several stage productions. In 1993, he made his television debut in the drama series "Dobre Dva". Gardev has since appeared in dozens of films and television shows, most notably the crime thriller "Peshtera" (2002), the war film "Zarandzhik" (2005), and the romantic comedy "Love on the Rocks" (2009).
