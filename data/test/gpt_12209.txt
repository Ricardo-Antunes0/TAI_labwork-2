The 1983 NatWest Trophy was an English association football match contested by the winners of the 1982–83 Football League and the 1982–83 FA Cup. The match was played on 9 May 1983 at Wembley Stadium in London, and was the final match of the 1982–83 season.  The match was won by Liverpool, who beat Tottenham Hotspur 2–1 thanks to goals from John Aldridge and Ian Rush.
