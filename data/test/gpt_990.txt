Bristol Airport , at Lulsgate Bottom, on the outskirts of the city of Bristol, England, is the largest airport in the South West and the seventh busiest in the United Kingdom. It has two terminals, a domestic terminal and a international terminal, both of which are linked by a central walkway. The airport is operated by Bristol Airport Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Bristol City Council.  The airport is home to several airlines, including British Airways, EasyJet, Ryanair and Vueling. It also has a significant cargo operation. The airport handled over 3.5 million passengers in 2016 and is predicted to reach 5 million passengers by 2021.
