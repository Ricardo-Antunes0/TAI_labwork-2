Chartered financial consultant (ChFC) is the "advanced professional designation" of a financial consultant who has passed an exam administered by the Chartered Institute of Financial Analysis. The ChFC credential sets the standard for financial consultants and demonstrates that the individual has attained a level of knowledge, skill and experience in the financial services industry.   The ChFC credential is not a license or certification. It is an advanced professional designation that indicates that the holder has attained a level of knowledge, skill and experience in the financial services industry. The ChFC credential sets the standard for financial consultants and demonstrates that the individual has attained a level of knowledge, skill and experience in the area of financial planning and analysis.
