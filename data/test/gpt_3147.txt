Gae Aulenti (; 4 December 1927 – 31 October 2004) was an Italian fashion designer.  Gae Aulenti was born in Milan, Italy, on 4 December 1927. She began her professional career as a dressmaker in 1950, and in 1954 opened her own fashion house.  Aulenti's designs were highly influential and popular during the 1960s and 1970s. Her work often featured luxurious fabrics and intricate detail. She won numerous awards, including the prestigious Grand Prix de la Couture in 1978. Aulenti died in Milan on 31 October 2004 at the age of 78.
