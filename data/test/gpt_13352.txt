The Battle of Vernon, Florida was a military engagement that took place on 18 May 1945, as part of the American Civil War. The battle pitted Confederate units led by General Wade Hampton against Union forces commanded by General William T. Sherman.   The battle resulted in a Confederate victory, although it was a costly one for the Confederates. The battle resulted in the death of Hampton and several other Confederate officers, and the capture of over 1,000 Union soldiers.
