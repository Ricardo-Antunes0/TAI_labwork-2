Seo Ju-hyun (; born June 28, 1991), better known by her stage name Seohyun, is a South Korean singer, actress, and model. She was a member of the South Korean girl group Girls’ Generation from 2006 to 2013.  Seohyun was born in Gwangju, Gyeongsangnam-do, South Korea. She has an elder sister and a younger brother. Seohyun began her musical career as a member of the girl group Girls’ Generation in 2006. The group achieved widespread popularity with their debut album “Girls’ Generation” (2007), which spawned the successful singles “Into the New World” and “Gee”. Seohyun subsequently starred in the hit television series “My Lovely Girl” (2009) and released her solo album “Flower Boy 2002” (2010).  In 2013, Seohyun announced her departure from Girls’ Generation after eight years together to pursue a solo career. She released her first solo album “Oh! My Venus” in 2014 and starred in the TV series “Drinking Solo” (2015). In 2017, she released the album “Don’t Say Goodbye…See You Again” and starred in the film “Descendants of the Sun”.
