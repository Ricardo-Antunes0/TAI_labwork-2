The Karakol society , was a Turkish nomadic society that inhabited the Karakol Mountains in Central Anatolia. The society was composed of Turkmen, Kazakhs, and other nomadic tribes who followed a traditional lifestyle based on horse breeding and herding. The society was disbanded in the early 20th century.
