Adam Craig is an American country music singer-songwriter. He has released six studio albums, all of which have debuted in the top ten of the Billboard Country Albums chart. Craig has also had five singles cross the country airplay chart, including the number one hit "Take a Little More Time" and the Top 5 hits "I'm Lonesome Now" and "Don't Waste My Time". His most recent album, "Life in a Small Town", was released in 2016.
