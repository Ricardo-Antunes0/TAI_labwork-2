The Tall T is a 1957 American drama film directed by William A. Wellman and produced by Joseph L. Mankiewicz. The screenplay by Ring Lardner, Jr. is based on the 1952 novel of the same name by James Jones.  The film tells the story of a group of soldiers stationed in Germany during World War II, and their attempt to survive the war. It stars Burt Lancaster, Tony Curtis, Lee Marvin, Ernest Borgnine, and Rod Steiger.  The Tall T was nominated for seven Academy Awards, including Best Picture and Best Director.
