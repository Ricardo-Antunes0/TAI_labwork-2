Quiscat was a prominent 18th century Native American chief.  Quiscat was the son of Chief Gros Ventre and brother of Chief Yellow Bird. He was known as an accomplished warrior and was a leader of the Quiché people during the early 18th century. Quiscat played a significant role in the Battle of Zuni in 1710, during which he led a force of Quiché warriors against the Spanish. Quiscat died in 1715 at the age of 39.
