She was the first woman to receive a PhD in economics from Yale University.  Born on August 1, 1916, in New York City, Angela Calomiris attended Barnard College and earned her undergraduate degree in 1938. She then received her PhD from Yale University in 1951. After earning her degree, Calomiris taught at the University of California, Berkeley, and the University of Chicago before joining the faculty at Yale in 1966. She served as dean of the School of Management at Yale from 1973 to 1977. Calomiris died on January 1, 1999.
