The Buffyverse or Slayerverse is a media franchise based on the television show Buffy the Vampire Slayer, created by Joss Whedon. The franchise includes comics, video games, and other media.  The Buffyverse comprises of all things related to the original series, including but not limited to:   - Buffy the Vampire Slayer (1996-2003) - Angel (1999-2004) - Firefly (2002-2005) - Dollhouse (2009-2013) - Angel: After the Fall (2011) - Doctor Whooves (2012)
