Njaan (English: "I") is an Indian Malayalam-language film written and directed by P. A. C. Thomas. The film stars Kalabhavan Mani and Divya Spandana in the lead roles.  The film tells the story of a family living in a small village in Kerala. The patriarch of the family, played by Mani, is a hardworking man who strives to provide for his wife and four children. Although they are happy with their simple life, the family is shaken by the sudden death of one of their members. Njaan explores themes such as grief, love, and resilience.
