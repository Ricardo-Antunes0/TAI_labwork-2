The Woodbridge Brook is a tributary of the Delaware River in Gloucester County and Salem County, New Jersey in the United States.  The Woodbridge Brook rises in Morris County, New Jersey, and flows through Woodbridge Township, Freehold Borough and Mantua Township before joining the Delaware River in East Greenwich Township. It is also designated as a Wild and Scenic River by the National Park Service. The brook is considered to be a high quality cold water fishery.
