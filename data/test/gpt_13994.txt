Lurë is a former municipality in the Shkodër County, northern Albania. At the 2015 local government reform it became a subdivision of the municipality Shkodër. The population at the 2011 census was 7,472.   Lurë is located in the central part of the Shkodër County, on a plain north of the Dragor River. It borders the municipalities of Koplik, Korçë, Bajram Curri and Elbasan. It is bordered by the Republic of Macedonia to the east and south-east, and by Greece to the south-west.
