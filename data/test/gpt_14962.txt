Bahia Mahmud Awah (born 1960) is a Somali artist who has been living in Sweden since 1987. She is known for her paintings, sculptures, and installation works which deal with the themes of identity, exile, and memory.  Bahia Mahmud Awah was born in 1960 in Mogadishu, Somalia.
