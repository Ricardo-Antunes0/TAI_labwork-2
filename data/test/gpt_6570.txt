Ashby Woodson (December 27, 1877 – May 15, 1966) was an American artist who is best known for his landscapes.  Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Woodson studied at the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts and the Académie Julian in Paris. He was a member of the Philadelphia Group and the Woodstock School of Painting and Sculpture. His work is in the collections of the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
