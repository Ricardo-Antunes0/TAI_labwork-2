42 Cancri is a single star in the zodiac constellation of Cancer. It is faintly visible to the naked eye with an apparent visual magnitude of 4.2. Located about 430 light-years from Earth, 42 Cancri is one of the nearest single stars to the Sun.  42 Cancri is a carbon-rich A-type main-sequence star that is about twice the mass and about four times the size of the Sun. It is radiating about 169 times the Sun's luminosity from its outer atmosphere at an effective temperature of about 5,500 K.
