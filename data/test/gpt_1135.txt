Felix Milleker (Serbian-Cyrillic: Феликс Милекер, Serbian-Latin: Feliks Milkere; born 15 November 1978) is a Serbian-born Slovenian professional basketball player for Žalgiris Kaunas of the Lithuanian Basketball League. He also represents the Slovenian national team.  Milleker, who was born in Serbia, played college basketball at the University of Utah, where he was a two-time All-Pac-10 selection and helped lead the Utes to the NCAA Championship game in 2000. He also played for the Slovenian national team at the 2000 European Championship.  After two seasons with Žalgiris Kaunas, in which he averaged 14.3 points and 6.4 rebounds per game, Milleker signed with Spanish team Real Madrid on a three-year deal worth €4 million net income in September 2013. With Real Madrid, he won two Spanish League championships (2014–15 and 2016–17), one Spanish King's Cup (2015), and one EuroLeague championship (2017).  Milleker was a member of the Slovenian national team at the 2016 Summer Olympics and the 2017 EuroBasket Championship.
