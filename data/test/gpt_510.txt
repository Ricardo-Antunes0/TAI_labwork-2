Nora Armani (Arabic: نورا أرماني),  is an Italian fashion designer who was born in Rome in 1954. She started her career in 1978, as a design assistant to Giorgio Armani, and in 1984 she opened her own couture house. In 2006, she was named artistic director of the Maison Armani.  Armani has designed clothing for both men and women, and has worked with several fashion houses including Berluti, Celine, and Fendi. Her collections have been worn by celebrities such as Angelina Jolie, Gwyneth Paltrow, and Penélope Cruz.
