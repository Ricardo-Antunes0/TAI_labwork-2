"Strong Again" is a song by British recording artist Cheryl, taken from her third studio album, "The Light of Day" (2018).  Written by Cheryl and Stargate, "Strong Again" is a mid-tempo pop ballad about overcoming personal struggles. The song's lyrics depict the protagonist standing up to their fears and fighting back against their adversaries.  "Strong Again" was released as the album's second single on 8 November 2018. The song became a commercial success, topping the charts in several countries.  The accompanying music video was directed by Brett Ratner and features Cheryl portraying a woman who has been through a lot of pain and suffering. It received positive reviews from critics, who praised the singer's emotional performance.
