Petersburg Mountain is a mountain located in the U.S. state of New Hampshire. The mountain is located in the White Mountains, southeast of the town of Jaffrey.   The mountain is composed of Precambrian and Paleozoic rock, and is the highest point in New Hampshire east of the Connecticut River.
