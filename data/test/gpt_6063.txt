HelioSphera S.A. is a Greek company founded in 2006 as a spin-off of the University of Patras' School of Electrical and Computer Engineering. It is headquartered in Patras, Greece.  HelioSphera develops and manufactures next-generation Spherical MEMS sensors, which are used in a variety of areas including automotive, industrial, medical, security, consumer and telecommunication markets. Its products are used by leading global brands.  The company has developed a unique technology that enables it to manufacture spherical MEMS sensors at a cost that is lower than traditional flat-panel sensors. HelioSphera's spherical sensors offer significant advantages over traditional flat-panel sensors in terms of performance, size and weight.
