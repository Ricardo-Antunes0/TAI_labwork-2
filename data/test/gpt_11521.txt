Sweetbriar Hall (also Sweet Briar Hall and Sweet Briar Cottage) is a historic home located at Winston-Salem, Forsyth County, North Carolina. The main house was built about 1840, and is a two-story, five bay, gable roofed brick dwelling with a hipped roof. It has a one-story rear ell. The property also includes a contributing smokehouse (c. 1840), well house (c. 1840), and chicken house (c. 1870).  The Sweet Briar Mansion is the ancestral home of the U.S. Vice President, Joseph R. Biden Jr.. It is also the childhood home of his wife Jill Biden and their two children Hunter Biden and Valerie Biden Biden.  The property was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1978.
