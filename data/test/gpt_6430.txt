He Ting (; born February 1962) is a Chinese contemporary artist. He is noted for his large-scale installation artworks and videos.  He Ting was born in February 1962 in Shanghai, China. He graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts of Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, in 1988.  He Ting's artworks often depict the human form and its relation to nature. His installations often incorporate large amounts of water, often in the form of cascades or pools, as well as reflective surfaces to create an immersive experience for viewers. He Ting's videos typically consist of long takes of people engaged in everyday activities, such as cooking or commuting, which are then edited together to create a more contemplative and meditative experience.
