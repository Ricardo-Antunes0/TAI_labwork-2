Nio Joe Lan (; also known by his stage name "Dok2") is a Taiwanese singer, songwriter, and actor. He is best known as the frontman of the Taiwanese rock band Mayday, and for his roles in Taiwanese films such as "The Unemployed" and "The Way We Are".  Nio Joe Lan was born on February 24, 1984, in Taiwan. He is the son of a Baptist minister. Lan started playing the guitar at age 10 and began writing his own songs at 12. In 2002, he formed the Taiwanese rock band Mayday with friends, and they released their debut album that same year. The band went on to achieve international success, with their singles "Hello" (2005) and "Believe" (2007) charting in Taiwan and Southeast Asia, respectively. In 2009, Lan starred in the hit Taiwanese film "The Unemployed", which earned him a nomination for Best Actor at the Golden Horse Awards. In 2012, he starred in the film "The Way We Are", which won him the Best Actor award at the 16th Golden Rooster Awards.  Lan has also ventured into acting, appearing in films such as "My Friend's Wedding" (2013) and "Life Is Beautiful" (2016). In 2018, he starred in the war drama "The Way Home", which was released to critical acclaim.
