SkyMarshall Arts is a Norwegian electronic dance music production and remixing collective founded in Oslo, Norway in 2009 by vocalist/producer/DJane Annet Essmark. SkyMarshall Arts has released several successful singles and remixes, most notably "Hey Mama" with Dada Life and "I Wanna Feel" with Zedd. In 2015, the group released their debut full-length album "The Sky Is The Limit".
