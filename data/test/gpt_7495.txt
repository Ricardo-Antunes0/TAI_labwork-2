Mickey One is a 1965 American neo-noir crime film directed by Don Siegel and written by Robert Benton. It stars One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest and Bonnie and Clyde actor Dean Martin, together with Jack Palance, Natalie Wood, and Ricky Nelson.  The film tells the story of Mickey One (Martin), a professional criminal who is released from prison after serving a long sentence. He is hired to pull off a bank heist, but things go awry and he ends up on the run from the law.
