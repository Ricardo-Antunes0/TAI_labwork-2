Captain Jack Sparrow is a fictional character and the protagonist of the Pirates of the Caribbean film series. He is portrayed by Johnny Depp and first appeared in the 2003 film Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl.  Captain Jack Sparrow is a notorious pirate who has made many enemies during his time at sea. He is also known for his quick wit and sharp tongue, as well as his ability to charm people and take advantage of situations. Despite his misdeeds, Sparrow is a brave and loyal friend to those he trusts, and he is willing to do whatever it takes to save his crew and take down his enemies.
