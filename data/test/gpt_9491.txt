Wyke is a rural and suburban village in the London Borough of Sutton, England. It is located in the far southeast of the borough, on the border with the London Borough of Bromley. Wyke is part of the Wyke ward of Sutton Council and had a population of 4,446 at the 2011 Census.  The village has a traditional market cross and two churches – St Botolph's Church, which is a Grade II* listed building and All Saints' Church. The latter also has a pre-school and a primary school. There are also a few small businesses in the village, including a hairdresser and two cafes.   Wyke has two parks – Wyke Park and The Green – which are used for a number of events throughout the year. Wyke is also home to Wyke Cricket Club and Wyke Football Club.
