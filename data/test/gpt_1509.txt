Louisiana Highway 3127 (LA 3127)  is a state highway located in the U.S. state of Louisiana. The highway runs from LA 3127 Spur in Franklin to LA 3129 in Richland.  LA 3127 begins at an intersection with LA 3127 Spur in Franklin. The highway heads northeastward, paralleling the Tensas River for a short distance before turning to the east and intersecting LA 3129 just east of Richland. LA 3127 terminates at LA 3129.
