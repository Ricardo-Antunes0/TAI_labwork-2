Mark Chasan is a digital media pioneer, entrepreneur, and investor. He is the Co-founder and CEO of Socialmatic, a social media management platform that helps organizations manage their social media presence. Prior to Socialmatic, Chasan was the CEO of Hootsuite, which he founded in 2008.  Chasan is also a noted angel investor and advisor, having invested in companies such as Twitter, Foursquare, and Medium. He has served on the boards of directors of companies such as Foursquare and Postmates. In addition to his work in digital media, Chasan is also a prolific author, having written two books on entrepreneurship: "Startup: A Silicon Valley Adventure" and "The Start-Up of You".
