Alversund  is a village in Alver municipality in Hordaland county, Norway. The village is located on the northeastern side of the island of Alver, about west of the city of Bergen. The village has a population (2013) of 536.  Alversund was historically part of the municipality of Askvoll, but was transferred to Alver in 1964.
