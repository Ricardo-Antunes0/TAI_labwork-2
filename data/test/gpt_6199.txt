"Baldr Sky" is a Japanese adult visual novel developed by KEY and published by VisualArt's. It was released in Japan on November 22, 2015 for Windows and PlayStation 4. On October 15, 2017, it was announced that "Baldr Sky" will be localized for release in North America by NIS America on January 12, 2018 for PlayStation 4.   "Baldr Sky" is a story-driven game with freely selectable dialogue options that affect the course of the game. The game features multiple endings based on player choices.  The game received "mixed or average" reviews according to review aggregator Metacritic.
