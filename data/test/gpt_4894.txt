Count Sesto is the title borne by a fictional character in the novel "The Catcher in the Rye" by J.D. Salinger.  Sesto is a minor character in the novel, appearing only once, in Chapter 1. He is a teenager who is picked on by Holden Caulfield and other boys at school. Sesto has no friends and resorts to violence to deal with his problems. Holden ridicules and terrorizes him until Sesto finally lashes out, punching Holden in the face.
