Iowa Highway 930 (Iowa 930) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Iowa. It runs from Interstate 35 in Sioux City to U.S. Route 61 in Ames.  Iowa Highway 930 begins at Interstate 35 in Sioux City and runs eastward through the city before turning northeastward and intersecting US 61 just outside of Ames. The highway then continues northeastward to its eastern terminus at US 61 just outside the city of Des Moines. Iowa 930 is a two-lane highway for its entire length.   Iowa 930 is designated as a scenic byway from Sioux City to Ames. The byway is also designated as a historic route because it was one of the original state highways in Iowa. The highway was constructed between 1921 and 1923 as part of the original state highway system.
