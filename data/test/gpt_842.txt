Diuris protena, commonly known as northern goldenrod, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family. It is native to central and northeastern North America, where it can be found in many types of habitat, from wet meadows to dry uplands.  D. protena is a perennial herb up to 90 cm (3 ft) tall, with a taproot and rhizomes. The leaves are divided into small, narrow leaflets, each with a serrated margin. The flowers are yellow or orange, with a long stem and small, hairy petals.
