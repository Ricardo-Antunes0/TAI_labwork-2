Hina Aoyama's work is characterized by its delicate, intricate designs and its use of bright, often fluorescent colors. She has exhibited her work around the world, including at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Hirshhorn Museum and Sculpture Garden in Washington, D.C., and the Centre Pompidou in Paris.
