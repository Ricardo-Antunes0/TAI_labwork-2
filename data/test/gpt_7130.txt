The Anderson Servants are an amateur baseball team from Anderson, South Carolina. The team was founded in 1937 and is a member of the United States amateur baseball association, the National Association of Amateur Baseball Leagues (NABL).  Anderson is located in Anderson County, South Carolina, approximately southeast of Greenville. The population was 7,477 at the 2010 census. The team's home field is Anderson Park.  The Anderson Servants are made up of amateur players from all over Anderson County and surrounding counties who come together to play baseball. Games are typically played on Saturday mornings throughout the summer. The team is coached by Tony Anderson and his son, Tyler Anderson.
