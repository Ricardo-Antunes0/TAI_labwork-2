Sachse  is a city in Collin County, Texas, United States. The population was 50,673 at the 2010 census.  Sachse is a major city in the Dallasâ€“Fort Worth metroplex and is situated approximately between Dallas and Fort Worth. It is part of the DFW Metropolitan Statistical Area.  The city was founded in 1892 by German immigrants and named for Karl Theodor Sachse, a German political economist and philosopher of the late 18th century. The Sachse Independent School District serves area students.  Sachse is home to the corporate headquarters of Koch Industries, as well as Abbott Laboratories and ExxonMobil. The city also hosts the Toyota Motor Manufacturing North America plant, which employs about 6,000 people.
