Larry Madowo (born 14 July 1987) is a Kenyan long-distance runner.  Madowo was born in Nairobi, Kenya to parents of Kenyan and Tanzanian descent. He attended Nairobi Boys High School and later studied at the University of Oregon, where he ran for the Oregon Ducks track and field team in National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) competition.  At the 2013 World Championships in Moscow, Madowo finished seventh in the men's 10,000 metres. He also competed at the 2012 and 2016 Olympics, finishing eleventh in the 10,000 metres at London 2012 and seventeenth at Rio de Janeiro 2016.  Madowo is a six-time winner of the Boston Marathon, including a record-breaking victory in 2017. In October 2018 he was disqualified from the Boston Marathon after testing positive for EPO.
