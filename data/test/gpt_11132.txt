Pietro De Giorgio (born 16 February 1983) is an Italian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie A club Lazio.  De Giorgio started his career at hometown club Carrarese before moving to Serie C1 club Brescia in 2001. He made his Serie A debut in 2003, and played for various clubs, including Reggina, Fiorentina, Lazio, Napoli and Palermo, before joining Lazio in 2016.  De Giorgio has represented Italy at international level, making his debut in 2006. He has since played at Euro 2008 and the 2010 World Cup.
