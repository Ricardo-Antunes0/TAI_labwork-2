Selfie with Bajrangi is an action-comedy animation film directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The film features an ensemble cast of Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra, Shahid Kapoor, and Jim Sarbh. It is produced by Bhansali and Sri Shankar Raja.  The film tells the story of a man- buffalo relationship and the various shenanigans that take place between them. Bajrangi (Ranveer Singh) is a holy man who is looking for a new home after being kicked out of his previous one. He comes across Baby (Deepika Padukone), a calf who has been abandoned by her herd and soon falls in love with her. Meanwhile, Bajrangi's long-lost friend Babu (Shahid Kapoor) arrives in town with his gangster friend Mukesh (Jim Sarbh) and decides to take advantage of the situation.
