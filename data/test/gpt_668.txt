The film stars Mohanlal, Lakshmi, Jagathy Sreekumar and Anju Aparna in lead roles.    Kunjikoonan is a 2002 Indian Malayalam-language drama film directed by P. A. Backer and written by Kannan Thamarakrishnan. The film stars Mohanlal, Lakshmi, Jagathy Sreekumar and Anju Aparna in lead roles. The story revolves around the life of a middle-aged man, Kunjikoonan (Mohanlal), who is trying to revive his dying family business. The film's soundtrack was composed by A. R. Rahman and the cinematography was handled by Fazil.   The film won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Malayalam in 2003.
