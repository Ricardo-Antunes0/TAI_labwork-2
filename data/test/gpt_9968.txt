"Puente" is a latin pop song by Colombian singer-songwriter Jenni Rivera. It was written by Rivera and produced by her father, Porfirio "Porgy" Rivera. The song was released as the lead single from her third studio album, Puente (2015).  "Puente" is a latin pop ballad with influences of R&B and soul. The lyrics deal with the protagonist's feelings of love and loss. The song garnered positive reviews from music critics, who praised its catchy melody and emotive lyrics. It became a commercial success, topping the charts in several countries including Colombia, Mexico, and Venezuela. The song was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Latin Pop Song.
