Henri Fluchère (1898–1987) was a chairman of the French Communist Party (PCF) from 1967 to 1978.  Henri Fluchère was born in 1898 in Lille, France. He joined the French Communist Party in 1922 and served as its chairman from 1967 to 1978. During his tenure, he helped rebuild the party after its disastrous defeat in the 1968 French presidential election. Fluchère also played a role in the party's alliance with the French Socialist Party during the 1970s. He died in 1987.
