The 2018 Rose Bowl was a college football bowl game played on January 1, 2018, at the Rose Bowl stadium in Pasadena, California. The 103rd Rose Bowl Game was a part of the 2018 NCAA Division I FBS football season.  The UCLA Bruins (10–3) played the Michigan State Spartans (9–4) in a rematch of the 2017 College Football Playoff National Championship Game. The Bruins were making their first appearance in the Rose Bowl since the 1995 season, while Michigan State was making their first appearance since 2011.  The game was televised by ESPN and was won by UCLA 31–27. This was the Bruins' seventh Rose Bowl win and sixth under head coach Jim Mora. It was Michigan State's tenth appearance in the Rose Bowl and second under head coach Mark Dantonio.
