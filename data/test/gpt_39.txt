Formed in 2003, the band released two albums before disbanding in 2007. The band's sound blended electronic and acoustic elements with dreamy vocals and lush instrumentation. Their self-titled debut album was released in 2003 to critical acclaim, peaking at number 54 on the Billboard 200. The follow-up, 2006's Here Comes the Sun, saw a more subdued response, but still reached the top 100 on the charts. In 2007, Valentino disbanded shortly after releasing their third album, which failed to achieve similar success.
