"Wifey" is a song by American R&B recording artist Rihanna from her fifth studio album, "Loud" (2015). The song was written by Barbadian singer Rihanna, American songwriter and producer Sia Furler, and Australian singer-songwriter and producer Skylar Grey.  The song was released on January 29, 2016, as the album's lead single. "Wifey" is a dance-pop and funk song that lyrically speaks about a female partner's devotion to her husband. The song received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Rihanna's vocals. It became a commercial success, topping the charts in Austria, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Germany, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Poland, Scotland, Sweden and the United Kingdom. "Wifey" also reached the top ten in Australia, Spain and the United States.  Rihanna performed "Wifey" live for the first time at the 2016 Brit Awards on February 24. The song was also included in the set list for her Loud World tour (2016).
