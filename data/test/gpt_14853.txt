McGavock Dickinson "Mac" Gayden (born June 5, 1951) is an American singer-songwriter, guitarist and record producer. He has been active as a solo artist since the early 1980s, releasing seven studio albums and charting six singles on the US "Billboard" Hot 100. Gayden also achieved success as the frontman of the alternative rock band 10,000 Maniacs, which he formed in 1985. The band's biggest hits include "I Want a New Drug", "Crazy" and "The World I Know".
