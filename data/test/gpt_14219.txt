He was the head coach of the Samoa national team from 2010 to 2016, and also coached the Samoa Sevens team.  Fono played as a loose forward for Western Samoa and the All Blacks, appearing in 47 tests between 1985 and 1998. He also coached Western Samoa in their first ever test match in 2003.
