Coast is a consumer magazine about the American West that was founded in 1978. The magazine has a circulation of about 116,000 and is published monthly by the Coaster Group, a privately held company based in Bend, Oregon.  Coast is devoted to providing readers with information about the outdoors, travel and lifestyle, as well as cooking and home decorating. The magazine has won numerous awards, including a National Magazine Award for General Excellence in 1998.
