Subhash Alias Rajan Kashinath Naik is an Indian economist and the current Vice-Chancellor of the Central University of Gujarat. Previously, he served as the Chief Economic Advisor to the Government of India from August 2013 to January 2014.  Naik was born in 1954 in a small town in Gujarat, India. After completing his undergraduate studies at the Mumbai University, he did his postgraduate studies at the London School of Economics, where he obtained his M.A. in economics in 1977 and his Ph.D. in 1986.  Naik has held several academic positions, including Professor of Economics at the Mumbai University, Visiting Professor at the Johns Hopkins University and a member of the Monetary Authority of India's (MAI) monetary policy committee. He has also served as the Dean of Faculty and Pro-Vice-Chancellor (Academic) at the Central University of Gujarat.  Naik has been a columnist for various publications, including The Economic Times, DNA and The Hindu. He has authored several books, including The Economic Evolution of India and Globalization: The New Competition.  Naik was appointed as the Vice-Chancellor of Central University of Gujarat on 6 September 2016.
