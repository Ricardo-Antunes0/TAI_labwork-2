Myles Lorenz Garrett (born December 29, 1995) is an American football defensive end for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Texas A&M.  Garrett was drafted by the Browns with the first overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft. He became the first player in Browns history to be selected with the first overall pick. He has been named to the 2018 Pro Bowl and was voted first-team All-Pro by his peers.
