The PNS Mehran is the naval station of the Pakistan Navy, located in Karachi. The naval station was established in 1975 and has a land area of .  The PNS Mehran is a major naval base and home port of the Pakistan Navy. It is also the headquarters of the Naval Staff Corps. The Karachi-based PNS Mehran is also the largest naval base in Southern Asia.  The PNS Mehran comprises two main areas: the Main Area, which is home to most of the naval and auxiliary establishments; while the Aft Area houses various support facilities, including a hospital, schools, and residences for officers and their families. The PNS Mehran also has a harbour that can accommodate up to 50 ships.  The PNS Mehran is currently commanded by Vice Admiral Shahid Mehmood Osmani.
