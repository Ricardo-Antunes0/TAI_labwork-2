Epichorista aspistana is a species of moth of the Tortricidae family. It is found in Ecuador.  The wingspan is about 18Â mm. The ground colour of the forewings is white, suffused with brownish and with blackish markings, including a fascia from the costa near the base to beyond the middle. There is also a fascia near the apex and a small black spot at the end of the cell. The hindwings are whitish, but browner in the apical third.
