Gwen Grant is an English writer primarily known for her children's books.  Gwen Grant was born in 1961 in Manchester, England. She has a degree in English from the University of Bristol and worked as a teacher before becoming a full-time writer in 1988. Her first book, The Grown-ups, was published in 1992. She has since written over 20 books for children, including the popular Mummy's Little Monster series. Grant has also written a number of adult novels, including The Diary of a Young Girl and The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo. She has been awarded several prestigious literary awards, including the Carnegie Medal and the Guardian Children's Fiction Prize.
