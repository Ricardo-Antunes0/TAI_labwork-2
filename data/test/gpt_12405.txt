The Nike Literary Award  is one of several annual literary awards presented by the company Nike, Inc. The award is presented to an American author for a book of fiction, nonfiction, or poetry published in the United States during the previous year.   The award was first presented in 1978 and has been given annually since then, with the exception of 1984 and 1985. The prize is worth US$10,000.   As of 2017, the award has been given to 226 authors.
