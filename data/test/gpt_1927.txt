The Conjuring 2 (known in the United States as The Conjuring 2: The Enfield Poltergeist) is a 2017 American supernatural horror film directed by James Wan and written by Chad Hayes and Carey Hayes. It is the sequel to the 2013 film The Conjuring, and the second installment in The Conjuring franchise. The film stars Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson, Ron Livingston, Frances O'Connor, Madison Wolfe, and Joey King.  The story follows the Perrons as they travel to England to help a woman named Lorraine who believes she is being haunted by the spirit of her dead daughter. The Perrons soon discover that the spirit is not alone, and must face a coven of witches determined to destroy them.  The Conjuring 2 was released in the United States on July 22, 2017, and grossed over $232 million worldwide. It received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised Wan's direction and the performances of its cast.
