Born in Lucerne, Oscar Werwath studied at the Zurich School of Arts and Crafts and the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Brussels. He was a member of the Association Nationale des Beaux-Arts de Belgique and exhibited at the Salon d'Automne in Paris and at the Venice Biennale. After World War II, he settled in Basel.
