Ard Crags is a fell in the English Lake District, near Keswick. The fell is situated on the eastern side of Borrowdale, and has an altitude of 927 metres (3,010 ft).     The fell is named after Ard Crag, which is the highest point on the fell. The other tops on the fell are Broad Crag to the south-west, Pillar Crag to the north-west and Seat Crag to the north-east.     Ard Crags can be reached from Borrowdale by following the Borrowdale Way from Keswick. The walk takes about two hours round trip.
