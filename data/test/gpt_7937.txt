"Best Burger" is the fifth episode of the first season of the American television series "Breaking Bad". It originally aired on AMC in the United States on July 16, 2008.  Walter White and his former student Jesse Pinkman hatch a plan to steal $2 million from the Heisenberg drug cartel by cooking crystal meth and selling it under the guise of being legitimate products. To prevent suspicion from falling on them, they cook burgers for a conference of food critics that are in town for a festival.   The burgers are a success, and the two men use the money to buy a new car and start a new life. However, their new life is short-lived when they are discovered by the cartel, who force them to cook more meth.   Jesse is killed in an attempt to blackmail Walter, and Walter is arrested by the DEA. He is sentenced to life in prison with no chance of parole.
