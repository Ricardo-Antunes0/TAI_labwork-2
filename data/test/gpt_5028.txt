Craig Richard Norwich (born December 15, 1955) is an American actor, best known for his roles as Dr. Mark Sloan on the ABC medical drama series Grey's Anatomy and Agent John Casey on the CBS spy thriller series The X-Files. Norwich has also appeared in a number of feature films and television series, including The War of the Worlds, Star Trek: Voyager, ER, and Queer as Folk. He has received critical acclaim for his work on both series, and has been nominated for awards such as the Golden Globe Award and Screen Actors Guild Award. Norwich was born in Los Angeles, California, the son of actress Lois Nettleton and actor Richard Norwich.
