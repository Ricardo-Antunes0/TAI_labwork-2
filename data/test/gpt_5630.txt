Crowd manipulation is the intentional or unwitting use of methods to cause a group of people to act in a certain way, typically by influencing their emotions or behavior. It can be achieved through the use of persuasive communication, social influence and/or psychological tactics.  Crowd manipulation can have negative effects on individuals, groups and societies. It can be used for personal gain, to manipulate public opinion, to control the behavior of participants in a crowd or mob, or for political purposes.
