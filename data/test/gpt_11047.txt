Astra 2D is one of the Astra satellites, built by Airbus Defence and Space. Astra 2D is a dual-purpose satellite, designed to provide telecommunications services and to support meteorology, oceanography and land survey.  The Astra 2D satellite was built by Airbus Defence and Space using the Astra 2D platform. The spacecraft is equipped with a hybrid propulsion system, which uses both electric and chemical propulsion. The satellite has a design life of 10 years, and is operated by Eutelsat Communications.
