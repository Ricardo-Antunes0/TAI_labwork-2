Highway 138 (AR 128, Ark. 138, and Hwy. 138) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Arkansas. It runs from U.S. Route 165 in Prairie Grove to U.S. Route 71 in Monticello.  Highway 138 begins at an intersection with US 165 in Prairie Grove, running northeast to an interchange with I-40 near Monticello. The highway then continues northeast to its northern terminus at an interchange with US 71 in Monticello. The route is a two-lane rural highway for its entire length.  Highway 138 was created in 1957 as a renumbering of AR 128.
