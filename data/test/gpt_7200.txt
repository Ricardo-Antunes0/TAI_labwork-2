SoFi Stadium is a sports and entertainment complex in SoFi, Malta. It is the home ground of SoFi Lions F.C., which plays in the Maltese Premier League. The complex also includes a stadium for concerts and other events, as well as a gym and a swimming pool.  SoFi Stadium was inaugurated on 16 December 2013, with a match between SoFi Lions F.C. and Hibernians F.C.. The stadium has a capacity of 3,000 spectators, making it the smallest in the Maltese Premier League.  The stadium is named after SoFi Group, one of Malta's largest financial institutions. The naming rights were acquired by SoFi in November 2012 for a period of five years.
