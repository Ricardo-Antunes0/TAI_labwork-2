Dain Ba Enkh (Mongolian: War and Peace) is a novel by the Mongolian author Erdeniin Batuu. It was first published in 1988.  The novel is set in the late 1980s in Mongolia during the time of the country's socialist revolution. It tells the story of a young peasant, Dain, and his struggles against an oppressive government and the poverty that pervades life in rural Mongolia.
