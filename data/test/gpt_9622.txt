GT Racer is a Cinéma vérité television series produced by French broadcaster TF1. Premiering on TF1 on 5 October 2006, the show is based on the video game GT Racing World Championship and follows a professional racing team as they compete in various international events.  The show is presented by Cyril Hanouna and Yann Barthès, and features commentary from Patrick Senna, Olivier Panis, and Jean-Éric Vergne. The first season was won by the Proton Competition team.
