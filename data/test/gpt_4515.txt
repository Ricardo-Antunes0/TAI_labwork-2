The World of Concrete is an annual international concrete art exhibition that takes place in different cities around the world.  Concrete is a versatile material with a long history. It has been used for construction since the late 1800s, and has become an important part of modern architecture and urban planning. The World of Concrete celebrates this versatile material with an annual international exhibition that showcases the best concrete art from around the world.  The World of Concrete is a platform for artists to share their work, and it provides a forum for discussion and debate about concrete art. The exhibition has been held in different cities around the world each year since 2007, and it is scheduled to continue until 2020.
