Gotcha! The Sport! is a video game for the Nintendo DS. It was developed by Next Level Games and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment.  The game is a party game that uses the DS touchscreen to allow players to compete in various mini-games. The game was released on October 24, 2009 in North America, and on October 29, 2009 in Europe.  Gotcha! The Sport! follows the story of four friends who are trying to win a championship in their sport of choice: baseball, tennis, golf, or soccer. Players can control their characters through a series of mini-games that take them through different stages of the competition.
