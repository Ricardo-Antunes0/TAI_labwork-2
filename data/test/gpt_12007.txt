Aerocar International's Aerocar (often called the Taylor Aerocar) is an American two-seat, single engine, high-wing monoplane that was designed by Glenn Hammond Taylor and first flown in 1934. The Aerocar has been produced in several versions and remains in production today by the company founded by Glenn Hammond Taylor.  The Aerocar has been praised for its graceful lines, handling, and ruggedness. It remains a popular aircraft with pilots and enthusiasts around the world.
