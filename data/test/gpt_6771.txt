Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2009. The band consists of Dan Reynolds (vocals and guitar), Wayne Sermon (guitar), Ben McKee (bass), and Mark McGrath ( keyboards and percussion).  Formed in 2009, Imagine Dragons quickly gained a following with their innovative blend of pop rock. Their debut album "Night Visions" was released in 2012 to critical acclaim and top 10 charting singles "Radioactive" and "Demons". The album has since been certified 5x platinum in the United States. In 2015, they released their second album "Smoke + Mirrors", which debuted at number one on the Billboard charts and yielded two top 10 singles "Believer" and "Thunder". The album has since been certified 3x platinum in the US.  In 2017, they released their third album "Evolve", which debuted at number one on the Billboard charts. The album spawned the number-one singles "Believer" and "Thunder".  Imagine Dragons have sold over 15 million records worldwide.
