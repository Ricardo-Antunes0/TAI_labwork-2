Balvarran is an old laird's house and estate in the Scottish Highlands. The house, which was originally built in the 16th century, is now a tourist attraction and wedding venue.  Balvarran is located in the Lammermuir Hills, about north of Perth and west of Bonnie Doon. The estate covers an area of and includes a range of facilities for visitors, including a golf course, a spa, a trout fishery, and an 18-hole golf course. The house is built on a promontory overlooking the River Tay.
