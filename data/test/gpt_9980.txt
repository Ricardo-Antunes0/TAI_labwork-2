Synodontis filamentosus, known as the longfin synodontis, is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Synodontis.  The longfin synodontis is endemic to Lake Malawi where it is found in the upper reaches of large, clear, fresh-water lakes. This fish grows to a maximum length of 24 cm. The longfin synodontis is a specialist feeder on fish larvae and juveniles, and also takes small crustaceans and molluscs.
