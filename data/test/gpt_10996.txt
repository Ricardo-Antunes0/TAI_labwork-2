Mercy Maston (born November 10, 1992) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Tasha Smith in the Fox musical comedy-drama series Empire and as Evie Boyd in the Netflix supernatural drama series Stranger Things.  Maston was born on November 10, 1992, in Louisville, Kentucky. She is of African-American and Native American descent. Maston has been acting since she was a child, making her debut in the 2004 feature film The Family That Preys. She has since appeared in films such as The Help (2011), The Butler (2012), and Selma (2014). Maston also had recurring roles on the television series Empire and Chicago P.D. from 2016 to 2017. In 2018, she starred as Tasha Smith in the Fox musical comedy-drama series Empire and as Evie Boyd in the Netflix supernatural drama series Stranger Things.
