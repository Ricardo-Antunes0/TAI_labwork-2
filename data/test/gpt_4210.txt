The Jacob Pledger House is a historic house located in the West End of Milwaukee, Wisconsin.  The Jacob Pledger House was built in 1857 for Jacob Pledger, a businessman who was one of the city's earliest developers. The house is a large two-story wood frame structure, with a gable roof and a central entrance flanked by two large, projecting porches. The interior features high ceilings and elaborate woodwork, and features original artwork and furniture. The house was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1975.
