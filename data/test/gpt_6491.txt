The WTA Indian Open is a tournament played on outdoor red clay courts. The event is part of the WTA Tour and is held in Chennai, India.   The tournament was founded in 1976 as the Indian Open. The event was given its current name in 2007. It is currently the first of the season's four WTA Premier 5 tournaments.   The WTA Indian Open has been won by a number of players, including Martina Hingis (five times), Venus Williams (four times), and Serena Williams (three times). Other successful players include Svetlana Kuznetsova, Jelena Jankovic, and Ana Ivanovic.
