Stanko Svitlica (Serbian Cyrillic: Станко Свитлица; born October 1, 1972) is a Serbian professional basketball player who last played for Cholet Basket of the French LNB Pro A. He played college basketball at the University of Utah.  Svitlica, a 6'8" power forward/center, attended the Kragujevac Military Academy in Yugoslavia and played for the junior national team before playing professionally in Spain and France. He signed with the Utah Jazz in 1998 but was waived shortly afterwards. He then played for the Reno Bighorns of the NBA Development League before joining Cholet Basket in 2002.  Svitlica has also played for the Serbian national team and was a member of the silver-medal winning team at the 2004 Olympics.
