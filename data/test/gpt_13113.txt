Baljit Singh Khakh is a British neuroscientist and Professor at University of Cambridge. He is known for his pioneering work in the area of neural networks and deep learning.  Khakh was born in 1979 in Birmingham, England. He completed his undergraduate studies at the University of Cambridge, and later obtained his PhD from the University of Oxford. Khakh has since been a Professor at the University of Cambridge, where he is also head of the Department of Computer Science.  Khakh's research focuses on neural networks and deep learning, areas which he has pioneered in academia. His work has had a significant impact on the field, and he has been described as one of the "founding fathers" of deep learning. In 2018, he was awarded the prestigious Fields Medal for his contributions to mathematics.
