The Joint Economic Forecast (Gemeinschaftsdiagnose, GD) is a joint report of the Federal Statistical Office of Germany (Destatis) and the Federal Reserve Bank of Frankfurt am Main. It was first published in 1991 and is released every six months.  The GD is a broad-based economic analysis of the German economy, covering all sectors and regions. It provides detailed economic data on Germany's economy, as well as forecasts of future developments. The GD is an essential tool for business, policy makers, investors and others who need to understand the German economy.
