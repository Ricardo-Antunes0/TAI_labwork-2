Dean Buffalini (born February 23, 1964) is an American musician and songwriter. He is best known as the lead singer and guitarist for the rock band Dusty Drake.  Dean Buffalini was born on February 23, 1964, in Lodi, California. He started playing guitar at the age of 12, and formed Dusty Drake in 1987. The band released four studio albums before splitting up in 2001. Buffalini has since released two solo albums and toured extensively throughout the United States and Europe. He has also worked as a session musician, most notably for Alanis Morissette.
