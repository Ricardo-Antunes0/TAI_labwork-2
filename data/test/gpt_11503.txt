Rhian Sheehan is a New Zealand composer and musician. She plays the ukulele, guitar, and piano.  Sheehan was born in New Zealand in 1984. She began playing the ukulele at the age of six and later learned to play the guitar and piano. Sheehan's music is based on traditional folk and classical music styles, and she has released two albums: "The Last Great Sea" (2007) and "Sea of Tranquility" (2010). Sheehan has also released several singles, including "The Weight of Water" and "Matilda". Sheehan has performed live in countries such as Australia, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Chile, Denmark, France, Germany, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Norway, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom, and United States.
