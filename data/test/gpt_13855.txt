Start Something is the second studio album by American singer and songwriter Halsey, released on March 3, 2019, by Astralwerks.  The album was preceded by the release of the single "Without Me", which topped the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and achieved multi-platinum certification in countries such as Australia and Canada. The album's second single, "Goodbye", was released on February 12, 2019.   Start Something is a collection of 13 tracks that explore heartbreak, self-discovery and resilience. The album is dedicated to Halsey's late mother and features a guest appearance from X Ambassadors frontman Dan Reynolds.  In an interview with "Billboard", Halsey said, "I wanted to make an album that felt like it was telling a story from beginning to end, like there were no breaks. I wanted it to be a cohesive piece of work where you can hop on one track and be right in the middle of the story."   "Start Something" received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album has an average score of 73, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".   In a review for "The Fader", Sydney Gore wrote that "the album's lush production and emotive lyrics serve as a reminder that Halsey is one of pop music's most gifted storytellers."
