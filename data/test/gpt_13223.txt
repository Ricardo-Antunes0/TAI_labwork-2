Maoulida Darouèche (born 7 February 1990) is a French actress, singer and comedian.   Born in Marseille, Darouèche began her career as a comedian in her native city, appearing on television shows such as "Le Grand Journal" and "Le Petit Journal". She made her debut in cinema in the 2010 film "Les Misérables", playing Fantine. In 2012, she starred in the comedy film "Le Dernier Mot". In 2013, she played the lead role in the French drama film "L'Enfant".  In 2014, she starred in the French comedy film "Un prophète". That same year, she lent her voice to the animated feature film "The Secret of Kells", playing Fionnula. In 2016, she starred in the French drama film "Montmartre".
