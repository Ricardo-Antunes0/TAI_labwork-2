Ethen Godrey Roberts (born July 15, 1990), better known by his stage name Ethen, is an American rapper and songwriter from Memphis, Tennessee. He is the founder of the record label Stoned Immaculate and has released two studio albums, 2013's The Good Life and 2016's I'm Fine.  Roberts was raised in Memphis and began rapping at the age of 12. In 2011, he released his first mixtape, Ghetto Gospel. The following year, he released his first studio album, The Good Life. Critics praised Roberts for his lyrical content and unique sound, with NPR calling him "a promising new voice in Memphis rap". In 2013, Roberts released I'm Fine, which was met with critical acclaim. He won Best New Artist at the Memphis Music Awards that year.
