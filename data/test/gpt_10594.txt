The Rapture Tour was a concert tour by the American hard rock band Aerosmith in support of their album Music from Another Dimension! (1993). The tour began on May 30, 1993 in Hershey, Pennsylvania and ended on December 10, 1993 in Honolulu, Hawaii.  The Rapture Tour was the second tour by Aerosmith in support of their album Music from Another Dimension!, following their successful 1990 World Tour. The tour was noted for its elaborate stage production and elaborate costume changes by the band members.
