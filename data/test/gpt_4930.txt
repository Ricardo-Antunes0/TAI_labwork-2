Congratulations is the second studio album by American rapper Eminem. It was released on May 17, 2002, by Interscope Records. The album was produced by Eminem, Dr. Dre, and The Bass Brothers.  The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, with first-week sales of 860,000 copies. It also reached the top ten in countries including Canada, France, Germany, and the UK. The album has been certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA), and has sold over 15 million copies worldwide.  "Lose Yourself" was released as the album's lead single on August 25, 2001. The song became a global success, topping the charts in numerous countries and becoming one of Eminem's best-selling singles. "Just Lose It" and "The Real Slim Shady" were released as the album's second and third singles, respectively. Both songs reached the top ten in many countries, while "The Real Slim Shady" became an international hit and was certified platinum by the RIAA. "Mockingbird" was released as the album's fourth single on March 19, 2002; it reached the top ten in several countries and became Eminem's fourth number one on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart.
