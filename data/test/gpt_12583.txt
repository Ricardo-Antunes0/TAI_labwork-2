The Obama Doctrine is a catch-all term for the policies and philosophies of President Barack Obama relating to foreign policy. The doctrine emphasizes diplomacy, international cooperation, and engagement with the world, with the goal of promoting American interests and values.  The Obama Doctrine is based on three principles: first, that America is a global leader and must be willing to lead by example; second, that America must be willing to work with others to solve global problems; and third, that America must remain open to new ideas and opportunities. The Obama Doctrine has been credited with leading to an increase in American diplomacy, international cooperation, and engagement with the world.
