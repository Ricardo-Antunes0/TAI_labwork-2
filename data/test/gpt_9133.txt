Eugénie Droz (born Eugénie Zahn 21 March 1888, died 8 December 1975) was a French actress. She appeared in more than seventy films from 1924 to 1975. She is particularly remembered for her roles in Marcel Pagnol's Marseille Trilogy (1937-1939), for which she was awarded the Palme d'Or at the 1939 Cannes Film Festival.
