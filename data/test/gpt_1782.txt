Tepin (foaled March 14, 2011) is a Thoroughbred racehorse best known for his victory in the 2013 Kentucky Derby.  Tepin is a bay horse bred in Kentucky by the Brown-Eyed Susan Ranch. He was sired by the Triple Crown champion and 2006 Horse of the Year Gulch, out of the mare Topaz. His dam, Betty Boop, was a half-sister to the stallion Ghostzapper.  Tepin began racing as a two-year-old in 2012, finishing fourth in two races and winning one. In 2013, he won four of six races, culminating with his victory in the Kentucky Derby.  Tepin retired from racing at the end of the year, and was transferred to a training stable in Florida. He has not raced since 2016 and is considered retired.
