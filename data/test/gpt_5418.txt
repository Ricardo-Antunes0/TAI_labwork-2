Martin Brian Spanjers (born February 2, 1987) is a Belgian professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Spanjers was drafted 122nd overall by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft. He has played in the NHL for the Edmonton Oilers, Philadelphia Flyers, Columbus Blue Jackets, and Calgary Flames.  Spanjers was born in Uccle, Brussels, Belgium. He has two sisters and an older brother. His father, Brian Spanjers Sr., played ice hockey for the Belgian national team.
