Akaflieg Berlin is one of around thirteen German akafliegs, or air clubs. Akafliegs are informal organizations of aviation enthusiasts, and Akaflieg Berlin is mainly interested in model aircraft.  Akaflieg Berlin was founded in 1977 as an association of enthusiasts of model aircraft.
