The 11th BET Awards took place at the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles on June 25, 2011. The show was hosted by comedian Mo'Nique and featured performances by Usher, Ludacris, and Ciara.  The BET Awards is an annual entertainment awards show that celebrates African American music and artistic achievements. The show was first presented in 1986 and has been broadcast annually since 2001.
