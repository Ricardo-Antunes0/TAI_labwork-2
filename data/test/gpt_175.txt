Monkey Cage Falls is a  waterfall located in  the  town of Old Forge, in Jefferson County, New York. The falls is located on the east side of the town, on private property.  The falls are named after the monkey cage that once stood at the base of the falls. The cage was used to transport goods up and down the falls on ropes. The falls were part of a large mill complex that was destroyed by a fire in 1930.
