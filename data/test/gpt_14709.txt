Geoffrey Ian Cullen (born 16 March 1977) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a midfielder. He made over 200 appearances in the Scottish Premier League and also played in the Football League for Reading.  Cullen started his career with Rangers, playing for the club from 1994 to 1998. He then moved to English club Reading, where he spent four seasons before rejoining Rangers in 2003. He later played for Dunfermline Athletic and Falkirk.
