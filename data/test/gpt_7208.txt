Jayli Wolf (born 23 November) is a Canadian singer and songwriter. She was born in Toronto, Ontario to a Jewish family, and grew up in Mississauga, Ontario. Wolf started writing songs when she was just 13 years old, and released her first EP at the age of 16. She has since released two more EPs, as well as her debut album, Life in Technicolor. Wolf has since toured throughout North America, Europe, and Asia. Jayli Wolf's music is a mix of folk and pop with an indie-rock vibe.
