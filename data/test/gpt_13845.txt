John August O'Korn (born November 22, 1994) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Kansas City Royals of Major League Baseball (MLB). He made his MLB debut in April of 2016.  O'Korn attended Pasadena High School in Pasadena, California. He played college baseball at Rice University, where he was a two-time All-American and won the Golden Spikes Award as the nation's top collegiate baseball player in 2015. The Royals selected O'Korn in the first round of the 2015 MLB draft.
