Worthington is a neighborhood of Louisville, Kentucky, United States. It is located in the east side of the city, bordered on the north by Bardstown Road, on the east by I-264, on the south by Bardstown Road again, and on the west by Preston Highway. The population was 10,895 at the 2010 census.  Worthington is a primarily residential neighborhood with a mix of single-family homes and apartments. It is home to a large number of businesses, restaurants, and churches. One of the most notable landmarks in Worthington is Calvary Baptist Church, which is one of the largest churches in Louisville.
