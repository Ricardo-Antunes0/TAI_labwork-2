"Choctaw Bingo" is a southern rock song by American singer-songwriter and musician John Fogerty. The song was released as the third single from his albumCenterfield.  The song is about a man who is going through a rough time in his life and is looking for salvation. The song was inspired by the story of Choctaw Indian Bingo, a man who lost his farm to a bank foreclosure and went on to win over $2 million in bingo games.
