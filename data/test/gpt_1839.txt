365  is Taiwanese Mandopop trio boyband formed in 2009, with members Chen Ming-chieh, Ko Chen-ju, and Lin Chia-hsi. The group has released six studio albums, three EPs, and one compilation album.  Formed in 2009, 365 is a Taiwanese Mandopop trio boyband. The group has released six studio albums, three EPs, and one compilation album. The most successful album of the group is their first release "365" which peaked at number one on the Taiwan Hot 100 chart and sold over 300,000 copies.
