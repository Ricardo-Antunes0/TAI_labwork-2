Brian Vaughn Bradley Jr. (born September 27, 1986), better known by his stage name Astro, is an American rapper and songwriter. He is the founder of the record label 300 Entertainment and released his debut album, The Return of Astro, in 2013.  Born in Cleveland, Ohio, Astro began rapping at the age of 10 and made his first appearance on a track with The Diplomats in 2002. He later formed the group Savemoney with fellow rappers T-Minus, MJG and Streetlife Crip. In 2006, Astro released his first solo album, The Return of Astro. The album was produced by Kanye West and John Legend and peaked at number 42 on the US Billboard 200 chart. In 2013, Astro released his second album, Untitled Unmastered. The album received critical acclaim and peaked at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart.
