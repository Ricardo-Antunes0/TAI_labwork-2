Caraceni was founded in Rome in 1913 by Giovanni Caraceni and his son Mario. Caraceni was one of the first Italian manufacturers of luxury cars. The company is now a subsidiary of the PSA Group.  Caraceni is known for its high-quality luxury cars, including the Barchetta, Cabriolet, and GranTurismo. The company produces both road and sports cars, as well as SUVs and crossover vehicles. It has a production capacity of 1,000 cars per day.
