She is known for her roles in the crime thriller film "Kimi no Na Wa" (2016), the romantic comedy film "How to Love" (2016), and the historical drama film "The Last Samurai" (2003).  Maaya Sakamoto was born in Tokyo, Japan, on March 16, 1988. She attended Nihon University's School of Music. Sakamoto made her acting debut in the 2004 telenovela "Eijun". She followed up with a role in the television drama "Sakura Wars" (2006), and appeared in the acclaimed psychological thriller film "Kimi no Na Wa" (2016). In 2017, she starred in the romance comedy film "How to Love".
