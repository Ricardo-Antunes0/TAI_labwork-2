Tan Sri Dato' Seri Utama Dr. Rais Yatim (born 28 October 1935) is a Malaysian business magnate. He is the founder of Yatim Group, which is one of the largest conglomerates in Malaysia.  Rais Yatim was born in 1935 in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia. He was educated at the University of Malaya and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.  Rais Yatim founded his first business, an import-export business, in 1957. In 1970, he founded Yatim Group, which became one of the largest conglomerates in Malaysia. Rais Yatim is also a philanthropist and has donated money to a number of charities. In 2009, he was awarded an honorary doctorate by the University of Malaya.
