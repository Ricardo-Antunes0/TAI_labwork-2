Pied Piper (Hartley Rathaway) is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics, usually as an adversary of the X-Men.  He is a mutant who can control the elements, and has the ability to generate and project concussive blasts of air, water, or earth. He is also a skilled hand-to-hand combatant and a formidable strategist.  Pied Piper first appeared in The X-Men #38 (September 1966), and was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby.
