The Wahta Mohawks are a community of Mohawk First Nation people located in the town of Wahta, Ontario. The community has a population of about 1,000 people and is served by a single elementary school. Originally settled in the 17th century, the Wahta Mohawks are one of the oldest Mohawk settlements in Ontario. Today, the Wahta Mohawks are engaged in traditional hunting and fishing, as well as economic development initiatives.
