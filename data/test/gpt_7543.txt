The 2020 Royal Rumble was the 33rd annual Royal Rumble professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by WWE. It took place on January 27, 2020, at the Alamodome in San Antonio, Texas.  The Royal Rumble match is a professional wrestling match in which a single competitor, who is randomly chosen by a coin toss at the beginning of the match, enters the wrestling ring and competes against all other participants for the right to win the match. The first performer to eliminate all their opponents is the winner of the match.
