Pop 2 is the fourth mixtape by American rapper 21 Savage. It was released on August 25, 2019, by Atlantic Records and Apple Music.  The mixtape features guest appearances from Offset, Ty Dolla $ign, Metro Boomin, Pharrell Williams and Dua Lipa. The album's production is primarily handled by Metro Boomin, 21 Savage himself, and Southside.  "Pop 2" was announced by 21 Savage on July 31, 2019. The project was initially set to be a double album but was later cut down to a single album due to its length.  "Pop 2" is 21 Savage's fourth project since he left Cash Money Records in December 2018. The album's lead single "Ain't My Fault" was released on July 10, 2019. The song features vocals from Offset and peaked at number three on the US "Billboard" Hot 100.
