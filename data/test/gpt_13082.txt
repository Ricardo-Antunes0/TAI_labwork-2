GRID-Arendal is a center collaborating with the University of Oslo, the Norwegian University of Science and Technology, and the Norwegian University of Life Sciences. Its aim is to develop new methods and tools for data-driven decision making in complex systems.  GRID-Arendal was founded in 2006 as a joint initiative between the University of Oslo, the Norwegian University of Science and Technology, and the Norwegian University of Life Sciences. The main objectives of GRID-Arendal are to develop new methods and tools for data-driven decision making in complex systems, and to disseminate these innovations to the scientific community.
