Mohammed Sanousi or Mohammed Nasser Al-Sanousi (, born October 1, 1946) is a retired Egyptian professional football player.  He played as a defender for the Egyptian national team and was part of the squad that won the African Cup of Nations in 1970. He also played for CAF club sides Tunisian club SM Erzurum and Egyptian club Al-Ahly.  He retired in 1977 after playing for Al-Ahly. He later became head coach of Zamalek SC, with whom he won the Egyptian Premier League title in 1997.
