A refuge island, also known as a safe harbour or a port of call, is an island or a part of an island that is used for the purpose of providing refuge from a storm, shipwreck, or other hazard.  A refuge island may be a small, isolated island, or an inhabited island that is used for the purpose of providing refuge from a storm, shipwreck, or other hazard. It may also be a large island that is used for the purpose of providing refuge from a storm, shipwreck, or other hazard. A refuge island may provide shelter from wind and waves, and from the extremes of weather. It may also provide food and water supplies, medical services, and other necessary resources.
