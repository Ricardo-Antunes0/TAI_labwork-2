Warrior Kings: Battles is a real-time strategy video game developed by Polish company ArtCraft Entertainment and published by Paradox Interactive. The game is based on the mythology of the medieval Polish kingdom of Kraków.  The game is set in the year 1031, five years after the end of the events of the previous game in the series, Warrior Kings: The Battle for Kraków. The player controls one of three factions – the Poles, the Germans or the Hungarians – and competes against other factions for control of Kraków and its surrounding territories. The game features a variety of units and structures, as well as a strategic map that can be zoomed in on to see individual units.  Warrior Kings: Battles was announced at Gamescom in August 2013, and was released on February 26, 2014.
