Aletta Stas-Bax (born 1965) is a Dutch artist. She lives and works in Amsterdam.  Aletta Stas-Bax was born in 1965 in The Hague, Netherlands. After graduating from the Academy of Art and Design in Amsterdam in 1990, she began her career as a graphic designer. In 1996, she started painting full-time, creating abstract paintings that are often characterized by their use of bright colors and large shapes. Her work has been exhibited in galleries and museums around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City and the Centre Pompidou in Paris. In 2018, she was awarded the Prince Bernhard Award for Arts and Sciences.
