Antoine Franklin McColister (born May 11, 1988), better known by his stage name Ace Hood, is an American rapper from Houston, Texas. He debuted in 2006 with appearances on mixtapes and guest appearances on albums by other artists. In 2009, he released his debut album, Tha General. The following year, he released his second album, Pain & Gain. In 2013, he released his third album, Ace Hood: The Mixtape. In 2014, he released his fourth album, I'm the One: The Album.
