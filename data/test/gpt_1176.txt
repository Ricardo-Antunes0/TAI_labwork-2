"An Honest Mistake" is a song by American rock band Blink-182. The song was written by bassist and vocalist Mark Hoppus, guitarist and vocalist Tom DeLonge and drummer Travis Barker, and produced by Hoppus.  The song was released as the lead single from the band's sixth studio album, California (2015). The song peaked at number four on the US "Billboard" Hot 100, becoming the band's 20th top-ten hit on the chart. It also reached number one on the Rock Digital Songs chart and number two on the Alternative Songs chart. The song won a Grammy Award for Best Rock Song in 2016.  The song is about making an honest mistake that leads to something good happening.
