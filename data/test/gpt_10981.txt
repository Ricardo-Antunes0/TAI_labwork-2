Idiophantis habrias is a moth of the family Geometridae. It is found in Cameroon, the Central African Republic, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Uganda, Kenya and Tanzania.  The wingspan is about 35Â mm. The ground colour of the forewings is dark reddish brown, gradually paler towards the apex and termen. The hindwings are almost uniform light brown. Adults are on wing from October to December in both Kenya and Tanzania.
