"Mighty Long Fall/Decision" is the ninth single by Japanese rock band SCANDAL. The song was released on October 5, 2010, and is the third single from the band's sixth studio album "REVELATION" (2010).  The song is a ballad that tells the story of a love that cannot be saved. The lyrics describe the protagonist's feelings of betrayal and loss after their loved one chooses to leave them for someone else. The song was written by Mayu Shimizu and composed by Kenichi Matsubara.  "Mighty Long Fall/Decision" was used as the theme song for the drama "Sukeroku no Basho" which aired in Japan from September 25 to December 21, 2010.  SCANDAL performed "Mighty Long Fall/Decision" at the 2010 J-Rock Live! Vol. 2 concert on October 18, 2010 at the Saitama Super Arena in Saitama, Japan. They also performed the song at the 2010 J-Rock Live! Vol. 3 concert on November 1, 2010 at Zepp Nagoya in Aichi, Japan.
