Internal Salvation is the sixth full-length studio album by the American metalcore band As I Lay Dying. It was released on June 9, 2019, through Sumerian Records.  As I Lay Dying is an American metalcore band from San Diego, California, formed in 2000. The band has released six full-length studio albums, two EPs, and one compilation album. Internal Salvation is their sixth full-length album and their first since 2016's Awakened.  Internal Salvation was recorded at Soundstage Studios in Nashville, Tennessee with producer Mike Elizondo (Lamb of God, Deftones, Slipknot). The album was mixed by Troy Van Leeuwen (Orelsan, Deftones, Korn) at Rise Above Studios in Vista, California.
