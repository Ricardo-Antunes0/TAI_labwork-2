Regina Paz "Gina" La'o Lopez (December 27, 1969 – May 6, 2009) was an American actress, singer and model. After starting her career as a model, Lopez made her feature film debut in the 1991 comedy Hit Me with Your Best Shot. Her breakthrough role came in the 1994 romantic drama film Selena which earned her a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress. She went on to appear in films such as The Wedding Planner (2001), Monster's Ball (2001), and Maid in Manhattan (2002). Lopez also had recurring roles on the television series My Wife and Kids and Melrose Place.   Lopez was nominated for an Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series for her performance on the Fox series Glee. She released her debut album On the 6 in 2007 which spawned the hit single "Ain't No Other Man". In 2008, Lopez starred in the comedy film Out of Sight which earned her a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress – Motion Picture Comedy or Musical. She returned to television in 2009 with the sitcom AllAboutTheLadys which was cancelled after one season. Lopez died from lung cancer at the age of 47.
