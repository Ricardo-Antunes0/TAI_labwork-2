Renaissance (also known as Paris 2054: Renaissance) is a 2006 science fiction film directed by Ridley Scott, produced by David Heyman and starring Kevin Bacon, Giovanni Ribisi, and Keira Knightley. Set in a future Paris that has been rebuilt after a devastating earthquake, the film tells the story of a love triangle between Bacon's character, a wealthy businessman; Knightley's character, a young artist; and Ribisi's character, a hustler.
