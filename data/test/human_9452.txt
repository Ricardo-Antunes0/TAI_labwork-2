Rusudan Bolkvadze (born November 6, 1959) is a Georgian actress. She graduated from 55th State School in Tbilisi and went to Shota Rustaveli Theatre and Film State University of Georgia. In 1980 she enrolled at the Studio led by the Georgian director Mikheil Tumanishvili. Currently she is one of the leading actresses in Tumanishvili Film Actors Theatre.
