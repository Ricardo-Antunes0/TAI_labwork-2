The Telefones are a musical group based in Madrid, Spain. Formed in 1992, the group consists of Iván Navarro (vocals and guitar), Sergio Paredes (bass guitar and backing vocals), and José Luis Paredes (drums and percussion).  The Telefones are known for their eclectic mix of pop, rock, and electronica, as well as their innovative use of multimedia technology. They have released six studio albums and two EPs, as well as several singles and videos.
