The Columbus Open, also known as the Bank of America Tennis Championships, is a professional tennis tournament played on outdoor hard courts. It is part of the ATP World Tour 250 series of the 2018 ATP World Tour. The tournament takes place at the Ohio State University in Columbus, Ohio, United States, from August 10 through August 16, 2018.  The event was first played in 1922 as the Columbus International Tennis Tournament. The first women's edition was held in 1976 and the event became part of the ATP World Tour in 1981.
