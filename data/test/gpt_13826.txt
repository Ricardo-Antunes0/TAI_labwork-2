Herbert Ruggles Tarlek, Jr. is a character in the novel "The Catcher in the Rye" by J.D. Salinger.  Herbert Ruggles Tarlek, Jr. is a character in the novel "The Catcher in the Rye" by J.D. Salinger. He is a loner who is expelled from boarding school and briefly works at a gas station before being drafted into the United States Army during World War II. He is eventually discharged and returns to New York City, where he becomes involved with Juliet Huddy and her brother Buddy.
