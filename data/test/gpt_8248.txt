Pudhukottaiyilirundhu Saravanan  is a 2004 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film written and directed by Gautham Menon. The film, produced by Menon's own company, A. R. Rahman's Arts House, stars Karthi and Anushka Shetty in the lead roles, with Sathyaraj, Rima Te Wiata and T. Rajendar playing supporting roles.  The story is about a young man Saravanan (Karthi) who is from a poor family and has to work to help support his mother (Rima Te Wiata) and younger sister (Sanusha). Saravanan falls in love with an upper-caste girl Pootha (Anushka Shetty) but she is already married to a wealthy man. Saravanan decides to break free from his family and live life on his own terms. Pootha eventually realises her mistake and returns to her husband. The film ends with Saravanan and Pootha getting married.   The film was released on 14 February 2004 to positive reviews from critics. It won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Tamil and was also nominated for the Vijay Awards for Best Feature Film and Best Cinematography.
