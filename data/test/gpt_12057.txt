Startled Insects, later known as The Insects, were an American rock band formed in Gainesville, Florida in 1981. The band was active until 1998.  Startled Insects was originally formed in Gainesville, Florida in 1981. The group was active until 1998. During their career, the Insects released six albums and one compilation album. They achieved commercial success with their singles "Walk on the Wild Side" and "The Loco-Motion".
