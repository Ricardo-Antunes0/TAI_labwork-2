Autonomies   is an Israeli dystopian novel written by Yossi Klein Halevi. It was published in Hebrew in 2006, and translated into English in 2007.  The novel tells the story of a group of Jews who have fled the Holocaust and settled in a utopian society in the Israeli desert. But as the society starts to unravel, the members must decide whether to stay and fight for their freedoms, or flee and abandon everything they have built.
