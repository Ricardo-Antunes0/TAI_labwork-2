West Rainton is a village in the borough of Stockton-on-Tees and ceremonial county of County Durham, England. The population of the village was 2,045 at the 2011 census.  West Rainton is located 4.4 miles (7.1 km) southeast of Stockton-on-Tees, 3.9 miles (6.1 km) southwest of the town of Hartlepool and 8.5 miles (13.2 km) northwest of Newcastle upon Tyne.  The village is situated on the B6376 road, which connects it with Hartlepool to the northeast and Stockton-on-Tees to the southwest. The A167 road also passes close by, connecting West Rainton with the town of Middleton-on-Tees to the east and the villages of South Rainton and West Rainton Moor to the south.  The nearest railway stations are Hartlepool station, 3.9 miles (6.1 km) to the northeast, and Newcastle Central station, 8.5 miles (13.2 km) to the northwest.
