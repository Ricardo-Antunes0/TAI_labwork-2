Red Cockroaches (Spanish: Cucarachas Rojas) is a genus of cockroaches in the family Ectobiidae. There are about 35 described species in this genus.  The red cockroach is distributed throughout the Americas except for the Antilles. It is common in the southeastern United States, and can also be found in central and south America. It is not found in Europe or Asia. The red cockroach is a common pest in homes, and can cause damage to property by eating food, creating messes, and flying around.
