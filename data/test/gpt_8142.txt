Deep Conversation is the fourth studio album by American rock band Linkin Park. It was released on November 18, 2017, through Warner Bros. Records. The album was produced by the band and Mike Elizondo.  The album is a departure from the nu metal sound of their previous albums, and is composed of progressive and atmospheric rock songs. The album's sound has been likened to that of Radiohead and U2.  "Deep Conversation" was met with generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's experimentation with their sound and found it refreshing. The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, becoming Linkin Park's sixth consecutive number-one album in the country. It also reached the top 10 in several other countries.
