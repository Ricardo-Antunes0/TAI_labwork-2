Simtek (Simulation Technology) was an engineering consultancy firm, founded in 1984 in Cambridge, UK. The company provided engineering services to the telecommunications and computer industries.  Simtek was founded by Dr. Steve Furber and Dr. Robin Palmer, two engineers from the University of Cambridge's Department of Electrical Engineering. The company initially provided engineering services to the telecommunications and computer industries. In 1994, Simtek was acquired by MIPS Technologies, a Silicon Valley-based computer hardware and software company. In 1998, MIPS Technologies sold Simtek to Tele Atlas Ltd., a British satellite navigation company. In 2001, Tele Atlas Ltd. merged with British Aerospace Ltd., forming Aerospace Group plc. In 2003, Aerospace Group plc was acquired by EADS Astrium SAS, a European aerospace manufacturer.
