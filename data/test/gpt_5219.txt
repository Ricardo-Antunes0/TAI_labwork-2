Grind is a 2003 American comedy film written and directed by Robert Rodriguez.  Set in the fictional town of Humble, Texas, Grind follows the exploits of local Sheriff John T. Stone (Johnny Depp) as he tries to keep the peace in a town full of weirdos, criminals, and eccentrics. The film also features introduction scenes for each of the film's main characters, including Elvis (Tim Curry), the Witch (Linda Fiorentino), Granny (Dee Wallace), and the Abominable Snowman (Kevin Bacon).  The film was both a critical and financial success, grossing over $51 million on a budget of $5 million. It was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Makeup.
