The Werribee River is a perennial river located in the south-east suburbs of Melbourne, Australia. The Werribee River is approximately 25 kilometres long, and flows through the municipalities of Bayswater, Werribee and Melton.  The Werribee River is a major tributary of the Yarra River, and together they form part of the Melbourne metropolitan water supply system. The Werribee River is also recognised as an important recreational resource, with a variety of aquatic and non-aquatic activities available along its length.
