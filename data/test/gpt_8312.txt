"Wooly Bully" is a song originally recorded by the Australian rock band AC/DC. The song was written by the band's lead guitarist, Angus Young, and was released as the third single from their 1976 album Highway to Hell.  "Wooly Bully" is a hard-rocking song with a driving rhythm and heavy guitar riffs. The song is about a man who is a bully and is not afraid to use violence to get what he wants. The lyrics talk about how the bully is eventually brought down by his own actions.
