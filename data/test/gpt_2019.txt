Goeznovius (died c. 675 according to one source) was a Frankish monk and composer, who is best known for his 9th century hymn In Praise of Divine Providence.  Goeznovius was born in the 7th century in what is now the Netherlands, and died circa 675. He was a Frankish monk and composer, who is best known for his 9th century hymn In Praise of Divine Providence. The text praises God's providence, which Goeznovius saw as a protecting hand throughout his life. The hymn is still popular today, being performed in churches around the world.
