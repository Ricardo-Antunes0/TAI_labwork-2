Kanon Takao was born on September 9th, 1988 in Tokyo, Japan. She is a Japanese voice actress who works for Aoni Production. She has worked in roles in anime, video games and films. Kanon has been particularly noted for her roles as Yoko Littner in the Death Note film series and Mio Akiyama in the Attack on Titan television series.
