John Stephen Akhwari (born 1938 in Mbulu, Kenya) is a Kenyan composer and sound artist.  Akhwari was born in 1938 in Mbulu, Kenya. He studied at the Royal College of Music, London, where he was a pupil of R. Murray Schafer. In 1968 he founded the Sonic Arts Group, based in London. Akhwari has composed music for theatre, film, and radio, as well as performing his own works. He has been awarded a number of prizes, including the prestigious composition prize from the BBC in 1990.
