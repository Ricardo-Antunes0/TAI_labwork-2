The Sun Trail is a children's fantasy novel by Canadian author Beverley Lewis. It is the first book in the series The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe.   The Sun Trail tells the story of four children who are magically transported to a world of talking animals, magical forests, and wondrous rivers. To save their home from destruction, these youngsters must journey on foot to find a way back. Along the way, they encounter clever creatures and dangerous challenges that will test their courage and strength. Will they be able to return home before winter destroys the land?
