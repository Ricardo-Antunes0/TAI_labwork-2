New Race was a Detroit rock-styled super-group consisting of vocalist/guitarist Brett Gurewitz (formerly of Bad Religion), bassist Jay Bentley (formerly of The Jayhawks), drummer Joey Castillo (formerly of The Dead Kennedys) and guitarist Kerry King (formerly of Slayer).  Formed in 2001, New Race released two albums, "Detroit Rock City" and "Sonic Reducer". The group disbanded in 2003.
