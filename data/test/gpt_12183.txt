Elvis is the nickname of Erickson S-64, a military helicopter that was in service with the United States Army from 1971 to 1987. After being retired from military service, Elvis was donated to the Elvis Presley Foundation, which used it for charity and entertainment purposes. Elvis is now on display at the Graceland Museum.
