Desire is the fourth studio album by American rock band Hurts, released on September 8, 2018, through Capitol Records. The album was preceded by the single "Desire" on August 10.  The album's first single, "Desire", was released on August 10, 2018 and reached the top of the charts in Austria, Germany, Switzerland and Sweden. The second single "Broken" was released on December 6, 2018 and reached the top ten in Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Ireland, Italy, Norway and Spain. The third single "Lonely Tonight" was released on February 15, 2019 and reached the top five in Austria, Germany, Switzerland and Belgium.   The album was well-received by music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 81%, based on 10 reviews.
