Follow That Girl is a musical adapted from the 2006 novel of the same name by Rebecca Serle. The show, which is set in the fictional town of West Egg on Long Island in 1922, tells the story of a young woman, Scout, who is trying to find her way in the world. Her journey takes her from the sheltered life she has known in her hometown to the bright lights of New York City, where she meets a variety of people and experiences different kinds of love.  The musical was first performed in Chicago at the Victory Gardens Theatre from February 14 to March 20, 2016. It was subsequently produced at the National Theatre in London, where it opened on October 11, 2016 and closed on January 21, 2017. The Broadway production, which opened on April 20, 2017 and closed on August 5, 2017, was directed by Bartlett Sher and choreographed by Andy Blankenbuehler.
