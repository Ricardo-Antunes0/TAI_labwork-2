Maharao Shardul Singh ji Shekhawat was born on 25th July 1886 in the Shekhawat-Kotli State of Rajasthan. His father, Maharao Ranjit Singh, was ruler of the state and an important figure in the politics of Central India.   Shardul Singh was educated at the Prince of Wales' College, Dehra Dun and at Balliol College, Oxford.   In 1911 he became a barrister and took part in the Indian National Congress movement. He served as Minister for Education in the Government of India from 1930 to 1934.   He died on 17th June 1966.
