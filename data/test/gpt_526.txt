Punch, or The London Charivari was a weekly British magazine founded in 1841. It was published until 1967.  Punch was founded by Henry Mayhew and Edward Lloyd, with the first edition appearing on 14 October 1841. It was originally a weekly magazine but became a daily publication from 1892 to 1937. During the 1940s and 1950s it was published as a magazine twice a week.  The magazine was revived as a monthly publication in 1967, and ceased publication in December 2002.
