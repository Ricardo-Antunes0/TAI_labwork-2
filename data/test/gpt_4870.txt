Cauda Pavonis are an English deathrock band from the 1980s.  Formed in the early 1980s, Cauda Pavonis were one of the earliest deathrock bands. The band's music was heavily influenced by gothic rock and heavy metal, with a dark, brooding atmosphere. Cauda Pavonis released two albums, 1984's "Grave New World" and 1985's "The Haunted Palace", before splitting up.
