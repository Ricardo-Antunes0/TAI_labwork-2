Year 1437 (MCDXXXVII) was a common year starting on Saturday of the Gregorian calendar.  It was the year of the accession of Henry V of England.  Events [ edit ]  In Europe [ edit ]  The Hussite Wars come to an end with the Battle of Trenčín on 31 August.  The Hussites are defeated and their leader, Jan Žižka, is killed.  In Africa [ edit ]  The Mali Empire reaches its greatest extent under Mansa Musa. He establishes a capital at Timbuktu and begins a period of great prosperity for the empire.  Mansa Musa also builds several hundred mosques and universities throughout his empire.
