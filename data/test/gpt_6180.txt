Sandinista lanceolatum (synonyms include Aphonopelma lanceolatum and Ophioglossum lanceolatum) is a species of orchid endemic to Nicaragua.  Sandinista lanceolatum is a terrestrial orchid that grows in wet forests and cloud forests at elevations of 2,000 to 3,000 meters. The flowers are white with a pink tinge and are pollinated by hummingbirds.
