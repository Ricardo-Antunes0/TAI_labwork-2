The Levi Foss House is a historic house located at 302 East Main Street in Naples, Florida. It is a two-story, Federal style brick house with a gable roof. The house was built in 1821 by Levi Foss, a prosperous merchant who served as an elected member of the local legislature. The house is now used as the headquarters of the Naples Historical Society.
