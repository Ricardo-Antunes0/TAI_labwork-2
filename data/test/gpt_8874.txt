Samuel King Vaughan (April 20, 1824September 28, 1892) was an American jurist and politician who served as the nineteenth Governor of Texas.  Born in Indiana, Vaughan moved with his family to Texas in 1836 and began his legal career. Vaughan served in the Texas Senate from 1858 to 1860 and was later elected as the 19th governor of Texas, serving from 1870 to 1875. Vaughan later served as a federal judge in Texas. He died in 1892.
