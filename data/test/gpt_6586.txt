Simon Haynes is an Australian writer of science fiction, horror and fantasy. He has been published in magazines and anthologies including Asimov's, Analog, and Realms of Fantasy.  Simon Haynes was born in Melbourne, Australia in 1971. He graduated from the University of Melbourne with a degree in English Literature in 1995. He has since worked as a journalist, public servant, and book editor.  Haynes's first novel, The Unincorporated Man, was published in 2008. His second novel, The Stone Sky, was published in 2014. His third novel, The Hummingbird's Daughter, was published in 2017.
