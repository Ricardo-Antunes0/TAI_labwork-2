Emelia Brobbey (born 6 January 1982) is an English singer and songwriter.  Brobbey was born in Wrexham, Wales, to Welsh parents. She began her musical career as a backing vocalist for the Welsh pop band The Big Moon, before releasing her first solo album, "Only Love", in 2009.  Brobbey's second album, "The Kid", was released in November 2011. The album was preceded by the single "Hands", which reached number five on the UK Singles Chart. In January 2013, Brobbey released the single "Goodbye My Lover".  In early 2013, Brobbey announced that she would be taking a break from music to focus on her personal life. She returned to the music scene in 2016 with the EP "All I Need".
