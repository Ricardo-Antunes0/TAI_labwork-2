Flower of Queen  is a 2015 Indian Hindi-language drama film written and directed by Vikas Bahl.  The film stars Anushka Sharma, Ranveer Singh, and Shahid Kapoor in lead roles. The story is set in 1990s India and tells the story of a love triangle between a young man, an older man and a woman.  The film was shot in various locations in India, including Jaipur, Varanasi, Mathura and Delhi. The soundtrack was composed by Pritam.
