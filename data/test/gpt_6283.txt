Your Own Thing is a rock-styled musical project by singer/songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Jesse Sykes. The project was announced in October 2015 and Sykes began pre-production on the album in February 2016. Your Own Thing is Sykes' first solo album since he left the country band The Lone Bellow in 2013.   Your Own Thing was produced by John Leckie and will be released on April 7, 2017.
