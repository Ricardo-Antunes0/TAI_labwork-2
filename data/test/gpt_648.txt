"Ain't Your Mama" is a song recorded by American country music artist Florida Georgia Line. It was released in March 2017 as the third and final single from the group's sixth studio album, Dig Your Roots.  The song was written by Brian Kelley, Tyler Hubbard, and Josh Kelley and produced by Dave Cobb.  "Ain't Your Mama" is a ballad about a man who is trying to find himself and who is looking for someone to tell him that he isn't alone. The song features a string section and is lyrically about self-discovery.  "Ain't Your Mama" received positive reviews from critics. Heather Phares of AllMusic called it "one of the album's most affecting ballads". Country Weekly reviewer Matt Bjorke rated the song four out of five stars and said that it "shows off FGL's chops as a storytellers".
