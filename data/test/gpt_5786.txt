The Fin is the name of two supervillains appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The first Fin, created by writer-artist Jim Lee and first appeared in The New Warriors #95 (September 1994), is a mutant with the power to turn his body into water. The second Fin, created by writer Dan Slott and first appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #542 (November 2, 2012), is a mutant with the power to control ice.  Both Fins are enemies of the X-Men.
