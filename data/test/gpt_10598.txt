Idse is an island in Strand municipality in Møre og Romsdal county, Norway. The island is located about northeast of the town of Molde, about north of the village of Øygarden, and about west of the island of Senja. The island has an area of and has a population (2013) of 849.  The island is part of the traditional district of Molde. It was part of the municipality of Molde until 1964 when it became an independent municipality. The municipality is now divided into three parts: Idse in the northeast, Molde in the center, and Øygarden in the southwest.  The inhabitants are called "Idseboere". There are no villages on the island; all residents live in the three main settlements: Molde, Øygarden, and Idsebotn.
