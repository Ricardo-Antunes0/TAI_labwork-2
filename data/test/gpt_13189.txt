She started her music career as a vocalist and songwriter in 2001. She released her first album, "Distant Dreams", in 2003. Her second album, "The Healing", was released in 2006. She won the al-Fardan Award for Best Female Artist in 2009 and the Zayed Award for Best Female Artist in 2010. She also received the Best Singer and Best Album Awards at the Bahraini Music Awards in 2009 and 2010, respectively.   Al Bahathri died on 7 November 2016 after a long battle with cancer.
