Pretty Addicted is an English electronic music group, consisting of producers and DJs Alana Haines and Dan Snaith. The group released its debut album, "Pretty Addicted", in March 2013.  The duo first met while studying music production at the University of Leeds in the UK. After releasing a series of singles and EPs, Pretty Addicted released their debut album "Pretty Addicted" in March 2013. The album was preceded by the single "Want You". The song peaked at number 83 on the UK Singles Chart.  Pretty Addicted have toured internationally, including appearances at SXSW, Glastonbury and EDC Las Vegas. In 2014 they released a second album, "Expectations".
