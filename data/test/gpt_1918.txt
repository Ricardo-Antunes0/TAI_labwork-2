Lyneham was born in Canterbury, Kent, England on 13 August 1945. He started his musical career in 1963 as a keyboard player with the Canterbury folk-rock band The Mellow Folk. In 1968, Lyneham joined Yes, and played keyboards on their first three albums before leaving the band in 1971 to start a solo career. He returned to Yes in 1980 and played on their albums "90125" and "90126". He retired from touring in 1993 but continued to write and record music until his death from cancer on 24 September 2003.
