Zäzilie Wang (1 February 1909, Vienna — 9 October 1993, Vienna) was an Austrian-born Chinese artist and author.  Zäzilie Wang was born on 1 February 1909, in Vienna, Austria-Hungary to a Chinese father and Austrian mother. In 1931, she married the Austrian painter and sculptor Rudolf Wang. The couple had two daughters. Wang began her art career in the 1930s as a painter and sculptor, working in a Expressionist style. She later turned to writing, publishing several books of poetry and short stories in her native Austrian language. Wang died on 9 October 1993, in Vienna.
