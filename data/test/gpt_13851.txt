Sovana is a denominazione di origine controllata (DOC) wine from the province of Vercelli, Piedmont, Italy. It is a red wine made from a blend of cabernet sauvignon, merlot and sangiovese grapes.  The DOC designation is given to wines made from at least 70% cabernet sauvignon, 30% merlot and 10% sangiovese grapes. The term "sovana" derives from the Latin word for snake.
