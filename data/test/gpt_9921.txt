"Prisencolinensinainciusol" (stylized on the single cover as "PRISENCOLINENSIINCIUSOL") is the sixth studio album by Japanese rock band Porno Graffitti, released on February 3, 2018. It is their first album in six years and the first since their hiatus in 2014.  The album was announced in December 2017, with the release of two singles: "Kimi no Na Wa" and "Uta Ni Soba de". The album's title is a play on words, translating to "Prisencolinensinainciusole", meaning "prisencoline sulfide".  The album was well-received by music critics, who praised its experimental sound and the band's return to their roots. It debuted at number one on the Oricon Albums Chart and has sold over 113,000 copies as of May 2018.
