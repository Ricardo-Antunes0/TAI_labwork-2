The Armenian whiskered bat (Myotis hajastanicus), also known as the Armenian fruit bat, is a species of bat found in Armenia and Turkey.  The Armenian whiskered bat is the smallest of the three whiskered bats. It has a body length of 58 mm and a wingspan of 132 mm. The Armenian whiskered bat is brown with a white whisker-like mustache on its muzzle and a white patch on its chest. It roosts in caves and mines, feeding on insects.
