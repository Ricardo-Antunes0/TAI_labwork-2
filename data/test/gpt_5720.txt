Ludwig II is a sculpture of King Ludwig II of Bavaria, made by the sculptor Leo von Klenze in 1864. The work is a marble statue of Ludwig II seated on a throne, with one arm extended and the other resting on the armrest. It is located in the courtyard of the Royal Palace in Munich.
