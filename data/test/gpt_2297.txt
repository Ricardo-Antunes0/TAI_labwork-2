Oakley, Inc., based in Lake Forest, California, is a manufacturer of sunglasses and eyewear. The company was founded by James "Jim" Oakley in 1941.  Today, Oakley is a leading manufacturer of sunglasses and eyewear, with a wide range of products for both men and women. The company's products are sold in more than 150 countries and through more than 3,000 retailers.  Oakley has a strong commitment to environmental responsibility. The company has been certified as a "Gold Certified" environmental partner by the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC), and it has made significant contributions to conserving the environment through its product development and manufacturing processes.
