NGC 7318 (also known as UGC 12099/UGC 11905) is an open cluster located in the constellation of Bootes. It was discovered by John Herschel on October 7, 1835.   The cluster is around 10,000 light-years away from Earth, and has an estimated 200 stars. It is a small and faint object, and was only observed using large amateur telescopes.
