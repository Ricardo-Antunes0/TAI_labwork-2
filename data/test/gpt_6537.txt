David Casa (born 16 November 1968 in Madrid, Spain) is a Spanish artist and musician.  Casa studied painting at the University of Barcelona and music at the Conservatorio Superior de Música de Madrid. He has exhibited his work in Spain, France, Germany, Italy, Switzerland and Belgium. He has recorded two solo albums and collaborated with such artists as Carlos Baños, Jorge Drexler, Paco de Lucía and Iñaki Echeverría.
