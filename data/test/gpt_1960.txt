Promethium is a chemical element with the symbol Pm and atomic number 83. It is a transitional element that lies between the elements thallium and lead, and is thus not found in nature in its pure form. However, promethium is one of the most commonly used rare earth elements in industry, as it has a wide range of properties that make it useful in many different applications. Promethium is also used as a radioactive isotope source.
