Nolan Gerard Funk (born July 28, 1986) is an American actor, singer and rapper. He is known for his roles as Screech in the popular 1990s TV sitcom Saved by the Bell, which he starred in from 1991 to 1998, and its spin-off series Jessie from 1998 to 2007.  Funk was born in Los Angeles, California, to a Jewish family. He attended Fairfax High School in Los Angeles, and began his acting career appearing in television commercials before landing his breakthrough role as Screech on Saved by the Bell. The show ran for six seasons and helped him land roles in films such as The Mask (1994), Big Daddy (1995), The Faculty (1998) and Napoleon Dynamite (2004). Funk also released several albums during his time on Saved by the Bell, including 1995's Just Me, 1995's Funky Bunch and 1998's One More Try. In 2007, he starred in the Disney Channel sitcom Jessie and released one album on the Walt Disney Records label. He currently stars as Max on the Fox comedy series New Girl.
