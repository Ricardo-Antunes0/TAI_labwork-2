Michael James Lewis is an American race car driver. Born in 1985 in Orlando, Lewis began his racing career in 2002, competing in karting. He made his professional open-wheel racing debut in 2004, driving for Racing Technology Group in the USF2000 Championship. Lewis won seven races and the championship that year. He also competed in the Indianapolis 500 that year, and finished fourth. Lewis made his single-seater debut in 2005, driving for Carlin Motorsport in the GP2 Series. He finished second in the championship standings that season. Lewis moved to the GP3 Series for 2006, driving for ART Grand Prix. He won four races and the championship that year. Lewis moved to Formula One for 2007, driving for Renault F1. He finished ninth in the 2007 Formula One World Championship.
