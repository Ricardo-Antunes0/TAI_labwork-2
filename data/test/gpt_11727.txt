Adrian Richard Cashmore (born 23 July 1973) is an English football manager and former player. He is the manager of League Two club Grimsby Town.  Cashmore began his career as a trainee at Ipswich Town, before progressing through the club's youth system. He made his debut in the Football League in 1995 and played for Ipswich until 1998. He then joined Cardiff City, where he played for four seasons. After leaving Cardiff, Cashmore spent two seasons with Birmingham City.  In 2002, Cashmore signed for Portsmouth, where he played for two seasons. He then joined West Bromwich Albion on a free transfer in 2004, where he played for three seasons. In 2007, Cashmore joined Sheffield United on a free transfer, where he played for two seasons. After leaving Sheffield United, Cashmore joined Bristol Rovers on a free transfer in 2010. He spent one season with Rovers before joining Grimsby Town in 2011.  Cashmore has also represented England at Under-21 and Under-23 level.
