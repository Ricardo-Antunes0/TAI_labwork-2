J.Lately, born Jeremy Namkung, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer.  After graduating from high school in 2006, J.Lately moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in music. He started out by writing songs for other artists, but eventually found his own voice and began producing his own music. In 2013, J.Lately released his debut album, Let's Get Lost. The album received positive reviews and charted on the Billboard Top 50 charts. Since then, J.Lately has released two more albums, 2016's All I Wanna Do and 2018's Lost in the Fire. He has toured extensively throughout the United States and Europe and continues to write and produce new music.
