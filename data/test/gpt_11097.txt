Chris Wiesendanger (11 October 1965) is a German composer, conductor and music educator. He has been the director of the Hochschule für Musik Mainz since 2006.  Wiesendanger was born in Frankfurt am Main, West Germany. He studied at the Hochschule für Musik Mainz with Heinrich Schenker and Wolfgang Rihm, graduating in 1988. He also studied conducting with Nikolaus Harnoncourt.  Wiesendanger has worked as a freelance composer since 1988, and has written for theatre, orchestra, choir and solo instruments. His works have been performed in Europe, the USA and Japan. He has won several awards for his music, including the Deutscher Schallplattenpreis and the Grawemeyer Award from the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.  Wiesendanger is currently the director of the Hochschule für Musik Mainz.
