"Summer Lovin'" is a single by house music producer and DJ, Steve Angello. The song is released as the fourth single from his debut album, Wild Youth. It was released on 24 May 2014 through Size Records.  The song was written by Angello and produced by him and longtime collaborator Sebastien Leger. The song is a house track that features guest vocals from Christina Novelli and vocals from M.I.A.   Angello told "Mixmag" that "Summer Lovin'" is about "the feeling you get when you're free, out there in the sun, dancing all night."   The music video for the song was directed by Emil Nava and filmed in Miami Beach, Florida. It premiered on 24 May 2014 on Steve Angello's YouTube channel.
