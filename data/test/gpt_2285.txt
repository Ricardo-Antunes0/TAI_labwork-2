Wipas Raksakulthai (; born c. 1973) is a Thai artist who specializes in paintings on silk.  Raksakulthai was born circa 1973 in the northern province of Chiang Rai. He studied at the Fine Arts Faculty of Chiang Mai University, graduating in 1996. From 1997 to 2001, he studied at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in Illinois, United States, where he received a Master of Fine Arts degree.  His paintings are often abstract and feature bright colors and striking patterns. His works have been exhibited in Thailand, China, Japan, and Europe.
