The Municipal Revenues Collection Center — (CRIM)—is a financial intelligence unit of the Ministry of Finance of the Philippines.  CRIM gathers and analyses financial information on municipalities and their component units such as local government units, Sangguniang Panlalawigan, barangays, and PSUs. It also provides support to the Philippine National Police in its investigations into financial crimes.  The creation of CRIM was part of the effort to improve governance and financial transparency in the Philippines. The agency is responsible for providing timely and accurate financial information to policy makers, the public, and the private sector.
