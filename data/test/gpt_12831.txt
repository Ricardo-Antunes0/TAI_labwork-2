Krivonogov made his debut in the Soviet Top League in 1961 for FC Dynamo Moscow. He played for FC Dynamo Moscow, FC Spartak Moscow, FC Lokomotiv Moscow, FC Krylia Sovetov Samara, FC Zenit Saint Petersburg, and FC Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk. He also played for the Soviet national team.  In the 1970s and 1980s, Krivonogov coached FC Krylia Sovetov Samara, FC Zenit Saint Petersburg, and FC Lokomotiv Moscow. He also coached the Russia national team from 1994 to 1996.
