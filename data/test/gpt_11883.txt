Neil deMause (born November 19, 1965 in Cleveland, Ohio) is an American historian of popular culture and media. He is the author of books including The Age of Show Business: How Show Business Has Shaped America, From vaudeville to TV (2007), The TV Age: The History of Television in America (2012), and The Return of Show Business: How the Entertainment Industry Is Reconstructing Itself (2016).  DeMause is also the editor of the journal Television & New Media and the editor-in-chief of the book series Popular Television. He has been a professor at New York University since 2003.
