Joker is a 1993 Indian Telugu-language comedy-drama film written and directed by S. Shankar. Produced by K. Raghavendra Rao and K. Viswanath, the film stars Rajnikanth and Soundarya in the lead roles with music composed by Ilaiyaraaja.  The film tells the story of Anand (Rajnikanth), a ruthless business tycoon who is forced to take up a new job as a joker in a circus after his company is bankrupted. Anand clashes with the circus' other performers, including Soundarya (Soundarya), and falls in love with her.
