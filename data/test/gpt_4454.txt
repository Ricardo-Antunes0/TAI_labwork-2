The Fated Sky is the title of a concept album by the American progressive metal band Skyclad. It was released on February 3, 2009 in the United States, Australia and Europe. The album was recorded in 2008 at The Mill in Richmond, Virginia with producer Kevin Shirley and was mixed by John Fryer at Audiohammer Studios in Auburn, Alabama.  The Fated Sky is the title of a concept album by the American progressive metal band Skyclad. It tells a story of a fallen empire and a chosen people who fight to restore it.
