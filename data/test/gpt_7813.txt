Charles Okechukwu Esimone (born 31 December 1970) is a retired Nigerian international footballer who played as a defender.  Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Esimone played club football for Spanish club Atlético Madrid and English club Newcastle United. He also played for the Nigeria national team, appearing in the 1998 World Cup and the 2000 Africa Cup of Nations.  Esimone retired from professional football in 2003 after playing 268 league matches and scoring five goals for Atlético Madrid. He later became a football coach, working with both Atlético Madrid and Newcastle United's youth teams.
