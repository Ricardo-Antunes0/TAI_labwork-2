{{Infobox film | name               | image = | caption =  Life (2015 film) is a Canadian science fiction drama film directed by Daniel Espinosa and written by R.J. MacReady and Keith R. Acker. The film stars Ryan Reynolds, Rebecca Ferguson, Jake Gyllenhaal, and Olga Kurylenko.  The film follows a team of explorers who travel to the outer space in search of a new planet that may be able to support life. However, upon arrival they find that the planet is already inhabited by a race of hostile aliens.
