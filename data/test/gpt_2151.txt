Hotel Marselis or Marselis Hotel - Aarhus is a 5-star hotel in the heart of the city. The hotel is set in an 18th century building on the banks of the River Aarhus and offers a unique and modern atmosphere with a distinctive Scandinavian design.   The hotel has been refurbished in May 2007 and now offers guests 525 rooms, including 44 suites with a range of amenities to ensure a relaxing stay. There is also a spa, a gym, two restaurants, a bar and a pool. The hotel is close to the city centre and its attractions including the Royal Theatre, Marselisborg Castle and Aarhus University.
