J. O. Tobin (March 28, 1974 â€“ November 3, 2008) was an American economist, and the Robert W. Woodruff Professor of Economics at Yale University.  Tobin was born in 1974 in Boston, Massachusetts. He received his B.A. from Columbia University in 1996, and his M.A. and Ph.D. from Yale University in 2000 and 2004, respectively.  Tobin was a member of the National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) since 2004 and a fellow of the Econometric Society since 2006. Prior to joining the NBER, he was an assistant professor of economics at Brown University from 2002 to 2004, and an assistant professor of economics at Harvard University from 1999 to 2002.  Tobin died on November 3, 2008 after a long battle with cancer.
