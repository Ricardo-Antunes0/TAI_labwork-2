Margazhi 16 is a 2011 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film directed by P. Vasu and written by M. Saravanan. The film stars Arjun Sarja, Divya Bharathi, and Kushboo in lead roles with Nassar and Sathyaraj in supporting roles. The film was produced by Saravanan under the banner of his production house, Sathya Creations.  The film is about three friends—Arjun, Divya, and Kushboo—who decide to get married on the same day because their friends have all gotten married before them. They each believe that their marriage will fix their broken relationships with their respective friends. The three marriages prove to be disastrous in different ways, but they eventually learn to appreciate each other more than they ever thought possible.   The film was released on 22 September 2011 to mixed reviews from critics. However, it became a commercial success, grossing over ₹200 crore (US$30 million) at the box office.
