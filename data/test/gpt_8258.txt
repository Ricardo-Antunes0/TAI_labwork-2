Kosher Love is a 2017 Canadian documentary film directed by Eli Holzman. It follows the love story of two Orthodox Jewish young adults, Raffi and Miriam, as they navigate the ups and downs of a relationship while living according to halakha, or Jewish law.  Kosher Love tells the story of two Orthodox Jewish young adults, Raffi and Miriam, who are in love despite living under halakha, or Jewish law. Halakha dictates that Jews must marry within their religious community and abstain from sexual relations outside of marriage. As such, Raffi and Miriam must navigate the ups and downs of a relationship while living according to strict guidelines. Despite the challenges, Raffi and Miriam are determined to find happiness together.
