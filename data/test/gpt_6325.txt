On April 18, 2015, Blitzen Trapper released their eighth studio album, "Live Harvest." The album is a compilation of live recordings from throughout their career, as well as some new material.  Blitzen Trapper has been one of the most popular and influential Americana bands of the last two decades. Formed in 2001 in Portland, Oregon, the group consists of vocalist, guitarist, and songwriter Dave Currie, bassist and backing vocalist Jay Farrar, and drummer Eric Slick. They are known for their anthemic folk-rock sound, with influences including the Beatles, Bob Dylan, and the Eagles.
