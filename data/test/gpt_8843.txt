Maximillian Zorin is a fictional character and the protagonist of the novel 1984 by George Orwell. Zorin is a Russian aristocrat who is sent to England to negotiate the sale of Russian weapons to the British government.   Zorin is a fascinating and enigmatic figure. He is intelligent, articulate, and confident, but also manipulative and unscrupulous. His interactions with the English people reveal their weaknesses and vulnerabilities. Ultimately, Zorin uses these insights to his advantage, achieving his objectives in a manner that both astonishes and impresses his opponents.
