MJ Department Stores Sdn Bhd is a department store chain in Malaysia. It was founded in 1984 by the late Mohd Nazrin Mohamed and is headquartered in Metrojaya, Selangor.  Metrojaya is one of the largest shopping complexes in Malaysia, comprising a mix of high-end luxury brands and budget outlets. The complex has over 2,000,000 square feet (200,000 m2) of retail space and features a variety of restaurants, a cinema complex and an ice rink.  MJ Department Stores Sdn Bhd operates over 180 stores across Malaysia.
