The Dumbest Magnets is the third studio album by American rock band We the Kings. It was released on June 17, 2017, through Atlantic Records.  The Dumbest Magnets is the third studio album by American rock band We the Kings. The album was produced by Dave Sardy and was recorded at several studios in California. The Dumbest Magnets is a return to the band's roots, incorporating elements of rock, punk, and surf music into their sound.  The album has generally received positive reviews from critics, who praised its accessibility and the band's return to their signature sound. It debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, selling 115,000 copies in its first week. The album has been certified Gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
