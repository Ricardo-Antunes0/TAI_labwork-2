Artifact is a 2012 American documentary film directed by Aaron Scott and produced by Kerry Bishé. The film tells the story of the restoration of the world's first motion picture camera, the Lumière brothers' Cinématographe, and the people who made it happen.  The film premiered at the 2012 Toronto International Film Festival, and was released theatrically in the United States on September 18, 2012. It was nominated for the Grand Jury Prize at the 2012 Sundance Film Festival.
