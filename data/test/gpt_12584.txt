Monroe Mendelsohn Research (MMR), Inc. (and its predecessors) has been engaged in the field of Jewish genealogy since 1966. The company's primary focus has been the tracing of Jewish ancestry and family history.   MMR also offers a wide variety of genealogical services, including surname research, Jewish genealogy, Holocaust research, and family history consulting. The company maintains databases of more than one million names and addresses, as well as millions of records of births, marriages, and deaths.   The company's headquarters are in Jerusalem, Israel.
