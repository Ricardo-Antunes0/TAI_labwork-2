Dilson Domingos Funaro (October 23, 1933 — March 20, 1978) was a Brazilian professional footballer who played as a defender.  Funaro was born in São Paulo and played for São Paulo, Corinthians, Internacional and Flamengo.  Funaro died in 1978 at the age of 43 from a heart attack. He was posthumously inducted into the Brazilian Football Hall of Fame in 1991.
