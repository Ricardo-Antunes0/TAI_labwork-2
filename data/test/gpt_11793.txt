Vivian Alvarez Solon (born 30 October 1962) is a Filipino politician and the current Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Philippines, in office since May 14, 2019. She is also the representative for the 2nd District of Leyte.  Solon was born in 1962 in San Jose, Leyte to parents Lorenzo Alvarez and Mercedes Solon. She finished her elementary and high school education at San Jose Elementary School and San Jose High School respectively. She then studied at the University of the Philippines Diliman, where she earned a degree in Mass Communication.  Solon started her career as a journalist with Radio Veritas in 1984. In 1988, she joined ABS-CBN as a newsreader and correspondent. She later became head of news for ABS-CBN Regional TV Networks.  In 2001, Solon was elected as a Member of the House of Representatives representing the 2nd District of Leyte. In 2007, she was reelected without opposition.
