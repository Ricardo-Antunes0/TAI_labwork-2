Grant Ujifusa was born on January 4, 1988, in Sacramento, California. He is an American actor and voice actor.  Ujifusa started his acting career in the 2006 television series "The O.C.". He has since appeared in various television shows and movies, most notably appearing in the films "The Green Hornet" (2011), "The Wolverine" (2013), and "Captain America: The Winter Soldier" (2014). He also voiced the character of Gizmo in the 2013 animated film "Monsters University".  Ujifusa is also a voice actor, having worked on various video games including "Mass Effect 3", "Dragon Age II", and "Final Fantasy XV".
