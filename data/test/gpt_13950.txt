Escape from Atlantis is a board game for 2-4 players, designed by Antoine Bauza and published by French game company Asmodee. The game is set in the mythological city of Atlantis and its players must guide their characters through a series of challenges in order to escape before the city is destroyed.  Escape from Atlantis is a strategic game where players must work together to escape from the city of Atlantis before it is destroyed. Players must navigate their characters through a series of challenges, using their resources wisely to survive. The game can be played in either cooperative or competitive modes and is suitable for both adults and children.
