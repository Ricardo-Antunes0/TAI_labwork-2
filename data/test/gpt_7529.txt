La Loma Foods, formerly named Loma Linda Foods, is an American food company that specializes in Mexican food. It was founded in 1976 by brothers Rudy and Gilbert Loma and is headquartered in San Diego, California.  La Loma Foods produces a variety of Mexican food products, including chiles rellenos, burritos, enchiladas, tamales, and tacos. The company also produces pre-made meals and frozen foods. La Loma Foods has a distribution agreement with Walmart.
