HMAS Westralia (F95/C61) was an auxiliary cruiser of the Royal Australian Navy (RAN).  Westralia was ordered from Lithgow Co-operative Engineering Company in Sydney on 23 February 1961, as part of the RAN's auxiliary cruiser project. The ship was laid down on 10 October 1961, launched on 15 July 1962 and commissioned into the RAN on 15 November 1963.  Westralia was decommissioned on 25 March 1984 and sold for breaking on 30 September 1984.
