Roadkill Rising is a compilation box set of live recordings by the American heavy metal band Megadeth. It was released on November 4, 2016, through Capitol Records. The set consists of three discs and features performances from shows in support of their latest album Dystopia (2015), as well as material from their earlier albums Countdown to Extinction (1988) and Rust in Peace (1990).  The box set was recorded at the Hollywood Palladium in Los Angeles on September 30, 2016, and October 1, 2016, and the Hammersmith Apollo in London on June 10, 2017. It was produced by Kevin Elson and mixed by Andy Sneap.
