Radio Bergeijk is a Dutch satirical radio station. It broadcasts on FM in the Netherlands, and online. The station was founded in 1993 by Jeroen van der Velde, and is based in Bergeijk.  Radio Bergeijk is known for its satirical programmes, which often criticise Dutch politicians, the media and other institutions. The station has also been known for its music programming, which includes alternative and heavy metal music.  In 2006, Radio Bergeijk received a Netherlands Media Award for Best Radio Programme.
