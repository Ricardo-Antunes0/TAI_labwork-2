Tales from the Crypt Presents: Bordello of Blood   The Bordello of Blood was a notorious red-light district located in the Tenderloin district of San Francisco. Opened in 1871, it was one of the largest and most notorious bordellos in North America. The brothel was raided numerous times by law enforcement, but it continued to operate until its closure in 1934. Today, the building which housed the bordello is used as a restaurant and nightclub.
