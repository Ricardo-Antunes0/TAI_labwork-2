Division 60, Somali National Army (Somali: Qeybta Dhu60), is one of the nine military divisions of the Somali National Army. The division is headquartered in the town of Baidoa, in the central region of Somalia.  The division was established in 1991, shortly after the start of the civil war in Somalia. It is one of the most highly equipped and trained divisions in the Somali National Army. The division is involved in a number of active combat operations against the Islamist insurgent group al-Shabaab.  The division has a significant presence in the central and southern regions of Somalia. In February 2012, it captured Mogadishu from al-Shabaab control.
