USC Tower, formerly AT&T Center, SBC Tower, and South Park Tower, is a skyscraper in downtown San Diego, California. It is the tallest building in San Diego and the seventh tallest building in California. The tower was completed in 1991 and has been the headquarters of South Park Digital Media since it was built.  The South Park Center is also a popular tourist destination, as it is located in the heart of downtown San Diego. The tower has a number of high-end restaurants and stores, as well as a number of large viewing windows that offer great views of the city.
