Nandeeswarudu is a 2012 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by K. S. Ravikumar. The film features Venkatesh and Trisha Krishnan in the lead roles.  Nandeeswarudu is set against the backdrop of the Andhra Pradesh forest department and its fight against illegal logging. The story revolves around two policemen, Nandhakumar (Venkatesh) and Gopi (Trisha Krishnan), who are investigating the murder of a forest officer. They are aided by a journalist, Radha (Rajeshwari), and her photographer friend, Ravi (Prabhu). Nandhakumar falls in love with Radha, but she is already married to Ravi. Gopi starts to develop feelings for Nandhakumar, but he is already married to his childhood friend Geetha (Lakshmi). The film culminates with a series of daring raids on timber smuggling camps by Gopi, Nandhakumar and their team.
