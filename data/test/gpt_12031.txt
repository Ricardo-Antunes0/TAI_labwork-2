Mason Alan Dinehart (born April 30, 1936) is a retired American professional basketball player who played in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Chicago Bulls, Philadelphia 76ers and Detroit Pistons. He also played in France for Champagne Chalons and Grenoble.  Dinehart played college basketball at the University of Oregon, where he was a two-time All-Pacific Coast Conference (PCC) selection. He was drafted in the second round of the 1958 NBA draft by the Chicago Bulls and played with them for six seasons. He then moved to the Philadelphia 76ers, where he spent four seasons before joining the Detroit Pistons in January 1971. In his NBA career, Dinehart averaged 8.1 points and 3.2 rebounds per game.
