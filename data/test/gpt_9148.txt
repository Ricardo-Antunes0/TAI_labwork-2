Ana Oncina (born 1989) is a Spanish-born Argentine artist who lives and works in Berlin.  Ana Oncina was born in 1989 in Barcelona, Spain. She studied at the Escuela Superior de Arte San Fernando in Madrid and at the National Academy of Fine Arts of Argentina in Buenos Aires. Her art practice focuses on video installation and performance, exploring notions of temporality, space and memory. She has exhibited her work at institutions including the Centre Pompidou in Paris, the Museum of Contemporary Art in Buenos Aires, and the Whitechapel Gallery in London. Oncina was awarded the prestigious Guggenheim Fellowship in 2016.
