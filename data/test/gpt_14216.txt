dell'Arte Opera Ensemble is an opera company in the United States. The company was founded in 1984 by music director and conductor James Levine, who served as its artistic director until his death in 2017.  The company is based in New York City and performs at the Metropolitan Opera House and the Royal Opera House in London. It has also toured throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America. Dell'Arte has released six recordings on Deutsche Grammophon: "James Levine: A Life in Music" (1995), "Dell'Arte presents Verdi" (1996), "Dell'Arte presents Mozart" (1998), "Dell'Arte presents Rossini" (2000), "Dell'Arte presents Donizetti" (2002), and "James Levine: The Complete Symphonies" (2008).
