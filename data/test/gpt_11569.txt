Keenan Kai-James Cornelius is an American martial artist, film and television actor and stuntman. He has appeared in films such as Blade II, The Punisher, and Doomsday. He is perhaps best known for his role as John Diggle on the CW television series Arrow.  Keenan was born in Palmdale, California, on October 12th, 1982. He began training in martial arts at the age of six, and had his first martial Arts movie role as a child actor in the 1989 film Ghost Dog: The Way of the Samurai. He also appeared in such films as Jackie Chan's Who Am I? and The Crow: City of Angels. As an adult, Keenan has appeared in such films as Blade II (2002), The Punisher (2004), and Doomsday (2008).  He has also appeared in television series such as CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Alias, Nikita, Deadwood, Stargate SG-1, and Arrow. In 2011 he co-starred with Dominic West in the action thriller film Haywire. Keenan played John Diggle on the CW television series Arrow from its debut in 2012 to 2017.
