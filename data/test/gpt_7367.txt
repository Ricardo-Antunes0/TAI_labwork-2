Elahi Bux Soomro or Illahi Bukhsh Soomro (Urdu: الله بكثر ابو سعود‎) (born 21 August 1952) is a Pakistani politician who has been the Prime Minister of Pakistan twice, serving in office from 2008 to 2013 and from 2018 to present.  Soomro was born in 1952 in the town of Soomro in the Sukkur District of Sindh. He was educated at the University of Karachi, earning a bachelor's degree in law. He also holds a master's degree in business administration from the University of London.  Soomro worked as a lawyer before becoming involved in politics. He served as the Secretary General of the Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) from 1992 to 1997 and was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a PPP candidate from Sukkur in 1997. He subsequently served as Minister for Law and Justice from 1997 to 2002 and again from 2008 to 2013, becoming the Prime Minister for the first time on 18 September 2008. He was re-elected as Prime Minister on 19 May 2013, and remained in office until his second term ended on 18 May 2018. On 11 August 2018, he was elected as Prime Minister for a third time, defeating Nawaz Sharif in a vote by the National Assembly.
