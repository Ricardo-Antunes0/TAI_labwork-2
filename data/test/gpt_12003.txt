David "Elsewhere" Bernal (born August 2, 1979) is an American actor, director, and screenwriter. He was born in San Francisco, California.  Bernal first gained recognition for his roles in the films The Motorcycle Diaries (2004), Milk (2008), and The Last King of Scotland (2006). His performance as Che Guevara in the biographical film Vicky Cristina Barcelona (2008) earned him critical acclaim and an Academy Award nomination. Bernal subsequently appeared in the films The Place Beyond the Pines (2013), Sicario (2015), and The Book Thief (2013). In 2017, he starred in the drama film The Florida Project.
