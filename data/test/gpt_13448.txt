Bloodwork, also known as Bloodworx (US), and Bloodwork 2 (UK), is a 2007 American action film written and directed by Kevin Williamson, and starring Sylvester Stallone, Wesley Snipes, and Jason Statham.  The film is about three scientists who are hired to create a serum that will make people immune to blood-borne diseases. In the course of their work, they come up with a serum that also makes them super-strong and able to regenerate any missing body parts. The serum is tested on volunteers, with disastrous results. The scientists are then forced to use the serum on themselves in order to protect their loved ones.
