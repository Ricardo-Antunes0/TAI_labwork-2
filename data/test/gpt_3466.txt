The wildlife of Singapore is surprisingly diverse and includes a wide variety of animals and plants. With a population of about 5.5 million people, Singapore has plenty of space to allow for a wide variety of animals and plants to live. Singapore is home to over 600 species of birds, 350 species of mammals, and over 1,000 species of plants. The country's diverse geography and climate also allows for a wide variety of animal life, with many animals living in rainforest, mangrove, and coastal habitats.
