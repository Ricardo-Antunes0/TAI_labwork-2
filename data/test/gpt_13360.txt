The Shirley Foundation, based in the United States, was founded in 1971 by Shirley Chisholm, the first African American woman elected to the United States Congress. The Shirley Foundation is a charitable organization whose aim is to promote democratic values and civil rights. The Foundation has made grants totaling more than $130 million.
