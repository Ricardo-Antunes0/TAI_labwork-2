State of Decay is a series of zombie-apocalypse video games developed by Undead Labs and published by Microsoft Studios. The first game, State of Decay, was released in 2013 for Xbox 360, Xbox One, and Windows 8. The second game, State of Decay 2, was released in May 2016 for Xbox One and Windows 10.   The first game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where the player character is a survivor trying to build a safe haven from the zombie apocalypse. The player must scavenge for supplies, make alliances with other survivors, and fight off the zombies to protect their base. The second game is set several years after the events of the first game, and focuses on rebuilding society after the zombie apocalypse.
