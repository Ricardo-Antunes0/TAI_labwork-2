, a.k.a. The Pitfall and Kashi To Pitfall, is a 1962 American adventure film directed by Don Taylor and starring Richard Crenna, Angie Dickinson, and Jacques Serna.  The film is based on the novel of the same name by James Rollins.  The film tells the story of two mountaineers, John Thornton (Richard Crenna) and his partner-in-crime, Maurice Belloni (Jacques Serna), who are attempting to cross the Andes in search of new climbing routes. After a series of misadventures, they fall into an elaborate trap set by the evil Spanish conquistador Pizarro (Angie Dickinson).
