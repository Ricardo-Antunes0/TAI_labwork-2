Steven Delano Smith (born March 31, 1969) is an American former professional basketball player. He played at the power forward and center positions for 12 seasons in the National Basketball Association (NBA) with the Charlotte Hornets, Indiana Pacers, San Antonio Spurs, and Memphis Grizzlies. Smith won an NBA Championship with the Spurs in 2003.  Smith was born and raised in North Carolina. He attended college at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he was a three-time All-American and won the National Player of the Year Award in 1992. He was selected first overall by the Hornets in the 1992 NBA draft. Smith is also a two-time NBA All-Defensive Team selection, and was named to the NBA 75th Anniversary team in 1996.  Smith has been married to Dayna Smith since 1997. They have two children: son Steven Jr. (born 1998) and daughter Sydney (born 2002).
