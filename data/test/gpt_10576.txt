Taston is a hamlet in Spelsbury civil parish, in the English county of Wiltshire.  Taston is a small hamlet in the parish of Spelsbury, Wiltshire. It is located 2 miles east of Cirencester and 7 miles north-west of Chippenham. The nearest town is Swindon. The hamlet has a population of around 100.   The name Tatnall is recorded in the Domesday Book as belonging to the Wykeham family. Tatnall Hall, which was built in the 16th century, is now a private residence. The hamlet has a pub, the White Hart.
