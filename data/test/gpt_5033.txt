Fujisankei Communications International, Inc. (FCI) is the largest and most diversified telecommunications company in Japan. The company provides services including voice, data, video, and messaging through its own networks and those of its affiliated providers. FCI also provides managed services, such as voice and data hosting and cloud computing, to businesses and individual consumers. In addition, the company operates a number of retail stores in Japan.  Fujisankei Communications International was founded in 1976 as a cable television operator. The company's current operations include providing telecommunications services through its own networks and those of its affiliated providers; operating retail stores; providing managed services, such as voice and data hosting and cloud computing; and investing in various technologies. The company has operations in Japan, China, Taiwan, South Korea, Thailand, the Philippines, Indonesia, Australia, New Zealand, the United States of America, and Europe.   As of March 31, 2019, Fujisankei Communications International had a total consolidated revenue of US$32.1 billion. The company's subsidiaries have a combined workforce of about 343,000 people.
