Rammstein Tour 2016 is the unofficial name for the worldwide concert tour by German heavy metal band Rammstein. The tour started on January 20, 2016 in Lisbon, Portugal and is scheduled to end on December 3, 2016 in Mexico City, Mexico. The tour consists of sixty-eight concerts in six continents.  The tour grossed over $211 million from ticket sales and merchandise sales. Rammstein has sold out all their concerts on the tour.  The official announcement of the Rammstein Tour 2016 was made by the band's website on October 10, 2015. The first leg of the tour started on January 20, 2016 in Lisbon, Portugal and ended on March 30, 2016 in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The second leg of the tour started on April 10, 2016 in Mexico City, Mexico and ended on May 12, 2016 in Santiago, Chile.  The third leg of the tour started on June 8, 2016 in Berlin, Germany and ended on July 14, 2016 in Rotterdam, Netherlands. The fourth leg of the tour started on August 4, 2016 in Vienna, Austria and ended on September 8, 2016 in Paris, France. The fifth leg of the tour started on October 2, 2016 in Milan, Italy and ended on November 5th 2016 in Barcelona, Spain. The sixth leg of the tour started on December 3rd 2016 in Mexico City and will end on January 19th 2017 in Los Angeles.
