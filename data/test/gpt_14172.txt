Françoise Blime (born Françoise Dutertre July 11, 1967) is a French actress and model.  Born in Reims, France, Blime began her modeling career in 1985 and quickly established herself as a leading fashion model. She made her acting debut in the 1988 film Les Visiteurs. She has since appeared in a number of acclaimed French films, including the drama The Horseman of the Apocalypse (1999), the thriller The Book Thief (2009), and the comedy-drama Love (2015). Blime has also appeared in several international films, including The Pianist (2002) and The Messenger (2009).  Blime was married to French actor Bruno Gollnisch from 1992 to 1999. They have one child.
