Produce Pandas (; fully stylized as  PRODUCE PANDAS) is a Japanese rock band formed in Tokyo in 2007. The band consists of vocalist and guitarist Ayaka, drummer Hirotaka, bassist and keyboard player Rio, and guitarist Takuya.  Produce Pandas are known for their energetic live performances and their eclectic mix of pop, rock, and electronica. The band has released three studio albums, "Heartfelt Scenario" (2010), "Future Shock" (2013), and "The Best of Produce Pandas" (2017).
