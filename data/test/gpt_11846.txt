The name Ragnhild Sigurdsdotter may refer to:  1) A princess of Norway, daughter of Sigurd Jarl and Ragnhild Estridsson, and sister of Harald Hårfagre. She married Haakon I of Norway.  2) A historical Viking queen who is primarily known for her role in the Battle of Hjörvatn where she led a successful charge against the Danes, ultimately resulting in the death of their king, Guthrum.  3) A fictional character from the video game "Dragon Age II" who is the sister of Varric Tethras and one of the companions of Hawke.
