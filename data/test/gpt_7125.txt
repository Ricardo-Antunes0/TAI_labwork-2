Bundeli Utsav (Bundeli Festival) is a cultural festival that is celebrated in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. It is one of the most important festivals in Bundelkhand.     The festival is held every year in the month of March or April. It is a week-long event that features a variety of cultural activities. The main attraction of the festival is the traditional wrestling competition called "Bundeli Maha Kheli".     The festival has been celebrated for centuries and it is believed that it was instituted by the Bundelkhand king Shambhu Prasad Singh in memory of his father, Rani Padmini.     The festival is also known as "Bundeli Bihu" or "Bundeli Puja".
