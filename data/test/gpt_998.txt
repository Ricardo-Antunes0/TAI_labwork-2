Juliano Mer-Khamis (; ; born Juliano Khamis; 20 June 1976) is an Italian-born Egyptian actor and singer.  Mer-Khamis was born in Rome, to Egyptian parents. He began his acting career in 1994, appearing in several Egyptian soap operas. He later moved to Italy, where he became known for his roles in Italian television series. He has also appeared in international films, including "The Pianist" and "The Brothers Grimm". Mer-Khamis has received critical acclaim for his work in both television and film.
