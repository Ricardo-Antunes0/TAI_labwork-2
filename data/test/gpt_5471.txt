He was considered one of the biggest actors in the Indian film industry, and is best known for his roles in commercially successful films such as Bhoomi and Singham. Devgan also had a successful career in television, appearing in popular shows such as Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi and Yeh Jo Hai Mujhse Dosti Karo.  Devgan was born on 25 May 1969 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India to a Marathi family. He had a Telugu descent from his mother. Devgan's father worked as an engineer for a telephone company and his mother was a homemaker. Devgan graduated from St. Xavier's College, Mumbai in 1987, and then completed his training at the National School of Drama in New Delhi.  Devgan made his film debut with the role of Arjun in the 1988 film Saajan which was directed by Rajkumar Santoshi. He subsequently appeared in critically acclaimed films such as Bhoomi (1991), Singham (2011), Paa (1992), Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai (2003), Dabangg (2010), Kabhi Alvida Naa Kehna (2006), and Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (2012).
