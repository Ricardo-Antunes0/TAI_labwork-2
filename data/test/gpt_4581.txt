The Bunny Museum is a museum in Hamilton, New Zealand, that displays historical and contemporary bunnies. The museum was founded in 2007 by Judy and Mike Fitzgerald, who also run the Bunny Museum Shop on Victoria Street. The collection spans from the early 1800s to the present day, with a focus on New Zealand bunnies. There are exhibits on breeding and raising bunnies, as well as a collection of rabbit-related memorabilia. The Bunny Museum is open Monday to Saturday 9am to 5pm and Sunday 11am to 4pm.
