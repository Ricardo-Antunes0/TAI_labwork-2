Mask-A-Raid is a 1931 Fleischer Studios Talkartoon animated short film, directed by Dave Fleischer. The film features Betty Boop as a vengeful avenger who masquerades as a scary monster to frighten her would-be tormentors.  Betty Boop stars in this funny, scary short film about a vengeful avenger who masquerades as a scary monster to frighten her would-be tormentors. Betty is dressed up like a fearsome mask-wearing monster and goes on a rampage, frightening the heck out of her would-be tormentors. This hilarious short film is sure to delight audiences of all ages.
