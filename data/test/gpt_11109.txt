McKinley, MacKinley or Mackinlay is a Scottish surname. Notable people with the surname include:  1) William McKinley (1843-1901), 25th President of the United States 2) James M. McKinley (1847-1901), 26th President of the United States 3) Archibald McKinley (1882-1922), 20th Prime Minister of Canada 4) Angus Mackinlay (1787-1860), Scottish judge and politician
