Macabre Eternal is the fifth album by American death metal band Cannibal Corpse. It was released on October 11, 2007 by Metal Blade Records.  The album is a return to the band's classic death metal sound, and is described as containing "fast, brutal, and brutally catchy" songs. The lyrics are about death, violence, and the macabre.  The first single from the album, "Evisceration Plague", was released on September 24, 2007. The song has been described as "a brutal track that shows off the band's trademark brutality". The second single, "Call of The Hungry Dead", was released on November 18, 2007.
