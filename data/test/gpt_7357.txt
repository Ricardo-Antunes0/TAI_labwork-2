Soultans is an all-male R&B band notable for their energetic live performances and catchy pop-oriented songs.  Formed in 2003 in Los Angeles, Soultans is an all-male R&B band that has become well known for their energetic live performances and catchy pop-oriented songs. The band's sound is a mix of soul, funk, and disco with a splash of hip-hop. Soultans' music has been featured in several television shows and commercials, and they have toured extensively throughout the United States and Europe.
