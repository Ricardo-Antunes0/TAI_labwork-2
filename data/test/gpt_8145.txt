Anna Daučíková (born August 18, 1950) is a Czech actress. She has appeared in more than sixty films and television shows since 1976. Her work has notably included roles in films such as The Vanishing (1988), The Unbearable Lightness of Being (1987), and Amadeus (1984).  Daučíková was born in Prague, Czechoslovakia. She started her acting career in 1976 with a role in the film Kolya. She has since appeared in more than sixty films and television shows, most notably roles in The Vanishing (1988), The Unbearable Lightness of Being (1987), Amadeus (1984), and Černý Petr (1994). She also directed a short film, Vitál (1998).
