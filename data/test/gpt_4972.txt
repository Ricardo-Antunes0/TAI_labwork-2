Wikked Lil' Grrrls is Canadian singer Esthero's third studio album, released on June 24, 2003 through East West Records. The album is produced by The Neptunes and features guest appearances from soul singer India Arie, rapper Ludacris, and producers The Neptunes and Pharrell.  The album has been generally well-received by music critics, who praised Esthero's unique vocal style and the production of the album. It has reached #5 on the Canadian Albums Chart and has been certified Platinum by the Canadian Recording Industry Association (CRIA).
