Wings University Tour was a UK concert tour by the British pop band Wings. The tour began in February 1978 and ended in December 1978.  Wings University Tour was the first tour by the British pop band Wings since the 1973 tours which were followed by the 1974 tour. The 1978 tour consisted of 23 concerts in England and Scotland. The largest concert was at Wembley Stadium where Wings played to a crowd of over 100,000 people.
