Jutok Kaneko (金子寿徳) (1958 – 24 January 2009) was a Japanese author, journalist and television personality.  Kaneko was born in 1958 in the city of Osaka. After graduating from high school, she worked for several newspapers before becoming a freelance writer in the early 1990s. In 1998 she started working for public broadcaster NHK, becoming a news anchor and a regular panelist on talk shows. She also wrote columns for various magazines and wrote several books. Jutok Kaneko died of brain cancer on 24 January 2009 at the age of 53.
