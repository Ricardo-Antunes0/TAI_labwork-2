Stephen George "Steve" Pratt (born 15 October 1971) is an English former professional footballer who played as a striker.  Pratt began his career as a trainee at Manchester United, but failed to make a first-team appearance. After being released by United, Pratt signed for Colchester United in 1995. He spent two seasons at Colchester, before moving to Norwich City in 1998. Pratt spent four seasons with Norwich, helping the club to promotion to the Premier League in 2000. He then moved to Portsmouth, where he spent three seasons. Pratt then played for spells at Shrewsbury Town and Luton Town before retiring in 2006.  Pratt has also worked as a television pundit and referee.
