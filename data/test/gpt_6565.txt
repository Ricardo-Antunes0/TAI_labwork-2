Paribar  is a 2004 Bengali  Indian suspense thriller film directed by Soumitra Chatterjee. The film stars Rituparno Ghosh, Supriya Pathak and Soumitra Chatterjee in the lead roles.  The story revolves around a group of people who are trapped in a hotel room, with no way out, as the building is set on fire. They have to use their wits to survive.
