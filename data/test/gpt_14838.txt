Pricing Partners, founded in 2005, is a French technology company specializing in the development of pricing solutions for the pharmaceutical, health care and life sciences industries.  Pricing Partners was founded in 2005 by a team of entrepreneurs with a shared passion for technology and business. Our team has over 25 years of experience in the pharmaceutical, health care and life sciences industries, and our innovative pricing solutions have helped some of the world's leading companies achieve cost savings and improved profitability.  Our product line includes pricing software, consulting services, and training materials. We offer a comprehensive suite of solutions that help our customers save money on drug development costs, negotiate better drug prices, and more efficiently manage their pricing programs.  We are headquartered in France and have offices in the United States, Germany, Spain, Italy, Belgium, Netherlands, Switzerland, Austria and Japan. We are privately held and our shares are traded on the Euronext Paris exchange (PNPP).
