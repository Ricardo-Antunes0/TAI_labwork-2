The Aurora Plastics Corporation introduced the A/FX line of injection molded plastic parts in 1978. The A/FX line offered a variety of new features not found on other injection molded products at the time, including:  -An integrated floating rib design that eliminated the need for a separate rib mold -A "floating" skirt design that improved part clarity and reduced part distortion -An "A" frame design that improved part strength  The Aurora A/FX line remains one of the most popular injection molded plastic parts lines today.
