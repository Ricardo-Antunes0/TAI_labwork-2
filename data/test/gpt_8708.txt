Van Thai Tran (; born October 19, 1978) is an American football coach and former player who is the current head coach of the Jacksonville Sharks of the American Arena Football League (AFL).  Tran played college football at California. He was selected in the third round of the 2000 NFL Draft by the San Francisco 49ers. He played for the 49ers, Tampa Bay Buccaneers, and Cleveland Browns.  Tran became a head coach in 2013, when he was hired as the offensive coordinator/quarterback coach for the Arizona Rattlers of IFL. In 2016, he was named head coach of the Sharks.
