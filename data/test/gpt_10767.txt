The series was created by Kenichi Matsuyama and directed by Akiyuki Shinbo, and aired on TV Tokyo from October 6, 2014 to March 30, 2015.  The story follows Natsuki, a high school girl who discovers that she has the ability to transform into a dragon. With this power, she sets out to fight evil and protect the people she cares about.
