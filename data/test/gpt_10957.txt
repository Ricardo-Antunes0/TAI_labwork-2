Anjaana (English: Unknown, Hindi: अन्जाना, Urdu: انجانا) is a 2003 Indian Hindi-language mystery thriller film written and directed by Priyadarshan. Produced by Aditya Chopra and his mother, Uma Deo, the film stars Ajay Devgn, Aishwarya Rai, Rani Mukherjee and Sanjay Kapoor in pivotal roles.  The film is about a detective (Devgn) investigating the murder of a woman in Mumbai. He is assisted by his assistant (Rai), who is also in love with him. The investigation leads them to the city's underworld and reveals a conspiracy.
