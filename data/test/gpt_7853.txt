It was announced during the PlayStation Experience event in December 2017 and is in development by FromSoftware, the developers of "Bloodborne" and "Soulsborne".  "Ikenie no Yoru" is a horror adventure game set in a dark world where the players explore an abandoned mansion. The player takes control of a female protagonist who must explore the mansion to find a way out before it collapses.  The game is based on the Japanese horror manga series "Ikenie no Yoru" by Tsukasa Fushimi. The manga has been adapted into an anime film which was released in Japan on July 29, 2018.
