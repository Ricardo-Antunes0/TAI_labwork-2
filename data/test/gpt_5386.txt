Baatarsuren Togtokhbayar (; born January 18, 1971), better known by his stage name Baatarsuren Shuudertsetseg (), is a Mongolian musician and singer. He is well known for his work in the traditional Mongolian music genre khoomi.  Baatarsuren Shuudertsetseg was born on January 18, 1971, in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. He started his musical career in 1990 as a member of the folk ensemble Khoomiin Tsagaan. In 1996, he formed the group Baatarsuren with fellow singers Bayarjargal and Gombaagiin Ganbold. The group became well known for its traditional Mongolian music style khoomi. In 2010, Baatarsuren released his solo album "Khoomiin Tobchiin".
