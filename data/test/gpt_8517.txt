Byrnes is a surname. Notable people with the surname include:  1. John Byrnes (born 1928), American politician who served as the 31st President of the United States from January 1961 to January 1969 and as the 36th Vice President of the United States from January 1961 to January 1973 2. James Byrnes (1878–1952), American lawyer, diplomat, and U.S. Senator from South Carolina, who was a major figure in U.S. foreign policy during the early part of the Cold War 3. Richard Byrnes (1868–1938), Irish-American businessman, who was one of the founders of American Express 4. F. Richard Byrnes (1892–1964), American military officer and diplomat who was General Counsel of the Joint Chiefs of Staff during World War II
