Vaazhkai Padagu  is a 1965 Indian Tamil-language film, directed by K. Balachander. The film stars Sivaji Ganesan, Jayalalithaa and T. R. Ramachandran in lead roles.  The film's story is set in the backdrop of the 1962 Indian independence movement. The film's plot centres on a rivalry between two leading freedom fighters, one of whom is murdered. Jayalalithaa plays the role of the murdered woman's sister.  The film was a commercial success, and won accolades from critics. It has been cited as an important work in Tamil cinema, and is credited with popularising the Kollywood genre.
