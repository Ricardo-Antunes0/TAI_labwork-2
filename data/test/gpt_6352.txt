The phrase Defence Force(s) (or Defense Force(s) in some cases) can refer to many different types of military organizations, including but not limited to:  -A national military force or armed forces of a nation -A military force or armed forces of a particular state or region -An organization tasked with the responsibility of defending a particular territory, region, or nation -A paramilitary organization that is sworn to protect a specific area or institution  The term "defence force" can also be used in a more generic sense to describe all military organizations, regardless of their specific designation.
