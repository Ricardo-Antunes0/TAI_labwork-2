The Ranjit Sagar Dam, also known as the Koshi Sagar Dam, is an arch dam on the Koshi River in India. It is located in the state of Chhattisgarh. The primary purpose of the dam is hydroelectric power generation and it supports a large irrigation scheme. Construction began in 1980 and it was completed in 1985.
