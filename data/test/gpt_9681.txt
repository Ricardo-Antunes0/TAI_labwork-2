WaHu Student Apartments (also known simply as WaHu), is a residential campus for students attending the University of Hawaii at Manoa. It is located in northeast Honolulu, on the eastern edge ofahu Heights, adjacent to Waikiki.  WaHu was originally developed as a university-owned complex, but it now operates as an apartment complex managed by a private company, WaHu Property Management Corporation. The complex contains 1,113 apartments and was built in 1959. WaHu is one of the oldest complexes on the UH Manoa campus.   As of fall 2010, WaHu was home to 2,090 undergraduate and graduate students.
