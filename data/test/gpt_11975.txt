Nawaiwaqt  is an Urdu daily newspaper published from Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan.  Nawaiwaqt is one of the oldest Urdu newspapers in Pakistan. It was first published on 15 December 1875. It is one of the most popular Urdu newspapers in Pakistan and it has a circulation of around 150,000 copies. Nawaiwaqt is currently owned by the Jang Group.
