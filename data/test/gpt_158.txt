Kevin J. Boyle (born February 7, 1980) is an American writer, filmmaker, and journalist.  Boyle was born in Boston, Massachusetts. He has written for "The New York Times", "The Guardian", "Slate", and other publications. He is the creator and host of the podcast "This American Life". In 2018, he was a finalist for the Pulitzer Prize in Feature Writing.  Boyle's films have screened at the Sundance Film Festival, the Toronto International Film Festival, and the London Film Festival. He is also a documentary filmmaker. His first documentary, "The Interrupters", about community activists working to stop gun violence, was released in September 2018 to critical acclaim.
