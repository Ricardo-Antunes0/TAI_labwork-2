Sanheyuan (Chinese: ; pinyin: sānhéyuàn; Wade–Giles: san1-ho2-yüan4) is a county-level city in the northwest of Sichuan province, China. It is under the administration of the prefecture-level city of Chengdu. The population was 5,245,000 as of 2010.  Sanheyuan has a long history dating back to Neolithic times, and has been an important center for trade and commerce in the area. In recent years, it has become known for its agriculture, notably its production of rice.
