is a Japanese shōjo manga series by Ume Aoki. It was serialized in Kodansha's Magazine Weekly Shōnen Sirius from February 2002 to November 2006, with a total of thirteen volumes released. The manga has been licensed in North America by Tokyopop, who published the first nine volumes before closing their doors in 2009. The series was then licensed in the United Kingdom by Yen Press, and the series ended with volume 10 on September 25, 2016.  The story follows the lives of high school students Haruhi Suzumiya and her friends. Haruhi is a genius who can do anything she sets her mind to, but she is bored with her life and has no friends. One day, she falls through a hole in the ground into a world where humans are replaced by aliens called espers. Haruhi quickly becomes friends with two of the espers, Nyamo and Koizumi, and begins to investigate this strange world. Along the way, she makes more friends and solves various mysteries.
