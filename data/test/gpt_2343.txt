Oleg Mikhaylovich Gazmanov (; born 22 July 1982) is a Russian professional football player. He plays for FC Krasnodar Krai.  Gazmanov was born in Khabarovsk Krai, Russia. Gazmanov started his career with FC Krylia Sovetov Samara, making his debut in the Russian Second Division in 2000. He moved to FC Terek Grozny in 2001 and helped the club win the Russian Premier League in 2003. Gazmanov then spent two seasons with FC Rubin Kazan, before moving to FC Lokomotiv Moscow in 2006. He won the Russian Cup with Lokomotiv in 2007 and was named Russian Footballer of the Year that season. He then spent two seasons with FC Dynamo Moscow, helping them win the Russian Premier League and Russian Cup in 2009. In 2011, Gazmanov joined FC Anzhi Makhachkala. He helped Anzhi win the Russian Cup in 2013 and 2014 and reach the UEFA Europa League final in 2015, where they lost to Sevilla. Gazmanov signed for FC Krasnodar Krai on 1 July 2016.  Gazmanov made his international debut for Russia on 11 November 2003 against Estonia and has earned 20 caps, scoring 3 goals.
