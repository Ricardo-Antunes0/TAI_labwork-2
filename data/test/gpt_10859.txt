Planes of Conflict is an accessory for the 3.5 edition of the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game.  Planes of Conflict is an accessory for the 3.5 edition of the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game that provides GMs and players with a variety of new rules to help them create and play campaigns focusing on air, land, and sea warfare. The book includes a variety of new rules, options, and subsystems to help GMs create exciting and dramatic campaigns featuring air, land, and sea combat.
