"Cómo Tú" ("Like You") is a pop ballad performed by Mexican singer-songwriter Thalía. The song was written by Thalia, her brother and music producer Erik Kirsch, and produced by Kirsch. It was released as the fourth and final single from her ninth studio album, "Diva" (2018).  "Cómo Tú" is a pop ballad with a tropical house beat. The song is about a young man who falls in love with a woman who is very different from him, but he is still drawn to her.   The song received positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Thalia's vocals. It reached the top 10 in Mexico, Spain, Portugal, Italy, and Argentina.
