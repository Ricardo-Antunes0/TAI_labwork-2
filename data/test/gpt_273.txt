Mills & Reeve LLP is a British law firm, founded in 1815. The firm has offices in London, Manchester, Birmingham, Edinburgh and Belfast.  The firm has a long history of providing legal services to a wide range of clients in the UK and overseas. It has particular strengths in corporate law, litigation, financial services and property. The firm has a strong international presence with offices in over 20 countries.  Mills & Reeve LLP is one of the largest law firms in the UK, with over 1,000 lawyers working across its six offices.
