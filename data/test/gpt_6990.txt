Colin Teague is a British film and television actor.  Teague was born in Redditch, Worcestershire, England to parents who were both teachers. He attended King Edward VI School in Birmingham, before studying acting at the University of Birmingham.  Teague began his professional acting career in 2004 with a small role in the independent film "The Boat That Rocked". He subsequently appeared in a number of television roles, including appearances in the British series "The Bill" and "Holby City". In 2009, he co-starred in the British romantic comedy film "Hot Fuzz", which received critical acclaim and earned him a nomination for the BAFTA Award for Best Actor. In 2011, he starred in the critically acclaimed British drama film "The Town", for which he was nominated for the BAFTA Award for Best Supporting Actor.  Teague has also appeared in a number of Hollywood films, including the 2013 action film "World War Z", the 2014 crime thriller "Edge of Tomorrow", and the 2016 science fiction film "War for the Planet of the Apes". Teague will appear in the upcoming action film "Kingsman: The Golden Circle" and the mystery drama film "Murder on the Orient Express".
