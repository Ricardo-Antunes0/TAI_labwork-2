"1901" is a song by French indie rock band Phoenix. It was released as the lead single from their fourth studio album "Ti Amo" (2009).  "1901" is a pop song with a tempo of 95 beats per minute. The song is written in the key of E major and has a basic chord progression of E–G–B–D. The lyrics are about the protagonist's love for another person, and how they want to keep that love alive for all eternity.   "1901" reached number one on the French Singles Chart, and was also successful in other European countries. The song received positive reviews from music critics, who praised its catchy hooks and lyrical content. It has been compared to the work of artists such as The Beatles and Radiohead.
