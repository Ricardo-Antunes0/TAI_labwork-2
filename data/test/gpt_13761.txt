Péter Török (born 22 October 1979) is a Hungarian-born biologist who works at the University of Cambridge.  Török was born in Budapest, Hungary in 1979. He completed his undergraduate studies at the University of Cambridge, where he was awarded a Bachelor of Arts degree in 2002. He then completed a Master of Science degree in 2004, and a Doctor of Philosophy degree in 2006, both in the Department of Zoology at the University of Cambridge.  Since 2006, Török has been a Research Associate in the Department of Zoology at the University of Cambridge. His research investigates evolutionary developmental biology and molecular ecology. He is particularly interested in understanding how genes and proteins interact to drive development and evolution.
