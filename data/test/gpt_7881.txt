Lundin Peak is a summit located in the southern Cascade Range of north-central United States. The peak is located in the Mount Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest, east of North Bend and south of the town of Greenwater.  Lundin Peak is a monadnock, a mountain peak that rises abruptly from a broad base. It is located in a cirque on the northern side of Lundin Glacier, which descends steeply to the east.
