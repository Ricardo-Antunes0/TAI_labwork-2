Saint Philip's Seminary is an apostolate of the Catholic Church and the oldest seminary in continuous operation in the United States. It is located in Baltimore, Maryland.  Founded in 1785, Saint Philip's Seminary is the first seminary in continuous operation in the United States and one of the oldest seminaries in the world. The seminary educates candidates for the priesthood and religious life. Saint Philip's Seminary has produced more than 300 priests, many of whom have served throughout the United States and around the world.
