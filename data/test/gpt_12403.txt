Mista Thug Isolation is the debut studio album by American rapper 21 Savage. It was released on August 25, 2018, by Interscope Records.  The album was produced by Southside and Metro Boomin, with additional production from Zaytoven and Cardo. The album features guest appearances from Offset, Quavo, Lil Yachty, London On Da Track, and PnB Rock.  The album has generally been well-received by music critics, with many praising 21 Savage's lyrical content and the production of the album. It has debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, making it 21 Savage's first number one album.
