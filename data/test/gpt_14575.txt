Kanna Thorakkanum Saami  is a 1986 Indian Malayalam film, directed by P. Padmarajan. The film stars Mohanlal, Menaka, Srividya and Sridevi in lead roles. The film had musical score by M. S. Viswanathan.  The story is about a man, who lost his wife in a traffic accident and has to take care of his two children. He decides to migrate to Canada in search of a better life for his kids. While in Canada, he meets and falls in love with a woman from Finland. However, when he tries to return home, he is refused entry because he is not an Indian citizen. He is forced to stay back in Canada and fight for his rights.
