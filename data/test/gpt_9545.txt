The Globe of Peace is a world map designed by the French artist André-Jacques Garneray in 1648. The map is one of the most renowned maps of its era and has been widely reproduced. It depicts the world in terms of its principal religions and nations, with decorative elements in the form of human figures, plants, and animals.
