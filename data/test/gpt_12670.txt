English singer George Michael released five studio albums, one compilation album, thirty-one singles and fifteen promotional singles during his career. He achieved commercial success in the late 1980s and early 1990s with songs such as "Faith", "Freedom '90", "I Want Your Sex" and "The Edge of Heaven". After a public battle with addiction to drugs and alcohol, Michael announced his retirement from music in 1998. However, he returned to the music industry in 2005 and has since released two further studio albums.
