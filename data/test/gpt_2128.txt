AusNet Services (previously SP AusNet) is an Australian telecommunications company that provides broadband, phone and internet services throughout New South Wales. AusNet Services is a subsidiary of Southern Cross Austereo.  AusNet Services commenced operations in 1997 as a rural telephone company. In 2003, it became the first company in Australia to provide broadband services over the public internet. In 2006, it acquired rival broadband provider iiNet and added phone and internet services to its portfolio. In October 2009, AusNet merged with SP AusNet to form AusNet Services.   AusNet Services serves more than 2 million customers across New South Wales, Queensland, Victoria, Western Australia and South Australia. The company has more than 1,000 employees and operates from offices in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide.
