The Pinnebog River is a  river in the Cape Breton Highlands of Nova Scotia, Canada. It is a tributary of the Bras d'Or River.  The Pinnebog River was named after a bog near its source that was full of pinnebog (a type of moss). The river flows through a wild and rugged area, with many cliffs and waterfalls. The Bras d'Or River joins it near the town of Bras d'Or.
