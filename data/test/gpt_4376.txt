Corina Șuteu was the Culture Minister of Romania from 2 December 2007 to 2 December 2008.  Șuteu was born in Bucharest on 2 December 1958. She studied law at the University of Bucharest, and then worked in the legal profession.   Șuteu was a member of the Social Democratic Party (PSD). She was elected to the Romanian Parliament in 2004, and was Minister for Regional Development in the Government of Prime Minister Adrian Năstase from 2004 to 2007.   On 2 December 2007, Șuteu was appointed Minister for Culture by Prime Minister Traian Băsescu. Her term ended on 2 December 2008.
