KKPT (94.1 FM) is an American commercial FM radio station licensed to serve the community of Casper, Wyoming. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and airs a country music format.  KKPT first went on the air in 1941 as KWCD-FM, which was owned by the West Wyoming Broadcasting Company. In 1961, the station changed its call letters to KKPT and became an affiliate of the ABC Radio Network. In 1988, KKPT became an affiliate of the Fox Broadcasting Company and switched to a country music format.
