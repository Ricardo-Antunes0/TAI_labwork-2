Recea may refer to the following places:   - Recea, Bacău County, Romania - Recea, Bihor County, Romania - Recea, Ialomiţa County, Romania - Recea, Suceava County, Romania - Recea River, a tributary of the Jiu River in Romania  The Recea River is a tributary of the Jiu River in Romania. It is located in the counties of Bacău and Bihor.
