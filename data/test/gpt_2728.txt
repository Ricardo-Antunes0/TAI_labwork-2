Amanda Donohoe (born 29 June 1962) is an Irish former international cricketer who played for the Irish national team in a career spanning 18 years from 1985 to 2000. She made her debut for the Irish national team in 1985, and went on to play 116 Test matches, scoring 10,029 runs at an average of 34.11.
