Snoopy's Reunion is the 34th prime-time animated television series produced by The Walt Disney Company. It is the first new prime-time animated series produced by Disney Television Animation in over a decade, and premiered on October 3, 2015, on Disney XD.  The series follows the adventures of a dog named Snoopy as he reunites with his old friends from World War I and II. The show is loosely based on the comic book series "Peanuts" by Charles M. Schulz.
