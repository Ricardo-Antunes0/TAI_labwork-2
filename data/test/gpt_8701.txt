The 2005–06 UEFA Cup,  the 35th season of Europe's secondary club football tournament, commenced on 14 August 2005 and concluded on 17 May 2006 with the final at the Olympic Stadium, Athens.  The competition was contested by 92 teams, including all 54 teams from the 2004–05 season and the 32 teams which qualified by playing in the UEFA Champions League. The first round featured 32 teams, with the eight winners progressing to the second round. The second round featured 16 teams, with the eight winners proceeding to the quarter-finals. The quarter-finals featured four teams from each group, with the two winners proceeding to the semi-finals. The semi-finals featured two teams from each group, with the winner proceeding to the final.  A total of 12 clubs reached the final, which was contested by Marseille and Barcelona. Marseille became the first team from outside of Europe to win a UEFA Cup title, while Barcelona became the first team ever to retain their European crown. As champions, Barcelona received €25 million as well as a place in the 2007–08 UEFA Champions League group stage.
