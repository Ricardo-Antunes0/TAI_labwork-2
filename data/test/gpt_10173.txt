Octavio Zaya is an art critic and curator.  Octavio Zaya is an art critic and curator who has written extensively on the history and contemporary significance of Latin American art. He is the founder and director of the Instituto Octavio Zaya, which he founded in 2000 in Buenos Aires, Argentina.  Zaya has exhibited his own work internationally, and has curated numerous exhibitions, including significant retrospectives of José Clemente Orozco and Diego Rivera. He has also published widely on Latin American art, including a book on the subject, La mirada de los artistas: ensayos sobre la pintura y las artes en América Latina (2006).
