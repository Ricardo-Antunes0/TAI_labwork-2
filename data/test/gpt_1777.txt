NHL Tonight (formerly NHL on the Fly: Live) is an American weekly television show that is produced by the NHL. The show airs on NBCSN in the United States and is simulcast on Sportsnet in Canada.  NHL Tonight is a pre-game show that previews the upcoming NHL game of the night. The show also features interviews with players and coaches from both teams, as well as analysts and reporters.   The show debuted on October 5, 2002, as NHL on the Fly: Live. It was renamed NHL Tonight in 2006.
