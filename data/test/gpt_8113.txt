LockLizard Limited is a British software company specialising in the development of secure password management and authentication solutions. The LockLizard solution is a form of two-factor authentication that uses a physical security token, such as a key fob, to provide an added layer of security.  LockLizard was founded in 2006 by Andy Burton and Mark Harrison. The company has offices in London, Birmingham, and Manchester. LockLizard has developed solutions for both the private and public sector. The company's products include the LockLizard Password Manager, the LockLizard Authenticator, and the LockLizard Authenticator Pro.  LockLizard has been featured in publications such as The Guardian, The Telegraph, PC World, Forbes, and Computerworld UK. In February 2016, the company was acquired by Synchronoss Technologies Ltd.
