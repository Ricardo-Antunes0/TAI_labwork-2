The Folland Gnat is a British compact car that was manufactured from 1965 to 1984.  The Folland Gnat was a small, rear-wheel drive sports car that was manufactured by Folland Motors Ltd. from 1965 to 1984. The Gnat was powered by a 1.1L (1,100 cc) engine that produced 48 hp (35 kW). The car had a top speed of 115 mph (185 km/h) and could accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (97 km/h) in 11.2 seconds. The Gnat was marketed as a competitor to the Mini Cooper and other small sports cars of the era.
