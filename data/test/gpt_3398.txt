The Nokia N81 is a Symbian OS smartphone designed and marketed by Nokia. It was announced on 9 October 2009, and went on sale in November 2009. The N81 is a slider phone with a 5-inch qHD display, a 1GHz Snapdragon processor, 8GB internal storage, a 2MP rear-facing camera and a VGA front-facing camera.
