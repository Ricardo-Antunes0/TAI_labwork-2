Venla Luukkonen (born 2 March 1984) is a Finnish grappler and Brazilian jiu-jitsu practitioner. In 2014, she became the first Finnish female black belt and the first Finn to win the Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu World Championship. Since 2014, in addition to winning other major tournaments, Luukkonen reached the world championship final three more times, winning gold again in 2018.
