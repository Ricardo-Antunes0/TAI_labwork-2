Disco in Dream is the debut concert tour by American singer and songwriter Halsey. The tour began on July 20, 2018 in Lisbon, Portugal and concluded on October 12, 2018 in Los Angeles, California.  The tour was announced on May 2, 2018 and was initially scheduled to run until September 2019. However, due to the success of her debut album "Halsey", she extended the tour by another ten shows.  Disco in Dream features opening acts such as KYLE and Jack Antonoff of Bleachers.  The setlist for the tour is mostly composed of material from Halsey's debut album "Halsey" with some fan favorites from her previous music projects such as "Bad at Love" and "Closer".
