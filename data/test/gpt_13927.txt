RAJUK Bhaban  is the head office of the Rajuk Group, a conglomerate with interests in manufacturing, retail, real estate, hotels and hospitality. The company has presence in India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and the Maldives. The Rajuk Group was founded by Late Shri Rajukaraju C. Gopalakrishna in 1948. The Rajuk Group was founded with an aim to create opportunities for the local communities it operates in. The company has a commitment to sustainable development and has implemented initiatives such as Green Shield Certification for its environmental practices.
