The Stanley Cycle Show or Stanley Showcase is an annual exhibition of bicycles and associated parts and accessories, held in the United Kingdom.  The show is usually staged over two days at the Earls Court Exhibition Centre, London, and has been running since 1913. The show is organised by the British bicycle trade magazine, Bicycle Retailer and Cycle Specialist.  The show attracts around 130 exhibitors from all over the world, with a wide range of bicycles and parts on display. It is one of the most important events in the bicycle calendar, and is considered to be one of the most prestigious shows in the industry.
