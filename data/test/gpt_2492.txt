Urszula Sipińska (born September 19, 1947) is a Polish actress, director and screenwriter.  Urszula Sipińska was born in Łódź, Poland on September 19, 1947. Sipińska studied acting at the National School of Theatre in Warsaw and made her stage debut in 1967. She has appeared in a number of Polish and international films and television programmes, including "Romeo and Juliet" (1968), "The Fearless Vampire Killers" (1967), "The Painted Veil" (1985), "The Unbearable Lightness of Being" (1988), "Schindler's List" (1993) and "In Treatment" (2009). In 2003 she received the Officer's Cross of the Order of Polonia Restituta.
