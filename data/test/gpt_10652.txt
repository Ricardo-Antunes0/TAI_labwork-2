"Invincible" is a song recorded by Canadian rock band Hedley. It was released as the third and final single from their self-titled debut album in 2007.  The song has been well received by critics, who praised the band's hard rock sound and Hedley's vocal performance. "Invincible" reached number five on the Canadian Hot 100 chart, and was nominated for a 2007 Juno Award for Single of the Year.  "Invincible" was written by lead singer Jacob Hoggard and produced by John Feldmann of the band Goldfinger. The song is about a man who is unbreakable, no matter what life throws at him.
