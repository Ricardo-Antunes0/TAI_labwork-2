Mitchell Linn Hannahs (born September 16, 1967) is an American actor, comedian, and writer. He is best known for his work on the Fox comedy series Bob's Burgers, for which he has been nominated for two Primetime Emmy Awards, and for his role as Calvin Baxter on the NBC comedy series The Office.  Hannahs was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and raised in Columbus, Ohio. He attended Ohio State University and played football there before dropping out to pursue a career in comedy. He first appeared on national television as a featured player on the Comedy Central Roast of Chevy Chase. In 2014, Hannahs co-created and starred in the Fox sitcom Bob's Burgers with fellow Office alumnus Loren Bouchard. The show has been successful, receiving critical acclaim and numerous awards nominations, including two Primetime Emmy Awards (for Outstanding Animated Program and Outstanding Writing for a Comedy Series), three Screen Actors Guild Awards (including Outstanding Performance by an Actor in a Comedy Series), and three Writers Guild of America Awards (including Best New Series). In 2018 he starred in the Netflix original film The Spy Who Dumped Me alongside Mila Kunis.
