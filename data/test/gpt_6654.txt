McCutchenville is a census-designated place (CDP) in western Gallia County, Ohio, United States. The population was 1,812 at the 2010 census.  McCutchenville is located in western Gallia County at (39.712389, -82.026795). It is bordered to the southwest by the city of Gallipolis, to the southeast by the community of Scotts Mills and to the northeast by the community of Beverly.  According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of , all land.  As of the census of 2000, there were 1,840 people, 664 households, and 511 families residing in the CDP. The population density was 3,603.2 people per square mile (1,371.6/km²). There were 714 housing units at an average density of 1,615.3/sq mi (668.7/km²). The racial makeup of the CDP was 97.57% White, 0.33% African American, 0.57% Native American, 0.17% Asian, 0.50% from other races, and 1.21% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 1.78% of the population.
