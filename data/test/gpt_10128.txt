Marek Wesoły (born 4 January 1978) is a Polish footballer who plays as a defender for Legia Warsaw.  Marek Wesoły was born in Łódź, Poland. He started his career at hometown club ŁKS Łódź, and played for ŁKS Łódź II, Wisła Kraków and Legia Warsaw before joining Legia Warsaw in the summer of 2007. Wesoły has made over 230 appearances for Legia Warsaw since joining the club, and has helped them win the Polish Cup twice, in 2006 and 2007.
