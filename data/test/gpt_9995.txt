Sue Cassidy Clark is an American music journalist, author and radio personality. She has written for "The Village Voice", "The New York Times", "Blender" and other publications.  Clark has been a music critic for more than 25 years, working at various publications including "The Village Voice", "The New York Times", "Blender" and "Entertainment Weekly". She has also been a frequent guest host on NPR's music program, "All Things Considered".  In 2010, Clark released her first book, "The Record Collector's Guide to American Folk Music". The book was published by Oxford University Press and received positive reviews.  Clark currently hosts the nationally syndicated show "Sue Cassidy Clark's American Folk Music Roundup" on Public Radio International.
