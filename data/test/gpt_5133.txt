Sherry L. Middaugh (née Hamel, born October 12, 1949, in Kansas City, Missouri) is an American actress, who appeared in a number of television series during the 1970s and 1980s. She is perhaps best known for her role as the evil stepsister Dawn Wells on the ABC sitcom Three's Company.  Middaugh was born in Kansas City, Missouri, the daughter of Patricia (née Hamel), a homemaker, and Lester Middaugh, a sales representative. She has one brother and one sister. Middaugh attended Shawnee Mission East High School in Overland Park, Kansas. She began her acting career in the 1970s appearing on television series such as The Waltons, Barnaby Jones and The Six Million Dollar Man. Her most prominent role was as the evil stepsister Dawn Wells on the ABC sitcom Three's Company from 1978 to 1984. She also had a recurring role on the CBS sitcom The Mary Tyler Moore Show and appeared in the films The Return of the Pink Panther (1983), WarGames (1983), and The Cannonball Run (1981). In 1985 she guest-starred on an episode of Knight Rider. In 1988 she married actor John Savage. They divorced in 1992.
