Coltrane Jazz is the sixth studio album by American jazz saxophonist John Coltrane, released on the Prestige label in 1961. It is considered one of his most important recordings, and is often cited as one of the greatest jazz albums of all time.  The album features a wide range of musical styles, including soulful ballads, hard bop, and free jazz. It was recorded at the same time as Impulse! Records' landmark album The Impulse! Sessions, which also features Coltrane.  The album reached number two on the Billboard 200 chart and has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. It has been widely regarded as a cornerstone of 20th century jazz music.
