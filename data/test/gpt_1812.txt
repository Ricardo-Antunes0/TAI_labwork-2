Christianity in Africa arrived in Egypt in the first century AD and spread through trade and missionary work. The African continent was the last place in the world to receive the Christian message. Christianity in Africa has a long and complex history.  Today there are over 1 billion Christians in Africa, making it the most populous region of the world where Christianity is the majority religion. Christianity has played a significant role in African culture and history, influencing everything from art to literature. There are also many Christian churches and organizations operating in Africa, providing education and medical services to communities across the continent.
