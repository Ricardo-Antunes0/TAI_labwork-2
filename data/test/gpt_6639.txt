Rhode Makoumbou was born in 1976 in Saint-Louis, Senegal. He started his career with local clubs AS Saint-Louis and ASC Diaraf before moving to France in 1998 to play for FC Girondins de Bordeaux. He has since made over 200 appearances for the club, scoring 26 goals.  Makoumbou has represented Senegal at international level, debuting in 1998.
