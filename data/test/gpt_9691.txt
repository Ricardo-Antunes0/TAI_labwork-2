The Marikamba Temple in Sirsi, Karnataka, India, is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Marikamba, whose temple is said to have been built by the sage Narada. The temple complex consists of a main sanctum and four subsidiary shrines, all of finely sculpted sandstone. The temple is considered to be one of the most important pilgrimage centres in Karnataka. The deity is housed in a large granite image flanked by two smaller images of Ganesha. The temple is open from 7:30 am to 6:00 pm from Monday to Saturday, and from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm on Sundays.
