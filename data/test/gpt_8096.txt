That Was Then is an American drama television series that aired on CBS from September 24, 1988 to May 19, 1989.  The show is set in the fictional town of Westland in 1959, and follows the lives of the people who live there. It was created by James L. Brooks, who also wrote and directed most of the episodes.  The show stars William Daniels, Mimi Rogers, and Kim Delaney as the main characters.
