Two Eleven is the sixth studio album by American hip hop duo Wu-Tang Clan. It was released on November 11, 1997, by Loud Records and distributed by Priority Records.  The album is the follow-up to the group's 1995 album Wu-Tang Forever. The album is largely produced by RZA and features guest appearances from Method Man, Ghostface Killah, U-God, The RZA, and Raekwon.  Two Eleven received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and the group's lyrical performances. It peaked at number six on the US Billboard 200 chart and has since been certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
