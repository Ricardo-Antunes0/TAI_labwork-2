Dragon Age is a Canadian media franchise created by BioWare and produced by its studio in Edmonton, Alberta. The series is based on the role-playing video game series of the same name by BioWare, with elements of traditional fantasy fiction.   The series began with the release of Dragon Age: Origins in 2009, and subsequent titles were released annually. The fourth game in the series, Dragon Age Inquisition, was released in November 2014.
