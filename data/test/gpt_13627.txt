"Smug Alert!" is the second episode of the third season of the American animated television series South Park. The episode was written by series co-creator Trey Parker and directed by series co-creator Matt Stone. It first aired on Comedy Central in the United States on September 25, 2002.  In the episode, Professor Chaos (Scott Tenorman) creates a virus that makes people smug. The townspeople become too smug and careless, leading to their downfall.  The episode is often considered one of the show's best episodes, and has been frequently cited as an influence on later works of satire and dark humor.
