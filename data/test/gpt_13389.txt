Martin A. Apple is R&D Director and Chief Scientific Officer at Apple Inc. He has over thirty years of experience in the semiconductor industry.  Apple Inc. is one of the largest American technology companies, with a market capitalization of over $600 billion as of 2016. Apple produces hardware, software, and services including the iPhone, iPad, MacOS, Apple Watch, and AirPods. The company has been involved in several controversies, including the controversy surrounding its smartwatch software and the firing of former CEO Steve Jobs.
