Carol Creiniceanu (1 February 1939 – 14 October 2006) was a Romanian-born French actress.  Born in Bucharest, Creiniceanu began her career on stage, making her debut in 1957 at the National Theatre of Iaşi. She appeared in a number of television dramas before making her film debut in the 1966 comedy film "L'Assommoir". She went on to appear in numerous French films, including "Les Visiteurs" (1971), "Possession" (1981), "Les Liaisons dangereuses" (1988), "La Vie en rose" (1991), and "Amour et sexe" (2003). Creiniceanu won a César Award for Best Actress for her performance in the 1990 drama film "L'Enfant". She also starred in the 1997 mystery film "Murder on the Orient Express". Creiniceanu retired from acting in 2003. She died from cancer at the age of 67.
