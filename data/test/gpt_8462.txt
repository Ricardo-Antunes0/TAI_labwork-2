Conjestina Achieng (born 20 October 1977 in Kampala) is a Ugandan actress, singer and model.  Conjestina Achieng was born on October 20, 1977 in Kampala, Uganda. She is an actress, singer and model.  Achieng first came to prominence as a model and appeared in several Ugandan television commercials. In 2001, she made her acting debut in the Ugandan film "The Return of Dr. Moreau". She has since appeared in several notable Ugandan films, including "Kabaka: The Living Legend" (2002), "The Invisible War" (2003), "The Devil's Playground" (2005), and "The Last Tourist" (2006). In 2007, she starred in the Ugandan film "The Ladies".  She has also appeared in several international films, including the Kenyan film "Blood Diamond" (2006), the French film "Passion" (2007), the South African film "Tsotsi" (2005), and the Malaysian film "The Amazing Spider-Man 2" (2014).  Achieng has also released two studio albums: "Conjestina" (2005) and "Passion" (2007).
