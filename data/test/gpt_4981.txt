Wreckfest is a racing video game developed by Bugbear Entertainment and published by Team17. It was released on March 9, 2012 for Microsoft Windows and OS X. A PlayStation 3 version was announced at the Electronic Entertainment Expo 2011 but was cancelled.  "Wreckfest" is a vehicular combat game in which players race against each other in a variety of vehicle classes across a variety of tracks. The game features online multiplayer, as well as a singleplayer campaign that follows the career of competing drivers.
