Super Bowl commercials, colloquially known as Super Bowl ads, are an annual event in the United States which features a large number of commercials during the game's first half. They are considered one of the most important marketing events of the year, and are often watched by hundreds of millions of people.  Super Bowl commercials have become increasingly sophisticated and creative in recent years, with a greater focus on creative content and innovative advertising techniques. The cost of a 30-second ad can be as high as US$4 million, and agencies and advertisers spend months planning their campaigns in secret.  The Super Bowl is the annual championship game of the National Football League (NFL), which is the highest level of professional American football. The game is typically played on Sunday afternoon, in the United States during February or March. The Super Bowl is the most-watched television event in the world, with an average viewership of 111.3 million people over the last ten years.
