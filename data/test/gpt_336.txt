Abdel Rahman Sule is a South Sudanese political analyst and journalist who is the former editor-in-chief of the state-owned newspaper, The Sudan Tribune. He has been a vocal critic of the Sudanese government and has written extensively about human rights abuses in the country.  Sule was born in 1983 in Wau, South Sudan. He received his undergraduate degree from the University of Khartoum in 2004 and his Masters from the same institution in 2006. Prior to joining The Sudan Tribune, he was a reporter for the state-owned daily newspaper, Al-Sudan. Sule has also served as a spokesperson for various civil society organizations, including the South Sudan Journalists Association and the National Democratic Alliance.
