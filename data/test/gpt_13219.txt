Little Big Town is an American country music trio consisting of Karen Fairchild, Kimberly Schlapman, and Jay Townshend. Formed in 2001 in Nashville, Tennessee, the group has since released six albums.  The band has been nominated for two Grammy Awards, and their singles "Girl Crush", "The Last Great American Road Trip", and "Pontoon" have all charted within the Top Ten of the "Billboard" Hot Country Songs charts.
