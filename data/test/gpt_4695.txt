Superbeat: Xonic (, stylized as SUPERBEAT: XONiC) is a rhythm video game developed by Sega and published by Sega for the PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC. It was announced at the Tokyo Game Show on September 20, 2018.  Superbeat: Xonic is a rhythm game where the player controls characters in a dance-like setting to the music of various Japanese popular culture songs. The game features online and singleplayer modes, and players can create their own stages using tools provided.  Development of Superbeat: Xonic began in early 2018, shortly after the release of Sonic Forces. The game was designed as an homage to classic 1990s Sega games such as Sonic the Hedgehog 2 and Sonic Adventure 2.  Superbeat: Xonic is set within the fictional world of "Xonic", which is ruled by an evil deity known as "The Beast". The player controls characters who must defeat The Beast and restore peace to Xonic.  Superbeat: Xonic was announced at the Tokyo Game Show on September 20, 2018. A release date was not announced.   Superbeat: Xonic is available for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC.
