George T. Fair (January 13, 1856 – December 18, 1935) was an American football and baseball coach. He served as the head football coach at the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign from 1898 to 1899, compiling a record of 4–1. Fair was also the head baseball coach at Illinois from 1898 to 1899, tallying a record of 8–4.
