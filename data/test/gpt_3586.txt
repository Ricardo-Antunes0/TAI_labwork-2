Habbo Siebold "Hayke" Veldman (born 5 November 1987) is a Dutch footballer who plays as a midfielder for FC Groningen in the Dutch Eredivisie.   Veldman began his career with Feyenoord Rotterdam, making his professional debut in 2006. He went on to make over 150 appearances for Feyenoord, helping them win the Dutch Cup in 2011.
