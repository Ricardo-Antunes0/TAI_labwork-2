Olajompo Abayomi Akinyeye (born 21 March 1958) is a Nigerian economist and academic. He is the current Vice-Chancellor of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka.  Akinyeye was born on 21 March 1958 in Ibadan, Nigeria. After completing his primary and secondary education in Ibadan, he attended the University of Ibadan, where he earned a B.A. in economics in 1980. He then attended London School of Economics, where he earned an M.A. in economics in 1984 and a Ph.D. in economics in 1988.  After completing his academic training, Akinyeye returned to Nigeria to join the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, as a lecturer in 1988. He was promoted to professor in 1997 and became the university's Vice-Chancellor in 2006. In this role, Akinyeye has focused on improving the university's performance both academically and financially. In addition to his work at the university, Akinyeye has also served as chairman of the National Economic Development Authority (NEDA) from 2011 to 2013 and as a member of the African Development Bank (AfDB) board of directors from 2006 to 2009.
