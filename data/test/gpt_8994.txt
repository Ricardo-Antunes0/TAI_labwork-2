Hydroxyprogesterone caproate (OHPC), sold under the brand name Depo-Provera, is a progestin medication used to prevent pregnancy. It is also used to treat menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes, vaginal dryness, and mood swings. OHPC is available as a tablet or injection.
