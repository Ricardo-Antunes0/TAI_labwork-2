Andrew S. Currier is an English former footballer who played as a midfielder. He made more than 350 appearances in the Football League, including 183 for Manchester United.  Currier was born in Manchester, Lancashire, and began his career with hometown club Manchester United. He made his debut in the Second Division on 25 October 1984 against Oldham Athletic and went on to make 183 appearances for the club, scoring 22 goals. He helped United win the FA Cup in 1989 and League Cup in 1990. He left United in 1993 to join Leicester City and made 86 appearances for them before retiring in 1997. Currier later worked as a football coach with Manchester United's youth team.
