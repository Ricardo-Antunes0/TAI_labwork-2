He was best known as the long-time newsreader on Radio New Zealand National, where he worked from 1962 until his retirement in 1997.  Smith was born in 1924 in Wairoa, North Island, and educated at Kahurangi College. He worked as a journalist for the Manawatu Standard and the Wanganui Chronicle before joining Radio New Zealand National in 1962 as a newsreader. He held this position until his retirement in 1997. Smith was also a panelist on the TVNZ quiz show Brainstorm from 1981 to 1984. He died in 2009 at the age of 88.
