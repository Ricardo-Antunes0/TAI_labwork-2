Pooja Dhingra (born 1986) is an Indian film actress who predominantly appears in Bollywood films. She has appeared in over fifty films and has received critical acclaim for her work in the industry.  Dhingra made her acting debut in the 2001 Hindi film "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge". She later appeared in films such as "Lage Raho Munna Bhai", "Saawariya", "Hera Pheri", "Maine Pyar Kiya", "Besharam" and "Peepli Live". Her performance in the 2012 romantic drama film "Ek Main Aur Ekk Tu" garnered her critical acclaim, with critics comparing her to actresses like Aishwarya Rai and Kangana Ranaut. In 2017, she starred in the critically acclaimed crime thriller film "Badlapur".
