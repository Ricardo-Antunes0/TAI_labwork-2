Robinson Crusoe or long titled Robinson Crusoe: The Musical is a Broadway musical with book, lyrics and music by J.B. Priestley and a book by Don Black.   The show opened at the Prince Edward Theatre on November 10, 2009 and closed on January 1, 2010 after 33 previews and 163 performances.  The show tells the story of Robinson Crusoe, who is shipwrecked on an island and has to create his own society from scratch.
