When wealthy heiress Katharine Blake inherits a ranch in the American West, she hires her childhood friend, rancher John Stewart, to manage it for her. A clash of ideologies soon develops between Katharine and John, as the two argue about how best to use the land. But as their differences grow, Katharine and John start to fall in love with each other.       In the end, Katharine realizes that she can't turn her back on her love for the land and decides to sell the ranch to John. They both get their dream home on the ranch and can finally enjoy each other's company without worrying about business.
