The Thomas Hockley House (1875) is a historic house located at 104 W. Main Street in the town of Hockley, Iowa, United States. It was built by Thomas Hockley, a local farmer and businessman, in 1875. The house is a two-story, rectangular structure with a gabled roof and clapboarded exterior. It features a single-story porch extending across the front and side of the house. The house was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.
