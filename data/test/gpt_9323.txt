"The Lazy Song" is a song by American singer-songwriter Bruno Mars. The song was written by Mars, Jeff Bhasker, and Pharrell Williams and produced by the latter two. The song was released on October 14, 2011, as the lead single from Mars' second studio album Unorthodox Jukebox (2011).  "The Lazy Song" is a mid-tempo pop ballad that tells of a love interest who is lazy and does not want to do anything. The song received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised its production and the way that Mars delivers the vocals. It has reached number one in various countries, including Australia, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Norway, Sweden and the United Kingdom.
