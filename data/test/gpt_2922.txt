Evan Chambers (born 1963, in Alexandria, Louisiana) is an American painter.  Chambers was born in Alexandria, Louisiana. He studied at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and the University of California, Berkeley.  His paintings are characterized by their restrained use of color and the use of large expanses of white space. His work has been exhibited at museums including the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Centre Pompidou.
