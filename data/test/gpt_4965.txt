Something Magic is the ninth studio album by the British rock band Muse. The album was announced on 18 October 2014, and was released on 8 November 2014. It is the band's first studio album since The 2nd Law (2012).  The album was recorded at three different studios in London: Air Lyndhurst, Olympic Studios and The Church. It was produced by Muse and Chris Holmes. It features string arrangements by Daniel Pemberton and additional production by Paul Epworth.  The album received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its sonics and lyrical content. It debuted at number one in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Sweden and Norway, and has been certified platinum in the UK and gold in Ireland.
