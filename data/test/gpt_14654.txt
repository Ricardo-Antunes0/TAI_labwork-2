Rosa 'Zephirine Drouhin' (or 'Zéphirine Drouhin') is a French winemaker, viticulturist, and oenophile. She is the daughter of Émile Drouhin and sister of Jérôme Drouhin.  Drouhin was born in 1944 in Saint-Émilion, Bordeaux, France. After studying at the University of Bordeaux, she began her career as a winemaker in 1973. In 1979 she founded her own wine production company, Domaine Drouhin-Rothschild, which produces both red and white wines.  Drouhin has been involved in many wine competitions and has won many awards. In 2003 she was made a Chevalier de l'Ordre des Arts et des Lettres. In 2006 she was awarded the title of Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire (DBE).
