The Riverview Theater is a cinema in the city of Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Opened in 1931, it was the first cinema to be built in Melbourne after the Second World War.  The Riverview Theater was originally built as an art-deco style cinema in 1931. The cinema has since been extensively renovated and restored, and re-opened in 2007 under the new management of Palace Cinemas. The theater has a seating capacity of 1,812 people and features a range of new digital projection technologies.
