Nicole Houston Reed, known as Nikki Reed (born October 25, 1978) is an American actress and model. Reed began her career as a child model, appearing in print and television advertisements. She made her feature film debut in the teen drama film The Omen (1976), and appeared in the horror films The Haunting (1963) and Friday the 13th (1980). Reed subsequently appeared in television series including Dallas (1993–1999), ER (1994–2009), and Charmed (1998–2006). Her breakthrough role came in the drama series Dawson's Creek (1998–2003), for which she received critical acclaim.  Reed has since appeared in a number of films, including the dramas Gods & Monsters (1998), Snow White & the Huntsman (2012), and Fifty Shades of Grey (2015). She also appeared in the comedy film Wedding Crashers (2005) and starred as Tara Chace on the CW series Charmed from 2006 to 2011. In 2018, she reprised her role as Willow Rosenberg in Buffy the Vampire Slayer for a special episode.
