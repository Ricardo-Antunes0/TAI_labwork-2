Alkaased (foaled 19 February 2000) is an Irish Thoroughbred racehorse and broodmare. She was undefeated in two races as a two-year-old in 2002, including a victory in the Group One Moyglare Stud Stakes. She was then retired to become a breeding stallion in Ireland.  Alkaased is a bay mare with a white blaze and three white socks on her left hind leg. She was bred in Ireland by her owner, Dermot Weld. She was sent into training with Aidan O'Brien at Ballydoyle.  Alkaased made her racing debut as a two-year-old in 2002. She won her first race, the Group One Moyglare Stud Stakes, by nine lengths. She then finished second in the Group Two Coolmore Stud Stakes, behind Cat Ballou.  Alkaased was retired from racing at the end of 2002, and became a breeding stallion in Ireland. Her first foal was born in 2009, and her second foal was born in 2013.
