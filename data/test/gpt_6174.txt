Michael James Massimino  (born August 19, 1967) is an American former professional baseball catcher. He played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Los Angeles Dodgers, Philadelphia Phillies, and Boston Red Sox.  Massimino was drafted by the Dodgers in the first round of the 1987 Major League Baseball Draft. He made his MLB debut with the Dodgers in 1988. He played with the Dodgers, Phillies, and Red Sox over a twelve-year career. He won two World Series rings with the Red Sox in 2004 and 2007.  Massimino retired from MLB after the 2009 season. He has since served as a color commentator for MLB on Fox and TBS.
