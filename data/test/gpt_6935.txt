Lisnagade  is a large multivallate earthen embankment in South Mumbai, India, parallel to the shoreline of the Arabian Sea. It is located in the Bandra-Kurla Complex area. The embankment and its adjoining areas are used for recreational activities such as jogging, walking and cycling.  Lisnagade is also known for its nightlife. It is home to several pubs and clubs, as well as restaurants. The embankment forms part of the Marine Drive promenade, which runs along the coast of the Arabian Sea.
