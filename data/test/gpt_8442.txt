Super Mario All-Stars is a 1993 compilation video game for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. It is a remake of the original Super Mario Bros. game, with new levels and characters.  Super Mario All-Stars was released in 1993 as a compilation of the original Super Mario Bros. game, with new levels and characters. The game was met with critical and commercial success, selling over six million copies worldwide. It has been considered one of the best games on the Super Nintendo Entertainment System, and is often cited as one of the best compilation video games ever made.
