Fircroft College is a specialist adult residential college in the town of Fircroft, near the city of Hull, in the English county of East Yorkshire.  Fircroft College was founded in 1984 as a response to the need for specialist accommodation in the area. The college offers full-time and part-time courses in a range of professional and academic disciplines.  The college has a strong reputation for its provision of high quality accommodation, which is situated in a beautiful location close to the city centre. The college is also home to a number of social and sporting facilities, and its students are able to take advantage of local amenities such as shopping, restaurants and theatres.
