"No Me Ames" (English: "Don't Love Me") is a Mexican telenovela produced by Pedro Romero for Televisa in 2006.  The story is set in the city of Toluca, Mexico, and revolves around the love story of a young doctor, Sergio, and a woman, Irma, who are both passionate about their work. Sergio is married to Silvia and has two children, but he's never been able to forget Irma, who he meets again after many years. When Silvia finds out about their affair she's devastated and decides to take legal action to get her husband back.
