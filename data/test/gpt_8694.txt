Yellow Sky is a 1948 American Western film directed by Joseph Kane and written by Howard Koch. The film stars William Holden, Constance Bennett, and John McIntire. The film was released on May 3, 1948, by 20th Century Fox.  The film is about two brothers who travel to Montana in search of their father, who has been missing for years. Along the way, they meet a group of people who help them find their way.
