Pillucina is a genus of saltwater clams, marine bivalve molluscs in the family Arcidae, the ark clams.   The genus Pillucina is distributed throughout the tropical and subtropical Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. It includes about 28 species in 11 genera. The shells are small to medium in size, with a smooth or slightly ribbed surface. The operculum is broad and its margin is sharply indented. The siphon is short. The mantle cavity is deep and narrow. The foot is short and broad.
