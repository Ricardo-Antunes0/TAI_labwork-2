Prakash Belawadi is an Indian theater, film, and television actor. He has appeared in many popular Hindi-language television series, including "Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii" and "Kumkum Bhagya".  Belawadi was born in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He studied at the National School of Drama, New Delhi.  Belawadi made his television debut in the show "Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii" in 2009. He played the role of Ronnie. He also appeared in the show's sequel, "Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii 2", as Rajesh. In 2013, he played the role of Raghu in the show "Kumkum Bhagya". The show became popular and Belawadi received critical acclaim for his performance. He has also appeared in other shows, including "Monkey".
