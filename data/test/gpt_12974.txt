Steven Kons (born 17 September 1962) is an American composer and musician. He is known for his work in the video game industry, having composed the music for the games Deus Ex, Thief, and Mass Effect.   Kons was born in 1962 in Boston, Massachusetts. He studied music at Berklee College of Music and the University of Southern California. After working as a session musician and composer for years, he began working on video games in 1995. His work on Deus Ex led to a long-term collaboration with developer Eidos Montreal, with whom he has composed music for many of the company's titles including the Thief series, Mass Effect, and Deus Ex: Human Revolution. In addition to video games, Kons has also composed music for various films and television shows.
