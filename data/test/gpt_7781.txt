Maanthrikam  is a 1995 Indian Malayalam-language film directed by Sibi Malayil. The film stars Mohanlal, Manju Warrier and Jagathy Sreekumar in lead roles.  Maanthrikam is a story of a carpenter (Mohanlal) and his wife (Manju Warrier) who are struggling to make ends meet. One day, they are approached by a rich man (Jagathy Sreekumar) who offers to pay them well for their work. The couple is sceptical at first, but eventually agrees to take the job. The rich man also gives them an old car to work on. The carpenter and his wife start making a living from the job, but their happiness is short-lived as the rich man starts demanding more and more from them. Eventually, the carpenter and his wife have to go back to being poor.   The film has won several awards including the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Malayalam and the Kerala State Film Award for Best Feature Film.
