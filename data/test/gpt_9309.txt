Rishabhanatha, also  , Rishabhadeva,  or Rishabhanatha Purana, is a Hindu scripture dated to the 1st millennium CE that narrates the life of Rishabhanatha, one of the ten avatars of Vishnu.  Rishabhanatha is one of the most revered Hindu deities and his cult has gained considerable popularity in recent decades. The text describes Rishabhanatha as an exemplary yogi, who lived a simple and austere life and preached the gospel of non-violence. The deity is also revered for his healing powers.
