Abigail Rokison-Woodall (née Rokison) is an author, speaker and influencer based in the United Kingdom.  Abigail was born on the Isle of Wight, England, and has always been interested in writing. She studied creative writing at the University of East Anglia, graduating in 2013.  Following her graduation, Abigail worked as a freelance writer and editor, before becoming a full-time author in 2016. Her first book, The Woman Who Could Not Forget: A Memoir of My Mother’s Life, was published that year.  Since then, Abigail has spoken at numerous conferences and events around the world, sharing her unique story and advice on living life to the fullest. She is also a regular contributor to magazines and newspapers, and has appeared on BBC Radio 4 and BBC Radio 5 Live.  In 2018, Abigail released her second book, The Journey: A Memoir of Hope and Healing. It tells the story of her journey from despair to hope following the death of her mother.  Abigail is married with two young children. She lives in the UK with her family.
