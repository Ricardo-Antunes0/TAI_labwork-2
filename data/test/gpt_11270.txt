Yoo Young-chul (; born 18 April 1970) is a South Korean actor and comedian. He is known for his roles in the television series My Little Lover (2004), The Proposal (2009), and Goblin (2014).  Yoo was born in Seoul, South Korea, and graduated from Konkuk University with a degree in theater arts. After graduation, he worked as an actor and comedian in various theaters. In 2004, he starred in the South Korean television series My Little Lover, which gained him widespread recognition. In 2009, he starred in the hit South Korean film The Proposal and won numerous awards, including the Excellence Award at the 2009 Baeksang Arts Awards. In 2014, he starred in the South Korean film Goblin and received critical acclaim for his performance. He is currently starring in the KBS2 drama Mr. Sunshine (2017).
