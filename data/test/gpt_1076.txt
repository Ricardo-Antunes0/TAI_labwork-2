Steven Robertson (born 1 January 1977) is a Scottish football player who currently plays for Partick Thistle.  Steven Robertson began his career with Dumbarton in 1996 and made over 150 appearances for the club before joining Partick Thistle in 2006. He has since made over 120 appearances for the Jags and has won the Scottish First Division championship in 2009, 2011 and 2015.
