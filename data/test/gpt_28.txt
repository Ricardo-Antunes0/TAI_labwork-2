Viatcheslav Mikhailovich Kharlamov (Вячеслав Михайлович Харламов, born October 25, 1945) is a Russian professional ice hockey player. He played for the Soviet Union and Russia in international competition. He is currently the coach of HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Kharlamov was born in Chelyabinsk, Soviet Union. He played for the Soviet Union national ice hockey team at the 1972 Winter Olympics and the 1980 Winter Olympics. He also played for Russia at the 1976 and 1984 Winter Olympics.
