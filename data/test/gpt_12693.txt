Hodthorpe and Belph is a civil parish in the District of North Lincolnshire, England. The parish includes the villages of Hodthorpe and Belph, as well as the hamlet of Goldthorpe. Hodthorpe is located about east of the town of Boston and about north-west of the city of Lincoln. Belph is located about south-west of the village of Hodthorpe. The population at the 2011 census was 1,691.  Hodthorpe and Belph was a civil parish in the District of North Lincolnshire from 1894 until 1934 when it became part of the new Rural District of Boothby Graffoe. In 1974 it became part of North Lincolnshire district.
