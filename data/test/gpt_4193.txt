"Pay in Blood" is an uptempo rock song written by American rock band Kiss and produced by Bob Ezrin. It was released as the second single from the band's 1979 album Dynasty.  "Pay in Blood" is a hard rock song with a heavy metal sound. The song is written in the key of A minor and has a tempo of 143 beats per minute. Kiss plays fast paced drums and guitars throughout the song. Gene Simmons sings lead vocals, with Paul Stanley providing backing vocals.  "Pay in Blood" was released as the second single from the band's 1979 album Dynasty. It reached number six on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart in the United States and number eleven in Canada. The song also reached number one on the "Billboard" Hot Rock Tracks chart, number two on the "Billboard" Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, and number four on the "Billboard" Hot Adult Contemporary Tracks chart. In Australia, the song reached number thirty-six on the Kent Music Report chart.  The music video for "Pay in Blood" was directed by Peter Fonda and features Gene Simmons performing in a dark studio with shadows moving behind him. Paul Stanley is also featured in the video, playing guitar in a separate room.
