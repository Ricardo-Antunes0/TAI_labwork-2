Hawthorne Books is an independent publisher founded in 2007 by Matthew Battles and Sarah Peacock. We publish books of all genres, from darkly comic crime fiction to heart-wrenching memoirs, and aim to bring the best of contemporary writing to readers around the world.  We believe that books can change the world, and we make them with the hope that they will. We believe in democracy of the bookshelf, in the power of stories to connect us with one another and with ourselves. We believe in the power of words to make us smarter, more compassionate, and more understanding.  We are committed to publishing writers who are changing the way we see the world, and we hope that you will join us in supporting their work. Thank you for considering Hawthorne Books as your source for great books.
