is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Haruko Kumota. The manga follows Futaba, a high school girl who befriends a talking cat named Nyanko-chan. Futaba discovers that Nyanko-chan is a magical creature that can transform into any object she desires. Together, the two explore Tokyo and its inhabitants.  The manga was serialized in the magazine "Weekly Shōnen Jump" from March 2003 to March 2009. It was collected into twenty-six tankōbon volumes. The manga was licensed for an English language release in North America by Viz Media, and was published from June 10, 2009 to December 17, 2009.
