Andrew Klaber is the Chief Executive Officer of the software company Red Hat, and the former President of Red Hat. He was appointed to this position in October 2006. Previously, Klaber was Vice President of Technology at Intel Corporation from 1998 to 2006.  Klaber has over 20 years of experience in the technology industry, including ten years at Intel Corporation where he held a number of positions including Vice President of Technology and General Manager of the Intel Architecture Group. He has also held positions at Sun Microsystems, Microsoft, and Amazon. Klaber received his B.S. in Electrical Engineering from UCLA, and his M.B.A. from Stanford Graduate School of Business.
