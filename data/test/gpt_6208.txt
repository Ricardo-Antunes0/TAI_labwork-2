Filiberto Ojeda Ríos (April 26, 1933  – May 12, 2006) was a Mexican actor and comedian.  Ojeda was born in the city of Toluca, State of Mexico. After completing his primary and secondary education in Toluca, he began his theatrical career in 1957, working with the Teatro del Ángel. In the early 1960s, he made his debut in television with "La Voz de México", where he would remain for more than 20 years.  Ojeda is best known for his work on "El Chapo", where he starred as Agustín Fernández. He also appeared in numerous telenovelas and movies, including "La hija de los milagros", "El Crimen del Padre Amaro", "Inocencia y Pecado", "El Día de la Madre" and "Los Abrazos Rotos". He died from a heart attack at the age of 74.
