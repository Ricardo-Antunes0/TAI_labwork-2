Giorgio Frezzolini (born 21 January 1976) is an Italian artist who specialises in painting, sculpture, photography and video.  Frezzolini was born in Rome, Italy. He received his early artistic training at the Scuola Superiore di Belle Arti in Rome, graduating with a degree in painting in 2001.   He has exhibited his work internationally, including at the Museum of Contemporary Art, Chicago (2012), the Centre Pompidou, Paris (2010), the Museum of Modern Art, New York (2008), the Tate Modern, London (2007), and the Palazzo delle Esposizioni, Naples (2005). His work is held in major museums around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art, New York; National Gallery of Victoria, Melbourne; Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York; Los Angeles County Museum of Art; and Museo d'Arte Contemporanea di Roma.   Frezzolini lives and works in Rome.
