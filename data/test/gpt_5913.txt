NVR, Inc. is a company engaged in the development, manufacture and sale of network video recording (NVR) systems. The company's NVR systems are used to record, store and playback video footage from security surveillance systems, point-of-sales (POS) systems, and other industrial and commercial applications.  NVR was founded in 2006 by CEO David Segal and President Jeff Cohen. The company has headquarters in Sunnyvale, California. As of December 31, 2017, NVR had approximately 1,000 employees.  NVR's products are marketed under the brands Axis, Encase and Forte. The company's products are used by businesses and governments around the world. NVR has several partnerships with leading vendors of security surveillance software, including CyberArk Software Inc., Oracle Corporation, and Verint Systems Inc.
