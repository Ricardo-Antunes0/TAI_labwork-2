Saeed Al-Mutairi Bariq (; born September 24, 1984) is a Saudi professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Al-Ittihad .  Al-Mutairi was born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia on September 24, 1984. He started his career with local club Al-Faisaly before moving to Al-Ittihad in 2004. He has since played for several clubs in Saudi Arabia and the UAE, including Al-Nassr, Al-Jazira, and Al-Shabab.  In 2008, he moved to Europe to sign with Dutch side PSV Eindhoven. However, he failed to make an impact with the club and was loaned out to Danish side FC Midtjylland in 2009. He made 29 appearances for Midtjylland before returning to PSV Eindhoven in 2010.  In January 2011, he joined English club Manchester City on a six-month loan deal. He made his Manchester City debut on February 1, 2011 in a 1–0 victory over Bolton Wanderers and went on to make six further appearances before returning to PSV Eindhoven at the end of the loan spell.  In July 2011, he signed a two-year contract with Ukrainian side Shakhtar Donetsk.
