Frölunda Hockey Club, previously known as the HC Frölunda, is a professional ice hockey club based in Gothenburg, Sweden. The team is currently playing in the Swedish Hockey League (SHL).  Frölunda HC was founded on May 6, 1891. They play their home games at the Scandinavium in Gothenburg. Frölunda HC has won the SHL championship twice, in 2009 and 2013. The club's most successful period was from the early 1990s to the mid-2000s, when they won four league titles and two Swedish Championships. Frölunda HC has also qualified for the playoffs eleven times in fifteen seasons.
