California Farmer (1854-2013) was the state of California's official agricultural journal. Published monthly by the California State Agricultural Department, it was the official publication of the California Farmer's Association, and was the most widely circulated agricultural journal in the world.  The California Farmer was first published on February 1, 1854, as "The Californian Farmer". It was renamed The California Farmer's Journal in 1892, and became the California Farmer in 1949. The journal ceased publication with the March 2013 issue.
