The Pediatric Oncology Group (POG) was a professional organization of pediatric oncologists who met quarterly to share information and participate in collaborative research. The POG was founded in 1985 and ceased operations in 2003.   The Pediatric Oncology Group was formed in 1985 as a forum for sharing information and collaborative research among pediatric oncologists. The POG ceased operations in 2003, having accomplished its mission of providing a forum for pediatric oncologists to share information and collaborate.
