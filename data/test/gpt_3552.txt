Recycled Paper Greetings, Inc. (RPG) is a nonprofit organization founded in 2002 that designs, prints, and distributes greeting cards made from recycled paper.  RPG is headquartered in Brooklyn, New York and employs a staff of artists and writers. The organization's goal is to promote awareness and use of recycled paper products while providing affordable cards to those in need.  RPG's greeting cards are made from a variety of recycled materials, including post-consumer waste (PCW), pre-consumer waste (PCW), municipal waste, and industrial waste. The cards are printed on recycled paper using water-based inks.  RPG has distributed over one million cards worldwide. The organization's flagship product is the Recycled Paper Greetings Card, which retails for $5 USD.
