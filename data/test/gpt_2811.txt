Park Ji-ung (; born October 28, 1982), better known by his stage name Park Hyun-bin, is a South Korean actor and singer. He is best known for his roles in the television series Boys Over Flowers and the film The Host.  Park was born in 1982 in Seoul, South Korea. He studied at Dongguk University, but dropped out to pursue a career in acting. Park made his acting debut in 2002 with the television series Boys Over Flowers. He then starred in the successful films The Host (2006) and The Thieves (2008). In 2010, he starred in the action film The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift and received critical acclaim for his performance. Park starred in the crime thriller film Assassination which released in December 2016.
