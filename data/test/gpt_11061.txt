Jake Stormoen is an American actor known for his roles in the television series Brothers & Sisters and the film American Gangster. He also starred in the Broadway play The Normal Heart. Stormoen was born in Los Angeles, California, to a Norwegian father and an American mother. He has two siblings. Stormoen began his acting career in the early 2000s, appearing in minor roles on television shows such as Brothers & Sisters and ER. In 2007, Stormoen landed his breakthrough role as Jeremy Tucker in the HBO series Brothers & Sisters, for which he received critical acclaim. He continued to appear in supporting roles in films such as American Gangster and The Spectacular Now before playing the lead role in the Broadway play The Normal Heart.
