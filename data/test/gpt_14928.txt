A bandsaw (also written band saw) is a type of power saw used for cutting wood. It is a reciprocating saw that uses a band to drive the blade. The bandsaw is a very versatile tool, capable of cutting many different types of wood.   Bandsaws have been in use for centuries, and are one of the oldest types of power saws. They are still popular today, and are used for a variety of tasks, including cutting boards, furniture, and other wood products. A bandsaw can be a very powerful tool, and should be used with caution.
