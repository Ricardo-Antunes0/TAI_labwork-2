Nikola Ferić (10 May 1736 – 30 September 1816) was a Croatian writer, playwright, and poet.  Ferić was born in the village of Vrbica in central Croatia, then part of the Kingdom of Hungary. He was educated at the Jesuit college in Zagreb and later at the University of Vienna, where he studied law. In 1763 he opened a law office in Zagreb.  In 1784 he published his first book of poetry, "Pjesme". The following year he published his first play, "Kruna". In 1787 he published the novel "Bosnian Pero". He also wrote travel books about Italy and Greece. In 1801 he published his autobiography, "Pismo iz Kraljevine Hrvatske".  Ferić died in Zagreb.
