Soleto (Griko: ; Salentino: ; ) is a town and comune in the province of Lecce, southern Italy.  The town is located on a promontory overlooking the Gulf of Taranto, about southeast of Lecce and about northwest of Taranto.  Soleto is known for its wine, olive oil, and crystal. The town has a long history as a producer of textiles and for its furniture, notably the sofas made in the 19th century by the Cappelli family.
