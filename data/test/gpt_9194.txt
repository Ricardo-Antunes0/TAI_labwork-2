Terence Blanchard is a self-titled album by American jazz trumpeter, composer and bandleader Terence Blanchard. Recorded in 1984 and released on the ECM label, it features performances by Blanchard with Joe Henderson, Freddie Hubbard, Ron Carter and Roy Haynes.  Terence Blanchard is one of the most eminent and respected jazz trumpeters of the twentieth century. His album Terence Blanchard is a self-titled recording that was released in 1984. This album features performances by Terence Blanchard with Joe Henderson, Freddie Hubbard, Ron Carter and Roy Haynes. The album was recorded at the Van Gelder Studio in New Jersey.
