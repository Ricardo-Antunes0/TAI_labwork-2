9/11 is a 2017 American action drama film directed by Michael Bay and written by Eric Warren Singer and David Koepp. It is based on the 2001 terrorist attacks on the United States. The film stars Mark Wahlberg, Dwayne Johnson, Don Cheadle, and John Goodman.  The film tells the story of two firefighters who are forced to go into the destroyed World Trade Center in order to save people.   Principal photography began on September 8, 2016, in New York City. The film was released in the United States on May 18, 2017. It grossed over $1 billion worldwide.
