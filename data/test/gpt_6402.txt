Clara Edele Bengta Kruchow née Langberg (1915–1989) was a Swedish author and teacher. She is best known for her children's book series 'The Trolls of Trapped Valley'   Edele Kruchow was born Clara Edele Bengta Langberg in 1915 in the town of Borås, Sweden. After studying at the University of Stockholm, she started working as a teacher in 1946. Kruchow wrote her first children's book, 'The Trolls of Trapped Valley', in 1966. The series was published between 1968 and 1984 and has been translated into more than twenty languages. Kruchow died in 1989.
