Zvonimir "Zvonko" Bogdan (; born 5 January 1958) is a retired Croatian football defender.  Bogdan started his career playing for NK Dinamo Zagreb before transferring to Spanish club Atlético Madrid in 1980. He spent two seasons at the club, making 44 appearances and scoring three goals. After leaving Atlético Madrid, Bogdan played for Valencia CF, FC Barcelona and Sporting CP. He retired from professional football in 1996, having played 221 matches and scored 10 goals.  Bogdan represented Croatia at the 1988 Olympic Games and the 1990 World Cup. He also played for the Croatia national team at the 1986 World Cup and the 1990 World Cup.
