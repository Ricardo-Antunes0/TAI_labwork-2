Charles LaVerne Nelson (born February 23, 1960) is an American actor, voice actor and comedian. He is known for his work on the NBC sitcom Chuck and for his recurring role as Dr. James Bell on the CBS sitcom The Big Bang Theory. Nelson is also a jazz musician and has appeared as a guest artist with several big bands. In 1982 he moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in stand-up comedy.  Nelson's first TV role was on the NBC sitcom Chuck from 1992 to 1995. From 1995 to 1998, he starred as Dr. James Bell on the CBS sitcom The Big Bang Theory.
