Adam Willis is a fictional character from the Australian soap opera Neighbours, played by actor Shane Jacobson. He made his first screen appearance on 14 July 2004.  Willis comes from a wealthy family and is the brother of Laurel and Harry Willis. He is usually seen as the "bad boy" of the Willis family, although he has been known to show some good qualities from time to time. He initially began his career as a criminal, but he eventually became a successful businessman. In February 2012, it was announced that Jacobson had departed Neighbours and that Willis would be leaving the show at the end of the year.
