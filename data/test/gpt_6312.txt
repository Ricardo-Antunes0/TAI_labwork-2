Andrea Ivančević (born 21 August 1984) is a Serbian football defender who last played for Jagiellonia Białystok.  Andrea Ivančević started playing football with local side Javor Ivanjica before moving to Partizan in the late 1990s. He made his senior debut in 2003, and went on to make over 100 appearances for the club. In summer 2009, Ivančević moved abroad to Polish side Jagiellonia Białystok. He has since played for Hungarian side Pécs, Slovenian side Rudar Velenje and Macedonian side FK Vardar.
