Typhoon Abby, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Ketsana, was a powerful typhoon that affected the Philippines and China in 1983. The storm became a Category 5 super typhoon on the Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Wind Scale and caused extensive damage to both countries. In the Philippines, Abby killed at least 1,000 people and caused $3 billion in damage. In China, Abby killed at least 100 people and caused $1 billion in damage.
