The Comeback Tour was a headlining tour by American recording artist Justin Bieber. It started on March 3, 2017 in Europe and ended on December 13, 2017 in Australia. The tour was announced on February 17, 2017 and was produced by Scooter Braun and DNA Productions.  The tour was met with critical acclaim, with many calling it one of the best tours of 2017. It sold out most venues and has been credited for boosting Bieber's return to the public eye.
