The 6th season of the television series Arthur was released on November 17, 2015. It is based on the children's animated television series of the same name created by Don Bluth and his team at DreamWorks Animation. The season follows Arthur (voiced by Tom Hiddleston) and his friends as they try to stop the evil Lord Monochromicorn (voiced by John DiMaggio) from taking over the world.   The season was preceded by a spin-off series, The Legend of Korra, which aired for five seasons until its finale on February 14, 2014. A total of seventy-four episodes were produced for the sixth season of Arthur, which aired on Nickelodeon in the United States and Nicktoons in Canada.
