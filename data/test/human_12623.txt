Michael James Nazir-Ali (; born 19 August 1949) is a Pakistani-born British Catholic priest and former Anglican prelate who served as the 106th Bishop of Rochester from 1994 to 2009 and, before that, as Bishop of Raiwind in Pakistan. He is currently the director of the Oxford Centre for Training, Research, Advocacy and Dialogue. In 2021, he was received into the Catholic Church and was ordained as a priest for the Ordinariate of Our Lady of Walsingham on 30 October 2021. He is a dual citizen of Pakistan and Britain.
