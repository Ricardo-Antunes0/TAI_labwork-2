Dubai Destination (foaled 11 February 1999) is a Thoroughbred racehorse and sire who was bred in Ireland. He was owned and trained by Johnny Murtagh in the United States.  Dubai Destination is best known for his major success in the United States, where he won eight of his eighteen races. He recorded his biggest win in the 2006 Breeders' Cup Classic, which was held at Del Mar Racetrack in California. Dubai Destination also finished second in the 2007 Kentucky Derby and third in the 2008 Preakness Stakes.  After his retirement from racing, Dubai Destination became a successful breeding stallion, siring several major winners including Street Sense, Long Run, and Majestic Bull.
