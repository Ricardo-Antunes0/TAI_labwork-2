Godrej Interio is an Indian furniture company, headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was founded in 1924 by Sawant Laxmansingh Godrej.  The company offers a wide range of furniture products, including sofas, chairs, tables, beds, and wardrobes. It is one of the largest furniture companies in India, with a market share of about 30%. Godrej Interio also exports its products to countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, and Saudi Arabia.  Godrej Interio has been recognised for its quality products and innovative designs. The company has won numerous awards, including the National Award for Export Achievement in 2002, and the prestigious IFA Award for Furniture Design in 2005.
