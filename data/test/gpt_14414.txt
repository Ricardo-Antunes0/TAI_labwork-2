Héctor José Moreno Moreno (born June 8, 1984 in San Nicolás de los Garza, Nuevo León) is a Mexican racewalker. He competed in the men's 50 kilometres walk at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, but withdrew before the race began because of an injury. He finished thirteenth in the men's 50 kilometres walk at the 2012 Summer Olympics.
