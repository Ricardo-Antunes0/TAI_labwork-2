The New Pornographers are a Canadian indie rock band. Formed in 2002, the group consists of singer-songwriter Neko Case, guitarist-vocalist A.C. Newman, drummer Darren Jessee, and bassist Peter Buck.  The band has released six studio albums: "Mass Romantic" (2002), "The Royal Tenenbaums" (2004), "The New Pornographers" (2006), " Brill Bruisers" (2008), "Electric Version" (2010), and "Whiteout Conditions" (2012). They have also released two extended plays and one compilation album.  The band has received several awards and nominations, including a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album for their 2007 album, "Mass Romantic".
