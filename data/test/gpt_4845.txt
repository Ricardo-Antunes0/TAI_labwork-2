Atrangi Re  is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film written and directed by Ajith Kumar. Produced by D. Venkatesh and V. Suresh, it features debutant Anushka Shetty in the title role and Vinayaka in dual roles. The film is set in a fictional town of Atrangi and its surrounding areas, and tells the story of an unlikely friendship formed between a street-smart girl and a powerful gangster.  The film's plot revolves around the alliance formed between the girl, played by Shetty, and the gangster, played by Vinayaka. The two clash initially but eventually form an alliance to take on their common enemies. The film also stars Arjun Sarja, Sathyaraj, Jagapathi Babu, Rituparna Sengupta, Kalabhavan Mani and Saranya Ponnambalam in supporting roles.  Principal photography began in November 2019 and ended in February 2020. The film is set to be released on 15 August 2021.
