Black Sands Entertainment, Inc. is an American video game development company based in Marina del Rey, California. The company was founded in January 2014 by former Ubisoft employees David Vignocchi and Jason Rubin.  Black Sands Entertainment is best known for its work on the "Sunset Overdrive" video game, which it developed with Microsoft Studios under the working title "Project Spark". The company also developed "Halo 5: Guardians" for the Xbox One, "Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands" for the PlayStation 4, and "Forza Horizon 4" for the Xbox One and Windows 10.
