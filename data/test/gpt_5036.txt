Pinnamaneni Lakshmi Sahithi (born 1993 in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh) is an Indian artist who specializes in sculpture and installation art.  Lakshmi's sculptures are often made from recycled materials and she often incorporates traditional Indian art forms into her work. She has exhibited her work in India, the United States, and Europe. She was the recipient of the 2016 Sahitya Akademi Award in Visual Arts.
