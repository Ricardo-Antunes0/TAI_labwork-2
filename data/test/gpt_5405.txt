The Metcalfe Bond Stores is a heritage-listed retail building at 121-123 Elizabeth Street, Brisbane City, City of Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. It was designed by George Ferguson and built in 1924. It is also known as Metcalfe Bond Store and Metropolitan Stores. It was added to the Queensland Heritage Register on 21 October 1992. It was designed by George Ferguson and built in 1924. It is also known as Metcalfe Bond Store and Metropolitan Stores.  The Metcalfe Bond Stores is a significant example of commercial architecture from the 1920s in Brisbane. The store's design incorporates features typical of Ferguson's work including the use of Classical details and a restrained use of ornamentation. The building has been used for a variety of retail purposes since its construction, and remains an important part of the Elizabeth Street commercial precinct.
