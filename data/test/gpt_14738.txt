Aghia Sofia (, literally Holy Wisdom) is a railway station in the city of Thessaloniki, Macedonia. It serves suburban trains between Thessaloniki and Florina and is also a stop for regional services.  The station was opened on 10 October 1903 by the Ottoman State Railways.
