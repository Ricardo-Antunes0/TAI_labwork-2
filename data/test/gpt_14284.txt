Sugar Trap 2 is the fifth mixtape by American rapper Lil Yachty. It was released on August 18, 2019, by Quality Control Music and Atlantic Records.  The mixtape features guest appearances from Migos, Travis Scott, LIL Uzi Vert, and Playboi Carti. It is further preceded by the release of its lead single, "Minnesota", which peaked at number 47 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100.  The mixtape's production was handled by Yachty himself as well as Metro Boomin, Cardo, and Kuya Beats.  Sugar Trap 2 received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the mixtape received an average score of 71, based on 9 reviews.
