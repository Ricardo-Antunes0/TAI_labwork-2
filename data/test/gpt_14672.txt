Hugo McCloud (born 1980) is an American artist and sculptor.  McCloud was born in 1980 in Berkeley, California. He received his BA from the University of California at Berkeley in 2002, and his MFA from the School of Visual Arts in 2006.  McCloud's art has been exhibited in venues including the Museum of Modern Art, the Carnegie Museum of Art, and the Whitney Museum of American Art. He has also been awarded grants from the Guggenheim Foundation, the Pollock-Krasner Foundation, and the NEA.  McCloud lives and works in New York City.
