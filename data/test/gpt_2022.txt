Gandhinagar Rendava Veedhi  is a 1987 Indian Hindi-language romantic drama film directed by Mahesh Bhatt and produced by his father, P.C. Bhatt. The film stars Sanjay Dutt and Rekha in lead roles with music composed by Sonu Nigam.  The movie tells the story of an arranged marriage between two complete strangers, and their subsequent emotional journey towards understanding and love.
