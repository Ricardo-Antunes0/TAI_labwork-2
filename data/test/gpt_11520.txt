Myfanwy Bekker-Balajadia is a South African artist who has been working in the medium of painting since the early 1990s. She was born in Johannesburg, South Africa in 1965.  Bekker-Balajadia's work often explores the complex and contradictory narratives of apartheid-era South Africa through the lens of memory, history, and personal experience. Her paintings often employ a range of techniques, including watercolor, pen and ink, and collage. She has been the recipient of numerous awards and accolades, including an honorary doctorate from Rhodes University in 2009.  Bekker-Balajadia is currently based in Cape Town, South Africa.
