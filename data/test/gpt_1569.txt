Electronic Arts Victor was a joint venture between Electronic Arts and Victor Company of Japan. The company was founded in 1987 and was headquartered in Redwood City, California.  The company developed and produced video games for platforms such as the Nintendo Entertainment System, Super Nintendo Entertainment System, Game Boy, Game Boy Color, Gamecube, Playstation, Playstation 2, Xbox, Xbox 360 and Microsoft Windows. It also produced arcade games.  Notable games produced by the company include "Super Mario World", "The Sims", "Street Fighter II", "Uncharted", "Battlefield 1", "Mass Effect", "Dragon Age", "Fallout 4" and "Star Wars Battlefront".
