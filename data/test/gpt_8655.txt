Citizen of Glass is the third studio album by American progressive metalcore band Periphery. It was released on September 15, 2016, through Sumerian Records.  The album was recorded at Audiohammer Studios in Florida with producer Kyle Shutt, who has previously worked with bands such as Mastodon, Gojira and Devin Townsend. The album's lyrical themes are inspired by the works of H.P. Lovecraft and feature references to cosmic horror and the Cthulhu Mythos.  "Citizen of Glass" received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's return to their progressive metal roots and the album's overall experimentation. It debuted at number two on the Billboard 200 chart, selling over 50,000 copies in its first week.
