Central Street  is a pedestrian street in Harbin, Heilongjiang province, People's Republic of China. It stretches for about 1.5 kilometers from east to west, and is lined with a mix of old and new buildings. The street is home to a number of restaurants, cafes, and shops.  Central Street is one of the most famous streets in Harbin, and is known for its many antique stores and its lively nightlife. It is also popular with tourists, as it is close to many attractions in the city.
