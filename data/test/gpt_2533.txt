Şerife Bacı (literally: Sister Şerife), aka Şerife the She-Wolf, was a legendary female wolf who was said to live in the forests near the city of Konya in central Turkey.  The Şerife Bacı legend tells of a beautiful woman who was transformed into a wolf by an evil wizard. The woman wander the forests near Konya until she was eventually captured by a shepherd and his dog. The shepherd and his dog took care of her until she died, at which point she was buried in a nearby cave.  The Şerife Bacı legend is said to date back to the Middle Ages. Today, there is still a cave near Konya named after the Şerife Bacı legend.
