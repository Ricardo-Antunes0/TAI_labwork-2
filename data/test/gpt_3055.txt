Mahsun Kırmızıgül (born Abdullah Bazencir, March 26, 1968) is a Turkish singer.  Born in Ankara, Kırmızıgül began singing at an early age and started to gain popularity in Turkey during the early 1990s with her distinctive voice. She has since released numerous albums and singles, including hits such as "Bir Gün Bir Sever", "Seni Sevmeyi Sevemem" and "Güzel Gözlerin". Kırmızıgül has also participated in numerous television shows and concerts throughout her career. In 2006, she was awarded the Golden Butterfly Award for her achievements in the music industry.
