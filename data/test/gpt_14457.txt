Sean Ellis (born 1970) is a British film critic, journalist and author.  Born in London in 1970, Sean Ellis began his film criticism career in the early 1990s writing for a number of small-scale magazines, before landing a regular column on Sight and Sound from 1998-2004. Since then he has written for a variety of publications including Empire, The Guardian and The New York Times. He is also the author of two books on cinema, How to Watch Movies (2004) and The Film Experience (2010).
