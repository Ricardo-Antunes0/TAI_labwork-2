DC Comics, Inc. (or simply just DC) is an American comic book publisher. The company was founded by two teenage boys, Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster, in 1938 as National Periodical Publications. It was renamed DC Comics in 1964.  DC is the largest American comic book publisher and has been for many years. It has a wide range of superhero characters such as Batman, Wonder Woman, the Flash, Aquaman and Green Lantern. DC also publishes a number of children's comics, including "The Big Book of Little Comics", "The Big Book of Best Friends", "Superman Adventures" and "Wonder Woman Adventures".
