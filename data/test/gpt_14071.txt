He studied at the University of Music and Performing Arts in Vienna with Arnold Schoenberg and Joseph Haydn. In 1935, he founded the Vienna Contemporary Music Ensemble, which he directed until 1978. He also taught at the conservatories of Graz and Klagenfurt. Galimir's compositions include orchestral works, chamber music, vocal works and piano pieces. He was awarded the Austrian State Prize in 1967.
