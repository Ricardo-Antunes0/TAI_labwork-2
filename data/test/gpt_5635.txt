John Kennedy O'Connor (born 1964) is a retired Irish professional golfer.  Born in Dublin, O'Connor turned professional in 1986. He won twice on the PGA Tour, including a victory at the 1995 Deutsche Bank Championship. He also won five times on the Web.com Tour, including three times consecutively from 2004 to 2006.
