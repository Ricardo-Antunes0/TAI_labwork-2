Chaitali  is a 1975 Hindi film, produced and directed by B. R. Chopra. The film stars Shashi Kapoor, Rekha, Waheeda Rehman and Pran.  The film is based on the Bengali novel of the same name by Premchand. The story revolves around the love between a man and a woman from two different social backgrounds. It won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Hindi.
