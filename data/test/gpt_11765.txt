55 Cancri d (abbreviated 55 Cnc d), also designated HR 8799, is a star in the constellation Cancer. It has an apparent magnitude of 6.75, making it visible to the naked eye. 55 Cancri d is about 390 light years from Earth.  55 Cancri d is a giant star with a mass about sixty times that of the Sun and a diameter of around 190,000 kilometers. It is radiating 5,000 times as much energy as the Sun from its outer atmosphere at an effective temperature of 4,500 K.
