Lakshya  is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film written and directed by S.S. Rajamouli. It stars N. T. Rama Rao, Jr. and Ramya Krishnan in lead roles. The film is produced by K. Raghavendra Rao under the banner of Sri Venkateswara Creations and Devi Reddy's production house Aascar Films. The soundtrack and the film score were composed by M. M. Keeravani, with lyrics written by Veturi Sundararama Murthy.   The film tells the story of a man (NTR Jr.) who is forced to revisit his past with the help of a woman (Ramya Krishnan) he had loved years ago but had since lost touch with. Together, they search for answers to old mysteries and rediscover the true meaning of love.
