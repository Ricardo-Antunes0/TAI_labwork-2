Grand Hyatt Mumbai is a luxury 5-star hotel located in the heart of Mumbai, India. The hotel has a total of 527 rooms and suites, 8 restaurants and bars, a spa and a fitness center.   Grand Hyatt Mumbai is one of the newest luxury hotels in Mumbai and has been awarded 5 stars by the prestigious Forbes Travel Guide. The hotel offers an ideal location just a few minutes from the business district and the shopping malls of Colaba and Malabar Hill. It is also close to the Royal Bombay Yacht Club and the Gateway of India.   The hotel has been designed with every guest in mind with an exquisite blend of modern facilities and traditional Indian hospitality. All guestrooms are equipped with all the amenities necessary for a comfortable stay including an LCD TV, minibar, safe, satellite/cable TV and Wi-Fi. Some of the highlights of the hotel include its award-winning Spa which offers treatments such as Indian head massage, foot reflexology, herbal steam bath and hydrotherapy.   Grand Hyatt Mumbai is open from Monday to Saturday from 08:00am to 10:00pm and on Sundays from 10:00am to 06:00pm.
