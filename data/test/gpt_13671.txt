Sigrid Onégin (June 1, 1889 – June 5, 1969) was a French actress and singer.  Born in Paris, Sigrid Onégin made her stage debut in 1909. She was particularly noted for her roles in plays by Jean Anouilh and André Antoine. Her most famous role was that of Lola in the 1961 film version of Antoine's play "Lola". She also appeared in a number of films and television shows throughout her career. Onégin died in 1969 at the age of 78.
