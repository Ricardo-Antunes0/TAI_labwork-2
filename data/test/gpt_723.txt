WrestleMania 22 was the 22nd annual WrestleMania professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). The event took place on April 3, 2008, at the Orlando Citrus Bowl in Orlando, Florida.  The main event of the evening was a match for the WWE Championship between John Cena and The Undertaker. Cena had originally won the championship from The Undertaker at WrestleMania 21, but The Undertaker had a rematch clause in his contract and won the title back at WrestleMania 22. In the second match of the evening, Triple H defended the WWE Championship against Chris Jericho. Jericho went into the match as the number one contender, but Triple H retained the title. The third match of the evening was a tag team match between John Morrison and Big Show and Rey Mysterio and Alberto Del Rio. Morrison and Big Show were teaming together for the first time, and Mysterio and Del Rio were teaming up for the first time. Mysterio and Del Rio won the match.   The fourth match of the evening was a battle royal for the vacant WWE Intercontinental Championship. Seven men competed in the match, which was won by Santino Marella. The fifth match of the evening was a tag team match between Edge and Christian and The Hardy Boyz (Matt Hardy and Jeff Hardy). Edge and Christian won the match.
