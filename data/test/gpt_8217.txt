Pan Pan Narkprasert (; born July 22, 1983), better known by his stage name Pangina Heals, is a Thai singer, songwriter and actor.  Pangina was born on July 22, 1983 in Bangkok, Thailand. He started his musical career as a member of the boy band JTB in 2000. The group disbanded in 2002. In 2003, Pangina began his solo career with the release of the hit single "Kao Na".  Pangina has released seven studio albums, one live album, twenty-six singles and ten music videos. His most successful album to date is "Tripitaka" which was released in October 2015 and debuted at number one on the Thai Albums Chart. The album has sold over 200,000 copies.  In 2016, Pangina starred in the Thai drama series "Khao San Road". The series was highly successful and ended with a season finale that aired on December 10th of that year.   Pangina is currently working on his eighth studio album which is set to be released in late 2019.
