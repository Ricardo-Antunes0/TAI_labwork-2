Bad 25 is the 25th anniversary edition of the influential punk rock compilation album "Bad". The album was released on November 18, 2017.  Bad 25 is a compilation album of punk rock songs from various artists who were signed to major record labels during the 1980s. The album was produced by Dave Jerden and released on November 18, 2017, twenty-five years after the original album's release.  The album includes tracks from artists such as The Sex Pistols, The Clash, and The Ramones. Bad 25 has been met with critical acclaim, with many reviewers praising its compilation of old punk tracks.
