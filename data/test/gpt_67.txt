Forrest Gander (born 1956) is an American artist and sculptor.  Forrest Gander was born in 1956 in Pittsfield, Massachusetts. He studied at the Massachusetts College of Art, Boston, and the University of Washington, Seattle.  Gander's work is often described as surrealist or post-surrealist. His sculptures are often made from found objects, and often incorporate elements of mythology and folklore. Gander has exhibited his work internationally, including at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City and the Centre Georges Pompidou in Paris. He currently lives and works in Seattle.
