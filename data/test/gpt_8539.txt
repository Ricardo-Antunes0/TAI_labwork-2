The Forgotten Tales is a compilation album by English rock band Led Zeppelin. It was released on 15 November 1979, by Atlantic Records. The album features acoustic and electric versions of songs from the band's previous three studio albums, as well as newly written material.  The album has been certified platinum by the RIAA.  The album received mixed reviews from critics. Some praised the acoustic and electric versions of the songs, while others found the new material unimpressive. However, it is generally regarded as a weak effort by Led Zeppelin, with some reviewers calling it their worst album.
