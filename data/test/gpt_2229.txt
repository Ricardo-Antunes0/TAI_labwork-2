My Own Lane is the second studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on October 27, 2007, by Big Machine Records. The album produced five singles: "My Everything", "The Last Time", "Fearless", " and "Mine".  Swift promoted the album with a world tour that commenced in the United States in February 2008 and concluded in December 2009. My Own Lane received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Swift's voice. It has sold over 5 million copies in the United States and has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).  Following the release of her debut album, Taylor Swift began work on her second album. She collaborated with producers including Butch Walker, Nathan Chapman, Dan Wilson, and Shellback. Swift wrote or co-wrote all of the songs on My Own Lane. The album's lead single, "My Everything", was released in July 2007 and became a worldwide success, peaking at number one in thirteen countries. The second single, "The Last Time", was also successful, reaching number two in the United States and number one in Australia. Fearless was released as the third single in October 2007 and became Swift's first number-one single in Ireland. Mine was released as the fourth single in February 2008 and reached number two in the United States.
