Ross McLaren (born 24 October 1991) is a New Zealand actor, best known for his roles as Grant Mitchell in the television series Shortland Street and as Josh Lawson in the television series The Fosters.  Ross McLaren was born in Auckland, New Zealand on 24 October 1991. He is best known for his roles as Grant Mitchell in the television series Shortland Street and as Josh Lawson in the television series The Fosters. McLaren has also appeared in the feature films The Other Woman and The Bay.
