A for Prayagraj is 2021 book by R. K. Narayan. It is a story of a man who goes on a spiritual journey to pray at the revered Hindu pilgrimage site of Prayagraj.   The book tells the story of Ramanujan, a mathematician and astronomer who travels to India in search of spiritual enlightenment. He arrives in Prayagraj, the most holy place in Hinduism, and spends days worshiping at the temple of Shiva. Ramanujan discovers that he has a gift for mathematics and soon becomes one of the most brilliant mathematicians in India. However, his quest for spiritual enlightenment is not fulfilled and he eventually returns to his home in Cawnpore. A for Prayagraj is an elegant and thoughtful exploration of one man's journey toward enlightenment.
