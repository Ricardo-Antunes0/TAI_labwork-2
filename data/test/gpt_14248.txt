Tanya's Island is a 1980 Canadian-American fantasy-adventure film directed by Lewis Teague, written by Alvin Schwartz and produced by David L. Wolper. The film stars Tanya Roberts, Dennis Quaid, and Michael Caine.  High school student Tanya (Roberts) is sent to a remote island by her strict, opinionated parents to live with her grand parents, since her mother is traveling abroad. Tanya quickly discovers that the island is haunted, and she must team up with the local boy, Mick (Quaid), to rid the island of the ghosts before they haunt her forever.
