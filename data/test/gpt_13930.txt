The Anniston–Oxford metropolitan statistical area is the most populous metropolitan statistical area (MSA) in the U.S. state of Alabama, with a population of 592,593 as of the 2010 census. The metropolitan area extends outward into DeKalb and Shelby counties, and also includes the city of Tuskegee, which is not part of any MSAs.  The Anniston–Oxford MSA is located in the heart of the Deep South, and includes two of the state's most important economic centers: Anniston and Oxford. The area has a long history as a center of manufacturing, including automobile production and aircraft manufacturing. The area is also home to several universities and a number of military installations, including Fort Rucker and Maxwell Air Force Base.
