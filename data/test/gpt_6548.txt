Christopher Carl Goodnow (born 19 September 1959) is a retired American soccer player who played professionally in the North American Soccer League and Major League Soccer.  Goodnow played college soccer at the University of Virginia, where he was an All-American and College Player of the Year in 1978. He was drafted by the Seattle Sounders in the first round of the 1979 American Soccer League Draft, but did not sign with them. He signed with the San Diego Sockers of the North American Soccer League in 1979. He also played for the Philadelphia Atoms and Tulsa Roughnecks in the league. Goodnow played six seasons in MLS with the Los Angeles Aztecs, Colorado Rapids, Tampa Bay Mutiny and Dallas Burn. He finished his career with a total of 202 appearances and 45 goals.
