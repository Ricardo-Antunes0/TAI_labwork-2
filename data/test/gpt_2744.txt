Danie Mellor (born 13 April 1971) is an English professional footballer who played as a midfielder. He is currently manager of Eastbourne Borough.  Mellor, who started his career at Crystal Palace, made over 200 appearances in the Football League and Premier League, before having a four-year spell in the Scottish Premier League with Rangers. He also had a three-year spell in the Greek Superleague with AEK Athens.  Mellor's managerial career began with Eastbourne Borough in October 2016. In his first season in charge, Mellor led the club to the National League South play-offs, where they were defeated by Oxford United.
