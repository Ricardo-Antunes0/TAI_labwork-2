"Awakening" is the second single from Switchfoot's fourth studio album, "The Beautiful Letdown". The song was written by Jon Foreman, Chad Butler and Drew Shirley, and produced by Switchfoot. It was released to radio on October 5, 2012.  "Awakening" is a ballad about the protagonist's rebirth after a dark period in their life. The song is written from the point of view of a person who has lost hope and is facing an uncertain future. "Awakening" features acoustic guitars and piano and is slower-paced than the band's previous singles.  The song received positive reviews from music critics, who praised its emotional content and the band's stripped-back production. It debuted at number 36 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart, becoming Switchfoot's first single not to hit the top 20 in the United States. It also reached number two on the Rock Airplay chart and number one on Alternative Songs chart. The song has since been certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).  "Awakening" was written by Jon Foreman, Chad Butler and Drew Shirley, with production being handled by Switchfoot. It was recorded at Sound City Studios in Hollywood, California with producer Butch Vig. The song was mixed by Dave Schiffman at Westlake Recording Studios in West Hollywood.
