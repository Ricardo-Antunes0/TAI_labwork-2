Nevryo (or Neureo) is a sweet dumpling-shaped pastry from the Republic of Georgia. The dough is made from a combination of flour, sugar, butter and eggs, and is then filled with a variety of sweet or savory fillings. Nevryo are often sold by street vendors or in small stores.
