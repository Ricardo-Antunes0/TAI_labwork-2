{{Infobox station | name = Congress Heights |style=WMATA | image = Congress Heights station.jpg | caption = Congress Heights station is a Metrobus and Metro Subway stop in Washington, D.C. |location=Washington, D.C. | services = Metrobus and Metro Subway }}  Congress Heights is a Metrobus and Metro Subway stop in Washington, D.C. The station is located on the Red Line, and serves the Congress Heights neighborhood. The station opened on January 12, 1984, as one of the original stops on the Red Line.
