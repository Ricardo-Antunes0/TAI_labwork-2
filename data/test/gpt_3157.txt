German submarine U971 was a Type VIIC U-boat of Nazi Germany's Kriegsmarine during World War II.  German submarine U-971 was laid down on 2 October 1942 at the Blohm & Voss yard in Hamburg, launched on 3 January 1943, and commissioned on 6 March 1943 under the command of Korvettenkapitän Heinz-Wilhelm Müller.  U-971 served with the 5th U-boat Flotilla from its commissioning until its loss in May 1945. The boat completed five patrols and sank six ships, totaling 62,000 tons. U-971 was lost in May 1945 in the North Atlantic Ocean after being attacked by a British aircraft.  U-971 was a Type VIIC U-boat, built by Blohm & Voss.
