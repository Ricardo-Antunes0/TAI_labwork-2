"Kirinyaga" is a science fiction short story by Kenyan author Ngugi wa Thiong'o. Published in 1966, it tells the story of a group of settlers who are sent to an alien planet in order to mine its resources, only to find that they have been sent there by mistake.  The story has been praised for its complex plot and its exploration of themes such as colonialism and xenophobia. It has also been cited as an important precursor to post-colonial literature.
