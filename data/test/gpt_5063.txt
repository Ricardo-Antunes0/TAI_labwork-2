The Bisri Dam is a planned dam on the Bisri River in Sarlahi district of the Indian state of Bihar. The dam is planned to provide water for irrigation, power generation, and flood control. Construction on the dam began in 2009 and was scheduled to be completed in 2014. However, the dam has been delayed due to environmental and social protests. As of 2019, the dam has not been completed.
