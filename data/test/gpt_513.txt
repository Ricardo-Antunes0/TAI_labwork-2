Beijing Spring is a 2021 American documentary film directed and produced by Alexandra Pelosi. The film examines the 1989 Tiananmen Square protests and its subsequent suppression by the Communist Party of China.  The film premiered at the 2019 Sundance Film Festival, where it won the U.S. Documentary Special Jury Prize. It was subsequently acquired by IFC Films and released in theaters on July 12, 2021.
