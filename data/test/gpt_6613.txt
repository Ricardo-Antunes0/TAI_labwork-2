Jarcrew was a five-piece dance-punk band from Glasgow, Scotland. Formed in 2006, the band released two full-length albums before splitting up in 2009. The band's sound was heavily influenced by post-punk and new wave, with a strong focus on guitar and bass lines. Jarcrew toured extensively throughout their career, playing at festivals such as T in the Park and Glastonbury as well as headlining their own tours.
