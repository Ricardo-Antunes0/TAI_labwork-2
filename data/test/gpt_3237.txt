Nils G. Walter, Dr. Ing., is the Founder and CEO of Walter Energy, a privately held oil and gas company headquartered in Houston, Texas. Walter Energy has been involved in the exploration and development of oil and gas properties in the United States since 1989.  Dr. Ing. Nils G. Walter is a founding member of the Association for the Study of Peak Oil and Gas (ASPO), the world's leading think tank dedicated to understanding the physical, economic, and social implications of peak oil and gas. He is also a member of the American Institute of Mining, Metallurgy and Petroleum Engineers (AIMMPE), the Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), and the American Geophysical Union (AGU).  Walter Energy was founded in 1989 by Dr. Nils G. Walter with a focus on developing unconventional oil and gas reserves in North America. The company is headquartered in Houston, Texas, with operations in Oklahoma, Texas, Colorado, Wyoming, New Mexico, Louisiana, and West Virginia.
