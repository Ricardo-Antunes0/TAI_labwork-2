Martin Roe (born 1 April 1992) is an English footballer who plays as a striker for Championship club Leeds United.  Roe began his career with Coventry City, making his first-team debut in February 2010. He spent time on loan at Cheltenham Town and Huddersfield Town, before joining Rotherham United on a permanent basis in January 2013. He moved to Leeds United in July 2016.  Roe has represented England at under-16, under-17, under-18 and under-19 levels, scoring five goals in eleven appearances.
