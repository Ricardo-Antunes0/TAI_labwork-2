The Guadiana International Bridge  is a bridge that crosses the Guadiana River in Spain. It is the longest bridge in Europe, having a total length of 17.8 km. The bridge is also the widest one, with a width of 9.4 km. It is the only crossing route between Portugal and Spain, and it is also a major transportation link between the two countries. The bridge was inaugurated on 1 July 2004.
