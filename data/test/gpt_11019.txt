El Progrés is a coastal area of Badalona, Catalonia, Spain. It stretches from the sea to the hills and has a population of around 26,000 people.  El Progrés is a popular tourist destination for its stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea, as well as its beaches, promenades and shopping districts. The area has a long history, dating back to Roman times. Today, El Progrés is home to a variety of businesses and cultural attractions, including a casino, a theater and a number of museums.
