Taylor Lorenz (born  1979–1987) is an American photographer and artist. Lorenz's photographs depict a wide range of subjects, from landscapes to intimate portraits, and often explore the interplay of light and dark.  Lorenz was born in 1979 in Pawtucket, Rhode Island, to Janet (née Weinreb) and Charles Lorenz. She has two sisters, Allison and Allison Lorenz. Her family moved to the Boston area when she was young. She graduated from Newton South High School in 1997. After high school, Lorenz studied photography at the School of Visual Arts in New York City.  Lorenz's first exhibition was at the Eyebeam Art Center in New York City in 2002. Her work has been exhibited at the Museum of Fine Arts Boston; the Rhode Island School of Design Museum; the Center for Creative Photography, Rochester; and the Solomon R Guggenheim Museum in New York City. In 2015, she was the recipient of a MacArthur Fellowship.
