OpiumRoz (, translated: Opium of Roses) is a compilation album by Iranian singer-songwriter Sia Furler, released on 17 October 2018 by Concord Music Group. The album is a tribute to Furler's grandmother, Rose, who died in 2017.  "OpiumRoz" is a tribute to Sia Furler's grandmother, Rose, who died in 2017. The album features covers of songs written about or inspired by Rose, including tracks by artists such as Taylor Swift, Adele, and Lorde.  Sia Furler collaborated with producers including Greg Kurstin and John Hill, who had worked on her previous albums "This Is Acting" (2014) and "Cheap Thrills" (2016).   The album was met with critical acclaim upon release. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 83, based on 16 reviews.  The album was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Compilation Soundtrack for Visual Media at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards.
