Mika Kares (born 1 November 1978) is a Finnish actress, singer and songwriter. She has appeared in a number of theatrical productions, as well as several television shows and films.  Kares was born in Pori, Finland, the daughter of actors Jaakko and Riikka Kares. She attended the Sibelius Academy of Music, where she studied voice. Kares made her theatrical debut in 2001, appearing in the play "The Catcher in the Rye". She has since appeared in plays such as "Romeo and Juliet", "The Merchant of Venice", "The Glass Menagerie" and "A Streetcar Named Desire".
