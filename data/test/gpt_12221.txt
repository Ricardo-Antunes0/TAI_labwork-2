The Model 1200 and Model 1300 are a series of bolt-action rifles produced by Winchester Repeating Arms Company. The 1200 was first produced in 1948, and the 1300 was first produced in 1953. The 1200 is a rebarreled version of the Model 98 Winchester rifle. The 1300 is a lightweight, stainless-steel variant of the Model 1200.
