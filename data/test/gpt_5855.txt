Naib Subedar Jinson Johnson (born 15 March 1951) is a retired Indian Army officer who was awarded the Padma Bhushan, the third-highest civilian award, in 2003.  Jinson Johnson was born on 15 March 1951 in the town of Sultanpur Lodhi in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. He attended the Rajputana Military School and subsequently the National Defence Academy, graduating in 1971 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering.  He enlisted in the Indian Army that year and served for nearly three decades, including a stint as an instructor at the Indian Military Academy. He was commissioned as a Subedar in 1973 and served in various positions, including command of a battalion and brigade, before retiring from active service in 2003.  Johnson has been awarded several medals and commendations, including the Vishisht Seva Medal, the Param Vishisht Seva Medal, and the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal. He is also a recipient of the Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award.
