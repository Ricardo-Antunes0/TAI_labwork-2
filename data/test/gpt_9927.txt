Tarazu  is an Indian action thriller film directed by debutant Gopichand Malineni and produced by B.R. Mani. The film stars Suresh Gopi, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Mohanlal in the lead roles. The music is composed by A. R. Rahman. The story is about a man who has to fight for his life after being kidnapped by a group of terrorists.
