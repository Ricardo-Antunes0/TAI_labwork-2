The Wolleson–Nicewander Building, also known as the Wolleson Building, is a historic building located in the village of Wolleson, in Hamilton County, Ohio. It was built in 1887, and is a three-story, rectangular, brick building with a gable roof. It features an eclectic mix of Romanesque and Gothic Revival style elements.  The Wolleson–Nicewander Building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
