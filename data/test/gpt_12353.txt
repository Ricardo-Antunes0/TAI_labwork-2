Logan McDonald (born 4 April 2002) is an Australian tennis player.  Logan McDonald was born on 4 April 2002 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. He started playing tennis at the age of six and progressed through the junior ranks. In 2018, he qualified for the Australian Open boys' singles event where he reached the second round.   In 2019, Logan McDonald won his first professional singles title at the ATP Challenger Tour event in Darwin, beating compatriot Noah Rubin in the final.
