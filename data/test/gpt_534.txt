Cancer Science is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal published by the American Association for Cancer Research. It covers all aspects of cancer research, including basic science, clinical trials, epidemiology, molecular biology, and pathology.  Cancer Science was established in 1951 as Cancer Research. At that time, cancer was considered a rare disease. Over the years, cancer has become more common and complex. Today, cancer is the leading cause of death in the United States. However, with advances in research and treatment, there is hope for a cure.  Cancer Science is the official journal of the American Association for Cancer Research. It is published monthly and contains original research articles as well as reviews of recent advances in cancer therapy and research.
