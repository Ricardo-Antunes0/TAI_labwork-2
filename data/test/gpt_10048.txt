Cara Dune is a fictional character in the    Frank Herbert Dune series of science fiction novels by the author. She is a female Fremen who is the daughter of Stilgar and the sister of Paul Atreides.  Cara Dune is introduced in the first novel, Dune, as the concubine of Paul Atreides, and she later becomes his wife. She is a skilled Fremen warrior and political leader, and her influence over her tribe is significant. She is killed in the final novel, Children of Dune, by Duncan Idaho, a member of the Harkonnen family.
