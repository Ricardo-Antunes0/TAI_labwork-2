Laurence M. Narag, Sr. was an enlisted man in the United States Navy during World War II. After the war, he became a successful advertising executive. He is best known for his work as president and CEO of Doyle Dane Bernbach, one of the leading advertising agencies in the United States.  Laurence Narag, Sr. was born on October 25, 1918, in Inglewood, California. He enlisted in the United States Navy during World War II, and served in the Pacific theater. After the war, he became a successful advertising executive. He is best known for his work as president and CEO of Doyle Dane Bernbach, one of the leading advertising agencies in the United States. Narag played an important role in developing American advertising into what it is today. He was inducted into the Advertising Hall of Fame in 1984. Narag died on September 21, 2005, at the age of 89.
