Anubias is a genus of aquatic and semiaquatic plants in the family Droseraceae. The plants are tropical andgrown in water with a low to moderate flow.  This genus is monotypic, containing only the single species Anubias nana. It is a perennial plant that grows up to 50 cm tall and has a cylindrical stem that may be branched. The leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, are ovate to elliptical in shape, and measure up to 10 cm long and 5 cm wide. The flowers are white and borne in terminal clusters at the ends of the stems.
