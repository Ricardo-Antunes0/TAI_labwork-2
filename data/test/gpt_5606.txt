Powertrain Ltd was a British company based in Swindon, Wiltshire that specialized in the design, manufacture and supply of automotive powertrains. The company was founded in 1984 and ceased trading in October 2014.  Powertrain Ltd was a leading supplier of automotive powertrains, specialising in diesel and petrol engines, transmissions and axles. The company’s products were used in a wide range of vehicles, including passenger cars, light commercial vehicles and heavy commercial vehicles.  Powertrain Ltd was founded in 1984 by David Higgs and Chris Naylor. The company began operations with a focus on the design and manufacture of diesel engines for the UK market. Over the years, the company has expanded its product range to include petrol engines, transmissions and axles. Powertrain Ltd was a leading supplier of automotive powertrains to the UK market. In 2013, the company had a workforce of around 120 people.  In October 2014, Powertrain Ltd ceased trading due to financial difficulties. The company’s assets were sold off in an auction later that year.
