He is best known for his time in World Championship Wrestling (WCW), where he is a two-time World Heavyweight Champion and one-time United States Heavyweight Champion. Adams also competed in Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW), where he is a two-time ECW World Heavyweight Champion.  Adams was born in Odessa, Texas, and grew up in Amarillo, Texas. He started wrestling at the age of eight and later became a amateur wrestler for the United States Naval Academy. He then went on to wrestle for various colleges and teams before signing with World Championship Wrestling (WCW) in 1990. Adams made his professional wrestling debut the same year as a preliminary wrestler and made his television debut on the August 7 episode of "World War 3".  Adams first gained prominence in WCW as a member of the nWo stable, which was headed by Kevin Nash. The nWo feuded with the American Wrestling Association (AWA) throughout most of 1991 and Adams was part of the team that won the AWA World Tag Team Championship from The Giant Baba and Stan Hansen on January 6, 1992. The nWo then feuded with the World Wildlife Federation (WWF) throughout most of 1992 and Adams feuded with WWF World Heavyweight Champion Hulk Hogan throughout that year.
