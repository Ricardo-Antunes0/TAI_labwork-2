Owen Wright is an American musician best known as the lead singer and guitarist of the rock band The Paper Kites. Wright has also released two solo albums.  Wright was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, on October 20, 1984. He began playing guitar at the age of eleven and started his own band, The Paper Kites, in 2002. The Paper Kites released their debut album, "The Great Eastern", in 2006 and followed it up with "The Great Western" in 2009. Wright's solo album, "The Last Great American Dream", was released in 2011.
