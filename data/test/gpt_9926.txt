Burkett Restaurant Equipment & Supplies is a family owned and operated business since 1975. We are a full-service restaurant equipment and supplies store with a focus on quality and customer service. We carry an extensive inventory of restaurant supplies, including cookware, bakeware, cutting boards, grills, ovens, refrigerators and freezers, dishwashers, kitchen furniture, and more.   We also offer complete restaurant services including installation, repair, and maintenance. We are a knowledgeable source for advice on all aspects of running a successful restaurant. Our team is available to help you find the right equipment for your business, solve any problems you may be experiencing, and provide support during your restaurant's growth.
