KDTH (1370 AM) is an AM radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. Licensed to Duluth, Minnesota, the station is currently owned by Cumulus Media and features programming from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.  KDTH was founded in 1922 by the Duluth News Tribune as KQDL. The station moved to its present frequency in 1949 and was renamed KDTH. It was acquired by CBS in 1995.
