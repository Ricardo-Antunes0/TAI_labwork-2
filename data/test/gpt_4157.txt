Francois "Faf" du Plessis ( ; born 3 December 1981) is a South African cricketer who is currently the captain of the South Africa national cricket team. He has also played for the Cape Town Blitz, Royal Challengers Bangalore, Mumbai Indians and Kings XI Punjab in Indian Premier League (IPL).  Du Plessis was born in Cape Town, South Africa. He made his international debut for South Africa against Zimbabwe in February 2004. Du Plessis has since made over 150 appearances for his country, scoring over 10,000 runs and taking over 1,000 wickets. In 2011, Du Plessis was named captain of South Africa. He led the team to the final of the 2015 ICC World Cup, where they lost to Australia by eight wickets.
