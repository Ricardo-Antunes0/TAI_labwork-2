Paula DeAnda is the debut album by American singer-songwriter Paula DeAnda. It was released on September 21, 2009, by Interscope Records. The album was produced by longtime collaborator Rick Rubin, and recorded between 2009 and 2010 at various studios in Los Angeles and New York City.  The album debuted at number nine on the "Billboard" 200 chart, selling 34,000 copies in its first week. It has sold 158,000 copies in the United States as of August 2013.  The album received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised DeAnda's debut vocal performance and the lyrical content of the songs. At the 52nd Annual Grammy Awards, "Paula DeAnda" won the Grammy Award for Best New Artist.
