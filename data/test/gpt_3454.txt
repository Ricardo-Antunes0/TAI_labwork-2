"Gotta Get Over" is a pop rock song written by Bob Seger and recorded by him in 1976. The song reached number nine on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart. It was also a number-one hit in Canada and Australia.  The song is about overcoming a difficult situation. Seger sings about wanting to get over the person he is upset with, whether it be an ex-girlfriend or a difficult past experience. The lyrics describe how he is going to work hard to move on and be successful.
