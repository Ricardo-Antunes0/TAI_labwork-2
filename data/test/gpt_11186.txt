Ugu is a town and a nagar panchayat in Bijnor district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.  Ugu is located at . It has an average elevation of 243 metres (796 feet).   India census, Ugu had a population of 10,871. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Ugu has an average literacy rate of 63%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 74%, and female literacy is 54%. In Ugu, 17% of the population is under 6 years of age.
