Kieran T. Block (born June 14, 1985) is an American actor and writer. He is known for his roles as Roger in the Fox comedy-drama series New Girl, and as Jake Peralta on the Netflix series Brooklyn Nine-Nine.  Block was born in Worcester, Massachusetts, to English professor parents. He has two sisters. After graduating from high school, he studied at the University of California, Santa Cruz before transferring to New York University's Tisch School of the Arts, where he graduated with a BFA in Drama in 2007.  Block's first acting role was in a production of The Catcher in the Rye at Santa Cruz High School. After graduation from NYU, he moved to Los Angeles and began appearing in television and film roles. In 2009, he appeared in the short film The Interviewer alongside Maria Bello and James Franco. That same year, he had a recurring role on HBO's series Entourage as Johnny Petralito. In 2010, he appeared in the independent films The Spectacular Now and What to Expect When You're Expecting.   In 2011, Block was cast as Roger on the Fox comedy-drama series New Girl.
