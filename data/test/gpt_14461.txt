Renishaw plc is a British engineering company headquartered in Sheffield, England. It has operations in the UK, Europe, the Middle East and North Africa.  Renishaw was founded in Sheffield in 1853 by two local businessmen, James Renishaw and William Pile. The company initially specialised in engineering tools and dies, but has since expanded its product range to encompass a wide range of manufacturing and engineering services. Renishaw is one of the UK's leading technology companies, with a strong focus on innovative products and services for the automotive, aerospace, defence and medical industries.
