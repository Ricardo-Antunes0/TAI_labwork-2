Krzysztof Pietroszek is a Polish/Canadian/American filmmaker and artist.  Krzysztof Pietroszek is a Polish Canadian American filmmaker and artist who has been working in the film and video arts since the early 1990s. He has been involved in a wide range of experimental, narrative, and documentary projects, as well as audio works, installations, and public art projects. His work has been exhibited at venues around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art (New York), the Centre Georges Pompidou (Paris), and the Whitney Museum of American Art (New York). He was awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship in 2006.
