Undead and Unwed is a paranormal romance novel written by American author L.J. Smith. The novel was published on September 12, 2009 by HarperCollins Publishers.  The novel tells the story of Sookie Stackhouse, a telepathic waitress in Bon Temps, Louisiana, and Bill Compton, a vampire who works for the Louisiana State Police. The two fall in love and are married in a human ceremony in Las Vegas. However, after returning to Bon Temps, they find that their marriage is not accepted by either their families or the community. They also find that vampires and werewolves are coming down with a mysterious illness. Sookie and Bill must find out what is causing the sickness and solve the mystery before it destroys them and all of Bon Temps.
