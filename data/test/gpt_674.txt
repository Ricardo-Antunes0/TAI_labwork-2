Brian D. Beaudreault (born 1960) is a Canadian artist who specializes in oil painting and mixed media.  Brian Beaudreault was born in Montreal, Quebec in 1960. He received his BFA from Concordia University in 1982 and his MFA from the University of Ottawa in 1986. Beaudreault has exhibited extensively across Canada, the United States, Europe, and Asia. His paintings explore the tensions and contradictions within human experience, often utilizing references to mythology, history, and literature.
