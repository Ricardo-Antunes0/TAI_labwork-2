"Typical" is the debut single from New Zealand singer-songwriter Kimbra. The song was released on 2 October 2012 as the lead single from her debut studio album, Vows.  "Typical" is a pop song written by Kimbra and produced by Greg Kurstin. The song was recorded in Los Angeles and London and was mixed by Dave site, who has worked with artists such as Pink, Katy Perry and Justin Timberlake. Lyrically, the song is about a break-up, and its accompanying video was shot in Los Angeles.  The song received generally positive reviews from music critics. They praised the production and Kimbra's vocals, but criticised the lyrics for being too similar to songs by other artists. "Typical" peaked at number four on the New Zealand Singles Chart and has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of New Zealand. It has been performed on television shows such as The X Factor UK and America's Got Talent and featured in commercials for L'Oreal Paris and Beats by Dre.  Kimbra was born in Wellington, New Zealand to parents of Sri Lankan descent. She began performing at open mic nights when she was just 15 years old, and eventually signed to Universal Music Group's RED Records in 2011. That year, she released her first extended play, Uncomfortable, which featured the singles "Settle Down" and "Can't Get You Out of My Head".
