CityScape Phoenix is a high-rise mixed-use development in Phoenix, Arizona. The centerpiece of the development is a 1,200-foot (366 m) tower with 62 floors and 478 units.   CityScape Phoenix was developed by The Ritz-Carlton Corporation and is located in the heart of downtown Phoenix. It is adjacent to the Phoenix Convention Center and adjacent to the new Chase Field, home of the Arizona Diamondbacks baseball team.
