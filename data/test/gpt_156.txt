IFK Ystad HK is a Swedish handball club from Ystad. The club was founded on 12 October 1946, and currently plays in the Swedish top division Handball League.  The club's home arena is the Ystad Arena. IFK Ystad HK has participated in the Swedish top division Handball League since the inaugural season of 1957/58. The club has won two Swedish Championships (1977, 1996) and one Swedish Cup (1976). IFK Ystad HK also competed in the European Champions Cup twice, reaching the quarter-finals in 1993 and 1995.
