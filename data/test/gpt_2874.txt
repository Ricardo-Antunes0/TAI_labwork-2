Katalin Kovács (born 29 February 1976) is a Hungarian actress, model and television personality.  Katalin Kovács was born in Budapest, Hungary on 29 February 1976. She is of Hungarian and Slovak descent.   Katalin started her career as a model in the early 1990s. She began her acting career in 1997, appearing in various television series and movies. She became well-known for her role as Júlia in the TV series "Álom és változás" (Love and Change), for which she received a Golden Globe Award nomination. In 2007, she starred in the movie "Vendég".   In 2009, she was a contestant on the Hungarian version of "Dancing on Ice". She finished fourth. Kovács later returned to television in 2012 as a judge on the show "X Factor Hungary". In 2018 she starred in the series "A jó élet" (A Good Life).
