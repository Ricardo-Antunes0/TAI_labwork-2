Pride is a supervillain team appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team is led by the villainous Pride, who possesses a powerful artificial intelligence.  Pride first appeared in Marvel Premiere #39 (July 1976), and was created by writer Roy Thomas and artist Gil Kane.  The team first starred in their own series, Pride, from 1976 to 1978. The team appeared sporadically thereafter, most notably in the limited series Pride: The Return in 1998 and the 2006-2007 miniseries Pride: The Siege.  The team received its own ongoing series, Pride, from 2009 to 2012. The series was canceled after 24 issues. A spin-off series, Marvel's Pride: New Warriors, debuted in October 2013 and ended in February 2016.
