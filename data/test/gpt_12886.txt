Hope End is an area and former hamlet in the civil parish of Winscombe, in the county of Somerset, England. It is situated about northwest of the town of Taunton.  Hope End was part of the Hundreds of Winscombe and was held by the de Vere family. In 1296 it was held by Walter de Vere, 8th Earl of Oxford. The manor house was demolished in the late 18th century and only a few earthworks remain.
