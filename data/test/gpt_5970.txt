Lim Jae-beom (Hangul: 임재범; born January 6, 1986), better known by his stage name Jay B, is a South Korean singer and actor. He is best known as a member of the K-pop boy band Big Bang, and as a member of the Korean variety show Running Man.  Jay B was born in Seoul, South Korea on January 6, 1986. He started his career as a member of the Korean boy band Big Bang, with fellow members G-Dragon, T.O.P, Taeyang and Seungri. The group debuted in 2006 with the single "Lies." In 2009, they released their first album "Big Bang" and won the Mnet Asian Music Awards for Best New Artist. In 2012, they released their second album "MADE," which peaked at number one on the Gaon Album Chart. In 2013, they released their third album "The Best of Big Bang," which peaked at number one on the Gaon Album Chart. In 2014, they released their fourth album "Bang Bang." That year they also made their acting debut in the television series Reply 1997. In 2016, they released their fifth album "Made II."
