Berta Rojas (born 23 September 1966) is a Chilean actress. She started her career in the late 1980s, appearing in various theatre and television productions. She achieved wider recognition for her performances in the telenovelas La Reina del Sur (2006-2007) and Amor Prohibido (2009-2010). In 2013 she starred in the drama film Los Últimos Días de Amarres.
