Alessandro Nunziati (born 16 September 1972), better known as Nunziato, is an Italian retired footballer who played as a midfielder.  Nunziato began his career with Serie C1 side Padova before moving to Serie A side Lazio. He then spent two seasons with Serie B side Pescara, before joining Serie A side Fiorentina in 1997. He spent six years with Fiorentina, scoring 25 goals in 182 appearances. He then signed for Serie A club Cagliari in 2003, but lasted only one season before retiring at the age of 34 due to injury.  Nunziati made 14 appearances for the Italy national football team, including three caps during the 1994 FIFA World Cup.
