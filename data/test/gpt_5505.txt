200 Cartas (released worldwide as Looking for Eric) is a 2005 Spanish-language drama film directed by Carlos Saura. It tells the story of a love affair between an older man and a young woman.  The film premiered at the 2005 Cannes Film Festival, where it won the prestigious Palme d'Or. It was also nominated for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.  200 Cartas was generally well-received by film critics. Rotten Tomatoes has a 73% rating, based on 35 reviews, with an average rating of 7/10. The site's critical consensus reads, "An affecting and visually stunning tale of love and loss, Carlos Saura's 200 Cartas is a powerful drama." Metacritic gives the film a score of 71 out of 100, based on 23 reviews, indicating "generally favorable reviews".
