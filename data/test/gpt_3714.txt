Eitan Tadmor (born May 4, 1954) is an Israeli author, memoirist, and journalist.  Tadmor was born in Tel Aviv, Israel. He studied journalism at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. He has worked as a journalist for "Haaretz", "The Jerusalem Post", and "The New York Times". His memoir, "A Tale of Love and Darkness: My Journey from Jerusalem to New York" was published in English in 2007. It tells the story of his relationship with his wife, the artist Nira Park, and their daughter, Liora.
