Isabel Margaret Dedring (born 10 August 1971) is a British philosopher. Dedring is a professor of philosophy at the University of Cambridge, where she also serves as a Fellow of Churchill College. Dedring's primary area of research is the philosophy of mind, particularly philosophical theories of intentionality and reference.  Dedring was born in London, England, to an English mother and a Swiss father. She was educated at the University of Oxford, where she obtained her Bachelor of Arts degree in 1993 and her Master of Arts degree in 1995. She then went on to study at the University of Cambridge, where she obtained her Doctor of Philosophy degree in 2002.
