Stella O'Malley is an Irish psychotherapist and writer.  Stella O'Malley was born in County Cork, Ireland in 1957. She has a degree in psychology from University College Cork and a postgraduate diploma in psychotherapy from the Dublin Institute of Mental Health. O'Malley worked as a psychotherapist for over twenty years before she started to write. Her first book, How to Be a Happy Camper, was published in 2006. She has since written three more books: The Perfect Dramatist: A Life of Theatre (2009), Stella O'Malley's Wild Ride (2011), and The Wisdom of Your Wounds: A Guide to Healing from Trauma (2013).
