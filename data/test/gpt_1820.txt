"Lose Yourself" is a song by American rapper Eminem from his album Recovery. The song was written by Eminem, Scott Storch and Dr. Dre.  "Lose Yourself" is a ballad about the protagonist confronting his fears and weaknesses, and coming to terms with who he is. The song was well received by music critics, who praised its emotional depth and lyrical clarity. "Lose Yourself" reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming Eminem's eighth number-one single on the chart. It also became his first song to top the UK Singles Chart, where it stayed at number one for seven consecutive weeks. The song has been certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for sales of over two million copies in the United States.  The accompanying music video for "Lose Yourself" was directed by Joseph Kahn and released on September 25, 2010. The video portrays Eminem as a washed-up actor who is struggling to stay afloat in a life that no longer suits him. It has been widely acclaimed for its visual effects and use of motion capture technology.
