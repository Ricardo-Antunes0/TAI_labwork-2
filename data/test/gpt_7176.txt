Mean Old Man is the 40th studio album by American rock band Stone Temple Pilots. It was released on October 6, 2019, through Atlantic Records.  The album was preceded by the release of the single "No One Knows" on August 10, 2019.  The album was produced by Brendan O'Brien and features appearances from Chris Cornell, Chester Bennington, and Scott Weiland.   The title track is a tribute to late Soundgarden frontman Chris Cornell.   Stone Temple Pilots announced the album's release date and title on September 12, 2019. The band also released the album's second single "No One Knows".   "Mean Old Man" was met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 71, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".
