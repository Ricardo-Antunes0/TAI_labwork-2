BoardGameGeek (BGG) is an online forum for board games, with over 275,000 registered users. BGG is a popular resource for board game fans, with information on over 10,000 games.  BoardGameGeek was founded in 1997 by John Nesterak and Dave Haynie. The site originally served as a forum for discussing and rating board games, with a database of information on over 2,500 games. In 2002, the site was purchased by Fygle Games, who continued to operate and expand the site. In 2013, the site was sold to Geeknet Inc., who continues to operate and expand it.
