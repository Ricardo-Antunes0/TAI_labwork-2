Insane Poetry is an American hip hop collective, consisting of rappers MF DOOM, Madvillain, and MF DOOM's brother Evidence. The group's name is derived from the 1973 horror film Insane.  Insane Poetry was formed in 1994, in the city of Detroit, Michigan. The group consists of MF DOOM (born Maurice Fitzgerald), Madvillain (born Madlib), and Evidence (born Leroy Jenkins). The trio is often credited with helping to revive the hip hop genre in the early 2000s, and have released four albums together: Madvillainy (2000), The Mouse & The Mask (2002), Hell Hath No Fury (2004), and King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard (2017).
