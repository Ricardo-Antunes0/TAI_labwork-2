The Totskoye nuclear exercise was a military exercise conducted by the Russian military in Totskoye, Rostov Oblast, from 10 to 19 June 2017.  The exercise was part of the large Zapad 2017 military drill, which involved tens of thousands of troops from over 30 countries. The exercise focused on the scenarios of a full-scale conflict with NATO forces in the Baltic region, as well as a conflict with China in the Far East.
