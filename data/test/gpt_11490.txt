Olayinka Olusola Omigbodun is the first Nigerian woman to be ordained a priest in the Catholic Church.  Olayinka Olusola Omigbodun was born on 5 October 1964 in Lagos, Nigeria. She is the first Nigerian woman to be ordained a priest in the Catholic Church. She was ordained a priest on 14 July 2004 by Cardinal Francis Arinze, Prefect of the Congregation for Divine Worship and the Discipline of the Sacraments.
