Acentronura breviperula, also known as the shortpouch centronure or the brevipouch centronure, is a species of centronure in the family Centronuridae. It is found in North America.  The shortpouch centronure is a small centronure found in the Western United States and Canada. It has a long snout and stout body. The background color of the body is light gray or tan, with black markings on the head and around the eyes. The shortpouch centronure feeds on small invertebrates, including crustaceans and insects.
