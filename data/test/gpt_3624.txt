Hello is a 1999 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film directed by S. Shankar and written by Ilaiyaraaja. The film stars Ajith Kumar, Aishwarya Rai and Jeeva in the lead roles. The music was composed by Ilaiyaraaja.  The film begins with two people who are strangers meeting each other and falling in love. They keep meeting each other and the love grows stronger. The film ends with the two people getting married and living a happy life together.
