The 2021 Giro d'Italia was the 104th edition of the Giro d'Italia, one of cycling's Grand Tours. The race started on 6 May in Verona, Italy, and finished on 2 June in Rome, Italy. The race was won by Dutch rider Tom Dumoulin of Team Sunweb.  The race was won by Dutch rider Tom Dumoulin of Team Sunweb.
