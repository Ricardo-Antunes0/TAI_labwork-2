"Xianü"  is a short story by Chinese author Mo Yan. Published in 1985, it tells the story of a woman who is raped and becomes pregnant as a result, and the difficulties she faces in trying to deal with her situation.  Xianü is a woman who has been raped and becomes pregnant as a result. She is faced with many difficulties in trying to deal with her situation, including social stigma and her own guilt. Despite these challenges, Xianü retains her dignity and perseveres through her experiences.
