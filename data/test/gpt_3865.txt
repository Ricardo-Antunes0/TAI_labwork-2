GospoCentric Records was an Inglewood, California based record label that specialized in gospel music. It was founded by brothers Rodney and Milton Green in 1978. The label released albums by such artists as the Winans, Toni Braxton, and Faith Hill. In 1997, GospoCentric merged with Elektra Records.
