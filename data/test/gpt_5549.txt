Heidi Marie Schellman (born 1957) is an American artist who creates large-scale installation art using found objects. Schellman was born in San Francisco, California. She received her BFA from the University of California, Berkeley in 1979. Schellman has exhibited her work around the world, and her pieces can be found in the collections of the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Smithsonian American Art Museum. Schellman is a member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences and the National Academy of Design.
