Columbus is a 2015 Telugu romantic comedy film written and directed by M. S. Vishwanath. The film stars Naga Chaitanya, Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles and features music composed by A. R. Rahman.  The film tells the story of two people who fall in love despite their differences. It is set in the backdrop of the Columbus Day holiday in the United States and deals with the issues faced by immigrants there.  The film was released on 14 October 2015 to positive reviews from critics. It became a commercial success, becoming the highest-grossing Telugu film of all time and was declared a Hit at the box office.  Karthik (Naga Chaitanya) is a Telugu youth who has just completed his studies and is looking for a job. He meets Anjali (Samantha Ruth Prabhu), an American woman who is visiting her family in India during Columbus Day weekend. Anjali is initially wary of Karthik, believing he is a thief, but they eventually fall in love and get married.
