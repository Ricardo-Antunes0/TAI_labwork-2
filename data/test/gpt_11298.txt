Mansonella perstans is a filarial nematode that is endemic to the United States. It is a member of the family of nematodes that are parasitic on the blood of vertebrates. It causes human schistosomiasis, an infestation caused by parasitic flatworms in the genus Schistosoma. The parasite lives in the human blood vessels and skeletal muscles.
