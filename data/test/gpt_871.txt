The ABS-CBN Film Archives, also called ABS-CBN Film Conservation Center, is a film archive and museum in the Philippines. It was founded in 1988 by the ABS-CBN Broadcasting Corporation. The archive holds more than 1 million film reels and documents the history of Philippine cinema.  The ABS-CBN Film Archives was originally founded by the ABS-CBN Broadcasting Corporation in 1988 as an archive to preserve Philippine cinema's history. The archive currently holds more than 1 million film reels, documenting the history of Philippine cinema. The archive also holds a variety of artifacts related to Philippine cinema, such as costumes, set pieces, and movie posters. The archives are open to the public and they offer a variety of programming related to Philippine cinema.
