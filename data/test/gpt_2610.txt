A concession stand (American English, Canadian English), also called a snack bar, is a type of stand in a sports stadium, arena, or other large public gathering place where food and drinks are sold. concession stands often have multiple outlets selling food and drinks, including hot dogs, popcorn, candy bars, soft drinks, ice cream, and fast food.   concession stands are often located in the upper levels of sports stadiums or arenas. They are also found in other large public venues such as malls and convention centers. In the United States, concession stands often bear the name of the sponsoring organization or team such as " Pepsi " or " Budweiser ", while in Canada they are often named after a local restaurant chain.
