Kavus was the 32nd ruler of Shirvan. He was the son of Hovhannes II and succeeded his father as ruler in 1048. He was an energetic ruler who consolidated Shirvan's position in the region and expanded its borders. He fought against the Byzantine Empire, unsuccessfully, and also fought against the neighbouring Qarakhanids. He died in 1073.  Kavus I was an energetic and successful ruler of Shirvan who consolidated the kingdom's position in the region and expanded its borders. He fought against both the Byzantine Empire and the neighbouring Qarakhanids, unsuccessfully, but is best known for his struggle against a plague which killed much of his population. He died in 1073.
