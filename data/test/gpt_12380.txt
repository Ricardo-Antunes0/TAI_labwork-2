He made over 350 appearances in the Football League, most notably for Birmingham City and Arsenal.  Williams was born in Small Heath, Birmingham, and played youth football for Birmingham West End before signing professionally with Birmingham in 1943. He made his first-team debut in December of that year, and went on to make 346 appearances for the club over the next seven seasons. He spent two years with Arsenal, before returning to Birmingham in 1955. He retired from football in 1961, after playing his final game for the "Blues".  Williams later worked as a coach with both Birmingham and Arsenal. He died on 12 March 2012 at the age of 89.
