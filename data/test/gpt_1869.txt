The Secret Witness is a 1931 American mystery film directed by William A. Wellman and written by John M. Stahl. The film stars George Raft, Kay Francis, Humphrey Bogart, and Edward G. Robinson. The story tells of a private detective who is hired to find out who killed a rich man.  The Secret Witness was released on October 10, 1931, by Warner Bros. The film was well received by critics and audiences and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Picture.
