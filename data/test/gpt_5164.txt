Alexandra Gripenberg, also known as Alexandra van der Vlies (born 12 November 1984 in Knokke-Heist, Belgium) is a Belgian actress and singer.  Gripenberg was born in Knokke-Heist, Belgium and started her career as a child actor. She trained as an opera singer at the Royal Conservatoire of Brussels. She made her acting debut in the Belgian television series De Blauwe Zone (2002). She has since starred in films such as Kicks (2005), The Class (2006), and The Illusionist (2006). Gripenberg has also sung in several films, including the song "I Will Always Love You" for the film A Mighty Heart (2007).
