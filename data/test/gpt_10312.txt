Shannon Eagland (born 16 May 1990) is an Australian professional golfer.  Eagland was born in Melbourne, Victoria. She attended Scotch College in Melbourne before playing college golf at the University of South Carolina. She turned professional in 2009.  Eagland has won four times on the LPGA Tour, including twice at the ANA Inspiration. She was part of the team that won the Solheim Cup in 2013.  She is married to American golfer and two-time PGA Tour winner Brooks Koepka.
