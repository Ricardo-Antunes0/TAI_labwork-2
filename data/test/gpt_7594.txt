Verdade Uma Ilusão is a live album by Brazilian singer and songwriter Caetano Veloso. It was recorded at the Estádio do Maracanã in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil on October 6, 1978, during his tour to promote the album "Verdade".  The album was released in 1979 on Veloso's own label, Bella Musica. The album was reissued in 1998 on CD and DVD by EMI Music Brazil.  The album features live performances of Veloso's songs from his 1978 album "Verdade", as well as two new tracks: "Ilusão" and "Nada".   "Verdade Uma Ilusão" was awarded a Platinum certification by the Brazilian Recording Industry Association (ABR) for sales of 100,000 units.
