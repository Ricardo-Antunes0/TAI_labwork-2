Rodney Ewing is an interdisciplinary artist in the fields of painting, sculpture, installation and video. He was born in the Bronx, New York City in 1961. Rodney Ewing received his BFA from the School of Visual Arts in 1983. Since then he has exhibited internationally including at the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Centre Pompidou, and the Guggenheim Museum. Rodney Ewing's work is in the collections of the Tate Museum, the Metropolitan Museum of Art, and the Art Institute of Chicago.
