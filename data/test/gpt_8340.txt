She was laid down on 22 November 1937 at the Arsenal Naval de La Frontera in Talcahuano, Chile, and launched on 10 October 1939. She was commissioned into the Chilean Navy on 21 March 1941. The Aldea served in the Chilean Navy until her decommissioning on 5 December 1966. During her career, she participated in several naval engagements, including the Invasion of Easter Island (1941), Battle of Cape Horn (1941), Battle of the Bismarck Sea (1943), Battle of Kula Gulf (1944), Battle of Iwo Jima (1945), and Battle of Leyte Gulf (1945). The Aldea was decommissioned on 5 December 1966 and sold for scrapping in 1967.
