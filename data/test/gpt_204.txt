Brummel & Brown is the name of a small distillery in Speyside, Scotland. The distillery is owned by William Grant & Sons and is located in the town of Dufftown.  Brummel & Brown was founded in 1824 by John Brummel and his son-in-law, George Brown. The distillery was originally located in the town of Elgin, but was relocated to Dufftown in 1892. The distillery has a capacity of just 240,000 liters per year and produces a range of whiskies including single malts, blends, and casks.
