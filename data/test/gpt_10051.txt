Disney's Typhoon Lagoon is a water theme park located at Walt Disney World Resort in Bay Lake, Florida. The park is themed after a typhoon and opened on May 25, 1992. It is the eleventh Disney park to open, and was originally named Typhoon Lagoon Rapids.  The park is themed after a typhoon, and features a large lagoon with seven water rides, including two coasters. The park is also home to the Disney's Yacht Club Resort, which includes a pool, tennis courts, and a beach.
