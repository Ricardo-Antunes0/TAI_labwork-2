Kabillion is a children's video-on-demand service owned by Kabillion Media LLC and launched in the United States on November 14, 2013. It is a spin-off of the British children's television series "Kabillion Kids" which premiered in the UK on CBBC on September 1, 2012.  Kabillion is a subscription video-on-demand service owned by Kabillion Media LLC and launched in the United States on November 14, 2013. The service is a spin-off of the British children's television series "Kabillion Kids" which premiered in the UK on CBBC on September 1, 2012. Kabillion features original programming, including animated series, live action series and movies for children of all ages. The service is available to subscribers in the United Kingdom and Ireland, as well as North America.
