The Wheelwright Block is a historic commercial building located at 410-412 East State Street in DeWitt, Michigan. It was built in 1892 and is a three-story, brick building with a terra cotta topping. It features an ornate cast iron cornice and decorative brickwork. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
