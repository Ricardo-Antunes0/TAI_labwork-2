The Love Dare is a non-fiction marriage-related book written by Dave and Jillian Fletcher. It was published on May 6, 2010.  The book is divided into 21 short, daily challenges, which are meant to help couples grow closer to each other. The book is based on the idea that "love is not a feeling, it's a choice".  The Love Dare has been released as a movie on March 31, 2016.
