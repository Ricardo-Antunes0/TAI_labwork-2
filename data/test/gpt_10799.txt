Peter Wentz Farmstead is a historical German-American farmstead on a hilltop in the rural community of Wentzville, Missouri. The property was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1991.  The Peter Wentz Farmstead is located on a hilltop in the rural community of Wentzville, Missouri. The farmstead consists of a main section, a barn, and a blacksmith shop. The main section is a 2-story frame house with a gabled roof and clapboard siding. The house has two exterior doors and four windows on each side. The barn is a 1-story frame structure with an attached garage. The blacksmith shop is a 1-story frame structure with an attached garage and an open hearth.
