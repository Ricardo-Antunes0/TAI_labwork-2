Rogers Communications Inc. is a Canadian communications company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario. The company was founded in 1881 as the Rogers Wire and Cable Company and is one of Canada's largest telecommunications companies with over 12 million customers. It has operations in wireline, wireless, satellite, media, and advertising. The company is also a leading provider of cable television services in Canada, with over 5 million subscribers. In May 2014, Rogers Communications announced that it would be merging with BCE Inc., its largest competitor. The merger was completed in December 2014.   Today, Rogers Communications is one of Canada's leading providers of broadband Internet, TV, voice and cloud services. With over 12 million customers and operations in more than 15 countries, the company is dedicated to delivering innovative products and unmatched customer service.
