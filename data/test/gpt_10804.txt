Gotzon Mantuliz Dudagoitia (born August 10, 1988) is a Spanish professional basketball player who plays for Sigal Prishtina of the Kosovo Basketball Super League. He is 1.92 m (6 ft 3 in) tall and he plays at the point guard position.  Born in Bilbao, Basque Country, Dudagoitia played youth basketball with Bilbao Basket and then with Basque powerhouse Real Madrid. He made his professional debut with the Madrid-Ibiza team during the 2006-07 season, but he left the club after one season to sign with BCM Gravelines of France.  After one season with Gravelines, Dudagoitia signed with CB Gran Canaria of the Spanish ACB League. In 2011, he moved to Olympiacos of the Greek Basket League, where he averaged 12.1 points, 4.5 rebounds and 3 assists per game in his first season there.
