Mila is an upcoming CG animated short film written and directed by Michael Gondry and produced by Aardman Animations. It is a co-production between Aardman and Studio Ghibli.  The film tells the story of a lonely woman who is visited by a creature that she initially mistakes for a child. The creature, who she later realises is a spirit, asks her to accompany it on a journey. Mila accepts and the two set off together.
