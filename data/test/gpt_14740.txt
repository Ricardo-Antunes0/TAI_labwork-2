Brandon Barnes (born October 10, 1978) is an American football cornerback who is currently a free agent. He was drafted by the Washington Redskins in the second round of the 2002 NFL Draft. He played college football at the University of Miami.  Barnes has also been a member of the Detroit Lions, Philadelphia Eagles, and Pittsburgh Steelers.
