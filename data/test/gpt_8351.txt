Roger D. Peng is an author and business executive who has worked in a variety of industries, including retail, publishing, and technology. He is the co-founder and CEO of SocialBee, a social media management company.  Peng was born in Taipei, Taiwan in 1975. He graduated from the University of California at Berkeley with a degree in economics and political science. Peng then worked as an analyst for JP Morgan Chase before moving to Boston to work in publishing.  In 2006, Peng co-founded SocialBee, a social media management company. The company has since grown to become one of the largest social media management companies in the world. In 2017, SocialBee was acquired by Outbrain.  Peng is also an author. His book "Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future" was published in 2014 and has been translated into 12 languages. The book has been praised for its lucid and accessible writing style and for its insights into the dynamics of startup culture.
