Alexis Lemaire (born 1980) is a mental health campaigner and writer. She is the founder and director of the mental health charity Mind.  Lemaire was born in 1980 in North London, England. She has a BA (Hons) in Social Anthropology from University College London. After completing her undergraduate studies, Lemaire worked as a community mental health worker for two years before beginning her career as a writer.  Lemaire has written for a number of publications, including The Guardian, The Observer, and The Huffington Post. Her work focuses on mental health and wellbeing, particularly in regard to gender and intersectionality. Lemaire is also a mental health campaigner, speaking on behalf of mental health charities such as Mind and Rethink Mental Illness.
