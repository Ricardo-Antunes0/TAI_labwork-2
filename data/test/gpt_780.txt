Commelina tuberosa is an herbaceous perennial plant that is native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small, annual plant that can reach up to 50 cm in height. It has a bushy, slender stem with several leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. The leaves are ovate-elliptic in shape and have a serrated margin. The flowers are small and yellow, and they are produced in clusters at the top of the stem.
