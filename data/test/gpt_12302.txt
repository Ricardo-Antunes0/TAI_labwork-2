Jeffrey M. Smith (born 1958) is an American historian, columnist and author who specializes in the history of the United States.  Smith is the founder and editor-in-chief of The American Thinker, a conservative online magazine. He also writes a weekly column for The Washington Times.  Smith was born in 1958 in Chicago, Illinois. He received his B.A. from DePauw University and his M.A. from the University of Chicago. He has been a professor of history at Hillsdale College since 1992, where he has been the John W. Pope Professor of History since 2003.
