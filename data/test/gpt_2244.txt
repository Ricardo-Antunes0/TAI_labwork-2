Sten (born 1982, in Rome) and Lex (born 1987, in Rome) are an Italian performance artist and video artist who use appropriated media to comment on social and political issues.  Sten and Lex have been collaborating since 2006, when they first exhibited their video works in Rome. Their work addresses a wide range of social and political topics, including the role of the media in society, immigration, religion, and the economy. In 2013 they were included in the Venice Biennale.
