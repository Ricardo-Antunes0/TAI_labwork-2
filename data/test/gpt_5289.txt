He began his music career as a DJ and producer in the early 2000s. In 2004, he released his debut album "Aqua". He has since released several additional albums and singles, including the Grammy-nominated album "Beautiful World" (2007), which featured the singles "Sakura" and "Last Night a DJ Saved My Life". Takayama has toured throughout North America, Europe, and Asia. He has also collaborated with artists such as Norah Jones, John Legend, CeeLo Green, and A-Trak.  Takayama was awarded the prestigious Edison Award in 2010 for his contributions to music. In 2013, he was awarded an honorary doctorate from York University in Toronto.
