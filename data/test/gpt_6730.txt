The Miller Publishing Company was established by Jacob C. Miller in 1883. The company became one of the largest publishing houses in the United States, publishing both fiction and non-fiction books. In 1960, the company merged with Farrar, Straus and Giroux to form the Farrar, Straus and Giroux Publishing Group.
