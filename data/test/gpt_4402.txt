William George Zane Jr. (born February 24, 1952) is an American actor, singer and producer. He started his career in the early 1970s as a member of the pop group Peter Zane and the Zanes. He is best known for his leading roles in the films A Kiss Before Dying (1976), Thepheles (1978), and Drives Me Crazy (1981). In the early 1990s, he starred in the TV series Search for Tomorrow. In 2003, he was inducted into the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
