Yan Lift, incorporated as Lift Engineering & Construction Corporation in 2003, is a privately held construction and engineering company located in the United States with its main offices in Los Angeles, California. It specializes in the design, engineering, construction, and management of high-rise commercial and residential towers.  Yan Lift was founded by entrepreneur and real estate developer Yaniv Rosenstein in 2003. Rosenstein has over 20 years of experience in the real estate industry and has developed and managed a number of high-rise commercial and residential towers. Yan Lift has worked on a number of high-profile projects including the Trump International Hotel & Tower in Chicago, Illinois; The Shard in London; The Address at Century City in Los Angeles; Rivercenter Tower in Austin, Texas; the W Hotel & Residences at L.A Live in downtown Los Angeles; the Salesforce Tower in San Francisco; and One57 at 57 West 57th Street in New York City. In 2018, Yan Lift was awarded the prestigious American Institute of Architects (AIA) Honor Award for Excellence in Construction Management for their work on the Salesforce Tower.
