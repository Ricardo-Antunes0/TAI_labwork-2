Boldina Gora is a historical area in the regional unit of South Tyrol, Italy. It encompasses the municipalities of Bolzano, Bruneck, Innsbruck, Klagenfurt and Mals. It is situated on the eastern slope of the Alps, about 10 km northeast of Innsbruck and about 25 km southeast of Bruneck. The area has been inhabited since prehistoric times and features a number of castles and fortresses including Schloss Lechfeld (12th century), Rheinkelried (13th century) and Boldogkovo (14th century).
