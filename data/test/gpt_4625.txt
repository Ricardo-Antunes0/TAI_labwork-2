Circus is a block breaker arcade game developed and published by Atari in 1982. The objective of the game is to clear the playing field by dropping blocks of the same color onto targets located above the player. The player controls a small red figure that moves around the playing field and drops blocks onto targets. Blocks fall from the bottom of the screen, and if they fall on top of a target, they disappear and score points for the player. The game is over when the playing field is cleared, and the player scores a certain number of points for each target they hit.
