Peter Seymour (born November 17, 1977 in Toronto, Ontario) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player.  Seymour spent his entire 10-year NHL career with the Anaheim Ducks, where he appeared in 559 games and scored 85 goals and 126 assists. In 2006-07, he led the Ducks in scoring with 86 points (33 goals and 53 assists) in 82 games. Seymour was selected to participate in the 2007 NHL All-Star Game and the 2007 Stanley Cup Final.  Seymour also played for the Calgary Flames, Nashville Predators, Philadelphia Flyers (two stints), Columbus Blue Jackets and Boston Bruins. He retired from professional hockey after the 2014-15 season.
