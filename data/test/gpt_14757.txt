Joonatan Jürgenson (born 24 October 1991 in Helsinki) is a Finnish professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Jürgenson made his SM-liiga debut playing with Jokerit during the 2009–10 season. On 30 June 2010, Jürgenson signed a three-year contract with Jokerit. On 3 February 2017, Jürgenson was traded by Jokerit to HC Sochi in exchange for Dmitri Afanasenkov.
