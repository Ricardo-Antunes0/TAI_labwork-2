The Bruckner Gesamtausgabe (Brucker's Complete Edition) is a monumental project to publish all of the works of Anton Bruckner in chronological order. The project began in 1954 and was completed in 1986.  The Bruckner Gesamtausgabe is the largest and most comprehensive edition of the works of Anton Bruckner ever undertaken. It comprises eleven volumes, each containing approximately three thousand pages of text, with over fifty thousand musical notations.  The Bruckner Gesamtausgabe is a valuable resource for scholars and musicians alike. It provides a complete and accurate text of all Bruckner's compositions, as well as extensive commentary and analysis. The edition is also available in electronic format, making it accessible to a wider audience.
