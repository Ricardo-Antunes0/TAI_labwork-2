"Celebrity Pharmacology" is the thirteenth episode of the first season of the American television series "Breaking Bad".  In the episode, Hank Schrader and the DEA investigate a suspicious shipment of drugs from Mexico. They are alerted to the presence of actor Bryan Cranston, who is secretly filming a movie in Albuquerque. The drugs are traced back to a Hollywood producer, who is connected to a Mexican drug lord. Cranston and the producer are arrested, and Schrader learns that the movie he was filming was actually a drug smuggling operation.
