Aaroh  is an alternative rock band from Mumbai, India. The band consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Ankit Tiwari, keyboardist and backing vocalist Rohan Jadhav, drummer Saurabh Sharma and bassist Vikas Nagpal.   The band was formed in 2007 by Ankit Tiwari, Rohan Jadhav and Saurabh Sharma. They released their first album "Aaroh" in 2009. The album was well received by music critics. In 2010, they toured extensively in India and abroad, performing at various festivals such as SXSW in Austin, Texas, Rock in Rio in Brazil and the ZoukOut festival in Singapore.  In 2013, the band released their second album "Ether", which was well received by music critics. The album peaked at number 9 on the Indian Albums Chart. In 2014, the band toured extensively in India and abroad, performing at various festivals such as the prestigious Wacken Open Air festival in Germany and the Coachella Valley Music And Arts Festival in California. They also performed at the London leg of the Indipop festival which was attended by 500,000 people.   In 2015, the band released their third album "Wanderlust". The album was well received by music critics. It peaked at number 4 on the Indian Albums Chart. The album also charted on various international charts including the UK Albums Chart and the Swiss Albums Chart. In 2016, they toured extensively in India and abroad
