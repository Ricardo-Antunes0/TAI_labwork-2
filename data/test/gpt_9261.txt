The Sun God Festival is an annual event in the city of Xi'an, Shaanxi province, China. Held on the fifteenth day of the fifth lunar month, it is a celebration of the life and work of Sun Wukong, a Monkey King who is said to have saved the Chinese people from a drought.  The Sun God Festival is an important part of Chinese culture and history. It is believed that Sun Wukong, the Monkey King, helped save the Chinese people from a drought and became an important figure in Chinese culture. The festival celebrates his life and work and is an important part of Chinese culture.
