African Parks is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that aims to conserve Africa's wildlife and natural resources. The organization was founded in 1966 by Flora Shaw and Allan Savory.   African Parks manages a network of 27 parks covering an area of more than 1.2 million square kilometers, or 565,000 square miles. The organization operates in four regions of Africa: the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Tanzania, and Zambia. African Parks is headquartered in Garowe, Somalia.
