Sonika Manandhar (born 17 January 1990) is an Indian actress who has appeared in Tamil and Telugu cinema. She is best known for her roles in the Tamil films Thaanaa Paarum Paattu and Papanasam (2013), and the Telugu film Srinidhi (2015).
