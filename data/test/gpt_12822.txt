The game was released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in October 2010. It is the follow-up to the 2006 video game Burnout 3 and the first in the "Burnout" series to be released for a new generation of consoles. The game's campaign is set in a fictionalized version of Los Angeles, and focuses on the rivalry between two street gangs, the Ballas and the Toreros. The player can compete in a variety of races and events, as well as form their own street gangs and take on other players online. "Dirty Drivin'' was positively received by critics, who praised its graphics, gameplay, and online multiplayer. It was also nominated for several awards, including Best Racing Game at the 2011 National Academy of Video Game Trade Reviewers Awards.
