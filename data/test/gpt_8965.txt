Winsome Fanny Barker (23 September 1907 – 10 October 1995) was an English actress and singer.  Barker was born in 1907 in Birmingham, England to Sarah (née Wood) and George Barker. She began her stage career in the 1930s, appearing in a number of West End productions. Her most successful role was as Nanny McPhee in the 1964–1975 BBC television series of the same name. She also appeared in films including "The Return of Sherlock Holmes" (1939), "The Man Who Knew Too Much" (1956), "Doctor Dolittle" (1998), and "Peter Rabbit" (2018). Barker died on 10 October 1995 in London.
