All Strange Away is a short prose story by American writer Stephen King. It was first published in The New Yorker magazine on July 7, 1982, and later collected in his anthology Different Seasons.  The story is about a group of high school students who travel to the fictional town of Castle Rock to spend the summer before their senior year of high school. While there, they become involved in a series of supernatural events.  All Strange Away has been adapted for film twice, first in 1997 and again in 2007.
