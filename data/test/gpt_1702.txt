Private Andrew J. Weaher or Weaber (May 4, 1892 – March 25, 1967) was an American soldier and a recipient of the Medal of Honor, the highest and most prestigious award for valor in the United States military.  Weaher joined the United States Army in 1917 and saw action in World War I. He was awarded the Medal of Honor for his actions during the Battle of Château-Thierry on July 3, 1918. After the war, he served in the Philippines and China before returning to the United States in 1945. Weaher died in 1967 at the age of 73.  Weaher was born May 4, 1892, in Watertown, New York.
