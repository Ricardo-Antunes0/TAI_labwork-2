Sergi Daniv (; born 2 October 1975) is a Ukrainian football referee.  Sergi Daniv was born on 2 October 1975 in Lviv, Ukraine. He is a referee who has officiated in the top leagues of Europe. In his career to date he has officiated in the Ukrainian Premier League, Belgian Pro League, Greek Super League, Russian Premier League, German Bundesliga and Spanish La Liga.
