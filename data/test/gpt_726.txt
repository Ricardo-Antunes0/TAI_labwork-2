The Wirrimbirra Sanctuary is a heritage-listed fauna sanctuary located in the outer metropolitan area of Perth, Western Australia. It was added to the Australian National Heritage List on 2 April 2007.  The Wirrimbirra Sanctuary is located in the outer metropolitan area of Perth, Western Australia, on the banks of the Swan River. The sanctuary comprises a total area of , including of wetland and of upland heath. The sanctuary is important for its populations of swamp wallabies, eastern grey kangaroos, western grey kangaroos, red foxes and brush-tailed possums.
