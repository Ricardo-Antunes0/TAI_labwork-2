Gudhi Padwa is a spring-time festival that is celebrated in the Indian state of Maharashtra.  The festival is dedicated to the Hindu god Gudhi Padwa and celebrates the beginning of Spring. It is usually celebrated in the month of March or April. Gudhi Padwa is also known as "Gudi Padwa Shivaratri".  Gudi Padwa is one of the most important festivals in Maharashtra. It is a time to celebrate the arrival of spring and to pray for a good year ahead. Families go out for a picnic and spend time together. There are lot of pujas (religious ceremonies) and processions held during the festival.
