Åke Green (; born 3 June 1941) is a retired Swedish footballer who played as a defender.  Born in Stockholm, Green played for local clubs IFK Norrköping and Djurgårdens IF before joining IFK Göteborg in 1966. He won the league title with Göteborg in 1969, and was also part of the team that reached the European Cup Winners' Cup semi-finals that season. Green spent two seasons with Juventus, before returning to Sweden to play for Malmö FF and Helsingborgs IF. He retired in 1980 after playing 174 games for Helsingborgs.  After retiring from football, Green worked as a football commentator for Swedish television.
