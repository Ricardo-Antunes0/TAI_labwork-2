Michele Tardioli (born 7 November 1970) is an Italian film actor, director and screenwriter. He started his acting career in the early 1990s, appearing in films such as Il Postino and La Ragazza con la pistola. His breakthrough role came in 1999, when he starred in Bernardo Bertolucci's The Last Emperor. Since then, Tardioli has appeared in a number of acclaimed films, including 2000's Malena, 2004's Il Divo and 2009's Il Grande Colpo. He has also directed and written several films, including L'Amico d'Infanzia (2003) and La Notte (2011).
