Christina Milian is the self-titled debut studio album by American singer Christina Milian. It was released on September 18, 2000, by Jive Records. The album peaked at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart and spawned four singles: "I'm Real", "When You Believe", "I Love You" and "All I Have".
