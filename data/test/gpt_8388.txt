Reto Bucher (born September 30, 1982 in Winterthur, Switzerland) is a Swiss professional ice hockey goaltender. He currently plays with HC Davos of the National League A. Bucher attended the University of Denver and played one season of NCAA Division I hockey with the Denver Pioneers before turning professional in 2002. He has played in the National League A, Swiss National League A, and Austrian Hockey League.  Bucher was drafted by the Edmonton Oilers in the fourth round, 103rd overall, in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He played one season of NCAA Division I hockey with the Denver Pioneers before turning professional in 2002. Bucher has played in the National League A, Swiss National League A, and Austrian Hockey League.  Bucher made his debut with HC Davos in the 2005-06 season. He won his first league championship with Davos in 2007-08. In 2009-10, he was named to the Swiss national team for the World Championships and helped Switzerland to a silver medal. In 2010-11, he won his second league championship with Davos.
