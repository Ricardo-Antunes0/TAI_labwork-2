Özge Özder (born 1 April 1978) is a Turkish singer, songwriter, and actress.  Özge Özder was born in 1978 in Istanbul, Turkey. She started her music career at the age of 12, when she won a singing contest. Özge Özder has since released six albums and starred in several television shows. She has been nominated for several awards, including the Best New Artist award at the Turkish Music Awards.
