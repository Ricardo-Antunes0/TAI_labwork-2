Chen Jianlong (; born 14 May 1989) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays for Chinese Super League side Jiangsu Sainty.  Chen Jianlong started his football career with hometown club Dalian Shide in 2006. After spending four years at Dalian Shide, Chen transferred to Liaoning Whowin in 2010. He made his debut for Liaoning Whowin on 8 March 2010 in a league game against Beijing Guoan. Chen scored his first goal for Liaoning Whowin on 5 May 2010 in a league game against Henan Jianye. Chen helped Liaoning Whowin win the 2013 Chinese FA Cup, and was named the tournament's best player.  On 29 July 2015, Chen transferred to Guangzhou R&F. On 11 September 2015, he scored his first goal for Guangzhou R&F in a league game against Tianjin Teda. On 26 January 2016, he scored his first AFC Champions League goal in a 1-1 draw against Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors. On 3 May 2016, he scored Guangzhou R&F's first ever Champions League goal in a 3-1 victory over Brisbane Roar. Chen was voted as the best player of the AFC Champions League group stage by the media and teammates.  On 25 July 2017, Chen transferred to Jiangsu Sainty.
