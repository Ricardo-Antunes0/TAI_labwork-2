MAG Aerospace is an American-owned military contractor that provides aircraft, missiles and defense systems to various government agencies and military customers. The company was founded in 1993 and has since grown to become one of the world's leading providers of air and missile defense systems.  MAG Aerospace is headquartered in San Antonio, Texas and has a workforce of more than 1,000 employees. The company's principal products include the Patriot Advanced Capability-3 (PAC-3) missile system, the AIM-120 AMRAAM air-to-air missile, the Block II Joint Strike Fighter (JSF) and the Airborne Early Warning and Control System (AWACS).
