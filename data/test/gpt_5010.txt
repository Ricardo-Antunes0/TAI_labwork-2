Saint Narek chapel is a chapel of the Russian Orthodox Church in the city of Tbilisi, Georgia.  The chapel is located on the banks of the Mtkvari River, in the Saburtalo district. The construction of the chapel started in 1878, and it was consecrated on October 15, 1884. The chapel was damaged during World War II, but was restored in the 1950s.  The main icon of Saint Narek is housed in the chapel. The icon is painted by Nikon Svanidze.
