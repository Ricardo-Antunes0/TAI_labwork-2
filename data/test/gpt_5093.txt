Sirena Huang (born May 18, 1994) is an American actress and singer. Huang began her acting career as a child in stage productions before making her debut in the television series "One Tree Hill" in 2009. She has since appeared in a number of feature films and television series, most notably recurring roles on the television series "The O.C." and "Nashville". Huang released her debut album, "Weightless", in 2018.
