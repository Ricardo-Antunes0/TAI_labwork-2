Kolexxxion, is a collaborative studio album by American rappers Lil Uzi Vert and Playboi Carti. It was released on August 25, 2018, by Atlantic Records and distributed by Warner Bros. Records.  The album was recorded in Atlanta, Los Angeles and London, and features guest appearances from Quavo, Swae Lee, Offset, Pharrell Williams, YG and 21 Savage.  Upon its release, "Kolexxxion" received generally positive reviews from critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has an average score of 71, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart with first-week sales of 116,000 copies. It also debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart. As of September 2018, the album has sold 341,000 copies in the United States.
