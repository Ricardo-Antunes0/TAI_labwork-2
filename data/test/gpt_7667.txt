Acioa edulis of the family Chrysobalanaceae.   The Acioa edulis is a tropical shrub or tree found in the Americas, ranging from the eastern United States to Brazil, and also in Trinidad and Tobago. The plant grows to a height of 2–6 m, with a trunk up to 1 m diameter. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, acute at the tip, and have a serrated margin. The flowers are white with a pink tinge, and are arranged in panicles 10–15 cm long. The fruit is a drupe 10–15 cm long and 5 cm wide, containing one or two seeds.
