is a Japanese manga written by Mako Kanno and illustrated by Yui Hara. The manga is a romantic comedy about two girls who were never supposed to be together but wind up falling for each other.  The story follows Nagi Aizawa and Kanako Aizawa, two girls who were never supposed to be together but wind up falling for each other. Nagi is the popular, outgoing, and rich daughter of a wealthy family and Kanako is the quiet, shy, and poor daughter of a struggling family. Despite their differences, the two forge an unlikely bond as they journey through their first love.  First Love Sisters has been serialized in Kodansha's Monthly Magazine since 2009 and has been collected into 12 volumes. The manga has been licensed in North America by Yen Press and in Europe by Panini Comics.
