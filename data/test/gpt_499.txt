Brazeau Presbyterian Church is a congregation of the Presbyterian Church in Canada. The church is located in Brazeau, a small town in the Rural Municipality of Ste. Rose du Lac, Manitoba.  The church was founded in 1892, and it has since served the community of Brazeau and surrounding areas. The congregation has a history of ministries in education, social work, and outreach to the disabled. The church also maintains a library and offers programming for youth and families.  The church is affiliated with the Presbytery of Manitoba and the Canadian Presbyterian Church.
