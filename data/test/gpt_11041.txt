Muthassi is a historical Malayalam novel written by Vaikom Muhammad Basheer. The novel tells the story of a Muslim boy, Muthassi, who is sent to Kerala by his father to escape religious persecution in the Maldives. The novel tells the story of a Muslim boy, Muthassi, who is sent to Kerala by his father to escape religious persecution in the Maldives. Muthassi ends up becoming a courtesan in the royal palace of Travancore. The novel has been described as "a lyrical and introspective exploration of love, loss and redemption" and has been praised for its "elegant writing style".
