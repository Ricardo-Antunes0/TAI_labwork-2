The 1955 Wilkes 160 was a NASCAR Grand National Series (now Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series) race that was held on October 4, 1955, at the Wilkes County Speedway in Wilkesboro, North Carolina.  Tommy Thompson won the race driving a 1955 Chevrolet. The race was shortened from its original 400-lap length to 250 laps because of a rain delay.
