Xperedon is an initiative started in 2010, which is aimed at promoting the use of XML in the software industry.  Xperedon was founded by a group of software professionals who wanted to promote the use of XML in the software industry. The initiative aims to help developers and businesses adopt XML as their standard format for exchanging data, and to make XML more accessible and easier to use. Xperedon also provides resources and support for developers who want to learn more about XML.
