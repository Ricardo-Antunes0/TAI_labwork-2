Sparkle is a 2012 American musical film directed by Michael Sucsy and written by Theresa Rebeck. The film stars Michelle Williams, Brittany Snow, and Jordana Brewster, and tells the story of two women who reconnect after years of being estranged.  The film was released on August 3, 2012, by 20th Century Fox. It received mixed reviews from critics, but was a box office success.  Two women who have been estranged for years reunite in Sparkle, a small town in the Midwest. They are both working as waitresses in a local diner. They share stories about their pasts and connect with one another.
