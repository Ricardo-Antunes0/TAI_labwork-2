Olusegun Oke Library (OOL) is the main library of the University of Ibadan, Nigeria. It was founded in 1949 as the Ibadan College Library.   The library has a collection of over 2 million items, making it one of the largest in West Africa. It offers access to over 130 journals and newspapers, as well as online databases and e-books. OOL also maintains a sizable collection of rare books, manuscripts and archives.
