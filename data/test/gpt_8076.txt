Istanbul Sapphire, or Sapphire, is a skyscraper under construction in the Şişli district of Istanbul, Turkey. It will be the tallest building in Europe when completed, with a planned height of 1,250 metres (4,093 feet). The building is being developed by the Turkish company Ingenious Properties and will consist of 133 floors, making it the second-tallest building in Europe after the Shard in London. When completed, it will be the sixth-tallest building in the world.  The tower is expected to serve as an office and hotel complex, with luxury apartments and shops on the lower floors. Construction began in 2014 and is scheduled to be completed in 2020.
