BCN Week is a cultural newsweekly started in Barcelona, Spain in 2009. It is published every Thursday and focuses on arts, culture, and creativity in the city and province of Barcelona.  Created by journalist and writer Mireia Pons and editor-in-chief of BCN Magazine, BCN Week is an independent cultural publication that promotes the city’s diversity through in-depth stories about art, design, music, food and more.  The magazine has a circulation of 10,000 copies and is available at newsstands and at BCN’s website (www.bcn.cat).
