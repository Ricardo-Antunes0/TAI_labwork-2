Treet (Armour Star Treet) is a canned fruit product manufactured by the Armour Star Foods company in the United States. The fruit is a mixture of diced fresh fruit and sweetened condensed milk.  Treet was invented in 1946 by Lawrence J. Shields and is currently manufactured by Armour Star Foods in the United States. The fruit is a mixture of diced fresh fruit and sweetened condensed milk. The product is canned in water and high fructose corn syrup.
