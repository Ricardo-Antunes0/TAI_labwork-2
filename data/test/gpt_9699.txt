Lillian Herstein was born on April 2, 1886 in New York City to Russian-Jewish parents. After graduating from Barnard College in 1909, she worked as a reporter for "The New York World". In 1916, she married Leonard Herstein, with whom she had two children. She published her first book, "Theatre Arts", in 1927. She died on August 30, 1967.
