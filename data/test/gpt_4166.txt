Bots!! is a massively multiplayer online game for PC and Mac, in which players control and battle bots in a 3D environment. The game was created by developer IllFonic and is published by PlayFirst.   The objective of the game is to defeat the opposing team's bot leader to earn points, which can be used to buy new bots, or upgrade existing bots. Bots!! also features a variety of different game modes, including Capture the Flag, Team Deathmatch, and King of the Hill.
