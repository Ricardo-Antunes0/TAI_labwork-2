The Daniyal Akhmetov Cabinet was the 6th cabinet of Azerbaijan Republic. It was in office from September 10, 2004 to October 9, 2004.  The cabinet was formed by the National Democratic Party of Azerbaijan after the 2003 parliamentary election. Daniyal Akhmetov, the leader of the party, was appointed Prime Minister. The cabinet consisted of 14 ministers, including 8 from the National Democratic Party.
