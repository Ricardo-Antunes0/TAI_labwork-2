The Alberni Valley Bulldogs are a Junior "A" ice hockey team from the city of Port Alberni, British Columbia. They play in the BC Hockey League.  The Alberni Valley Bulldogs were founded in 1979 and began playing in the Tier II Junior "A" Hockey League. In 2000, they were promoted to the BC Junior A Hockey League and have remained there since. The Bulldogs play their home games at the Port Alberni Civic Centre.
