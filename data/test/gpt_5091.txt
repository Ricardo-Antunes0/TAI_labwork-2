The Beethoven Project Trio is an American classical music trio consisting of violinist Hilary Hahn, cellist Renee Rosnes, and pianist Catherine Lamb. The trio was founded in 2002 by Hahn, Rosnes, and Lamb, who were then all students at the Curtis Institute of Music in Philadelphia.  The trio has made five recordings of Beethoven's complete piano sonatas, as well as the first recording of Beethoven's "Archduke Trio" for Hyperion Records. In addition to their recordings, the trio has given numerous performances throughout North America, Europe, and Asia.
