The RIAS Kammerchor (RIAS Chamber Choir) is a world-renowned choir based in Berlin, Germany. The choir was founded in 1885 as a men's choir and became a women's choir in 1975. The choir repertoire spans from the early medieval period to the present day, with a focus on baroque and classical music. The RIAS Kammerchor has made numerous recordings, most notably of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony.
