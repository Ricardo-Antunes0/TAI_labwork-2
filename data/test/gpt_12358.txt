Jamie's School Dinners is a four-episode documentary television series that aired on the British channel BBC Two from 7 January to 13 January 2009. The show follows the daily lives of pupils and staff at Jamie's School in Atherton, Greater Manchester, during the 2008-09 school year.  The show was produced by the independent production company Wall to Wall Productions and was filmed using a hidden camera. It was first broadcast on BBC Two on 7 January 2009 and was subsequently repeated on 13 January 2009.  The series received mixed reviews from critics. Some praised the show for its candid portrayal of school life, while others found it dull and unoriginal. Overall, the series has been seen by an estimated 1.5 million viewers.
