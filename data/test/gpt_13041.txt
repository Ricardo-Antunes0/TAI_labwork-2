Anitha Thampi (born 1968) is a Malayalam film actress and politician.  Anitha Thampi was born in 1968 in Kerala, India. She started her career as a model and then made her acting debut in the Malayalam film industry in 1994 with the film "Achan" directed by Sibi Malayil. She went on to appear in over 150 films in Malayalam, Tamil and Hindi. She has won several awards, including the Kerala State Film Award for Best Actress for her performance in the film "Oru Mazha Theerum" (2005).  Thampi has also acted in many Telugu, Kannada and Hindi films. In February 2018, she announced that she would be joining the Indian National Congress (INC) as its state president.
