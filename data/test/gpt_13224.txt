Hathiyan railway station  is  located in  Hathiyan village, in the district of Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. The station falls under the jurisdiction of the Agra division of the Northern Railways.  The station has two platforms. The platform 1 has a length of 50 m and platform 2 has a length of 100 m. The station has a building that was built in 1903 and renovated in 2007. The station has a ticket counter, waiting room, water fountain, and toilets. There is also a railway workshop at the station.
