Gesellschaft zu Fraumünster is a guild–like organisation for artists and art lovers in Frankfurt, Germany. It was founded in 1751 by a group of painters, sculptors and architects.  The society has had a profound influence on German art, notably with its promotion of Neoclassicism in the first half of the 19th century. Today, the society continues to promote art and culture through its exhibitions, publications, educational programmes and scholarships.   Gesellschaft zu Fraumünster is a registered association with federal statutes. It has a governing council and a general assembly. The society maintains studios and exhibition spaces in Frankfurt am Main as well as in Weil am Rhein, Rheinbach and Ratingen.
