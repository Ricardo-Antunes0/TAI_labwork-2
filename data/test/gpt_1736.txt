Olympia Regional Airport  is a public-use airport located three nautical miles (6 km) north of the central business district of Olympia, a city in Thurston County, Washington, United States. It is owned by the City of Olympia and operated by the Washington State Department of Transportation. The airport has one runway designated 18/36 with an asphalt surface measuring 3,000 by 75 feet (914 x 23 m). For the 12-month period ending December 31, 2009, the airport had 2,173 aircraft operations, an average of 107 per day: 98% general aviation and 2% air taxi.
