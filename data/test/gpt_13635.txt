Adil Zainulbhai is the chairman of Network18 Media and Investments, one of the largest media conglomerates in India. He is also the founder and chairman of Zee TV, the first private Indian television channel.  Zainulbhai was born on November 11, 1946, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. After completing his schooling from Scotch School in Mumbai, he completed his undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 1969. He then went on to study law at the University of Cambridge, England.  Zainulbhai began his career in journalism as a reporter for The Times of India newspaper in Bombay. In 1975, he founded Zee TV, which became one of India's most successful television networks. In 1998, he founded Network18 Media and Investments, one of India's largest media conglomerates. He has been a member of the Indian Parliament since 2004.
