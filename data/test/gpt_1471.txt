Titch is a British stop-motion children's television series created by Nick Park and produced by Aardman Animations. The show is about the adventures of an anthropomorphic red teddy bear named Titch and his friends. It premiered on BBC Two on 8 October 2009 and ended on 19 February 2013. A second series, co-produced with Netflix, began airing on 22 October 2013 and ended on 3 March 2014.  The first series of Titch was nominated for a BAFTA Children's Animation Award in 2010.
