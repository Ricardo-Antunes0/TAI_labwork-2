Akishio (SS-579) is a retired Japanese diesel-electric submarine, the sixth in the Akishio class. She was laid down on September 12, 1977, launched on November 9, 1978 and commissioned on November 21, 1980.  Akishio was decommissioned on February 15, 2007. She was stricken from the navy list on March 31, 2013 and sold for breaking up on December 6, 2013.
