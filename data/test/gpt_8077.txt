Orient Airways Ltd.  was an airline company based in London, England. It commenced operations on 1 January 1934, and became the first airline to operate a scheduled service between London and Cairo. The company was nationalised in 1947 and merged with British European Airways in 1967 to form British Airways. Orient Airways ceased operations on 10 November 1968.
