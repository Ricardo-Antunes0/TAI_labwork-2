Christian Langlois is a Canadian film director, screenwriter, and producer.  Langlois was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. He attended McGill University in Montreal, and then studied at the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts. Langlois began his career as a screenwriter, working on films such as The Machinist and The Thing. He also directed the films I'm Not There and Proof.  Langlois has been married to actress Samantha Morton since 2009.
