Séamus Qualter (born 1967) is an Irish author. He is the author of the novels The Book of Evidence and The Book of Lost Things.  Séamus Qualter was born in 1967 in County Wicklow, Ireland. He began writing at a young age, and published his first novel, The Book of Evidence, in 2002. The novel was shortlisted for the Irish Times/Mystical Fictions Prize and won the John Llewellyn Rhys Prize. The Book of Lost Things, Qualter's second novel, was published in 2007 and won the Irish Times/Lionel Gelber Prize.
