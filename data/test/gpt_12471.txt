Seethathodu is a village in the Pathanamthitta district of Kerala, India. It is located about 9 km from the town of Pathanamthitta.  The village is known for the Seethathodu waterfalls, which cascade down a hill to a small river below. The falls are a popular tourist destination, and can be reached by walking or by taking a bus ride from Pathanamthitta.  The village is also home to a Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Seetha. The temple is open to visitors throughout the day, and has a small museum containing religious artifacts from across Kerala.
