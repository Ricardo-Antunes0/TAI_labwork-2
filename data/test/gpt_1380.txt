Tool Box is the fifth studio album by American rock band Tool, released on June 18, 2006. The album was produced by the band and Danny Lohner, who had produced their previous album Lateralus. The album has been met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 78, based on 16 reviews.  "Tool Box" debuted at number three on the US Billboard 200 with first-week sales of 138,000 copies. The album has sold over 440,000 copies in the United States as of May 2009.
