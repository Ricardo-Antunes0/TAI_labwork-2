WMTU-FM 91.9 is a campus radio station broadcasting a college radio format. Licensed to Champaign, Illinois, the station is owned and operated by the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. WMTU-FM broadcasts from the McBride Student Center on the Urbana-Champaign campus.  WMTU-FM began broadcasting on October 1, 1966 as WHAM-FM, a student-run radio station licensed to Champaign. In 1978, WHAM-FM became WMTU, and in 1984 the station became an affiliate of the National Public Radio network. In 1990, WMTU became one of the first stations in the country to offer online streaming of its programming. In 2006, WMTU became an affiliate of Illinois Public Radio (IPR). In 2009, WMTU started broadcasting in HD.   In 2018, WMTU-FM changed their format from college radio to a mainstream rock format branded as "92.9 The Edge".
