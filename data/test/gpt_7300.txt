Jonas Gladnikoff (born 11 January 1985) is a Swedish footballer who plays for Östersunds FK.  Gladnikoff started his career with Hammarby IF, making his debut in 2003. He spent four seasons at the club, before moving to AIK in 2006. He spent two seasons with AIK before joining Bundesliga side Gladbach in 2009. He spent two seasons with Gladbach before joining English side West Ham United in 2011. He spent three seasons with West Ham United before joining Scottish Premiership side Rangers in 2015. He spent one season with Rangers before joining Östersunds FK in 2018.
