Neon Genesis Evangelion is often cited as one of the greatest anime series of all time.    Neon Genesis Evangelion is an anime television series produced by Gainax and directed by Hideaki Anno. The series is based on the 1995 manga of the same name by Hideaki Anno and Yoshiyuki Sadamoto. Neon Genesis Evangelion is often cited as one of the greatest anime series of all time.  The story follows Shinji Ikari, a young boy who has just begun high school in Tokyo-3, and his friends Asuka Langley Soryu and Shinji Ikari. One day, they are called into an office to be told that they have been chosen to pilot giant mecha known as Evangelions in a battle against an alien force known as angels. Though initially scared, Shinji gradually becomes determined to fight for humanity and protect his friends.  The series has been critically acclaimed, winning many accolades including the prestigious Shogakukan Manga Award for Best Male Character in 1997 and 1998, as well as the Japan Media Arts Festival Award for Excellence in Animation in 1996 and 1997. In addition, it was voted number one in a poll of Japanese fans conducted by TV Asahi in 2002.
