The Comilla University  is a public university located in Comilla, Bangladesh. It was established in 1930 and offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in various disciplines. The university has eight faculties and offers over 150 undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral courses. The university also has a library with over one million books and journals.   The Comilla University is one of the oldest universities in Bangladesh. It was established in 1930 as the Mohammedan Anglo-Oriental College. In 1953, it was renamed the Mohammadan Anglo-Bangladesh University. In 1970, it was upgraded to a public university and renamed the Comilla University.
