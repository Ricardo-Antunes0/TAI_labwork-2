Fort Tenedos was large earth-walled fort was located on the north-west coast of the island of Cyprus, a few miles north of the town of Limassol. It was captured by the Turks in 1571 during the Ottoman conquest of Cyprus. The fort was abandoned in 1878.  Fort Tenedos was built by the Venetians in 1489 as part of their defense system against the Ottoman Turks. It was captured by the Turks in 1571 during their conquest of Cyprus. The fort was abandoned in 1878.
