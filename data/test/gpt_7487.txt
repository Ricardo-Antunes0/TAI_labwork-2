"Boom Boom Pow" is a song by the American rapper Rick Ross. It was released on May 21, 2011, as the fourth single from Ross' fourth studio album Mastermind. The song was produced by The Runners.  The song is about a woman who is in a relationship with a man who is cheating on her. The woman is upset and wants to make things right with her man, but he won't listen to her. The song features guest appearances from T-Pain, 2 Chainz, and Pharrell.  "Boom Boom Pow" received generally positive reviews from music critics. David Jeffries of AllMusic called the song a "slick Neptunes production" and said that it "offers one of the album's highlights." Matt Collar of AllMusic wrote that the song has a "hot, hard-hitting beat" and added that Ross delivers "vicious lyrics about a woman determined to take back her man."  The song peaked at number nine on the US Billboard Hot 100 and number two on the US Billboard Rap Songs chart. It also reached number one in Canada and Sweden. In Australia, the song reached number four on the ARIA Singles Chart.
