Ahmed Gebrel (born January 22, 1991) is an Egyptian professional footballer who plays for Egyptian club Zamalek as a midfielder.  Ahmed started his career at Ismaily SC, before moving to Saudi club Al-Ittihad in the summer of 2008. He made his debut for the club in a Saudi Pro League match against Al-Nassr on August 10, 2008.  In January 2013, Ahmed joined Spanish La Liga side Mallorca on a six-month loan deal. He made his debut for Mallorca on February 2, 2013 in a Copa del Rey match against Racing Santander.  On July 20, 2013, Ahmed completed a permanent transfer to Mallorca after his loan spell expired. He made his debut for Mallorca on August 2, 2013 in a La Liga match against Valencia.  On July 16, 2014, Ahmed signed with Greek Superleague side PAOK FC. He made his debut for PAOK on July 29, 2014 in a Greek Cup match against OFI Crete.  On January 12, 2016, Ahmed joined Turkish Süper Lig side Konyaspor on a six-month loan deal. He made his debut for Konyaspor on February 10, 2016 in a Süper Lig match against Osmanlispor.
