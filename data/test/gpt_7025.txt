Shen Qiang (; born May 11, 1990 in Harbin, Heilongjiang) is a Chinese speed skater.  Shen Qiang won the bronze medal at the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. She also won a silver medal at the 2006 Winter Olympics in Turin, Italy. She also won a gold medal at the 2007 World Championships in Turin, Italy. Shen is the first female speed skater to win three world championship gold medals.
