Moyegeso is the Oba (King) of Itele, in the Igbo-Ukwuani region of southeastern Nigeria. He is a member of the Igbo royal family, and his father was the late Oba Adeyeye Ojo.  Moyegeso succeeded his father, Adeyeye Ojo, to the throne of Itele in 1998. Moyegeso is a religious and spiritual leader, and he is also a member of the Nigerian National Assembly. He has been involved in various humanitarian projects in his community, and he is also a prominent advocate for agricultural development in the area.
