Lim Ban Lim (;  194024 November 2006) was a Singaporean sculptor, painter and installation artist.  Lim was born in 1940 in Singapore to a Chinese family. He studied sculpture at the National Arts School of Singapore from 1959 to 1966. After graduating, he worked as an assistant to sculptor Tony Smith and then as an instructor at the school before establishing his own studio in 1977. Lim's work is characterised by its organic shapes and use of natural materials, such as clay, wood, metal and glass. He has exhibited his work in several countries, including Singapore, Taiwan, Japan, Thailand and Germany. Lim died in 2006.
