Hungária körúti stadion was a stadium in Budapest, Hungary. It was the home of the Hungarian national football team and hosted the 1956 Summer Olympics. The stadium was demolished in 2004.  The Hungária körúti stadion was built in 1954 and replaced the smaller Puskás Ferenc Stadium as the home of Hungary's national football team. It hosted the 1956 Summer Olympics and was one of the largest stadiums in Europe at the time. The stadium's capacity was reduced to 40,000 in 1988 due to safety concerns and it was eventually demolished in 2004.
