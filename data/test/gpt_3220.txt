JOSS (acronym for JOHNNIAC Open Shop System) is a computer system for managing shop activities using a graphical user interface. JOSS was originally developed by the Johniac Corporation in the early 1980s as part of their Comprehensive Shop Management System (CSMS) product line. JOSS is now available as a free and open source software application.  JOSS is designed to be easy to use, and its intuitive graphical user interface (GUI) makes it simple to navigate and manage your shop activities. JOSS features a wide variety of features to help you manage your shop, including inventory management, order processing, sales tracking, and more.  JOSS is free and open source software, meaning that you can download and use it without having to pay any fees. JOSS is also easy to install and use, so you can get started managing your shop quickly and easily.
