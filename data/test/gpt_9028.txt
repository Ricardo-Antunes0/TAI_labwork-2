is a Japanese television drama series. It was produced by studio A-1 Pictures and aired on TV Asahi from July to September 2010. The series is based on the manga Umechan Sensei by Keiichi Sigsawa.  The story follows the life of Nakajima, an eccentric and lonesome teacher at a prestigious private school in Tokyo. Though he has a passion for teaching, Nakajima is often ridiculed by his colleagues and students for his strange behavior and unorthodox teaching methods. However, as Nakajima starts to fall in love with one of his students, he comes to realize that the only way to truly make a difference in someone's life is to truly connect with them.
