Megève  is a commune in the Haute-Savoie department in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region in east-central France. It is located on the southern slopes of the Mont Blanc massif and at the foot of the Dentelles de Montmirail mountain range.  Megève is a popular ski resort, with over 1,000 metres (3,300 ft) of vertical descent on its slopes. The town is also a tourist destination owing to its well preserved medieval and Renaissance architecture, as well as its wide range of outdoor activities such as hiking, mountaineering, skiing, rafting and snowboarding. It is also home to the University of Megève and the Ecole Normale Supérieure de Médecine de Megève
