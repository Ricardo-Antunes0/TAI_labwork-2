Born in 1912 in Dublin, Curran was educated at University College Dublin and the King's Inns. He commenced his legal career in 1937, practising law with the firm of Burke, Whelan and Curran. In 1961, he was appointed a Judge of the Supreme Court of Ireland, serving until his appointment to the European Court of Human Rights in 1965. Curran served as President of the Court from 1971 to 1978. He retired from the judiciary in 1978 and died in 1985.
