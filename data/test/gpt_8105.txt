The  is an archaeological site in the Tsukinoki Shell Mound Historic State Park in the U.S. state of Oklahoma. The mound is located about west of the town of Prague.  The Tsukinoki Shell Mound Historic State Park was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1978. The park includes a shell mound, a Mississippian culture village, and a reconstructed Mississippian culture house.
