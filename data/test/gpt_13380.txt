Zhao Hailin (; born May 20, 1977) is a Chinese actress, singer and model. She is most notable for her roles in the television series Flowers of War (2013), My Lovely Girl (2015) and The Return of the Condor Heroes (2016).  Zhao was born in Wuhan, Hubei Province. She started her career as a model and appeared in commercials before making her acting debut in the television series Flowers of War in 2013. Zhao went on to appear in My Lovely Girl and The Return of the Condor Heroes, both of which received positive reviews from critics.
