Ece Temelkuran (born 22 July 1973, Izmir) is a Turkish musician, singer and songwriter. She is the lead vocalist, pianist and main songwriter for the Turkish rock band Urfa Ekspres.  Born in 1973 in Izmir, Turkey, Ece Temelkuran is a musician, singer and songwriter. She is the lead vocalist, pianist and main songwriter for the Turkish rock band Urfa Ekspres. Temelkuran has released six studio albums, one compilation album and thirty-six singles. Her most successful singles include "Güzel", "Ümit", "Dünya" and "Aşkımın".  In 2006, she represented Turkey at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "Dünya", finishing in fifth place.
