Carmen Electra is the self-titled debut studio album by American singer Carmen Electra. It was released on November 2, 1991, by Arista Records. The album peaked at number ten on the "Billboard" 200 and spawned three singles: "I'm Coming Out", "What a Girl Wants", and "Lookin' for a Boy".  The album was praised by music critics for its experimental and eclectic sound, as well as Electra's soulful vocals. Some reviewers found the album difficult to listen to, but praised its eclectic mix of genres. It has been certified platinum by the RIAA.  In 1992, Electra released her second studio album, Better Than Your Life.
