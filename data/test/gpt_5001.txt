Cyprus Popular Bank (from 2006 to 2011 the Cyprus Popular Bank PLC) was a Cypriot bank. It was headquartered in the city of Limassol.  The Cyprus Popular Bank was formed on 11 November 2006 as a result of the merger of the Cyprus Bank of Economic Development and the Cyprus Development Bank. On 15 February 2011, the bank was nationalized by the government of Cyprus. On 3 March 2011, it was merged with the National Bank of Cyprus.   In 2011, the bank had total assets of €5.551 billion and total liabilities of €8.358 billion. The bank had a capital ratio of 14 percent and a shareholders' equity ratio of 16 percent.
