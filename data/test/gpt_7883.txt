Finest Hour is the fourth comedy album by English rock band The Police, released in 1977.  The album was recorded at the height of the band's popularity and reached number four in the UK Albums Chart. The album was produced by Tony Visconti and includes the singles "So Lonely" and "Next to You".  In 2003, "Finest Hour" was ranked number 309 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time. In 2006, it was ranked number 45 on Q magazine's list of the "100 Greatest British Albums Ever".
