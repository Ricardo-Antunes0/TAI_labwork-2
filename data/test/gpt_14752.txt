Lee Howard "Big Lee" Weyer (September 3, 1921 – October 16, 1975) was an American football player and coach. He played college football at the University of Notre Dame, where he was a consensus All-American in 1943. He served in the United States Navy during World War II, where he played for the Navy Blue Jackets. After the war, Weyer became a football coach at his alma mater, Notre Dame. He was the head coach of the Fighting Irish from 1951 to 1969, compiling a record of 73–36–5.
