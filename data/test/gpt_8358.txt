Ostanes (from Greek ), also spelled Hostanes, was a Syrian rebel leader who headed the Syrian National Council.  Ostanes was born in the town of Qamishli in Syria in 1963. He studied at Damascus University before working as a journalist. He became involved in politics in the early 1990s, becoming a member of the Syrian National Council and later its president. In 2005, he was sentenced to 10 years in prison for his role in the Syrian government's suppression of protests against President Bashar al-Assad's rule. He was released in December 2011 following the uprising against Assad's regime.
