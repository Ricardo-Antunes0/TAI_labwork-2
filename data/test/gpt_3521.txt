Emilie de Ravin (; born 27 December 1988) is a Canadian actress and singer. De Ravin has starred in films like The Twilight Saga series, The Host, and The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo. She has also appeared on television shows like Supernatural, Lost, and Sons of Anarchy. De Ravin is the daughter of Québécois singer-songwriter Marie-Claire Ducharme and actor Christopher de Ravin.  Emilie de Ravin was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada on 27 December 1988. She is the daughter of Québécois singer-songwriter Marie-Claire Ducharme and actor Christopher de Ravin. De Ravin has two brothers: Orlando, who is also an actor, and Noah. Her family moved to Los Angeles when she was three years old.  De Ravin began her acting career appearing in television shows like Supernatural, Lost, and Sons of Anarchy before making her feature film debut in 2008 with the horror film Death Sentence. In 2009 she starred in the romantic drama The Twilight Saga: New Moon as Bella Swan's best friend Rosalie Cullen. The following year she reprised her role in the sequel Twilight Saga: Eclipse and played Alice Cullen in the spin-off film Breaking Dawn – Part 2. In 2013 she starred in the action thriller The Host as a young woman who is taken prisoner by a race of aliens that has invaded Earth.
