Praxille Gydé (born 5 September 1908) was a Czechoslovak film actress. She appeared in more than sixty films from 1928 to 1951.  Praxille Gydé was born on 5 September 1908 in Prague, then part of the Austro-Hungarian Empire. She started her acting career in 1928, appearing in a few short films. Her most notable role was as Martha in the film adaptation of the play Martha by Jaroslav Hašek, released in 1951. She died on 12 November 1986 in Prague.
