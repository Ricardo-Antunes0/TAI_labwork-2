Rahim Beqiri (; , 1 January 1957 – 5 December 2006) was an Albanian professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Born in Kukes, Beqiri started his career at KF Tirana, where he made his professional debut in the 1976–77 season. He played for the club until 1987, winning the Albanian Cup in 1980 and 1984. He then moved to Greece, where he played for Panionios and Iraklis. After returning to Albania, Beqiri played for Partizani Tirana and Flamurtari Vlorë. He retired in 2002.  Beqiri was a member of the Albania national team, making his debut in 1981. He played 54 matches and scored 8 goals for the country, including appearances at the 1982 World Cup and the 1990 European Championship.  Beqiri died on 5 December 2006 after a long battle with cancer.
