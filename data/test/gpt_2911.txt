geoDefense is a 2008 tower defense game for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 platforms, developed by Armor Games and published by Microsoft Studios.  GeoDefense is a game where players defend against an alien invasion by building towers and deploying troops to stop the aliens from reaching the base of their tower. The game features a single player campaign as well as online multiplayer.  The game was met with mixed reviews from critics. Some praised the graphics and gameplay, while others found the campaign unenjoyable and the multiplayer mode flawed.
