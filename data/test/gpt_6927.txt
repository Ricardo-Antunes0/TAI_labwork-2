In Greek mythology, the name Perimede (; Ancient Greek: Περιμέδη) may refer to:  1) One of the three Nereids, daughters of the sea god Nereus and Doris, wife of Peirithous.  2) A daughter of Asopus and niece of Thestius.  3) A nymph who lived in a cave near Mount Ida in Phrygia, who bathed Apollo with her tears.  4) One of the fifty Nereids, daughters of the river god Nerion and Doris. 5) A daughter of Asopus and Thestius.
