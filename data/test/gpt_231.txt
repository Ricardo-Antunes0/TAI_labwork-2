Hussain Jawad Parweez (; born 10 December 1989) is an English cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a left-arm orthodox bowler.  Born in Wolverhampton, Jawad made his debut for Staffordshire in the Minor Counties Championship in 2006. From 2006 to 2009, he represented Staffordshire in the MCCA Knockout Trophy, making his List A debut against Lincolnshire in the 2008/09 season. He has since played List A cricket for the Minor Counties Championship and MCCA Knockout Trophy sides of Staffordshire. In 2013, he made his debut for England Minor Counties against Wales Minor Counties. From 2013 to 2015, he represented the ECB Young Cricketers in the Under-19 Cricket World Cup Qualifier in Sri Lanka.  In January 2016, Jawad was named as one of twenty players to form part of England's squad for the 2016 Under-19 Cricket World Cup. He made his One Day International (ODI) debut against Ireland on 3 January 2016.
