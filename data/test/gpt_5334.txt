Carite State Forest (Spanish: Bosque Estatal de Carite) is a state forest in the Mexican state of Veracruz.  The forest is located in the municipalities of Carite, San Luis Potosi, and Tecpan de Galeana, and has an area of . The forest is mainly composed of oak, pine, cedar, and mahogany trees. There are also many different types of flowers and animals living in the forest.  The Carite State Forest was first declared a state forest in 1938.
