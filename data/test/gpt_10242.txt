Timothy Alan Dick (born June 13, 1953), known professionally as Tim Allen, is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Mike Baxter in the television sitcom Full House (1987–1995), Buzz Hickey in the TV series Home Improvement (1999–2004), Pete Martell in the TV series Last Man Standing (2015–present), and Buzz Lightyear in the Toy Story film series (1995–2010).  Allen has also appeared in a number of films, including The Santa Clause (1994), Toy Story 2 (1999), The Three Amigos (2003), Big Daddy (1999), The Proposal (2009), and Allen vs. Evil (2010). Allen has also written and produced several TV shows, including Home Improvement, Last Man Standing, and Tim Allen's Last Man Standing.
