20 Fantastic Hits is a compilation album by the British rock band Queen, released on 17 November 1984. It is a collection of their biggest hits from their earlier albums, with two new tracks, "I Want to Break Free" and "The Show Must Go On".  The album reached number one in the United Kingdom and was certified Diamond by the British Phonographic Industry (BPI) for sales of over 1 million copies. In December 1984, it was voted the best album of the year in a poll conducted by BBC Radio 1. The album has sold over 10 million copies worldwide.
