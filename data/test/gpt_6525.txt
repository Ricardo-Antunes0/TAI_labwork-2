Lead Balloon is a British television series that was first broadcast on BBC Two from 8 December to 24 December 2000. The show is a comedy-drama set in the fictional offices of the fictitious magazine "The Daily Balloon".  The series is written by and stars Richard Ayoade, who also created and wrote the pilot. The cast also features Nina Peacock, Noel Fielding, Matt Berry, and Bob Mortimer.  Lead Balloon was generally well received by television critics. It has a 74% rating on Rotten Tomatoes based on 27 reviews.
