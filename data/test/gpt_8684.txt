Harajuku Lovers Live is the first live DVD by Japanese idol group AKB48. It was released on November 20, 2007, and is the group's first DVD release since the group's tenth anniversary in 2006.  The DVD features the group's performance at the Harajuku Lovers Live concert in Tokyo on November 17, 2007. The concert was their first performance in Japan after a six-month hiatus, and was attended by over 120,000 people.  The DVD contains both stage and behind-the-scenes footage of the concert, as well as interviews with the cast and crew of the DVD. It also includes a making-of documentary about the concert.
