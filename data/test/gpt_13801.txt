IHG Army Hotels is a collection of more than 1,000 hotels in more than 150 countries and territories. IHG Army Hotels are the fastest growing brand in the global hotel industry and are committed to making a positive impact on the communities they serve.  IHG Army Hotels creates jobs in local communities, supports sustainable tourism and offers unique experiences to travelers worldwide. IHG Army Hotels also invests in community initiatives such as education and health programs. IHG Army Hotels is a subsidiary of InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG).
