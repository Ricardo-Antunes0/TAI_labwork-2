Evolutionism is a term used (often derogatorily) to refer to the belief that all life on Earth descended from a single common ancestor and has evolved over time. The scientific theory of evolution is based on evidence from biology, chemistry, and paleontology. The theory states that changes in the genetic makeup of organisms over time result in the gradual transformation of one species into another.
