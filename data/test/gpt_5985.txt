Fernando Jorge Lima de Moraes (born 21 July 1965) is a Brazilian jurist and politician who has been the Minister of Justice since 21 May 2018. Previously, he was the Attorney General of Brazil, serving in that post from 1 February 2014 to 21 May 2018.  Fernando de Moraes was born in São Paulo on 21 July 1965. He earned a law degree from the University of São Paulo, and later practiced law before becoming a judge in the state of São Paulo. He served as a federal judge for Brazil's Federal District Court from 2003 to 2007, and then became the Attorney General of Brazil in February 2014.
