Lorenzo Dechalus (born September 17, 1968), known professionally as Lord Jamar, is an American rapper, songwriter, and actor best known for his work in the hardcore rap group The Pharcyde and his solo career.  Born in Detroit, Michigan, Lord Jamar began rapping at the age of 14 and formed The Pharcyde with his friends O.C. and Yaniak in the early 1990s. The group released their debut album Bizarre Ride II the Pharcyde in 1992, which received critical acclaim and helped to popularize alternative hip-hop. Lord Jamar's solo debut album The Preface in 1999 was also well received, spawning the hit single "The Show". He has since released four more solo albums: 2009's Donuts, 2011's Black Messiah, 2013's All-American Alien Boy, and 2016's I Will Never Die. In addition to his music career, Lord Jamar has appeared in several films, including Bullet Boy (2005), Roll Bounce (2005), and Just Wright (2010).
