Girl$  is a 2010 Hong Kong-Chinese-Thai romantic comedy film written and directed by Wong Jing and produced by Stephen Chow.  The film is about two young people, a girl and a boy, who fall in love but are unable to overcome the cultural differences between them. The film stars Chow Yun-fat and Fan Bingbing, and was released in Hong Kong on December 25, 2010, in China on January 10, 2011, and in Thailand on February 2, 2011.  "Girl$" has received generally positive reviews from critics. It was praised for its humor and the chemistry between its cast members. It was also acclaimed for its visual effects and action sequences.
