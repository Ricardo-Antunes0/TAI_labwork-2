Sono is a 'tehsil'/block (administrative Block) in the Jamui district of Bihar, India. It has an area of 1,362.87 km² and a population of 136,003 (2001 census).  Sono is located at . It has an average elevation of 269 metres (886 feet).  The tehsil is divided into two Blocks: Sono and Jamui.  Sono is known for the rice mills and coal mines in the area. Jamui is known for its traditional jalebi and halwa shops. There are also many small scale industries in the tehsil.  There are several schools and colleges in Sono block including Rajendra Mahavidyalaya, Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Janki Devi Mahila Mahavidyalaya, Sishu Bhavan School, Govt. Girls Higher Secondary School, Govt. Boys High School and Gyanodaya Vidyalaya. There are also a few hospitals in the Block such as Panchsheel Hospital, SN Medical College Hospital and Sushila Devi Hospital.
