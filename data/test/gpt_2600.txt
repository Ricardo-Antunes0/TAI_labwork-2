"Flower Road"  is a song by the British rock band Queen. It was released as the fourth single from their album A Night at the Opera in 1985. The song reached number six on the UK Singles Chart.  The song is about a love affair that goes wrong. The lyrics describe a journey down a road lined with wildflowers, which leads to an unhappy ending.  The song was written by guitarist Brian May and bassist John Deacon. It was produced by Queen and Roy Thomas Baker.
