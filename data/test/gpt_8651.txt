Rico Bell ( Eric Bellis) is a Canadian actor and musician. He is known for his roles as Kevin Pearson on the Canadian soap opera "All My Children" and as Norm Peterson on the Fox series "King of the Hill".  Bell was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He began his acting career in the late 1990s, appearing in television series such as "The Outer Limits" and "The X-Files". In 2001, he landed his breakthrough role as Kevin Pearson on the Canadian soap opera "All My Children". He played the role until its cancellation in 2006. Since then, he has appeared in several films and television series, most notably playing Norm Peterson on the Fox series "King of the Hill" from 2007 to 2012.  Bell is married to actress Kathryn Greenwood and has two children.
