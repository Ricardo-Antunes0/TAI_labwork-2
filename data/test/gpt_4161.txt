Big Barn Farm is a British live-action family farm located in North Yorkshire, England. The farm has been working with a family of pigs since 1978, and has since expanded to include sheep, poultry, and bees.  The farm produces a wide variety of fresh produce, including pork, lamb, chicken, eggs, and honey. They also have a small shop on site that sells fresh produce as well as local cheese and bread.  The farm is open to the public Monday-Saturday from 10am-4pm, and Sunday from 1pm-4pm.
