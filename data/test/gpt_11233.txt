Velta Toma (31 January 1912 – 26 February 1988) was a Croatian painter, sculptor and graphic artist.  Born in Zagreb, Toma studied at the Academy of Fine Arts in Zagreb under Ivan Meštrović and Josip Hatzidakis. He worked in sculpture, painting and graphic arts and lived and worked in Zagreb until his death. He was a member of the Croatian Artists' Association, the Association of Independent Artists of Croatia and the Union of Soviet Artists. In 1980 he was awarded the Ivan Meštrović Medal.
