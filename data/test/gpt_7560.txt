Richard Whittle (born 31 December 1981) is an English professional footballer who plays as a striker for Championship club Reading. He has also played in the Premier League for Manchester City and Hull City.  Whittle began his career at local club Stockport County, before signing for Manchester City in 2002. He spent four seasons at the club, scoring 25 goals in 97 appearances, before joining Hull City in 2006. He spent three seasons at the club, scoring 34 goals in 107 appearances, before joining Reading in 2009. Whittle has since made over 200 appearance for the club, scoring 56 goals.
