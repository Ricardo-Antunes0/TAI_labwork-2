Morton is a Finnish restaurant chain consisting of 24 restaurants in Finland and Estonia. The first restaurant opened in Helsinki in 2009.  Morton was founded by Juha Morton, who is also the company's CEO. Morton is headquartered in Helsinki. The chain has a focus on modern European cuisine with an emphasis on local ingredients.   The restaurants are all located in busy shopping districts, such as the Central Square of Helsinki and the Kadriorg area of Tallinn. Morton has been awarded two Michelin stars for two consecutive years (2017, 2018).
