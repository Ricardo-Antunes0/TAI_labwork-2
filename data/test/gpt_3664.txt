Nicola Jane Reynolds (born 26 July 1972, in London, England) is a British actress, best known for her roles in the television series Spooks and Skins. Reynolds also appeared in the films Casualty and 28 Weeks Later.  Reynolds was born in London, England, to parents who were both actors. She has two siblings, one of whom is also an actor. Reynolds attended the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA), where she was awarded an honorary degree in 2013.
