The Firefly was the first operetta written by Jerry Herman. The libretto was written by Herman and Oscar Hammerstein II. The opera was first performed on October 25, 1943 at the New York State Theatre.  The Firefly tells the story of a young man who falls in love with a woman who is already married. The opera features a number of popular songs, including "The Boy Next Door", "The Lady Is a Tramp", and "The Galloping Gertie".
