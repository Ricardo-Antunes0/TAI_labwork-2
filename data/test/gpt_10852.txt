The Mampuri Wind Farms (also known as the Mampuri Wind Power Project) is a proposed wind power project in Mampuri, Balochistan, Pakistan. The project consists of 43 wind turbines with a total installed capacity of 52 MW.   The Mampuri Wind Farms project has been proposed by the Balochistan Renewable Energy Development Board (BREDB). The project is expected to cost around US$86 million and is planned to be completed by the end of 2011. The wind turbines are to be manufactured by the German company Enercon.
