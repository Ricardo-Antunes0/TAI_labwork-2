Stephen Rhys Browne (born 16 November 1995) is an English actor, best known for his role as Max Brown in the British soap opera EastEnders.  Browne was born in London, England, to a Welsh mother and an Irish father. He has two elder sisters, one of whom is actress Morgana O'Reilly. Browne began his acting career at the age of 10, appearing in a number of television commercials. He made his professional stage debut in the 2006 West End production of War Horse. Browne's breakthrough role came in 2008 when he was cast as Max Brown in the British soap opera EastEnders. He left the programme in 2012 to pursue other acting opportunities. In 2013, Browne made a guest appearance on the American television series The Mentalist and starred in the British feature film The Three Musketeers.
