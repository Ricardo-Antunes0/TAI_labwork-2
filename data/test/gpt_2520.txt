Brice Meuleman, S.J., D.D. (1 March 1862, Lier â€“ 25 March 1936, Brussels) was a Belgian Jesuit priest and theologian.  Brice Meuleman was born on 1 March 1862 in Lier, Belgium, one of the five children of Jean Baptiste Meuleman (1806-1892) and Jeanne-Louise Gheysens (1812-1892). He was educated at the Jesuit College of Lier and then studied theology at the University of Louvain.  In 1883 he was ordained a priest and from 1887 to 1905 he served as a professor of dogmatic theology at the Jesuit college in Brussels. In 1905 he became a member of the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith.  Meuleman died on 25 March 1936 in Brussels.
