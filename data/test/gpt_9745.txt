East Itchenor is the site of a former RAF airfield, which is now a business park. Itchenor was home to the Royal Air Force's No. 2 Flying Training School from 1938 until it closed in 1971. The airfield was also the home of No. 227 Operational Training Unit, RAF Regiment from 1966 until its closure in 1992.  East Itchenor is located in the county of Hampshire, England, some 12 miles (19Â km) east of Southampton. The A34 road runs through the site and there are regular bus services to Southampton and Bournemouth.
