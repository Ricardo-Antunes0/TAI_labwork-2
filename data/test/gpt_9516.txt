Christopher James Evans (born 1 April 1966) is an English media personality, actor and presenter. He is best known for his role as the lead character in the British sitcom, Top Gear, and as the first host of the U.S. late night talk show, Late Night with Seth Meyers.  Evans was born in Bromsgrove, Worcestershire, to a working-class family. He attended Bromsgrove School and Worcester College, University of Oxford, where he studied English Literature. After graduating from university, Evans worked as a journalist for a number of publications before becoming a presenter on UK children's television show The Big Breakfast. He then progressed to presenting roles on UK shows such as Saturday Live and Newsnight before being cast in the role of lead character in the British sitcom Top Gear.  Since leaving Top Gear in March 2015, Evans has starred in the U.S. television series Runaways and appeared in the films Fantastic Four and Passengers. In 2018, he starred as Ephraim Goodweather in Marvel's Black Panther film.
