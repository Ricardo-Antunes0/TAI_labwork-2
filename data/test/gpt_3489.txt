Gortnahoe , also known as Gortnahoo, is a small community located in the Municipality of Tullamore, County Offaly, Ireland. It is situated on the R335 regional road, 3 km east of the town of Tullamore and 5 km south-west of the village of Kinnegad.  Gortnahoe has a population of just over 100 people, most of whom are farmers. The community is made up of three farms: Gortnahoe Farm, Gortnahoo Farm and Gortnahoe Cottage Farm. There is also a small shop, a post office and a public house.
