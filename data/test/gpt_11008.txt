Belinda Jane Cordwell (born 21 September 1965) is an English former actress. She is best known for her roles in the BBC soap opera EastEnders and in the ITV soap opera Coronation Street.  Belinda Cordwell was born on 21 September 1965 in Wigan, Lancashire, England. She began her acting career in the 1980s, appearing in several television roles before landing her breakthrough role as Peggy Mitchell in the BBC soap opera EastEnders. Cordwell remained with the show for six years, before moving to ITV's Coronation Street, where she played Michelle Fowler for nine years. After leaving the show in 2003, Cordwell starred in several television productions, including the 2004 miniseries Elizabeth I and the 2006 film The Return of Sherlock Holmes. In 2009, she began hosting a lifestyle show on UKTV Style.
