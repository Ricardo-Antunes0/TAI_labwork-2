Jasmine Y. Zapata is an American physician who specializes in pediatric gastroenterology.  Jasmine Zapata was born in San Antonio, Texas, on October 15, 1973. She received her undergraduate degree from the University of Texas at Austin in 1996, and her medical degree from the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School in 2001. After completing her residency at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center, she joined the faculty at the University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston in 2004.  Zapata's research focuses on pediatric gastroenterology, and she has authored or co-authored several articles on the subject. She also serves as the associate director of the UT Southwestern Pediatric Gastroenterology Center, and is a member of the American Association for the Advancement of Science, the American College of Gastroenterology, and the Society for Pediatric Gastroenterology and Nutrition.  Zapata is married to Dr. David Zapata, and they have two children.
