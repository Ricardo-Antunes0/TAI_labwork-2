The Chiayi-Tainan Luka (嘉南勇士; literally "Chianan Braves") is a professional basketball team based in Chiayi, Taiwan. The team was formed in 1997 and is currently playing in the Taiwanese Basketball League.  The Chiayi-Tainan Luka is the most successful basketball club in Taiwan. The team has won eight national championships (1998, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2009) and six regional championships (2002, 2003, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2009). The team has also reached the final of the ABL Championship twice (2004 and 2006).
