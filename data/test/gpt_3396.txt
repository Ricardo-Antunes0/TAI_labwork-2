Avan Tudor Jogia (; born February 9, 1992) is an American actor and model. He is known for his role as A.J. DiLaurentis on the ABC Family television series Pretty Little Liars, for which he has been nominated for a Teen Choice Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award. He has two sisters, Sakshi and Shruti. Jogia's ancestry includes English, Scottish, Irish, German, and Italian. He attended North Hollywood High School.  In 2006, Jogia began his acting career by appearing in the short film "Distant Voices" and the television series House of Lies. In 2007, he appeared in the films The Wackness and Integer Shortage. In 2008, he starred in the films My Sister's Keeper and The House Bunny. In 2009, he starred in the films Easy A and The Informant! He also appeared in the 2010 films Jumping the Broom and About Time.  In 2011, Jogia appeared in the films Rock of Ages and Friends with Benefits.
