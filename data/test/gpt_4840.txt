Fed Up! Our Fight to Save America's Children from the Worst Schools in America by Paulette Bourgeois   Fed Up! is a nonfiction book written by Paulette Bourgeois. It was published in 2017 and deals with the issue of schools in the United States. The book has been well received and has been shortlisted for the 2018 National Book Award.  Bourgeois argues that America's schools are not doing enough to help children achieve their potential and that this problem is getting worse. She discusses the various problems that she believes are causing schools to fail children, including poverty, lack of resources, and teacher unions. Bourgeois also argues that America's schools are not doing enough to prepare children for life after high school. She recommends a number of reforms that she believes would help improve schools in America.
