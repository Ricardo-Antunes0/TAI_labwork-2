Timeless is the debut studio album by American singer Goldie. It was released on September 24, 2017, by Goldie's record label GOOD Music. The album was preceded by the single "Trust Me" (2017).   The album was generally well received by music critics, who praised its production and Goldie's vocals. It debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, with first-week sales of 75,000 copies.
