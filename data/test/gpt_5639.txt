Paruthiveeran  is a 2007 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by R. Shankar. It stars Rajinikanth, Aishwarya Rai and Abhishek Bachchan in the lead roles, with Kalabhavan Mani and Radha Ravi appearing in supporting roles.  The film is a remake of the Telugu blockbuster film "Gudaguda" (2005), which was also directed by R. Shankar. The story is about a man who falls in love with a woman who is married to another man.
