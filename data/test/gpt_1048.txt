"Marvin & Chardonnay" is a song by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. The song was written by Swift and produced by Jack Antonoff for her fourth studio album, 1989 (2014).  "Marvin & Chardonnay" is a mid-tempo pop ballad with a orchestral accompaniment. It tells the story of a young couple in love who are struggling to navigate their way through relationship issues. The song has been praised for its emotional depth and its lyrical content, which focuses on the difficulties of maintaining a relationship amid external pressures.  "Marvin & Chardonnay" was first released as the album's third single on October 27, 2014. It peaked at number 55 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 and at number 11 on the Canadian Hot 100.
