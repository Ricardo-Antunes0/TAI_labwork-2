Fan Zhendong (; born 22 January 1997) is a Chinese professional footballer who plays as a defender for Super League club Shanghai SIPG.  Fan started his professional football career with Shanghai SIPG in the 2014 season. He made his debut in the Chinese Super League on 25 October 2014 in a 2-1 home victory against Guangzhou R&F. On 20 September 2018, Fan scored his first goal in the Chinese Super League in a 4-0 home victory over Henan Jianye.
