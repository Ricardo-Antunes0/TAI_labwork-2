Aromas (Spanish for "Perfumes") is a census-designated place (CDP) in San Luis Obispo County, California, United States. The population was 9,948 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Santa Maria-Santa Barbara, CA Metropolitan Statistical Area.  Aromas is located at (35.214827, -120.859576).  According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of , all of it land.  As of the census of 2000, there were 10,053 people, 4,629 households, and 2,582 families residing in the CDP. The population density was 3,433.7 people per square mile (1,343.0/km²). There were 4,879 housing units at an average density of 1,571.7 per square mile (600.5/km²). The racial makeup of the CDP was 88.12% White, 2.09% African American, 0.30% Native American, 6.37% Asian, 0.03% Pacific Islander, 2.71% from other races, and 2.73% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 11.11% of the population.
