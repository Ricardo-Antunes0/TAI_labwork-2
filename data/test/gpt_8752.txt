Defecation is a deathgrind side project formed in the early 1990s in Montreal, Quebec, Canada by vocalist/guitarist Travis Ryan and drummer Arsenault. The band has since disbanded, but reunited in late 2010 to tour in celebration of their 20th anniversary.  Defecation was founded in the early 1990s by vocalist/guitarist Travis Ryan and drummer Arsenault. The band had released two demos before signing to a local record label and releasing their debut album, "Humanity Is Defecation", in 1994. The album was well-received, earning them a spot on the "Billboard" Heatseekers chart and widespread critical acclaim.  The band toured extensively in support of the album, touring with bands like Napalm Death, Morbid Angel and Cannibal Corpse. In 1996, they released their second album, "Torture Rack", which saw even more critical acclaim. The album was also featured on the "NME" "Best of '96" list.  The band disbanded in 1998 but reunited for a one-time tour in 2010 to celebrate their 20th anniversary. Since then, they have not released any new material.
