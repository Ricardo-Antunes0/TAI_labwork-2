It is the most popular manga magazine in Japan, with over 350 million copies sold as of 2006. In 2009, "Weekly Shōnen Jump" became the first manga magazine to be published in color.  The magazine features a mix of original one-shot and short story arcs, as well as serialized stories and collaborations between various authors. Many popular Japanese manga artists have started their careers by drawing stories for "Weekly Shōnen Jump". The magazine also releases graphic novels and special editions based on popular anime and video game series.
