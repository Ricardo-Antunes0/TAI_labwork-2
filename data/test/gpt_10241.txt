Autonomie Project was a fair trade, eco-friendly, and community-based cooperative that operated in the Lower Mainland of British Columbia, Canada from 2000 to 2002.  The Autonomie Project was a cooperative that aimed to provide sustainable and affordable clothing and textile products to local communities in the Lower Mainland of British Columbia, Canada. The cooperative was founded in 2000 by a group of volunteers and employees, and operated for two years before shutting down due to financial difficulties. The Autonomie Project was unique in that it was the first fair trade clothing cooperative in North America, and its products were made from organic materials.
