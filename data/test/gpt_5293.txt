He is a German painter, sculptor and installation artist. Kilfitt studied at the Hamburg Academy of Fine Arts from 1951 to 1957. In 1959 he moved to Paris where he continued his studies at the École Nationale Supérieure des Beaux-Arts from 1959 to 1962. In 1966 he participated in the Venice Biennale and in 1969 he had his first one-man show at the Galerie Agnes Martin in Cologne. He has had over 150 solo exhibitions throughout the world, including documenta 5 (1977), documenta 6 (1982), and documenta 7 (1987). In 2003 he was appointed an Honorary Professor at the University of the Arts London.
