Hell Island is a horror/adventure novella by American author Stephen King. It was first published in his 1978 book, Night Shift. The novel is about a group of people who are kidnapped and taken to an isolated island where they are subjected to various terrifying and violent ordeals.  The story follows the members of a team of trappers who are stranded on the island after their boat breaks down. They are soon faced with creatures that hunt by night, insane people who have been banished there, and a storm that threatens to destroy them all.
