Paul John Manafort Jr. (; born April 7, 1949) is an American political consultant, lobbyist, and former campaign manager and senior counselor to President Donald Trump. He served as the chairman and chief executive officer (CEO) of the Trump campaign from March 2016 until August 2016.  Manafort was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. After attending the University of Virginia and the University of Cambridge, he worked as a political consultant in Washington, D.C. from 1977 to 1983. He then served as an adviser to the presidential campaigns of Gerald Ford and Ronald Reagan before joining the Trump campaign in March 2016. Manafort resigned from his role with the campaign in August 2016 after being investigated by special counsel Robert Mueller for alleged ties to Russian interference in the United States presidential election of that year.  Manafort has been investigated for money laundering, tax evasion, and making false statements to federal investigators. He has pleaded not guilty to all charges.
