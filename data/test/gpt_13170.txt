Canaan Land is a 2020 American comedy-drama film directed by Stephen Frears and written by Steve Zaillian. It is set in the fictional seaside town of West Egg on Long Island, New York.  The film stars Joaquin Phoenix, Emma Stone, Darren Aronofsky, Rachel Weisz and John C. Reilly. The plot follows a group of adults who were children when their parents left for what was supposed to be a short vacation and never returned. Thirty years later, they reunite for the funeral of one of their parents and are forced to confront unresolved issues from their past.   Principal photography began on February 9, 2019 in Long Island, New York.
