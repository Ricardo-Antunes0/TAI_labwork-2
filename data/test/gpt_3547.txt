Peter Graystone is a Christian writer.   Graystone was born in the United States in 1951. He is a professor of philosophy at Boston College. Graystone specializes in ethics, and has written extensively on topics such as the nature of morality, the meaning of life, and the relationship between religion and secular society. He is also the author of several books, including Living With Values: A Guide to Moral Life (1997), The Meaning of Life: An Introduction to Eastern Thought (1999), and The Good Life: A Philosophy of Happiness (2002). Graystone has also published articles in magazines and newspapers, and he has appeared on numerous television shows and podcasts to discuss his work.
