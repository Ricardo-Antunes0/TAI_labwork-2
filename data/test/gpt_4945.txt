The Kahlert Mercantile Store is a historic commercial building located in downtown Hillsboro, Oregon. It was built in 1912 by Maurice Kahlert, a German immigrant who began the business as a shoe store. Kahlert's son, Harry Kahlert, took over the business in the 1930s and continued to run it until his death in 1968. The store is now owned and operated by the Kahlert family descendants.
