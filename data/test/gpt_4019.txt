Mitchell Josef Santner (born 5 February 1992) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship side Queens Park Rangers.  Santner progressed through the youth ranks at Tottenham Hotspur, before joining Queens Park Rangers in January 2011. He made his first-team debut in November 2011, and has since made over 100 appearances for the club. Santner has also represented England at under-17, under-19 and under-21 level.
