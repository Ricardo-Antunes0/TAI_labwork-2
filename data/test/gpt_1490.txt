Sunshine Empire was a Multi Level Marketing company that was based in the United States. The company was founded in 2005 by CEO, Rick Van Zandt.  Sunshine Empire was a Multi Level Marketing company that sold products such as weight loss supplements and home health products. The company claimed to have a successful system that could help people lose weight and improve their health.  Sunshine Empire was controversial because it was accused of being a pyramid scheme. The company was sued by the Federal Trade Commission in 2007, and was forced to shut down later that year.
