Ministry of Testing, also referred to as MoT, is the governmental body responsible for ensuring quality in the Malaysian manufacturing industry. It was established on 1 January 1985 as the Ministry of Industry and Trade.  The MoT is responsible for setting and enforcing quality standards in Malaysian manufacturing industries and for providing technical assistance to manufacturers. It also oversees the testing and certification of products in Malaysia. The MoT is also responsible for the development and implementation of industrial quality management systems.  The MoT is divided into four divisions: Industrial Quality Assurance, Product Certification, Testing, and Inspection. Each division is headed by a director general. The MoT also has a research arm, the Institute of Quality Assurance, which conducts research on quality issues in Malaysian manufacturing industries.
