The 2015 IKEA stabbing attack occurred on Saturday, 10 September 2015 at the IKEA Västerås store in Västerås, Sweden.   A Somali-born man, Muhyadin M. (30), stabbed eight people, four of them fatally, before he was subdued by security personnel and taken into custody. He had arrived in Sweden in 2014 as an unaccompanied minor and had been living in Västerås with family members. The motive for the attack is unknown.
