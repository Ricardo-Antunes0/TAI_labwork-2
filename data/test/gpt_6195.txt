"Whenever, Wherever" is a song by Colombian singer Maluma. The song was written by Maluma, Benny Blanco, and Shaffer Smith. It was released on November 11, 2017, as the third single from his fourth studio album "El Dorado".  The song is an electro-influenced ballad with a tropical house beat. Its lyrics talk about a love that never fades no matter where they are in the world.  "Whenever, Wherever" debuted on the Colombian Hot 100 at number 93 and peaked at number one the following week. It became Maluma's sixth number one single in Colombia and his third in a row. The song also reached the top ten in Mexico, Peru, and Venezuela.
