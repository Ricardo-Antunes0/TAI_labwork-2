Victoria Star 2 is a  passenger train operated by the Philippine National Railways. The train is the successor of the Victoria Star and was initially scheduled to commence operations in October 2016, but it was delayed due to a series of engine problems. The train has a maximum capacity of 1,090 passengers and is divided into two classes: Executive Class and Common Class.
