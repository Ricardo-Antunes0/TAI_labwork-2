Alexis Jordan is the self-titled debut and only album released by American R&B singer Alexis Jordan. The album was released on October 8, 2003, by J Records and featured production from The Neptunes, Timbaland and Missy Elliott.  The album spawned four singles: "I Believe in You", "More Than a Woman", "Take Me Away", and "If You Leave Me Now". The album peaked at number six on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, selling over 114,000 copies in its first week.  The album received mixed reviews from music critics. Some praised the album's production and Alexis Jordan's vocals, while others found the album formulaic and unoriginal. Despite the mixed reviews, the album went platinum in the United States.
