Jack Rooke is an English comedian and actor. He is known for his work on the sketch show Fresh Meat, and for his appearances on panel shows such as Mock The Week and 8 Out of 10 Cats.  Rooke was born in London, England, and grew up in Lewisham. After leaving school he worked a number of odd jobs before becoming a comedian in 2002. He started performing on the UK circuit, and then moved to the US to appear on shows such as The Daily Show with Jon Stewart and Late Night with Conan O'Brien. He has also had roles in films such as Hot Fuzz and Peter Jackson's The Hobbit. Rooke is married to actress Kathryn Newton.
