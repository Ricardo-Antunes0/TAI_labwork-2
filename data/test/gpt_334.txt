Sarajevo is a 2014 German-Austrian biographical television drama film directed by Oliver Hirschbiegel. It is based on the life of the war correspondent and photographer Robert Frank.  The film tells the story of Frank's experiences in Sarajevo during World War II, as well as his later travels through the former Yugoslavia. It stars Toni Servillo as Frank and Ivo Van Hove as his editor, Theo van Gogh.  The film had its world premiere at the 2014 Toronto International Film Festival. It was released in Germany on 26 October 2014, and in Austria on 4 November 2014.
