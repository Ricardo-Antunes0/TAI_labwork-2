Maria Lalou (born in 1977) is a French artist. She lives and works in Paris.  Lalou was born in 1977 in the south of France. She studied at the École Nationale Supérieure des Beaux-Arts in Lyon, before moving to Paris in 1999. There, she has exhibited her work at important institutions such as the Centre Georges Pompidou, the Musée d'Art Moderne de la Ville de Paris and the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum. Her art is characterised by its use of everyday objects as mediums for expression, often presenting them in unexpected or humorous ways.
