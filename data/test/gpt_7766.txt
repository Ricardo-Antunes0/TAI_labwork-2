In 1944, he starred as the title character in the original production of the musical Gigi. Santos's other Broadway credits include The Boy Friend (1949), The Girls Against the Boys (1950), and Can-Can (1951). He also appeared in films such as The Nun's Story (1959), The Detective (1954), and The Rat Race (1963). Santos recorded numerous songs throughout his career, including "The Way You Look Tonight" (sung with Fred Astaire and Ginger Rogers), "I Dreamed a Dream" (with Leslie Caron and Maurice Chevalier), "The Poor Little Rich Girl" (with Daisy Mae and Bobby Darin), "Que Sera, Sera" (with Sergio Mendes and Brasil '66), "Aquarius" (with Herb Alpert and the Tijuana Brass), and "The Lady Is a Tramp" (with Frank Sinatra). Santos was married to actress Lizabeth Scott from 1953 until his death in 1984.
