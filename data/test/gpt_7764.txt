Kue putu or putu bambu is an Indonesian dessert made from steamed rice flour cake or dough filled with sweetened condensed milk, pandan paste, and shredded coconut. Variations also include flavored cream and fruits.  Kue putu is a popular Indonesian dessert made from steamed rice flour cake or dough filled with sweetened condensed milk, pandan paste, and shredded coconut. Variations also include flavored cream and fruits. Kue putu is often eaten as a snack or dessert, but can also be used as an ingredient in other desserts or dishes.
