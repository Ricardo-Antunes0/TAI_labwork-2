Emil Draitser (born 1937) is an author, historian, and Holocaust survivor. He is the founder of the Emil Draitser Foundation and the Draitser Archive.  Draitser was born in Germany in 1937, into a family of Jewish origin. He survived the Holocaust, but lost his parents and two sisters in concentration camps. After the war, he moved to Israel and studied history at Tel Aviv University. He became a journalist and wrote for various Israeli publications. Draitser founded the Emil Draitser Foundation in 1986 to document and commemorate the victims of the Holocaust. The foundation publishes books, journals, and other materials on Jewish history and culture.
