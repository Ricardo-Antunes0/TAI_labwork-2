Big Mama is a 2000 short documentary film about an aging, big-breasted, black woman who lives with her daughter and grandchildren in a small Georgia town.  Big Mama is a documentary about an aging, big-breasted black woman who lives with her daughter and grandchildren in a small Georgia town. The film follows Big Mama as she celebrates her 90th birthday, tries to find a new job, and deals with the challenges of life in the 21st century.
