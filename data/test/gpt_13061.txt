Diana Morant Ripoll (born 25 June 1980) is a Spanish actress.  Diana Morant was born on 25 June 1980 in Ripoll, Catalonia, Spain. She is the daughter of actress María Ripoll and director Jordi Morant. Her uncle is filmmaker Jordi Morant.  Morant started her career at the age of four, appearing in a number of television commercials. She made her film debut in 1994 with the Spanish thriller film El Pecado de mi padre (My Father's Sin). Morant has appeared in a number of films throughout her career, including the Spanish thriller films La casa de papel (The House of Paper) and La sombra del viento (The Shadow of the Wind), the Spanish drama film Una sola llama (Only a Light), and the French drama film L'Olivier (The Olive Tree).   Morant has also appeared in a number of television series, including La casa de las Flores (House of Flowers), El príncipe de Bel-Air (The Prince of Bel-Air), Las Aventuras de Puss in Boots (Puss in Boots Adventures), and Los Simpson. In 2018, she starred in the Spanish psychological thriller film Los hijos de la noche (The Children of the Night).
