A fluyt (archaic Dutch: fluijt "flute"; ) is a type of small riverboat or sailing vessel found in the Netherlands and Belgium, typically used for fishing. The name is cognate with the English word "flute".  The fluyt is a typical Dutch fishing boat. It is a small riverboat or sailing vessel, typically used for fishing. It has a shallow draft, is usually equipped with a single mast and a square-rigged sail.
