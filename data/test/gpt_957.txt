Sanjeev Ramesh Kulkarni (born September 21, 1963) is a Canadian business executive and the former president and CEO of Sun Life Financial, a Canadian multinational insurance company.  Kulkarni served as Sun Life Financial's president and CEO from 2007 to 2015, during which time he led the company through a period of significant growth and transformation. Under Kulkarni's leadership, Sun Life Financial became one of Canada's leading financial institutions, with assets of over $1 trillion.  Kulkarni also served as the chair of the board of directors for the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board (CPPIB), and was appointed to the boards of directors for BCE Inc., CIBC World Markets Inc., National Bank of Canada, and Royal Bank of Canada.  Kulkarni was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Toronto, and a Master of Business Administration from Harvard Business School.
