Eurasia Aviation Corporation  was a Chinese airline that suspended operations in 2006.  Eurasia Aviation Corporation (EAC; ) was a Chinese airline which suspended operations in 2006. It was founded in 1994, and operated scheduled and charter flights to destinations in Asia, Europe, North America and South America. Its main base was Beijing Capital International Airport.  The airline suspended operations owing to financial difficulties, and filed for bankruptcy in 2006. As of 2009, its assets were auctioned off and its creditors were paid.
