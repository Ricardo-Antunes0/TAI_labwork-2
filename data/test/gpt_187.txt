Kalasipalya is a 2004 Indian Kannada romantic drama film written, directed and produced by S. S. Rajamouli. The film stars R. Narayan, Shruti Haasan and Sathyaraj in the lead roles. The story is set in the fictional town of Kalasipalya and revolves around the love between a rich man's son and a poor girl.  The film was shot across various locations in Karnataka, including Bengaluru, Mysore and Kolar. The music for the film was composed by V. Manohar and the cinematography was done by Praveen Kumar. "Kalasipalya" was released on 7 October 2004 to positive reviews from critics, who praised the cast and story-line.
