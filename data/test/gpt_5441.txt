SS Wexford was a steel-hulled, propeller-driven, cargo ship that was built in 1915 as the Canadian Pacific Steamship Company's SS Wexford. She was sold in 1922 to the United States Shipping Board and renamed SS Kingsland. In 1934, she was sold to the International Mercantile Marine Co., Ltd., of London, and renamed SS Canberra. In 1945, she was sold to the Greek government and renamed SS Orestes. In 1955, she was sold to the Spanish government and renamed SS Europa. She was scrapped in 1960.
