After leaving school he worked as a clerk in a solicitor's office before studying music at the Royal Academy of Music in London. In 1909 he began writing articles for "The Musical Times", and in 1916 he became its assistant editor. He was also a lecturer at the Royal Academy of Music and at the Royal College of Music. Louden died in 1965.
