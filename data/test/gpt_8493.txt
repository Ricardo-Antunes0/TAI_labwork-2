Agent Cody Banks is a 2003 American action comedy film directed by Brett Ratner and written by Evan Daugherty. The film stars Will Smith as a secret agent who is forced to work as a street sweeper to pay off his debts.  Agent Cody Banks was released on August 3, 2003, in the United States and grossed over $246 million worldwide. The film received mixed reviews from critics, with some praising its humor and action sequences, while others found its plot and characters unoriginal.
