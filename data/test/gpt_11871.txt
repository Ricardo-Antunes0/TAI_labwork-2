Gagrellula is a genus of harvestmen in the family Phalangodidae. There are about eight described species in Gagrellula.  These eight species belong to the genus "Gagrellula":  1. Gagrellula flavomaculata 2. Gagrellula intermedia 3. Gagrellula marginata 4. Gagrellula nigra 5. Gagrellula piccola 6. Gagrellula scalaris 7. Gagrellula viridissima
