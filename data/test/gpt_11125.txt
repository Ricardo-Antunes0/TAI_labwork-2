Two Distant Strangers is a 2020 American science fiction drama film written and directed by Australian filmmaker Courtney Solomon. The film stars Guy Pearce and Rebecca Ferguson, and tells the story of a pair of strangers who are forced to reunite after being marooned on a remote island.  The film is produced by Beau Flynn and Alex Heineman, and is one of several projects that Solomon is developing under the banner of her own production company, Rabbit Bandini Productions. Filming began in June 2019 in Queensland, Australia.
