The Lufthansa Innovation Hub in Berlin, Germany, is an innovation and technology center that supports the development of new products, services, processes and business models for Lufthansa.  The Innovation Hub was opened in October 2014 and is based in the former Lufthansa headquarters on Pariser Platz in Berlin. The Hub has a staff of around 120 people and works with partners such as universities, startups, venture capitalists and corporations.   The Innovation Hub focuses on four areas:   Product Development: This area focuses on developing new products and services for Lufthansa.   Processes & Systems: This area develops processes and systems for Lufthansa to improve efficiency and customer service.   Business Models: This area looks at how to develop new business models for Lufthansa.   Technology: This area develops technology for use by Lufthansa.
