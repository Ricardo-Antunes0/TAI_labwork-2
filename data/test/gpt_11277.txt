Zelotibia is a genus of African ground beetle in the subfamily Pterostichinae.   There are about 10 described species in Zelotibia.  These 10 species belong to the genus Zelotibia:  Zelotibia imbricata Zelotibia nigripes Zelotibia obtusa Zelotibia pallida Zelotibia pygmaea Zelotibia senegalensis Zelotibia sulfurea Zelotibia tuberculata
