Blizno  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Czerwionka-Leszno, within Szczecin County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately east of Czerwionka-Leszno and east of Szczecin.  The village has a population of 290.  Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship
