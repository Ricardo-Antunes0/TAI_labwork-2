3 Dumb Clucks is a 1937 short animated cartoon directed by Tex Avery and featuring Oswald the Lucky Rabbit.  The film features three anthropomorphic ducks, Daffy Duck, Elmer Fudd and Bugs Bunny, who are trying to outwit each other. The plot revolves around Daffy's adventures trying to catch a fish with a broken fishing rod; Elmer's attempt to catch a rabbit with a bow and arrow; and Bugs' attempts to catch a bee with a carrot.
