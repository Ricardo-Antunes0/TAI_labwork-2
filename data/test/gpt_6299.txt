Thietmar or Dithmar was a German Christian missionary and chronicler who lived in the late 8th century.  Born in the Westphalian town of Hersfeld, Thietmar is known for his missionary work in Saxony and Thuringia, as well as his written works, which include a chronicle of the German people from the time of the Roman Empire to his own day. He is also known for his descriptions of the East Roman or Byzantine Empire.
