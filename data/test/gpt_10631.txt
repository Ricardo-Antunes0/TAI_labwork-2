She Wolf  is the eighth studio album by American singer-songwriter Melissa Etheridge. It was released on September 18, 2016, by RCA Records. The album was produced by John Shanks and was recorded at various locations in Los Angeles and Nashville.  "She Wolf" is an eclectic and diverse album that explores Etheridge's roots in soul, country and rock music. The album debuted at number four on the "Billboard" 200 with first-week sales of 45,000 copies.  "She Wolf" received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised Etheridge's vocal performance and the variety of the tracks. Some reviewers noted that the album lacks the hooks of earlier releases and found it to be more experimental. However, most commentators found the album to be an enjoyable listen.
