The Yasme Foundation is a non-profit corporation that provides assistance to individuals and families in need. The Foundation was founded in 1984 by Ann and Arnold Yasme.  The Yasme Foundation provides assistance to individuals and families in need through a variety of programs and services. Programs and services include emergency assistance, food banks, housing assistance, financial assistance, education scholarships, and health and human services. The Yasme Foundation also operates the Yasme Community Center, a multi-purpose facility that provides programs and services to the residents of Yasme.
