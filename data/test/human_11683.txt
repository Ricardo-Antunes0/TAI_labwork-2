LM5: The Tour was the sixth concert tour by British girl group Little Mix, in support of their fifth studio album, LM5 (2018). The tour began on 16 September 2019 in Madrid, Spain and concluded on 22 November 2019 in London, England consisting of 40 concerts. A concert film of the tour was released in cinemas worldwide on 21 November 2020 titled LM5: The Tour Film. Background  Little Mix announced the tour on 18 October 2018 via their Twitter account. A second round of shows was announced on 25 October 2018. However, a planned date in Aberdeen on 4 October 2019 was subsequently cancelled due to unforeseen logistical issues. An extra date was added at Porsche-Arena, Stuttgart due to popular demand at 22 September 2019. On 21 March 2019 a new date was added on the Europe Leg.
