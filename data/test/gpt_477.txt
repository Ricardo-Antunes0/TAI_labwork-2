Kindred: The Embraced is an American television series created by Terri Minsky andproduced by Universal Cable Productions. It is a supernatural horror drama series, set in the present day, about a family of vampires who have been living among humans since the end of the Civil War.  The show premiered on October 12, 2014, on the cable network Syfy. On February 10, 2015, it was announced that the series had been renewed for a second season. The second season premiered on October 12, 2015. On July 22, 2016, it was announced that the series had been cancelled after two seasons.  The show has received generally positive reviews from television critics. Rotten Tomatoes has a 73% rating with an average rating of 6.7/10 based on 30 reviews. The site's consensus reads: "Despite its flaws, Kindred: The Embraced offers heartfelt character drama and atmospheric vampire tales." Metacritic has a score of 68 out of 100 based on 17 reviews, indicating "generally favorable reviews".
