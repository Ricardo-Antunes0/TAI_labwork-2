Knight Enterprises is an independent television production company based in Los Angeles, California. The company was founded in 1984 by Mark Gordon, who is its current President and Chief Executive Officer. Knight Enterprises produces a broad range of programming, including scripted and unscripted series, reality programming, and feature films.  The company has produced more than 150 series, including such critically acclaimed programs as "24", "ER", "Law & Order", "CSI: Crime Scene Investigation", "The Office", "Modern Family", "Once Upon a Time", and "Hawaii Five-0". In addition, Knight Enterprises has created or co-created more than 200 television movies and mini-series.  Knight Enterprises has also produced a number of successful feature films, including the Academy Award-nominated drama "The Help" and the blockbuster hit comedies "Knight and Day" and "Ted".
