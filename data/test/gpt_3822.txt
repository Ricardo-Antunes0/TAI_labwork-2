Matthew Mitchell Jr. (born March 18, 1999) is an American professional basketball player for the Detroit Pistons of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats.  Mitchell attended Bishop Gorman High School in Las Vegas, Nevada. As a junior, he averaged 17.3 points, 8.2 rebounds, and 2.8 blocks per game. As a senior, he averaged 22.5 points, 11.8 rebounds, and 3.4 blocks per game. He was named to the All-USA First Team by "USA Today" and was rated as a five-star recruit by both ESPN and Rivals.  In his freshman year at Kentucky, Mitchell averaged 10.5 points, 6.5 rebounds, and 1.9 blocks per game in 31 games (24 starts).
