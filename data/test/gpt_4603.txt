BIG TRAK / bigtrak is a programmable drum machine and synthesizer instrument, originally released in 1984 by Roland Corporation.  The original BIG TRAK was designed as a programmable drum machine and synthesizer instrument, released in 1984 by Roland Corporation. The machine featured 8-voice polyphony, 16 preset sounds, and a built-in sequencer. It was succeeded in 1988 by the BIG TRAK 2, which featured an expanded sound palette, 32 voices polyphony, and a built-in sampler.
