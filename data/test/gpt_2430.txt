The Thane Metro is a  Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) system under construction in Thane, India. It will connect Thane with Mumbai via the northern suburbs of the city. The project is expected to be completed in 2020.  The Thane Metro was conceived in 2008, and the feasibility study was completed in 2010. The project was launched in February 2014, and the tenders for the construction were awarded in December 2014.
