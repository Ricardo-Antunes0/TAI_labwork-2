Diadumene cincta is a small and delicate, perennial, herbaceous plant in the family Asteraceae. It is endemic to the eastern Cape Floristic Region of South Africa. D. cincta grows in moist and shady habitats near streams and rivers, between 600 and 1 000 m altitude.  The genus Diadumene was first described by Carl Linnaeus in 1753. The species D. cincta was first described by Pieter Cramer in 1786.
