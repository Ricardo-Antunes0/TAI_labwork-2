Snowy RWP, also known as  Commando, was a military dog who served with the Australian Army from 2007 to 2013. He was retired in 2013 and died in May 2014.   Snowy was born in 2007, and was initially trained to be a search and rescue dog. He later became a military dog, serving with the Australian Army from 2007 to 2013. During his time with the army, Snowy achieved several notable feats, including being deployed to Afghanistan in support of Operation Slipper in 2012 and participating in the 2013 Australian military intervention into Syria as part of Operation Falconer. In May 2014, Snowy died after suffering a stroke.
