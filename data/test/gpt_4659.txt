Lauren Lenentine (born June 24, 2000) is an American actress, singer and model. She is known for her role as Georgie in the Nickelodeon series "The Loud House".  Lauren Lenentine was born on June 24, 2000, in Austin, Texas, to parents Adrian and Lisa Lenentine. She has two older sisters named Quinn and Piper. She started modelling at the age of 10 and was signed to Ford Models in her hometown at the age of 12. In 2016, she appeared in the music video for "I Don't Wanna Live Forever" by Zedd and Maren Morris. That same year, she made her acting debut in the Nickelodeon series "The Loud House".
