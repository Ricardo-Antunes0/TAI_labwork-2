Ben Orloff (born April 26, 1987) is an American actor known for his roles on the Fox series New Girl and in the films The Fault in Our Stars and The Spectacular Now.  Orloff was born in Los Angeles, California, to Lisa (n√©e Kravitz) and actor/director Peter Orloff. His father is Jewish, and his mother is of Irish and English descent. He has three older sisters: one of whom is actress Mila Kunis. He attended Beverly Hills High School.  Orloff began his acting career in theater at the age of nine, appearing in productions of The Catcher in the Rye, The Elephant Man, and A Streetcar Named Desire. He made his feature film debut in 2007 with a minor role in the independent drama The Unemployed Man's Guide to the Good Life. He became known to audiences after playing Jake Dunphy on the Fox sitcom New Girl from 2011 to 2015. In 2013, he co-starred in the romantic comedy-drama film The Fault in Our Stars as Augustus Waters. In 2014, he appeared in the biographical drama film The Spectacular Now as Augustus "Gus" Waters Jr. Orloff will next appear as Alex Dimitrios in the action thriller film Snowden released in 2017.
