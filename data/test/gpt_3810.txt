Doležal (feminine Doležalová) is a Czech family name. The surname is derived from the Czech word "doležal" meaning "a nickel".   The Doležals are a prominent Czech family, descending from a ducal house which was first mentioned in the 12th century. The family has produced many high-ranking officials and businessmen, and several members have been awarded the prestigious Czechoslovak state honour, the Order of the White Lion.
