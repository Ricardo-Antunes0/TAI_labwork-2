Christina Dieckmann Jiménez (born April 22, 1977) is a Mexican actress and singer. She is best known for her roles in telenovelas such as La casa de la pradera and El señor de los anillos.  Dieckmann was born in Mexico City to parents of German and Swiss descent. She has two sisters and started acting in school plays. Her first television role was in the telenovela La casa de la pradera, where she played the role of Cecilia.  In 2001, Dieckmann starred in the Mexican film El señor de los anillos as the female lead, Galadriel. The film was a blockbuster hit in Mexico and won her numerous awards, including the Ariel Award for Best Actress. She reprised her role in the 2003 sequel, El señor de los anillos: La serie del libro II, which was also a critical and commercial success.  Dieckmann has also appeared in several American films, including The Ladykillers (2004), Thank You for Smoking (2006), and The Ides of March (2011). In 2011, she starred as Margarita in the Spanish-language remake of the romantic comedy-drama film Casa de mi padre.
