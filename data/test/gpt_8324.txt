"New Slang" is a song by American rapper Lil Dicky. It was released on March 15, 2018, as the lead single from his third studio album Professional Rapper 2 (2018).  The song was written by Dicky, Mikky Ekko, and Noah "40" Shebib, with production handled by Ekko and Shebib. "New Slang" is a hip hop song that features guest vocals from American rapper 2 Chainz.  "New Slang" is a satirical song that critiques the use of popular lingo in contemporary society. The song's lyrics discuss the overuse of emoji, emojis being used as replacements for words, and the trend of using catchphrases as metaphors.   Critical reception to "New Slang" has been positive. The song has been widely praised for its lyrical content and clever lyrics. Many critics have also noted the song's humorous tone and its references to popular culture.
