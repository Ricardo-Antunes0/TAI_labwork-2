Canada is a country in North America. It is bordered to the south by the United States of America, to the west by the Pacific Ocean, and to the north by the country of Nunavut. Its capital is Ottawa. Canada is a parliamentary democracy and a constitutional monarchy with Queen Elizabeth II as its head of state.  Canada is a diverse country, with a population of about 33 million people. It is the world's tenth-largest country by total area and the second-largest country in North America after the United States. Canada is a developed country, with a diverse economy that includes sectors such as information technology, automotive manufacturing, and aerospace. The country has three official languages: English, French, and Aboriginal languages.  Canada is an influential nation in the world, with a strong economy and a high standard of living. It is a member of numerous international organizations, including the United Nations, NATO, the G7+5, and NAFTA. Canada is also a member of the Commonwealth of Nations, and the Organization of American States.
