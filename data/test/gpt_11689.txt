MAK Technologies, formerly doing business as VTel, is a telecommunications company headquartered in McLean, Virginia. The company provides voice, data, and video services to businesses and consumers in the United States.  MAK Technologies was founded in 1992 as VTel, a provider of telecommunications services to businesses and consumers. In 2001, the company changed its name to MAK Technologies to better reflect its focus on technology solutions. In 2009, MAK acquired NCC Data Solutions, a provider of cloud-based voice and data communications solutions.  Today, MAK Technologies provides voice, data, and video services to businesses and consumers in the United States. The company's products include VoIP telephony and video services, hosted PBX systems, data communications solutions, and managed security services. It also offers a range of customer support features, including live chat and phone support.  MAK Technologies is headquartered in McLean, Virginia. The company has offices in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Spain, Italy, Belgium, Ireland, Germany, Austria, Sweden and Norway.
