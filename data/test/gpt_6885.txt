Pleitrange (Luxembourgish: Plaitreng) is a rural hamlet in the commune of Vianden, in the canton of Luxembourg.  Pleitrange lies on the right bank of the Sauer, 6 km northeast of Vianden and 8 km southeast of Rumelange. The hamlet has a population of 110.
