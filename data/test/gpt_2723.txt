Raging Silence is the 17th album by the progressive metal band Symphony X. It was released on March 4, 2019 through InsideOut Music.  The album was produced by Michael Romeo and features guest appearances from Joe Lynn Turner and John 5 of the band Slipknot. The album's title is taken from a lyric in the song "The Unforgiven III" and refers to the lack of communication and silence in society.  Symphony X began work on the album in early 2019, shortly after their farewell tour for their 18th album "The Divine Conspiracy". The album was mixed by Chris Bellman and mastered by Tom Coyne.  The first single from the album, "The Divine Conspiracy", was released on January 18, 2019.
