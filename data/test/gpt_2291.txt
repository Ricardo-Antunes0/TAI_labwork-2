Francisco J. Quintana (born April 30, 1974) is an American economist who currently serves as a professor of economics at Stanford University. He also served as the Deputy Assistant Treasury Secretary for Economic Policy from January 2013 until his appointment to the faculty at Stanford in September 2013.  Quintana is known for his work on macroeconomics and fiscal policy and has authored or co-authored several books, including "Macroeconomics: Theory and Policy" (2012), "The Private Sector and Public Policy" (2011), "The Penguin History of Economics" (2009), and "Economic Growth: An Historical Analysis" (2007).  Quintana was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States, on April 30, 1974. He received his bachelor's degree in economics from Amherst College in 1997 and his doctorate in economics from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2003.
