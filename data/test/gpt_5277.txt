La Falange , also known as FE/La Falange or the Falange Española Tradicionalista y de las JONS , is a far-right political party in Spain.  Founded on 17 October 1933, La Falange is a conservative, monarchist, nationalist and Catholic party. The party is considered fascist by some and neo-fascist by others. It has been banned from participating in elections several times.  La Falange advocates for Spanish nationalism, Catholicism and traditional values. Its central slogan is "España para todos", meaning "Spain for all". The party's youth wing is called Falange Juventud.
