Elvebakken Church  is a parish church in Bergen, Norway. It is located on the west side of the city center, just north of the Royal Palace. The church was originally built in the Romanesque style in 1250. It was expanded with a nave and a tower in the 13th century, but it was destroyed by fire in 1666.
