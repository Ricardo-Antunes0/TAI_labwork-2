Marie Lande Mathieu (born November 28, 1956) is an American actress, best known for her role as Marie Fontane on the television series Dynasty from 1981-1985.  Marie Mathieu was born on November 28, 1956 in Beaumont, Texas. She is the daughter of Irma (n√©e Lande) and Gil Mathieu. Her father was a petroleum engineer. Marie has two sisters, Lisa and Shannon. She attended Lamar High School in Beaumont, Texas and graduated in 1974. After high school, she studied drama at the University of Texas at Austin, where she met her future husband, actor David Baer.  Mathieu made her television debut in 1978 with a guest appearance on the TV series The Streets of San Francisco. She then appeared in a number of small roles on shows such as The Waltons, The A-Team, and Miami Vice before landing her breakthrough role as Marie Fontane on the television series Dynasty from 1981-1985. After Dynasty ended, Mathieu guest starred on episodes of such TV shows as Dallas, Falcon Crest, and Becker. She also made several appearances in feature films, including Flashdance (1983), Thelma & Louise (1991), and Mars Attacks! (1996). In 1998 she appeared in a made for TV movie about the life of singer Patsy Cline titled Patsy.
