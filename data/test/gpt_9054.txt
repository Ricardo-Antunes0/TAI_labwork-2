The Quake II engine''' is a game engine used in the first-person shooter video game Quake II. It was developed by id Software, and licensed to several other game developers. The engine is a modified version of the id Tech 3 engine, which was originally developed for the Quake video game series.  The Quake II engine was widely used in first-person shooters for many years, and is still used in some games. It has been compared to the Unreal Engine, and is considered one of the most popular game engines.
