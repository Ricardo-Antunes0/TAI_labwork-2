Soura is a notified area in Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir. The Soura area encompasses the neighbourhoods of Shakoora, Baba Kharak Singh, Ambala, and part of Lal Chowk.  Soura is an important residential and commercial area in Srinagar. The neighbourhood of Shakoora has a significant Muslim population, while Baba Kharak Singh is home to a large Hindu population. Ambala is predominantly Hindu, while Lal Chowk is home to a mix of Muslims, Hindus and Sikhs. Soura is also home to several government offices and hospitals.
