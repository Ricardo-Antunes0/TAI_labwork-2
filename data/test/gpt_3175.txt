The 1917 Bali earthquake occurred at 06:50 local time on December 26, 1917. With a magnitude of 8.3, it was the deadliest earthquake in recorded history. Between 6,000 and 10,000 people were killed, making it one of the deadliest earthquakes in world history. In addition, over 100,000 people were injured and over 1,000 villages were destroyed.
