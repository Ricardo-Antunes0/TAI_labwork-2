"Birthday" is episode 11 of season 3 of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and is written by Joss Whedon and directed by David Solomon.  Buffy's birthday is coming up and she's not sure what to get herself. Her friends suggest gifts, but Buffy doesn't really want anything. She decides to go out with her boyfriend, Spike, and see if he can think of anything special. When they get to a club, they see that it's packed with people celebrating birthdays. Buffy starts to feel left out so she suggests that they leave. Spike doesn't want to, but Buffy insists. They end up getting lost in the crowd and end up having a great time.
