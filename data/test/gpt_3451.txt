"Gatti" is a song by JackBoys, and is the lead single from their upcoming album. The song was written by JackBoys and produced by The Demolition Crew.  "Gatti" is a dance-pop song with a eurodance feel. The song is about a young man's love for a woman and features heavy use of synthesizers and electronic beats.  The song was released on May 15, 2018, and has since been met with critical acclaim.
