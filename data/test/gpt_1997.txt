Irfan Sabir (born December 19, 1977) is a Pakistani-born Canadian Sikhs musician, songwriter and producer.   Sabir was born in Lahore, Pakistan to a Sikh family. Sabir moved to Canada with his family at the age of six, settling in Surrey, British Columbia. Sabir began his musical career in the early 1990s as the lead guitarist for the Sikh band Amritsar. In 2001, Sabir released his debut solo album, "The Journey". Sabir has since released five more albums and has toured extensively throughout North America, Europe and Asia. He has collaborated with a variety of artists including Jai Uttal, Anoushka Shankar and Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan. Sabir has also worked on soundtracks for feature films and television shows.
