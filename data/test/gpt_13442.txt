Wendy Philips is a Canadian author. She was born in Toronto, Ontario, in 1965. She has worked as a journalist and writer since the early 1990s.  Phillips has written two novels, both of which have been published in English. Her first novel, The Imperfectionists, was published in 2009 and won the Arthur Ellis Award for Best Novel. Her second novel, The Weight of Water, was published in 2013 and won the Governor General's Award for English-language fiction.
