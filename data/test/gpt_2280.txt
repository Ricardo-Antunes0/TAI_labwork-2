Suchindra Bali is a Tamil actor, son of actor Sathyaraj and actress Suchindra. He made his debut in the Tamil film industry with the 2012 film "Pattinathar".  Suchindra Bali was born on 18 October 1988 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, to actors Sathyaraj and Suchindra. He made his debut in the Tamil film industry with the 2012 film "Pattinathar".  Bali appeared in a supporting role in the 2013 film "Ponnani", which won him critical acclaim. The following year, he starred in the comedy-drama film "Kadhalil Sodhappu", which became a sleeper hit. In 2015, he starred in the crime thriller "Kaadhal Arasi". The following year, he appeared in the romantic comedy "Kadhal Katti Pillaigal", which was poorly received.  In 2018, he starred in the drama film "Virumandi" and the romantic comedy "Vaaranam Aayiram".
