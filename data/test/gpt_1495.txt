Dmitrii G. Knorre (Russian: Дмитрий Георгиевич Кнорре; born December 22, 1978) is a Russian professional ice hockey center who is currently playing with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Knorre was drafted by the Nashville Predators in the first round, 15th overall, of the 1998 NHL Entry Draft. He played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for six seasons with Nashville and the Chicago Blackhawks before joining the Boston Bruins on a two-year contract as a free agent in 2009. After two seasons with Boston, he was traded to the Edmonton Oilers on March 3, 2011. He signed a one-year contract with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) on July 1, 2013.
