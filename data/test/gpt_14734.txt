Quarantine  is a Russian-Finnish dystopian drama film directed by Ruben Fleischer and written by Aaron Guzikowski. The film stars Charlie Hunnam, Max Martini, Rila Fukushima, and Djimon Hounsou. It is set in a future world where a pandemic has caused the collapse of society and a government quarantine is enacted to try and prevent the spread of the disease.  The film was announced in October 2018, with production starting in March 2019. It is set to be released in the United States on September 14, 2021.
