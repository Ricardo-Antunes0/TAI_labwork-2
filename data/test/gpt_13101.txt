John Haack is an American powerlifter and strongman. He has held the world record for the squat, deadlift and overall weight for a man (set in 2007).  Haack was born in Wichita, Kansas on May 4, 1972. He began powerlifting in 1995 and won the Junior World Powerlifting Championships in 1998. He first set the world record for the squat in May 2007 with a weight of 525 pounds (233 kg). In December 2007, he set the world record for the deadlift with a weight of 1,025 pounds (455 kg).
