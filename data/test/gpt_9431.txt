Alex O'Neill, better known under the stage name Ayokay, is a Canadian rapper and record producer from Brampton, Ontario. He achieved mainstream success in 2016 with the single "In My Feelings" which became one of the most-streamed songs of all time on Spotify.  Ayokay was born on December 9, 1997, in Brampton, Ontario. He started rapping at the age of 13 and first started making beats at 15. He released his first mixtape in 2016 and followed it up with his debut album "In My Feelings" later that year. The album peaked at number 2 on the Canadian Albums Chart and received critical acclaim. The song "In My Feelings" became one of the most-streamed songs of all time on Spotify and charted in several countries worldwide. Ayokay has since released two more singles "Hang With Me" and "Better Not Let Me Go".
