Khandhar (English: Ruins) is a 1984 Indian Malayalam-language film, directed by P. Venu and produced by G. S. Venkatesh. The film stars Mohanlal, Sheela, Jagathy Sreekumar and Sukumaran in lead roles. The story is about a man who returns to his village after many years to find that it has been destroyed and all his friends and family have died. He sets out to find out what happened and finds that the local political leader was responsible.  Khandhar is notable for its music composed by Ilaiyaraaja. The lyrics were written by Vayalar Ramavarma and Kaithapram Damodaran Namboothiri.
