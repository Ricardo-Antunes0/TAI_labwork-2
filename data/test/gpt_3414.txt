Theater der Zeit is a German-language monthly theater magazine published in Berlin since 1956. The magazine covers the theater scene in Germany and abroad.  The Theater der Zeit is one of the oldest theater magazines still published in Germany. It has a circulation of about 10,000 and is published by the Gesellschaft der Freunde der deutschen Theater (Society of Friends of German Theatre). The magazine has been awarded several times with the Deutscher Journalistenpreis.
