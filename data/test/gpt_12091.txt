Joseph McGann (born 1988), who goes under the name Joseph, is an Irish singer, songwriter and musician. McGann's first album, "Joseph", was released in September 2014.  McGann was born in 1988 in Dublin, Ireland. He started playing the guitar at the age of 11 and soon began writing his own songs. In 2009, McGann won the prestigious b3ta songwriting award for his song "The Wrong Boy". In 2012, McGann released his first EP, "Eyes Closed", which received critical acclaim. His second album, "Joseph", was released in September 2014 and features the singles "Invisible" and "Someone Else's Story".
