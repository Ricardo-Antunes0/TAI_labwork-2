Azhagaana Ponnuthan is a 2010 Indian Tamil-language historical drama film written and directed by Dharani. The film stars Akkineni Nagarjuna, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, and Nasser in lead roles, with Gouthami, Kalabhavan Mani, and Sathyaraj appearing in supporting roles.  The film tells the story of a feud between two families over a piece of land in the 19th century Tamil Nadu. The film's plot revolves around the clash between two generations of warriors: the older generation led by Thangamuthu (Nagarjuna) and his son Iyappa (Akkineni Nagarjuna), and the younger generation led by Valliammai (Samantha Ruth Prabhu). The two families wage war over a piece of land, with each side using all means possible to win.
