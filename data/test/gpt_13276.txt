Colin Steele McRae,  (5 August 1968 – 5 November 1994) was a Scottish racing driver who won the 1995 World Rally Championship.  Born in Dunfermline, Fife, McRae began rallying in his home country at the age of fifteen. He made his World Rally Championship debut in 1988, and by 1992 had finished second in the championship. In 1995 he won the championship with a record-breaking points total of 198. He also took three consecutive WRC victories (1996–1998) and finished third in the drivers' standings on three occasions (1995, 1997, 1998). McRae died in a crash during the 1994 Safari Rally.
