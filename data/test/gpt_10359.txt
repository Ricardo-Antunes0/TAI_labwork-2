HMS Swiftsure was a  74-gun third rate ship of the line of the Royal Navy, launched on 10 November 1787 at Woolwich Dockyard.   HMS Swiftsure saw action in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars, notably in the Battle of the Nile in 1798 and the Battle of Trafalgar in 1805. She also participated in the Battle of Copenhagen in 1801 and the Battle of Cape St Vincent in 1804. She was wrecked off Cape Finisterre on 4 November 1809.
