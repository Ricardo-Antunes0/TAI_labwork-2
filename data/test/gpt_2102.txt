Yawara! (also stylized as YAWARA!) is a Japanese visual novel developed by Key and published by MangaGamer. It was released on January 11, 2019 for Windows and macOS.  Yawara! is a story-driven romance game set in a colorful and mystical world of floating islands. You play as Setsuna, a young woman who has lost her way and is seeking redemption. Along the way you'll meet colorful characters and explore the island paradise that is Yawara!  The game follows a branching plot with multiple endings. You can choose to pursue love or adventure, or try to do both at the same time. Yawara! features an extensive cast of characters with their own motivations and desires, making for a rich and engaging story.
