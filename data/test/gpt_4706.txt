Domenico "Mimmo" Jodice (born 24 March 1934, in Catania) is a Sicilian comedian, singer and actor.   Mimmo Jodice is one of the most popular and acclaimed Sicilian comedians. He started his career in the late 1950s as a singer and comedian in provincial nightclubs, before making his television debut in the early 1960s. He achieved national fame in the 1970s with his television show "Biscotti e Mimmo", which ran for over ten years. Jodice has appeared in numerous films and television shows, and has won several awards, including two Golden Globe Awards. He currently lives in Palermo.
