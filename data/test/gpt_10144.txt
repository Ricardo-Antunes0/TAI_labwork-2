Appleton was a Canadian musical duo composed of sisters Shannon and Sarah Appleton.  The sisters, who were born in Fredericton, New Brunswick, in 1984, started making music together at the age of nine. After studying music at the University of Toronto, they relocated to Montreal in 2006 and began recording and touring internationally.   Their debut album, "Between", was released in 2010 on the independent label Painted Palms. The album was well received by music critics, who praised its intimate and atmospheric sound. In 2012 they released their second album, "The Ripening Seasons", which was also well received by critics. Their third album, "Where We Are Now", was released in 2016 and was praised for its emotive and cinematic sound.
