Dwight Eversley Yorke CM (born 3 November 1967) is an English professional footballer who plays as a left winger for Premier League club Manchester United and the England national team.  Yorke began his career at Manchester United, making over 500 appearances for the club before departing for rivals Liverpool in 2005. He spent two seasons at Anfield before rejoining United in 2007. Yorke has won 12 major trophies with United, including three Premier League titles, two FA Cups, and one Champions League. He has also represented England at international level, winning the 1998 FIFA World Cup and the 2004 European Championship.  Yorke was born in Hull, East Yorkshire, to English parents. He started playing football at the age of six for Northern Counties East Football League side Hessle Town.
