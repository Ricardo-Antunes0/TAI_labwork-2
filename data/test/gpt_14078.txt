Michael Burns (born 6 February 1969) is a former English cricketer who played for Derbyshire, Gloucestershire and Northamptonshire.   Burns made his first-class debut for Derbyshire against Yorkshire in 1988. He played Minor counties cricket for Staffordshire from 1988 to 1991, and for Gloucestershire from 1991 to 1994. He made his first-class debut for Northamptonshire against Warwickshire in 1994. Burns played for Derbyshire from 1995 to 1998, and for Gloucestershire from 1998 to 2002. He retired from all forms of cricket in 2002.
