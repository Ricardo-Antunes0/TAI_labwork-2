"Dat Sexy Body" is a song recorded by American singer and songwriter Justin Timberlake. It was written by Timberlake, Timbaland, and Danja. The song was produced by Timberlake and Timbaland. "Dat Sexy Body" is a hip-hop and R&B song that samples the 1998 single "One More Time" by British duo Wham! The song was released as the lead single from Timberlake's third studio album, The 20/20 Experience (2013).  The song was met with generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Timberlake's vocals. It reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming his sixth number one on the chart. It also topped the charts in Canada, Denmark, Finland, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden and Switzerland. The accompanying music video for "Dat Sexy Body" was directed by Jake Nava and features actress Mila Kunis.
