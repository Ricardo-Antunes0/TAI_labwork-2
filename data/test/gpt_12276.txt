Douglas Alexander Allan, CBE, FRSGS, FRSE, FMA (born 18 December 1945) is a Scottish Labour Party politician who was the Member of Parliament (MP) for Paisley and Renfrewshire South from 1987 to 2015.  Allan was educated at Craiglockhart Secondary School in Edinburgh and the University of Edinburgh, where he graduated with an MA in History. After university, Allan worked as a teacher and later as a journalist. He was elected to the Paisley and Renfrewshire South constituency in 1987, and served as its MP until his retirement at the 2015 general election.  Allan was appointed a Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE) in the 2013 Birthday Honours for services to politics. In November 2014, he was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society of Edinburgh (FRSE).
