Kalaphanta is a town in Banke District in the Bheri Zone of south-western Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 4,068 people living in 656 individual households.   Kalaphanta is located in eastern Banke District, at the southern end of the Banke Valley. The town is bordered by the Terai on the east, by Humla on the south, and by Kaski Zone on the west.
