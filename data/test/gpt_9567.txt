"Senza una donna"  is a song by Italian singer-songwriter Luciano Ligabue. The song was released as the second single from his tenth studio album, "Senza una donna" (2003).   The song was written by Ligabue and produced by him and Giorgio Tuinfort. It features vocals from Italian singer Mina.   "Senza una donna" is a ballad which tells the story of a man who has been hurt by love. Ligabue has said that the song is about "the loss of innocence."   The music video for "Senza una donna" was directed by Dado Moroni and filmed in Rome in 2003. It features Ligabue and Mina performing the song in a derelict building.
