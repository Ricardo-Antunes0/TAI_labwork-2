Evelyn Mora (born Kimia Moradzadeh; April 9, 1968) is a Mexican-born American actress and singer. Mora has appeared in both Hollywood and independent films, and has also sung in several languages.  Mora was born in Mexico City to Iranian parents, and moved to the United States when she was two years old. She studied at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts before making her film debut in the thriller film Urban Legend (1998). Mora went on to appear in such films as The Rules of Attraction (2000), The Village (2004), The Private Lives of Pippa Lee (2009), and Non-Stop (2014). Mora has also sung in several languages, including English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Japanese, and Persian.
