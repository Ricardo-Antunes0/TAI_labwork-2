Theo Hobson (born 1972) is a British artist who has been working in the video, performance and sound art mediums since the early 1990s. He is known for his installation-based work and for his use of technology in his work.  Hobson was born in 1972 in Stoke-on-Trent, Staffordshire, England. He studied Fine Art at Goldsmiths, University of London from 1990 to 1994. Hobson has exhibited his work internationally, including at the Museum of Contemporary Art, Chicago (2012); Centre Pompidou, Paris (2012); Museum of Contemporary Art Krakow, Poland (2010); and the Irish Museum of Modern Art, Dublin (2009). In 2013 he was awarded the prestigious Stefan Freund Award by the German foundation Kunstfonds.
