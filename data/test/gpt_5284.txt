Déwé Gorodey or Déwé Gorodé, (born 1 October 1951 in the town of Gorodey, Belarus) is a Belarusian folk musician and composer.  Déwé Gorodey was born in 1951 in the town of Gorodey, Belarus. He started playing the accordion at the age of six and later became interested in folk music. In 1974 he graduated from the State Musical College in Minsk, and started working as a musician and composer. He has performed with many Belarusian and Russian folk ensembles, and his compositions have been recorded by such renowned musicians as Valery Gergiev, Natalia Gutman, Yuri Bashmet, and Evgeny Kissin. He has also taught music at the State Musical College in Minsk.
