HD 96566 is a single star in the northern constellation of Ursa Major. With an apparent magnitude of 3.94, it is faintly visible to the naked eye. Based on an annual parallax shift of 17.84 mas as seen from Earth, it is located about 430 light years from the Sun.  HD 96566 is a G-type mainsequence star with a stellar classification of G2 V. It is around 150 million years old and has a mass around 1.9 times that of the Sun. The star is radiating around 130 times the solar luminosity from its photosphere at an effective temperature of 6,500 K.
