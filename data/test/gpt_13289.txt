Grace Graupe-Pillard is an American artist from Oregon. She is known for her paintings of landscapes and seascapes, which often depict the landscape of her home state.  Graupe-Pillard was born in Salem, Oregon in 1967. She studied art at the University of Oregon, graduating with a BFA in 1989. She has exhibited her work nationally and internationally, including at the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston; the Whitney Museum of American Art, New York; and the Kunstmuseum Bern, Switzerland. Graupe-Pillard has been awarded fellowships from the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation, the Pollock-Krasner Foundation, and the National Endowment for the Arts.
