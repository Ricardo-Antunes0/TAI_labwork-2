GoMedia is a British company which supplies software to the media and advertising industries. It was founded in 2003 by Guy Goodwin, Dave Smith and Matt Short.  The company's products include a range of advertising management software, as well as video and print production tools. GoMedia has clients in the UK, US, Europe and Asia.
