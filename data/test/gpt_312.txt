Kevin John Sheldon (born 14 June 1956) is an English actor and musician. He is known for playing the roles of Dr. Sheldon Cooper on the American television sitcom The Big Bang Theory, and for his role as Alan Partridge in the BBC sitcom I'm Alan Partridge.  Sheldon grew up in the town of Gravesend in Kent and attended Gravesend Grammar School, before studying at the University of Cambridge, where he obtained a degree in Natural Sciences. After graduating, Sheldon worked as a research assistant at CERN before becoming a full-time actor. He made his television debut in 1985, and appeared in several British television programmes before landing his first major American role as Dr. Sheldon Cooper on The Big Bang Theory in 2003.
