Alexander James Ashburner Nix (born 1 May 1984) is a British businessman and political consultant who has been Chief Executive of Cambridge Analytica, a data analytics company, since October 2014.  Nix was born in London to an English mother and an American father. He was educated at Eton College and the University of Cambridge, where he studied history. He then undertook a postgraduate course at the London School of Economics.  Nix began his career as a financial analyst in the City of London. In 2007, he co-founded SCL Elections, a political consulting company. SCL Elections worked for the Conservative Party during the UK general election, 2010 and the UK local elections, 2014.  In October 2014, Nix was appointed Chief Executive of Cambridge Analytica, a data analytics company. He has been criticised for his role in the 2016 United States presidential election.
