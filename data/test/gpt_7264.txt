Astroblepus is a genus of fish in the family Astroblepidae. The genus contains only one species, Astroblepus astroblepus.  Astroblepus astroblepus is a small, cryptic fish found in the Amazon and Orinoco River basins in Venezuela, Colombia, and Ecuador. It is the only known member of its genus. Astroblepus astroblepus is viviparous, giving birth to live young.
