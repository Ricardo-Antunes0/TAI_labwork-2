Tapioca Express (品客多; pinyin: Pǐnkèduō) is a Singaporean fast food chain which specializes in tapioca products.  Tapioca Express was founded in 1993 by current CEO and founder Ong Pheng Meng. The chain has over 100 branches in Singapore, Malaysia, Brunei, Indonesia, the Philippines and Thailand. The chain is known for its large variety of tapioca products such as noodles, dumplings, baos and desserts.
