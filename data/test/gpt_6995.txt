Marshlands Conservancy is a 147-acre nature preserve located in the borough of Queens in New York City. The conservancy is bounded by Northern Boulevard to the north, 46th Road to the south, and Kissena Boulevard to the east.  The marshlands preserve was acquired by the city in 1966 and opened to the public in 1974. The conservancy preserves a significant portion of the historic Kissena Marsh, one of New York City's largest wetlands. The marshland is home to a variety of wildlife, including migratory birds, wading birds, and reptiles.
