Meenakshi, also known as Sharmilee or Sharmili, is an Indian film actress who has appeared in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and Kannada films. She has also acted in a few Hindi films.  Born to a Tamil family in Chennai, Meenakshi made her acting debut in the 2000 Tamil film "Kadhalan". She then starred in successful films including the Telugu film "Premam" (2005), the Malayalam film "Vishwaroopam" (2010), and the Kannada film "Dharma" (2011).
