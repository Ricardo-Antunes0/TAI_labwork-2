Robert Emmet Chambers Jr. (born September 25, 1951) is an American convicted felon, who was sentenced to life in prison for the 1978 murders of his parents in their home in Menard County, Texas.  Robert Chambers was born on September 25, 1951, in Menard County, Texas. He was the third of four children. In 1974, Chambers married Sarah Darlene McClendon and the couple had two children.  In 1978, Chambers killed his parents with a shotgun. He then stole their car and fled to Arkansas. Chambers was eventually arrested in Louisiana and extradited back to Texas to face charges. He was convicted of murder and sentenced to life in prison.
