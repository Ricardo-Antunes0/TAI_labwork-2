Ram Mudambi is the Frank M. Speakman Professor of Religious Studies at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He is the author of over a dozen books, including "The Hindu Temple in America: Religion, Politics, and Architecture" (Princeton University Press, 2004), "Sanskrit and the Indian Renaissance" (Columbia University Press, 2002), "The Hindus: An Alternative History" (Oxford University Press, 1998), and "Textual Authority in Hinduism" (Columbia University Press, 1996).  Ram Mudambi received his B.A. from the University of Bombay in 1966 and his M.A. and Ph.D. from Columbia University in 1970 and 1973, respectively.
