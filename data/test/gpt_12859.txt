Dead Winter Dead is the ninth studio album by American death metal band Cannibal Corpse, released on April 6, 2019 through Metal Blade.  The album is Cannibal Corpse's first release with new drummer Alex Webster, who replaced Chris Kummerer in January 2019. It is their first album with guitarist Sergio Vega since 2016's "Evisceration Plague". The album was produced by the band and mixed by Jens Bogren.  The album's title track is the first single and was released on February 1, 2019. The song is about the 2017–2018 Russian military intervention in Syria.  The album has been met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album has received an average score of 79, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  Pitchfork wrote that "Dead Winter Dead" "finds [Cannibal Corpse] in fine form", rating the album 8.5 out of 10. Metal Injection rated the album 4 out of 5 stars, writing "though it’s not as exhilarating as some of their best work, there’s no denying that this is an accomplished and well-rounded offering from one of metal’s most iconic bands."
