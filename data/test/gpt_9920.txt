The Polygar Wars or Palaiyakkarar Wars were a series of three wars fought by the Tamil people against the Sri Lankan government. The first war, fought in the late 19th century, was a civil war between the native rulers of the Tamil region and the British. The second war, fought in 1971, was a conflict between the Sri Lankan army and the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam (LTTE), a militant separatist organization. The third war, fought in 2009, was a conflict between the Sri Lankan army and the Tamil National Army (TNA), an armed separatist organization.
