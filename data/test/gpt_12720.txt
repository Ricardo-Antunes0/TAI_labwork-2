Blair was born in Edinburgh on 8 October 1815, the daughter of the Duke of Buccleuch and his wife, the Hon. Charlotte Scott. She was styled as Duchess of Buccleuch from 1847 until her death in 1892. Blair was a prolific author, writing novels, short stories, poetry and articles. She also campaigned for women's rights and for the abolition of slavery.  Blair was made a Dame Grand Cross of the Order of the Thistle in 1882 and a Dame of the British Empire in 1887. She died at her home in Dumfries on 20 March 1892.
