Joseph Carl "JC" Tretter Jr. (born February 3, 1984) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Minnesota Twins of Major League Baseball (MLB). He previously played for the Chicago Cubs, Milwaukee Brewers, and Cleveland Indians.  Tretter attended the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where he played college baseball for the Illinois Fighting Illini. He was drafted by the Cubs in the seventh round of the 2004 MLB draft. He made his MLB debut with the Cubs in 2006, and has since been a regular member of their bullpen.  In 2017, Tretter was traded to Milwaukee in a trade that sent Jesus Aguilar to Cleveland. In 2018, Tretter was traded to Minnesota in a deal that sent Trevor Story to Milwaukee. Tretter has been a key part of the Twins bullpen throughout his career.
