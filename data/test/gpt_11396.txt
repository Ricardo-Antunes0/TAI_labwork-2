Nina Sky is an American musical duo, consisting of sisters Nina Sky and Skylar Grey. The duo was formed in 2007 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.  Nina Sky was formed in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 2007 by sisters Nina Sky and Skylar Grey. The duo released their debut album, "In the Room", in 2009. The album was produced by Grammy Award-winning producer Swizz Beatz and peaked at number six on the "Billboard" 200. The lead single, "Lose My Mind", reached number four on the Billboard Hot 100.  The duo followed up their debut album with the EP "Nina Sky vs. Nina Sky" (2010). The EP featured guest appearances from Fabolous, Lil Wayne, and T-Pain. They released their second album, "Sky's the Limit", in 2011. The album featured the singles "Can't Tell Me Nothing" and "I Wanna Love You".   The duo returned with their third studio album, "New Rules", in 2013. The album spawned the singles "Good Girls Gone Bad" and "She Got It Goin' On". They released their fourth studio album, "Nina Sky IV", in 2016. The album featured the singles "Walking on Sunshine" and "Money Maker".
