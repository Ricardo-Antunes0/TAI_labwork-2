Shehzad Sheikh  is a Pakistani television and film actor, who works in both Urdu and English language cinema. He has appeared in a number of critically acclaimed dramas, including "Tum Se Hi" (2010), "Om Shanti Om" (2011), "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai" (1997) and "Walking on Sunshine" (2007).  Shehzad Sheikh was born in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan on 2 February 1974. He attended the National College of Arts from 1991 to 1994. After working as an actor and assistant director for a number of years, he made his television debut with a guest appearance in the drama series "Tum Se Hi" in 2010. He then starred in the critically acclaimed drama series "Om Shanti Om", which aired on Pakistan's Geo TV network from 2011 to 2013. In 2013, he starred in the Bollywood film "Tere Bina", which was directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. The following year, he appeared in the English-language film "Walking on Sunshine", which was directed by Khalid Mohammed. In 2017, he appeared in the drama series "Dilwale" on Hum TV.
