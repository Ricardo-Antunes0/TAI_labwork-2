Aerodium is a designer and producer of aircraft and spacecraft materials and systems. Headquartered in La Jolla, California, the company has operations in the United States, Canada, Europe, Asia and Australia.  Aerodium was founded in 1971 by Laurence G. Zink and Hugh L. Young. The company's first products were aircraft materials and systems. Aerodium has since expanded its product line to include spacecraft materials and systems. Aerodium's current products are used in a variety of aircraft applications such as airliners, military aircraft, helicopters and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs).  Aerodium has offices in the United States, Canada, Europe, Asia and Australia. The company has production facilities in the United States, Canada, Europe and Asia.
