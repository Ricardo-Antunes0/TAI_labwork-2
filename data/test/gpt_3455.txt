Dharangaon is a city and a municipal corporation in Raigad district in the Indian state of Maharashtra.  Dharangaon is located at . It has an average elevation of 1023 metres (3352 feet).  The city is located on the coast of the Arabian Sea. The nearest airport is Jaipur International Airport, which is away. The city has a railway station on the Mumbai-Chennai Main Line.   India census, Dharangaon had a population of 144,436. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Dharangaon has an average literacy rate of 69%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 78%, and female literacy is 62%. In Dharangaon, 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
