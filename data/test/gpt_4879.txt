The discography of MercyMe, an American Christian rock band, consists of eleven studio albums, one extended play, fifty-one singles, and thirty-four music videos.  MercyMe was formed in 1992 in Tempe, Arizona by Bart Millard and Rick Hearn. The band released its first album, "Pushing the Limits" in 1995. The album sold over 30,000 copies and reached No. 30 on the "Billboard" Christian Albums chart. The band's second album, "Walking on Water", was released in 1997 and also sold over 30,000 copies. The album reached No. 4 on the "Billboard" Christian Albums chart and No. 5 on the "Billboard" Heatseekers Albums chart. MercyMe's third album, "So Much More to Me", was released in 1999 and also reached No. 4 on the "Billboard" Christian Albums chart and No. 5 on the "Billboard" Heatseekers Albums chart. The album was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). MercyMe's fourth album, "Fading West", was released in 2001 and reached No. 1 on the "Billboard" Christian Albums chart and No. 2 on the "Billboard" Heatseekers Albums chart.
