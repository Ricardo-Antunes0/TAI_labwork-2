The Saura Purana  is one of the eighteen Mahabharata-era Sanskrit texts, describing the history of the Saura clan of warriors. It is also called the Saura Puraá¹‡a.  The text is about 1000 verses long and covers events from the time of the Mahabharata to about 100 BCE. It is an important source of genealogical information about the Saura clan.  The text is notable for its description of a battle between the Pandavas and the Kauravas, as well as its account of a pilgrimage by the sage Vyasa to heaven.
