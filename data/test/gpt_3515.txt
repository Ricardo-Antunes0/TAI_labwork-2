American R&B recording artist Kelly Rowland began her music career in the late 1990s as a backup singer for TLC before breaking out as a solo artist with the release of her debut album Ms. Kelly in 2000. Rowland achieved international success with her 2002 single "Motivation" and subsequent albums Simply Kelly (2002), Here I Am (2005), and My Destiny (2007). Rowland has sold over 80 million records worldwide, making her one of the best-selling music artists of all time. In addition to her solo work, Rowland is also a member of the girl group Destiny's Child.  Rowland has released seven studio albums, three compilation albums, forty-six singles, and twenty-one promotional singles. She has won several awards, including five Grammy Awards, three American Music Awards, three Soul Train Awards, and two Brit Awards. She has also been nominated for three BET Awards, two NAACP Image Awards, and two World Music Awards. In 2013, she received an Honorary Doctorate from Howard University.  Kelly Rowland videography
