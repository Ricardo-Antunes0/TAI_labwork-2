Stuck Rubber Baby is a 1995 graphic novel by American cartoonist Charles Schulz. It is the seventh and final book in the Peanuts series, and was followed by A Charlie Brown Christmas.  The story follows Linus Van Pelt as he attempts to free his rubber doll, Lucy, from a jammed toy chest. He winds up solving various puzzles and riddles to get her out, with the help of his friends Snoopy, Woodstock, and Pig-Pen.
