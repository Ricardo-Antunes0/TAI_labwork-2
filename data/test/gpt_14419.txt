{{Infobox writer | name          | image        Arthur Hailey (born 12 October 1923) is an English-American author best known for his novels about wealthy people and their adventures in Hawaii.  Hailey was born in London, England, the son of a diplomat. He attended the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst and served in the British Army in North Africa and Italy during World War II. After the war, he studied at Oxford University. He moved to the United States in 1957. Hailey has written thirty-six novels, all set in Hawaii and based on his own experiences living there. His books have been translated into thirty languages.  Hailey has been married three times and has six children. He currently resides on Maui, Hawaii. }}
