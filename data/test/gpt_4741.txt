Takudzwa Ngwenya (born 22 July 1985) is a Zimbabwean footballer who most recently played for Western Cape Premier Division side Ajax Cape Town. Ngwenya has also played for Zimbabwe, Botswana, and South Africa.  Ngwenya started his career with Zimbabwe club Army before moving to Botswana club Platinum Stars in 2006. He then moved to South Africa to play for Kaizer Chiefs in 2007. Ngwenya joined Ajax Cape Town in the 2010/11 season.  Ngwenya has made 21 appearances for the Zimbabwe national football team and has been a part of the team at the 2010 and 2014 World Cup qualifiers.
