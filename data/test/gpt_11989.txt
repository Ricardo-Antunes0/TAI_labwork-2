The Kop Hill Climb is a popular hillclimb in the East Hampshire district of Hampshire, England. The course is just over 1.5 miles long and starts and finishes at Kop Hill golf club. It is a popular event with drivers, riders, and motorcycle enthusiasts from all over the UK and Europe. The event takes place over two days: the first day is a qualifying event which determines the starting grid for the main event on the second day.
