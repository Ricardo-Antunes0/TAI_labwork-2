Alex Beaulieu-Marchand (born March 3, 1994) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with the Montreal Canadiens of the National Hockey League (NHL).  Beaulieu-Marchand was drafted by the Canadiens in the first round, 13th overall, in the 2011 NHL Entry Draft. He made his NHL debut on October 9, 2013 against the Pittsburgh Penguins.
