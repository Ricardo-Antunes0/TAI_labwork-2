Idrissa Camara is a dancer and choreographer from Senegal who has worked on films such as Slumdog Millionaire and Youth.  Idrissa Camara was born in Dakar, Senegal, in 1978. She studied dance at the National School of Contemporary Dance in Dakar and then went on to study choreography at the Royal Danish Ballet School in Copenhagen.  Camara debuted as a dancer with the Dakar Opera in 1997, and went on to work on a number of films, including Slumdog Millionaire and Youth. She has also created numerous choreographies, including a piece for the London 2012 Olympic Games.  In 2013, Camara was appointed artistic director of the National School of Contemporary Dance in Dakar.
