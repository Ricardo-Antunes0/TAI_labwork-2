Cusi Cram (born September 22, 1967) is an American artist, musician, and filmmaker.  Cusi Cram was born on September 22, 1967 in Los Angeles, California. She began her artistic career in the early 1990s as a painter and sculptor. Her work has been exhibited at the Museum of Contemporary Art, Los Angeles; the Santa Monica Museum of Art; the Hammer Museum; and the Whitney Museum of American Art.  In the early 2000s, Cusi Cram began making films. Her first film, "Cusi Cram: The Life and Work of an Artist" (2003), is a documentary about her life and work. Her subsequent films include "The Luminaries" (2015), "Wildlife" (2017), and "Aura" (2018).  Cusi Cram is married to visual artist John Baldessari.
