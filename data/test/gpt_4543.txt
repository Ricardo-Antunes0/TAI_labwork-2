Government e Marketplace (GeM) is an online marketplace that connects government buyers and sellers worldwide.  GeM was created in 2009 as a way to increase access to government procurement opportunities for businesses and organizations around the world. GeM is a private company that is headquartered in the United States.  GeM offers a variety of services including a marketplace where government buyers can find and purchase products and services from businesses and organizations, and a search engine where government sellers can find and sell their products and services. GeM also provides resources such as training and support, marketing tools, and networking opportunities to help businesses and organizations win government contracts.  GeM is supported by a team of experts who provide customer support, manage the marketplace, and develop new products and services. GeM has over 2,500 customers including governments in more than 50 countries.
