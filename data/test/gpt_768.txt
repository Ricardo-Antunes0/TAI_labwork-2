Sara Baiyu Porritt, known professionally as Baiyu, is an English singer, songwriter and rapper. Born in Kingston upon Hull, she began her music career as a member of girl group Little Mix, before releasing her solo debut album "The Truth" in 2018.  Baiyu was born in Kingston upon Hull, England on 10 December 1994 to Jamaican parents. She began her music career as a member of girl group Little Mix, before releasing her solo debut album "The Truth" in 2018. The album spawned the singles "I Don't Wanna Live Forever", "Black Widow" and "Get Out".
