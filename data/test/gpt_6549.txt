Stephen W. "Steve" White (born April 13, 1951) is a retired United States District Judge of the United States District Court for the Southern District of Alabama.  Born in Montgomery, Alabama, White received a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Alabama in 1973 and a Juris Doctor from the University of Alabama School of Law in 1976. He was in private practice in Montgomery from 1976 to 1978, and then served as an Assistant United States Attorney for the Southern District of Alabama from 1978 to 1982. He was a judge on the Montgomery County Circuit Court from 1982 to 1996, and then served as a United States District Judge for the Southern District of Alabama from 1996 to 2006. He is currently a senior judge on the Alabama Supreme Court.
