Morgan the Escapist is a magic performer and illusionist who has toured throughout the United States and Canada since the early 1990s. He is known for his card tricks, escape acts, and elaborate illusions. Morgan also produces and hosts his own television show, "Morgan the Escapist," which airs on The Travel Channel.  Morgan was born on November 25, 1966, in Regina, Saskatchewan. He began performing as a magician at age 10 and quickly became a popular attraction in Saskatchewan and Alberta. In 1990, he moved to California and began touring throughout the United States and Canada. His show has been seen by millions of viewers and has won him numerous awards, including two Emmy Awards and two Canadian Screen Awards. Morgan is also a published author, having released a book entitled "The Morgan Method: How to Escape Anything" in 2009. He currently resides in Los Angeles.
