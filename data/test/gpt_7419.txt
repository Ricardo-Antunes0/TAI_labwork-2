Alfa Tofft (11 January 1911 – 29 September 1994) was a German film and television actor.  Born in Berlin, Tofft worked as a stage actor before making his screen debut in the war film "Die Gefangenen von Stalingrad" (1943). He was primarily active in television throughout his career, appearing in such programs as the soap opera "General Hospital" (1963–1971) and the crime series "Tatort" (1981–1989). Tofft died in Berlin aged 85.
