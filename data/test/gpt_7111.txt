Odludek is the debut solo album by Slovenian progressive rock band Divlje Jagode. It was released in 2002 by the independent record label Kscope.  Odludek is a concept album that tells the story of a prince who is cursed by a witch. The prince must journey through the world to find a cure for his curse, and on his way he meets different people and creatures who help him along the way.  The album was well-received by critics, who praised its strong musical and lyrical themes. It has since been regarded as one of the band's finest works.
