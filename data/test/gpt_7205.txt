The Countess of Derby usually refers to the wife of the eighteenth Earl of Derby, who was twice Prime Minister of the United Kingdom.  The Countess of Derby was born in 1851 as Lady Sarah Spencer. She married the Earl of Derby in 1875 and they had two children. Lady Sarah became Countess of Derby in her husband's title in 1898. She served as Prime Minister's wife from 1902 to 1906 and was also a member of the House of Lords. Lady Sarah died in 1927.
