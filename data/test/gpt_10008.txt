Mike Getchell is a retired American soccer player who played professionally in the North American Soccer League and Major League Soccer.  Mike Getchell was born on October 2, 1966 in Los Angeles, California. He attended UCLA where he played on the men's soccer team from 1983 to 1986. He was a first-team All-American in 1985 and 1986.  Getchell played professionally in the North American Soccer League (NASL) with the San Diego Sockers from 1987 to 1990 and the Tampa Bay Mutiny from 1990 to 1993. He also played for the Miami Fusion, Chicago Fire, New England Revolution, and D.C. United in MLS from 1993 to 2003. He finished his career with 164 appearances and 54 goals.  Getchell was inducted into the UCLA Athletic Hall of Fame in 2006 and the National Soccer Hall of Fame in 2013. He is also a two-time MLS All-Star (1995 and 1997).
