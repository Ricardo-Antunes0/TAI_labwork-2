"Bubble Pop!" is a song recorded by American singer Katy Perry for her fourth studio album, Prism (2013). It was written by Perry, Max Martin, and Shellback, with production handled by the latter two. The song was initially pitched as a collaboration between Perry and Rihanna, but the collaboration did not materialize.  "Bubble Pop!" is a bubblegum pop song featuring a heavy pop beat and synthesizers. The lyrics are about wanting to "pop" like a bubblegum toy. Lyrically, the song is about wanting to have fun and be happy. Perry performed the song live for the first time at the 2014 American Music Awards; she also performed it on The Graham Norton Show and during her Prism World Tour.  "Bubble Pop!" debuted at number 36 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart on the week of November 10, 2013. It peaked at number three on the chart on the week of January 6, 2014. The song has sold over 3 million copies in the United States.
