Salto al color is the eighth studio album by Italian singer Mina, released on 2 November 2007.   The album was preceded by the single "Ecco il mio amore" which peaked at number one on the Italian Singles Chart. The album also spawned three further singles: "Ti amo" (which reached number two), "Voglio te" (number four) and "Ti prego" (number six).  "Salto al color" received generally positive reviews from music critics, many of whom praised Mina's new direction and praised the album's production. The album was nominated for a Nastro d'Argento for Best Pop Album, but lost to Pino Daniele's "Sorrisi e canzoni".
