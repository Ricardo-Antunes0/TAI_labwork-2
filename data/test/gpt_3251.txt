German submarine U-736 was a Type VIIC U-boat of Nazi Germany's Kriegsmarine during World War II.  U-736 was laid down at the Blohm & Voss Shipyard in Hamburg on 7 October 1941, launched on 10 November 1942 and commissioned on 12 March 1943.  German submarine U-736 operated in the Mediterranean Sea and the North Atlantic Ocean, sinking six merchant ships totalling 39,841 gross register tons (GRT).
