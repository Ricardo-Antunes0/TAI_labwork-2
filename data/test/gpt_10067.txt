Mira Jangaracheva (sometimes Dzhangaracheva) (born 1952) is a Bulgarian composer. She is the founder and director of the Sofia Academy of Music, where she also serves as a professor. Jangaracheva has written over 50 compositions, including operas, ballets, chamber music, and choral works. She has been awarded several prestigious prizes, including the French Grand Prix de composition for her opera Kolyma.
