Orléans Ward (Ward 1) is a city ward in the city of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. The ward is bounded by Somerset Street to the south, Bank Street to the west, Hunt Club Road to the north and ByWard Market to the east.  The ward is home to a number of popular tourist attractions, including the National Arts Centre, the Canadian War Museum and the Ottawa River. The ward also contains a number of public housing projects and commercial districts.  Orléans Ward is currently represented by Anna Chiasson on Ottawa City Council.
