Michel Micombero (26 August 194016 July 1983) was a French composer and pianist.  Michel Micombero was born in Marseille, France, on 26 August 1941. He studied with Nadia Boulanger and Olivier Messiaen at the Paris Conservatoire, where he won first prize in 1965. Micombero also studied with Darius Milhaud and Luciano Berio.  Micombero's early works were inspired by serialism, but he later developed his own philosophical approach to composition which he called 'the expressive method'.
