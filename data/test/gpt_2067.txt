Gufo Temple  is located on the outskirts of the city of Xi'an in Shaanxi province, China. The temple is dedicated to the Gufo, a type of Chinese dragon. The temple was first built in the sixth century AD, and has been renovated and expanded many times since.   The Gufo Temple is a popular tourist destination in Xi'an, with a number of attractions including a large statue of the dragon goddess Gufo, a pavilion dedicated to the Nine-tailed Fox demon, and a garden containing numerous statues of Gufo. The temple is also home to a large library containing over 10,000 Buddhist texts.
