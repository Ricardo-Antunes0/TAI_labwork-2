Dawn Rider is a 2012 American Western action film written and directed by Chad Stahelski. It is the first installment in the trilogy of films based on the Marvel Comics character John Carter of Mars, and stars Andrew Garfield, naomi Watts and Luke Evans.  The film tells the story of Carter as he is transported to Mars after a battle with the evil Dejah Thoris, only to find that she has been replaced by a female version of himself called Dawn. With the help of a group of rebels, he sets out to find Dejah Thoris and restore her to her rightful place as queen.  The film was released in North America on March 9, 2012, and received mixed reviews from critics. However, it was a box office success, grossing over $363 million worldwide.
