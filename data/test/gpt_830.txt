Toronto Neighbourhood Centres ("TNC") is an association of neighbourhood groups in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.  The TNCs work together to provide a voice for their communities and advocate for the needs of their residents. Their goals are to foster a sense of community and to promote the vitality and vibrancy of their neighbourhoods.  The TNCs represent a wide range of neighbourhood interests, including seniors, youth, ethnic communities, environmental activists, and business owners. They come from all parts of Toronto, from the downtown core to the suburbs.  The TNCs have played an important role in shaping the character of Toronto's neighbourhoods over the past several decades. They work together to develop community-based programs and services that meet the needs of their constituents.  TNCs are registered as charitable organizations with the Ontario Society of Charities Registration Number: 1029822.
