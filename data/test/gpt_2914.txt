Lybius is a genus of African barbets. It contains two species, the red-billed lybius and the yellow-billed lybius.  The red-billed lybius is found in Angola, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, the Central African Republic, Chad, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Liberia, Mali, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Togo and Uganda. The yellow-billed lybius is found in Angola, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, the Central African Republic, Chad, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Liberia, Mali, Nigeria and Senegal.
