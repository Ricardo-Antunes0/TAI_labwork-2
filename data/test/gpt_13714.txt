The ninja tradition dates back to the 8th century, when a group of youths who were rejected from the samurai class began to use their skills for peaceful purposes. These skills included stealth, agility, and the ability to use weapons with precision.   Today, ninjutsu is still used for peaceful purposes, such as protecting people or property. Ninja training also emphasizes mental toughness, discipline, and self-reliance.
