Beat Freaks is an all-female breaking dance crew based in Charleston, South Carolina. The group was founded in 2006 by Melissa "Missy" Driggs and later joined by Tasha Reed. Beat Freaks is one of the few all-female crews to consistently perform at major competitions and festivals, including the prestigious World Championships of Breaking Dancing.  Beat Freaks is a touring dance crew that performs throughout the Southeast United States. The group's repertoire includes contemporary and traditional styles of breaking and choreographed sequences to popular music. Beat Freaks also performs at various community events and charity fundraisers across the region.
