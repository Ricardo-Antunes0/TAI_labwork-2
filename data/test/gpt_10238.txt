Madravati was the wife of Kuru King Arjuna. She was born to the Pandavas' uncle, Kunti. Madravati was very beautiful and loved by all the members of the Kuru dynasty.  Madravati was married to Arjuna, the most celebrated warrior of the Kuru dynasty. She was known for her radiant beauty and her many amorous affairs. Arjuna was greatly in love with Madravati and they had a beautiful son, Abhimanyu.  After Arjuna's death in the battle of Kurukshetra, Madravati retired to the forest with her son. They eventually died there, leaving behind a legacy of love and beauty.
