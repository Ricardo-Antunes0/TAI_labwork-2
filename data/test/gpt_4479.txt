Makhan Malai  or Malai Makkhan also known as Chicken Malai is a gravy dish made with chicken and malai (cream) sauce. It is popular in the Indian subcontinent, particularly in the states of Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, and Rajasthan.  Makhan malai is generally served with naan bread or roti. It is also an ingredient used in many other Indian dishes such as tandoori chicken, palak paneer, and lamb vindaloo.
