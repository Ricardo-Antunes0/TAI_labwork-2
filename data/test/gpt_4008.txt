Milkana Palavurova (Bulgarian: Милкана Палавурова) (born September, 1975 in Plovdiv) is a Bulgarian actress.  Milkana Palavurova was born in September 1975 in Plovdiv. She attended the State Academy of Music and Theatre in Sofia and graduated with honours in 1997. She has since appeared in many Bulgarian and international films and television productions. Her best-known roles include Aneta in the comedy film Peevskiy Otok (2002), the title character in the drama film Pravo na more (2005), and Danka in the comedy film Kukeri (2008).
