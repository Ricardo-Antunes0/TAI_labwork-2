The station is located in the 5th arrondissement of Paris. The station opened on 27 July 1937 as part of the original section of the Paris Métro. It is named after the French Revolution.   The station is served by Lines 4 and 12. It is also served by RER line C, which provides interchange with the suburban rail network.
