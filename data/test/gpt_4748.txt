The Canon EOS 650D, known as the Rebel T6i in North America, is a digital single-lens reflex camera that was announced by Canon on September 3, 2012. It is the successor to the Canon EOS Rebel T5i and the first camera in the Rebel T6 series. The camera is available in black or silver body with a 18-55mm lens.  The Canon EOS 650D features a new CMOS sensor that allows for faster shooting speeds and improved low-light performance, as well as a new Digic 5+ processor. The Rebel T6i also features an articulating LCD screen, multiple exposure modes, and Wi-Fi capabilities.
