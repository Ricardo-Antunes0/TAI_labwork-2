Saber Interactive Inc. is an American video game developer and publisher. It was founded in 1994 by CEO and President Joel H. Cohen, who also served as the company's Chief Technology Officer. The company is best known for developing the "Warcraft" series of video games, and its subsidiary WildTangent Games, which publishes and develops video games for mobile devices.  Saber Interactive is headquartered in Redwood City, California. The company employs over 200 people and has studios in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Denmark, Sweden, and Australia.
