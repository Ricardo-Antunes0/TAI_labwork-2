Inez García (1941–2003) was a Hispanic woman painter and sculptor who was known for her expressive and spiritual work. Born in New York City, Inez García received her BFA from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 1963. She then moved to Mexico City, where she worked with José Clemente Orozco and Francisco Toledo. García also participated in various group exhibitions in Mexico and abroad, including at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City (1972). She lived in Spain from 1978 to 2003, where she continued to work. Inez García died in Madrid at the age of 60.
