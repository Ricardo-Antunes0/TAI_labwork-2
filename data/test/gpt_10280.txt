Lightolier is a company that manufactures and sells light fixtures. It was founded in the early 1990s by two brothers, Rick and Ron Lightolier. The company initially produced light fixtures for car dealerships, but has since expanded its product line to include a variety of residential and commercial fixtures.  Lightolier is headquartered in San Diego, California, and has operations in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. The company has a manufacturing plant in Chino Hills, California, and a sales office in Carlsbad, California.
