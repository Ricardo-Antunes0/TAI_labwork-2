Dorothy June Sutor (6 June 1929 – 30 January 2004) was an English actress, best known for her roles in the television programmes Midsomer Murders and Bergerac.  Sutor was born in London, the daughter of Lilian (née Aldridge) and Edward Sutor. She trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) and made her stage debut in 1950 with the Old Vic company. She appeared in numerous television programmes, including Midsomer Murders and Bergerac, before retiring from acting in 1993. Sutor died in 2004 aged 77.
