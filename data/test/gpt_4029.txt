Eupatorium fortunei is a plant species in the Asteraceae family. It is endemic to the eastern United States, where it grows in moist areas of the Appalachian Mountains, from New York to North Carolina.  Eupatorium fortunei is a perennial herb up to 120 cm tall, with a hairy stem and oppositely arranged leaves. The flower heads are borne in clusters of up to 16, each head containing 8-10 flowers. The flowers are blue or violet, and have a sweet scent.
