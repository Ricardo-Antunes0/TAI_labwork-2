The Alchemist Cookbook is a horror film adaptation of the novel of the same name by Paulo Coelho. The film was directed by Gary Ross and stars Robert De Niro, Michelle Pfeiffer, and Al Pacino. The film was released on November 2, 2006.  The film tells the story of Santiago (De Niro), a Spanish traveler who, in search of a way to find wealth and happiness, travels to Egypt and meets a man known as The Alchemist (Pacino). The Alchemist teaches Santiago the art of alchemy, which allows him to transmute common objects into valuable ones. However, Santiago soon discovers that The Alchemist is a murderous sorcerer who plans to use his newfound power to become king of Spain.  The film received mixed reviews from movie critics. However, it grossed over $240 million at the box office worldwide.
