Austin Shakespeare is a professional, classical theater company located in Austin, Texas. The company was founded in 1984 by Dr. Frank Hoffman and his wife, Marsha Hoffman. The company is dedicated to producing high-quality classical theater productions that appeal to audiences of all ages.   Austin Shakespeare's productions are performed at the Driskill Theatre in downtown Austin. The Driskill Theatre is a historic, Art Deco venue that has been renovated and extensively restored by the company. The theater is home to Austin Shakespeare's mainstage and workshop productions, as well as occasional concerts and other events.   The company's mission is to produce classical theater that is both entertaining and educational. Through its productions, Austin Shakespeare seeks to provide its audience with an experience that will enrich their lives.
