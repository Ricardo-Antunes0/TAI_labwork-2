Stiftung Lesen (Reading Foundation) is a non-profit foundation based in Berlin, Germany, which promotes reading and literacy. The foundation conducts research on the effects of reading on children and adults, offers grants to projects that promote reading, and organizes literary events.  The Reading Foundation was founded in 1984 by the publisher Hans-Jürgen Papierhaus. It is headquartered in Berlin. The Reading Foundation has a staff of ten and a budget of over €2 million.
