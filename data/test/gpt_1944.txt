Shirley Sham (; born June 22, 1994) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Harper Fowley on the Disney Channel series Jessie (2013–2017), Mia in the Disney Channel series Sofia the First (2011–2013), and Addie in the Disney Channel series Austin & Ally (2011).  Sham was born in Livingston, Montana, to Karen (née Sullivan) and Scott Sham. She has a younger brother named Ryan. Sham grew up in Helena, Montana, and attended Helena High School. After graduating from high school, Sham moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting.  Sham made her acting debut in the feature film The Boy in the Striped Pajamas (2008). She subsequently appeared in television series including Entourage, The O.C., and Gossip Girl. In 2013, she played Harper Fowley on the Disney Channel series Jessie. The show ran for four seasons before concluding in 2017. In 2018, Sham starred as Mia on the Disney Channel series Sofia the First. The show ran for two seasons before concluding in 2019. In 2019, she starred as Addie in the Disney Channel series Austin & Ally.
