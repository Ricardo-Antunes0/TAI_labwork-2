Peder Jensen Lodehat (died 21 October 1416) was a Danish prelate and politician who served as Bishop of Roskilde from 1392 until his death.  Lodehat was born in Zealand, the son of Jensen Lodehat, a notary, and Margrethe Smedegaard. He studied theology at the University of Paris and was ordained as a priest in 1380. He returned to Denmark in 1392 and was appointed Bishop of Roskilde. He served in this position until his death in 1416.
