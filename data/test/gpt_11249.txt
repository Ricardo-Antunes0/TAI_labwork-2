Born in 1923, Ji Dengkui joined the Communist Party of China in 1940. He became a member of the Central Military Commission in 1978 and was appointed Chief of Staff of the PLA in 1978. Ji Dengkui served as Chief of Staff until 1982, when he was replaced by Wang Dezhi. After his retirement from the PLA, Ji Dengkui served as the Director of the General Staff Academy from 1988 to 1992. He died in 2002 at the age of 78.
