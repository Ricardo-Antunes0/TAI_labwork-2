The Amber chess tournament (officially the Amber International Chess Tournament, also known as the Amber Chess Classic) is an annual chess tournament held in the city of Amber, Hungary. The event was founded in 1978 and has been played every year since. The tournament is a part of the Hungarian Chess Championship, which is held in March and April.   The Amber chess tournament is the most important annual chess event in Hungary. It is also one of the oldest chess tournaments in the world. The tournament features top players from all over the world, and has been won by many of the world's best players. Past winners of the Amber chess tournament include Garry Kasparov, Veselin Topalov, Anatoly Karpov, and Viswanathan Anand.
