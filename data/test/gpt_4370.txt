The Xiaomi Redmi Note Prime is a high-end budget mobile phone produced by Xiaomi. It was announced on December 1, 2016, and was released in China on December 5, 2016. It is the successor to the Mi 4i and features a 5.5-inch (1440x2560 pixels) Full HD display, an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 625 processor, 4GB of RAM, and 64GB of internal storage. The camera features a 12MP rear sensor with an f/1.8 aperture and a 5MP front-facing sensor with an f/2.0 aperture. The battery is 3200mAh and supports Quick Charge 3.0.
