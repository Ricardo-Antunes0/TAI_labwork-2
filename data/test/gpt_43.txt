Locust Abortion Technician is the third full-length album by the American metalcore band As I Lay Dying. It was released on October 10, 2017, through Metal Blade Records. The album is the first to feature drummer Jordan Mancino and guitarist/vocalist Tim Lambesis since their departure from As I Lay Dying in 2013.  The album was produced by Jason Suecof and mixed by Jesse Berland. The cover art was created by John D. Hill.   The album's sound is described as "dark and crushing," with influences from death metal, progressive metal, and hardcore punk. "Locust Abortion Technician" is a concept album about the biblical story of the locust plague.  Upon release, "Locust Abortion Technician" received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album has an average score of 70, which indicates "generally favorable reviews."
