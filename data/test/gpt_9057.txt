Purvi Patel (born c. 1982) is an Indian-American woman who was sentenced to 20 years in prison for conspiracy to commit abortion after aborting her own fetus in 2012. Patel was initially convicted of murder, but the conviction was later downgraded to conspiracy to commit abortion.  Purvi Patel was born circa 1982 in India. She arrived in the United States in 2001, and became a permanent resident in 2007. In 2012, Patel underwent an abortion at a hospital in Ohio. Patel was subsequently charged with conspiracy to commit abortion and murder, but the charges were later downgraded to conspiracy to commit abortion. Patel has consistently maintained her innocence, and has argued that she did not know that the abortion would result in death.  Patel is currently imprisoned at the Federal Correctional Institution, FCI Terre Haute, in Indiana.
