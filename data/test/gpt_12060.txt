Ernesto A. Layaguin was an enlisted hospital steward in the United States Navy during World War II, and a recipient of the Navy Cross.  Born in San Antonio, Texas, on February 27, 1922, Ernesto A. Layaguin enlisted in the United States Navy in March 1942. Serving as an enlisted hospital steward during World War II, he was awarded the Navy Cross for heroism at the battle of Iwo Jima on February 19, 1945. After the war, Layaguin worked as a truck driver and later as a taxi driver. He died on December 16, 2002.
