Koombea INC is an American digital product design and development company. It was founded in 2012 by CEO and Co-founder, Shai Agassi.  Koombea specializes in developing digital products, including mobile apps, websites, and e-commerce platforms. The company has worked on projects for clients such as Nike, Uber, and Spotify.  Koombea has been featured in publications such as Fast Company and Forbes. The company has also won awards such as the 2016 Webbys “Best New Startup” award and the 2017 AIGA Design Award for Best Emerging Design Firm.
