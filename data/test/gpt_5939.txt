SKULLS UNLIMITED INTERNATIONAL, INC. is a commercial enterprise that manufactures and distributes realistic replica skulls and other novelty Halloween items. The company was founded in 1992 by Rick Baker, who is also the company's CEO. SKULLS UNLIMITED specializes in creating anatomically correct, full-size replicas of human skulls, as well as other sculptural Halloween props such as mummies, witches' hats, and skeletons. The company's products are sold through specialty retailers and online stores worldwide.
