Ross McLaren (born 24 October 1991) is an English actor, known for his portrayal of Luca McIntyre on the BBC soap opera Doctors. For his role as Luca, McLaren has been nominated at award ceremonies including the National Television Awards and the RTS Midlands Awards. Prior to appearing on the soap, he performed in various stage productions, including Big the Musical and Guys and Dolls. Life and career McLaren was born on 24 October 1991 in Scunthorpe, North Lincolnshire. McLaren studied at the Joyce Mason School of Dance, as well as the Millennium Theatre Arts School. After graduating, he appeared in 42nd Street at the Curve in Leicester. He then appeared in a touring production of Annie through New Zealand, and starred in a production of Singin' in the Rain at the Théâtre du Châtelet in Paris, in the role of Sid Phillips. In February 2021, it was announced that he had joined the regular cast of the BBC daytime soap opera Doctors as nurse Luca McIntyre. His debut appearance aired on 24 February 2021. For his portrayal of the role, he was longlisted for the Newcomer award at the 26th National Television Awards. Alongside appearing in Doctors, McLaren also starred in the 2022 UK tour of Singin' in the Rain, this time in the role of Cosmo Brown.
