The Avenel Football Club is an Australian rules football club based in Avenel, Victoria. The club was founded in 1922 and played its first match in 1923. The club has competed in the Victorian Football League (VFL) since 1925.  The Avenel Football Club is one of the oldest clubs in Victoria, having been founded in 1922. The club began competing in the Victorian Football League (VFL) in 1925 and has won five premierships, most recently in 2006. The club also has two minor premierships and one divisional premiership.
