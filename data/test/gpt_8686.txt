Stella Sierra (5 July 1917 – 19 November 1984) was a Mexican film actress.  Stella Sierra was born in 1917 in Mexico City, Mexico to parents of Italian descent. She began her career as a child actress and made her feature-length debut at the age of seventeen in the film El crimen del Padre Amaro (1935). Sierra achieved international recognition in the 1940s for her performances in such films as La doncella de Oro (1940), Las mujeres de la calle (1941), and El ángel exterminador (1943). She continued to appear in films throughout the 1950s, but began to focus on television and stage work in the 1960s. Stella Sierra died of cancer on 19 November 1984 in Mexico City.
