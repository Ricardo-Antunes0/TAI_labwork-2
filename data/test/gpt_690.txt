Beyond the Gap is a fantasy novel written by American author R. A. Salvatore. Published in 1991, it is the fourth book in the Forgotten Realms series.  The novel is set in the city of Baldur's Gate, and follows the exploits of the heroes Jarlaxle, Artemis Entreri, and Tormund Giantsbane as they battle against a group of powerful enemies known as the Archmage of Candlekeep and his followers.  "Beyond the Gap" was well received by critics, who praised its action-packed plot and well-developed characters. It has been cited as an influence on the roleplaying game "Diablo II".
