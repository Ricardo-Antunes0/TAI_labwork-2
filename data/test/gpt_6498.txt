Roger Demment (born 1950) is a former Major League Baseball manager who last managed the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim. He has also served as a bench coach for the San Diego Padres, Tampa Bay Rays, Boston Red Sox, Chicago Cubs and Los Angeles Dodgers.  Demment was born in 1950 in Syracuse, New York and attended the University of Miami. He played catcher for the Miami Hurricanes baseball team from 1969 to 1971 and was selected by the Houston Astros in the first round of the 1972 Major League Baseball Draft.
