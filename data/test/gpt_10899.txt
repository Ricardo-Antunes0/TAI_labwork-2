The Independent Social Alliance , known asISA, is a social justice advocacy organisation in the United Kingdom. The organisation was founded in 1987, and is based in London.  ISA has a long history of working on social justice issues, and has been involved in a number of campaigns over the years. The organisation has played a role in campaigns against poverty, inequality, racism, and climate change, and has also worked on issues such as mental health and homelessness. In recent years, the organisation has focused its work on campaign for universal social care.  ISA is an independent organisation, and does not receive any funding from government or any other source. The organisation relies on donations from members and supporters to carry out its work.
