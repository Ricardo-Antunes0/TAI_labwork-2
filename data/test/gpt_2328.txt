The Military Park station (formerly Broad Street station) is a SEPTA subway station on the Broad Street Subway in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. It is located at the intersection of Broad and Market Streets in Center City Philadelphia.  Opened on October 13, 1924, as part of the Broad Street Subway, it was originally called Broad Street Station. The station was renamed Military Park station on October 24, 1986, to commemorate the renaming of Philadelphia's Military Park to Veterans Memorial Park.
