David Levithan (born September 7, 1972) is an American author, essayist, and musician. He wrote the novel If You Were A Character in My Story, which was published in 2009. His second novel, Don't Let Me Be Lonely, was published in 2013.  Levithan was born in New York City to a Jewish family. He studied at Bard College and earned his MFA from the University of Pennsylvania. He currently lives in Brooklyn with his wife and two daughters.
