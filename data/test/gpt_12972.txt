Escape from Tomorrow is a 2013 American science fiction horror film directed by Robert Luketic and written by Steven Knight. The film stars Tom Hanks, Emma Roberts, and Paul Giamatti.  In the future, a society has been created in which people are constantly under surveillance by the government. When Max (Hanks) and his wife, Eve (Roberts), learn that their teenage daughter has been kidnapped by the government, they team up with a group of rebels to attempt an escape from the future.
