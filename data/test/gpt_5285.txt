Dr Hande Özsan Bozatlı (Born 1960 in Bursa, Turkey) is an artist who lives and works in Istanbul. Her practice encompasses sculpture, installation, photography, drawing and video. She has been awarded several prizes, including the 2002 Yildiz Technical University Prize for her sculpture 'Sarı Bekliyor' (translated as 'Worried Woman').  Bozatlı's sculptures explore the relation between humans and nature. She often uses everyday objects to create her artworks, such as chairs, tables and umbrellas. Her sculptures are often made from recycled materials, such as old furniture or carpets.  Bozatlı's work has been exhibited at many museums and galleries around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the National Gallery of Art in Washington D.C., and the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston.
