Bindiya Chamkegi is a 1984 Indian hindi-language romantic drama film directed by Ramesh Sippy. It stars Sanjay Dutt and Sharmila Tagore in the lead roles. The film's music is composed by Laxmikant-Pyarelal.  Bindiya Chamkegi is a story of two young people who fall in love with each other despite their families' disapproval. The film follows the couple's tumultuous journey as they fight against all odds to be together.
