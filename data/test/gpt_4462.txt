Biriyaani is a 2020 Indian Malayalam-language drama film written and directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan. The film stars Mohanlal, Biju Menon, and Ashwini Kalyanpur in the lead roles. The film is produced by Mohanlal's production company, Aashirvad Cinemas.  The film tells the story of a man who returns to his hometown after many years and discovers that everything has changed. He tries to reconnect with old friends and loved ones, but he soon realises that they are all gone and that he is now a stranger in his own town.
