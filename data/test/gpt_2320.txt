TRIZ , literally: "theory of inventive problem solving" is a problem-solving methodology and mindset. It has its roots in classical problem solving, and is based on the assumption that any problem can be broken down into smaller, more manageable problems. TRIZ was first developed in the Soviet Union in the 1950s and 1960s, and has been used to solve a wide variety of problems in many industrial and commercial settings.  TRIZ is often used as a tool for problem solving in a variety of industries, including technology, automotive, medical, aerospace, chemical, and consumer goods. TRIZ has also been used to help develop new products and processes, as well as to improve existing ones.
