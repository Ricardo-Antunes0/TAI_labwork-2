Monochrome Racing is a 2011 racing video game developed by Bitmap Brothers and published by Playdead. It is a 2D side-scrolling racing game set in a dystopian world, with the player controlling a vehicle that traverses a race track composed of derelict buildings and wreckage.  The game is based on the 2009 Flash game Monochrome, which was created by Bitmap Brothers and published by Playdead. The game was reworked for use in the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 platforms, and features new environments, vehicles, and characters.  "Monochrome Racing" received mixed reviews from critics. Reviewers praised the art style and design of the game, but criticized its repetitive gameplay and short length.
