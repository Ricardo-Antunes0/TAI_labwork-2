"Kool Thing" is a song by American hip hop group The Pharcyde. It was released as the lead single from their third album, Bizarre Ride II the Pharcyde (1994). The song was produced by the group's leader, Slim Thug and features vocals from the group's backup rapper Posdnuos.  The song is about a party and features the lyrics "I'm just tryna have a kool thing/All day, all night/I just wanna roll with the Kool Thing." The song attained commercial success, reaching number-one on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart and number-two in the United Kingdom. It also reached number-four in Australia and number-five in Canada. The song has been certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). Rolling Stone ranked it at number 44 on their list of The 500 Greatest Songs of All Time.  "Kool Thing" is one of the most well-known songs from The Pharcyde, and is considered their signature track. It has been featured in many films and television shows, including "The Simpsons", "South Park", "2 Fast 2 Furious", "The Wire" and "Grand Theft Auto IV". It was also used in an advertisement for Nike during the 1998 FIFA World Cup.
