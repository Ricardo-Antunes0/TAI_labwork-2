Arthur Rex: A Legendary Novel is a novel written by American author Stephen King. It was published on October 14, 2009, in hardcover and ebook editions. The novel tells the story of a group of seven teenagers who are terrorized by an entity that takes the form of a terrifying, grinning clown.  The novel was announced on King's official website on September 25, 2009. The first chapter was made available for free download the same day.  The novel was generally well-received by critics. Review aggregator website Goodreads has an overall rating of 4.48/5 stars, based on 9 reviews.
