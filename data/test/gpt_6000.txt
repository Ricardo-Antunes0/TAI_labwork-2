Coheed and Cambria is an American progressive rock band from Gainesville, Florida. The band was formed in 1994 by Claudio Sanchez (lead vocals, guitar), Travis Stever (bass, backing vocals), and Josh Eppard (drums).  The band's original lineup consisted of Sanchez, Stever, and Eppard. They released their debut album, "The Unheavenly Creatures", in 1995. Their second album, "In Keeping Secrets of Silent Earth: 3", was released in 1999. The band's third album, "Good Apollo, I'm Burning Star IV: Part 1", was released in 2001. "Good Apollo, I'm Burning Star IV: Part 2" was released in 2002. "The Amory Wars" was released in 2005. "The Unheavenly Creatures II" was released in 2009. "Songs from the Black Hole" was released in 2013.
