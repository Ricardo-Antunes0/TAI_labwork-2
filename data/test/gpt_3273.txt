Genevieve Nnaji (; born on 3 May 1985) is a Nigerian actress, model and television presenter. She is best known for her roles in the films Queen of Katwe, Proud Mary and the TV series Damita Jo.  Nnaji was born in Lagos, Nigeria to an Igbo father and a Yoruba mother. She moved to England at the age of six and grew up in North London. She started her career as a model and then moved into acting, appearing in films such as Queen of Katwe, Proud Mary and the TV series Damita Jo.
