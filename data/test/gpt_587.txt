Mahalaxmi is a municipality that lies in the Greater Noida district of Uttar Pradesh, India. It is located on National Highway 8 and is the administrative headquarters of Mahalaxmi tehsil. Lalitpur is a city located in Mahalaxmi tehsil, Greater Noida district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is located at the intersection of National Highway 8 and the Western Bypass. Lalitpur is one of the fastest growing cities in Uttar Pradesh. The city has an estimated population of 1,128,000 as of 2011.   Mahalaxmi was originally known as Lalitpur. The name Lalitpur means "City of Light" in Sanskrit. The city was established by Maharaja Lalit Singh who ruled from 1852 to 1907. Mahalaxmi was declared a municipality in 2001.
