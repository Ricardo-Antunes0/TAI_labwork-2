Royal Enfield Classic 350 and Classic 500 motorcycles are classic British motorcycles made by Royal Enfield. Introduced in 1957, the 350 and 500 models are still produced and sold in limited numbers.  The Classic 350 and 500 motorcycles have a classic British style with a retro look. They are made with quality materials, and have reliable performance. The motorcycles are easy to operate, and have a comfortable riding position.
