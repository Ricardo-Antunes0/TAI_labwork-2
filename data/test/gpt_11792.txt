Wang Kai (born 18 August 1983) is a Chinese actor and model. He began his acting career in 2002 with a minor role in the television series A Bite of China. He rose to prominence in 2006 with his role as the titular character in the historical epic television series The Legend of the Condor Heroes. He has since starred in a number of critically acclaimed films, including The Promise (2010), The Missing Picture (2013), and The Warlords (2015).
