Collateral is a 2004 American neo-noir action film directed and written by Michael Mann. The film stars Jamie Foxx, Tommy Lee Jones, and Monique Law.  Collateral is a neo-noir action film set in the present day, revolving around an investigation into the murder of a wealthy man. The film's plot concerns the battle between the police investigating the crime and criminals seeking to profit from it.   The cast features Jamie Foxx as an ex-convict who becomes a private detective tasked with investigating the murder, Tommy Lee Jones as a corrupt police detective who tries to stop him, and Monique Law as Foxx's love interest.
