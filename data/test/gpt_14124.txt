Soraya Ryan is an Australian judge. She is the first woman to serve on the Supreme Court of Victoria.  Soraya Ryan was born in Melbourne, Victoria in 1961. She studied at Monash University before completing a law degree at the University of Melbourne. She was admitted to the Victorian Bar in 1984 and became a judge of the Victorian Supreme Court in 2006.  Ryan has also served as a member of the Australian Federal Court and the International Court of Justice. She is currently a member of the Order of Australia, and was made an honorary fellow of Monash University in 2017.
