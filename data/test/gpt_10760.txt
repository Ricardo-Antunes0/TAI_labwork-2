John McGowan (1953-- ) is the John A. and Catherine T. MacArthur Professor of Mental Health Law at the University of Michigan Law School. McGowan is also the Director of the Risk Assessment Research Program at the school and a faculty member in the Department of Psychiatry.  McGowan's scholarship focuses on mental health law and its intersection with public policy. He has written extensively on issues such as deinstitutionalization, community-based treatment, civil commitment, and suicide prevention. McGowan is also known for his work on risk assessment and risk management in mental health settings.
