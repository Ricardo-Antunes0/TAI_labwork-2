The Makkah Declaration (2019) also known as the Riyadh Call for Global Islamic Solidarity, is an Islamic declaration of solidarity with Muslims across the world. The declaration was endorsed by more than 50 Muslim leaders from around the world, including the Grand Mufti of Saudi Arabia, Sheikh Abdulaziz al-Sheikh.  The declaration calls for Muslims to unite in support of their shared values and to work together to address common challenges facing their communities. It also calls for greater cooperation between Muslim communities and governments, and for efforts to promote Islamic values worldwide.  The Makkah Declaration was issued at a summit in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia on 10 May 2019.
