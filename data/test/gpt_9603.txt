Przybyszów  (, Prybyshiv; , Prybyshiv) is a town in the administrative district of Gmina Przybyszów, within Jasło County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately north-west of Przybyszów, north of Jasło, and south of the regional capital Rzeszów.  The town has a population of 2,000.  <br>
