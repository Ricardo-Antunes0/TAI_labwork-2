Ali and Nino is a novel about two young people from different backgrounds who fall in love and must overcome the barriers of class, religion and tradition to be together.  The novel is set in the years following World War II in northern Italy. Ali is a Muslim from a poor background, and Nino is a Christian from a wealthy family. Despite their differences, they fall in love and are determined to overcome the barriers of class, religion and tradition to be together.  Ali and Nino's story is a unique and powerful portrayal of the strength of love and the power of human connection. It is an inspiring story that will appeal to readers of all ages.
