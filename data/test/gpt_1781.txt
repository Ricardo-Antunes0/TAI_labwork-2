The Albacora-Leste Oil Field is an oil field located in the municipality of Albacora, Leste, in the state of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. The field was discovered in 1966 and has been developed by Petrobras. It is estimated to have reserves of up to 1.2 billion barrels (277 million metric tons).
