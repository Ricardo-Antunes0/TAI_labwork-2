Brad J. Bushman (born May 14, 1960 in Detroit, Michigan) is an American psychologist, who is the James S. McDonnell Professor of Psychology at the University of Kentucky.  Bushman's research focuses on the relationship between aggression and violence, aggression prevention, and mental health. He has also published work on the effects of media violence on children and adolescents, and the neuroscience of fear and anxiety. Bushman is a fellow of the American Psychological Association and the Academy of Social Sciences.
