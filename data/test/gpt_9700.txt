Blackroom was an upcoming FPS title from the creators of the hit game Rez. The game was set in a surreal and dark world, where players were tasked with escaping a black room.  Blackroom was initially announced in late 2014 as an indie title from the creators of Rez. The game was later re-revealed as a FPS title set in a surreal and dark world, where players were tasked with escaping a black room. In early 2017, it was announced that Blackroom would be published by developer Starbreeze Studios and released on Xbox One, PlayStation 4 and PC.
