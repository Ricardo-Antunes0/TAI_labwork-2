The dwarf sperm whale (Kogia sima) is a small cetacean that is found in the southwestern Atlantic Ocean and the eastern Pacific Ocean. It was first described by English naturalist Samuel Wallis in 1788. The dwarf sperm whale is the smallest of the sperm whales and has an average length of 3.3 meters and an average weight of 190 kilograms. The dwarf sperm whale is a passive predator that feeds on squid and small fish.
