Adrian Awasom (born October 25, 1983) is an American football linebacker for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League. He played college football at Virginia Tech.  Awasom was drafted by the Jaguars in the sixth round of the 2006 NFL Draft. He has also played for the Indianapolis Colts and Philadelphia Eagles. In football, he led his team to a state championship as a senior and was named an All-State selection.
