Chowder is an American animated television series created by John Kricfalusi for Cartoon Network. The series is about the adventures of the eponymous character, Chowder, and his friends, the surfer Fishlegs and the anthropomorphic clam Mimi-Bot.  Chowder follows the adventures of Chowder, a young land crab who dreams of becoming a great hero. Along the way, he teams up with his friends Fishlegs and Mimi-Bot to explore the world and solve puzzles.
