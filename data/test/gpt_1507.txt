In 1951 he won First Prize at the International composition competition in Zagreb, Yugoslavia.  Basterretxea's compositions include orchestral and chamber works, as well as vocal and choral works. His best-known work is his opera Guernica (1967), which has been performed around the world. He also composed music for films and television, including La leyenda de las tortugas (1968), El amanecer (1974) and Los ninos perdidos (1981).  Basterretxea died on 22 September 1983 in Vitoria-Gasteiz.
