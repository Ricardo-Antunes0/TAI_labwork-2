The WTVY-TV Tower is a 1,901 ft (553 m) tall skyscraper in Birmingham, Alabama, that is the tallest building in the city. The tower was completed in 1975 and has 34 floors. It is currently the ninth-tallest building in the United States.  The WTVY-TV Tower was constructed as part of a larger development project that also included the nearby First National Bank Building and the Regions Bank Tower. The complex was designed by architect Minoru Yamasaki and cost $121 million to build. The tower was originally owned by the ABC television network, which leased it to several different companies over the years. In 2006, WTVY-TV Broadcasting Company purchased the tower from ABC for $7 million.
