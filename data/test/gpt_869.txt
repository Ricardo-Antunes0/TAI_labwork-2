"He's a Liar" was the first single from the album "The Marshall Mathers LP 2" by Eminem. The song is about Eminem's breakup with his then-girlfriend, Kim.  The song was written by Eminem, Scott Storch and Dr. Dre. It was produced by Dr. Dre and Eminem.  "He's a Liar" is a fast-paced hip hop song with a hard beat. It is composed in the key of G minor and has a tempo of 128 beats per minute. The lyrics discuss Eminem's break-up with Kim and his feelings about it.  The song received generally positive reviews from music critics. Many reviewers praised the song's lyrical content and called it one of the strongest on "The Marshall Mathers LP 2".
