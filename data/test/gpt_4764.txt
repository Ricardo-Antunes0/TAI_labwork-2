Awara is a 2012 Bengali romantic action thriller film directed by Abhinay Deo and produced by Anjan Dutta. The screenplay was written by Deo.  The film stars Rituparna Sengupta, Jisshu Sengupta and Prosenjit Chatterjee in the lead roles. It tells the story of a love triangle between a schoolteacher, her student and the man she loves.
