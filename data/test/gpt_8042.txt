MV Imperial Transport was an oil tanker that was sunk on 15 February 1941 in the North Atlantic by a German U-boat. The wreck is located at a depth of 1,500 metres (4,900 ft) off the coast of Newfoundland, Canada.  The MV Imperial Transport was built in 1938 in the United Kingdom for the Imperial Oil Company.
