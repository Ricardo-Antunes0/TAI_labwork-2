Sun Jihai (;  ; born 30 October 1966) is a Chinese actor. He is best known for his roles in the television dramas Spring Dreaming (1999), Twenty-Four Hours (2002), and Destiny of the Dreamer (2006).  Sun was born in Wuhan, Hubei, on 30 October 1966. He started his acting career in the late 1980s, appearing in a number of television dramas. In 1999, he starred in the television drama Spring Dreaming, which was well received by critics. His subsequent roles in Twenty-Four Hours and Destiny of the Dreamer were also praised by critics. In 2006, Sun received a Golden Horse Award for Best Actor for his role in Destiny of the Dreamer. He has since retired from acting.
