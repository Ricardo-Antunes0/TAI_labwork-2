Gérard Gasiorowski (1930–1986) was a French photographer, artist and writer. He is known for his black-and-white images of the people and places of his native Poland.  Gasiorowski was born in 1930 in Warsaw, Poland. He attended the Academy of Fine Arts in Krakow, where he studied painting and sculpture. In the early 1960s, he moved to Paris and began photographing people and places in his native Poland. His work has been exhibited throughout Europe and North America, and has been published in numerous journals and books. He died in 1986 in Paris.
