The Dulaji class are a class of research ships operated by the Republic of India. The class was built in the 1990s and is based on the German Type 209 design.  The class is named after the Dulaji Hills, a mountain range in Gujarat state, India. The first ship in the class, RSS "Dulaji", was launched in December 1997 and entered service in 2000. The class has six ships as of 2016.
