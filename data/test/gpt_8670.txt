Pjetër Zarishi or Dom Pjeter Xarishi (1806–1872) was a 19th-century Albanian cleric, writer and historian.  Born in 1806 in the village of Zarishi near Gjirokastër, Pjetër Zarishi was ordained a priest in 1834. He served as the Bishop of Gjirokastër from 1838 until his death in 1872. He is best known for his historical work, particularly his monumental work "History of the Albanian People" (1859–1868).
