"Youngstown" is a song by Bruce Springsteen. The song was written about the Youngstown, Ohio steel mill strike of 1977. It was released as the first single from Springsteen's album Born in the U.S.A., and peaked at number 10 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart.  The song has been covered by many artists, including The E Street Band, The Darkness, Joe Cocker, Johnny Cash, and Elvis Presley.
