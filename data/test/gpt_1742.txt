No Sick Days is the 9th studio album by American rock band Linkin Park. It was released on March 6, 2016, through Warner Bros. Records. Recording sessions for the album took place over the course of two years in various studios throughout California.  Linkin Park entered the studio in 2015 to begin work on their ninth studio album. The band's frontman Chester Bennington spoke about how the album differed from their previous work, saying "It's definitely more aggressive than anything we've ever done before." He also hinted at a political message within the album, saying "There's definitely a political message that I'm trying to get across with this record. I think it's important that we step up and start speaking out about the things that are happening in the world."   No Sick Days was met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 76, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, selling 173,000 copies in its first week.
