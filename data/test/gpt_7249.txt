Bahram Mirza  was a Safavid prince, son of Abbas Mirza and brother of Ebrahim Mirza. He was governor of Herat province from 1663 until his death in 1676.  Bahram Mirza was born in 1590, the son of Abbas Mirza, who was the governor of Herat province. When his father died in 1663, Bahram Mirza succeeded him as governor. He served in this position until his death in 1676. During his time as governor, he strengthened the Safavid foothold in southern Afghanistan and made various improvements to the province's infrastructure.
