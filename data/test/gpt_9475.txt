Aadhavan is a multitalented artist.  As an actor, he has played roles in several Tamil and Telugu films. He has also done music for films and has composed several award-winning songs. As a musician, he has toured extensively across India and abroad. He is also a poet and has written several books.  Aadhavan was born on 5 October 1967 in a Tamil Brahmin family in the city of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the eldest son of S. Aadhavan and K. Srinivasan. He has two sisters, Sifya and Sakthi.  Aadhavan attended Sri Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda School, Chennai, where he was introduced to theatre by his teacher, Gnanasekaran. He later studied at the National School of Drama, New Delhi, graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 1991.  Aadhavan made his acting debut in 1991 with the Tamil film "Kadhal Kannadi". He followed it up with roles in "Ponnar Shankar", "Kaadhal Arangal" and "Nee Enna Thangai". In 2002, he starred in the Telugu film "Amaravati", which won him acclaim for his performance as an autistic man.
