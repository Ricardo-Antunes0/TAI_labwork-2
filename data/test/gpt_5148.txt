Brother Clyde is the debut studio album by American rapper Clyde Carson. It was released on July 14, 1995, by Jive Records. The album peaked at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart and spawned the singles "I'm a Player" and "I Wanna Rock".  Brother Clyde is the debut studio album by American rapper Clyde Carson. It was released on July 14, 1995, by Jive Records. The album peaked at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart and spawned the singles "I'm a Player" and "I Wanna Rock".   The album was well-received by music critics, who praised its production and Carson's delivery. It has been certified platinum by the RIAA.
