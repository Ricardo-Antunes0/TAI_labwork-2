Sharon Rudahl (born 1947) is an American sculptor.  Rudahl was born in 1947 in Chicago, Illinois. She studied sculpture at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where she was awarded a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 1970. She subsequently moved to New York City and became associated with the artist group Terminal Services. Rudahl's early work was characterized by its use of industrial materials and an emphasis on the physicality of her sculptures. Her later work has been more conceptual, focusing on ideas such as time, space, and memory. Rudahl has exhibited her work throughout the United States, as well as in Europe and Asia. She currently lives and works in New York City.
