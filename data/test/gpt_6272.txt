The USC Trojans baseball program represents the University of Southern California in college baseball. The Trojans have been a member of the NCAA Division I Baseball Championship since 1978 and have appeared in the College World Series 12 times.  USC has had a long and successful baseball program, which has produced many great players. Some of the most notable Trojans baseball players include Mike Scioscia, Reggie Jackson, Ozzie Smith, and Don Mattingly. The Trojans have won ten National Championships, most recently in 2004.
