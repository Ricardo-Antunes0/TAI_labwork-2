Dan Ryan is a retired professional Halo player who was ranked in the top 10 of the world for a majority of his career. After leaving competitive Halo, Dan has continued to be involved in the community by hosting tournaments and working as a caster.  Ogre 1 is a custom game engine created by Dan Ryan for Halo 3. It is considered one of the most advanced custom game engines to date, and has been used in several tournament finals.
