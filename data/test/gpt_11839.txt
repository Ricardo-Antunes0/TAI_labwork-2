Mark Wetmore (born June 29, 1953) is an American actor, director, and producer. He has appeared in a number of feature films and television series since the early 1980s, including roles on the soap opera Another World and the sitcom Night Court. He has also directed several episodes of Night Court and contributed to the show's writing staff.  Wetmore was born in the Bronx, New York City, and raised in Lockport, New York. He graduated from Lockport High School in 1971. Wetmore then studied at SUNY Binghamton, graduating with a degree in theater arts in 1975.  Wetmore began his acting career in film and television roles in the early 1980s. He appeared as Eddie on the soap opera Another World from 1982 to 1983. He then appeared as Jerry Markowitz on the sitcom Night Court from 1984 to 1989. Wetmore directed three episodes of Night Court during its final season: "The Wedding", "Ain't Misbehavin'" and "Goodbye, Jerry".  Wetmore has since appeared in a number of feature films and television series. In 1994 he played Dr. David Styles on the short-lived medical drama Diagnosis: Murder. Wetmore also appeared in several episodes of ER during its first two seasons, and on the sitcom The Office during its first three seasons.
