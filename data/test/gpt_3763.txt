Bela Talbot is a fictional character on the HBO television series "The Sopranos", played by Edie Falco.  Angelo "Bela" Talbot is the head of the Soprano family crime syndicate in New Jersey. He has a volatile relationship with Tony Soprano, as well as with his nephew A.J. Soprano. Talbot is also involved in illegal activities such as loan sharking, extortion, and money laundering.  Falco received critical acclaim for her portrayal of Talbot, which has been cited as one of the highlights of the show.
