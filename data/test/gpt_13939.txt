Dave McSharry (born 10 July 1990 in Dundalk, County Louth) is an Irish professional footballer who plays for Shelbourne in the League of Ireland.  McSharry started his career in the youth system of Dundalk F.C. and made his senior debut in 2009. He spent time on loan at Shelbourne, Bray Wanderers and Athlone Town over the course of four seasons before signing for Shelbourne in January 2015.
