Oropom (Oworopom, Oyoropom, Oropoi) is an African language spoken by the Oropom people in Ghana. It is similar to the Fanti language, but has a few more loanwords from English. There are about 5,000 speakers of Oropom, most of whom are in the Volta Region.  Oropom is a member of the Niger-Congo family and is closely related to the Fanti language.
