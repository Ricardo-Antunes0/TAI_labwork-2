Liam Murray Davis (born 2 August 1984) is an English cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-fast bowler.  Davis was born in Bristol, England, and played for Gloucestershire County Cricket Club before moving to Nottinghamshire County Cricket Club in 2009. He made his first-class debut for Nottinghamshire in the 2009 County Championship against Sussex. He made his List A debut for Nottinghamshire in the 2009 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy against Gloucestershire. Davis has since played for Nottinghamshire in the 2010 County Championship, 2011 County Championship, 2012 County Championship, 2013 Friends Life t20, 2014 Royal London One-Day Cup, 2015 NatWest t20 Blast and 2016 NatWest t20 Blast.
