Larry Donelle Drew II (born March 5, 1971) is an American retired professional basketball player and current coach.  Drew played college basketball at Southern Illinois University Carbondale and was drafted by the Detroit Pistons in the first round of the 1993 NBA draft. He also played for the Philadelphia 76ers, Orlando Magic, Milwaukee Bucks, Atlanta Hawks and Los Angeles Clippers. Drew is best known for his time with the Pistons, where he won two championships (2004 and 2006). He also played in the 2007 championship series with the Hawks.
