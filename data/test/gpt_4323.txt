Hombisilu is a 1978 Indian Kannada-language romantic drama film written and directed by P. S. N. Saradha and produced by V. S. Nagaraj. The film stars Rajkumar, Jayalalithaa, Sridevi and Mohan in the lead roles.  The film tells the story of a love triangle between a man, a woman and her brother. The climax of the movie is based on the story of Radha and Krishna from the Hindu epic Mahabharata.  The soundtrack of the film was composed by M. M. Keeravani and the film was released on 17 August 1978.
