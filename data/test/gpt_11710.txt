Panorama Film Studios, sometimes referred to as PFS, is a motion picture and television production company located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.  Panorama Film Studios was founded in 1946 by Norman Jewison and Harry G. Lennix. The company has produced a wide variety of films, including "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, "The Chronicles of Narnia", "X-Men", "Spider-Man", and "Batman Begins". They have also produced many Canadian television shows, including "The Simpsons", "MacGyver", "The Outer Limits", and "Stargate SG-1".
