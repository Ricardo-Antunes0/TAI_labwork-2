The International Thespian Society (ITS) is an international organization of theatre professionals. Its purposes are to promote the art of theatre, to foster the highest standards of theatre practice, and to recognize excellence in theatre.  The ITS was founded in 1916 and has more than 13,000 members in over 120 countries. It is the largest theatrical organization in the world, and its headquarters are in Chicago, Illinois. The ITS presents more than 300 performances annually at its member theaters around the world. It also produces and distributes theatrical works.
