Mafi is the name of:  Alfie Kohn's book on the philosophy of education, An Education: What It Is, What It Ain't, and How to Get It; the Mafia, an Italian-American criminal organization; a game similar to poker, played with cards  Mafia is a term used to describe a number of different things. In the context of criminology and sociology, it is used to refer to an Italian-American criminal organization. In the book An Education by Alfie Kohn, the term is used to describe a system of education that is not conducive to the individual. The Mafia is also a card game similar to poker.
