Myton School is a coeducational secondary school with academy status in Myton, near Wakefield, West Yorkshire, England. It has around 1,000 pupils aged 11 to 18 and has a current sixth form intake of around 350 students.  The school was founded in 1876 as a private boys' school by the Reverend Nathaniel Myton. It became a public school in 1904 and became an academy in 2011.  The school is now part of the Wakefield Metropolitan Borough. The headteacher is Mr Tim Dutton.
