Lorraine was a battleship of the French Navy. She was named for the French region of Lorraine.  Lorraine was laid down on 21 September 1911 at the Arsenal de Toulon, launched on 25 May 1913, and commissioned into the French Navy on 5 September 1914. She spent her entire career in the Mediterranean Sea, where she participated in battles against the Ottoman Empire, Italy, and Britain. Lorraine was decommissioned on 1 July 1931 and stricken from the naval register on 1 August 1931. She was sold for scrap on 6 December 1932.
