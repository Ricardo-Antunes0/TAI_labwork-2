After is a 2012 fantasy thriller film directed by Alex Proyas and written by Proyas and Chad Hodge. The film stars Evan Rachel Wood, James McAvoy, and Gemma Arterton.  The film tells the story of a mother (Wood) and her two children (Mackenzie Foy and Beau Knapp) who are forced to evacuate their home when a massive storm hits the area. While on the road, the mother comes into contact with a group of people who offer them shelter. The mother initially distrusts these people, but she eventually warms up to them. However, she soon realizes that they are not who they seem to be. The mother and her children are then forced to fight for their lives against these people.
