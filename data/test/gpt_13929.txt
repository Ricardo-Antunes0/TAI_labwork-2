The Blizzard is a quarterly football magazine that covers the National Football League. The magazine was founded in 2002 by Tim Brando and John Ourand. The magazine is headquartered in Tampa, Florida.   The Blizzard is the only magazine dedicated to NFL football coverage in the Tampa Bay area. The magazine has a circulation of 18,000 copies and is mailed to subscribers throughout Florida and parts of Georgia.  The Blizzard covers the NFL with a focus on team news, player profiles, game previews, and analysis. The magazine also includes features on Tampa Bay sports figures and news from around the NFL.
