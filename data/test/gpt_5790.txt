Music Machine II (AKA The Music Machine: The Second Story) is a 1988 American science fiction action comedy film written and directed by Gary Goddard. The film stars Jeff Conaway, Leslie Nielsen, and Bebe Neuwirth.  The film is a sequel to the 1987 film Music Machine, which was also written and directed by Goddard. In the original film, the Music Machine was a large circular machine that played music from the future. In the sequel, it has been rebuilt and is now a small toy car that can play music from any era.  The film follows the adventures of two friends who use the Music Machine to travel through time to different eras to retrieve musical instruments that have been stolen by villainous aliens.
