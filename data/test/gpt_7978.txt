Reckless (also known as Born Reckless and Hitchy) is a 1935 American film directed by William A. Wellman and written by Sidney Buchman. The film stars Humphrey Bogart, Lauren Bacall, and Claire Trevor.  The film is set in San Francisco during the Jazz Age. It tells the story of Harry Morgan (Bogart), a down-and-out gambler who becomes involved with a young woman (Bacall) and her gangster boyfriend (Trevor). Morgan becomes desperate to win back the woman he loves and put an end to the gangster's criminal activities.
