Nikki Holland (born in Las Vegas, Nevada) is an American singer and songwriter. She has released three studio albums: "Nikki Holland" (2009), "The Fire Within" (2012), and "Heartbreak Road" (2015). Holland has also released four singles: "I Believe in a Thing Called Love", "Good Grief", "My Life Would Suck Without You", and "All I Wanna Do".  Holland was a contestant on the second season of the television series "The Voice". She finished in third place.
