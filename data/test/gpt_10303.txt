HMS Urge was a British U-class submarine, of the third group built. She was laid down on 12 September 1940, launched on 15 December 1941 and commissioned on 17 May 1942.  Urge served in the Mediterranean and North Atlantic theaters of World War II. On 5 May 1944, she was sunk in the Gulf of Lion by depth charges from the German U-boat U-584. All 33 crew were lost.
