Mississippi Highway 504 (MS 504) is a state highway in Mississippi. The highway runs from U.S. Route 61 in Wiggins to U.S. Route 82 in Clarksdale.   MS 504 is a two-lane road that traverses the north-central part of the state. The highway connects Wiggins and Clarksdale, two of the most important towns in the state. MS 504 is also a major connector between the north and central parts of Mississippi.
