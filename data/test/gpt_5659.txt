Winifer María Fernández Pérez (born ) is a Spanish female volleyball player. She was part of the Spain women's national volleyball team.  Fernández was born in Valencia, Spain. She has played with Valencia VBC since 2007. With the club she has won five National Championships (2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014). Fernández also played with Valencia-based club VC Mónaco from 2011 to 2013. In 2014 she moved to Atenas Volleyball Club, where she played until 2018.   Fernández made her debut for the Spain women's national volleyball team in 2009. She played at the 2009 Mediterranean Games and at the 2010 World Championship qualification. With Spain she reached the final at the 2013 World Championship in Italy, where they lost to Russia. Fernández was a member of the Spanish team that won silver medal at the 2015 European Championship in Bulgaria.   In 2018 Fernández signed with Italian club Atenas Volleyball Club for the 2018–19 Serie A1 season.
