Bullcroft Colliery was a coal mine situated in the village of Bullcroft, North Yorkshire, England. The mine was operational from 1871 until 1984 when it was closed due to mining subsidence.  Bullcroft Colliery was one of the oldest coal mines in North Yorkshire, opening in 1871. The mine was located in the village of Bullcroft, and employed around 100 miners. Towards the end of its life, the mine was plagued by mining subsidence, and was eventually closed in 1984.
