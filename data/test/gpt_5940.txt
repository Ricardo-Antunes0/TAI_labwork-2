The Ħal Resqun catacombs at Gudja, are a series of underground tunnels and caverns in the vicinity of Qormi, Malta. The catacombs were used as a cemetery from the late 18th century until the early 20th century. Today, the catacombs are open to the public as a tourist attraction.
