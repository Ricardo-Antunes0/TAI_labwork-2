Purvis Short (born July 2, 1957) is an American actor and comedian.  Purvis Short was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He is the son of Shirley Short, an actress and dancer, and the late Purvis Short, an actor and comedian.  Short has appeared in a number of films and television shows throughout his career, including roles in The Princess Diaries, 48 Hrs., The Whole Nine Yards, and House of Wax. He also hosted the short-lived game show Aftermath on ABC.  In 2013, Short released his first stand-up comedy album, Ain't Nothin' Funny.
