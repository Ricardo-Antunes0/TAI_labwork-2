Christmas Møllers Plads is a major junction in the city centre of Copenhagen, Denmark. The square is named for Christian Møller, who was Prime Minister of Denmark from 1848 to 1851.  The square is located in front of the Royal Palace and in front of the Town Hall. It is bordered by Frederiksberg Palace to the south, Kongens Nytorv to the west, Rådhuspladsen to the north and Christiansborg Palace to the east.
