The Soul Enterprise is often described as a "nonprofit social enterprise" that provides sustainable employment and training opportunities to at-risk youth. The Soul Enterprise was founded in 1997 by Bill McNeal and his wife, Margi.  The Soul Enterprise provides job training and employment opportunities to at-risk youth in the Nashville area. The Soul Enterprise also offers educational opportunities, including access to computer technology and job skills training.  The Soul Enterprise is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
