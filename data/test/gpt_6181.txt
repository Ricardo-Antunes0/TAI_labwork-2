Kenneth George Beamish (born 25 August 1947) is a former Australian rules footballer who played with Footscray in the Victorian Football League (VFL).  Beamish, a wingman, was recruited by Footscray from Subiaco in the North West Football League. He made his debut for the club in 1965 and played a total of 133 games over the next nine seasons. His most successful season was 1968 when he kicked 44 goals from 18 games. He won the club's best and fairest award in 1971.     After playing his last game for Footscray in 1972, Beamish joined North Melbourne for the 1973 season. He played just one game for the Kangaroos before returning to Footscray. He retired from football at the end of the 1974 season.
