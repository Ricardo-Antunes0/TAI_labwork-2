Brut y Brenhinedd ("Chronicle of the Kings") is a Welsh medieval chronicle.  Brut y Brenhinedd is the earliest known Welsh record of royal ancestry. It was written in the 12th century, and is a history of the dynasties of Gwynedd and Powys. The work is divided into three sections, covering the reigns of Owain Gwynedd (1063–1070), Gruffudd ap Llywelyn (1071–1093), and Maredudd ap Gruffudd (1094–1107).
