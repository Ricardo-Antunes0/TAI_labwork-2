Jacques Vilfrid, born 23 January 1923, in Paris, is a French writer and journalist.  Jacques Vilfrid was born in Paris on 23 January 1923. He studied at the University of Paris and then worked as a journalist for various newspapers. Vilfrid's first novel, Les Fiancés, was published in 1955. He received the Prix Goncourt for his novel Le Dernier Jour des Filles Lyriques in 1978. Vilfrid has written novels, short stories, and essays, and has been awarded the Légion d'honneur and the National Order of Merit.
