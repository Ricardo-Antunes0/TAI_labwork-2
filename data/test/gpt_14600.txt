Thokottu is a commercial junction to the south of the city of Kochi in Kerala, India. It is an important bus stop on the route between Ernakulam and Thrissur. The junction connects NH 47 (Palakkad-Kochi) with NH 47A (Ernakulam-Thrissur). It is also a major junction for buses travelling between Cochin and Palakkad. The junction has a number of commercial establishments, including banks, hotels, and restaurants.
