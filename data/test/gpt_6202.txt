Bajirao Mastani is a 2015 Indian Hindi-language historical drama film written and directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. It is based on the 1816 novel of the same name by Ranjit Singh Kalra. The film stars Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh, and Priyanka Chopra in lead roles, with Shahid Kapoor, Sanjay Dutt, and Aditya Roy Kapur in supporting roles.  The film tells the story of 18th-century Maratha ruler Bajirao Mastani and his relationship with the powerful Panna Bai. The story has been described as a "watershed moment" in Maratha history, and was praised for its direction, cinematography, music, and costumes.
