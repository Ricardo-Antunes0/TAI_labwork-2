Jamis Bicycles is an American distributor of bicycles and bicycle parts.  Jamis bicycles was founded in 1974 by brothers Jay and Paul Jamis. The company has been headquartered in Seattle, Washington since its inception. Today, Jamis is one of the largest bicycle distributers in North America, selling bicycles, bicycle parts and accessories through a network of more than 1,600 authorized dealers.  The company's product line includes road, mountain, commuter and touring bikes; cyclocross bikes; and kids' bikes. In addition to its own brands, Jamis distributes bikes from notable brands such as Trek, Giant, Cannondale and Specialized.  The Jamis brand is known for its innovative designs and quality construction. Bicycles are available in a range of prices, with models designed for both recreational riding and serious competition.
