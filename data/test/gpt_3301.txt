NEMO Equipment, Inc. is a company based in Irvine, California, that manufactures and sells diving equipment. The company was founded in 1994 by two brothers, Jens and Ulf NEMO.  NEMO Equipment manufactures a wide range of products for scuba diving, snorkeling, freediving, and spearfishing. The company's main products are masks, tanks, regulators, and pumps. NEMO Equipment is known for its innovative designs and high quality materials.  NEMO Equipment has a global presence with sales offices in the United States, Europe, Asia, and South America. The company's product line includes both entry-level and high-end products. The company's flagship product is the NEMO Titan freediving mask, which has been featured in several diving magazines and has won multiple awards.
