Gerber Reservoir is an irrigation impoundment created by the Gerber Dam on the Sacramento River in Northern California. The reservoir is east of Red Bluff and south of Auburn. Gerber, who was instrumental in its construction. The primary purpose of the reservoir is irrigation, with a secondary purpose of providing hydroelectric power. Recreation opportunities are available on the lake, including fishing and swimming.
