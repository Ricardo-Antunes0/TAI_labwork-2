Beth Louisa Morgan (born 27 September 1981) is an English cricketer. Morgan is a left-handed batsman who bowls right-arm slow-medium. She was born in Middlesbrough, North Yorkshire.  Morgan made her debut for Durham Women in the 2000 Women's County Championship. She played for the county until 2007, making 58 appearances and scoring 455 runs at a batting average of 16.08, with a highest score of 44 not out. In 2002 she made her debut for the England Women's cricket team against Wales women. She has since played for England in both Test and One Day international matches, as well as making appearances for the England women's cricket team in the Women's Cricket World Cup.  Morgan made her Twenty20 international debut for England against New Zealand on 2 September 2009. In April 2010 she was named as one of the twelve players to represent England in the 2010 ICC Women's World T20 Qualifier tournament in Bangladesh. She made her T20I debut for England against South Africa on 21 May 2010 and scored her first ever T20I run, an unbeaten 22 from 10 balls. Morgan was part of the England team that won the ICC Women's World T20 championship in Australia in March 2013.
