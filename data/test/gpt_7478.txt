The 33  is a 2015 English-language Spanish-language horror film directed by David F. Sandberg and written by Eric Heisserer. The film stars Jeremy Renner, Elizabeth Olsen, and Jake Gyllenhaal.  The film tells the story of a team of specialists who are sent to investigate a series of murders in rural Colorado. They soon realize that the killings are being carried out by an entity known as "The 33".
