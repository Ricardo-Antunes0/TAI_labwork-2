Filth is the debut album of the British heavy metal band Maranatha. It was released in 1984 through Combat Records.  The album was recorded at Abbey Road Studios and produced by David Gilmour. The album has been described as "a milestone in modern heavy metal" and is noted for its fast, aggressive, and virtuosic playing. The album's lyrics are about religious themes and the dark side of human nature.  The album has been reissued several times, most recently in 2006 with bonus tracks and a new booklet.
