Meesha Madhavan  is a 2002 Indian Tamil romantic comedy film written and directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Madhavan and Meera Jasmine in the lead roles, with R. Madhavan, Prakash Raj and Sathyaraj appearing in supporting roles. The soundtrack was composed by A. R. Rahman.  The film tells the story of a struggling director, Meesha (Madhavan), who falls in love with a wealthy woman, Kavya (Jasmine), after she helps him get his stalled film back on track. However, Kavya's family is not too keen on the idea of their daughter getting married to a poor man and they set out to break up the couple. The film culminates with Meesha and Kavya getting married against all odds.
