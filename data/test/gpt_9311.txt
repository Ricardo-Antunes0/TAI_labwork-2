Orangutan Island is an American documentary television series which aired on Discovery Channel from 2007 to 2009. The show follows the lives of two orangutans, Kiko and Roro, as they live on an island in the Borneo rainforest.  The series was created by award-winning filmmaker Lynne Ramsay and produced by Ramsey's production company, On the Road Films. It is narrated by actor Christopher Plummer.  The first season of the series aired in 2007 and consisted of 10 episodes. Season two aired from 2008 to 2009 and consisted of 12 episodes. Both seasons were released on DVD in 2009.
