Ismail Darbar is an Indian Bollywood film actor and producer. He has appeared in over 100 films in Hindi, Telugu, and Kannada languages.  Darbar was born on 1 January 1941 in the city of Mangalore in Karnataka, India. After completing his schooling from St. Aloysius High School, Mangalore, he started his career as a stage actor and worked with several theatre groups before making his debut in the 1970s in Hindi films. He achieved major success in the 1980s with films such as "Chandni", "Hum Aapke Hain Koun..!", and "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge". Darbar has also produced and acted in several films, most notably "Chak De India" (2003) and "Kal Ho Naa Ho" (2007).
