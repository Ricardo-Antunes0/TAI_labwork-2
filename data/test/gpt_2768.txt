Diamond Park was a baseball stadium in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The stadium was located at the corner of Yonge and Dundas Streets and it opened in 1905. The ballpark was home to the Toronto Blue Jays (1905-1957), the Toronto Maple Leafs (1957-1971), and the Toronto Argonauts (1972-1977). The stadium closed in 1977 and was replaced by a new facility, SkyDome.
