The Baseball Project is a supergroup composed of fourteen internationally acclaimed artists who create collaborative works around the theme of baseball. The project was initiated by artist and baseball enthusiast Jeff Koons in 2005.  The Baseball Project is an international collaboration of fourteen artists, who create collaborative works around the theme of baseball. Jeff Koons initiated the project in 2005 with the intention of bringing together a group of artists with a common interest in the game, and to provide a forum for their creative interchange.   The artists involved in The Baseball Project come from a variety of disciplines, including painting, sculpture, photography, and video. Their works explore the history of baseball, its players, and its stadiums. The Baseball Project has exhibited at venues around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Centre Pompidou in Paris, and the National Gallery of Victoria in Melbourne.
