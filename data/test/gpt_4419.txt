"Ghost" is the debut single by American singer Halsey. It was released on August 12, 2017, by Def Jam Recordings. The song was written by Halsey, Benny Blanco, and Max Martin and produced by the latter two.  "Ghost" is a synth-pop and electropop song that lyrically discusses the aftermath of a relationship. It garnered generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its catchiness and production. The song has since peaked at number one on the US "Billboard" Hot 100, becoming Halsey's first number-one single in the country. In the United Kingdom, it became her first number-one single on the UK Singles Chart. "Ghost" has also charted in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden and Switzerland.
