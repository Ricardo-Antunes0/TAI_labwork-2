James Anthony "Seamie" Heffernan (born 17 July 1961) is an Irish former professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Heffernan started his career with Sligo Rovers, making over 200 league appearances for the club. He then moved to English side Wolverhampton Wanderers, where he made over 150 appearances. He then had a short spell with Italian side AC Milan before moving back to England to play for Queens Park Rangers. He then had a spell in the United States with Los Angeles Galaxy before retiring in 2000.  Heffernan represented Ireland at international level, making 14 appearances between 1984 and 1990.
