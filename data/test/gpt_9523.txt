"Awesome God" is a contemporary worship song by Hillsong United. The song is about praising God for His greatness and for all that He has done in the believer's life.  The song was written by Joel Houston, Alex Scally, and Lecrae. It was released on March 3, 2016, as the lead single from the band's tenth studio album "Love Wins". "Awesome God" has reached number one on the Christian Songs chart and the Hot Christian Songs chart. The song has also reached the top ten of the Canadian Hot 100 and the UK Singles Chart.  On November 10, 2017, "Awesome God" was nominated for a Grammy Award in the Best Song Written for Visual Media category.
