Khullja Sim Sim (transl. Open Sesame) is a 2013 Indian Tamil-language action film written and directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Vikram, Arya, R. Sarathkumar and Trisha in lead roles. Produced by C. S. Amudhan and edited by A. K. Sekar, the film tells the story of a group of men who are falsely accused of a crime they did not commit and must fight against the government to prove their innocence.  Khullja Sim Sim is set in a fictional village in Tamil Nadu, India, during the early 1980s. The film's plot centres around a group of men who are falsely accused of a crime they did not commit and must fight against the government to prove their innocence.   Khullja Sim Sim was shot in Chennai and Kodaikanal during March and April 2013. The soundtrack was composed by Ilaiyaraaja, with lyrics written by Vaali.
