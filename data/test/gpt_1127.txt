Shefford was a railway station on the Shefford Railway, which opened on 5 September 1867. The station closed to passengers on 3 November 1956, and to goods on 7 November 1966.  The Shefford Railway was a short-lived railway line that ran between Shefford and Wokingham in Berkshire. It was opened in 1867, and closed to passengers on 3 November 1956, and to goods on 7 November 1966.
