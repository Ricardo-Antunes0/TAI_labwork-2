Rear Admiral Rajanathan "Rajan" Kadiragamar, MVO (born 10 December 1952) is a decorated officer of the Sri Lankan Navy, who has served in a variety of senior commands.  Kadiragamar was born in Jaffna on 10 December 1952. He was educated at Royal College, Colombo and the National Defence College, Chittagong. He entered the Sri Lanka Naval Service in 1970 and rose to the rank of Rear Admiral. He has served in a variety of senior commands, including the Naval Staff and the Western Naval Command. In 2006 he was appointed Commander of the Eastern Naval Command. Kadiragamar retired from active service in 2010.  Kadiragamar has been awarded the Vishisht Seva Medal, the Maha Vir Chakra, and the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal. He is married with three children.
