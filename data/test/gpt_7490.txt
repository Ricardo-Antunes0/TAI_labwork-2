"Cutbacks" is the 17th episode of the fourth season of the American television comedy series 30 Rock, and the 71st episode of the series overall. It was written by Tina Fey and directed by Don Scardino. The episode aired on NBC in the United States on October 10, 2009.  In the episode, Jack Donaghy (Alec Baldwin) makes a series of budget cuts at his show, 30 Rock, which leads to conflict with his boss NBCUniversal CEO Jeff Zucker (Christina Applegate). Meanwhile, Liz Lemon (Fey) tries to find a new job after being let go from her old one and Kenneth Parcell (Jack McBrayer) starts dating a woman who is not sure she wants to be in a relationship.  "Cutbacks" received generally positive reviews from television critics. They praised the writing and performances of the cast and commended the episode for its humor.
