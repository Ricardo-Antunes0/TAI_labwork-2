The discography of the Bajan four-piece band Cover Drive consists of one studio album, one extended play, and six singles.  Cover Drive was formed in Toronto, Ontario in 2013 by vocalist/guitarist David Williams, drummer Tyler Smith, and bassist/vocalist Michael McCarron. The band signed to Atlantic Records in early 2014 and released their debut album, "The Deep End", that year. The album peaked at number eleven on the Canadian Albums Chart and was certified gold by the Canadian Recording Industry Association. "The Deep End" was followed up by the singles "Laugh Now Cry Later" and "Walking on Sunshine". In June 2017, Cover Drive released their second album, "The Flame". The album reached number four on the Canadian Albums Chart and was certified platinum by the Canadian Recording Industry Association.
