Streams is a 1999 album by various artists, released on the independent record label Kranky.  The album features contributions from Animal Collective, the Dirty Projectors, Grant Green, David Bowie, the Faint, and Sonic Youth.  The album was released to mixed reviews from critics. Some praised its eclectic mix of styles while others found its lack of cohesion frustrating. However, its popularity among alternative music fans has led to it being considered one of the seminal releases of the 2000s indie rock movement.
