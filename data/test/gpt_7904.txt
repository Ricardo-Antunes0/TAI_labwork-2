Frank Van Passel (born 23 June 1964) is a Dutch retired footballer who played as a midfielder.  Van Passel made his professional debut with Go Ahead Eagles in 1984. He went on to play for Feyenoord Rotterdam, FC Twente, Feyenoord Amsterdam, SC Heerenveen and ADO Den Haag, winning the Dutch championship with Feyenoord Amsterdam in 1995.  He made his international debut for the Netherlands in 1989 and played at the 1994 World Cup.
