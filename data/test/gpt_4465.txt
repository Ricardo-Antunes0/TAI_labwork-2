OneHope is an international ministry based in the United States, which helps people find hope in times of crisis.  OneHope was founded in 2006 by husband and wife team Rob and Staci Johnson. The ministry's goal is to provide hope to people who are facing difficult challenges, and to help them find resources and support to overcome those challenges. OneHope operates through the distribution of resources, including books, audio materials, and online resources.  OneHope has a network of over 2,000 volunteers in the United States and around the world who provide assistance to people in need. The ministry also operates a crisis hotline, which is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. In addition, OneHope offers training and resources to churches, schools, and other organizations.  OneHope is headquartered in Troy, Michigan.
