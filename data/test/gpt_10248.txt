Tegwen Malik (born 21 January 1975, in Hargeisa, Somaliland) is a Somali long-distance runner. She represented her country at the 2000 Sydney Summer Olympics, where she finished in thirty-second place in the women's marathon. Tegwen also competed at the 2001 World Championships in Athletics and the 2003 World Championships in Athletics, but without reaching the final round. In 2002 she was named Somali Sportswoman of the Year.
