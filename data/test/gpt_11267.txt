Sail Out is the debut extended play by American singer-songwriter and record producer Halsey. It was released on December 2, 2017, by Astralwerks and Interscope Records.  The extended play consists of six tracks, including the lead single "New Americana". "Sail Out" is Halsey's first studio album since her debut studio album, Hopeless Fountain Kingdom (2015).  The extended play received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the extended play has an average score of 73, based on 11 reviews.  Jillian Mapes of "The Fader" gave the extended play a positive review, writing that "Halsey has delivered one of the year's most anticipated debuts with Sail Out. The six tracks here are cohesive and well-crafted, with a distinctly personal feel that makes them all the more impressive."   Tamar Herman of "Billboard" also gave the extended play a positive review, writing that "Sail Out is an accomplished debut EP from Halsey – full of emotive songs about love and loss that are sure to resonate with her fans."
