La Ruffiana is an older female character who first appeared in the 1981 Italian children's animated series "The Adventures of Milo and Otis". La Ruffiana is a wise old dragon who lives in a cave on the peak of Mount Ruffino. She is often consulted by Milo and Otis on their adventures.
