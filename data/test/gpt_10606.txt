Carlos Goez (December 13, 1939 – December 10, 1996) was a Brazilian artist and sculptor.  Carlos Goez was born in Rio de Janeiro, on December 13, 1939. He studied at the School of Fine Arts of the University of Brazil, where he obtained his BFA in 1961. In 1965 he moved to Paris, where he worked for several years as a sculptor and muralist. In 1970 he returned to Brazil and began working in clay. He exhibited his work at the Museum of Modern Art in São Paulo (1971), at the Sao Paulo Biennial (1972), and at the Venice Biennale (1976). He also participated in group exhibitions in Brazil, Spain, France, and the United States. Goez died in São Paulo on December 10, 1996.
