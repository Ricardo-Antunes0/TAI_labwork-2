Robert Arthur Allison (born December 3, 1937) is an American former racecar driver. Allison started racing in 1956 and won the NASCAR Grand National Series championship in 1981. He also finished second in the 1982 and 1983 championships. Allison also competed in the Indianapolis 500 from 1965 to 1990, winning twice (1969 and 1986). He was inducted into the National Motorsports Hall of Fame in 1992.
