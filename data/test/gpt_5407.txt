Tell Jemmeh , also known in Hebrew as Yalla Yahweh, is a Hebrew word meaning "Come, let us go up to the Mount." It is the name of an ancient Canaanite god.  The word "Jemmeh" has no English translation, but it is occasionally used as a term of address for God. It appears in the Bible in the book of Joshua as the name of the deity who Joshua addresses as "Yalla Yahweh". In the book of Judges, it is used to refer to God as the "Lord of Hosts." The Septuagint translates it as "Ruler of the Heavens and Earth."
