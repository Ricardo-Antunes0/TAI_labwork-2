Bhadokhar is a village in Rahi block of Siwan district in the Indian state of Bihar.  Bhadokhar is located at . It has an average elevation of 94 meters (305 feet).   India census, Bhadokhar had a population of 5,648. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Bhadokhar has an average literacy rate of 53%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 61%, and female literacy is 47%. In Bhadokhar, 17% of the population is under 6 years of age.
