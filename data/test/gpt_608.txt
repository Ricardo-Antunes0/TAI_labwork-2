Fairlight is an isometric projection arcade adventure game developed and published by Ocean Software for the Amiga, Commodore 64, and ZX Spectrum. It was also ported to the Atari ST and Commodore Amiga CD32.  The game was written by Paul Elliott and produced by Ian Livingstone. It was originally released in 1987.  Fairlight tells the story of a young girl, Dana, who is kidnapped by pirates and taken to an unknown island. There she must find her way back home, aided by a talking fish named Fairlight who can help her explore the island and solve puzzles.
