Mun Kyong-dok (; born October 12, 1957) is a retired South Korean football player and coach.  Mun Kyong-dok played for Pohang Steelers, Suwon Samsung Bluewings, Daegu FC, Gyeongnam FC and Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors in the K-League. He also played for the South Korea national team, and was a participant at the 1986 FIFA World Cup. Mun Kyong-dok was a head coach at Pohang Steelers, Daegu FC and Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors.
