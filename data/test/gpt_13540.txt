G-Force is a 1980 studio album by the rock band Aerosmith. It was the band's fourth album and produced their biggest hits to that point, including the number one singles "Walk This Way" and "Love in an Elevator".  The album was well received by critics, who praised the band's hard rock sound and the songs' catchy melodies. However, it was not as successful as their earlier albums, reaching only No. 8 on the "Billboard" 200 chart.
