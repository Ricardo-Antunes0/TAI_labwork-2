Surin railway station was opened on 6 December 1934 as part of the Eastern Line of the State Railway of Thailand. The original building was destroyed by an earthquake in 1975, but a new building was rebuilt and opened on 1 November 1978.  The station is served by all trains travelling between Bangkok and Trat. It is also the terminus of the Surin branchline from Trat.
