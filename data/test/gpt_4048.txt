The pilot episode of the American dramedy-mystery television series "Desperate Housewives" aired on October 3, 2005 on the ABC television network. The show was created by Marc Cherry, who also served as the show's executive producer and writer.  The series follows the lives of the residents of Wisteria Lane, a suburban street in suburban Orland, California. The show focuses on the relationships of its female characters and their various struggles and tribulations.  The pilot episode introduces the characters of Edie Britt (Marcia Cross), Lynette Scavo (Felicity Huffman), Paulette Burdette (Annie Potts), and Bree Van de Kamp (Ed O'Neill). Each character is struggling with a different problem or issue, and the three couples become friends as they share their stories and woes.
