Undead is a paranormal romance book series written by Sarah Zettel. It follows the story of Liv andDeclan, two teenagers who are immunocompromised and must constantly fight for their lives against the undead.  The books are set in a world where the undead have risen and spread chaos. Liv and Declan are two teenagers who are immunocompromised, meaning that they have a very low chance of surviving in a world full of zombies. They must constantly fight for their lives, relying on their skills as fighters and their wits to stay alive.
