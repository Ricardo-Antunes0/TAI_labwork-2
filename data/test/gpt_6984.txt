Klangspuren, also Klangspuren Schwaz, subtitled Tiroler Festival Klangkunst, is an annual music festival in Schwaz, Tirol, Austria. The festival was founded in 2006 and takes place over three days in late September.  The festival programme features both classical and contemporary music from all over the world. It has a strong focus on contemporary classical music and has twice been awarded the prestigious Austrian Music Prize.
