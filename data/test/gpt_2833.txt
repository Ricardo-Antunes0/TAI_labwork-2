Marshall Ayub (born 5 December 1988) is a Pakistani cricketer. He made his Twenty20 debut for Karachi Kings in the 2013–14 season.  Ayub made his first-class debut for Sindh in the 2010–11 Inter-Provincial Trophy. He made his List A debut for Sindh in the 2011–12 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy, playing six matches before being transferred to Peshawar Zalmi the following season. Ayub made his first-class debut for Peshawar Zalmi in the 2012–13 Inter-Provincial Trophy, playing six matches before being transferred to Karachi Kings the following season. Ayub made his Twenty20 debut for Karachi Kings in the 2013–14 season.
