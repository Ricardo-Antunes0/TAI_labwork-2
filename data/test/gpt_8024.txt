Agatha Lin, born in Qinglong in the Sichuan Province of China in 1949, is a contemporary Chinese painter. She moved to Beijing in the early 1970s and began her career as an artist in the city's vibrant art scene. Her work is marked by its use of bright colors and lively brushstrokes, and often features scenes from everyday life.  Lin has exhibited her work throughout China and abroad, and has been the recipient of numerous awards, including the prestigious National Art Award of China in 1994. In addition to painting, Lin is also a skilled sculptor and has created several pieces of public art. She currently lives and works in Beijing.
