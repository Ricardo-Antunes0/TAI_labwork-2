Kattathurai is a village located in Kanyakumari district, Tamil Nadu. It is situated about 20 kilometers from the district headquarters of Kanyakumari.  The village has a population of about 1,000 people and is home to a number of temples, including the Sri Kattathurai Amman Temple and the Kattathurai Thiruvizha Perumal Temple. The village also has a number of schools, including the Kattathurai Government Secondary School and the Sree Durga Vidyanikethan School.
