Ruzayevsky District  is an administrative and municipal district (raion), one of the twenty-four in Nizhny Novgorod Oblast, Russia. It is located in the south of the oblast. The area of the district is . Its administrative center is the town of Ruzayevka. Population: 36,435 (2010 Census); The population of Ruzayevka accounts for 61.5% of the district's total population.
