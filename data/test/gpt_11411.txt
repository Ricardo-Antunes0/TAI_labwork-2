Eibert Johannes Calvinus Tigchelaar (born 1959, Sint-Niklaas, Belgium) is a Belgian artist known for his paintings, sculpture, and installations.  Tigchelaar was born in 1959 in Sint-Niklaas, Belgium. He studied at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Antwerp from 1978 to 1985, receiving his diploma with the thesis "The Animation of Form." He then continued his studies at the Accademia di Belle Arti di Venezia, Italy, from 1985 to 1988, receiving a masters degree with the thesis "The Animation of Form II."  Tigchelaar has exhibited his work in Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Sweden, and the United States of America. In 2002 he was the recipient of the Grand Prize for Painting at the Biennale di Venezia. In 2006 he was awarded the Prince Bernhard Award.
