Katy Fox is a fictional character from the Fox TV series "New Girl" played by Zooey Deschanel. Fox describes the character as "smart, sassy, and unapologetic", and notes that she is described as "the new girl at the school who everyone wants to get to know."  Fox was born on November 18, 1986, in Santa Barbara, California. She is an only child and was raised in a Catholic household. When she was 15 years old, her family moved to Oregon. Fox studied at Oregon State University, where she earned a degree in communications.  After graduating from college, Fox moved to New York City to pursue an acting career. She began her career performing in plays in New York City. In 2011, Fox guest starred on the NBC series "Parenthood". That same year, she was cast in the recurring role of Jess on the Fox sitcom "New Girl". The series premiered on September 20, 2011, and concluded on May 15, 2015.  In 2016, Fox starred in the independent romantic comedy-drama film "The Meddler". The film was released on September 22, 2016.   In 2017, Fox starred opposite Ben Stiller in the comedy-drama film "Stuber". The film was released on June 15, 2017.   In 2018, Fox starred alongside Ryan Reynolds in the action comedy film "Deadpool 2". The film was released on February 12, 2018.
