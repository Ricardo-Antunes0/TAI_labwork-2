Guoshan Stele  is a stele located in the Wulingyuan Scenic and Historic Site in Shaanxi Province, China. It is a National Key Cultural Monument of China.  The Guoshan Stele was erected in 806 AD by Emperor Xianzong of Tang, to commemorate his victory over the rebel general Guo Kan. The inscription on the stele is in the Old Chinese script.  The stele stands 73.5 cm high and is made of sandstone. It has a circumference of 85 cm and weighs about 10 kg.
