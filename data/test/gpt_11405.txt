Jason Bourne  is the title character of a series of action thrillers written by Robert Ludlum. The first book in the series, The Bourne Identity, was published in July 1974 and was followed by five sequels over the next decade.   The Bourne series is set during the late 1970s and early 1980s and features an amnesiac CIA agent, Jason Bourne, who is pursued by the agency and other government agencies. The novels are generally based on real-life events and have been praised for their fast-paced action, suspenseful plot, and detailed depictions of espionage techniques.
