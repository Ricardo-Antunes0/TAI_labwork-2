Bianca Jasmine Lawson (born March 20, 1979) is an American actress, singer and model. Lawson was born in Atlanta, Georgia and raised in Birmingham, Alabama. She is the daughter of singer-songwriter Anita Baker and actor Bruce Lawson. Lawson made her acting debut in the 1997 film Sister Act 2. She has since appeared in films such as The Preacher's Wife (2008), The Help (2011), and Straight Outta Compton (2015). Lawson has also starred in the television series Girlfriends' Guide to Divorce (2010-2013) and Empire (2015-2018). She has also been a regular cast member on the ABC singing competition show The Voice since its inception in 2012.
