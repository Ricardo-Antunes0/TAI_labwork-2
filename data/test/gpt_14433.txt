Pterostylis bicornis, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a species of orchid endemic to eastern Australia. It has a single leaf and up to twelve brightly coloured flowers with a long spur on the back of the petals. The flowers are pollinated by bees.  Pterostylis bicornis grows in wet areas, often near creeks and rivers. It is found in New South Wales, Victoria, Queensland, and the ACT.
