The Queer Lounge is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that provides a safe and welcoming space for LGBTQIA+ individuals and their allies. The Queer Lounge provides resources and support to its members, organizes social and educational events, and offers a variety of programming that focuses on self-expression, creativity, and empowerment.  The Queer Lounge has been providing safe and welcoming space for LGBTQIA+ individuals in Springfield since 1994.
