Anastasiya Vladimirovna Kuzmina (, ; n√©e Shipulina; born 5 September 1984) is a Russian professional tennis player.  Kuzmina has won three singles titles on the WTA Tour, including two at the Kremlin Cup in Moscow. She also reached the semifinals of the 2007 Wimbledon Championships, and was a semifinalist at the 2008 US Open. She has also won four doubles titles on the WTA Tour, including two at the Kremlin Cup.  Kuzmina made her WTA tour debut in October 2003 and achieved her first top-10 singles result in April 2004, when she reached the semifinals of the Kremlin Cup. She reached her best singles ranking of world number 10 in April 2007. Her best doubles ranking was world number 4 in March 2007.   She married Ukrainian player Sergiy Stakhovsky on 17 July 2009. They have one child born in October 2010. Kuzmina and Stakhovsky divorced in December 2016.   Kuzmina represented Russia at the Fed Cup from 2004 to 2009, winning a bronze medal in 2006. In doubles, she has won seven medals (two golds, four silvers and one bronze).
