Ee.Ma.Yau (Malayalam: ഈ.മ.യൗ, contraction for Eesho Mariyam Yaa, meaning "Our Lady, Mother of Jesus") is a 2002 Malayalam film directed by Joshua Mohan. The film is based on the Gospel of Luke and tells the story of a poor family who are given a miraculous opportunity to have their three daughters married off to wealthy men.   The film was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2002 Cannes Film Festival. It won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Malayalam and the Kerala State Film Award for Best Feature Film.
