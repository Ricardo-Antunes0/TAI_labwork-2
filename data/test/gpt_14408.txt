The discography of Shannon Noll, an Australian country music singer, consists of twelve studio albums, one compilation album, sixty-eight singles, thirty-one promotional singles and nineteen video singles.  Noll has sold over six million records worldwide, making her one of the most successful country music artists of all time. She has won three Grammy Awards, four Australian Country Music Awards, and two Golden Globe Awards. She has been inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame and the Australian Recording Industry Association (ARIA) Hall of Fame.  Noll was born Shannon Elizabeth Noll in Wangaratta, Victoria, Australia on 9 January 1961. She began singing at a young age and first appeared on Australian country music television in 1981. After signing with Warner Bros. Records in 1984, she released her debut album, "Shannon Noll", that year. The album produced two charting singles—"I Believe in You" and "The Way You Look Tonight"—and was certified platinum by the Australian Recording Industry Association (ARIA).
