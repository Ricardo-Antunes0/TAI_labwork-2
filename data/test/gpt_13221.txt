Raymond Reginald 'Ray' Bailey (born 16 May 1931) is a former English cricketer who played in 4 Tests and 5 ODIs from 1958 to 1960.  Bailey was born in 1931 in Birmingham, England. He played as a wicket-keeper for Warwickshire from 1953 to 1957, and for Worcestershire from 1958 to 1960. He made his first-class debut for Warwickshire against Oxford University in 1953. He made his Test debut for England against the West Indies in 1958. He played 5 Tests and 5 ODIs for England.
