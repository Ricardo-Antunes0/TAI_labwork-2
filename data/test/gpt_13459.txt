Ferdinand Sumague Topacio (born 9 November 1965) is a Filipino politician who has been the Mayor of Navotas since May 2016.  Born in Navotas City, Ferdinand Topacio is a business tycoon and the son of Ferdinand Sumague, Sr., a former president of the Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation. He is married to Elisa Topacio with whom he has three children.  Topacio served as a councilor of the Municipality of Navotas from 2001 to 2003 and as its vice mayor from 2003 to 2006. He was elected mayor in May 2016, succeeding Jojo Binay, who had been elected Vice President of the Philippines in May 2015.
