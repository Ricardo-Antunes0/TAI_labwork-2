The Specials, also known as The Specials or The Specials Original Soundtrack, are a British ska, rocksteady and reggae band. Formed in 1981 in the London area, they were among the first ska revivalists.  The band has had several line-ups, with the current incarnation consisting of Jerry Dammers (vocals and saxophone), Neville Staple (guitar), Carlton Barrett (bass guitar), and Clive Chalmers (drums). Original members included vocalist and saxophonist Rico Rodriguez, rhythm guitarist Bunny Wailer, and bassist Morris Williamson. The band achieved international success with their singles "Ghost Town", "The Message", and "No One Can Ever Know".  The Specials were ranked number 72 on VH1's 100 Greatest Artists of All Time.
