The Shorter Jewish Encyclopedia (SJE; ) was an English-language encyclopedia of Jewish life, history, and culture. It was published by the Jewish Publication Society of America from 1906 to 1985.  The Shorter Jewish Encyclopedia was the first comprehensive English-language encyclopedia of Jewish life, history, and culture. The first edition was published in 1906, with a revised and enlarged edition appearing in three volumes in 1922. A second edition was published in 1944, and a third edition, revised and enlarged, appeared in 1965. An updated fourth edition was published in 1982, and a fifth edition was published in 2000. A sixth edition was published in 2017.
