Jaws is a 1974 novel by American author Peter Benchley. It tells the story of a great white shark that attacks a small town on Amity Island, and the efforts of the townspeople to destroy it.  The novel has been adapted for the screen numerous times, including three highly successful films: Jaws (1975), Jaws 2 (1978), and Jaws 3-D (1983). It was also adapted for a Broadway musical, Jaws: The Musical (1986).  Jaws was the highest-grossing film of all time until Star Wars was released in 1977.
