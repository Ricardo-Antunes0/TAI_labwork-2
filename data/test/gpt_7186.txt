Elixir Studios was a British video game developer based in Guildford, Surrey. The company was founded in 2003 by brothers Tom and Andy Fenton, who had previously worked together at Crystal Dynamics.  Elixir Studios was best known for its work on the "Need for Speed" franchise, most notably "", "", "", and "". The company also developed the mobile game "Crazy Taxi: Rush Hour". In March 2018, it was announced that Elixir Studios had ceased operations.
