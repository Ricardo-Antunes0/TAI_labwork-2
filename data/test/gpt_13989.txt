Jaypee James de Guzman (born April 20, 1995) is a Canadian professional soccer player who currently plays as a midfielder for Toronto FC in Major League Soccer.  De Guzman was born in Toronto, Ontario, to parents from the Philippines. He played youth soccer for the Toronto Lynx and Vaughan Azzurri before joining the Toronto FC Academy in 2011. He made his professional debut for Toronto FC on July 19, 2015, in a match against Montreal Impact. He was named to the MLS All-Star team for the 2016 season.
