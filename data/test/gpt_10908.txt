Tajik (Tajik: , , , ), also known as Tajiki Persian, is the primary language spoken in Tajikistan. It is also spoken in Afghanistan and Uzbekistan.  Tajik is an Indo-European language of the Iranian branch, and is written in the Perso-Arabic script. The language has dialects, including Pamir dialect, Isfaraati dialect, and Kulob dialect. The language has a population of about 8 million speakers, making it the sixth most spoken language in the world.
