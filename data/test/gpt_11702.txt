The Citrus Center, also known as the Florida Citrus Center, is an entertainment and exhibition complex in Orlando, Florida, United States.  The Citrus Center is a complex of venues that hosts concerts, theater performances, conventions, trade shows and other events. The centerpiece of the Citrus Center is the Orlando Arena, which can seat up to 18,000 people for concerts and 16,000 people for theater performances. The arena also features a retractable roof that allows it to host events in all weather conditions.   The Citrus Center is located adjacent to the Amway Center and the University of Central Florida's home football stadium, the George M. Steinbrenner Field at Raymond James Stadium. The Citrus Center is also next to the Amway Center parking garage and the Amtrak station.
