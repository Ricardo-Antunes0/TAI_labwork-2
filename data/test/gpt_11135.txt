Lawson Aschenbach (born November 22, 1983) is an American professional ice hockey defenseman who is currently playing with the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League (NHL).  Aschenbach was selected by the Detroit Red Wings in the second round, 52nd overall, of the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. After playing four seasons at the University of Minnesota where he won a Calder Cup in 2004 as captain of the Golden Gophers, Aschenbach made his NHL debut with Detroit in 2006. He played for the Red Wings, Colorado Avalanche, and San Jose Sharks before joining Chicago in 2015.
