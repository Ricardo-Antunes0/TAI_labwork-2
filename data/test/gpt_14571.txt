Quiero (Spanish for I Want) was released as the third and final single from Selena's fourth studio album, Amor Prohibido (1994). The song was written by Selena, her brother A.B. Quintanilla III and former member of the Tejano group Los Dinos, Raul Bova.  "Quiero" is amoderate tempo ballad about a young woman who wants to be loved and cared for. The lyrics are about a girl who is looking for someone who will understand her and make her feel special. The song has been described as "melancholic" and "touching".  "Quiero" peaked at number thirty-two on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and number one on the US Hot Latin Tracks chart. It also peaked at number eight on the UK Singles Chart. The song was certified Gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) in 1995.
