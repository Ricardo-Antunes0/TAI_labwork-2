Bryan John Trottier (born July 17, 1956) is a Canadian former ice hockey player who played at the professional level for the New York Islanders, Montreal Canadiens, and Pittsburgh Penguins. He was a four-time Stanley Cup champion and a member of the NHL Hall of Fame.  Trottier was drafted in the first round, ninth overall, by the New York Islanders in the 1975 NHL Entry Draft. He played his entire career with the Islanders, amassing 925 points (373 goals, 602 assists) in 1,224 games. Trottier won four Stanley Cups with New York (1980, 1982, 1985, 1994), and was also a member of the team that won the Stanley Cup in 1980 (as part of the "Patrick Division" champions).
