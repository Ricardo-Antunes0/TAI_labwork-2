Derrick, a rover, was recruited from Williamstown in the 1928 VFL Draft. He made his league debut in 1929 and played a starring role in Footscray's 1931 premiership triumph. Derrick missed the 1934 season through injury but returned to play in 1935 and 1936. He retired at the end of the 1936 season, having played 132 games for Footscray.  Derrick served as coach of Footscray in 1938 and 1939. He also coached Victoria in interstate matches during the 1940s. Derrick died of cancer at the age of 52.
