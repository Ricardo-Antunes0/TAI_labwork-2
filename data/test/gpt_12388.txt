Katherine 'Kath' Gillespie Sells MBE is a Scottish businesswoman and philanthropist. She is the founder, CEO and President of Inspirato Group, a global digital agency.  Katharine Gillespie was born in Glasgow, Scotland in 1952. She graduated from the University of Strathclyde with a degree in Business Administration in 1974. After graduation, she worked for a number of small businesses before starting her own company, Inspirato Group, in 1988.   Inspirato Group is a global digital agency that provides marketing and branding services to businesses across a range of industries. The company has offices in the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, France, Germany and Sweden. Gillespie has been described as a "trailblazer" and "pioneer" in the field of digital marketing. In 2009, she was awarded an MBE for her services to business and philanthropy.
