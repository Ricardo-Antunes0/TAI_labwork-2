Extemporaneous commentary (or extemp com for short) is a form of public speaking in which a speaker spontaneously and on the spot gives their opinion on a given topic or issue. It is an often lively and engaging way to communicate, and can be an effective way to add flavor and personality to speeches and presentations.  Extemp com can be a great way to get your point across quickly and easily, and can be a powerful tool for impressing audiences. However, it is important to be aware of the risks associated with extemp com, as it can be difficult to control the flow of your thoughts and can lead to careless or even embarrassing remarks.  Overall, extemp com is an interesting and dynamic form of public speaking that can be very effective in communicating your ideas quickly and easily. However, be aware of the risks involved, and use it wisely!
