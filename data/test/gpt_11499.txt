Afrotelphusa is a genus of moths in the family Geometridae.  There are about 17 described species in Afrotelphusa.   Some species of Afrotelphusa can be found in the Afrotropical ecozone.  Afrotelphusa species are usually small, with wingspans of 2-5 cm. They have a distinctive pattern on their wings, which can be very colourful and striking. They are nocturnal and fly low to the ground, catching prey with their proboscis.
