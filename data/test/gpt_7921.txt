Machines Dream are a Canadian progressive rock band led by guitarist and vocalist Ross Benton.  Formed in Toronto, Ontario in 1990, Machines Dream have released six studio albums, two EPs, and one live album. The band's music has been described as progressive rock, metal, and metal rock. They have toured internationally, playing headlining shows in places such as Germany, Italy, Spain, Japan, and the United States.  In 2006, the band released their first album in 14 years entitled "The Great Unknown". The album was well received by critics and saw a resurgence in popularity for the band. In 2009, Benton left the band to focus on his solo career. He was replaced by Ryan Wombles.  In 2018, the band released their eighth studio album "The New World", which was well received by critics.
