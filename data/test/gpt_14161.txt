NASIG (formerly, the North American Serials Interest Group, or NASIG) is a nonprofit organization with the mission of promoting the sharing and use of serial literature. NASIG was founded in 1969 and now has over 1,000 members in North America.  NASIG is the governing body for the sharing and use of serial literature in North America. It sponsors and promotes the reading and discussion of serials, provides educational resources, maintains a registry of serials, and provides support for the creation and distribution of serials. NASIG also works to increase awareness of serial literature among libraries, bookstores, and other institutions serving the reading public.
