SureFire, LLC. is an American company headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona, that manufactures firearms accessories and ammunition. The company was founded in 1992 by Curt A. Thompson and is a subsidiary of Vista Outdoor Inc.  SureFire produces firearm accessories, such as laser sights, muzzle brakes and flashlights, under the brands XM-L2, L2A3, AF1, Scout Light, Defender Light and Scout Mount. These products are distributed in the United States, Canada and internationally.  SureFire is known for its high-quality products and customer service. The company has a history of developing innovative products that improve the accuracy and performance of firearms. SureFire has also been involved in charitable work, donating $5 million to help rebuild Haiti after the 2010 earthquake.
