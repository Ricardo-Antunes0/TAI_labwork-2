Christopher Andrew Swallow (born 27 December 1981) is an English cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and right-arm medium-pace bowler.  Swallow made his debut for Hampshire in the 2001 Minor Counties Championship against Dorset. From 2001 to 2003, he represented the county in 11 Minor Counties Championship matches, the MCCA Knockout Trophy, and the NatWest Trophy. In 2002, Swallow made his List A debut against Northamptonshire in the 2nd round of the 2002 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy. From 2003 to 2006, he represented Hampshire in 14 List A matches, the MCCA Knockout Trophy, and the C&G Trophy. In his 14 List A matches, Swallow scored 307 runs at an average of 23.50, with a high score of 54 not out against Sussex in the 2005 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy. He also took 11 wickets at an average of 31.92, with best figures of 3/38 against Warwickshire in the 2006 C&G Trophy.
