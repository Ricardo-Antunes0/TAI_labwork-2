Totally Dance is an album in the Dance Dance Revolution series. Released on July 26, 1998, Totally Dance is the sixth album in the DDR series and is the first DDR album released for the Sony PlayStation.  This album was released for the Sony PlayStation console and contains twelve songs. The songs are all remixes of earlier DDR songs, with new background music and animations. Totally Dance was met with mixed reviews from fans of the DDR series. Some praised the new music and animations, while others found it too similar to earlier DDR songs and were disappointed that no new tracks had been added.
