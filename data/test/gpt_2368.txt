"The Special Two" is a song by American recording artist Justin Bieber. It was written by Bieber, Max Martin, and Shellback and produced by the latter two under their stage name The Messengers. The song was released as the fourth and final single from Bieber's debut album, My World (2009).  The song was well received by music critics, who praised its production. Commercially, "The Special Two" reached the top ten in several countries, including Canada, Germany, and the United States. A music video for the song was directed by Colin Tilley and premiered on December 18, 2010. In it, Bieber and his backup dancers perform choreographed dance routines on a stage set to the song's accompanying electronic music.  "The Special Two" was nominated for Best Pop Song at the 53rd Annual Grammy Awards.
