The Mitsubishi 3G8 engine is a range of engines manufactured by the Japanese automaker Mitsubishi. They debuted in the 2006 Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution VII and have been used in various models since then.  The 3G8 engine is a inline-6 engine that is based on the previous 3G7 engine. It has been redesigned to make more power and torque while using less fuel. The 3G8 engine is also smoother than its predecessor, making it more enjoyable to drive.
