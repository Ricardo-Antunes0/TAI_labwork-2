Burkeville is an unincorporated community in northern Hopkins County, Texas, United States. It is located on U.S. Route 281 about 15 miles northeast of the county seat of Plainview.  The community was founded in 1892 by the Burke family, who settled there after moving from Tennessee. The town was named for the Burke family's ancestor, Colonel William Burke.
