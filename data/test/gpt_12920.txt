Igor Mišan (, born 5 May 1990) is a Serbian football midfielder who plays for Red Star Belgrade.  Igor Mišan started his career in his homeland with OFK Beograd. After two seasons, he moved to Germany and joined FC Augsburg, spending two years there. In June 2013, he signed for Red Star Belgrade.  Mišan was a member of the Serbian under-20 team that competed at the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup in Egypt.
