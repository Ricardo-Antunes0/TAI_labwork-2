Josep Maria Bonet (1903, La Seu d'Urgell – 1977, Barcelona) was a Spanish painter and sculptor.  Josep Maria Bonet was born in 1903 in La Seu d'Urgell, Catalonia. He studied at the School of Fine Arts of Barcelona, where he was a pupil of Jaume Plensa and Lluís Domènech i Montaner. In his early career, he specialized in painting abstracts and landscapes. However, he became better known for his sculpture, which often features realistic human figures in fragile or distorted environments. His work has been exhibited at the Museum of Modern Art of Barcelona, the Modern Art Centre of Valencia, and the Palau de la Música Catalana. He died in 1977.
