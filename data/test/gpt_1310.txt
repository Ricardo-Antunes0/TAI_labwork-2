Eliminator is a children's quiz show in the UK, originally broadcast on BBC1 from 2 February 1984 to 17 January 1988, and then repeated on BBC2 until 6 January 1999. It was produced by London Weekend Television and was hosted by Bob Holness.  Eliminator was a children's quiz show where contestants had to answer questions in various rounds, with the first contestant to score ten points winning the game.
