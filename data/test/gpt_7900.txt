is a manga series written and illustrated by Yūki Tabata. The story follows the life of Himeko Ōgi, a high school student who has a unique alabaster skin that gives her supernatural powers.  Six years ago, Himeko Ōgi was struck by a mysterious illness that caused her to lose her hair and her skin turned alabaster. In order to save her life, her parents took her to an underground medical facility where they experimented on her with treatments that left her with superhuman abilities.   Now a high school student, Himeko is forced to keep her true identity a secret from the people she loves. She spends her days attending school and trying to live a normal life while hiding the fact that she can transform into a white dragon. However, when she learns that terrorists are planning to kill the president of Japan, she must use her powers to stop them.
