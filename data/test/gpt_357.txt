"Sweet Banana" is a Rhodesian song and dance performed by South African singer Mafikizolo. The song is about a man who is in love with a woman and wants to take her away to a place where there are no problems.  The song was released as the lead single from Mafikizolo's album "She's Got the Power" in 2007. It reached number one on the South African Music Chart and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry of South Africa (RISA).  The song has been praised for its catchy lyrics and its unique sound. It has been featured on several television shows and has been used in commercials.
