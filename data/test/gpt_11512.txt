Shanna Lynn Moakler (born March 28, 1975) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Bree Van de Kamp on the ABC television series Desperate Housewives, and as Rebecca Pearson on the NBC television series The Office. Moakler has also starred in the films Exchange Alley, I Now Pronounce You Chuck and Larry, and the Broadway musical The Producers.  Shanna Lynn Moakler was born in Grosse Pointe Farms, Michigan, to Barbara (née Lampkin) and Michael Moakler. She has two older sisters, Tracy and Toni. She became interested in acting at an early age and made her debut at the age of nine in a small role in the movie Heartbeats. Moakler attended Grosse Pointe South High School where she was a member of the choir and theater programs. After high school, she studied acting at the University of Southern California, graduating in 1997 with a BFA in Theater.  After college, Moakler moved to New York City to pursue a career in acting. She got her first major role on the ABC TV series Desperate Housewives as Bree Van de Kamp from 2005 to 2012. Her performance earned her three consecutive Golden Globe Award nominations for Best Actress – Television Series Musical or Comedy (2007–09). In 2007 she also won a Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Actress in a Comedy Series. Moakler's breakout role was as Rebecca Pearson on the NBC sitcom The Office from 2005
