"Comic Perversion" is the fifteenth episode of the fourth season of the American animated television series South Park. It originally aired on Comedy Central in the United States on December 10, 1997.  The episode is about a group of boys who become obsessed with comic books and superheroes, and start to behave in disturbing ways.  Cartman is the most disturbed of the group, and he starts to think that being a supervillain is the ultimate goal of his life. But soon enough, the other boys catch on to Cartman's obsession, and they start to imitate his twisted behavior.   The episode culminates with a battle between Cartman and Stan as the two men try to outdo each other in their comic book-inspired villainy.
