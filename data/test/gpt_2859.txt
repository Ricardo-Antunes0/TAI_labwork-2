Godzilla vs. Kong is a 2021 American science fiction monster film produced by Legendary Pictures and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. It is the twenty-first film in the Godzilla franchise, and the fifteenth in the Monsterverse series.  The film is written and directed by Michael Dougherty, from a screenplay by Zach Shields and Max Borenstein. It stars Peter Dinklage, Tom Hiddleston, Brie Larson, John Goodman, and Corey Hawkins.  The two monsters square off in a battle to determine who is the king of the Monsters of Earth.
