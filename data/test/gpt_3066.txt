Kadhal Kadhai , also known as Velu Kadhai is a 2009 Tamil romantic drama film written and directed by newcomer R. Sundarrajan. The film stars debutant Madhavan and Meena in the lead roles, with Prabhu, Trisha, Madhumitha and Raja in supporting roles. The story centres around the blossoming love between an agricultural worker Velu (Madhavan) and a city girl Anusuya (Meena).  Kadhal Kadhai was released on 12 December 2009 to positive reviews from critics. The film won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Tamil and was also nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Tamil Film. It was also nominated for the Vijay Awards for Best Film and Best Screenplay.
