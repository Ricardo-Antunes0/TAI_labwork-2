Bianca Fallon Mann (born 21 February 1995) is an Australian actress and singer. She has starred in the Australian television series Neighbours since 2013, and in the American television series Scream Queens since 2015.  Mann was born in Melbourne, Victoria, to American parents. She has two older sisters. Her mother is a casting director and her father is a music producer. Mann started her musical career as a teenager, singing in choirs and performing in local theatre productions. In 2013, she was cast in the Australian television series Neighbours as Paige Patterson. The show ended in 2016. In 2015, she was cast in the American television series Scream Queens as Chanel Oberlin. The show ended after three seasons in 2018.   Mann has appeared in several films and television shows since 2015, including The Death of Stalin (2017), The Nun (2018), Aquaman (2018), and Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018).
