Eskimo Recordings is a Ghent-based record label founded by Joris Voorn and friends in 2006. The label concentrates on releasing contemporary dance music, with a focus on techno, house and minimal.  Eskimo Recordings was founded in 2006 by music lovers Joris Voorn and friends. The label concentrates on releasing contemporary dance music, with a focus on techno, house and minimal. Eskimo Recordings has released music by artists such as Ricardo Villalobos, Marcel Dettmann, Nicolas Jaar and more.
