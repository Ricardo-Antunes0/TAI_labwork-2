The Lucky Bullets is a Norwegian rockabilly band from Oslo. The band was formed in 2006 by guitarist Vegard Aune and singer-guitarist Morten Harket.  The Lucky Bullets released their debut album "The Lucky Bullets" in 2007. The album was produced by Reidar Kjellsen of the Norwegian rock band Hives, and mixed by Tony Visconti. The album peaked at number 9 on the Norwegian Albums Chart. In 2009, the band released their second album "Smoke and Mirrors". The album was produced by John Feldmann of the American punk rock band Goldfinger, and mixed by Andy Wallace.  In 2010, the band released their third album "The Killer EP". The album was produced by Ben Kweller, and mixed by John Congleton. In 2011, the band released their fourth album "Now or Never". The album was produced by Dave Cobb of the American rock band Cobbler, and mixed by Bob Ludwig.  In 2012, the band released their fifth album "No Guts No Glory". The album was produced by Justin Broadrick of Godsmack, and mixed by Tom Coyne. In 2013, the band released their sixth album "Church of Rock 'N' Roll". The album was produced by Rick Rubin of Def Leppard, and mixed by Brendan O'Brien. In 2014, the band released their seventh album "Crazy Nights". The album was produced by Richard Fortus of Cheap Trick, and mixed by Andy Wallace
