11th Hour is the 11th novel of the Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. It was published on September 30, 2007 in the United Kingdom by Bloomsbury and on October 14, 2007 in the United States by Scholastic Press.  The story follows Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows as he races against time to find and destroy Voldemort's remaining Horcruxes.
