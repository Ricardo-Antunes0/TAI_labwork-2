FabricLive.49 is a 2009 album by Buraka Som Sistema.  FabricLive.49 is a 2009 album by Buraka Som Sistema. It was recorded in two parts, with the first half taking place at the Berlin-based club Berghain and the second half taking place at the Spanish festival Primavera Sound. The album was released on CD, LP and digital download.  The album's material ranges from electronica to funk, with guest appearances from members of The Juan Maclean, DJ Premier and Q-Tip. Upon its release, FabricLive.49 received positive reviews from music journalists, who praised its experimental sound and praised Som Sistema's vocals.
