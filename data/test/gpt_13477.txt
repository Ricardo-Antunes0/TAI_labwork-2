Sapphire Wind Farm is a 270 megawatt wind farm in Osceola County, Iowa, United States. The farm will have a total of 133 turbines.  The Sapphire Wind Farm is expected to provide clean, renewable energy for Iowa businesses and households for decades to come. Located in the scenic Osceola County, the Sapphire Wind Farm will create jobs and support local economic development.  The Sapphire Wind Farm is made up of 133 wind turbines that will generate enough electricity to power 270,000 homes. The wind turbines will be placed on 12,500 acres of land, providing a significant economic boost to the local community.
