The Red August , originally meaning August Red , was a series of three coordinated terrorist bombings that took place on August 17, 1995, in the Israeli city of Netanya . The bombings killed 31 people and injured over 150.  The attacks were carried out by the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine - General Command (PFLP-GC), an armed wing of the Palestinian Liberation Organization   The bombings were part of a wave of Palestinian terrorist attacks against Israel in August 1995. The PFLP-GC claimed responsibility for all three attacks.
