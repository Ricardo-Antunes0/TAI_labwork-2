Choi Soo-young (; born February 10, 1990), also known mononymously as Soo Young, is a South Korean actress and singer. She made her acting debut in the television series Dream High in 2008 and has since appeared in a number of popular television series and films.  Soo Young first rose to fame as a member of the girl group Girls' Generation, who debuted in 2007. The following year, the group released their first album,cyber girl, which became a critical and commercial success. Girls' Generation went on to release two additional successful albums before disbanding in 2013. In 2014, Soo Young made her solo debut with the EP Winter Story. Her subsequent single "Party" achieved widespread popularity and topped the charts in several countries.   Since making her solo debut, Soo Young has continued to appear in various projects including the KBS2 drama Reply 1997, the MBC drama My Love From the Star, and the SBS drama Twenty Again. In 2018 she starred in the tvN drama The Heirs which was critically acclaimed and won several awards including Best Drama at the Baeksang Arts Awards.
