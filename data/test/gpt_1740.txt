Johnny Răducanu (born Răducan Crețu; 1 December 1984) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for FC Astra Giurgiu.  Răducanu started his career with Universitatea Craiova, making his senior debut in the 2002–03 season. He spent six seasons at Craiova, winning the Liga I championship in 2006 and 2007. In 2009, he moved to Turkish side Adanaspor, where he won the Turkish Cup in his first season. He rejoined Craiova in 2011, helping them win the Liga I championship that year. In 2013, Răducanu joined Rapid București. He spent two seasons with Rapid, helping them reach the semifinal of the Romanian Cup in 2014.  On 8 July 2016, Răducanu signed for FC Astra Giurgiu. He made his debut for Astra on 22 July 2016 in a 0–0 away draw against Universitatea Cluj.
