OutServe-SLDN was a network of LGBT military service organizations formed in 2006 to provide support to LGBT military personnel and their families.   OutServe-SLDN was founded in 2006 as a network of LGBT military service organizations to provide support to LGBT military personnel and their families. The organization has since grown to include over 50 member organizations, providing resources and support to over 15,000 personnel and their families. OutServe-SLDN works to ensure that all members of the military have the opportunity to serve their country proudly, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity.
