Dream "A"live is the eighth studio album by Japanese J-pop group Dreams Come True. It was released on September 20, 2017, by the record label Avex Trax.  The album's lead single, "Ai wa Kitto" (愛はきっと), was released on January 12, 2017. The song peaked at number 2 on the Japanese Oricon singles chart and sold over 148,000 copies in its first week.  The second single, "Namida no Iro ni" (涙の色に), was released on March 18, 2017. The song peaked at number 1 on the Japanese Oricon singles chart and sold over 321,000 copies in its first week.  The album's third single, "Kimi no Na Wa" (君のな WA), was released on May 18, 2017. The song peaked at number 1 on the Japanese Oricon singles chart and sold over 483,000 copies in its first week.  The fourth single, "Kimi no Na Wa (Instrumental)" (君のな WA (instrumental)), was released on July 22, 2017. The song peaked at number 1 on the Japanese Oricon singles chart and sold over 655,000 copies in its first week.  The fifth single, "Aishiteru" (愛してる), was released on October 6, 2017.
