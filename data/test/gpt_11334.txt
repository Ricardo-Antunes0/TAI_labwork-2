Seek Shelter is the fifth studio album by American heavy metal band Slipknot. It was released on October 5, 2009, through Roadrunner Records. The album was produced by Slipknot and Ross Robinson.  The album's lyrical themes concern personal struggles and relationships, including addiction, family, and self-esteem. Slipknot promoted the album with a world tour that began in October 2009 and ended in January 2010.  The album received mixed reviews from music critics. Some praised its technical ability, while others found its sound derivative of other metalcore and nu metal albums. It debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart and has sold over 260,000 copies in the United States.
