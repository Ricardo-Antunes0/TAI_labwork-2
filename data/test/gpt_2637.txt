The Somerville Journal is a weekly newspaper in Somerville, Massachusetts. It was founded in 1849 and is the second-oldest continuously published newspaper in New England after the Boston Globe.   The paper covers news, arts, sports, and lifestyle topics in the city of Somerville and surrounding neighborhoods. It also publishes opinion columns and letters to the editor. The Journal maintains a website and a print edition that is distributed throughout Somerville and parts of Boston.
