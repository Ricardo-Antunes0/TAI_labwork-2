Zagreb was the second of three Beograd-class Destroyers of the Yugoslav Navy. She was laid down in 1937 and completed in 1940. Zagreb served in the Royal Yugoslav Navy until she was surrendered to the Allies in 1945. After the war, she was transferred to the Italian Navy, where she served until 1962.
