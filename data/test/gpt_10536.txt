Krutetsky was born in the city of Leningrad to a family of artists. He started his artistic career in the late 1950s, painting abstract and figural art. In the early 1960s, Krutetsky began working with the iconic Russian Pop artists such as Victor Vasnetsov and Ilya Kabakov. His work often featured bright colors and catchy cartoonish characters.
