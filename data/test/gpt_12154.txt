Tesla Giga Shanghai (, or Gigafactory 3) is a proposed factory in Shanghai, China, that would be the largest lithium ion battery plant in the world.  The factory is planned to have an annual production capacity of 500,000 electric vehicles by 2020. It is also planned to produce solar roof tiles and batteries for home energy storage products.  The factory has been proposed as part of the government's plan to reduce air pollution in Shanghai.
