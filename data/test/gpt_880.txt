"Erotic City ("make love not war Erotic City") is a 2003 American neo-noir film directed by Peter Segal and written by Roger Avary. The film stars Kim Basinger, Michael Douglas, Angelina Jolie, Jamie Foxx, and Christopher Walken.  The story is set in a future society where the government has outlawed all sexual activity, leading to a society where people are forced to engage in sexual acts in order to survive. The film revolves around a pair of mismatched detectives who are investigating the murders of people who have apparently engaged in illicit sexual activities.  "Erotic City" was poorly received by critics, who criticized its plot and characterization. However, it was a box office success, grossing over $105 million worldwide.
