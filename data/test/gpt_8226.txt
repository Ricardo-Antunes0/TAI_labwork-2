Shine: The Hits is the first compilation album by Australian pop singer Kylie Minogue. It was released on 14 November 2003 by Parlophone Records. The album features fourteen tracks, including the singles "Shine" and "Can't Get You Out of My Head".  The compilation was well received by music critics, who praised its retrospective feel and the selection of Minogue's most popular songs. "Shine: The Hits" became Minogue's third consecutive number-one album in Australia, and also charted in several other countries. It won the 2004 ARIA Award for Best Compilation Album.
