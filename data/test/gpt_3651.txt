The Oliver Kelley Farm is a farm located in the town of Livingston, in Columbia County, New York. The farm is one of the oldest in the town, and has been in the Kelley family since 1792.  The farm is a working farm, with several hundred cows, pigs, horses, and other animals. The farm also grows corn, wheat, and other crops. The Kelley family also makes their own cheese and yogurt.  The farm is open to the public for tours.
