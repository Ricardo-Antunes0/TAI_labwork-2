The Desert Inn, also known as the Desert Inn Motel, is a historic building located in Palm Springs, California. The motel was built in 1952 by developer Roy Williams. It is a six-story structure, with a red-tile exterior and a white wood frame interior. The motel features a distinctive design with curved walls and an oriental-inspired entrance. The property includes a casino and a swimming pool.   The Desert Inn was added to the National Register of Historic Places on October 20, 1982.
