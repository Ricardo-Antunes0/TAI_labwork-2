Hospitality Suite is a 1992 stage play by American playwright David Mamet. The play is set in a hotel and revolves around the employees and guests of the hotel.  Hospitality Suite was Mamet's fourth play and was first produced on Broadway in 1992. The play received mixed reviews from critics but it was a popular success with audiences. The play has been performed on Broadway numerous times since its debut and has been adapted for television several times.
