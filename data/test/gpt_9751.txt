"Panda" is the debut single by American rapper Desiigner. The song was released on June 3, 2016, by Kanye West's record label GOOD Music and Def Jam Recordings. The song was produced by Hit-Boy, with additional production from Kanye West and Mike Dean.  "Panda" is a psychedelic R&B song that lyrically discusses the influence of drugs on Desiigner's life. The song debuted at number 61 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.  The music video for "Panda" was directed by Colin Tilley and premiered on July 6, 2016. It features Desiigner as a panda in a zoo, surrounded by people and animals who are under the influence of drugs.
