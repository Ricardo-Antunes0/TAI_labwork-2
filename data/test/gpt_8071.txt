Vinita Vasu was born in Mumbai, India in 1968. She is self-taught artist who lives and works in Mumbai. Vasu's paintings are often abstract and her use of color is unique. Her work has been exhibited in several exhibitions in India and abroad including the Royal Academy of Arts, London (2004), Art Institute of Chicago (2006), Asia Society, New York (2006), Hirshhorn Museum and Sculpture Garden, Washington D. C. (2007), Museum of Contemporary Art, Mumbai (2008), and The Centre for Contemporary Arts, Glasgow (2009).
