A total of twelve tankōbon volumes were published by Shueisha.  The series is set in the fictional town of Ueno, located in the prefecture of Shonan, in Japan. The story follows the members of the Junai Gumi, a group of juvenile delinquents who must band together to protect their turf from rival gangs.
