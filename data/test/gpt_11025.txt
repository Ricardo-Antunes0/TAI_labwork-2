The Reichsannalen are a class of annals kept by the Holy Roman Empire, covering the years from 962 to 1806. The annals are unique in that they are one of the few sources of non-royal information about medieval Germany. They are also a valuable source for historians studying the relations between the Holy Roman Empire and its neighbours.
