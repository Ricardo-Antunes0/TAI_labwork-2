The Mitsubishi Tredia is a subcompact sedan manufactured by Mitsubishi Motors since 1984. It has been sold in many markets around the world and is one of the most popular small sedans on the market. The Tredia is available in three trim levels: GL, GLS, and SE. The GL is the base model, while the GLS and SE offer more features and are priced higher. The Tredia has been generally well-received by critics, with praise for its fuel economy, comfortable seating, and reliable engine.
