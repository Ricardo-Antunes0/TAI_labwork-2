Lincoln Cathedral, Lincoln Minster, or the Cathedral of Lincoln, is an Anglican cathedral in Lincoln, England. It has been the seat of the Bishop of Lincoln since the 10th century and is one of the largest and most important churches in England. The current dean and chapter are Thomas John Harrison, the Bishop of Lincoln, and the Canons.  The cathedral was founded in 676, making it the oldest church in Lincolnshire.
