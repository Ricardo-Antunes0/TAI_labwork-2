Stirling James Hinchliffe (born 23 November 1970) is a British Conservative Party politician who has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for Thirsk and Malton since 2015.  Hinchliffe was educated at the independent school Eton College, where he was captain of the rugby team and played in the 1st XV in 1987. He studied History at St John's College, Cambridge, graduating with first class honours in 1993.   Hinchliffe worked as a journalist before being elected to Parliament at the 2015 general election. He is a member of the Conservative Party and served as a councillor for the town of Malton from 2003 to 2015.
