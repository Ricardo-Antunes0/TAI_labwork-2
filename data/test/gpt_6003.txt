Walter Meskell (born 1946) is an American artist and writer. Meskell lives and works in Cambridge, Massachusetts.   Walter Meskell was born in 1946 in New York City. Meskell earned his BFA from the School of Visual Arts in 1969, where he was a student of David Smith. Meskell has exhibited widely, including solo exhibitions at the Guggenheim Museum, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Kunstmuseum Basel. He has also participated in group exhibitions at venues such as the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Museum of Modern Art, and the Tate Gallery in London. Meskell is the recipient of numerous awards, including a Guggenheim Fellowship (1985), a MacArthur Fellowship (1987), and a National Endowment for the Arts Fellowship (1991).
