, S. 102, is a symphonic poem written by Hungarian composer Franz Liszt. The work was composed in 1841 and is dedicated to Princess Carolyne zu Sayn-Wittgenstein.  The work is scored for flute, clarinet, bassoon, horn, trumpet, timpani, strings and piano. It lasts around 42 minutes and is in A minor.  The symphonic poem tells the story of a heroine who dies young. Liszt employs a variety of techniques to create a moving and evocative piece.
