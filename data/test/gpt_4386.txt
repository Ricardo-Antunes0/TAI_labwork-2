8 First Dates  is a 2012 American romantic comedy film directed by Michael Goldwyn and written by Jim Herzfeld. The film stars Robert Downey Jr., Jennifer Aniston, and Jake Gyllenhaal.  The film tells the story of Cal (Downey Jr.), a man who is dating five different women at the same time. He is Introduced to Rachel (Aniston) on a blind date, and they hit it off. They go out on subsequent dates and discover that they have a lot in common. They decide to continue seeing each other, but their respective friends and families are not too sure about the match.   In the end, all of Cal's five current girlfriends end up marrying other people, but Rachel and Cal stay together and are married in a staged ceremony at the end of the film.
