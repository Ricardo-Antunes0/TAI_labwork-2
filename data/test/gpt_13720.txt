Annalisa Piras  is a London-based Italian-British artist whose practice spans sculpture, installation, performance and film.  Annalisa Piras was born in Rome, Italy in 1986. She moved to London in 2006 where she has been living and working since. Piras’ practice spans sculpture, installation, performance and film. Her sculptures explore the relationships between movement, gravity and form. Piras’ installations often reference found objects or everyday objects in unexpected ways, often creating spatial illusions or offering a new perspective on familiar objects. Piras’ performances often involve interaction with the viewer, exploring the possibilities of performance as a means of communication. Her films explore themes of identity and representation through the use of narrative and documentary techniques.
