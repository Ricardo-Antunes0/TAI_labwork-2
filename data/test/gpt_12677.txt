Andrew Stuart Pearson (born 25 September 1957) is a former English cricketer. A left-handed batsman and wicket-keeper, he played for Yorkshire County Cricket Club from 1978 to 1987 and for Somerset County Cricket Club from 1987 to 1992.  Pearson was born in Malton, North Yorkshire. He made his first-class debut for Yorkshire against Gloucestershire in 1978. He played 147 first-class matches for Yorkshire, scoring 2,786 runs at an average of 26.81 and taking 214 catches and 10 stumpings. He also played 20 List A matches for Yorkshire, scoring 468 runs at an average of 27.90 and taking six catches and one stumping. Pearson played three Tests for England against India in 1984, Sri Lanka in 1985 and Zimbabwe in 1988. He scored 309 runs at an average of 22.50, taking three catches and two stumpings in the Tests, while he scored 346 runs at an average of 24.50 with one catch and two stumpings in the three List A matches he played for England.
