Grand Aire Express was an American airline that began operations in 1984. The airline served destinations in the United States and Canada. It ceased operations in 2001.  Grand Aire Express was founded in 1984 by two entrepreneurs, Gary and Marguerite Shafer. The airline was headquartered in Addison, Texas, and operated flights to destinations in the United States and Canada. In 2001, Grand Aire Express filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection and ceased operations six months later. At the time of its closure, the airline had operated over 100 flights per week.
