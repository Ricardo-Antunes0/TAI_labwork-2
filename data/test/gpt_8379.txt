Bocking Windmill or Bocking Churchstreet Windmill is a Grade II* listed windmill in Bocking, Suffolk, England.  The mill was built in 1772 and is one of the oldest surviving windmills in Suffolk. It is currently used as a private residence.  The mill is made from coursed squared sandstone and has four double Patent sails with a span of . The milling machinery is still in working order and can be seen from the public footpath.
