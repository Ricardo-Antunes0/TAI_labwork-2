Rade Šerbedžija (, ; born 27 July 1984) is a Bosnian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Premier League club Liverpool and the Bosnia and Herzegovina national team.  Born in Zenica, SR Bosnia and Herzegovina, Šerbedžija started his career at local side FK Obilić before moving to France to join Ligue 1 club Monaco in 2002. He spent three seasons with the club, helping them to win the Ligue 1 title in 2004 and reach the semi-finals of the UEFA Champions League. He then moved to Dutch club Feyenoord, where he won three Eredivisie titles and the Dutch Cup in 2007.  Šerbedžija joined Premier League club Liverpool in July 2007 for a fee of £10 million, making him the most expensive Bosnian player of all time. He made his debut for Liverpool on 17 August 2007 in a 2–1 away win against Manchester United. Šerbedžija has since established himself as one of the most consistent players in Liverpool's midfield, helping them to win four league titles, two FA Cups, and an UEFA Champions League title during his time at the club. In 2017, he was voted by Liverpool supporters as their fifth greatest ever player.  Šerbedžija has represented Bosnia and Herzegovina at both youth and senior level.
