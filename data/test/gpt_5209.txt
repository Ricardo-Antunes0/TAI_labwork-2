Papa Benji is a comedy web series created by and starring comedian, actor and writer Andrew Santino. The series, which is self-produced and released through his own independent production company, is set in the fictional town of West Egg in Long Island, New York.   The series follows the daily lives of Benji (Santino), his wife Lisa (Kaitlin Olson) and their two children. The show is primarily a mockumentary style sitcom, with Santino playing himself as the eccentric, lovable father who constantly clashes with his wife and children, but ultimately loves them very much.
