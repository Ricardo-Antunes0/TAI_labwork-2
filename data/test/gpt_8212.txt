Akakir (, also spelled Akakeer) is a small town in the Aleppo Governorate, northwestern Syria. Nearby localities include Akhtarin to the southwest, al-Zahraa to the south, Kafr Nabl and al-Bab to the east, and Sarmin to the north. According to the Syria Central Bureau of Statistics (CBS), Akakir had a population of 5,686 in the 2004 census. In February 2013, it was reported that Akakir had been captured by the Syrian Army and that its residents were resettled in rebel-held areas in Aleppo.
