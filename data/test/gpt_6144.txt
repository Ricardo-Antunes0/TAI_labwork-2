Valwood School is an independent, coeducational, college preparatory school located in the Hudson Valley region of New York, United States. The school was founded in 1934 by Dr. James H. Valwood, a psychiatrist and educational reformer.  Valwood School is composed of a Pre-Kindergarten through 12th grade program, and offers a unique, college preparatory program that emphasizes the development of individual strengths and abilities. The school is accredited by the New York State Board of Regents and the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools.  The student body is composed of students from throughout New York State and from around the world. Valwood School's student body is largely affluent, with a diversity of cultural backgrounds and interests. The school's faculty is highly qualified and experienced in both the academic areas of instruction and in the fields of education and counseling.
