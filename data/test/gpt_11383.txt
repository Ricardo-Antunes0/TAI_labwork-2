Poppleton is the main character in a series of children's books written by British author Dick King-Smith. The books follow the adventures of Poppleton, a small, unlucky, and somewhat grumpy 11-year-old boy who is constantly pestered by his older brother and sisters.  Poppleton is a small, unlucky, and somewhat grumpy 11-year-old boy who is constantly pestered by his older brother and sisters. One day, Poppleton finds a mysterious key that leads him on an adventure to find a treasure. Along the way he meets new friends, solves mysteries, and battles evil creatures.
