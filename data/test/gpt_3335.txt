Dunston Checks In is a 1996 Canadian-American slasher film directed by David DeCoteau and written by Karen Lafferty. The film stars Dina Meyer, Jonathan Brandis, and Stacey Nelkin.  Dunston Checks In is a horror film set in a motel. A group of friends are spending the night at the motel when one of them is killed by an unknown assailant. The remaining friends start to investigate their friend's death, and soon discover that the killer is still on the loose.
