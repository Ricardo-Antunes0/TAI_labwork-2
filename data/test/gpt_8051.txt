Onehundredhours (also known among fans as '100h', pronounced "one hundred hour") is an upcoming British science fiction film written and directed by Chris Evans, produced by Kevin Macdonald, and starring Evans, Noomi Rapace, and Luke Evans. The film is set in the year 2047, and follows a team of two people who are sent back in time to the 20th century to prevent the world's first atomic bombing from happening.  The film began principal photography in March 2018 in the United Kingdom.
