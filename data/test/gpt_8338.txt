Adolf Süsterhenn (1905–1974) was a German constitutional lawyer.  Born in 1905 in Munich, Adolf Süsterhenn studied law at the universities of Freiburg, Heidelberg and Munich. He became a member of the Bavarian Bar Association in 1923 and the Berlin Bar Association in 1928.   He served as a judge on the regional court of Munich from 1941 to 1945, during which time he was also a member of the Nazi Party. After the war he resumed his legal career, serving as a judge on the regional court of München-Oberbayern from 1951 to 1974.   Süsterhenn was also a professor at the universities of Freiburg and Heidelberg, and a visiting professor at several other universities. He died in 1974.
