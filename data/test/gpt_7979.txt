Yeo Jin-goo (, born August 13, 1997) is a South Korean singer and actor. He debuted as a singer in 2016 with the single "It's You".  Yeo Jin-goo was born on August 13, 1997, in Busan, South Korea. He started his musical career as a singer in 2016 with the release of the single "It's You". He has since released two more singles, "Love in Silence" and "Good Night". Yeo Jin-goo made his acting debut in the 2017 film "The King".
