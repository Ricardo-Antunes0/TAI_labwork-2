Castlevania: Dracula X is a 1995 platform game for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. It is the tenth game in the Castlevania series, and is a prequel to the series' first game, Castlevania. The game was developed by Kojima Productions and published by Konami.  The game follows Jonathan Morris, a reporter who is investigating Dracula's return to Wallachia. He is joined by his friends Simon Belmont and Alucard, and must defeat Dracula in order to prevent his return and save Wallachia.  Castlevania: Dracula X received mixed reviews from critics, although it was praised for its graphics and music. The game has sold over one million copies worldwide.
