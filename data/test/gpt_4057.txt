"Feelings" is a song by South African rapper Zonke. It was released as the lead single from his fifth studio album "Revival" on 6 December 2018.  "Feelings" is a pop and R&B song with a tempo of 128 beats per minute. The song was written by Zonke, Kamasi Washington, and James Fauntleroy, and produced by Washington.  The accompanying music video for "Feelings" was directed by Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini and premiered on 9 December 2018. It features Zonke performing the song in a variety of locations around Johannesburg.
