The Toot-a-Loop Radio or Panasonic R-72 is a discontinued model of a transistorized radio that played records and cassettes. It was made from 1977 to 1984.  The Toot-a-Loop Radio was a transistorized radio that played records and cassettes. It was made from 1977 to 1984 by Panasonic. The Toot-a-Loop Radio had a detachable cartridge system, which allowed it to be used with either records or cassettes.
