Mark Bo Chu (born May 12, 1989) is an American professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Davos of the National League (NL) of Switzerland.  Chu was drafted by the Colorado Avalanche in the third round, 78th overall, in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft. On July 5, 2010, he signed a two-year contract with the Anaheim Ducks. He was traded to the New York Rangers on February 27, 2012, in exchange for a third-round draft pick in the 2012 NHL Entry Draft. On July 1, 2013, Chu signed a two-year contract with HC Davos of the National League (NL).
