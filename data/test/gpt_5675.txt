Bibhas Roy Chowdhury (; born 1 August 1951 in Dhaka, East Pakistan) is a Bangladeshi writer and filmmaker.  He has written over 20 books, including novels, short stories, and essays. He has also directed several feature films, including Pratidwandi (1983), Jukti Takko Na (1988), and Bishnupriya (1998). His films have received critical acclaim and been awarded several awards, including the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in 1998.  Chowdhury was born in Dhaka, East Pakistan, in 1951. He studied at the University of Dhaka and the University of California, Los Angeles. He has lived in Bangladesh since the early 1980s.  Chowdhury's writing has been described as lyrical and evocative, and his novels have been praised for their portrayal of rural life in Bangladesh. He has also written essays on film, culture, and politics. His most recent book is a biography of Rabindranath Tagore.
