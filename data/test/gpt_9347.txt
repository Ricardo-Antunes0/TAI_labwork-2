When Nietzsche Wept is a 2007 American documentary film by director Emile De Antonio. The film tells the story of Friedrich Nietzsche's sister Elisabeth FÃ¶rster-Nietzsche and her efforts to recover her brother's unpublished manuscripts, after his death in 1881.  The film was produced by the Elisabeth Nietzsche Foundation, with financial support from the German Federal Film Fund and the Swiss National Film Fund.  "When Nietzsche Wept" had its world premiere at the 2007 Toronto International Film Festival. It was subsequently released in theaters in the United States on February 4, 2008. The DVD was released on March 17, 2008.  The film has been favorably reviewed by critics. On review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, the film has a 73% rating, based on 26 reviews. Metacritic reports a 66 out of 100 rating, based on 15 reviews.
