Jamea Al Kauthar is an independent academic research center located in Kuwait City, Kuwait. It was founded in 2009 as a joint initiative of the Kuwait Foundation for Scientific Research (KFSR), the Kuwait Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, and the Kuwait Center for Strategic Studies.  Jamea Al Kauthar is a research center that specializes in Islamic Studies. It is staffed by a team of experts in various fields of Islamic studies, including theology, jurisprudence, history, and anthropology. The center also offers courses on Islamic Studies to both undergraduate and graduate students.   Jamea Al Kauthar has published several books on Islamic subjects, as well as articles in academic journals. It has also organized several conferences on Islamic studies, which have attracted scholars from across the Arab world.
