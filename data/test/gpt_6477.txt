Steven Paul Holcomb (April 14, 1980 – May 5, 2010) was an American singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist who was best known as the lead vocalist of the band Bright Eyes.  Holcomb was born in 1980 in Omaha, Nebraska. He started playing the guitar at age six, and eventually developed a strong interest in folk music. He formed Bright Eyes in 2000 with his childhood friend and bandmate Conor Oberst. The band released two albums before disbanding in 2006. Holcomb subsequently released two solo albums, and formed a new band, the Paper Kites, in 2009. He died from an accidental drug overdose on May 5, 2010 at the age of 36.
