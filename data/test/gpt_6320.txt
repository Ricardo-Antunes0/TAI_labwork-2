Keith Morton Houchen (born 25 July 1960) is a former English cricketer. Houchen was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm off break. He was born at Westminster, London.  Houchen made his first-class debut for Hampshire in 1981 against Gloucestershire. He made seventeen further first-class appearances for the county, the last of which came against Oxford University in 1988. In his seventeen first-class appearances, Houchen scored 514 runs at an average of 18.36, with a high score of 78 not out. With the ball, he took 43 wickets at an average of 27.11, with a best performance of 7/106.
