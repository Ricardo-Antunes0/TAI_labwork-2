Cee Vee is an unincorporated community in northern Harris County, Texas, United States. It is located on U.S. Route 59, about northeast of Houston. Cee Vee has a post office with the ZIP code 77024.  Cee Vee was first settled in the late 19th century by immigrants from the Czech Republic and Slovakia. The community was named for a local creekside valey. The first post office was established in 1898. In the early 2000s, Cee Vee became a popular recreational area for suburbanites who wanted to escape the noise and traffic of Houston.
