The Ford Transit Connect is a compact van manufactured by Ford. It was introduced in 2006 as a replacement for the Ford Courier. The Transit Connect is available in three trim levels: Base, E-150, and E-250.  The Transit Connect was designed for the North American market and is available in both manual and automatic transmissions. It is powered by a 1.6L I4 or 2.0L I4 engine. The Transit Connect has a payload capacity of 1,500 pounds and a fuel economy of 26 miles per gallon in the city and 38 miles per gallon on the highway.
