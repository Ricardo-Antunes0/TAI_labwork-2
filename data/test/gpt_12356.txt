Mathias Payer (born 1981) is a Liechtensteinian artist who creates installations and sculptures.  Born in 1981 in Liechtenstein, Mathias Payer began his artistic career as a sculptor, creating installations and works on paper. In 2003, Payer moved to Berlin, Germany, where he continued his work as an artist. In 2010, he participated in the Venice Biennale with his installation "The Act of Painting". Payer's work has been exhibited in museums and galleries around the world, including the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum in New York City, the Centre Pompidou in Paris, and the Kunstmuseum Bern in Switzerland.
