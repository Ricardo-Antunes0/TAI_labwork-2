The Gadsden Hotel is a historic building located in the heart of downtown Gadsden, Alabama. The hotel was opened in 1892 and is the oldest continuously operated hotel in Jefferson County. Today, the hotel offers guests a variety of accommodations, including 49 guest rooms and suites, a bar and grill, meeting space, and a convenience store. The Gadsden Hotel is a popular tourist destination for visitors to Gadsden and Jefferson County.
