Alniaria alnifolia (syns. Sorbus alnifolia and Aria alnifolia) is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae, native to southeastern Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa.  The trees are typically 1–3 m tall, with a spread of up to 4 m. The leaves are ovate-elliptical, 10–25 cm long and 4–8 cm broad, with a serrated margin. The flowers are white or pink, with five petals, and are produced in clusters of 10-30 at the top of the tree. The fruit is a cone 10-20 cm long and 5-10 cm wide, containing several small seeds.
