Miranda Nild (born 1 April 1997), also known by her mononym Miranda, is a Norwegian singer, songwriter and actress. She is the daughter of entertainer and television personality Tone Nild and actor Trond Olsen.  Nild was born and raised in Oslo. She started her musical career as a backing vocalist for her father's band, Tone & Miranda. In 2018, she released her debut solo album, "Miranda". The album peaked at number one on the Norwegian Albums Chart and was certified gold.
