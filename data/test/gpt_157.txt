Movies Anywhere (MA) is a cloud-based digital movie service that gives users access to a library of Hollywood films and TV shows across devices. MA allows users to rent or buy movies and TV shows for playback on personal computers, tablets, smartphones, and other devices. The service also allows users to access movies and TV shows with Chromecast, Apple TV, Xbox, Roku, and Amazon Fire TV.  Movies Anywhere was founded in October 2012 by Redbox and Netflix as a joint venture. The service became available in the United States on April 6, 2013. As of February 2019, Movies Anywhere has over 50 million active users across more than 1,000 million devices.
