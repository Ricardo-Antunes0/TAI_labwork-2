The Talley television relay station is sited on the summit of Mount Talley in the White Mountains of New Hampshire. The station was built in 1950 and is part of the White Mountain National Forest. It is used to relay signals from television stations in Boston and Providence, Rhode Island to viewers in southeastern New Hampshire, Vermont, and Maine.
