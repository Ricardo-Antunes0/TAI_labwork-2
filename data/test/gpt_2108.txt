Claudio Castagnoli (born 27 December 1980) is an Italian professional wrestler and actor, best known for his time in World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), where he is signed to the company as a performer and contractor under the ring name Cesaro. He is also known for his work in Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA), where he is a one-time TNA World Heavyweight Champion.  Castagnoli has also competed in Japan's New Japan Pro Wrestling (NJPW) under the ring name Hideo Saito, where he is a two-time IWGP Junior Heavyweight Champion. In February 2017, Castagnoli signed a contract with WWE, re-joining the company full-time.
