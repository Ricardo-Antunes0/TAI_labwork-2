Vijana Jazz Orchestra (also known as Vijana Jazz) is a contemporary jazz ensemble based in Vijana, a town in central Nepal. The ensemble was formed in 2002 by composer and saxophonist Dipak Karmakar and drummer Sunil Manandhar.  Vijana Jazz Orchestra has received critical acclaim for its virtuoso playing, ranging from traditional Nepalese folk music to modern jazz. The ensemble has performed at major international festivals, including the Montreux Jazz Festival, the Moscow International Jazz Festival, and the North Sea Jazz Festival.
