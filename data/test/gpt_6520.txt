Brodie Martin (born 6 November 1988) is an Australian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Melbourne Victory in the A-League.  Martin started his career at Perth Glory, before signing with Melbourne Victory in 2010. He made his debut for the Victory in 2011 and has since played over 100 games for the club, winning the Premiers' Plate in 2014 and 2016. He has also represented Australia at under-20, under-23, and senior level.
