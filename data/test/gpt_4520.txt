Gettysburg is a 1993 American epic war film directed and produced by Edward Zwick. The screenplay was written by Eric Roth and based on the novel of the same name by Michael Shaara. The film stars Tom Hanks, Tim Robbins, Laura Linney, Edward Zwick, and Bob Gunton.  The film tells the story of the Battle of Gettysburg from multiple perspectives: Union and Confederate soldiers, civilians, journalists, and politicians. It is one of the most highly acclaimed war films of all time, with five Academy Award nominations including Best Picture.
