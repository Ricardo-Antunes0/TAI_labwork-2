Kottiyoor is a village and Grama Panchayat in Kottayam District in the state of Kerala, India. The population is about 2,000 people. It is located about 10 km from Kottayam town.   Kottiyoor is famed for its 'green fields' that are dotted with coconut groves. The people are mainly farmers and the main crops are ragi, cotton, and groundnut. There is a small-scale industry consisting of a few handloom dyes and a small number of rubber goods manufacturers. The village has a primary school, a government health centre, and a few other basic amenities.   The people of Kottiyoor are friendly and welcoming. They enjoy socialising together and many of them are well-known for their talents in traditional arts such as music and dance.
