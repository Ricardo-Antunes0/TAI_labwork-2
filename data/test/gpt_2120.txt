The Kleinjena Forest is a natural area of about 2 square kilometers located in the Kleinjena municipality. It is home to many rare and endangered plants and animals.  The Kleinjena Reservoir was constructed in the 1930s to provide drinking water for the growing population of Kleinjena and the surrounding area. The reservoir has been renamed the "Hermann-von-Suhr-Reservoir" in honor of the local politician and WW II war hero who helped to make it possible.
