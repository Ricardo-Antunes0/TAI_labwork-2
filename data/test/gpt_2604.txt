Totem Skin were a Swedish hardcore punk band formed in Uppsala in 1984. The band was active until 1991, when they disbanded.  Totem Skin were a hardcore punk band from Uppsala, Sweden. Active from 1984 to 1991, the band released four albums and five singles. Their debut album, "Punk is Dead", was released in 1986 and was well received by the underground punk scene. However, their follow-up album, "Punk is Alive and Well and Living in Sweden", was less successful and the band disbanded shortly thereafter.
