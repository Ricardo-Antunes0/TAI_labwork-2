Kaunhara ghat is a river bank in the city of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It is located in the Colaba district.  The Kaunhara ghat is a promenade on the banks of the River Mahim. It stretches from Colaba to Worli. The promenade has a wide variety of shops and restaurants. The Kaunhara ghat also has a fountain with a statue of Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.
