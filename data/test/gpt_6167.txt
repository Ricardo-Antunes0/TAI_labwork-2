Further Seems Forever is an American rock band from Gainesville, Florida. Formed in 2001, the band consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Derek Miller, lead guitarist and backing vocalist Branden Steineckert, bassist and backing vocalist Jesse Lacey, and drummer Daniel Davison.  Further Seems Forever's sound is a mix of indie rock, post-hardcore, and emo. The band's debut album, "Wasting Light", was released in 2002 and charted on the Billboard 200 at number 169. The album was later certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). The band's second album, "The Amulet", was released in 2004 and charted on the Billboard 200 at number 10. The album was later certified platinum by the RIAA. In 2006, the band released their third album, "Where You Want To Be", which charted on the Billboard 200 at number 35. The album was later certified gold by the RIAA. In 2007, the band released their fourth album, "Citizen", which charted on the Billboard 200 at number 24. The album was later certified gold by the RIAA. In 2009, the band released their fifth album, "Goodness". The album charted on the Billboard 200 at number 3. Further Seems Forever has sold over 1 million records worldwide.
