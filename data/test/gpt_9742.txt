The Mason Building is a historic commercial building located at 140 West 57th Street in Midtown Manhattan, New York City.  The building was constructed in 1924 as a speculative office building by the distinguished architectural firm of McKim, Mead and White. The building is a landmark structure in its design and construction, and has been designated a New York City Landmark.  The building is currently used as the headquarters of the J. P. Morgan Chase Manhattan Bank.
