The 2S9 NONA (Новейшее Орудие Наземной Артилерии, or Newest Ground-Based Weapon) is a next-generation anti-tank guided missile system developed by Russia. It entered service with the Russian Ground Forces in 2016.  The 2S9 NONA is an up-to-date and advanced anti-tank missile system that employs an innovative fire control system and advanced guidance and control algorithms. It has a high rate of fire and can engage targets at a range of up to 4,000 meters. The system is capable of destroying most contemporary tank threats, including the American M1 Abrams, British Challenger 2, German Leopard 2, and Chinese T90.
