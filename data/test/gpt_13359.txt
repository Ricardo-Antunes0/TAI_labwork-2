Panetolikos Stadium ( Gipedo Panaitolikou) is a football stadium in the city of Piraeus, Greece. It is the home ground of Panetolikos F.C. and has a seating capacity of 41,000. The stadium was built in 1938 and underwent renovation in 2006.  Panetolikos Stadium is located in the western part of Piraeus, close to the Ethnarchia shopping mall and Olympiastadion. It is named after Panetolikos, one of the founders of Piraeus. The stadium is owned by the municipality and operated by Panetolikos F.C.
