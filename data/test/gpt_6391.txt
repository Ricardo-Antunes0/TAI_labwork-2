Molly Line is an American news correspondent and television personality.  Molly Line was born in Brooklyn, New York, on October 2, 1978. She is the daughter of writer and journalist Ann Line and business executive Donald Line. She has two sisters, Katie and Kyra.  Line began her career as a television reporter for WCBS-TV in New York City, where she worked from 1998 to 2003. In 2003, she joined CBS News as a correspondent for the CBS Evening News with Katie Couric. She left CBS News in 2006 to become a news correspondent for ABC News.  Line has appeared as a guest host on "Good Morning America", "20/20" and "The View". In 2010, she became a co-host of the Fox News Channel morning show "Fox & Friends Weekend". She left Fox News Channel in September 2013 to become a correspondent for NBC News.  Line was married to television producer Michael Davies from 2004 to 2013. They have one child, a son named Ezra.
