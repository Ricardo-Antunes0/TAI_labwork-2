The Baby New Year is a personification of the New Year and is celebrated in some parts of Asia on January 1. The Baby New Year is also known as the Lunar New Year in China, Vietnam, Taiwan, and Thailand. In Cambodia, Laos, and Myanmar it is known as Tết Nguyên Đán (New Year's Day).
