Stormswept is a 1923 silent film starring Rudolph Valentino and Douglas Fairbanks. It is based on the story "The Lady of the Lake" by Arthur Conan Doyle.  In the film, Valentino plays Sir Percy Blakeney, a British soldier who is falsely accused of treason and imprisoned in a French prison. Fairbanks plays his American friend, Andrew Henry, who comes to France to try and rescue him.
