Lorraine Schwartz is an American bespoke high-end fashion designer known for her glamorous and couture-inspired gowns. She has designed for some of the most high-profile figures in the world, including Hillary Clinton and Sarah Ferguson.  Schwartz was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to a Jewish family. After graduating from Temple University with a degree in fashion design, she moved to New York City to start her career. She worked as a designer for several high-end fashion houses before opening her own business in 1998.  Schwartz's designs are known for their luxurious and couture-inspired styles. Her clients have included Hillary Clinton and Sarah Ferguson, both of whom have worn Schwartz designs at various events and ceremonies. In addition to designing clothing, Schwartz also sells her own line of accessories and home furnishings.
