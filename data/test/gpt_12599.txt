Hercules is a 1997 American animated musical action film produced by Walt Disney Feature Animation and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The film was directed by Roger Allers and John Musker and stars Ron Perlman, Rene Auberjonois, Christa Miller, Jonathan Hyde, and Dan Castellaneta.  The film is loosely based on the Greco-Roman legend of Hercules, a demi-god who performs many heroic deeds. It tells the story of how Hercules becomes a slave of the god Zeus, then rebels against him and tries to free his family and friends.  The film was well received by critics, who praised its animation, music, and humor. It was also a commercial success, grossing over $260 million worldwide. A direct-to-video sequel, Hercules & The Lost Kingdom, was released in 2009.
