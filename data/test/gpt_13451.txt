Carole Johnstone is a Scottish short story writer, journalist and broadcaster.  Born in Glasgow, Scotland on 10 January 1947, she attended the University of Glasgow, where she studied English Language and Literature. She then worked as a journalist, firstly for the "Glasgow Herald" and then for "The Scotsman". In 1984, she became a programme presenter with BBC Scotland. She has also been a regular broadcaster on BBC Radio 4, and has written short stories, edited books and been a judge on the annual Scottish Book Awards. She was made an honorary fellow of the University of Glasgow in 2009. Johnstone has received several awards, including an Honorary Doctorate from Heriot-Watt University in 2006.
