Christian de Vietri (born 1981, Kalgoorlie) is an Australian artist. His work has been exhibited at the Whitechapel Gallery, the National Gallery of Victoria, and the Melbourne Museum.  De Vietri's paintings are characterised by their use of vibrant colours and a lively sense of composition. He often paints scenes from everyday life, using a wide range of techniques to create vibrant and colourful works that are both humorous and thought-provoking. de Vietri is also well known for his murals, which often depict scenes from Australian history or popular culture.
