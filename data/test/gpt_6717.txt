Prasophyllum praecox, commonly known as the early morning orchid, is a species of orchid endemic to Victoria. It has a single leaf and up to twelve greenish-white flowers with a labellum that is striped yellow and red.  The early morning orchid is a small, terrestrial orchid that grows in subalpine and alpine areas of Victoria. It has a single, thin leaf and up to twelve greenish-white flowers with a labellum that is striped yellow and red. The flowers are open from morning until lunchtime.
