Aeroport is a station on the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya Line of the Moscow Metro. It was opened on December 30, 1935 as part of the first stage of the Metro, together with six other stations on this line. The station has two exits, one on either side of Kutuzovsky Prospekt, and is located between Teatralnaya and Dzerzhinsky Prospekt.   Aeroport is a large station with a capacity of 6,000 passengers per hour in each direction. It has two side platforms and four tracks. The walls and ceilings are decorated with murals depicting aviation scenes from Russian history.
