Intruder may refer to: Film and television  An intruder is someone who enters a place or property without consent or authorization. This term can be used in a criminal or legal context, as well as in the context of personal safety.  In film and television, an intruder is often used as a plot device to create tension or suspense. For example, in the movie Thelma and Louise, the two female protagonists are pursued by police officers who believe they have committed a crime by running away from them. In this situation, the presence of an unknown intruder provides a powerful source of tension for the viewer.
