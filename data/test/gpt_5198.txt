Moncure, founded in 1881, is a small town in Harnett County, North Carolina. The population was 1,211 at the 2010 census. It is the county seat of Harnett County. As of the census of 2000, there were 1,236 people, 458 households, and 324 families residing in the town. The population density was 1,623.4 people per square mile (611.4/km²). There were 476 housing units at an average density of 715.5 per square mile (270.2/km²).
