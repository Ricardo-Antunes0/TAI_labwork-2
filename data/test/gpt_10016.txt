Honor Courtney Maude (née King; Wem, Shropshire; 11 January 1903 – 8 November 1995) was a British author, journalist and broadcaster.  Maude was born in Wem, Shropshire, the daughter of the Reverend Canon James King and his wife Ada (née Courtney). She was educated at Cheltenham Ladies' College and then at the University of London, where she obtained an honours degree in English literature.   She married the broadcaster R. D. Laing in 1947. They had two daughters. She died in 1995, aged 91.
