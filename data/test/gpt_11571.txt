Mervyn Conn (born 5 February 1935) is a British actor, best known for his roles in television dramas such as Z-Cars and Midsomer Murders. He has a younger brother, Julian. Conn was educated at summer boarding school in Wales before attending the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London.  Conn made his acting debut in the 1957 BBC production of "The House by the Sea", followed by appearances in television series such as Z-Cars and Midsomer Murders. He achieved international recognition for his performance as Detective Chief Inspector John Barnaby in the long-running British police drama series Midsomer Murders, which ran from 1992 to 2004. Conn also appeared in the BBC drama series Wallander, which aired from 2003 to 2013. In October 2013, he played King George III in the West End production of Simon Stephens' play The Audience.
