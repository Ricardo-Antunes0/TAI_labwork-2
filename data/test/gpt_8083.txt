Ricky Lee Glenn (born April 12, 1989) is an American professional baseball pitcher who is currently a free agent. He previously played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Cleveland Indians and Atlanta Braves.  Glenn attended the University of Arizona where he played college baseball for the Arizona Wildcats. He was drafted by the Cleveland Indians in the first round of the 2009 MLB Draft. Glenn made his MLB debut in 2011 with the Indians and finished his career with the Braves in 2017.  Glenn has received recognition for his pitching abilities, earning a number of awards including a Silver Slugger Award and a Gold Glove Award. In 2018, Glenn signed with the KBO's Nexen Heroes.
