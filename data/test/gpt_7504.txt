Brand Factory is a chain of retail stores in the United States that specializes in selling branded merchandise. The company was founded in 2009 by CEO and President, Trevor Blackwell.  Brand Factory operates stores in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. The company's stores sell branded merchandise, including clothing, accessories, home goods, and food items. The company's products are sold through a variety of channels, including physical stores, online retailers, and mobile apps.  Brand Factory is headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The company has a total staff of 120 people. The company has raised $8 million in funding from investors include Sequoia Capital and Accel Partners.
