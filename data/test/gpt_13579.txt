Kirill Reznik (born August 9, 1974) is a Russian journalist and author.  Kirill Reznik was born in Leningrad, USSR, now Saint Petersburg, Russia, on August 9, 1974. He graduated from the Saint Petersburg State University of Information Technologies in 1996 with a degree in journalism. Reznik has worked as a correspondent for various Russian newspapers and magazines, as well as for the Russian news agency RIA Novosti. His first book, "Moscow's War: The Retreat of Communism and the Battle for Global Power", was published in 2003. Reznik lives in Moscow with his wife and two young children.
