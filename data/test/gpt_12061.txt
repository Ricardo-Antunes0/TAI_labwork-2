Walter Woon started his footballing career with Young Lions before moving to S-League side Geylang International in 2006. In 2009, Walter Woon transferred to Malaysian Premier League side Johor Darul Ta'zim where he has since played over 100 games and won three consecutive Malaysia Premier League titles. He also won the Malaysian FA Cup with JDT in 2013 and 2017.  Walter Woon was called up to the Singapore national team in early 2013 for the 2013 AFF Suzuki Cup.
