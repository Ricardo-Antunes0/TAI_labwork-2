Nightschool: The Weirn Books is a supernatural comic book series written and illustrated by Tomm Coker. The series follows the adventures of a group of teenagers who attend a mysterious night school that teaches them magical skills in order to defend against supernatural threats.  The series has been praised for its unique take on the supernatural genre, its well-developed characters, and its5 artstyle. It has been nominated for several awards, including the Eisner Awards, and was named one of the Best Comics of 2012 by The Escapist.
