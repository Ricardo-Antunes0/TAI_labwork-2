Described as "a magazine of new writing, photography and art", Tsotso is a self-published literary and visual magazine based in Cape Town, South Africa.  Tsotso was founded in 2013 by Cape Town-based writer and artist, Sam Miller. The magazine is distributed free of charge at events and venues around Cape Town and is also available online.   Tsotso's stated aim is to "promote the work of emerging writers, photographers and artists from South Africa and around the world" by publishing new writing, photography and art. In addition to its own work, Tsotso has featured writing and photography from notable international authors and photographers, including J.M. Coetzee, Nadine Gordimer, Anne-Marie Schubert, Rian Hughes, Stefan Sagmeister, Aaron Jay Kernis and Peder Zetterberg.
