Andrade is a surname of Galician origin, meaning "of Andrade". Notable people with the surname include:  1. Miguel Andrade (born 1936), a Chilean poet, playwright, and novelist who won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1982 2. Jonas Andrade (born 1978), an American artist who works in video and performance art 3. Andre Fili (born 1966), an American mixed martial artist and former WBA, IBF, and WBO world featherweight champion
