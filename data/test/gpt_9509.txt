Hastings was born in 1839 to Vice-Admiral George Fowler Hastings and his wife, Charlotte. He joined the Royal Navy in 1854 and served in the Crimean War. He became a captain in 1875 and commanded the HMS "Britannia" in the Second Anglo-Afghan War. He became Vice-Admiral of the White Nile Squadron in 1892 and retired in 1896. Hastings died in 1913.
