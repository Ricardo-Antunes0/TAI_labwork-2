Fevik Church  is a chapel of the Church of Norway in Fevik, Nordland. It is located on the north side of the village, and has been in use since the 14th century. The present building was completed in 1892.   The church is built of stone and has a gable roof. The interior is decorated with frescos dating from the 16th century. The altar is from 1892 and the pulpit from 1910.   The church was closed for renovation in late 2009 and reopened on 1 October 2010.
