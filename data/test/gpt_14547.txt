Wang Wenhua (Simplified Chinese: 王文华) (born 17 July 1988) is a Chinese football player who currently plays for Beijing Guoan in the Chinese Super League.  Wang Wenhua started his professional football career with Beijing Guoan in 2007. He then transferred to Shanghai Shenhua in 2010. Wang Wenhua was loaned to Liaoning Whowin in 2013 and returned to Beijing Guoan in 2014. He was loaned to Tianjin Teda in 2015 and transferred back to Beijing Guoan in 2016. Wang Wenhua signed with Tianjin Quanjian in 2017.  Wang Wenhua made his international debut for China on 6 September 2009 against South Korea. He has since made a total of 22 appearances for China, scoring 2 goals.
