Joseph Lo Bianco AM (born 7 July 1954) is an Australian actor and musician. He has appeared in numerous television and film roles, including the role of Detective Steve Crozier in the Australian police drama series Blue Heelers.  Lo Bianco was born in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. After completing a degree in music at the Melbourne Conservatorium of Music, Lo Bianco began his acting career in the early 1980s, appearing in television roles including the role of Tony Mafioso in the Australian soap opera Neighbours. He went on to appear in film roles including The Adventures of Priscilla, Queen of the Desert (1994), The Great Gatsby (2013), and Don't Tell Mom the Perks of Being a Wallflower (2014).
