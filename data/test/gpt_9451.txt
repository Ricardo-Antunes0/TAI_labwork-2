Looking 4 Myself is the seventh studio album by American rapper Lil Wayne. It was released on September 25, 2009, by Cash Money Records. The album produced five singles: "Lollipop", "A Milli", "Money In The Grave", "Lighters Up" and "Schizophrenic".  The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 with first-week sales of 1,081,000 copies. It has since sold over two million copies in the United States. The album also reached the top five in countries such as Australia, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway and the United Kingdom.  The album received mixed reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album received an average score of 54, based on 11 reviews.   The album's lead single "Lollipop" was a commercial success peaking atop the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and being certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). The second single "A Milli" also charted within the top 20 of the Hot 100 chart and attained double platinum certification from the RIAA. The third single "Money In The Grave" peaked at number six on the Hot 100 chart and was certified gold by RIAA.
