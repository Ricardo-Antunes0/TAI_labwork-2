Drifters is a British sitcom created by Simon Nye and produced by Kudos Film and Television for BBC Two. The series follows the lives of a group of drifters, or people who have left structured employment and settled down anywhere from the street to a caravan park, as they try to get by.  The show was announced in February 2016, with Filming starting in July of that year and airing throughout 2017. The first series consisted of six episodes.  The show has been well received by critics, with many praising its humor and writing. It has also been nominated for a BAFTA Television Award for Best Comedy Series.
