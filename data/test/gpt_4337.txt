Stroud District Council is the local authority for the town of Stroud in Gloucestershire, England. The council was created in 2009 by the merger of the former Stroud District and the former Cotswold District councils. It is a metropolitan district, with a population of about 108,000.  The council is divided into 11 wards and has 60 members. The current Leader of the Council is Councillor Chris Carter.
