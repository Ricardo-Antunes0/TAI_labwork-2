Caractères (French for characters) is a name used for a typeface family designed by Adrian Frutiger in the 1960s. It was originally released as a typeface for the French newspaper Le Monde, and is now available in many different formats.  Caractères is a modern typeface that has a distinctive, geometric design. It is suitable for a variety of applications, including text, logos, and web graphics. The font is available in multiple weights and styles, and can be used on both Mac and Windows systems.
