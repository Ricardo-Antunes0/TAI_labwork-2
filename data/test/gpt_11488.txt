Kamen Rider Super-1 is the first Kamen Rider series, preceded by Kamen Rider Black and Kamen Rider Green. The show follows the story of a young man named Shiro Amada who, after being struck by a mysterious light while on a surfing trip, gains the power to transform into Kamen Rider Super-1, a superhero who fights against evil.  The show was popular in its time, and has since been considered a classic of Japanese tokusatsu superhero television. It has been followed by several sequels and spin-offs, including Kamen Rider Agito and Kamen Rider Decade.
