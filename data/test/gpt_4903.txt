He was killed in action during the Battle of the Bulge at Bastogne, Belgium, on April 22, 1945.  Gurke was posthumously awarded the Medal of Honor for "conspicuous gallantry and intrepidity while serving as a rifleman in Company A of the 26th Infantry Regiment" during the Battle of the Bulge. He was also awarded three other medals for his service in World War II: The Purple Heart Medal, The Bronze Star Medal with "V" for Valor, and The European-African-Middle Eastern Campaign Medal with two Bronze Stars.
