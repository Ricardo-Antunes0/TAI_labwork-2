Stonewall is a 2015 American coming-of-age drama film directed by Roland Emmerich and written by Dean Israelite. It is based on the 1969 Stonewall Riots, which were a series of protests conducted by gay men in New York City's Greenwich Village against discrimination and police harassment.   The film stars Jeremy Irvine, Jonny Beauchamp, and Nick Zano, and tells the story of a young man who becomes involved in the Stonewall Riots.
