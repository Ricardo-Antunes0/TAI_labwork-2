A member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church), Taylor served as the president of the church from 1875 until his death.  Taylor was born in Taylorsville, New York, on 1 November 1808. He married Maria Lawrence in 1830 and they had six children. In 1844, Taylor and his family moved to Nauvoo, Illinois, where he served as a member of the Nauvoo City Council and as a member of the First Presidency under Joseph Smith. Taylor played a significant role in leading the church after Smith's death and during the Utah War. He died in Ogden, Utah, on 25 October 1887.
