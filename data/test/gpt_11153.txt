Orica House (formerly ICI House) is a hotel located in central London, England, on the corner of Argyle Street and Russell Square. It is close to the British Museum, Regent's Park and Oxford Street.  Orica House is a five-star hotel, with 189 rooms and suites. The hotel has an extensive spa and fitness centre, a wine lounge, a restaurant and bar, meeting rooms, a business centre and concierge service. The hotel also has a library and a fitness centre.   The hotel was opened in 1998 as part of the ICI Group. In 2007 it was sold to Orica Hotels Limited.
