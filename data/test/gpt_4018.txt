Leslie Fish is a folk musician, author, and recording artist from the United States. Born in Boston, Massachusetts, Fish began playing guitar at the age of nine and released her first album, "Leslie Fish", in 1984.   Fish's music is often categorized as folk, but she has also been associated with country, folk rock, and Americana. Her music has been featured on television shows, including "The West Wing", "Grey's Anatomy", and "Bones". Fish has also released several albums of children's music.  In 2002, Fish was named one of the Living Legends of Folk by the American Folklife Center. She has received awards from the National Endowment for the Arts and the Boston Music Awards.
