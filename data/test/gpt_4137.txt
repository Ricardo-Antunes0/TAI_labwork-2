Black Robot is a California-based hard rock band formed in early 2006. The band consists of lead singer and songwriter, John Bowers, guitarist and backing vocalist, James "Jimmy" Kerr, bassist and backing vocalist, Andy "Andy" Doster, and drummer and percussionist, Cameron "Cam" Fenton.  Black Robot's sound is a mix of hard rock and heavy metal with elements of blues rock and funk. The band has released one EP, one full-length album, and one single.  Black Robot is currently unsigned.
