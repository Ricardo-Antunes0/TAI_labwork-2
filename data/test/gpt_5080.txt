The National is a Welsh daily news newspaper. It is the flagship title of the National Newspaper Group, which is owned by the National Union of Journalists.  The National was founded in 1877 as "Y Ddraig Goch" (The red dragon). It was relaunched in 1988 as a tabloid newspaper and became a morning newspaper in 1994. In 2003, it became a broadsheet newspaper.  The National has been awarded two Paul Foot awards for journalism.
