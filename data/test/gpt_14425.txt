Misa Amane is a young woman who has the power to kill anyone with a single written note. She first meets Light Yagami when he comes to Japan in order to track down the person who killed his father. Misa helps Light find the Death Note, and they soon fall in love. However, Misa is also a criminal mastermind, and she uses the Death Note to commit murders. The movie ends with Light defeating Misa and taking back the Death Note.
