After graduating from high school in Tokyo, he studied at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City. Kunizawa made his acting debut in the Japanese TV series "Rurouni Kenshin" (1998). He subsequently starred in the films "Spirited Away" (2001), "The Cat Returns" (2002), "My Neighbor Totoro" (1988), "The Place Beyond the Pines" (2013), and "The Great Passage" (2017). Kunizawa has also sung alongside Norah Jones, Chilly Gonzales, John Legend, and Norah Jones on the soundtrack of "The Cat Returns".
