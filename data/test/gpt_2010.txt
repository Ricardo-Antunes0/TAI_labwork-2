Byron Wallen (born July 1969) is a Canadian artist who creates large-scale public artworks.  Wallen was born in 1969 in Toronto, Canada. He received his BFA from Ryerson University in 1991, and his MFA from the University of California, San Diego in 1996. Wallen has exhibited his work internationally, including at the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Los Angeles County Museum of Art, and the Museum of Modern Art in New York. He has been awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship and a DAAD Fellowship from the German government. His works are in the collections of the Tate Gallery, London; the Museum of Modern Art, New York; and the National Gallery of Canada, Ottawa.
