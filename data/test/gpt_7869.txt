Bravely Default II is a role-playing video game developed by Silicon Studio and published by Square Enix for the Nintendo 3DS. It is the sequel to Bravely Default, which was released in Japan in December 2010 and in North America and Europe in February 2011.  The game's story takes place several years after the events of Bravely Default and follows the return of the crystal-powered villains, called "The Four Great Calamity". To stop them, the player must travel to different worlds and defeat them using a variety of characters from the first game.  Bravely Default II was well-received by critics, who praised its gameplay, graphics, and storytelling. The game shipped more than 1 million copies in Japan and became a commercial success. A remastered version of the game was released for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in 2018.
