Nordhordland District Court  was a district court located in Bodø, Norway. It was disbanded on 1 January 2009.  The district court was established on 1 January 2009 when the old Bodø Court of Appeal was split into two separate courts, the Eastern and Western District Courts. The Nordhordland District Court had jurisdiction over the districts of Nordhordland, Sogn og Fjordane and Møre og Romsdal.
