Peter Bordier Høj  (born 29 April 1966) is a Danish businessperson and the co-founder and CEO of If.com. He is also a non-executive director of A.P. Moller-Maersk A/S.  Høj was born in 1966 in Aarhus, Denmark to Jens Høj and Inger Bordier. He has a degree in business administration from Copenhagen Business School.  Høj started his career at McKinsey & Co., where he worked on projects such as the development of the Danish airline SAS' reservations system and the restructuring of Danish telecom company TeliaSonera. He then co-founded If.com, an online marketplace for buying and selling used items, with Søren Skou. If.com was acquired by eBay in 2005 for US$266 million. Høj is also a non-executive director of A.P. Moller-Maersk A/S.
