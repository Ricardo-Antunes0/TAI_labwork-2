Scene It? is a popular series in which users are asked to identify movie scenes. The first series, released in 2001, was a spin-off of the popular board game Scrabble. Scenes are presented in a format similar to that of a game show, with questions followed by clues that allow users to determine the scene. There are currently six series in the franchise: "Scene It?", "Scene It! Presents", "Movie Moments", "Ultimate Scene It?", and " Scene It? XL". Each series has its own set of hosts and features different types of questions.
