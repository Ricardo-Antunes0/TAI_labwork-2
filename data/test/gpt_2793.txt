The First Stone: Some questions about sex and violence  The First Stone is a 2002 Australian-American psychological thriller film written and directed by Peter Jackson, based on the novel of the same name by Robert Galbraith. The film stars Nicole Kidman, Colin Farrell, Jude Law and Naomi Watts.  The film revolves around a retired detective and his new partner investigating the brutal murder of a young woman. As they delve deeper into the case, they uncover disturbing connections to a previous case involving a young boy. The detective soon finds that he is not the only one with suspicions about what really happened.
