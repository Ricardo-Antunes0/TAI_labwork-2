City Bureau is an American  non-profit organization that helps cities improve their public policies and services.  City Bureau was founded in 1975 by John D. Rockefeller III and his wife Christy. The organization's stated mission is to help cities improve their public policies and services. City Bureau provides research and advice to city officials, conducts public education programs, and provides technical assistance to city governments.   City Bureau has offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Philadelphia, San Francisco, and Washington, D.C.
