Tafsir Meshkat is an exegesis on Qur'an written by Imam al-Tabari. The book consists of 27 volumes and covers the Qur'an from beginning to end.  Tafsir Meshkat is one of the most renowned and respected tafsir texts in Islamic scholarship. It is widely used by ulema, students of Islamic jurisprudence, and Sunni Muslims in general. The text covers a vast range of Qur'anic topics, including exegesis, grammar, syntax, literature, history, theology, and jurisprudence.  Imam al-Tabari was a highly respected Islamic scholar and historian who lived in the 10th century. He is best known for his monumental work Tafsir al-Qur'an (Exegesis of the Qur'an), which covers the Qur'an from beginning to end. Tafsir Meshkat is based on Tafsir al-Qur'an and covers the same range of topics.
