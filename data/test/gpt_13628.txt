released outside Japan as Shin Megami Tensei: Persona 4  Persona 4 is an action role-playing video game developed and published by Atlus for the PlayStation 2. It was released in Japan on September 15, 2004, in North America on April 16, 2006, and in Europe on May 5, 2006. A remake for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 was released in Japan on November 18, 2009, in North America and Europe on February 14, 2010, and in Australia on March 30, 2010. A port for the PlayStation Vita was released in Japan on December 6, 2011, in North America and Europe on May 24, 2012, and in Australia on June 7, 2012.   The game's story follows high school student Yu Narukami who moves to Inaba with his friends after his parents are killed. There he meets a talking cat named Koromaru and meets a female demon who offers to help him if he can defeat her former masters. After defeating them Yu returns to Inaba to find that most of his friends have gone missing. He learns that they were summoned to the moon by a group of people known as the Phantom Thieves of Hearts. Yu joins them and together they attempt to stop the group from taking over Inaba and stealing the hearts of people throughout the world.
