Downtown Saint Paul is an official neighborhood located in Saint Paul, Minnesota, United States. It is bounded by the Mississippi River on the west, Interstate 35W on the north, Interstate 94 on the east, and Payne Avenue on the south. The boundaries are approximate as they are not governed by any official plan or legislation. Downtown Saint Paul is a commercial and cultural center for Saint Paul. Its many notable landmarks include the Cathedral of Saint Paul, The Minnesota State Capitol, and the Xcel Energy Center.
