1001 Woodward is an office building in the Midtown Detroit district of Detroit, Michigan, United States. Completed in 1984, it is a landmark skyscraper and the headquarters of Caesars Entertainment Corporation. At 1,050 feet (320 m), it is the fifth tallest building in the city.  1001 Woodward was designed by the architectural firm of Skidmore, Owings and Merrill and built at a cost of $123 million. The building was named for Michigan Avenue (1001 Woodward Avenue) in Detroit which was renamed after General Ulysses S. Grant in 1879.
