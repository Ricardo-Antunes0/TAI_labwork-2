The Harvey Cedars Hotel is the last remaining hotel in the state of Oregon built in the 1920s. The hotel is located on the Oregon Coast, in the town of Florence.  The Harvey Cedars Hotel is a historic structure that is listed on the National Register of Historic Places. It was designed by architectural firm of Cannon and Ballou and built in 1927.  The Harvey Cedars Hotel is a five-story structure with a basement. It features prominently placed arches and columns, a wraparound porch, and period features such as wood trim and tile floors. The hotel is now operated as a bed and breakfast.
