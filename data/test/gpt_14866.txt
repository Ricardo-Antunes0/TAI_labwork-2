The Pacific Sisters are a collective of Pacific Island women activists who have come together to build a better future for their communities. We work together to create positive change in our home islands, and to advocate for the rights of Pacific Islanders everywhere.  Our work is inspired by ourPacific values of resilience, community, and respect for nature. We believe that through solidarity and collective action, we can build a better tomorrow for all our people.
