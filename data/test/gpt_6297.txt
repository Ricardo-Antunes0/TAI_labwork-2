Emmanuel "Manny" Mijares is a multi-platinum songwriter, record producer, and musician who has written and produced songs for artists such as Jennifer Lopez, Sean Paul, Shakira, and Enrique Iglesias.   Mijares was born in the Dominican Republic and raised in the Bronx, New York. He started writing songs at a young age, and his first success came as a songwriter for Jennifer Lopez. He has since written and produced songs for a wide range of artists, including Sean Paul, Shakira, Enrique Iglesias, and Ricky Martin. Mijares has won three Grammy Awards and has sold over 100 million records worldwide.
