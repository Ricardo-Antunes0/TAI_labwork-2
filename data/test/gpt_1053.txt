The 2018 Texas Bowl was a college football bowl game played on December 28, 2018, at the NRG Stadium in Houston, Texas. The game was the first edition of the Texas Bowl and featured the Texas Longhorns of the University of Texas against the Memphis Tigers of the University of Memphis.  The game was originally scheduled to be played on January 2, 2019, but was moved to December 28 due to a scheduling conflict with the College Football Playoff National Championship.  The Longhorns were ranked No. 23 in the Coaches Poll and No. 24 in the AP Poll going into the game, while the Tigers were unranked in both polls.
