Santiago Salazar a.k.a. DJ S² is a Spanish musician, DJ, and producer. He is known for his work as a DJ and as the lead singer of the band El Gran Silencio.  Born in Alicante, Valencia, Salazar started his musical career as a DJ at local nightclubs in his teenage years. He then formed the band El Gran Silencio with fellow DJs and musicians, and released their debut album in 2007. The album peaked at number 8 on the Spanish Albums Chart. In 2010, he released the album Santiago Salazar & El Gran Silencio, which charted at number 20 on the Spanish Albums Chart. He has also released singles including "Por Ti", "Fuego", and "Día de los Muertos".
