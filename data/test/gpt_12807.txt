Malaluba Gumana (born 1953) is an Australian photographer and artist.   Malaluba Gumana was born in 1953 in Perth, Western Australia. She studied at the Western Australian Academy of Art from 1973 to 1976, after which she travelled and lived in Asia for four years. Gumana has exhibited her photography throughout Australia, Asia and Europe. Her work is generally concerned with natural environments, with a focus on the beauty and mystery of the natural world. Gumana has been awarded several prizes for her photography, including the Archibald Prize in 2001.
