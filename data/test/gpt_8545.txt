X-UAV UAVs are china UAVs developed by the Chinese military.  The Chinese military has been developing X-UAVs for over a decade. The first generation of X-UAVs were based on the WS-10 unmanned aerial vehicle platform. Later models were based on the WS-15 platform.  As of 2016, the Chinese military was fielding a variety of X-UAV types, including the Lijiang-5, the Wing Loong III, and the Z-10. These aircraft are used for surveillance, target acquisition, and battlefield management applications.
