"Chain Mail" is a song by Mancunian rock band The Smiths. It was released as the second single from their third studio album, The Queen Is Dead.  The song was written by Morrissey and was produced by The Smiths. It is a acoustic ballad with a mournful melody. The lyrics concern Morrissey's disillusionment with the British punk rock scene and his feeling that he had been left behind by his peers.  The song peaked at number 47 on the UK Singles Chart and number 10 on the Irish Singles Chart.
