Indian 2 is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by R. Sarath Kumar. The film stars Suriya and Anushka Shetty in the lead roles, with Prabhu Deva, Vadivelu and Jaffna Boban in supporting roles. Principal photography began on 10 January 2019 in Chennai.  The film is produced by Sarath Kumar under the production banner of Sarath Kumar Filmworks and features music composed by Harris Jayaraj.
