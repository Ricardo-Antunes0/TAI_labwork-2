Viki Shock (born 8 April 1975, in Montreal, Quebec) is a Canadian actress, voice actress, and singer.  Shock began her career as an actress in the Quebec television series "Le Cercle". She then moved to Los Angeles and starred in the television series "Party of Five". Her feature film debut was in the comedy film "Office Space". After "Office Space", Shock appeared in the horror films "The Hills Have Eyes II" and "The Texas Chainsaw Massacre III". In 2002, she starred in the science fiction movie "Outland".   Since 2003, Shock has starred as Viki Van Houten on the American television series "Beverly Hills, 90210". The show ended its ten-year run on 25 May 2010. Shock has also released two albums, both of which were certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. Shock has also lent her voice to various animated projects, including "The Simpsons" and "Futurama".
