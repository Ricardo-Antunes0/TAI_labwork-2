Barrie Leslie Konicov (c. 1939) is a Canadian artist who has been characterized as a minimalist painter. He is known for his paintings of landscapes and cityscapes, often using a limited palette of colors.  Konicov was born in 1939 in Toronto, Canada. After graduating from high school, he studied at the Ontario College of Art inToronto, where he received his BFA in 1961. He then moved to New York City, where he continued his studies at the School of Visual Arts. In 1965, he returned to Toronto and began teaching at the Ontario College of Art and Design until his retirement in 2006.  Konicov's paintings are often characterized as minimalist paintings.
