Ceyhun Eriş (born 15 May 1977) is a Turkish actor.  Ceyhun Eriş was born in Istanbul on 15 May 1977. He completed his high school education at the Istanbul Technical University in 1995 and then started his acting career.  Ceyhun Eriş has appeared in a number of television series and films since 2000. He is best known for his roles in the television series "Umutların Kitabı" (2005), "Aşk Kadınları" (2007), and "Ölüm Sevgisi" (2008).
