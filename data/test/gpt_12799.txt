The Illegitimates is a six-issue, 2013 American comic book written and illustrated by Rick Remender. It is a sequel to the 2009 miniseries, Deadly Class.  The Illegitimates is set in a world where a class of children have been born with extraordinary abilities. These "illegitimates" are rejected by society, and must hide their powers in order to survive. The series follows the lives of two such teenagers, who find themselves on the run from a powerful government organization known as The Program.
