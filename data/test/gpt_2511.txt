Abetxuko (in Basque and officially, according to the Basque government, Etxako) is a municipality located in the province of Guipúzcoa, in the autonomous community of Basque Country, northern Spain. It has a population of 1,641 (INE 2006). The municipality is crossed by the river Urumea.  Abetxuko is located in the province of Guipúzcoa, in the autonomous community of Basque Country, northern Spain. The municipality covers an area of and has a population of 1,641 (INE 2006). The main economic activity is agriculture.  The municipality is crossed by the river Urumea. The main tourist attraction is the monastery of San Isidoro de Abetxuko. There are also many natural parks such as Iruña-Ayerba de Baja and Iruña-Ayerba de Alta.
