Shared Interest Society Limited was founded in 2009 by two friends, Adam and Rachel, who wanted to create a way for people to share interests and make new friends. Shared Interest Society allows members to post interests and connect with others who share similar interests. Members can also join groups focused on specific topics or hobbies, and use the forum section to share ideas and advice.
