Elizabeth Mary Rata (born 1952) is a New Zealand artist.  Rata was born in 1952 in Wellington, New Zealand. She completed a Bachelor of Fine Arts at the University of Auckland in 1977 and a Master of Fine Arts at the University of Waikato in 1982. Rata has exhibited extensively throughout New Zealand and internationally, including at the Venice Biennale in 1984 and the Sydney Biennial in 1988. Her work is held in public and private collections including the Auckland War Memorial Museum, the Govett-Brewster Art Gallery, and the National Gallery of New Zealand.
