Electrotherm (India) Limited was founded in 1983, with a focus on the manufacturing of advanced engineering equipment for the thermal power industry. The company has since diversified into the production of industrial gases and other related products.   Electrotherm is a leading engineering manufacturer in India, specialising in the production of advanced engineering equipment for the thermal power industry. The company has a diversified product range, encompassing industrial gases and other related products. Electrotherm is headquartered in Mumbai, India, and has manufacturing facilities in India and China.
