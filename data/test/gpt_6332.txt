Tenement is an American three piece rock band from Minneapolis, Minnesota. The band was formed in 2011 by vocalist/guitarist Jesse Brown, drummer Max MacDowell, and bassist Devin O'Keefe.   Tenement's sound is based around Brown's powerful and emotive vocals and MacDowell's powerful drumming. The band's music is often described as heavy and bluesy, with influences from alternative rock, post-punk, and grunge.
