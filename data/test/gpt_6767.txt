Repercussions were an American R&B, soul and funk band from the early 1970s. Formed in the Detroit area in 1971, the group achieved national success in the late 1970s and early 1980s with hits such as "Let Me Touch You", "I'm Gonna Tell My Kids", and "Ain't No Stopping Us Now". The group disbanded in 1984.  Repercussions were one of the most popular soul and funk bands of their era, scoring several hits on the US pop charts including "Let Me Touch You" (1976), "I'm Gonna Tell My Kids" (1977), and "Ain't No Stopping Us Now" (1979). The group disbanded in 1984, although they reunited for a tour in 1992.
