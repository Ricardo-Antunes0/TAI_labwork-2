Maia Luisa Brewton (born September 30, 1977) is an American actress and model. She is known for her roles as Pam on the Fox sitcom New Girl and as Remy on the NBC sitcom Parks and Recreation.  Born in West Hollywood, California, Brewton is of English, Irish, and Scottish descent. She was raised in a Catholic family. She has two sisters, one of whom is actress Eliza Dushku, with whom she appeared in the 1995 film The Relic. Brewton attended Beverly Hills High School, where she played soccer and was captain of the volleyball team.   Brewton began her modeling career at the age of 16 when she was chosen to be the face of Guess clothing's "Uniforms for All" campaign. She has since modeled for such brands as H&M, Nike, Calvin Klein, and Marc by Marc Jacobs. In 2006, she starred as Nia in the film Soul Food Junkies opposite Tika Sumpter and Kerry Washington.   Brewton made her television debut in 2001 as Pam on the Fox sitcom New Girl. The series ran for six seasons from 2011 to 2017. In 2016, Brewton starred as Remy on the NBC sitcom Parks and Recreation. The series ran for seven seasons from 2015 to 2020.
