Rachael Killian (born 15 July 1994) is an Irish singer and songwriter. She is the winner of the third series of The X Factor in Ireland, in 2013. Her debut album, entitled "Rachael", was released in Ireland on 12 October 2013 and in the UK on 17 October 2013.  Killian was born in Dublin, Ireland. At the age of six, she started taking singing lessons with Donal Logue. Killian auditioned for The X Factor in 2012, aged 16, and made it to the live shows before being eliminated in week four. She returned to the show as a guest judge for the semi-final and final stages, before winning the third series on 17 November 2013 with her song "I Believe in a Thing Called Love". Killian's debut album "Rachael" was released on 12 October 2013, preceded by the singles "I Believe in a Thing Called Love" and "The Way You Look Tonight".
