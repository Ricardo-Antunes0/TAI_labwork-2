Arif Yunusov, also known as Arif Yunusov the Second (born 27 October 1983) is a Kazakhstani professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Astana of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Yunusov played youth hockey in his native Kazakhstan before moving to North America to play NCAA Division I hockey with the University of Vermont. After two seasons with the Catamounts, he signed with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) in 2006. He helped Sochi win the KHL championship in 2007 and was named to the KHL All-Star team that season.  In 2008, Yunusov signed with HC CSKA Moscow of the NHL.
