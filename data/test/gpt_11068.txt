The Hotel Suisse is a heritage hotel located in the heart of the city of Geneva. The hotel is a member of the Leading Hotels of the World and offers guests a unique accommodation experience.  The Hotel Suisse was founded in 1885 and is one of the oldest hotels in Geneva. The hotel has been refurbished several times over the years, most recently in 2007. Today, the hotel features 260 rooms, including 34 suites, a spa, a conference center and a restaurant.  The Hotel Suisse is located in the heart of Geneva, just steps from the city's famous shopping districts and various attractions. The hotel also offers easy access to all of Geneva's major train stations.
