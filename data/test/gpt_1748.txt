Erica Schroeder (born April 27, 1975), is an American actress and model. Schroeder was born in Bellevue, Washington and raised in Seattle. Schroeder has appeared in films such as "The Fast and the Furious" (2001), "Con Air" (1997), "The Mask" (1994), "A Walk to Remember" (2002), "Armageddon" (1998), and "The Cider House Rules" (1999). Schroeder has also appeared on television series such as "ER", " NYPD Blue", and "Desperate Housewives".
