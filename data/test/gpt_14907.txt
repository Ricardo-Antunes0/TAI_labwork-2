Extremeroller was a steel stand-up roller coaster at Six Flags Great America in Gurnee, Illinois. The ride opened on May 3, 1983 and operated until February 1, 2008.  The coaster was originally built by Arrow Development and manufactured by Premier Rides. The ride was the first stand-up roller coaster ever built at Six Flags Great America. It was also the first steel roller coaster in the park and the first stand-up roller coaster in the Midwest.
