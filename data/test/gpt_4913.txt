The "Dark Horse" Tour was a worldwide concert tour by American rock band Guns N' Roses. The tour began on July 1, 2008 in Hartford, Connecticut, and ended on November 17, 2008 in Manila, Philippines.  The tour was announced in February 2008 and originally consisted of six legs that would travel throughout North America. The European leg followed in May and June 2008. The Australian leg followed in August and September 2008. The Japanese leg followed in October and November 2008. The South American leg followed in January 2009. And finally, the Chinese leg followed in March 2009.  The tour was met with mixed reviews from music critics. However, the concerts themselves were met with generally positive feedback from fans who were eager to see the reunited Guns N' Roses perform their classic material live.
