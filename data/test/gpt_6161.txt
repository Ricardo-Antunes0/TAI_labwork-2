Another Round is a culture podcast co-hosted by comedian Hari Kondabolu and journalist Chandinihar Das. It covers a range of topics including politics, current events, religion, race, sex, and pop culture.  The show was originally conceived as a monthly podcast, but has since expanded to a weekly format. It is produced by Howl Media and released on Thursdays.  Kondabolu and Das first met while working on the TV show "The Problem with Apu" which aired on HBO. Das was the showrunner and Kondabolu was one of the writers. The two bonded over their shared interest in talking about complex issues in an accessible way.  "Another Round" is also known for its recurring segments including "Guess Who's Coming to Dinner?", "A Day in the Life", "Race Matters", and "Gender Bender".
