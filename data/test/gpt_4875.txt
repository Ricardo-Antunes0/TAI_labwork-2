Joanne Giannini (born March 15, 1953) is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Dr. Lisa Cuddy on the television series House and as Ms. Kettle on the television series Bob's Burgers.  Giannini was born in Buffalo, New York, to Italian-American parents. She began her career as a child actor, appearing in television shows such as The Donna Reed Show and The New Dick Van Dyke Show. Giannini made her Broadway debut in the 1971 production of Bye Bye Birdie. She later enjoyed critical and popular success in films such as The Godfather Part II, The English Patient, and The Talented Mr. Ripley. Giannini has also released several albums and recorded several songs, most notably the ballad "A Thousand Miles" which she performed for the House episode "Cuddy State".  Giannini currently resides in California.
