Wolfgang Liebe was born on 22 June 1911 in Bad Kissingen. He studied art history at the University of Freiburg, where he received his doctorate in 1933. He then worked as an art historian at the Staatliche Kunsthalle Karlsruhe from 1934 to 1937, and at the Kunsthistorisches Museum Vienna from 1937 to 1944. From 1944 to 1949 he was a professor at the University of Frankfurt am Main, where he remained until his retirement in 1978. Liebe died on 25 October 2000 in Frankfurt am Main.
