Ben Knight (born 1 December 1975) is an English actor. He is known for his roles in the BBC soap opera EastEnders, in which he played Phil Mitchell from 2003 to 2006, and in the ITV mystery drama Midsomer Murders, in which he played Detective Chief Inspector Tom Barnaby from 2007 to 2011. He has also starred in the BBC crime drama Cuckoo, and in the Channel 4 comedy-drama series Peep Show.
