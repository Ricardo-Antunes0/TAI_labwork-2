Laura Fabris is an Associate Professor for the Practice of Visual Arts at Yale University. She teaches photography, film, and new media. Fabris' work focuses on the areas of documentary photography and visual anthropology and explores the ways in which technology, globalization, and cultural dynamics are shaping contemporary visual cultures. Fabris has been awarded fellowships from the Guggenheim Foundation, the National Endowment for the Arts, and the John Simon Guggenheim Memorial Foundation.
