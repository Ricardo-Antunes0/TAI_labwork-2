Pacific Strike is a World War II aerial combat game for Microsoft Windows developed by Degica and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was announced at E3 2019 and is currently in development by Degica.  The game is a twin stick shooter where players take control of aircraft from the United States, Japan, and China in a series of aerial dogfights across iconic locations from the Pacific Theater of War. The game is based on the Unreal Engine 4 and will feature online multiplayer for up to four players.
