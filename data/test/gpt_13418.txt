Mohammad Abdur Rouf is a retired member of the High Court of Pakistan and a judge of the Supreme Court of Pakistan. Abdur Rouf was born on 5 December 1943 in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. He received his undergraduate education from the University of Karachi and his law degree from the University of Punjab. Rouf practiced law in Karachi before being appointed a judge of the High Court of Sindh in 1982. He was transferred to the Supreme Court of Pakistan in 1987 and served as its chief justice from 2006 to 2007. Abdur Rouf retired from the judiciary in May 2007.
