The 1993 Royal Rumble was the sixth annual Royal Rumble professional wrestling pay-per-view event produced by World Wrestling Federation (WWF). It took place on January 24, 1993 at the Boston Garden in Boston, Massachusetts. The main event was a 30-man Royal Rumble match, in which the first wrestler eliminated would win the WWF Championship.  The 1993 Royal Rumble was notable for the debut of Hulk Hogan and the return of The Undertaker. Hogan, who had retired from professional wrestling in July 1992, won his first WWF Championship at the event.
