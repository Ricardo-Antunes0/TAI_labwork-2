The 1987 Fiesta Bowl was a college football bowl game played on January 1, 1987, at the University of Phoenix Stadium in Glendale, Arizona. It was the 12th edition of the Fiesta Bowl and the final one before the bowl's move to ESPN in 1990.  The game featured the #7 Nebraska Cornhuskers and the #10 Miami Hurricanes. Nebraska won 45-38 in a game that saw five lead changes and 10 ties.  This was also the final game as head coach of Miami coach Jimmy Johnson.
