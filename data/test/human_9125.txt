Adrian Awasom (born October 25, 1983) is a former American football defensive end. He was a member of the Toronto Argonauts of the CFL and the Minnesota Vikings of the National Football League. He was signed by the New York Giants as an undrafted free agent in 2005. He played college football at North Texas. Early years Awasom was born in Cameroon and moved with his family to the United States when he was four, growing up in Fort Bend County, Texas. Awasom attended Stafford High School where he was a three-sport start in football, basketball, and track.  A two time All-District member and caught 22 passes for 350 yards at tight end as a senior.  As a member of the basketball team, Awasom was a two-time All-State member averaging 16.7 ppg and 16.7 rpg as a senior and also earning All-Area honors.  In track, he was a two-time All-State performer with a personal best of 189'74". Awasom is one of three players from Stafford HS to play in the NFL, others being Boris Anyama and Craig Robertson.
