Shakti: The Power is an Indian action-drama film written and directed by Ashutosh Gowariker. The film stars Aamir Khan, Kangana Ranaut, and Rani Mukherjee. The story follows the journey of a single mother (Kangana Ranaut) who sets out to avenge the death of her husband in a terrorist attack.  The film is set in Delhi after the September 11 attacks and tells the story of a woman (Kangana Ranaut) who sets out to find those responsible for her husband's death. She becomes involved with the police investigation into the attack and must fight against the corrupt officials who are trying to stop her from finding justice.
