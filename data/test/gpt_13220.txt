Independents Day is the sixth studio album by American rock band 311. It was released on June 17, 2001, through BMG. The album features the single "All Mixed Up", which reached number one on the "Billboard" Hot Modern Rock Tracks chart.  The album spawned several other popular singles, including "Down", "Don't Stay", and "So What". 311 released two more albums after this one, but they were not as commercially successful. The band went on hiatus in 2006 and did not release any new music until 2014.  "Independents Day" has been certified Gold by the RIAA.
