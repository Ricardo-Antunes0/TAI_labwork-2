Mademoiselle Charlotte is a loa who manifests as a beautiful young woman. She is the patron goddess of love, beauty, and all things romantic. She is also the goddess of the moon and of childbirth.   Mademoiselle Charlotte is often worshiped as a deity of the heart, and she is often invoked to bring love and happiness into the lives of her followers. She is also known for her healing powers, and her devotees often seek her guidance in matters of love and heartache.
