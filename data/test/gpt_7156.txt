Liangzi Lake , originally Fan Lake , is a freshwater lake in the southeast of the city of Wuhan, Hubei province, China. The lake covers an area of about 240 km2 and has a water volume of about 5,000 cubic meters.  The lake is about 100 km southeast of Wuhan and 10 km from the city's downtown. It is fed by the Qingshan Stream, which also flows into the Yangtze River. The lake has a rich ecosystem with many varieties of fish and aquatic plants. There are also many islands in the lake.
