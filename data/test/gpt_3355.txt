Arjoun (, also spelled Arcun or Arjoon), is a city in southwestern Iran. It is located in the Arjoun District of Kermanshah Province, near the border with Iraq. At the 2006 census, its population was 48,834.   Arjoun is situated in the fertile plain of Khuzestan at an altitude of 1,500 meters. It has a hot and dry climate throughout the year. The city is known for its pottery and carpets. There are also several mosques, a natural springs and a castle in Arjoun.
