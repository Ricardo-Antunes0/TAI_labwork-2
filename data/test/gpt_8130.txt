Advance Wars is a turn-based strategy video game series created by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo. The first game in the series, Advance Wars, was released in 2001 for the Game Boy Advance. It has been followed by fifteen sequels, five spin-offs, and a manga adaptation.  The games are set in a science fiction world and feature armies of mobile units that battle each other on a battlefield divided into hexagonal tiles. The player commands units and directs their movements on the battlefield to defeat the enemy army or capture strategic points. The objective of the game is to achieve victory by either destroying all enemy units or capturing all enemy bases.  The Advance Wars games have received critical acclaim, with praise going to their gameplay, graphics, and music. They have also been praised for their replay value and for their educational value, teaching about military strategy.
