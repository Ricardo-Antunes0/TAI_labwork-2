First Statement was a Canadian literary magazine founded in Toronto in 1978 by Geoffrey Grigson and Gordon Smith. The magazine published poetry, fiction, and criticism, as well as special issues devoted to a single author or theme. Contributors included Irving Layton, Margaret Atwood, Michael Ondaatje, and Gail Matheson.
