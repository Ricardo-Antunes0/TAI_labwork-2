Backdrop CMS is an Open source, community-developed, content management system (CMS) for web development. It is based on the WordPress content management system and uses the same MySQL database. Backdrop offers a variety of features not found in WordPress, such as a customizability interface, built-in theme support, advanced post types, and a versatile taxonomy.
