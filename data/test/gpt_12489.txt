Leliana is a fictional character from BioWare's Baldur's Gate series of video games. She is the companion of protagonist Gorion the Gray in the first game, and then of the player-controlled protagonist in its sequel, Baldur's Gate II.  Leliana is an elven ranger who specializes in combat spells and healing. She was imprisoned by the forces of darkness for centuries, until she was freed by the player in Baldur's Gate II. Leliana accompanies Gorion through his journey to recapture his son, Bhaal, and defeat the demon lord Malacath.  Leliana has been well received by critics, with many praising her development as a character and her role in the games' plotlines. She has been included on several "best female companions" lists.
