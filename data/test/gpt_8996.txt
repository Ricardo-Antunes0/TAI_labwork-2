Kevin M. Donegan (born 1958) is a professor of English and Comparative Literature at the University of California, Irvine. His research focuses on nineteenth-century British and American literature, with a particular focus on the Romantic period.  Donegan's publications include books on William Wordsworth, Samuel Taylor Coleridge, and Percy Bysshe Shelley, as well as articles on Romanticism and nineteenth-century American literature. He has also edited or co-edited several books, including "Romanticism in American Poetry" (1999) and "British Romanticism: Critical Essays" (2001).  Donegan was born in 1958 in Providence, Rhode Island. He received his B.A. from Brown University in 1980, and his M.A. and Ph.D. from Harvard University in 1983 and 1986, respectively.
