The Old Punch Bowl is a medieval public house located in the City of London, England. It is a Grade I listed building.  The Old Punch Bowl is one of the oldest pubs in London, dating back to the early 15th century. The current building was erected in the mid-16th century, and was originally known as The Queen's Head. The pub underwent a major restoration in the early 1990s.  Today, the Old Punch Bowl is a popular tourist attraction and social meeting place, hosting live music and comedy performances as well as traditional pub fare including fish and chips.
