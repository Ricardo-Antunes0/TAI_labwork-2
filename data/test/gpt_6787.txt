The Saxony Apartment Building is a historic apartment building located in downtown Milwaukee, Wisconsin. The building was designed by the architectural firm of Walker and Weeks and built in 1927. It is a six-story, concrete structure with a limestone facade. It features an elaborate terra cotta ornamentation on its facade, including a band of stylized animals around the top of the building.  The Saxony Apartment Building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
