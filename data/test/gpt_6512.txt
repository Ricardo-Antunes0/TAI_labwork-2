Mag Earwhig! is a 1997 album release by the British rock band The Cure, their eighth studio album. It is the first album to be released by the group on their new record label Fiction Records.  The album was mixed by Kevin Armstrong and recorded at AIR Studios in London during October 1996. It was produced by The Cure's longtime collaborator Robert Smith, who also wrote or co-wrote all but one of the album's 10 tracks.  "Mag Earwhig!" received mixed reviews from critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album received an average score of 61, based on 14 reviews.  The title track was released as the first single from the album in May 1997 and reached number 16 on the UK Singles Chart. The second single, "Lovesong", reached number 8 on the UK Singles Chart.
