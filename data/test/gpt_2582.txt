Chimney Farm is a historic farm property located near the town of Taylorsville, Greene County, New York. It has been in the family of the Van Antwerp family since 1792. The farmhouse is a two-story, five bay frame structure, with a gable roof and clapboarded exterior. It features a two-story, hip roofed ell and is surrounded by a large stone wall.  The property includes a working dairy farm with a creamery, smokehouse, and corn crib. There are also several outbuildings including a barn, chicken coop, carriage house, and milk house. The farm was used as a filming location for the 1985 movie "The Appleton Family Movie".
