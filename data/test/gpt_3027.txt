Welch is a town in northern Craig County, Oklahoma, United States. The population was 1,859 at the 2010 census.  Welch is located in northern Craig County at (35.965903, -99.539195). It is bordered to the north by the town of Pawnee, to the east by the town of Newcastle, to the south by the town of Caney, and to the west by the town of Dumas.  According to the United States Census Bureau, Welch has a total area of , all of it land.  As of the census of 2000, there were 1,826 people, 621 households, and 437 families residing in the town. The population density was 1,419.4 people per square mile (553.3/km²). There were 759 housing units at an average density of 605.1 per square mile (235.2/km²). The racial makeup of the town was 97.20% White, 0.38% African American, 0.68% Native American, 0.19% Asian, 0.04% Pacific Islander, 0.68% from other races, and 1.72% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 2.92% of the population.
