Lagarde or Bodega Lagarde is a family-owned winery in the Ribera del Duero region of Spain. The winery was founded in 1975 by Antonio Lagarde and his wife Isabel. Lagarde produces a wide range of wines, including reds, whites, and rosés.  Lagarde is known for its production of Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Carmenere, Tempranillo, and Zinfandel. The winery also produces sparkling wine and a Rosé wine. The winery's wines are sold both domestically and internationally.
