Solvorn Church  is a parish church in Solvorn in the municipality of Ålesund, Norway. It is located in the village of Solvorn. The white, wooden church was built in 1892 and has a seating capacity of about 260 people. The church was consecrated on 12 October 1893 by Bishop Gunnar Thomassen. It is part of the Ålesund parish in the Lister deanery in the Diocese of Møre og Romsdal.
