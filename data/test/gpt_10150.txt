Edward Wynne (born 3 June 1961) is an English actor and voice artist.  Wynne was born in London, the son of actress Margaret Rutherford and playwright James Wynne. He has appeared in a number of British films and television programmes, including "The Jewel in the Crown", "Bridget Jones's Diary", "Poldark", "Midsomer Murders" and "Doctor Who". He also voiced the title character in the UK cartoon series "The Flintstones".
