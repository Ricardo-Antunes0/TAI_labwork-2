The Mendiola Football Club 1991 is a Spanish football club based in Mendiola, in the autonomous community of Andalusia. Founded in 1920, it plays in Tercera División – Group 2, holding home games at "Estadio de Mendiola", with a capacity of 6,000 spectators.   Mendiola Football Club 1991 was founded on 1st of January 1920 and played its first game against Arenas Club de Getxo on 9th of February 1920.  The club finished its formation in the regional leagues and then had a spell in Tercera División B Group 2 during the 1998-1999 season. In 2000-2001 season, Mendiola finished second in its group and was promoted to Tercera División. In 2003-2004 season, Mendiola relegated from Tercera División after finishing last in its group.  In the 2006-2007 season, Mendiola finished as runners-up in its group and was promoted back to Segunda División B. The 2008-2009 season was less successful for Mendiola and it was relegated back to Tercera División again.   In the 2010-2011 season, Mendiola finished third in its group and was promoted back to Segunda División B.
