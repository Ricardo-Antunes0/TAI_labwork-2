Katariina "Kati" Ahonen (born 7 November 1966) is a Finnish journalist and television presenter.  Born in Helsinki, Kati Ahonen started her career as a journalist at the age of 21, working for MTV3 and YLE TV2. She has since worked as a presenter and newsreader for several Finnish television channels, including Nelonen and MTV3. She has also presented programmes for the Finnish Broadcasting Company and The Voice of Finland.  Ahonen was married to journalist Jukka Ahonen from 1998 to 2004. They have one son.
