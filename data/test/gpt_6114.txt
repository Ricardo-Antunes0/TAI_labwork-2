Sean Patrick Connelly (born 26 June 1970) is an Irish former professional footballer who played as a midfielder. He made more than 350 appearances in the English Premier League and other top European leagues, and represented Ireland at international level.  Connelly started his career at Bohemians, before moving to Liverpool in 1990. He spent six years at Anfield, making over 150 appearances, before moving to Portsmouth in 1997. He spent two seasons at Fratton Park, before joining Tottenham Hotspur in 2000. He spent four seasons at White Hart Lane, making over 100 appearances, before moving to Fulham in 2004. He spent two seasons at Craven Cottage, before retiring in 2007.   Connelly made his international debut for Ireland in 1993. He played 55 times for his country, scoring three goals.
