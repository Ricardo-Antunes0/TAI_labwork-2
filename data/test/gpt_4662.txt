Casino Raiders II is a 1991 Hong Kong action-adventure film directed by Wilson Yip and produced by Raymond Chow. It is a sequel to the 1987 film Casino Raiders.  The film stars Jackie Chan, Sammo Hung, Yuen Biao and Jet Li.  The film tells the story of a team of casino raiders who are hired by a corrupt Chinese general to rob a casino in Macau. The team is opposed by the local police force and a rival gang of casino raiders led by Andy Lau.  The film was shot in Macau and Hong Kong and was released in Hong Kong on 27 October 1991. It was released in the United States on 25 January 1992.
