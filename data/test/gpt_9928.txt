Swan Sovereign (previously Dirty Martini) is a progressive metal band from Helsinki, Finland. The band was formed in 2007 by guitarist and songwriter Jouni Hynninen and keyboardist Tuomas Holopainen. The band has since released two albums, "The Great Escape" (2009) and "Phoenix" (2011), and has toured extensively throughout Europe and North America.  Swan Sovereign's music is characterized by its intricate, often chromatic guitar work, Hynninen's soaring vocals, and Holopainen's lavish keyboard textures. The band has been compared to the likes of Dream Theater, Porcupine Tree, and Opeth.
