Hi'-Neighbor! is a 1934 Our Gang short comedy film directed by Edward L. Cahn.  The gang, consisting of Benny the Bat, Granny, Hadley, and Ralphie, go to their next-door neighbor's house for a game of hide-and-seek. While there, Benny falls in love with their neighbor's daughter (played by May Wynn). The girl seems to return his affections, but her father is not too pleased. Benny eventually gets the upper hand and proposes marriage to the girl. The father is finally convinced and they marry.
