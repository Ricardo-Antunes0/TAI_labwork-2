Solar Plexus is a compilation album released by English rock band Muse in 2006. It comprises tracks from the band's first three studio albums, Showbiz (2000), Origin of Symmetry (2001) and Absolution (2003), as well as some B-sides and remixes.  The album was produced by the band and recorded at various studios around the world. It was released on Virgin Records in the UK and Elektra Records in the US.  The album has generally been well received by critics, who praised its cohesion and variety. It has been certified silver by the British Phonographic Industry and gold by the US Recording Industry Association of America.
