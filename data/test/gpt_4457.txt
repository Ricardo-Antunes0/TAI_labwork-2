Francesca Calearo (born 16 January 2002), known professionally as Madame, is a Maltese singer and songwriter.  Madame was born in Mosta, Malta on 16 January 2002. She started singing at the age of six and began writing her own songs when she was thirteen. Madame's music is inspired by soul, R&B, and pop genres.  In October 2018, Madame released her debut single "Chase Me" which featured American rapper 2 Chainz. The song peaked at number two on the Maltese Singles Chart and was certified gold by the Maltese Music Industry Association. In November 2018, Madame released her second single "In My Feelings" which featured American rapper Drake. The song peaked at number one on the Maltese Singles Chart and was certified platinum by the Maltese Music Industry Association.
