Trans National Place, also known as 1155 N. Milwaukee Ave., is a building in Chicago's Near North Side. Completed in 1928, the building originally housed a Woolworth's department store and a theater. The building was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1978.   Today, Trans National Place is home to several businesses, including a daycare center, a hair salon, and a pizzeria. The building is also used for community events, such as concerts and movie screenings.
