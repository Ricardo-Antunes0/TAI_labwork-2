Creature 3D is a 2014 Indian 3D computer-generated animated fantasy film produced by Anurag Kashyap and Vikas Bahl for Phantom Films. It is an adaptation of the 2008 Indian children's novel of the same name by Vikas Swarup.  The film tells the story of a young boy named Ajay who is cursed with a monstrous form after a mishap with a witch. With the help of a friendly fairy, Ajay must find a way to break the curse and return to his normal self.
