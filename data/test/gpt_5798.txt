Michael Remmers (born April 11, 1989) is an American football offensive tackle for the Carolina Panthers of the National Football League (NFL). He was drafted by the Panthers in the seventh round of the 2012 NFL Draft.  Remmers played college football at Florida State. He was named to the All-ACC First Team as a junior in 2009 and senior in 2010. He also received honorable mention recognition from the Associated Press and first-team All-American honors from Walter Camp Football Foundation.  Remmers has also been a member of the NFLPA, serving as its president from 2012 to 2014.
