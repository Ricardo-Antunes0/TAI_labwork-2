Blood's Too Rich by Luke Doucet & Sean Phillips is a novel about a young artist, struggling to make a living in the city, and his affair with a wealthy older man.  The novel is set in Montreal during the early 1990s and tells the story of a young artist, Vincent, and his affair with a wealthy older man, Augustus. Vincent is desperate to make a living in the city and finds himself attracted to Augustus, who offers him financial support and encouragement. However, as their relationship deepens, Vincent begins to realize that he's not just being used; Augustus genuinely cares for him. Despite the risks involved, Vincent decides to stay with Augustus.  Blood's Too Rich is an exploration of love, sex, and power. It is also a reflection on the difficult journey from poverty to success in modern society.
