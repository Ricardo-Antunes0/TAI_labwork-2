Juan Carlos Botero is a Colombian researcher, art historian, and museum director. He served as director of the Museum of Fine Arts of Bogota from 1979 to 1988, and was a professor at the Universidad de los Andes from 1990 to 2003.  Botero has written extensively on Colombian visual art and has been recognized as one of Colombia's most significant artists. His work has been exhibited in museums around the world, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City and the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston. He has been awarded several prestigious prizes, including the CUNY Distinguished Scholar Award and the National Order of Arts and Letters Award.
