Centennial Neighborhood District is a national historic district located in the West End of Davenport, Iowa, United States. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982. The district includes 33 contributing buildings and 1 contributing site. It is a residential district that was developed between 1890 and 1910. The district includes representative examples of Queen Anne, Romanesque Revival, and Bungalow / American Craftsman style architecture.
