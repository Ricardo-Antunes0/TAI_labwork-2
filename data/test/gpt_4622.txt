Pennsylvania Route 690 (PA 690) is a  state highway in Pennsylvania. The southern terminus is at US 6 near Selinsgrove, and the northern terminus is at PA 322 in Bellefonte. PA 690 runs through the center of Schuylkill County, connecting Selinsgrove to Bellefonte.  PA 690 was designated in the 1930s. The southern terminus of the route was at US 6 in Selinsgrove. The route extended north to Bellefonte along what is now PA 322. By the 1960s, PA 690 had been extended north to its current terminus at PA 322.
