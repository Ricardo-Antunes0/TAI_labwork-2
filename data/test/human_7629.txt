Lakatnik Point (, ‘Nos Lakatnik’ \'nos la-'kat-nik\) is a point on the northwest coast of Smith Island, South Shetland Islands situated 1.6 km north-northeast of Lista Point, 11 km north-northeast of Cape James, 2.66 km southwest of Garmen Point and 22 km southwest of Cape Smith.  Formed by an offshoot of Neofit Peak.  Bulgarian early mapping in 2008.  Named after the settlement of Lakatnik in western Bulgaria.
