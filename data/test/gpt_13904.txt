This Man's Navy (aka Airship Squadron No. 9) was a squadron of the United States Navy during World War I. It was formed on 5 February 1918 at Naval Air Station North Island, San Diego, California and was disestablished on 1 June 1919.  This Man's Navy was made up of volunteers from the United States merchant marine. The squadron operated dirigibles and airplanes from Naval Air Station North Island in San Diego, California. The squadron's insignia was a blue diamond with a white anchor in the center.
