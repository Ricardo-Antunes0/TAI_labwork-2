Some Liar is a 1919 American silent drama film directed by Maurice Elvey and starring Marguerite Clark and Sidney King.  The film is set in a small town in the American Midwest and tells the story of a woman who is falsely accused of murder. She must use all her wit and cunning to prove her innocence before time runs out.   The film was released on Blu-ray and DVD on September 12, 2019.
