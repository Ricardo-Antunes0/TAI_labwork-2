Dermot Moran  is an Irish philosopher and writer. He is the author of several books, including The Inner Life of Animals (1998), What Are We to Do with Our Lives? (2004), and The Animal Project: A Vision for the Human-Animal Connection (2009).  Moran was born in Dublin, Ireland, in 1957. He studied at Trinity College, Dublin, and at the University of California, Berkeley. He has been a professor at the University of California, Santa Cruz since 1997.  Moran's work addresses a wide range of philosophical and religious issues, including ethics, aesthetics, animal rights, and environmentalism. His book The Animal Project is considered a seminal work in the philosophy of animal rights.
