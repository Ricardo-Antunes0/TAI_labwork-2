Samsaram Oka Chadarangam   is a 1971 Indian Tamil-language drama film, directed by K. Balachander and produced by M. A. Thirumavalavan. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao, Jayalalithaa, Sridevi and M. G. Ramachandran in lead roles, with music composed by M. S. Viswanathan.  The film tells the story of a poor family in a village who are struggling to make ends meet, until they discover a gold mine on their property. The father decides to take his family to the city to find work, but they are unable to get by on their meager earnings and end up returning to the village after a few years. The father is eventually able to retire and live a comfortable life with his family.
