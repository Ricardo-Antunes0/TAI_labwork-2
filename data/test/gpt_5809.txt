The 2004 NBA draft was held on June 24, 2004 at the Theater at Madison Square Garden in New York City. The draft was televised in the United States by ESPN.   The Philadelphia 76ers, with the first overall pick, selected Philadelphia native Kwame Brown. Other top picks included Oklahoma City Thunder's Kevin Durant (2nd), Seattle SuperSonics' Russell Westbrook (3rd), and Chicago Bulls' Derrick Rose (4th).
