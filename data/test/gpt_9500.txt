Chitale Bandhu Mithaiwale is an Indian snacks company that manufactures Mithaiwale, a type of sweets made from milk, sugar, cardamom and pistachios.  Chitale Bandhu Mithaiwale was founded in the city of Mumbai in 1996 by two brothers, Raghunath and Rajesh Chitale. The company produces Mithaiwale, a type of sweet made from milk, sugar, cardamom and pistachios. The company has production facilities in Mumbai and Pune. Chitale Bandhu Mithaiwale is a part of the Chitale Group, which also includes a dairy products company, a biscuit manufacturer and a bakery.
