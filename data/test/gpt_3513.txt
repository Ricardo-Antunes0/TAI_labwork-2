Attack Squadron 145 (VA-145) was an aviation squadron of the United States Navy. The squadron was established on 20 September 1945 and disestablished on 1 September 1967.  VA-145 was one of the first operational jet squadrons in the U.S. Navy, flying Republic P-47 Thunderbolt aircraft from 1946 to 1950. From 1950 to 1954, the squadron flew McDonnell F2H Banshee aircraft. The squadron flew Grumman F7F Tigercat aircraft from 1954 to 1957, and then received McDonnell Douglas F4 Phantom II aircraft in 1957.   The squadron was re-designated Attack Squadron 145 (VA-145) on 1 September 1967 and disestablished on 1 September 1967.
