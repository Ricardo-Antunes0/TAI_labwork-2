is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by CLAMP. The series was originally serialized in Kodansha's Weekly Shōnen Magazine from 1993 to 1996, and collected into twenty-four tankōbon volumes.  The story follows Tsubasa, a young boy who is able to fly using the power of his wind magic. He joins a group of pilots who fly in an organization known as the Syclone to protect the world from evil.
