Elaan is a 1971 Bollywood Sci-Fi thriller film directed by K. A. Abbas and produced by Ram Kapoor. It stars Shashi Kapoor, Dharmendra, Waheeda Rehman and Nutan. The film's music is composed by Laxmikant-Pyarelal.  Set in the future, India has become a totalitarian state ruled by a single party. Elaan is a dissident who is trying to escape to freedom in the United States. She is helped by a scientist, Dr. Singh, and his son, Rajesh. They are discovered and captured, but Rajesh escapes and helps Elaan to reach the US Embassy. She is then able to contact her husband in India and he helps her to escape to freedom.
