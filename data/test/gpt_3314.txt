The Analects (; ; Old Chinese: [r]u[n]l[a]i[k]ts, "words of advice") is a Chinese book of political philosophy and wisdom attributed to Confucius. The work consists of sayings and commentary on the ancient Chinese system of government, which Confucius believed to be the best in the world.  The Analects has had a significant influence on East Asian culture, particularly in China. It has also been influential in Japan, where it is known as the Five Classics. In Korea, where Confucianism is also influential, the Analects is considered to be the foundation of the Joseon dynasty.
