Burn My Eye was the debut EP by the British heavy metal band Judas Priest. It was released on 7 February 1979 through Columbia Records.  Judas Priest's debut album, "Burn My Eye", was released on 7 February 1979 through Columbia Records. The five-track EP had been recorded in late 1976 at EMI's studios in London and featured the band's original lineup of Ian Hill, Glenn Tipton, K.K. Downing, and Rick Altmann. The songs "Iron Man" and "You've Got Another Thing Comin'" were released as singles and reached the top 10 of the UK charts.  The EP was remastered and reissued on CD in 2002 with three bonus tracks: "Blood Red Skies", a live version of "Freewheel Burning", and a cover of Thin Lizzy's "The Boys Are Back In Town".
