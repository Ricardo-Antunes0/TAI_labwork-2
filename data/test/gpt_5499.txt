She studied at the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston, and the School of Visual Arts in New York. Her first solo exhibition was at the Yvon Lambert Gallery in Paris in 1965. She was awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship in 1971 and a MacArthur Fellowship in 1988. Joan Micklin Silver died on July 12, 2004 in her home town of New York City.
