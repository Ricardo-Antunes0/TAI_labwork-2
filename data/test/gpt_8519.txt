Aleksey Lesnichiy (also Aliaksei Liasnichy, ; born March 7, 1984) is a Belarusian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Minsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL).  Lesnichiy was drafted by the Nashville Predators in the second round, 53rd overall, of the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He has also played for the Boston Bruins, Ottawa Senators, and Chicago Blackhawks.
