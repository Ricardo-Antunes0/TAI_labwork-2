Edwin Louis Jucker (July 8, 1916 – May 2, 1994) was an American football player, coach, and college athletics administrator. He served as the head football coach at Brown University from 1951 to 1955, compiling a record of 20–15. Jucker also served as the head men's basketball coach at Brown from 1951 to 1954, tallying a mark of 22–17. Jucker then served as the commissioner of the Ivy League from 1959 to 1967.
