Morgan Pressel (born May 23, 1988) is an American professional golfer who currently plays on the LPGA Tour. She has been a member of the tour since 2006. Pressel has won two LPGA Tour events, the 2012 Kraft Nabisco Championship and the 2016 Walmart Women's Open.  Pressel was born in Cincinnati, Ohio. Her father, Bob Pressel, is a former professional golfer who won three major championships (the U.S. Open, PGA Championship, and Masters) in the 1970s. Morgan began playing golf at age five, and by age eleven she was playing in national and international tournaments. She attended The University of Southern California, where she played for the USC Trojans women's golf team. In 2006, she became a full-time member of the LPGA Tour.
