Kalavu Thozhirchalai (Tamil: களவு தொழிற்சாலை) (English: Thieving Forest) is a 1988 Tamil-language drama film directed by S. A. Chandrasekhar. The film features Karthik, Sarath Kumar, Aishwarya and Radha in lead roles. The story is about a forest where the trees steal anything that comes their way, including people.  Kalavu Thozhirchalai tells the story of a forest where the trees steal anything that comes their way, including people. One day, a hunter named Kalavu enters the forest and is captured by the trees. Kalavu is kept captive in the forest for months until he is finally rescued by a group of boys who venture into the forest to find him. Upon his return to the village, Kalavu tells his story of captivity to horrified villagers, who vow to never let the forest escape their notice again.
