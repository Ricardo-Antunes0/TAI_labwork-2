Richard Gerald Neuheisel Jr. (; born February 26, 1957) is a retired American football quarterback who played for the University of Washington, the Baltimore Ravens, and the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League (NFL).  Neuheisel attended Archbishop Mitty High School in San Francisco, California. He played college football at the University of Washington and was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the first round of the 1979 NFL Draft. He also played for the Chiefs from 1993 to 1995. Neuheisel was inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame in 2006.  Neuheisel has been involved in broadcasting since retiring from football. He currently serves as a color commentator for Pac-12 Networks' college football coverage and is a weekly guest host on "The Mike & Mike Show" on ESPN Radio.
