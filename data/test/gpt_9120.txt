Biswajeet Pradhan is a spatial scientist, modeller, and cartographer. He is particularly interested in global land cover change and its effects on climate and ecology.  Biswajeet Pradhan received his B.Sc. from the University of Delhi in 2002, and his M.Sc. from the University of Edinburgh in 2004. He then completed his Ph.D. at the University of Edinburgh in 2009, under the supervision of Professor John Kennedy.  From 2009 to 2012, Biswajeet Pradhan was a postdoctoral research associate at the University of California, Irvine. In 2012, he joined the faculty of the Department of Geography at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County as an assistant professor.  Biswajeet Pradhan has published several papers on global land cover change and its effects on climate and ecology. His work has been featured in Science, Nature, Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, and other leading journals.
