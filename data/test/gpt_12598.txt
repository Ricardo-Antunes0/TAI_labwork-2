Bawlf  is a village in Alberta, Canada. It is located on the east side of Highway 16, west of Edmonton, in the Municipal District of Willow Creek.  The village has a population of about 350 people, consisting of a mix of Anglo and aboriginal people. The economy is based on agriculture and tourism. There is a small store, a post office, a church, and a hotel. The village is served by Edmonton International Airport.
