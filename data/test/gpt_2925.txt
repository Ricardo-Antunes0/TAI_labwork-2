Ali Nuhu Mohammed (born 15 March 1974) is a retired Nigerian professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Mohammed started his career with Sunshine Stars in the Nigerian Premier League, before moving to German side Hertha BSC in 1996. He spent two seasons with Hertha, before returning to Nigeria and joining Enyimba International. He spent two seasons with Enyimba, before joining Norwegian side Molde FK in 2000. He spent four seasons with Molde, before retiring in 2006.  Mohammed made 63 appearances for the Nigeria national team, scoring six goals. He was part of the Nigeria team that won the African Cup of Nations in 2002.
