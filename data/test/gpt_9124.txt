Born in Dorking, Surrey, McDame was educated at Eton College and Trinity College, Cambridge. He made his first-class debut for Cambridge University against the Marylebone Cricket Club in June 1848. He played two further first-class matches for the university in that season, both of which were against Sussex. In his three first-class appearances for Sussex, he scored a total of 47 runs at an average of 8.33. He died at the age of about eighty years old.
