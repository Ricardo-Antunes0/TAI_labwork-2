Markéta  Vondroušová (; born 28 June 1987) is a Czech model and actress who is known for her work in the fashion industry.  Markéta Vondroušová was born on 28 June 1987 in the city of Pilsen, Czech Republic. She started modelling at the age of 16 and has since appeared in campaigns for brands such as Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Dior and Yves Saint Laurent.   Vondroušová has also appeared in a number of television commercials, including ones for Audi, Samsung and L’Oréal. In 2013, she won the Miss Universe Czech Republic title, which led to her first major acting role in the Czech feature film The Unseen (2013), in which she starred alongside Jaroslav Reznik and Ondřej Vetchý.
