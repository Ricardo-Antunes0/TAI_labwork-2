The Seal of Niue, or the Public Seal of Niue, is the official seal of Niue. The seal is affixed to all legal documents and certificates issued by the Niue government. The design of the seal is based on the national flag of Niue.  The seal was first created in 1866, and has been modified only a few times since then. The current design was adopted in 1997. The seal is made of brass and measures in diameter.
