Craig Adam Terrill (born June 27, 1980) is an American professional basketball player for the New Orleans Pelicans of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college ball at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.  Terrill was drafted by the Portland Trail Blazers in the first round of the 2002 NBA Draft. After four seasons with the Blazers, he was traded to the Indiana Pacers in 2004. He spent two seasons with Indiana before being traded to the Atlanta Hawks in 2006. He signed with the Pelicans in 2013.  Terrill is a two-time All-Star and has won two gold medals with Team USA at the FIBA World Championship.
