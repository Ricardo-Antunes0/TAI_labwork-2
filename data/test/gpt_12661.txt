Dandam Dashagunam is a 2011 Indian Kannada-language romantic comedy film written and directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It stars R. Madhavan and Shruti Haasan in lead roles, with Anand Nag and Puneeth Rajkumar playing supporting roles.  The story is about two people, Raghu (R. Madhavan) and Hamsa (Shruti Haasan), who meet in college and fall in love but are unable to decide on whether to get married or not. They eventually get married and go through a series of hilarious misadventures as they try to adjust to married life.
