TerraTech is a construction sandbox game with a heavy focus on terraforming and planetary colonization. Players must construct factories, laboratories, and other infrastructure to support their efforts of turning a desert planet into a thriving oasis, or building a space station in the middle of a hostile asteroid field.  TerraTech is an open-world construction sandbox game with heavy focus on terraforming and planetary colonization. The player must construct factories, laboratories, and other infrastructure to support their efforts of turning a desert planet into a thriving oasis, or building a space station in the middle of a hostile asteroid field. TerraTech features procedurally generated planets, allowing for endless replayability.
