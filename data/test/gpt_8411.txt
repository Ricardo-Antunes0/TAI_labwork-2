Tamia Monique Carter (born January 9, 2000), better known by her stage name Flo Milli, is an American rapper and singer. Born and raised in Atlanta, she first came to prominence in 2017, when she released her debut single "Tootsie Slide". The song peaked at number 20 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).  In 2018, Milli released her second single "Only U", which peaked at number two on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart. The song was also certified platinum by the RIAA. In addition to her music career, Milli is also a contributing writer for "Teen Vogue" and has appeared in several fashion campaigns for brands including Nike, Saint Laurent and Lululemon.
