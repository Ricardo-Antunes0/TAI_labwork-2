Sir Leslie Colin "Les" Patterson (born 1 July 1939) is a former Australian rules footballer who played with Melbourne in the Victorian Football League (VFL).   Patterson, originally from Shepparton, played 179 games for Melbourne, including 57 finals appearances. He was a member of the club's premiership team in 1963, and made four appearances in the interstate competition. He also represented Victoria at interstate football, and captained the side in the 1966 Hobart Carnival.
