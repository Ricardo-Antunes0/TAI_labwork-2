Wildsmith Shoes is a high-end English handmade shoemaker.  Founded in 1871, Wildsmith Shoes is one of the oldest shoemakers in the UK. The company is based in Northampton, England and makes a range of hand-crafted shoes, including oxford shoes, brogues, derby shoes and Chelsea boots.  Wildsmith Shoes is known for its attention to detail and unique style. The company has a reputation for making some of the finest English handmade shoes available, and its products have been featured in magazines such as GQ and Vogue.
