Nakhchivan necropolises - is historical monuments located in Nakhchivan city, in the west of the Republic of Azerbaijan. There are necropolises of the 2nd century BC and 1st century AD.  The necropolises are located on a plateau above the city and date back to the period of the Parthian and Sasanian empires. The most famous necropolis is that of the Bagratid princes, which is surrounded by a wall.   The necropolises were excavated in the 19th century by Russian archaeologists.
