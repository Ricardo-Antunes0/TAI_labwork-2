Going Bovine is a 2009 surreal dark comedy-drama film written, directed and produced by Michael Bacall. The film stars Aaron Paul, Dakota Fanning, Justin Bartha and Emile Hirsch.  The film tells the story of two brothers who are forced to go on the run after one of them is implicated in a cattle mutilation. The brothers must use all their ingenuity to avoid the authorities and survive in a post-apocalyptic world where bovine populations have exploded.
