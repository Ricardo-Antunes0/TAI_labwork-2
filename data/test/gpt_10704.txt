The M20 is a motorway in Kent, England. It runs from the A2 at Ashford in the east to the M25 junction 10 at Hooley in the west.  The motorway was constructed between 1978 and 1987, and is a major route between London and the south-east of England. The M20 is the only motorway in England to have a dual carriageway all the way from end to end.
