PTV GmbH (Planung Transport Verkehr) is a German public transport operator. The company was founded on 1 January 2002 as a merger of the former state-owned Verkehrsbetriebe Berlin-Brandenburg (VB) and the private Berliner Verkehrsbetriebe (BVG).  PTV GmbH operates the public transport services in Berlin and Brandenburg. The company's services include the Berlin U-Bahn, the Brandenburg S-Bahn, bus lines, trams and ferries.
