Malick Sidibé (1935 – 14 April 2016) was a Senegalese musician, composer and singer. He was born in Dakar.  Malick Sidibé was born in 1935 in the city of Dakar, Senegal. He started his musical career as a singer with the traditional ensemble of griots, and later joined the band of jazz trumpeter Cheikh Anta Diop. In 1969, he composed his first song, "Dakar", which became a hit on Senegalese radio. He continued to compose and record songs throughout the 1970s and 1980s, and his music has been widely popular in Senegal.  Malick Sidibé died on 14 April 2016 at the age of 83.
