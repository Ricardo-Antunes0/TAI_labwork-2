Serangoon MRT station is an underground Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) station on the North-South Line in Serangoon, Singapore. The station is also the only MRT station in Serangoon.  Serangoon MRT station opened on 28 July 1987 as part of the North-South Line's Phase 1. It is situated below ground level at the junction of Serangoon Road and Upper Serangoon Road.
