Katie Rogers is a fictional character from the American television series, "Breaking Bad". She is played by actress Anna Gunn and first appeared in the show's pilot episode, "Pilot".  Katie was born in Albuquerque, New Mexico on February 11, 1966. She is a high school chemistry teacher who is diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. In order to secure her financial future after her death, her husband leaves her $10 million in his will.  Rogers appeared in all 52 episodes of the show's first season. Her character was killed by Walter White in the Season 1 finale, "Felina".
