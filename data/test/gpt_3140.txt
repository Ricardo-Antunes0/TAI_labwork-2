Chanakya is a 2005 Indian Tamil-language comedy-drama film written and directed by S. Shankar. It tells the story of a small-time political analyst, Chanakya, who is drafted by a corrupt politician to come up with a plan to win the forthcoming state elections. Chanakya uses his shrewd intelligence and cunning to outwit the politician and his cohorts, ultimately leading to the politician's downfall.  The film stars Vishal, Trisha Krishnan and Karthik in lead roles, with music composed by Ilaiyaraaja. It released on 7 January 2005 to generally positive reviews from critics, who praised the humour and originality of the screenplay. The film was commercially successful, grossing over ₹10 crore (US$1.5 million) at the box office.
