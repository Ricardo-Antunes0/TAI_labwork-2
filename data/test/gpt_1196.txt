The Bedtime Record is a defunct record label which was founded in 1984 by Dave Marsh and Kim Fowley. The label released primarily punk and new wave music, but also released some country and heavy metal albums.  The Bedtime Record was one of the first labels to release punk and new wave music on cassette tape, and helped to pioneer the cassette tape revival in the early 1980s. The label's most well-known artists include The Dead Kennedys, The Misfits, Bad Brains, and The Sex Pistols.
