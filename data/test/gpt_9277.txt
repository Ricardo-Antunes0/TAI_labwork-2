Oginohana Akikazu  (born 18 November 1967) is a Japanese comedian, actor and singer.  Akikazu was born in Tokyo on 18 November 1967. He began his career as a comedian in the early 1990s, and has since appeared in a variety of TV programs and films. In 2006, he starred in the film "Tokyo Sonata". He has also released several albums, including the 2006 album "Kimi no Koe" (Your Voice).
