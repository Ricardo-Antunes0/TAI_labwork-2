The Gornergrat Railway (; GGB) is a metre gauge railway in the Western Alps, located in the canton of Valais. It links Gornergrat, a high mountain resort, with the village of Zermatt. The railway is operated by the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) and was opened in 1897.     The Gornergrat Railway is popular with tourists for its views of the Matterhorn and other peaks, as well as its luxury hotels. It is also used for access to the Gornergrat Glacier.
