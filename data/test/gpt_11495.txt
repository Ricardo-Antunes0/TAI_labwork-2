Alasdair Cochrane (born 31 March 1978) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a defender.  Cochrane began his career with St Johnstone, making more than 150 appearances in all competitions before joining Rangers in 2001. He spent four years at Ibrox, before moving to Sunderland in 2004. Cochrane made more than 100 appearances for Sunderland, before leaving to join Dundee United in 2007. He spent three seasons with Dundee United, making over 100 appearances, before joining Kilmarnock in 2011. He retired from playing in 2013 after struggling with a knee injury.  Cochrane has also represented Scotland at under-21 level, and played twice for the full Scotland team.
