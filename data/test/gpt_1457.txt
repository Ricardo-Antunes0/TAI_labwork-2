Lani Billard (born 20 June 1979) is a professional billiards player from the United Kingdom.  Lani Billard was born on 20 June 1979 in London, England. He began playing billiards at the age of 12, and quickly became a promising player. After several years of playing competitively, Billard made his professional debut in 1999. He has since won numerous tournaments, including the European Open in 2004, and the UK Championship in 2005. In 2007, he won the World Championship title, becoming the first British player to do so.
