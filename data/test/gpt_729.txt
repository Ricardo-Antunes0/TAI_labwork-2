Nothing Wrong is the third studio album by Scottish rock band Biffy Clyro. It was released on 2 October 2017 through Atlantic Records.  The album was preceded by the singles "Go" and "The Weight".  "Nothing Wrong" is the band's most diverse and sonically complex record to date, incorporating elements of funk, electronica, and world music. The album's lyrical content addresses topics such as anxiety, depression, self-doubt, and loneliness.  Biffy Clyro have been working on the album for over two years, during which time they undertook extensive touring in support of their previous album "Opposites" (2015). The album was recorded at various studios around the world including Stockholm, Montreal, London, and Los Angeles.
