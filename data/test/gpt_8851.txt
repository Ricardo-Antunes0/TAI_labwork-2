Ignaz Karl Soppron (1821–1894), better known as Ignjat Sopron, was a Hungarian painter.  Born in Sopron, in the Kingdom of Hungary, Ignjat Sopron studied at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts, Budapest under János Fodor and János Thorma. He moved to Vienna in 1848 and began to exhibit his work there. He returned to Hungary in 1857 and became a leading painter of Romanticism in Hungary. He died in Sopron.
