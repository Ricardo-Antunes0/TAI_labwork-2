Mansoor Amjad , born 14 December 1987, is a Pakistani cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-pace bowler.  Amjad made his first-class debut for Rawalpindi in the 2007–08 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy. He made his List A debut for Rawalpindi in the 2008–09 Quaid-e-Azam One Day Cup, and also played for Rawalpindi in the 2009–10 Multan Cup. He made his first-class century, against Sargodha, in the 2010–11 Quaid-e-Azam Trophy.  Amjad made his Twenty20 debut for Karachi Kings in the 2016–17 Pakistan Super League. In October 2018, he was named in Pakistan's squad for the 2018 ICC World Twenty20 Qualifier.
