Richton Park is the penultimate station along the Metropolitan Line of the London Underground. Located on the border of the London Boroughs of Harrow and Brent, it is in Travelcard Zone 3. The station is served by the Metropolitan line, and is in Travelcard Zone 3. It opened as Richton Park on 20 November 1907 as an extension of the Metropolitan Line from Harrow on the Hill to Rickmansworth.
