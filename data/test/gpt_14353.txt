Sirf Ek Minute is a 2021 Indian Hindi-language romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Shreyas Talpade. The film stars Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra, and Siddharth Malhotra in lead roles. The film is produced by Aditya Chopra and is a remake of the Telugu film Sirf EK Palle (2015).  Sirf Ek Minute is a story about two people who are complete opposites but manage to fall in love. Rajesh is an ambitious and successful entrepreneur, while Meera is a stay-at-home mom who is content with her simple life. When their families get together for the wedding of Rajesh's sister, the two strangers find themselves spending more time together than they'd anticipated. Despite their initial reservations, the two eventually fall in love, but they must face the challenges of their different lifestyles before they can commit to each other.
