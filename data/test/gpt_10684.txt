Firaaq  is a 2014 Pakistani family drama television series directed by Shahid Nadeem and written by Nadeem Ahmed. It is produced by Asif Ali Zardari's production company, Nadeem-Shahid Productions. The series stars Saba Qamar, Danish Taimoor, and Faisal Qureshi in lead roles.  The series tells the story of a family from the rural areas of Pakistan who are forced to leave their home due to political unrest. They migrate to the city and start a new life, but their troubles continue as they are faced with money problems and social discrimination.
