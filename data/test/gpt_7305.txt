Phillip Michael Pressey (born February 17, 1991) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Colorado Rockies of Major League Baseball (MLB). He made his MLB debut in 2014 with the Kansas City Royals.  Pressey attended high school at Stanford University and played college baseball for the Stanford Cardinal. The Cincinnati Reds selected him in the first round of the 2012 MLB draft. He made his professional debut with their Class A Advanced affiliate, the Louisville Bats, that year.
