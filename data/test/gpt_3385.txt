Tag, known in Japan as , is a 2015 American romantic comedy film directed by Anne Fletcher and written by Fletcher and her husband, David Levien. The film stars Ed Helms, Anthony Mackie, and Mary Steenburgen.  Tag was filmed in Charleston, South Carolina. It premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival on September 10, 2015 and was released on October 7, 2015.   Tag is a romantic comedy film set in Charleston, South Carolina, about two friends who become lovers while playing tag together.   Ed Helms stars as Phil, a down-on-his-luck salesman who is best friends with Anthony Mackie's Wayne. When Wayne's wife leaves him for a richer man, Phil offers to be his new tag partner.   While playing tag together, Phil and Wayne realize they have feelings for each other. They decide to take things slow, but the attraction is too strong to keep hidden.   Mary Steenburgen co-stars as Phil's nosy next-door neighbor Margo. She tries to get inside the couple's secrets even as she starts to fall for them herself.   Tag was generally well-received by critics. Rotten Tomatoes has a 73% rating with an average rating of 6/10 based on 22 reviews.
