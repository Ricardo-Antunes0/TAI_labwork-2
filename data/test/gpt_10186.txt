Abderrazak Belagrouz  is an Algerian academic and author who specializes in international relations. He is also a critic of the Algerian government.  Belagrouz was born in 1962 in Oran, Algeria. He has a doctorate from the University of Paris IV-Sorbonne, where he also received his licentiate in philosophy.  Belagrouz began his career as a lecturer at the University of Algiers, where he later became an associate professor. From 2002 to 2003, he served as Algeria's ambassador to France. In 2004, he was appointed director of the Arab Centre for Research and Strategic Studies (CARSS), a position he held until 2007.  Belagrouz is a critic of the Algerian government and its human rights record. He has also written on international relations.
