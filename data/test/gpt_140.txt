The Beijing Olympic Tower  is located in the heart of Beijing, China. The tower was designed by the architectural firm of Foster and Partners and was completed in 2006. The tower is located just a few hundred meters from the iconic Bird's Nest Stadium.  The Beijing Olympic Tower is a 555-meter high, ultra-modern skyscraper that is an important part of the cityscape of Beijing. The tower is a key part of the city's new business district and is used for office space, retail stores, apartments, and hotels.  The tower has a floor area of 1.2 million square meters and contains over 1,000 luxury apartments, as well as a hotel with 1,500 rooms. The tower also contains a gym, a spa, restaurants, and a cinema. The tower is popular with tourists because it offers great views of the city skyline.
