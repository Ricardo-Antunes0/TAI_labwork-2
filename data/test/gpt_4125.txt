Mary Theresa Brunner (born December 17, 1943) is an American author of suspense, horror and mystery novels. She has written more than fifty books, including the popular suspense novels The Girl Who Kicked the Hornet's Nest and Dragnet.  Brunner was born in 1943 in Binghamton, New York, to a Catholic family. After graduating from college, she worked as a journalist and then as a public relations executive. She began writing fiction in the early 1980s and has since become one of the most successful authors in the genre. Her books have been translated into more than thirty languages and sold more than fifty million copies worldwide. Brunner currently lives in southern California.
