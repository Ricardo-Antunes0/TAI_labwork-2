Born in Buenos Aires, Rodolfo Irazusta studied at the University of Buenos Aires and then at the University of Heidelberg, where he obtained his doctorate in 1928. He taught Literature at the University of Buenos Aires from 1928 to 1951, and then at the University of Cuyo from 1951 until his death.
