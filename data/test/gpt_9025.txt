Qatar Sports Investments (QSi) is a closed-end investment company based in Qatar. It was founded in 2009 by Sheikh Khalid bin Hamad Al Thani, the son of Qatar's former emir Sheikh Hamad bin Khalifa Al Thani.  QSi is best known for its ownership of the French football club Paris Saint-Germain F.C., which it bought in May 2013 for a reported $360 million. The company also owns a number of other sports clubs, including Olympique de Marseille, Valencia CF, and Atletico Madrid, as well as a number of media properties.
