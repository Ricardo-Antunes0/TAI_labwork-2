The Mahak Society to Support Children with Cancer (Mahak) is a Canadian not-for-profit organization that provides support, resources and advocacy to families affected by cancer.  Founded in 1993, Mahak is the largest organization dedicated to supporting families affected by cancer in Canada. Mahak operates a national network of chapters and affiliates, as well as a toll-free helpline and online resource center. Mahak offers support to families through programs and services such as free booklets and information sessions, financial assistance, bereavement support groups, educational materials for caregivers, social events and more. The organization also works to raise awareness about cancer and its prevention among the public. In total, Mahak has helped support more than 10,000 families since its inception.
