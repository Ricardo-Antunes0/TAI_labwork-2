2033 also billed as 2033: Future Apocalypse is a 2014 American post-apocalyptic science fiction film directed by Robert Zemeckis. The screenplay by Zemeckis, Jack Thorne, and Dean Parisot is based on the novel of the same name by Philip K. Dick. It stars James Franco, Julianne Moore, and Josh Brolin.   In the future, the world is a bleak and dangerous place. The United States has collapsed into a feudal society ruled by a single ruler, while Russia and China have become powerful empires. The only hope for the future is to find a way to travel back in time to before the collapse, and prevent it from happening.  2033 was released in theaters on October 14, 2014. It received mixed reviews from critics. However, it was commercially successful, grossing $306 million worldwide.
