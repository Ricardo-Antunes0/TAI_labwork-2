Huntington Prep or Huntington St. Joseph Prep is an all-male Jesuit Catholic boarding school in West Hollywood, California, United States. It was founded in 1876 by the Society of Jesus. The school has an enrollment of about 1,100 students from over 40 countries and offers a four-year program leading to a Bachelor of Arts degree.  Huntington Prep is one of the most selective schools in the United States and consistently ranks as one of the best schools in the country. Students come from all levels of society and many have accomplished great things, such as billionaires, CEOs, actors, athletes, and journalists.
