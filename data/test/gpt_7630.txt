The Adelaide Fringe, formerly Adelaide Fringe Festival, is an annual arts festival that takes place in Adelaide, Australia. First held in 2002, the Adelaide Fringe currently features over 200 performances in over 50 venues across the city.  The Adelaide Fringe is one of Australia's most celebrated and acclaimed arts festivals, presenting world-class theatre, dance, music, comedy and art exhibitions from both local and international artists. The Fringe features a wide range of genres and styles, with performances for all ages and interests.  The Adelaide Fringe Festival is run by an independent not-for-profit organisation, Fringe SA. The Festival is dedicated to promoting creativity and artistic expression through the medium of performance arts.
