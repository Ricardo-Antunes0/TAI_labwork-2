Cerebellar degeneration is a condition in which cerebellar function deteriorates over time. It can cause problems with balance, coordination, and movement. Cerebellar degeneration can be caused by a number of different factors, including age, injury, and disease. There is currently no cure for cerebellar degeneration, but there are treatments available that can help improve the symptoms.
