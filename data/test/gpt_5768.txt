Grzegorz Małecki (born 18 October 1967) – a Polish journalist, author and publicist.  Born in Kraków, Małecki graduated from the University of Warsaw in 1995 with a degree in political science. He has worked as a correspondent for the Polish news agency PAP in Moscow and for the Polish edition of Newsweek magazine. He is the author of eight books, including two volumes of memoirs.
