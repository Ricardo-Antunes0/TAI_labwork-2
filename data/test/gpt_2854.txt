Shenzhen Middle School , commonly referred as Shenzhen Middle School (SMS) is a public middle school in Shenzhen, Guangdong, China. It was founded in 1957 as a junior high school.    Shenzhen Middle School has a history of over 57 years. It started as a junior high school in 1957. In 1981, it moved to its current location on Jiefang Road and became a full-scale public middle school. In 2006, the school was approved to become one of the top 100 schools in the country.  The Shenzhen Middle School campus covers an area of 447,000 square meters and has a enrollment of over 10,000 students. The school provides education for students from grade 6 to grade 12. The school has 132 faculty and staff.   The Shenzhen Middle School curriculum is based on the National Curriculum for Secondary Schools in China. The school offers 26 courses, including 10 courses in core subjects and 16 courses that are chosen from other areas such as arts, sports, technology, etc.   The Shenzhen Middle School motto is "Caring for each other; Dedication to learning."
