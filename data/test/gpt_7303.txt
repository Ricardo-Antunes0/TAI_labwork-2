Feminine Fancy is a studio album by American singer-songwriter Sia, released on November 20, 2018, by Interscope Records. It is her seventh studio album and first in five years.  Sia wrote and recorded the album over a period of two years in various locations around the world, with producers Greg Kurstin, Benny Blanco, and Shellback. The album was preceded by the release of the single "The Greatest" on September 28, 2018.  "Feminine Fancy" is a concept album about female identity and self-love. It features songs about empowerment, self-acceptance, and self-worth. The album explores themes such as body autonomy, mental health, and sexual liberation.  Sia has said that "Feminine Fancy" is her most personal album to date. She described it as "about becoming your own person and not letting anyone else dictate who you are or what you should do".  "Feminine Fancy" received widespread acclaim from critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has an average score of 84, based on 12 reviews.   The album topped the US "Billboard" 200 chart for two consecutive weeks and has sold over 1 million copies in the US as of November 2019. It also reached number one in Australia, Canada, France, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden and the UK.
