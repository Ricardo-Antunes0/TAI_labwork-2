Francísco Ximénez (November 28, 1666 – c.1729) was a Spanish Golden Age painter who specialized in religious and mythological subjects.  Ximénez was born in Valencia, a city in the province of Valencia, in 1666. He studied at the School of the Jesuits in Valencia, under the tutelage of Francisco Pacheco. In 1689 he moved to Madrid, where he became a member of the Royal Academy of Painting and Sculpture. He died in 1729.
