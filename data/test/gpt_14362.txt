Nishikunisaki District was located in the southeastern part of the prefecture. The district included the towns of Nishikunisaki and Matsumoto (both from Nishikunisaki District), the village of Oba (from Ōita Prefecture), and the towns of Kuwana and Kamioka (both from Ōita City).
