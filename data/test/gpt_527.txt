The 33rd G8 summit was held at Lough Erne in Northern Ireland from 13-15 June 2013.  The G8 is an international forum of the world's eight leading industrialized countries: the United States, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and Russia. The group met annually to discuss and address global economic issues. The summit was first convened in 1975 and has been held at different locations throughout the world. The 2013 summit was hosted by the United Kingdom and was attended by President Barack Obama and Prime Minister David Cameron of the United Kingdom, Chancellor Angela Merkel of Germany, Prime Minister Matteo Renzi of Italy, King Abdullah II of Jordan, and President Xi Jinping of China.
