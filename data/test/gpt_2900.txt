The Kingdom Holding Company  is a multinational investment holding company headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The company was founded in 2005 by Prince Al Waleed bin Talal.  The Kingdom Holding Company is engaged in a wide range of businesses including retail, tourism, telecommunications, media, banking, and real estate. The company also has investments in a number of multinational companies such as Apple, Amazon, and Starbucks. The Kingdom Holding Company has a portfolio of investments that totals over $100 billion.   The Kingdom Holding Company is one of the world's largest private investors. The company has invested in a number of high-profile projects including the construction of the Kingdom Tower in Riyadh and the redevelopment of the Mecca Royal Mosque. The company also owns a large stake in Twitter.
