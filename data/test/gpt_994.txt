The Discovery District is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the city of Portland, Oregon. The district is located in the heart of downtown and encompasses nearly two square miles. It is known for its many museums, galleries, parks, and restaurants.   The district was originally conceived in the early 1800s as a commercial center for the city. However, it did not become a popular tourist destination until after World War II. In the 1960s, the district began to experience a resurgence in popularity as a result of its proximity to the downtown core and its many cultural attractions. Today, the Discovery District is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the United States and is known for its unique architecture and history.
