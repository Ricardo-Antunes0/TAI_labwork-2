Alessio Benedetti (born 19 May 1990) is an Italian professional footballer who plays for Serie A club Napoli as a central defender.  Born in Avellino, Campania, Benedetti started his career with hometown club Avellino. He made his Serie B debut in the 2008–09 season, and played for Avellino in the Lega Pro Prima Divisione until 2012. In January 2012, Benedetti was signed by Serie A club Catania on a six-month loan deal. He made his Serie A debut on 30 January 2012, in a 2–1 away defeat against Roma.  On 1 July 2012, Benedetti was signed by Napoli for €600,000 on a five-year contract. He made his Napoli debut on 10 August 2012, in a 4–1 home victory over Chievo. In the 2013–14 season, he played 28 times in the Serie A and scored two goals. On 7 May 2014, he was named Napoli captain by new manager Rafa Benitez. In the 2014–15 season, he played 34 times in the Serie A and scored one goal. On 8 May 2015, he helped Napoli to win the Coppa Italia Final against Juventus after he scored a goal in the penalty shootout.  Benedetti has represented Italy at under-21 level.
