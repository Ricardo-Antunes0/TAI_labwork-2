Staff Sergeant Olaf  Sean George Schmid, OC (born October 1, 1969) is a Canadian Forces officer and para-athlete who has won six Paralympic gold medals in three different events.  Olaf Schmid was born on October 1, 1969, in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. He is a staff sergeant in the Canadian Forces and has competed in six Paralympic Games. Schmid has won six gold medals in three different Paralympic events: the 400 metres T42 event, the 800 metres T52 event, and the 1500 metres T54 event. Schmid has also won two silver medals in the 800 metres T52 event and the 1500 metres T54 event.
