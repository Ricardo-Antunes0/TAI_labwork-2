Rong Kluea Market , also known as the Night Bazaar, is a large open-air market in the Old Quarter of Vientiane, Laos. The market is located on the north side of the city near Wat Phokaeng.   The Rong Kluea Market is one of the most famous markets in Laos and is known for its cheap and abundant goods. The market is open every day except Monday, and it is busiest on Saturdays and Sundays.
