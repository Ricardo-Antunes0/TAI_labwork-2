It was released on March 8, 2018, by FNC Entertainment.   The album was produced by the band's frontman Jung Yong-hwa and longtime collaborator and record producer Kim Do-hyun. The album's lead single, "I Love You", was released on January 18, 2018.  Chiljip Psy-da has received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 71, based on 10 reviews.  Mark Deming of "AllMusic" said that "Chiljip Psy-da" "shows that [CNBLUE] have lost none of their energy or their ability to write catchy hooks", and praised the album's "stronger songs" and "powerful vocals".
