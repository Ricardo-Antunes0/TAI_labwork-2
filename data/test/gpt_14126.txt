Happy Feet Two is a 2011 computer-animated musical film produced by the Australian studio Blue Tongue Entertainment and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The sequel to the 2006 film Happy Feet, it is directed by George Miller and written by Phil Johnston and David Howard. The film stars Elijah Wood, Robin Williams, Jason Sudeikis, and Elle Fanning and continues the story of an penguin named Mumble (Wood) who joins a troupe of penguins on their journey to find a new home.  Happy Feet Two was released in Australia on November 18, 2011, in the United States on December 14, 2011, and in the United Kingdom on December 18, 2011. It grossed over $484 million worldwide and received critical acclaim, with praise going to the animation, music, humor, and characters. A spin-off film, Happy Feet Three, was released on March 14, 2016.
