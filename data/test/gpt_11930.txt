Pascimāmnāya Śrī Śāradā Pītham or Dwarka Sharada Peetham is a Śāradā Peetham located in Dwarka, New Delhi, India. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Sharada.  The temple is believed to have been built by the Pandavas in the form of a chariot. It is also said that the deity of the temple, Goddess Sharada, manifested herself from the navel of Lord Vishnu.
