Hankou–Yichang railway , or Hanyi railway, is a railway that connects Hankou, Hubei Province and Yichang, Hubei Province in China. The railway is part of the Wuhan–Chengdu railway.  The Hankou–Yichang railway was built between 1909 and 1915 as part of the Wuhan–Chengdu Railway. Construction began in 1909 and the line was opened to traffic on December 28, 1915. The Hankou–Yichang railway is long and has a total of of track. The line is operated by the Hanyi Railway Administration.
