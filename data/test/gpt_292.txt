There have been two baronetcies created for persons with the surname Hanmer. The first creation was in the Baronetage of England on 21 December 1611 for Sir Edward Hanmer. The second was in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom on 3 July 1876 for Admiral George Hanmer.   Both creations were for members of the Hanmer family, a prominent English aristocratic family. The first creation was for Sir Edward Hanmer, who was the son of Sir Thomas Hanmer, 1st Baronet and stepson of Sir John Popham. The second creation was for Admiral George Hanmer, who was the grandson of Admiral George Hanmer, 1st Baronet and son of Admiral Thomas Hanmer, 1st Baronet. Both creations were repealed on 8 December 1958.
