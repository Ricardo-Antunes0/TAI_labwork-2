Renzo Andrés Reggiardo Barreto (born 1 July 1977) is a Uruguayan professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Argentine club Lanús.  Reggiardo began his career with Defensor Sporting in Uruguay. He moved to Europe in 2000, and signed with Dutch club PSV Eindhoven. He spent two seasons with PSV, winning the 2002–03 Eredivisie. Reggiardo then transferred to Spanish club Valencia, where he won the Copa del Rey in 2006 and the La Liga championship in 2007. After two years with Valencia, Reggiardo joined Italian side Napoli, where he won the Coppa Italia in 2009. He was Napoli's first-choice goalkeeper from 2009 to 2013, before joining French team Monaco, where he won the Ligue 1 title in 2014 and the Coupe de France in 2015.  In January 2017, Reggiardo joined Argentine club Lanús.
