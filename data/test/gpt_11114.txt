Mindaugas Ežerskis (born 2 August 1977 in Kaunas) is a Lithuanian professional basketball player who last played for Žalgiris Kaunas. He is 6'2" (1.88 m) tall and he weighs 178 pounds (81 kg).  Ežerskis started his professional career with BC Žalgiris in 1995. From 2001 to 2003, he played with BC Lietuvos Rytas. In 2004, he moved to the Italian team Reggio Emilia. He also played for the Spanish team Caja Laboral in 2006-2007. In 2007, he joined Žalgiris Kaunas. He was named the Lithuanian League Player of the Year in 2010 and 2011.
