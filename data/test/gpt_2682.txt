Hamlet Watling (born Kelsale, Suffolk, 1818, died London, 1894) was an English painter and illustrator who specialized in the production of theatrical and advertising posters. He is best known for his illustrations of Shakespeare's plays, particularly Hamlet.  Hamlet Watling was born in 1818 in Kelsale, Suffolk, England. He was educated at the Royal Academy of Arts in London and became a professional painter and illustrator. Watling specialized in the production of theatrical and advertising posters and is best known for his illustrations of Shakespeare's plays, particularly Hamlet. Watling died in London in 1894.
