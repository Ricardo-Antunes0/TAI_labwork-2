The Electric Confectionaires were a four-piece musical group from Liverpool, England, who were active in the early 1960s. The group was formed in 1962 by brothers Tony and Terry Jones (keyboards and vocals), Michael Green (guitar), and John Simpson (bass). The group released two singles, "Gonna Tell My Mama" and "Swinging on a Star", before splitting up in 1964.
