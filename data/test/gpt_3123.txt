Taras Hryhorovych Shevchenko , also known as Taras Shevchenko National Monument , is a Ukrainian poet, author, and painter. He is considered to be one of the founders of the modern Ukrainian literary movement. Taras Shevchenko is celebrated for his poetry and paintings. His poems often explore the beauty of rural life and the Ukrainian people's traditional culture. His painting style is highly emotive and often captures the intensity of natural scenes. Taras Shevchenko is also renowned for his strong advocacy of Ukrainian nationalism and for his role in the struggle for Ukrainian independence from Russia.
