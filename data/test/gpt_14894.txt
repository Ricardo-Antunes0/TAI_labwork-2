Lome Fa'atau (born 23 October 1975 in Lome, Togo) is a Togolese football midfielder who is currently playing for AS Dragon in the Tunisian Ligue Professionnelle 2.  Fa'atau began his career with hometown club Lome, before moving to France to play for FC Metz. In 1999, he moved again, this time to Saudi Arabia to play for Al-Nassr. He returned to Togo in 2001, and played for CR Belouizdad and ASEC Mimosas before joining AS Dragon in 2006.  Fa'atau has made appearances for the Togolese national team, and was a member of the team that competed at the 2006 FIFA World Cup. He also played at the Africa Cup of Nations in 2002 and 2006.
