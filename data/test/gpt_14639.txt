May Sun (born 1954) is a Los Angeles-based American artist whose sculptures are created from large, brightly colored pieces of fabric.  Born in 1954, May Sun's art is inspired by brightly colored pieces of fabric. Her sculptures are created from large pieces of fabric that are then pieced together to create visually stunning works of art. Sun's work has been featured in many exhibitions and her pieces can be found in museums and private collections around the world.
