Little Anita's is a Mexican and New American restaurant in the heart of the arts district of downtown Los Angeles, California.  Opened in 2009 by Mexican-born chef Anita Lo and American restaurateur Zachary Golub, Little Anita's is a contemporary Mexican cuisine restaurant that has quickly become one of the most popular restaurants in the downtown arts district.  The restaurant's menu features a variety of contemporary Mexican dishes, including innovative takes on classic dishes such as carnitas tacos and mole enchiladas. The wine list features both Mexican and international wines, and the cocktails are crafted with fresh ingredients and unique flavors.  Little Anita's is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, who come for the unique atmosphere, delicious food, and interesting wine list.
