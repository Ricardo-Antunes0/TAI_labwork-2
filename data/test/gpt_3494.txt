Adam Dorrel (born December 2, 1974) is an American actor and comedian.  Dorrel was born in Lubbock, Texas, to a Catholic family. He attended the University of Texas at Austin, where he met future "Saturday Night Live" cast member Horatio Sanz. Dorrel and Sanz formed a comedy duo and performed at the Uproar Festival in 1994.  Dorrel's first major role came in the movie "Reality Bites". He has since appeared in roles in films such as "The Waterboy", "The Whole Nine Yards", and "The Pacifier". Dorrel also appeared on "The Tonight Show with Jay Leno" and "The Late Show with David Letterman".  Dorrel currently stars in the CBS sitcom "The New Adventures of Old Christine".
