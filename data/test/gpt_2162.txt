Three vessels of the Royal Navy have borne the name HMS Coureur:   The first HMS Coureur was a 100-gun third rate ship of the line, launched in 1792. She served in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars, before being sold in 1814.  The second HMS Coureur was a 98-gun fourth rate ship of the line, launched in 1812. She served in the Napoleonic Wars and the War of 1812, before being sold in 1827.  The third HMS Coureur was an 80-gun sixth rate ship of the line, launched in 1824. She served in the Napoleonic Wars and the War of 1812, before being sold in 1865.
