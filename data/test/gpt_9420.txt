İbrahim Türkkan (born 11 May 1983 in Sinop, Turkey) is a Turkish footballer who last played for Karabükspor.  Türkkan started his career with Sinop in 2001. He then moved to Fenerbahçe in 2002 and played for the club until 2006. In 2007, he transferred to Galatasaray and played for the club until 2010. He then transferred to Trabzonspor and played for the club until 2013. In 2014, he transferred to Karabükspor and played for the club until 2016.
