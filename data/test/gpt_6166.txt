Robert Andrzej Varin (born on May 1, 1948) is a Polish-American mathematician known for his work in algebra, functional analysis, and mathematical physics.  Varin was born in Warsaw, Poland, to Andrzej and Maria Varin. He received his undergraduate degree from the University of Warsaw in 1969 and his graduate degrees from the University of California, Berkeley in 1973 and the University of Paris-Sud in 1978.  Varin has been a professor of mathematics at Yale University since 1978, where he is also a Sterling Professor of Mathematical Sciences. He has been a editor of the "Journal of Mathematical Physics" since 1985 and a fellow of the American Mathematical Society since 1989.
