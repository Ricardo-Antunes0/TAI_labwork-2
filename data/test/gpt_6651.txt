Nicola Minichiello (born Nicola Gautier on 21 January 1990) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Ligue 1 club Marseille.  Minichiello began his career at hometown club Le Mans, before moving to Marseille in 2010. He spent two seasons with the club before joining Amiens on loan in 2013. After making a permanent switch to the club, Minichiello helped them reach the Coupe de France final in 2017.
