Richard S. Jaffe (born February 27, 1950) is an American business executive who is the current president and chief executive officer (CEO) of PepsiCo.  Jaffe was born in Baltimore, Maryland. After graduating from high school, he received a bachelor's degree in business administration from the University of Maryland in 1972. Jaffe then worked for a variety of companies, including Procter & Gamble and Borden, before joining PepsiCo in 1984 as its vice president of marketing. He became PepsiCo's CEO in 2000 and was named chairman of the board in 2007. Jaffe is a member of the National Board of Directors of the Boy Scouts of America. He also serves on the boards of directors for the private equity firm The Blackstone Group and the health care company Cigna.
