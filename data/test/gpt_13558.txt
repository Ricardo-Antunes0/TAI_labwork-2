Sean Hedges-Quinn is a British sculptor, animator, and artist, who has been working in the field of animation since the early 1980s.  Hedges-Quinn's early animation work was primarily on short films, including shorts for BBC Animation and Terry Gilliam's Time Bandits. He also worked on the popular British children's television show The Wiggles. More recently, Hedges-Quinn has focused on creating character animation for feature films and TV commercials.  Hedges-Quinn was born in England in 1957. He attended the Slade School of Fine Art in London and graduated with a BA in 1978. He then attended the Royal College of Art in London, where he received his MA in 1982.
