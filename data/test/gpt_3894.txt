Full Moon High is a 1981 American slasher film directed by Larry Cohen and written by Cohen and his then-wife Cathy Konrad. The film stars Jessica Biel, Corey Feldman, and John Cusack.  The film tells the story of three high school students who are stalked and killed by a masked killer during a full moon.  The film was released on March 20, 1981, and was met with mixed reviews. It has since become a cult classic.
