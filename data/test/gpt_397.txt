Bunocephalus is a genus of banjo catfishes found in tropical and subtropical waters of the eastern and central Indo-Pacific. The genus contains six recognized species, all of which are popular aquarium fishes. These slender, elongated fish grow to a length of about 2.5 inches (6.4 cm).
