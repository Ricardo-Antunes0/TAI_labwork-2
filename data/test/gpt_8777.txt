Adakkakundu is a village in Kalikavu Panchayat, Kozhikode district, Kerala, India.  Adakkakundu is a village in Kalikavu Panchayat, Kozhikode district, Kerala, India. It is located about 5 km from Kozhikode city centre. The place is famous for its black rocks and is a tourist spot. The Malabar Coast Road passes through the village. There are many small and big shops in the village. There are two banks in the village and a post office. The population of the village was 2,271 in 2001 census.
