Tarantism is the debut album by Los Angeles-based indie rock band Tarantism. It was released on October 9, 2009, through Paper Bag Records.  The album was recorded at Audiohammer Studios in Oakland, California and produced by the band's drummer, Tim Sult. It was mixed by John Congleton at The Observatory in Santa Ana, California.  The album has been well received by music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 80, based on 11 reviews.  The album's first single, "Death Club", was released on July 10, 2009 and reached number 73 on the "Billboard" Hot 100. The second single, "Inertia Creeps", was released on September 18, 2009 and reached number 96 on the same chart.
