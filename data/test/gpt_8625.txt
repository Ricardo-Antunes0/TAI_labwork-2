Michel Ferlus (born 1935) is a French sculptor, painter and writer.  Ferlus was born in 1935 in the city of Lyon, France. He is a sculptor, painter and writer. Ferlus has exhibited his work in France, Switzerland, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Germany, The United States of America, Canada and China. He has been awarded the Prix de Rome in sculpture in 1978 and the Grand Prix National de la Jeunesse in 1989.
