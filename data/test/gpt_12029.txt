Yuvraaj Parashar is an Indian Bollywood model and actor who has appeared in numerous Hindi-language films. He made his acting debut in the 2003 drama film "Zabardast". He has since starred in several commercially successful films, including the box office hits "Chalte Chalte" (2007), "Dabangg" (2010), and "Bang Bang" (2015).   Parashar was born on 20 October 1984 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He was discovered by a model scout while he was working as a salesman at a computer showroom in Mumbai. He made his debut in 2003 with the drama film "Zabardast". From 2006 to 2008, Parashar appeared on the television show "Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs". During this time, he also modeled for several advertisements.  Parashar's breakthrough came with the 2007 action-comedy film "Chalte Chalte". The film became a blockbuster hit and established him as an actor. He subsequently starred in several other commercially successful films, including the 2010 comedy film "Dabangg" and the 2015 action film "Bang Bang". In 2017, he starred in the biographical drama film "Padmaavat".
