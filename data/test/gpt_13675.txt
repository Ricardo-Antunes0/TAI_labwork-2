Jauréguiberry was a pre-dreadnought battleship constructed for the French Navy in the early 1890s. She was laid down at the Arsenal de Toulon in 1888 and launched in 1890. She was armed with a main battery of four guns and had a maximum speed of 26 knots. Jauréguiberry served with the French Navy for just two years, before being stricken from the naval register in 1892. After her retirement from active service, she was used as a hulk until she was scrapped in 1907.
