Joyce Spencer, also known as DJ Jazzy Joyce, is an American disc jockey, record producer, and remixer. She is best known for her work as a turntablist and hip hop producer, most notable for her work with the Beastie Boys and Missy Elliott.  Joyce Spencer was born in Boston on July 10, 1971. She started DJing in high school, and also began producing music in the early 1990s. Joyce's first big break came when she produced the Beastie Boys' debut album Licensed to Ill. Following that success, she produced albums for Missy Elliott, The Notorious B.I.G., and others. Joyce has also released several solo albums and remixes, as well as collaborating with a variety of artists including Jay-Z, Lil Jon, and will.i.am. Joyce currently resides in New York City.
