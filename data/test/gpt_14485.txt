Hélène de Portes, born  Hélène Rebuffel, (born October 1, 1912, date of death unknown) was a French actress.  Hélène de Portes was born in Paris on October 1, 1912, the daughter of a banker. She made her stage debut in 1935, and quickly became a leading lady of the French stage. She appeared in films in the 1940s and 1950s, but is best remembered for her roles in the films of Louis Malle. She died in obscurity, probably from a heart attack, in unknown circumstances.
