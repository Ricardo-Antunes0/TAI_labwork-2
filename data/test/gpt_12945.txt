BC Canis Minoris is a variable star in the constellation Canis Minor. It is approximately 120 light years from Earth.  The star is a B-type main sequence star with a stellar classification of B8 V. It is about two and a half times the size of the sun and has about 1.2 times the mass. It is radiating about 165 times the Sun's luminosity from its atmosphere at an effective temperature of 5,500 K.
