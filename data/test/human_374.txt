Harsh Kumar is ophthalmologist, who is reported to have described nine laser surgical procedures for glaucoma and anterior segment of which one procedure is cited by Shields Text Book of Glaucoma, a known reference book on glaucoma management.He is the director of Glaucoma Services at Centre for Sight, New Delhi and also serves at the Indraprastha Apollo Hospitals, Delhi. He was honoured by the Government of India in 2015 with Padma Shri, the fourth highest Indian civilian award. Biography Harsh Kumar graduated in medicine (MBBS) from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, secured his master's degree (MD) in ophthalmology from Dr. Rajendra Prasad Centre for Ophthalmic Sciences, both with gold medals and did senior residency at R. P. Centre to pass out with the best senior resident award. He joined his alma mater as a member of faculty and served the institution as an additional professor of the glaucoma unit. He works at Centre for Sight, Delhi as the director of Glaucoma unit and at the Apollo Hospital, Indraprastha, as a consultant surgeon.
