O'Doherty is a surname, part of the name O'Doherty, Irish Gaelic O'Doherty, meaning "descendant of Doherty". Notable people with the surname include:   1. Maurice O'Doherty (1879-1947), Irish revolutionary and politician, leader of the Easter Rising 1916 2. Rory O'Doherty (1953-), Irish comedian and actor 3. Brendan O'Doherty (born 1971), Irish author and journalist  4. Martin O'Doherty (born 1966), Irish author
