Katargam is a suburb in Surat city, Gujarat, India. It is one of the newer suburbs of Surat and is expanding rapidly.  Katargam is located on the eastern edge of Surat city, at a distance of 8 km from the city centre. The suburb is spread over an area of around 203 acres. Katargam is well connected with Surat by various modes of transportation. It has an established market with basic amenities like hospitals, schools and shopping complexes.
