Katherine Irma Davis Mayfair is a fictional character, a supervillainess appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby and first appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #14 (July 1964).  Katherine Mayfair first appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #14 (July 1964), and was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby. She is a supervillainess appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.  The character is a member of the Sinister Six, a team of six super villains consisting of Doctor Octopus, Electro, Mysterio, Sandman, Super-Skrull, and Venom. Katherine is the sister of Peter Parker's archenemy, Norman Osborn. The character has appeared in various incarnations, most notably as the villainous Katherine 'Kitty' Pryde in the X-Men animated series.
