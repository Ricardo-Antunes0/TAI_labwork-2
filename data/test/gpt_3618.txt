Silent Souls (Russian: Овсянки, "The Buntings") is a Russian rock band from Saint Petersburg, formed in early 2006. The band plays post-hardcore and screamo with a heavy metal influence.  Silent Souls was formed in 2006 in Saint Petersburg, Russia. The band's members are vocalist and guitarist Denis Kuzmin, bassist Vladimir Aksyonov, and drummer Alexei Kuzmin. Silent Souls is known for its heavy metal influenced screamo music. The band's debut album, "Killed By Words", was released in 2009.
