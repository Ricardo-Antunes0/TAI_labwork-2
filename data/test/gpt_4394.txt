The Amazon molly (Poecilia formosa) is a species of fish in the family Poeciliidae. It is found in the Amazon and Orinoco River basins in South America.  The Amazon molly is a small, colorful fish that can reach a length of 18 inches (45 cm). They are popular as aquarium fish and can be found in many pet stores. They are considered to be a species of "molly" because they are born without a backbone, and so they are able to move around easily in water.
