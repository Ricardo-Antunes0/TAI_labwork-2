Isa Melikov (, born 21 October 1980, in Shumen) is a Bulgarian football midfielder. He currently plays for Litex Lovech in the Bulgarian First League.  Isa Melikov was born on 21 October 1980 in the town of Shumen, Bulgaria. He started his career with local club Pirin Blagoevgrad before moving to Litex Lovech in 2002. After helping Litex to promotion to the Bulgarian First League, he earned himself a move to Second Division side Cherno More Varna in 2005. He made his debut for Cherno More on 12 September 2005 in a 2-1 victory over Velbazhd Pechora. Isa Melikov went on to make 44 appearances for Cherno More, scoring 5 goals before departing at the end of the 2006-07 season. Isa Melikov then signed for newly promoted Akademik Sofia.  Isa Melikov has represented Bulgaria at Under-21 level and made one appearance for the senior team in a 2006 World Cup qualifier against Spain.
