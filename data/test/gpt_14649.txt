Václav "Vinny" Prospal (born February 17, 1975) is a Czech professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC České Budějovice of the Czech Extraliga (ELH).  Prospal was drafted in the second round, 45th overall, by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1994 NHL Entry Draft. He played with HC Slavia Praha and HC České Budějovice before joining the Vancouver Canucks in 2002. He spent three seasons with Vancouver before being traded to the Montreal Canadiens for a fifth-round draft pick in 2004. After two seasons with Montreal, Prospal was traded to the Calgary Flames for a fourth-round draft pick in 2006. He spent two seasons with Calgary before being traded to the Florida Panthers for a third-round draft pick in 2008. Prospal signed a two-year contract with HC České Budějovice on July 2, 2009.
