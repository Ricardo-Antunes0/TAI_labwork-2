Bumblescratch is an original sung-through musical with a book by Tony-winner Jerry Zaks (The Sunshine Boys, The Producers) and music and lyrics by Tony Award winner Glenn Slater (The Bridges of Madison County, The Heiress).  Bumblescratch tells the story of two mismatched siblings who find themselves bound together by their love for one another – and their uncontrollable laughter. When their parents die suddenly, the siblings are left to fend for themselves in a cruel and unforgiving world. But as they learn to trust each other and laugh at the absurdity of life, they discover that family is the most precious thing of all.  Bumblescratch is a joyous celebration of the power of laughter – and the strength of family.
