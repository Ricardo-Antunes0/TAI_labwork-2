John Charles Kunich is an adjunct professor of theology at Princeton Theological Seminary. He is the author of numerous books, including "The Mystery of God: History, Doctrine, and Worship" (1998), "Prayer: A History" (2004), and "Theology in an Age of Revolution: A New Vision for Christian Living" (2011).  Born in Chicago, Illinois in 1942, John Charles Kunich attended the University of Notre Dame before receiving his B.A. from Yale University in 1964 and his M.A. from Yale Divinity School in 1965. He served as a Presbyterian pastor from 1965-1971, after which he became an adjunct professor of theology at Princeton Theological Seminary. He is the author of numerous books, including "The Mystery of God: History, Doctrine, and Worship" (1998), "Prayer: A History" (2004), and "Theology in an Age of Revolution: A New Vision for Christian Living" (2011).
