Hugh Richard Edward Tomlinson QC (born January 15, 1957) is a British barrister and retired judge who served as the Lord Chief Justice of England and Wales from 2012 to 2016. He also served as a Law Lord from 2009 to 2012.  Born in 1957, Hugh Tomlinson was educated at Eton College and Trinity College, Cambridge, where he read law. He was called to the Bar at Inner Temple in 1980 and became a QC in 1994. He practised law initially with the firm of Latham & Watkins, before becoming a judge in the Crown Court in 1998. He served as a Judge of the High Court of Justice from 2006 to 2009, and then as a Law Lord from 2009 to 2012. On 3 July 2012, he was appointed Lord Chief Justice of England and Wales, taking office on 1 October 2012. He retired on 31 December 2016.  Tomlinson has been married since 1985 to Clare Wood; they have two children.
