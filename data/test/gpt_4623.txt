The Szymański's Mutual Exclusion Algorithm is a mutual exclusion algorithm used to solve the two-person Prisoner's Dilemma. It is named after its discoverer, Władysław Szymański. The algorithm works by first solving the problem for one player, and then using that solution to determine how to best solve the problem for the second player.
