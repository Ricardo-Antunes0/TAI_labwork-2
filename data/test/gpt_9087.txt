The 1987 NSWRL season was the 80th season of the New South Wales Rugby League (NSWRL). Eight teams competed for the J.J. Giltinan Shield and Premiership, with the Parramatta Eels winning the premiership.  The season saw the Wentworthville Magpies make their first grand final appearance, losing to the Canberra Raiders. The Newcastle Knights also reached the grand final, but lost to the Canterbury-Bankstown Bulldogs.
