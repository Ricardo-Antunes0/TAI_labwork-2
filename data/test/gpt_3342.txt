Vincent Gaston Dethier (February 20, 1915 – February 25, 1990) was a French philosopher who is best known for his work on phenomenology and existentialism.  Vincent Dethier was born in 1915 in Châlons-en-Champagne, France. After studying at the University of Paris and the University of Strasbourg, he became a professor at the latter institution in 1954. He died in 1990.
