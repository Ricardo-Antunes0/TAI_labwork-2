Connie Regan-Blake (born January 20, 1947) is an American actress, singer and dancer.  Born in Kingston, New York, Regan-Blake is best remembered for her role as Lisa Garland on the ABC television series "The Brady Bunch" from 1969 to 1974. She also starred in the NBC television series "The New Dick Van Dyke Show" from 1971 to 1972 and in the CBS television series "The Waltons" from 1972 to 1983.  Regan-Blake also appeared in numerous films and TV shows throughout her career, including roles in "The Towering Inferno", "The Godfather Part II", "The Breakfast Club", "Night of the Living Dead", "Hellboy", "Altered States", "Fargo", "Die Hard", and "Tootsie".
