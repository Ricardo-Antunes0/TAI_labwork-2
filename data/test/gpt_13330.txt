Straightneck squash is a cultivated variety of squash that is a cross between the zucchini and the Hubbard squash. It has a very slender neck and a straight shape.  Straightneck squash are popular for their uniform, slender shape and sweet flavor. They are easy to grow and can be harvested early in the season when they are small. Straightneck squash can be eaten cooked or raw, and can be used in a variety of recipes.
