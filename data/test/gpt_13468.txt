Bad Witch is the ninth studio album by American metal band Pentagram. It was released on November 3, 2017, through AFM Records.  Bad Witch is the ninth studio album by American metal band Pentagram. The album was produced by Dave Otero and recorded at his Black Lodge Studios in North Hollywood, California. It was released on November 3, 2017, through AFM Records.  The album is a return to the more traditional heavy metal sound of Pentagram's earlier work, with songs that are "full-on" and "in-your-face". According to guitarist Scott Rockenfield, "It was important that we went back to basics and wrote songs that we ourselves would want to listen to."
