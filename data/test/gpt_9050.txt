Tavares Lamont Taylor (born January 6, 1993), better known by his stage name Lil Reese, is an American rapper. He is signed to Young Money Entertainment and Cash Money Records, and is best known for his single "Freestyle".  Lil Reese was born in Atlanta, Georgia. He started rapping at the age of 10 and was discovered by Birdman and Lil Wayne at a talent show. Reese made his recording debut on the mixtape Dedication 3, released in 2011. In 2012, he released the single "Freestyle" which peaked at number 50 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100.
