Virgil Matthew Vasquez (born June 7, 1982) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Arizona Diamondbacks of Major League Baseball (MLB).  Vasquez attended Bishop Gorman High School in Las Vegas, Nevada, and played college baseball at the University of California, Davis. He was drafted by the Diamondbacks in the fifth round of the 2002 Major League Baseball draft. Vasquez made his MLB debut with Arizona in 2006. He has spent most of his career with the Diamondbacks, but has also played for the Baltimore Orioles and Houston Astros.
