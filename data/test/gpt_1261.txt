Enzo is an Italian given name derivative from the Latin word "hēnārus", meaning "of the wild boar". The name has been in use since Roman times. There are many variants of the name, including: Enzo, Enrique, Endre, Endri, Endrew, Ensar, Enzar, Enzio.   The name is not particularly popular in Italy but is more common in other countries such as Spain and France. It is also quite popular in the United States. The name was ranked at #827 on the 2009 list of U.S. baby names by the Social Security Administration.
