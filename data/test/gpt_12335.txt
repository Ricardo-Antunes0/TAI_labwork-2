Weisstein was born in 1939 in Brooklyn, New York to Russian Jewish immigrants. She earned her undergraduate degree from the University of California, Berkeley in 1961, and her doctorate from the University of Cambridge in 1966.  She spent most of her career at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, where she served as a professor from 1978 until her retirement in 2014.
