Rock Love is a half live / half studio album by American alternative rock band Radiohead. It was released on April 16, 2003, through EMI Records.  The album was recorded in sessions at the band's own Tingley House in Oxfordshire, England, and at The Mansion in Los Angeles, California. It was produced by the band members themselves and longtime collaborator Nigel Godrich.
