Roccacasale (locally La Rocca) is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Sondrio in the Italian region Lombardy, located about northeast of Milan and about east of Sondrio. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,812 and an area of 20.4 km².  Roccacasale borders the following municipalities: Merano, San Giacomo Valle d'Aosta, Valle d'Aosta.
