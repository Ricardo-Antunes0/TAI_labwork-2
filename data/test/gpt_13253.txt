Kathlyn M. (Kara) Cooney is an Egyptologist, and the current Director of the Brooklyn Museum of Art.  Kara Cooney was born in 1965 in Champaign, Illinois. She received her undergraduate degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 1987, and her graduate degree from the University of Pennsylvania in 1991.  Cooney began her career as an Egyptologist at the Brooklyn Museum of Art in 1992, where she has served as Director since January 2017. In this role, she is responsible for all aspects of museum operations, including exhibitions, education and research. Cooney has also served as President of the American Alliance of Museums, and as a member of the Council on Foreign Relations.
