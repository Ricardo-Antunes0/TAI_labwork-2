The Kolkata Police Force (KPF) is one of the oldest in India and was constituted on 1 April 1853. The force currently has a strength of 1,393 officers. The Kolkata Police Force is responsible for maintaining law and order in the city of Kolkata as well as its suburbs. The Kolkata Police also conducts investigations into crimes and apprehends criminals.   The Kolkata Police Force is headed by a Commissioner of Police. The headquarters of the Kolkata Police Force is located at Salt Lake, Kolkata. The current commissioner of police is Sumit Ganguly.
