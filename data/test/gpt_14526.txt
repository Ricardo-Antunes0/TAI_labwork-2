Robert John McClory (born October 10, 1963) is an American businessman and entrepreneur who is the founder, chairman and chief executive officer of Crystal Cathedral Ministries, a non-profit Christian ministry.  McClory was born in Hollywood, California, the son of Margaret (n√©e O'Malley) and Robert J. McClory. He is of Irish descent. McClory has an undergraduate degree in business administration from the University of Southern California.  McClory founded Crystal Cathedral Ministries in 1990 with the stated purpose of "building a global Christian community that reflects the love and compassion of Jesus Christ." The ministry operates a large campus and ministry center in Orange County, California, as well as numerous other churches around the world. In 2007, McClory sold Crystal Cathedral to the Roman Catholic Diocese of Orange for $47 million.  In addition to his work with Crystal Cathedral Ministries, McClory is also involved in other business ventures including owning and operating a winery in Napa Valley, California. He also serves on the board of directors for several other organizations including Saddleback Church and World Vision. McClory was married to his wife, Patti, from 1984 until their divorce in 2006. They have two children together.
