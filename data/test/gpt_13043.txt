Manuel Oliveira “Youngie” Martin  (born December 22, 1990) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for English club Crystal Palace and the Portugal national team.  Martin started his career at hometown club Vitória de Guimarães, before moving to Benfica in 2011. He spent two seasons with the Lisbon club, before joining Palace in 2014. He made his debut for Portugal in a 2014 FIFA World Cup qualifying match against Latvia.
