Abstract Theory is the debut solo album by American electronic music producer and DJ A-Trak, which was released on October 21, 2011, by Fool's Gold Records. The album features guest appearances from Waka Flocka Flame, MF DOOM, The Roots, and Big Sean.  The album is a departure from A-Trak's previous work as a DJ and producer for Mad Decent Records, with the majority of the material focusing on synthesized beats and samples with sparse vocals. The album has been met with generally positive reviews from music critics.
