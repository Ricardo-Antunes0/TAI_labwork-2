The 2004 National Lacrosse League season is the 17th season of the National Lacrosse League. The season began on February 6, 2004 and ended with the championship game on August 24, 2004. The Rochester Knighthawks won their first NLL championship in franchise history.  The 2004 NLL season was highlighted by the Toronto Rock reaching the league's final four for the first time in franchise history. Toronto took down the Philadelphia Wings, Denver Mammoth, and Calgary Roughnecks to make it to the league championship game. Rochester Knighthawks then defeated the Rock in a hard-fought game 4 to win their first league championship.
