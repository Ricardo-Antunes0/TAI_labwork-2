The Vampire Lovers, also styled as Vampyre Lovers, are a Japanese rock band formed in 1984.  The band is best known for their 1985 hit single "Boys Be Ambitious", which reached number one on the Oricon Singles Chart. The song was written by the band's frontman, Hideki Matsutoya. The Vampire Lovers have sold over 30 million records worldwide, making them one of the most successful Japanese rock bands of all time.
