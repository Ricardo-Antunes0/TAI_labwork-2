Weightlifting was introduced to Armenia in the early 1990s. The first competitions were held in 1996.   There are currently about 60 weightlifters registered with the Armenian Weightlifting Federation. The federation is affiliated with the International Weightlifting Federation.   The main competitions are the Armenia national championships, which are held every two years. Armenia has been a member of the European Weightlifting Federation since 2001.   Weightlifting has been quite successful in Armenia, with many weightlifters qualifying for the Olympic Games. In 2012, Artur Davtyan became the first Armenian man to qualify for an Olympic weightlifting competition.
