The 175th Mixed Brigade , was a formation of the British Army in the First World War. It was formed in January 1917 from troops from the disbanded 34th (Ulster) Division and served on the Western Front until July 1918 when it was disbanded.  The brigade was constituted as follows:   - The 1/4th Battalion, Northamptonshire Regiment  - The 1/4th Battalion, Sussex Regiment  - The 1/5th Battalion, Durham Light Infantry  - The 1/5th Battalion, Royal Scots Fusiliers  - The 1/6th Battalion, Royal Welsh Fusiliers  - The 1/7th Battalion, London Scottish  - The 1/8th Battalion, London Irish Rifles
