Thrill-Ville USA was an amusement park in the Philadelphia suburb of Willow Grove, Pennsylvania. Opened on Memorial Day weekend in 1975, the park was billed as "The World's Most Thrilling Place!" and featured a wide variety of rides and attractions, including a log flume ride, a roller coaster called "The Beast", and a large water park.
