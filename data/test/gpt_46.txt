Vedhala Ulagam  is a 1948 Indian Tamil-language film directed by A. P. Nagarajan and produced by G. N. Ramachandran. The story is based on the Hindu epic "Mahabharata".  The film stars M. G. Ramachandran, T. R. Mahalingam, Sowcar Janaki, Kaviri Kothari and Bhanumathi in the lead roles. The film has music composed by V. Dakshinamoorthy and was released on 14 February 1948.  The film tells the story of the Kuru dynasty, who are cursed by the god Duryodhana for their unfairness in the "Mahabharata". Duryodhana plots to kill them all, but Arjuna, Krishna and their allies save them and end the curse.
