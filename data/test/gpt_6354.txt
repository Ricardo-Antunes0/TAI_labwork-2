Andrea Somers is a fictional character from the American soap opera "Days of Our Lives", created by writer/producers Bernie Brillstein and Laura Dern. The role was originated by actress Deidre Hall on July 17, 1987. Somers is the daughter of Tom and Jill Somers, who reside in Salem with their children, Scott, Julie and Casey.  Andrea is a passionate young woman with a heart of gold. She is headstrong and determined, but also kind and loving. Andrea has had a difficult life, but her strong character has helped her overcome many challenges. She is one of the most popular characters on "Days of Our Lives", and fans love to see her interact with her friends and family.
