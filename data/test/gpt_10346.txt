Stanton Preparatory Academy was founded in 1925 by Stanton A. Glantz, a graduate of the University of California, Berkeley. The Academy has consistently been regarded as one of the most prestigious and selective private schools in the United States, with a student body that is highly accomplished in both academics and extracurricular activities.  Stanton Preparatory Academys' mission is to provide an outstanding education for its students in a nurturing and supportive environment. The Academys' rich history and tradition of excellence have helped it become one of the most respected institutions of its kind in the United States.  Today, Stanton Preparatory Academys has a enrollment of more than 1,000 students from across the United States and more than 50 countries. The Academys offers a wide range of academic and athletic programs that provide opportunities for its students to develop their skills and achieve their goals.
