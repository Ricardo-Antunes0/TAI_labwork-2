Nicholas Gunn is a classically trained songwriter, musician, and singer who has recorded and performed extensively in the United States, Europe, and Australia.  Nicholas Gunn was born in San Diego, California in 1974. After completing his studies at the University of California, Santa Barbara, Gunn began his musical career as a songwriter and performer in the United States. His first album, The Nicholas Gunn Band, was released in 2001. Since then, Gunn has released six albums and toured extensively throughout the United States, Europe, and Australia. His latest album, Sundown at the Oasis, was released in 2016.  Gunn's music is rooted in traditional country and folk music styles but also incorporates elements of rock, blues, and folk-pop. His lyrics are often poetic and contemplative but also explore themes of love, loss, and loneliness. His music has been praised for its evocative and soulful soundscape but also for its intelligent and catchy melodies.
