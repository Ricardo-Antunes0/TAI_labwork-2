Michael Roos (born Mihkel Roos, October 5, 1966) is a Canadian professional ice hockey coach and former player. He is the current head coach of the Buffalo Sabres of the National Hockey League (NHL).  Roos played professional ice hockey in North America, Europe and Asia. He served as an assistant coach with the Vancouver Canucks and San Jose Sharks before being named head coach of the Florida Panthers in April 2010. In May 2014, he was relieved of his duties as head coach of the Panthers after posting a record of 36–45–11 in five seasons. He was then hired as head coach of the Buffalo Sabres on May 9, 2016.
