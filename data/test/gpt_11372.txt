Mugguru Maratilu  is a 1946 Telugu-language Indian feature film, produced and directed by K. V. Reddy. The film stars N. T. Rama Rao, Akkineni Nageswara Rao, A. V. Narasimha Rao, Gummadi Venkata Ramana, Kalyanamala and Surya in lead roles. The music was composed by Ghantasala.  The film is based on a novel of the same name by Balakrishna Reddy.  Mugguru Maratilu is a story of a man who is born with a leprosy and his fight against it. The film follows the man's struggles from childhood to adulthood and his eventual triumph over the disease.
