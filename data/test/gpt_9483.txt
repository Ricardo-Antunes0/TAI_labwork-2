The Zürichberg is a wooded hill rising to 443 m in the municipality of Zürich in the canton of Zürich in Switzerland. The hill is a popular location for recreation and is the site of several ski resorts. The slopes at Oberhasli, Unterhasli, Grindelwald and Kleine Scheidegg are all within easy reach of the hill. There are also many hiking trails, including the Berner Albtraum (Bernese Alps dream).
