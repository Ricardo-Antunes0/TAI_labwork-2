James M. Manyika is a Zimbabwean-American consultant, author, and public speaker. He is the co-founder and CEO of the consulting firm Strategy&.  He has written extensively on a wide range of topics, including economic development, innovation, globalization, technology, and management. He has been a visiting professor at the Harvard Business School and the London School of Economics.  Manyika has appeared as a commentator on television and in newspapers and magazines around the world. He is a regular contributor to The New York Times, The Economist, and The Financial Times.  He was named one of the world's 50 most influential people by FT magazine in 2013 and again in 2016.
