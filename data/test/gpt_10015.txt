Yorgo Carlo George Kehdy  (born in Cairo, Egypt on February 26, 1976) is an Egyptian-born Greek singer-songwriter and actor.  Kehdy's family moved to Greece when he was two years old and he grew up in Thebes and Athens. He started his musical career in the early 1990s as a member of the band "Gizo". In 1998, Kehdy released his first solo album, "Noumena", which was followed by "Eirene" (2000), "Kalispera" (2002), and "Symfonia" (2005).  Kehdy has collaborated with a number of Greek artists including Antony and the Johnsons, Vasilis Hatzimihlaras, Mikis Theodorakis, and Giorgos Karagounis. He has also appeared in a number of films, including "Oi Anatoli" (2001), "Anchorwoman" (2005), and "Never Let Me Go" (2010). In 2013, Kehdy released his latest album, "To Be Free", which was preceded by the singles "Laktos" and "Piano".
