Emmanuel-Janvier Luzolo Bambi Lessa (born 31 January 1992) is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain.  Born in Saint-Ouen, France, Bambi Lessa started his career with local side FC Sète. In the summer of 2009, he was signed by French side Lille OSC, where he made his professional debut on 10 August 2009 in a 3–1 Ligue 1 win over Valenciennes FC. After just five appearances and one goal during the 2009–10 season, Lessa was loaned to Belgian club Cercle Brugge. He made his debut for Cercle Brugge on 5 August 2010 in a 2–1 Belgian First Division A win over K.A.A. Gent.  In June 2011, Bambi Lessa was signed by Serie A club Internazionale on a four-year contract for a €7 million fee. He made his debut for the " Nerazzurri" on 11 September 2011 in a 2–1 victory over Cagliari Calcio in the Coppa Italia. On 15 December 2012, he scored his first goal for Inter in a 3–2 victory over Palermo Calcio in the Serie A.
