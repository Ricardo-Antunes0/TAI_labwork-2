Idaho  is a state in the Northwest region of the United States. Idaho is the 14th largest by area and the 41st most populous of the 50 United States. The state's capital is Boise.  Idaho is bordered by Oregon to the south, Wyoming to the west, Nevada to the north, and Utah to the east. It has a total area of , almost half of which is land and half of which is water. The state has a population of more than 1.3 million people.  Idaho was admitted to the Union on July 3, 1890, becoming only the fifth state after Wyoming, Utah, New Mexico, and Arizona to join the Union. The Constitution of Idaho provides for a republican form of government with a governor as its head and a unicameral legislature.
