Digital curation is the selection, preservation, maintenance, and presentation of digital content. It encompasses the process of identifying, acquiring, organizing, managing, and presenting digital information in a way that supports its intended purpose.  Digital curation is used in a variety of contexts, including digital archiving and information management, online publishing and research, and educational resources.
