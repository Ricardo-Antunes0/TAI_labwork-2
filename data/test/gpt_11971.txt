Grant Comes East: A Novel of the American Civil War is a historical novel by James McBride. It tells the story of the American Civil War from the perspective of African Americans, who are used as cannon fodder by both sides.  The novel was published in 2009 and won the 2009 Pulitzer Prize for Fiction. It was also a finalist for the 2009 National Book Award and the 2009 Orange Prize for Fiction.
