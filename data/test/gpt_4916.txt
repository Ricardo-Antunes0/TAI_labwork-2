Rod Reddy (born 1 March 1954) is an Australian singer, songwriter and record producer.  Reddy was born in Melbourne to an Indian father and Australian mother. He began his career as a session musician, working with a range of artists including Tina Turner, Joe Cocker and Peter Allen. He released his first album in 1984, and has since released 10 more albums, as well as writing songs for other artists. He has won five ARIA Awards, including Best Male Artist in 1986 and 1992.
