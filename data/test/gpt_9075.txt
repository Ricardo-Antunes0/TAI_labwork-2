Sharon Calahan  is an American cinematographer.  Sharon Calahan was born in Los Angeles, California. She began her career as a still photographer, working for such magazines as American Photo and W.  She began working as a cinematographer in the late 1980s, shortly after graduating from the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts. She has since worked on a variety of projects, including the independent films The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou (2004), Juno (2007), Take Shelter (2011), and The Theory of Everything (2014).
