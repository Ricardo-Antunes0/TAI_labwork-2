Sagina Mahato is a 1970 Bengali film. It was directed by Jnanpith Award-winning filmmaker Rituparno Ghosh and stars Soumitra Chatterjee and Suchitra Sen.  The film is set in the time of the Indian independence movement. Sagina is married to a revolutionary, but she is also in love with another man. The film portrays the various struggles and emotions experienced by Sagina during this time.
