Joe Comerford (born 1947) is an Irish composer.  Comerford was born in 1947 in Dublin, Ireland. He studied at the Royal Irish Academy of Music and the University of Utah, where he received his M.A. in 1971. He has been a resident composer at the Tanglewood Music Center since 1975.  Comerford's works have been performed throughout North America, Europe, and Asia. He has been awarded several commissions, including an opera from the Seattle Opera Company and a symphony from the Los Angeles Philharmonic. He was awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship in 1986.  Comerford is currently Professor of Composition at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
