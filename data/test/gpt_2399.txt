Ángel Azteca Jr.  (born June 1, 1974) is a Mexican professional wrestler who is currently working for AAA. He is the son of professional wrestler Ángel Azteca.  Azteca Jr. learned his wrestling skills from his father and made his professional wrestling debut in 1995, working for his father's own promotion, Empresa Mexicana de Lucha Libre (EMLL). In 2001, Azteca Jr. began working for International Wrestling Revolution Group (IWRG), where he became one of the promotion's top stars. In 2006, he signed with AAA, where he has remained a mainstay ever since. He has won numerous high-profile championships in AAA, most notably the Mexican National Heavyweight Championship twice and the World Heavyweight Championship once.
