NurShahila binti Amir Amzah (born 13 August 1978) is a Malaysian actress, singer and model.  Shila began her career as the lead singer of the Malaysian rock band The Outsiders, which she formed at the age of 16. The Outsiders subsequently released two albums before splitting up in 2001.   Since then, Shila has appeared in numerous films and television series, most notably playing the role of Puan Sri in the successful TV series, 7th Heaven. She has also released two solo music albums and starred in several stage productions. In 2010, she was awarded the title of "National Artiste" by the Malaysian government.  Shila married Malaysian actor Mohd Zaid Ibrahim in 2004. The couple have one child, a daughter named Aisha (born 2006).
