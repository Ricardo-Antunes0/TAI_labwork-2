Kumar Sarvjeet (born 3 April 1975) is an Indian film actor and producer who appears in Hindi, Telugu and Punjabi films. He has also appeared in Tamil and Kannada films.    Sarvjeet was born in Amritsar, Punjab, India. He started his career as a model, appearing in several print and television commercials. He made his acting debut in the 2001 Hindi film "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai" and went on to appear in several successful films, including "Bobby", "Pyaar Kiya To Darna Kya", "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" and "". In 2014, he produced and starred in the critically acclaimed "Om Shanti Om".
