Keith Michael Chadwick (born 10 March 1953) is an English former footballer who played as a midfielder in the Football League for Brentford, Gillingham and Southend United.  Born in London, Chadwick started his career with hometown club Brentford, before signing for Gillingham in 1974. He made over 200 appearances for the "Gills", scoring 25 goals, before joining Southend United in 1981. He went on to make over 130 appearances for the "Shrimpers", scoring seven goals.  After leaving Southend, Chadwick retired from playing and settled in Essex. He now works as a television pundit and has also been involved in coaching.
