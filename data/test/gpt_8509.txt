Gibraltar United F.C. is a football team based in the British Overseas Territory of Gibraltar. The club was formed in 2006 and currently competes in the Gibraltar Premier Division. The club's home ground is the Victoria Stadium.  Gibraltar United F.C. was formed in 2006, after a group of football enthusiasts decided to create a team to represent Gibraltar in domestic and international competitions. The club made its debut in the Gibraltar Premier Division in 2008, and has since been competing in the league every season. In addition, the club has also participated in several cup competitions, most notably the Gibraltar Cup and the Iberian Cup.   The Victoria Stadium has been the club's home ground since its inception, and has undergone several renovations over the years to meet the needs of both the players and the fans. The stadium has a capacity of 1,000 fans, and is regularly used for football matches, as well as concerts and other events.
