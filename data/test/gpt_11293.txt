West End Eurovision is an annual fundraising event organised by the West End Eurovision Committee. Held in the West End of London, it is the UK's annual national final for the Eurovision Song Contest.  The event was first held in 2014, and has since been held annually. It is a charity event, with all proceeds going to charitable causes. The 2018 edition was held at The O2 arena in London and saw the winning song 'I Wanna Dance With Somebody (Who Loves Me)' performed by Katrina and The Waves.
