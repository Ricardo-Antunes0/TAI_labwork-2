Hearts is the fifth studio album by American singer and songwriter Kelly Clarkson. It was released on September 19, 2018, by RCA Records.  After releasing her fourth studio album, "Stronger" (2017), Clarkson took a break from music. She announced her upcoming fifth album in February 2018, with the first single "What's My Name?" released in March. The album's title was revealed on August 17, 2018, along with the release of the second single "I Don't Wanna Live Forever" featuring Zedd. The album's third single, "Never Really Over", was released on September 12.  "Hearts" is a pop and rock album that lyrically discusses heartbreak and relationships. It has been noted for its darker themes and more emotional sound than her previous work. The album has received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised Clarkson's vocals and the album's overall production.
