The Boulevard Gardens Apartments is a 960-unit affordable housing development located in the city of Camden, New Jersey. The development was completed in 2006 and is managed by the nonprofit organization Community Housing Partnerships.  The Boulevard Gardens Apartments was developed as part of a larger effort to revitalize Camden's South Ward, which has been plagued by poverty and crime for decades. The development features studio, one-, and two-bedroom apartments that are designed to be affordable for low-income families. Amenities include a pool, playground, and community center.
