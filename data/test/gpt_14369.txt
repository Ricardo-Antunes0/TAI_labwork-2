Settha Palace Hotel, is a historical, luxury hotel located in the center of Bangkok, Thailand.   The hotel was opened in 1929 as the Settha Palace Hotel by King Prajadhipok of Siam, and was later renamed the Settha Palace Hotel in 1946. The hotel is a member of the Leading Hotels of the World (LHW) and is also a member of the World Association of Super Luxury Hotels (WASL).  The Settha Palace Hotel has been awarded two stars by the Michelin Guide since 2002. The restaurant has been awarded one Michelin star each year since 2007.
