Sharon Kaye Parker  is an Australian author and journalist. She is best known for her mystery novels, which have been published in the United States and Australia.  Parker was born in Melbourne, Australia, in 1961. She has a degree in journalism from the University of Melbourne and worked as a reporter for several Australian newspapers before becoming a freelance writer. Her first novel, The Sleeping Dolls, was published in 2001. Her books have since been translated into over 20 languages.  Parker is married to American author Jeffery Deaver and they have two children.
