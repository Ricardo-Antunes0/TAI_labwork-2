Ahmed Abdulkadir Warsame was born circa 1985 or 1986 in Somalia, according to U.S. government reports. He is a Somali-born suspect in connection with the 2013 Boston Marathon bombings, which killed three people and injured more than 260 others. On April 19, 2015, Warsame was indicted on charges of conspiracy to use a weapon of mass destruction and providing material support to a terrorist organization. If convicted, he could face life in prison.
