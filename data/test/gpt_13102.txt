P was a short-lived American alternative rock band formed in Los Angeles, California in 2002. The band consisted of vocalist and guitarist John K. Samson, drummer Josh Freese, bassist Chris Chaney, and keyboardist Mark Hoppus.  After releasing their debut album, "P", in 2002, the band went on hiatus and did not reunite until 2009. In 2009, the band released their second album, "Goodbye Yellow Brick Road". The album was met with mixed reviews from music critics, but it was a commercial success. The band went on a hiatus again in 2011 and did not reunite again until 2015. That year, they released their third album, "Hospitality".   The band announced their breakup in December 2017.
