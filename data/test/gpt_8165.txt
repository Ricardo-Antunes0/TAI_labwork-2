Bettison's Folly is a tower in Hornsea, East Yorkshire, England. It was built in 1885 as the home of the Hornsea Lighthouse, which is now a museum. The tower is made from local sandstone and has an observation deck on the fourth floor. It is open to the public on weekdays from 10am to 4pm and on Saturdays from 10am to 2pm.
