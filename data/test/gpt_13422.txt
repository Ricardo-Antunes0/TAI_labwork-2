Charlott Cordes (born 6 December 1988)  is a French actress, singer, and television presenter. Cordes began her career as a child star in the French soap opera La Famille Cordes, appearing from 1994 to 1998. She has since appeared in a number of films and television series, most notably in the successful French comedy film The Intouchables (2011), for which she was nominated for the BAFTA Award for Best Actress. In 2015, she starred in the romantic drama film La La Land, for which she was also nominated for the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress.
