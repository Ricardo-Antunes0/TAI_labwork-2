ViaFast (corporately styled VIAFast) was an abandoned online travel agency.  ViaFast was an online travel agency founded in 2006. The company ceased operations in 2013. ViaFast offered travel services to customers in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Central America, South America, the Caribbean, Europe, and Asia. ViaFast offered a variety of travel products and services, including airfare, hotel stays, car rentals, and cruises. ViaFast also offered a variety of travel insurance products and services.
