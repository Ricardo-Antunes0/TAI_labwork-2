Izabella Miko (born Izabella Anna Mikołajczak; 21 October 1988) is a Polish singer, songwriter and actress.  Mikołajczak was born in Kraków, Poland. She started her musical career as a contestant on the Polish version of The X Factor (Poland) in 2006, where she finished in 4th place. Mikołajczak then signed to EMI Poland and released her self-titled debut album in 2007. The album reached number one on the Polish Albums Chart. In 2010, she released her second album, entitled MTV Unplugged. The album spawned the singles "Million Miles" and "Seksualna Odpowiedzialność", both of which reached top 10 positions on the Polish Singles Chart. In 2012, she released her third album, entitled Izabella Miko – Nowa Era. The album featured the singles "Niezależnie" and "To nie ja". In 2014, Mikołajczak released her fourth album, entitled Niepodległość. The album featured the single "Myślę o Tobie". In 2016, she released her fifth album, entitled Czarna Dama. The album featured the single "Lustro".
