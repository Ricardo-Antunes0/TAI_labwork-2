Attilio Cubeddu (, ; born 2 March 1992) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for S.S.C. Napoli.  Born in Naples, Campania, Cubeddu started his career at hometown club S.S.C. Napoli, and made his Serie A debut on 24 October 2009 in a 2–1 away win over A.S. Roma. On 17 September 2013, he scored his first goal in the league, netting the second in a 4–2 home victory over Chievo Verona; he finished the season with six goals in all competitions, helping Napoli to the 2013 Coppa Italia Final where they were defeated by Juventus.  On 3 July 2014, Cubeddu signed for Marseille on a five-year contract for a fee of €8 million. He made his debut for the club on 14 August in a 1–0 Ligue 1 win over Caen, starting and playing 71 minutes before being substituted for Maxime Lopez.  In January 2017, after suffering relegation with Marseille, Cubeddu joined SSC Napoli on a one-and-a-half-year deal for an undisclosed fee. He scored his first goal for Napoli in their 3–2 home victory over Cesena on 10 December 2017.
