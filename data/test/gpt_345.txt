Gulf Coast: A Journal of Literature and Art is a quarterly magazine that publishes literary and visual arts criticism, interviews, and features.   Gulf Coast was founded in 1998 as a literary magazine by students at the University of Houston. The journal has since grown to be one of the most respected voices in contemporary literary and visual arts criticism, with a circulation of over 2,000.  In addition to its print edition, Gulf Coast publishes an online magazine twice a month.
