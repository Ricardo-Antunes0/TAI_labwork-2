Isaiah Sykes (born December 2, 1991) is an American football cornerback for the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Baylor.  Sykes was signed by the Browns as an undrafted free agent in 2014. In his rookie season, he appeared in all 16 games and recorded one sack. He was also a member of the Browns' Super Bowl LI championship team. In 2016, he started all 16 games and recorded career-highs with four interceptions and 10 passes defended. He also received first-team All-Pro honors from the NFL.
