Jesse Roth (born August 5, 1934) is an American writer, artist, and filmmaker. A self-taught artist, Roth has been a documentary filmmaker since the early 1960s. His work has focused on social issues and the environment.  Roth was born in 1934 in New York City. He studied at the School of Visual Arts and the Art Students League of New York. He is married to the writer and sculptor Ellen Lippmann. They have two children.  Roth began his filmmaking career as a documentary filmmaker in the early 1960s, making films about social issues and the environment. His films have been shown at festivals worldwide and have been broadcast on PBS and other networks. In 1998 he was awarded an honorary doctorate from Wesleyan University. Roth is also a painter and sculptor.
