Chloroscombrus is a genus containing two species of small air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Heterobranchia.  The genus Chloroscombrus contains two species of small air-breathing land snails, terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusks in the family Heterobranchia. The type species is Chloroscombrus chrysolophus.  The distribution of these two species is disjunct. C. chrysolophus is endemic to Southeast Asia, where it is found in Thailand, Burma, and Indonesia, while C. robustus is found in Australia and New Zealand.
