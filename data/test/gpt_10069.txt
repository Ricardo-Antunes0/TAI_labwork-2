The Aermacchi or Macchi MB-326 is a light military aircraft designed by the Italian aircraft manufacturer Aermacchi. The MB-326 was first flown on 20 December 1978. The MB-326 is a single-seat, twin-engine, low-wing monoplane.  The MB-326 is used by the Italian Air Force for training and liaison purposes. In addition to the Italian Air Force, the MB-326 is also used by the Italian Navy for maritime patrol and search and rescue missions. The MB-326 has also been exported to countries such as Austria, Belgium, Chile, Ecuador, Egypt, Greece, Portugal, Spain and Tunisia.
