Seri Iskandar (Jawi: سري إسكندر, ) is a royal town located in the state of Johor, Malaysia.   The town is situated on the north-west coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Seri Iskandar is also home to the Iskandar Royal Forest Park, which is one of the largest tropical rainforest reserves in Southeast Asia. The town is also home to two international airports - Sultan Iskandar Airport and Senai Airport.
