I Burn (stylized in sentence case) is an American rock band from San Diego, California. Formed in 2009, the band consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Jesu, drummer Ryan, bassist Darryl, and keyboardist Owen.  I Burn's debut album, "Transcending Obscurity", was released in 2012 through Sumerian Records. The album was well-received by critics, who praised Jesu's vocals and the band's heavy metal sound. I Burn toured in support of Transcending Obscurity throughout the United States and Europe. In early 2013, the band released their second album, "To the Crown". The album was also well-received by critics, who praised Jesu's vocals and the band's progressive metal sound.  I Burn's third album, "The Wretched and the Divine", was released on May 5th 2019 through Sumerian Records. The album was produced by Nick Raskulinecz (Tool, Slayer, Deftones) and features guest appearances from Jason Newsted (Metallica) and Troy Sanders (Megadeth).
