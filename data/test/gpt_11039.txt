Everything We Love is a live album by the American rock band Red Hot Chili Peppers, released on September 16, 2016. The album is the band's nineteenth studio album and was produced by Danger Mouse.   The album was announced on June 2, 2016, with a release date of September 16, 2016. The first single from the album, "The Getaway", was released on July 22, 2016. "Dark Necessities" was released as the second single on September 9, 2016.   The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart with first-week sales of 185,000 copies.
