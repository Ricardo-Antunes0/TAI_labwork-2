William E. Watson was born in New York City.  He is Professor of History at Immaculata University in Malvern, Pennsylvania.  He received his PhD in Medieval History from the University of Pennsylvania.  He is the director of the Duffy's Cut Project.  He was a Commonwealth Speaker for the Pennsylvania Humanities Council  2006-2007, and was also the recipient of the Lindback Distinguished Teaching Award at Immaculata University for the year 2006-2007.
