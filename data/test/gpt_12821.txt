Charles Andrew "Andy" Doyle is the ninth and current Bishop of the Episcopal Diocese of Texas.  Doyle was born in Huntington, West Virginia, on December 2, 1952. He was ordained a deacon in 1977 and a priest in 1978. Doyle served as rector of St Luke's Church in Houston from 1978 to 1996, and then served as the bishop suffragan of the Episcopal Diocese of Texas from 1996 to 2003. On July 1, 2003, Doyle was consecrated as the ninth bishop of the Episcopal Diocese of Texas by Presiding Bishop Katharine Jefferts Schori. On October 9, 2014, Doyle announced his retirement effective February 1, 2020.
