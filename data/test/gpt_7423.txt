Temple Run is a video game franchise created by Imangi Studios and published by Playrix. The first game, Temple Run, was released on the App Store in March 2013.  Temple Run is a fast-paced and challenging running game where the player navigates a character through an obstacle course while avoiding obstacles and enemies. The player can run, jump, and slide to avoid obstacles and reach the end of the course. The player can also collect coins and other rewards along the way.
