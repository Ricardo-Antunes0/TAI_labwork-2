Tattoo You is the 16th British and Irish film, released on 10 July 2006. The story is set in a small town in Ireland and revolves around the rivalry between two young boys, one of whom has just moved to the town and the other who is the son of the original town bully.  The film was directed by John Carney and written by Brian Taylor. It stars Colin Farrell, Sam Rockwell, Alan Rickman and Julie Walters.
