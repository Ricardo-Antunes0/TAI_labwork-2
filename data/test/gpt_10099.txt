bloggorah.com, also known as blogorrah, was an American blog-hosting and social networking website. The site was launched on May 27, 2007, by then-18-year-old entrepreneur Zach Grienke.  blogorrah was a social networking website that allowed users to share and connect with each other through blogs. Users could add blogs to their profiles, or follow other users' blogs. Blogs were categorised by topic, and users could vote on posts and make comments. Blogs were also ranked by popularity.  blogorrah was acquired by Webmedia Corporation in November 2008. At the time of the acquisition, blogorrah had approximately 150,000 registered users. blogorrah was shut down in February 2009.
