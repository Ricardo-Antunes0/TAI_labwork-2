Grand Hyatt Washington is a hotel in Washington, D.C.  The hotel, which opened in 2000, is located at 1333 H St NW in the West End neighborhood, just south of the White House and east of the Capitol. The hotel is managed by Hyatt Regency Washington D.C.  The hotel has a total of 620 guest rooms and suites, including 36 luxury rooms and suites. The hotel also has a spa, two restaurants (including a sushi bar and a steakhouse), a lounge, and a bar. It is also home to the Grand Ballroom and the Hyatt Regency Library.
