Surf Factor 8 is the eighth album by the American rock band Journey, released on September 12, 1981. The album was produced by J. D. Souther and recorded at the Record Plant in Sausalito, California.  In 1981, Journey released their eighth studio album, Surf Factor 8. The album was produced by J. D. Souther and recorded at the Record Plant in Sausalito, California. The album peaked at number 17 on the "Billboard" 200 chart and spawned the singles "Don't Stop Believin'", "Open Arms", and "Faithfully". The latter two songs were written by Steve Perry and Jonathan Cain, respectively.
