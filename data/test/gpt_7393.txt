Broers is a Dutch patronymic surname meaning "brothers". The surname is most common in the province of Friesland.   The Broers family originates from the city of Groningen in the north of the Netherlands. Broers is one of the most common surnames in the province of Friesland, where it is the twenty-sixth most common surname. The Broers family traces its roots back to a certain Jan or Johannes Broers who lived in the early seventeenth century. Broers is also a common surname in Belgium, where it is the seventy-second most common surname.
