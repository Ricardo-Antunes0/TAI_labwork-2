Rosa 'Bonica 82,  (aka MEIdomonac), is a variety of pink rose, with a deep pink hue and large, fragrant flowers.  The Rosa 'Bonica 82' is a vigorous rose that grows to a height of 3 feet, with a spread of 2 feet. The blooms are large and showy, and the aroma is strong and sweet. This rose is hardy in most climates, and is best suited for USDA zones 7 through 9.
