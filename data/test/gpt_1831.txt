"Mockin' Bird Hill" is a song written by Bob Dylan and performed by him on his 1966 album, "The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan".  The song is a lament for the loss of love, and is sung in a simple, almost mournful style.  The song has been covered by a number of other artists, including The Byrds, who released their version as a single in 1965.  Dylan has also performed the song live on several occasions, most notably at the 1966 Newport Folk Festival and at the 1969 Woodstock Festival.
