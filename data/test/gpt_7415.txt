Leifchild Stratten Leif-Jones, 1st Baron Rhayader, PC (born 6 July 1942) is a Welsh Labour Party politician who has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for Aberavon since 1987. He served as Secretary of State for Wales from 1999 to 2001, and Deputy Prime Minister from 2010 to 2012 in the first coalition government led by David Cameron.  Leif-Jones was a member of the National Assembly for Wales for the Welsh Labour Party from 1983 to 1987, and was leader of the Welsh Labour Party from 1984 to 1987. He became an MP in 1987, and served as Minister for Trade and Industry from 1999 to 2001 in the first Labour government led by Tony Blair. He was Deputy Prime Minister in the first coalition government led by David Cameron from 2010 to 2012.  On 10 September 2013, he was created a Baronet, of Rhayader in the County of Glamorgan, after being appointed Knight Grand Cross of the Order of St John in the 2013 New Year Honours.
