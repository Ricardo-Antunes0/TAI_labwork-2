Shannon Patricia Elizabeth O'Brien (born April 30, 1973) is an American actress, comedian, and model.  Born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, O'Brien began her acting career in improv and sketch comedy before making her television debut in the sitcom Spin City (1996-1999). She has since appeared in a variety of roles on television, including recurring roles on the sitcom New Girl (2011-2013) and the legal drama The Good Wife (2009-2017), as well as starring roles in the films The 40 Year Old Virgin (2005), I Love You, Man (2009), and This Is the End (2013).  O'Brien has also had recurring roles on the animated sitcom The Simpsons since its first season in 1990, as well as appearing in several of the show's feature films. In 2017 she starred in the comedy series Shannon O'Brien's Show, which was cancelled after one season.
