James McCluskey (1 November 1950 in Salsburgh, County Fermanagh, Northern Ireland) is a former Irish international rugby union player and referee. He played club rugby for Limerick and was a member of the Ireland national team from 1971 to 1984. His refereeing career began in 1976 and he refereed at international level until his retirement in 1992. McCluskey also coached Ireland national rugby union team from 1995 to 1998.
