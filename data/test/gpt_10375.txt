Dr. Noa Regev (born February 17, 1982) is an Israeli singer, songwriter and actress.  Noa Regev was born in Tel Aviv, Israel in 1982. She began her musical career as a backing singer for various Israeli artists, before releasing her own album in 2006. Her first major success came with the song "Hallelujah", which reached number one on the Israeli charts. She has since released two further albums and embarked on a successful international tour. In 2013, she appeared in the Israeli film adaptation of the best-selling novel Neve Tzedek.
