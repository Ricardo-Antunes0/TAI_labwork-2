Mercedes Valdivieso (March 1, 1924 – August 5, 2014) was a Mexican actress and singer. She won the Silver Medal for Best Actress at the 1956 Latin American Film Festival for her work in the film La hija de la Mafia. Valdivieso also appeared in films such as El Pecado de la Rabia (1957), La mujer del paraguay (1959), and Los hijos de Juan Ponce de León (1960).
