The Grail Quest is a  historical novel by British author Thomas Malory, first published in 1475. It tells the story of theQuest for the Holy Grail, a quest undertaken by King Arthur and his knight companions to recover the cup used by Jesus at the Last Supper.  The novel is composed of five books, and is typical of medieval romances in its episodic nature and use of flashbacks. Although it is set mainly in Britain, it has been translated into many languages and has been popular throughout the world ever since its publication.
