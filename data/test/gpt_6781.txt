"Dance Macabre" is a song by Swedish heavy metal band Ghost. It was released as the third single from the band's third studio album, Infestissumam.  The song has been described as being "disturbed, dark and sinister" and features a fast-paced, heavy metal beat. Lyrically, it deals with themes of death and darkness.  The song reached number 14 on the Swedish Singles Chart and number 9 on the Finnish Singles Chart.
