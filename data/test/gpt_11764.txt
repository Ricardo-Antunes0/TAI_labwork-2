Udo Landbauer (born 12 April 1986 in Vienna) is an Austrian footballer who plays for FC Wacker Innsbruck.  Landbauer started his career with Rapid Wien. He transferred to FC Wacker Innsbruck in the summer of 2007. Landbauer made his first team debut for Wacker on 5 September 2007 in a match against FK Austria Wien. He played 7 games for Wacker in the Austrian Bundesliga that season, scoring 1 goal. In January 2008, he signed a contract extension with the club until June 2013.  Landbauer made his international debut for Austria on 11 November 2007 against Switzerland. He has since earned 8 caps and scored 1 goal. His last international game was on 9 October 2009 against Bulgaria.
