Hologymnosus annulatus, the ring wrasse or ringed wrasse, is a tropical marine fish belonging to the family Labridae. It is found in the western Atlantic Ocean and the Caribbean Sea.  The ring wrasse has a slender body with a pointed snout and a large, bright blue eye. The fish has numerous black rings around its body. It can reach up to 20 cm in length.  The ring wrasse feeds on small fish and crustaceans. It is an active predator that swims around quickly, hunting its prey.
