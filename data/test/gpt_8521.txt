Frank Jao is a prominent Vietnamese American artist and sculptor. He was born in Saigon, Vietnam in 1954 and immigrated to the United States with his family as a child. Jao's sculptures explore the intersections of humanity, nature, and technology. His work has been exhibited at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Whitney Museum of American Art in New York City, the Los Angeles County Museum of Art, and the Saatchi Gallery in London. Jao has also been awarded the prestigious Guggenheim Fellowship and has been the recipient of a MacArthur Fellowship. He currently lives and works in New York City.
