Gurumukh Sajanmal Sainani is an Indian general who has served in the Indian Army since 1971. He has been Chief of Army Staff (CAS) since December 2014.  Sainani was born on 24 October 1951 in Panipat, Haryana, India. He attended the National Defence Academy, Pune, and the Indian Military Academy, Dehradun. He has served in various command and staff positions in the Army, most notably as General Officer Commanding-in-Chief of the XVIII Corps in Jammu and Kashmir from 2007 to 2009 and as Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Operations) from 2009 to 2010.  Sainani has been married to Radha since 1978. They have two children.
