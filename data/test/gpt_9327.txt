Brent Lee Hawkins (born September 1, 1983) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Pittsburgh Pirates of Major League Baseball (MLB). He has played in MLB for the Cardinals, Brewers, and Pirates.  Hawkins attended Mira Loma High School in Rancho Cucamonga, California, and played college baseball at the University of Southern California (USC) before being drafted by the Cardinals in the sixth round of the 2005 MLB draft. He made his MLB debut with the Cardinals in 2007. In 2009, he was traded to the Brewers and spent three seasons with them before being traded to the Pirates in 2013. Hawkins has been a member of the United States national baseball team and has won a gold medal at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.
