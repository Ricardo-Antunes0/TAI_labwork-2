Frederick James Dobbin (10 October 1879 – 12 January 1928) was an English film actor. Dobbin was born in London, the son of actor-manager Frederick Dobbin and his wife, the actress Lillie Langtry. He made his stage debut in 1899 and appeared in more than two hundred films between 1912 and 1928. Dobbin was married twice: firstly to the actress May Whitty (1903–1920) with whom he had two children; and secondly to the actress Sybil Thorndike (1924–1981). He died of a heart attack in London aged 52.
