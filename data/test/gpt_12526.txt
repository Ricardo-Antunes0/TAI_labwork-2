Philip Holder (born 19 January 1952) is an English musician and composer, best known as the drummer of the progressive rock band Yes. Holder was also a member of the jazz-rock fusion group Asia.  Holder was born in London, England on 19 January 1952. Holder began playing drums at an early age, and by the time he was 15 he had formed his first band. Holder attended Guildhall School of Music and Drama in London, where he studied music composition. After leaving Guildhall, Holder joined Yes in 1970. Holder has been with Yes ever since, except for a brief period in the early 1980s when he toured with Asia. Holder has also written music for television and film, and has released three solo albums. Holder is currently the drummer for Yes tour 2007/2008.
