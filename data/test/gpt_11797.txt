Iain Philip Stables is a New Zealand-born Australian professional racing jockey. He is the current riding coach of Australia's top-rated horse The Wolf.  Stables was born in Auckland, New Zealand in 1978 and began riding horses at the age of five. He moved to Australia in 1993 and began his racing career in Sydney. Stables won the Australian Jockey Club Gold Cup in 1998 and 2002, and the Cox Plate in 2003. He also won the Melbourne Cup three times (1998, 2002, 2007). He retired from riding at the end of the 2013 season.  Stables became a riding coach in 2014 and has worked with The Wolf, who won the Melbourne Cup that year.
