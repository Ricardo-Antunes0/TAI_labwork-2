Challenge 2 is a 2012 Indian Bengali-language mystery thriller film directed by Rituparno Ghosh and produced by Ronnie Screwvala. It is the second installment in the "Challenge" series and stars Rajesh Khanna, Prosenjit Chatterjee, Supriya Pathak and Moushumi Chatterjee.  The film tells the story of a retired Indian Police officer, Keshav Roy (Rajesh Khanna), who is approached by a mysterious old man (Prosenjit Chatterjee) to help solve a series of murders that have been committed in the city. Roy is initially reluctant to get involved but eventually decides to take on the case.  The film was shot over a period of two months in Kolkata, West Bengal and released on 6 October 2012.
