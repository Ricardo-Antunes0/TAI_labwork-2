Gore Creek is a tributary of the North Fork of the Gunnison River in southern Colorado. The creek is approximately 22 miles (35 km) long, and flows through the Gore Range of the Rocky Mountains. Gore Creek has been designated as a Wild and Scenic River by the Colorado State Legislature.  Gore Creek is named after one of its major tributaries, Gore Creek. Gore Creek was so named on account of the gore or notch in a ridge that it follows.
