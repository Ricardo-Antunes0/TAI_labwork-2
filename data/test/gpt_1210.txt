Einfrid Daghild Halvorsen (born 13 October 1937) is a Norwegian historian. He is Professor Emeritus at the University of Oslo.  Halvorsen was born in Oslo, Norway. He took the cand.jur. degree at the University of Oslo in 1960 and the dr. jurid. degree in 1967. He worked as a research assistant at the University of Oslo from 1960 to 1962 and at the National Archives from 1962 to 1965. From 1965 to 1969 he was an associate professor at the University of Oslo, and from 1969 to 1973 he was professor at the same institution. He was appointed Professor Emeritus there in 2003.  Halvorsen specializes in Norwegian history, particularly medieval history, but also has written extensively on various other aspects of Norwegian history, including constitutional history and historiography. He has been a member of the Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters since 1983, and a member of the Royal Society of Sciences and Letters since 1990.
