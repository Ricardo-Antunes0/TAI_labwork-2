He played with a variety of local bands before joining Count Basie's orchestra in 1941. He remained with the band until 1946, when he left to join Woody Herman's band. He later played with Lucky Millinder, Don Byas and others. In the 1960s he worked with Art Blakey and the Jazz Messengers. He retired in the early 1970s.
