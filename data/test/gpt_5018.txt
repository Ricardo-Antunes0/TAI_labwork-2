Pinnawala Open Zoo (also called Pinnawala Zoo:   Pinnawala is a zoo in Sri Lanka. It was established in 1976 and is located in the town of Pinnawala, a small town in northern province of Sabaragamuwa. The zoo has a collection of over 2,900 animals representing about 250 species. The zoo is open from 8:00 am to 4:00 pm from Monday to Friday and from 8:00 am to 3:00 pm on Saturday. Admission is free for all visitors.
