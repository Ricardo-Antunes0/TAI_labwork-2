Jean Arcelin is a French and Swiss painter, sculptor and graphic artist.  Jean Arcelin was born in 1949 in Lausanne, Switzerland. He has exhibited his work internationally, including at the National Gallery of Victoria in Melbourne, Australia, the Palais de Tokyo in Paris, the Alte Pinakothek in Munich, and the Centre Pompidou in Paris. Selected exhibitions of his work include "Jean Arcelin: Paintings" (1987), "Jean Arcelin: Sculptures" (1991), "Jean Arcelin: Graphics" (1998), and "Jean Arcelin: Retrospective" (2003). He is a member of the Salone del Mobile in Milan and the Royal Academy of Arts in London. Jean Arcelin was awarded the Prize of the City of Lausanne in 2001.
