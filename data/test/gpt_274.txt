Three is a Telugu film, starring Rajiv Kanakala and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles. The film is directed by Kodi Ramakrishna and produced by S. S. Rajamouli under the banner of his production company, Sri Venkateswara Creations.  The story revolves around a man who is struggling to make a living and his subsequent attempt to reconnect with his estranged daughter.
