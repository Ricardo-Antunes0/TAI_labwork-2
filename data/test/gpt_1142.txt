Laurie Tisch (born 1951) is an American businesswoman, the founder and CEO of The Tisch Group, a holding company that owns a number of businesses across a range of industries.  Laurie Tisch was born in 1951 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She attended the University of Pennsylvania, where she earned her undergraduate degree in 1973.   Tisch began her career as a sales representative for a telecommunications company. In 1980, she founded her own telecommunications company, which later became one of the largest in the United States. In 1997, she founded The Tisch Group, a holding company that owns a number of businesses across a range of industries.  Tisch is a philanthropist and has donated millions of dollars to charity. She is also a member of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Board of Governors and the Council on Foreign Relations. Tisch has been married to businessman Jeffrey Tisch since 1985.
