Rajagopalan is a renowned Indian vector mathematics researcher and professor at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. He has made significant contributions to the field of mathematical physics, particularly in the area of geometric vector spaces and their associated operators. His research has been published in leading journals such as "The Journal of Mathematical Physics", "SIAM Journal on Applied Mathematics", and "Journal of Physics A: Mathematical and General". He also served as editor-in-chief of the "Journal of Vector Analysis" from 2006 to 2010.  Rajagopalan received his bachelor's degree in mathematics from the University of Madras in 1976 and his master's degree in mathematics from the University of California, Berkeley in 1977.
