Heavy Rock Radio is an album by the British heavy metal band Judas Priest, released in 1979.  Heavy Rock Radio is the fourth studio album by the British heavy metal band Judas Priest, released in 1979. It was their first album with their new lead singer, K.K. Downing. The album saw a change in direction for the band, moving away from the traditional heavy metal sound of their earlier albums and towards a more hard rock and blues influenced sound. Despite this shift, Heavy Rock Radio remains a popular and influential album among heavy metal fans, and has been frequently cited as one of the band's best albums.
