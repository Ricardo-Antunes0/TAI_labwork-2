The Formosan termite (Coptotermes formosanus) is a subterranean termite found in Taiwan and adjacent islands. It is the only known subterranean termite in the world.  The Formosan termite is a small, slender termite about 1 cm long. It has a dark brown color and a flat head. The Formosan termite lives in colonies of up to 1000 individuals in the underground. It feeds on wood and other cellulose materials.
