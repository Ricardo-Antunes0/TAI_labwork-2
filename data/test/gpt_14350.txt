Paulo de Sacadura Cabral Portas (born 12 September 1977) is a Portuguese politician who has been a member of the European Parliament since 2014. He served as Minister of State for Foreign Affairs from 2011 to 2014.  Portas was born in Lisbon, Portugal, on 12 September 1977. He also holds an MBA from the Harvard Business School.  After completing his legal studies, Portas worked as a lawyer for several years. From 2001 to 2006, he was a research fellow at the Centre for European Reform (CER). Between 2006 and 2007, he was a consultant to the European Commission's Directorate-General for Justice and Consumers.  He entered national politics in 2007, when he was elected Member of Parliament for the Social Democratic Party (PSD). He served as Minister of State for Foreign Affairs from 2011 to 2014, in Prime Minister Pedro Passos Coelho's second cabinet.  Portas is a member of the European Parliament with the PSD and sits on the Committee on Foreign Affairs and on the Delegation for Relations with Israel.
