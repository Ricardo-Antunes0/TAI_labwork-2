The Vermont State Colleges System (VSCS) is a group of colleges and universities in the U.S. state of Vermont. The system has twelve institutions, including Bennington College, Champlain College, the University of Vermont, and Vermont Technical College.  The VSCS was created in 1967 as a merger of the Vermont State College (now the University of Vermont) and the Vermont Junior College (now Champlain College). The colleges had been founded in 1862 and 1891, respectively. The system has a total enrollment of 23,000 students.  The VSCS is divided into five colleges: the University of Vermont; Bennington College; Champlain College; Green Mountain College; and the Vermont Technical College.
