Meadowbrook Country Club is a country club located in the town of Rutherford, New Jersey, United States. The club was founded in 1905 and has an 18-hole golf course designed by noted golf course architect A.W. Tillinghast. The club also has a nine-hole golf course, a driving range, a pro shop, a clubhouse with a restaurant and lounge, and a fitness center.  Meadowbrook Country Club is one of the country's most scenic clubs. The club's 18-hole golf course is located on rolling hills and features tree-lined fairways and mowed greens. The Club also boasts a nine-hole golf course that is shorter but just as challenging as the 18-hole layout.   The Meadowbrook Clubhouse offers golfers of all skill levels an excellent setting to play their round of golf. The Clubhouse includes a pro shop, lounge and restaurant with panoramic views of the golf course, as well as a fitness center complete with state of the art cardiovascular equipment and full indoor tennis facilities.
