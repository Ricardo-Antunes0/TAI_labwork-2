{{Speciesbox |image=Carex breviculmis flowerhead5 NT - Flickr - Creative Commons License |name=Carex breviculmis |origin=North America |habitat=Woodlands and meadows |references=  }}  The Carex breviculmis is a species of sedge found in North America. It is a perennial herb with a tuft of slender, grass-like leaves at the base of a branching stem. The flowers are small, blue, and arranged in spike-like inflorescences.
