Tomonohana Shinya (born 23 June 1964 as Shinya Yamamoto) is a Japanese actor, singer and musician.  Tomonohana Shinya was born on 23 June 1964 in Tokyo, Japan. He is a Japanese actor, singer and musician. He is best known for his roles in the television series "Nanako no Taizai" (1998-1999), "Kimi no Na Wa" (2007-2008), "Kimi no Na Wa 2" (2011-2013), and "Papa no Iu Koto ga Suki na yo!" (2015-2016).
