Much Ado, Bernard J. Taylor's musical version of William Shakespeare's play, tells the story of a love triangle between Benedick, Beatrice and Hero.  This musical tells the story of two young lovers, Beatrice and Benedick, and the rivalry between them. While Beatrice is sweet and gentle, Benedick is witty and charming. However, their love is threatened by the arrival of Hero, Benedick's former friend and rival for Beatrice's affections. The battle of wits and hearts that ensues is a delight to watch.
