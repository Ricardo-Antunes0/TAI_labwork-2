Country Funk 1969–1975 is a compilation album by American country music artist George Jones. It was released on September 27, 1995, by MCA Nashville.  The album features selections from Jones' 1969 album "George Jones' Country Soul" through his 1975 album "Country Fever". The album reached number 6 on the "Billboard" Top Country Albums chart.  "Country Funk 1969–1975" received generally positive reviews from music critics. At AllMusic, critic Stephen Thomas Erlewine rated the album 4 out of 5 stars, calling it "a must-have for any fan of George Jones' country-rock era".
