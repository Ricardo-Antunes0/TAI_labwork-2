Thunderball is a 1965 British spy film directed by Terence Young and written by Peter Cook and Robert Fryer. It is based on the novel of the same name by Ian Fleming.  The film stars Sean Connery, David Niven, Maud Adams, and Peter Sellers. It was the third in the James Bond series and was produced by Eon Productions.  Thunderball tells the story of Bond's attempt to stop SPECTRE, a secret organisation led by Franz Sanchez, from detonating a nuclear bomb in Britain.
