Ek Thhi Naayka is a 16 episode Indian telenovela which aired on Star Plus from 1 March to 15 March 2016. The show is produced by Ekta Kapoor's Balaji Telefilms and stars Karthi and Nithya Menen in the lead roles.  The show tells the story of Mallika (Nithya Menen), a woman who returns to her ancestral village after marrying a man from a different culture. She is met with hostility from her family and friends, who believe that she has betrayed them by marrying out of caste. Mallika must find ways to win back their trust while also trying to balance her new life with her husband's demanding work schedule.
