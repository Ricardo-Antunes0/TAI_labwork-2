Rhonda Childress is an IBM Fellow Vice President and Distinguished Engineer. She has over 35 years of experience in the technology industry, including 12 years with IBM. Childress has held a number of leadership positions in IBM, including president of IBM Global Services and president and CEO of IBM Global Technology Services.  Childress has been involved in numerous industry initiatives and has been recognized for her contributions to the technology sector. She was named one of the world's 100 most powerful women by Forbes magazine in 2009, and she was also named one of Fortune magazine's "50 Most Powerful Women in Business" in 2006 and 2009. In 2012, Childress was inducted into the Georgia Women's Hall of Fame.
