Shiawassee Township is a civil township of Shiawassee County in the U.S. state of Michigan. The population was 2,697 at the 2010 census.  Shiawassee Township was organized in 1831, and named for the Shiawassee River. The township included all of present-day Shiawassee County north of the Shiawassee River when it was organized. In 1858, a portion of the township was transferred to Lowell Township to form the present Lowell Township. There were 1,272 housing units at an average density of 44.1 per square mile (17.6/km²).
