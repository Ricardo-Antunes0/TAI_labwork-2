Lone Oaks, also known as the Benjamin F. Shearer House, is a historic home located near Lynchburg, Lynchburg County, Virginia. It was built in 1855, and is a two-story, five bay, Federal style brick dwelling. It has a gable roof and features a two-story rear ell. It was the home of U.S. Representative Benjamin F. Shearer (1832-1916).  Lone Oaks was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
