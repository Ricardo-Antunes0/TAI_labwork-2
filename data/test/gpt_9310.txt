Frog Rock is a landmark located on the east bank of the Connecticut River, about 6.5 miles (10 km) south of Middletown, Connecticut.  Frog Rock is a large sandstone outcrop with a steep eastern face, rising to a height of 190 feet (58 m). The rock is heavily quarried for its sandstone blocks and slabs, which are used in construction and other industries. The site also contains a small pond and several hiking trails.
