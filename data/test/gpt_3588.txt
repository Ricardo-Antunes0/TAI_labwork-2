Peter Trippi is editor-in-chief of Fine Art America magazine and the author of The Art of Political Marketing: How to Sell Your Ideas and Influence People. He has been involved in politics and marketing for more than 25 years.  Trippi is known for his work in the political marketing field, where he has helped to craft successful campaigns for politicians at all levels of government. His work has been featured in The New York Times, Forbes, TIME, and other publications. Trippi is also a frequent speaker on the subject of politics and marketing.  Trippi was born in Detroit, Michigan, in 1963. He received his bachelor's degree from the University of Michigan in 1985 and his master's degree from New York University in 1990.
