AmericaSpeaks was a Washington, D.C.-based non-profit organization founded in 1993 by journalist James Moore. The organization's stated mission was to "promote the accurate and complete understanding of the English language in the United States." The organization's stated mission was to "promote the accurate and complete understanding of the English language in the United States." AmericaSpeaks published a quarterly magazine, "AmericaSpeaks: The Journal of English Language Arts," as well as a website.  AmericaSpeaks was headquartered in Washington, D.C., and had regional offices in Boston, Chicago, Dallas, Detroit, Los Angeles, Miami, New York City, and San Francisco. The organization also collaborated with other organizations to promote English language education. AmericaSpeaks ceased operations in 2006.
