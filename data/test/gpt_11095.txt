Dominik Paris (born 14 April 1989) is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for FC Ingolstadt 04.  Paris began his career with SC Freiburg and spent time on loan at SC Paderborn 07, 1. FSV Mainz 05, and VfL Wolfsburg before signing for FC Ingolstadt 04 in July 2013. He made his Bundesliga debut in April 2014 and was awarded the club's Player of the Year award in 2017.
