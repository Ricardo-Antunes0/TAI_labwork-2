Cole Robert Winn (born November 25, 1999) is an American soccer player who currently plays for Seattle Sounders FC 2 in the United Soccer League (USL).  Cole Winn was born on November 25, 1999, in Puyallup, Washington. He joined Sounders FC2's youth academy in 2016. Winn made his professional debut on April 14, 2018, as a substitute in a 4-1 loss to Orange County SC.
