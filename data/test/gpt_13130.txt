Gears Tactics is a turn-based tactics video game developed by The Coalition and published by Microsoft Studios for the Xbox One and Windows 10. It is the sequel to 2016's Gears of War 4, and was released on October 11, 2019.  The game is set in the same fictional universe as the other Gears games, and features characters and settings from the previous games. The game is a tactical combat game in which players command squads of soldiers in combat against enemies. Gears Tactics offers a new "Tactical Horde" mode that allows up to four players to cooperate in controlling an area, while the "Operations" mode returns from Gears of War 4 and allows players to create their own campaigns with unique objectives.
