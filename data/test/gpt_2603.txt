Paola Corso (May 28, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania) is an American actress, singer and model. She is known for her roles in the films The Blair Witch Project (1999), The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring (2001), and The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers (2002), and for her voice acting in the animated films Anastasia (1997) and Cars 2 (2011).
