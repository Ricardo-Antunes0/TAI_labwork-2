The Conference Basketball League (CBL) was a professional basketball league that operated in North America from 1985 to 2004. The league was founded as the National Basketball League (NBL) Western Conference in 1985, and added the Eastern Conference two years later. The league's name was changed to the Conference Basketball League in 1992. In 2002, the CBL merged with the National Basketball League (NBL) to form the National Basketball Association (NBA).
