Jean-Marc Lalonde  (born August 19, 1935) is a French Canadian author and journalist.  Born in Montreal, Quebec, Lalonde studied literature at the University of Montreal and became a journalist with the "Montreal Star". He has also worked for "Le Devoir", "La Presse" and "Le Monde".  Lalonde's books include the novels "Le Grand Nord" (1981), "Le Jardin d'Éden" (1985), and "Les Fausses Confidences" (1996). His non-fiction books include "Le Québec et sa culture" (1979), "Québec, province maudite" (1987), and "L'Amérique du Nord à la lumière du Québec" (2002).
