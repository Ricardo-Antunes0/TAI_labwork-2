James Reimer (; born March 15, 1988) is a Canadian professional ice hockey goaltender who is currently playing with the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League (NHL).  Reimer was selected by the Leafs with the second overall pick in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft. He has since played for the Maple Leafs, Carolina Hurricanes, and Buffalo Sabres. Reimer has also played in the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) for HC Sochi and Atlant Moscow Oblast.  Reimer was born in Peterborough, Ontario, and attended Peterborough Collegiate Institute. He played major junior with the Oshawa Generals of the Ontario Hockey League (OHL) before being drafted by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the second round, 46th overall, of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.  Reimer made his NHL debut on October 12, 2007 against the Ottawa Senators. He became the first player to record a shutout in his NHL debut since José Théodore did so on October 10, 1938. Reimer also became just the fourth goaltender in Maple Leafs history to post a shutout as a rookie. Reimer was named Rookie of the Month for October 2007 after posting four wins while posting a goals against average (GAA) of 1.96 and save percentage (.933) of .941.
