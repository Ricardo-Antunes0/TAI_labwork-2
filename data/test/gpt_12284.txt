Akkaraipattu (Tamil: அக்கரைப்பற்று, Sinhala: අක්කරපත්තුව) is a traditional form of puppetry from the Tamil-speaking regions of southern India. Akkaraipattu is a two-dimensional puppet show performed with wooden puppets and painted scenery. The story line generally revolves around the exploits of a heroic character or a mythological creature.
