Doryodes fusselli is a moth of the family Geometridae. It is found in North America, where it has been recorded from Arizona, California, Colorado, Illinois, Kentucky, Maine, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, New York, Ohio, Oklahoma, Ontario, Pennsylvania, Tennessee and Texas.  The wingspan is about 25–30 mm. The forewings are light tan with a dull brown fascia from the costa at one-third to the dorsum before the tornus. There is a brown-edged black dot near the apex and a black dash near the tornus. The hindwings are light tan with a brown-edged black discal spot and a faint brown fascia from the dorsum before the tornus to beyond the middle.
