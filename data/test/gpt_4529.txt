Sayulita is a small town in Mexico about 45 minutes north of Puerto Vallarta. It is known for its clear turquoise waters, stunning white-sand beaches, and relaxed atmosphere. Sayulita is a popular tourist destination due to its close proximity to Puerto Vallarta and the many activities available in the area, such as diving, windsurfing, snorkelling, and kayaking. The town has a small shopping centre with locally made souvenirs and crafts, as well as restaurants serving Mexican and international cuisine.
