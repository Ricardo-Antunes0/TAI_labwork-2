Mack and Mabel (often stylized as Mack & Mabel) was an American animated sitcom created by Don Bluth and released on ABC from September 17, 1971 to May 6, 1973. The series follows the adventures of Mack and Mabel, a married couple who live in a suburban neighborhood.   The series was produced by Bluth Productions and distributed by Walt Disney Television. The show's voice cast includes Don Messick as Mack and Ruth Buzzi as Mabel.
