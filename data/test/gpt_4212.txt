Desafío de Estrellas is a Mexican musical competition that began airing on Televisa in 2003. It is a talent show that pits young singers against each other in both domestic and international singing competitions.  The show has been hosted by various hosts over the years, but the most recent was Diana Ríos. The show typically features six or seven singers competing against each other in various singing contests, with the winner of each heat advancing to the next round. The grand prize is a recording contract with Sony Music Mexico.  Since its inception, Desafío de Estrellas has helped launch the careers of many famous Mexican singers, including Fher Olvera, José José, and Thalía.
