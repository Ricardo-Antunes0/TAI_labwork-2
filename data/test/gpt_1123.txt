La Cala Resort is a residential and commercial complex located in the municipality of Cala d'Or, on the north coast of Menorca, Balearic Islands, Spain.   The complex is composed of a residential area, with a total of 245 apartments, a shopping mall and a hotel with an indoor pool, spa and gym. It is operated by the Cala d'Or Tourist Development Company.  The resort was built in 1992 as a residential and commercial complex, with a total of 245 apartments. In 2006 it was expanded with a new shopping mall and an indoor pool, spa and gym. The complex is operated by the Cala d'Or Tourist Development Company.
