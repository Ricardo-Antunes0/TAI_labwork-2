Nanba Nanba  is a 2002 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film directed by K. S. Ravikumar and written by S. N. Balakrishnan. The film stars Kamal Haasan, Trisha Krishnan and Rajinikanth in lead roles.  The film tells the story of three friends: Raju (Kamal Haasan), a journalist; Thangavelu (Rajinikanth), a painter; and Kanimozhi (Trisha Krishnan), a college student. Raju falls in love with Thangavelu's sister, Kanimozhi. The two families get embroiled in a conflict over their relationship.
