Danny Patrick (born 25 April 1978) is a British film director, screenwriter and producer. He is known for his films The Duelists (2011), The Last Goodbye (2015) and A Star Is Born (2018).  Patrick was born in 1978 in Maidenhead, Berkshire, England. He graduated from the University of East Anglia with a degree in Film and Television Studies.  Patrick's first feature film, The Duelists, was released in 2011. It was based on the true story of two brothers who fought a duel to the death. The film received positive reviews and was nominated for the BAFTA Award for Best British Film.   Patrick's second film, The Last Goodbye, was released in 2015. It is a psychological thriller directed by Patrick and written by him and David Barron. The film stars Tom Hiddleston and Mia Wasikowska and has been critically acclaimed.   Patrick's next film, A Star Is Born, was released in 2018. It is an adaptation of the 1937 novel of the same name by William Golding and stars Bradley Cooper and Lady Gaga. The film has received widespread acclaim from critics and has been nominated for numerous awards, including Academy Awards for Best Picture and Best Director.
