My Little Princess is a 2011 French-Romanian animated film written and directed by Isao Takahata. The film is based on the 1992 French novel of the same name by Francesca Simon.  The film tells the story of a young girl, Violet, who is sent to live with her cruel stepmother and her stepsister in a small town after her parents are killed in a car accident. Violet must learn to survive in a world where she is not wanted and where she is constantly misunderstood. Despite her difficult circumstances, Violet discovers the power of friendship and love, and eventually finds her way back to her family and home.
