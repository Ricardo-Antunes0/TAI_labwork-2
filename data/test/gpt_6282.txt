U.S. Marshals is a 1998 American action film directed by Antoine Fuqua and written by Andy Mikita. The film stars Angelina Jolie, Tommy Lee Jones, and Ving Rhames.  U.S. Marshals tells the story of a team of U.S. marshals who are sent to capture a fugitive, but the marshals soon find themselves in a race against time to stop the fugitive from escaping.
