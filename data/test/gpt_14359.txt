Surakiart Sathirathai (; ; born June 7, 1978) is a Thai professional boxer who has held the IBF light-middleweight title since 2016.  Sathirathai was born in 1978 in the southern province of Pattani, Thailand. He started boxing at the age of sixteen and turned professional in 2000. He won his first eight fights by knockout, including an eight-round decision over future world champion Fernando Vargas.  In March 2010, Sathirathai fought Argentine veteran Lucas Matthysse to a twelve-round draw in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The fight was televised in Thailand and attracted a peak audience of over 2 million viewers.  In February 2016, Sathirathai defeated American Jermall Charlo by unanimous decision to win the IBF light-middleweight title. The fight was held at the Phones 4u Arena in Manchester, England.   Sathirathai is scheduled to defend his title against Australian Jake Donovan on November 17, 2018, at the Siam Paragon Stadium in Bangkok, Thailand.
