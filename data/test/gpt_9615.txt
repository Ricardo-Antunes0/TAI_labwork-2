The Kunya  is a river in central Albania. It is a left tributary of the Devoll River.   The Kunya rises in the Mat mountain range and flows through the municipalities of Dibër, Lushnjë, Tirana, Fier, Pogradec and Korçë. The river is approximately long and has a drainage basin of .  The Kunya is fed by numerous tributaries including the Mala Mala, the Zagoni, the Krujë, the Selanik and the Lapaj.
