Deswa  is a 2011 Indian Bhojpuri language romantic comedy film directed by Rajesh Khanna and produced by Khanna and Ashok Roy. It stars Arjun Rampal, Dimple Kapadia, Sunny Deol and Riteish Deshmukh in lead roles. The film is about a man who falls in love with his best friend's wife.  The film is set in the fictional village of Deswa, which is located in the state of Uttar Pradesh. The story follows the ups and downs of the relationship between Raghu (Arjun Rampal) and Radha (Dimple Kapadia), as they try to navigate their way through the challenges of a true love affair.  The film was shot extensively in the villages of Greater Noida and Uttar Pradesh's Saharanpur district. The soundtrack was composed by Anand-Milind. "Deswa" was released on 14 December 2011 to mixed reviews from critics but became a sleeper hit at the Box Office.
