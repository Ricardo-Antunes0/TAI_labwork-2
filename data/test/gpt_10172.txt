Nikitas M. Kaklamanis (; born 1 April 1990) is a professional Greek footballer who plays as a midfielder for PAOK FC.  Kaklamanis started his career at AEP Paphos, making his debut in the 2006–07 season. In January 2009, he moved to Olympiacos Piraeus. He made his debut for the club on 12 February 2009 in a 3–1 home victory against Panionios, coming on as a substitute for Konstantinos Tsiolkas in the 73rd minute. He scored his first goal for Olympiacos Piraeus against Anorthosis Famagusta on 5 May 2010.  On 14 July 2013, Kaklamanis signed a three-year contract with Panathinaikos F.C.. Kaklamanis made his professional debut for Panathinaikos in a 2–1 away loss to Asteras Tripolis on 20 July 2013. On 22 October 2013, he scored his first goal for Panathinaikos in an 8–0 home victory over Levadiakos. On 20 September 2014, he played the full 90 minutes in a 2–1 home victory over Veria, helping to secure a spot in the 2014–15 UEFA Europa League group stage.
