Lybrate is a mobile healthcare technology company that enables patients to communicate with their doctors and nurses remotely.  Lybrate was founded in 2014 by a team of medical professionals who were passionate about making remote healthcare accessible to everyone. Our mission is to help people maintain their health and connect with the people who are best equipped to help them, wherever they are.  We offer a suite of mobile apps that allow patients to communicate with their doctors and nurses remotely. Our apps are designed to be simple to use and easy to access, no matter where you are in the world.  Our team of experts is constantly working to improve our products and make them more accessible and user-friendly. We believe that every person deserves access to the best possible medical care, no matter where they are in the world.  Thank you for reading!
