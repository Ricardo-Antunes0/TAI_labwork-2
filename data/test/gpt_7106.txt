The Vaal River Barrage Reservoir is a man-made water body located in the Free State province of South Africa. It forms part of the Vaal River system and was constructed between 1966 and 1975 as part of the apartheid-era Water Act. The Vaal River system forms the largest artificial river in the world and together with the Thukela Reservoir, it supplies water to major metropolitan areas in South Africa, including Johannesburg, Pretoria and Cape Town.
