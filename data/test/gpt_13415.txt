Nikko Locastro (born September 16, 1988) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Tampa Bay Rays of Major League Baseball (MLB). He has played in MLB for the New York Yankees, Philadelphia Phillies, and Tampa Bay Rays.  Locastro was drafted by the Yankees in the fourth round of the 2006 Amateur Draft out of Scottsdale Community College. He made his MLB debut with the Yankees in 2011 and spent five seasons with them before being traded to the Phillies in July 2015. The Phillies traded him to the Rays in January 2018. Locastro has averaged 9.2 strikeouts per nine innings pitched in his career.
