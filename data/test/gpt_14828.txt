Steve Linsdell, (born 1955), is a British scientist who specializes in water resources.  Linsdell was born in Liverpool, England. After completing his undergraduate studies at the University of Liverpool, he received his Ph.D. from the University of Cambridge in 1984. He has been a professor at the University of Arizona since 1991, where he is also the director of the university's Water Resources Institute.  Linsdell's research employs a variety of methods to study water resources, including basin-scale modeling, field studies, and laboratory experiments. He has published more than 200 papers and books on the subject. He is also a fellow of both the American Physical Society and the Royal Society of Chemistry.
