Global Integrity is an independent, nonprofit organization based in Boston, Massachusetts. Global Integrity was founded in 2001 by former diplomats, journalists and business executives who were concerned about the decline of global democracy and the rise of international corruption.  Global Integrity's mission is to promote democratic values, transparency and accountability in the international system and to support the building of an ethical global society. The organization conducts research, produces publications and provides advocacy and training to institutions, businesses and individuals around the world.  Global Integrity is funded principally by individual donors, foundations and corporations. The organization has a staff of more than 30 full-time professionals.
