Shaun Patton (born 1995) is an Irish professional footballer who plays as a right back for Wolverhampton Wanderers.  Patton started his career with Dundalk, making his senior debut in the 2014–15 season. He spent two seasons with the club, before joining Wolves in July 2017. He made his debut for the club in the EFL Cup against Bristol City, and went on to make forty-two appearances for Wolves in all competitions during the 2017–18 season.
