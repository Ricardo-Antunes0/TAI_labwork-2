Murray County is a county located in the U.S. state of Oklahoma. As of the 2010 census, the population was 7,698. The county seat is Murray.  Murray County is included in the Tulsa, OK Metropolitan Statistical Area.  The Murray County Courthouse was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1980.  According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of , of which is land and (0.4%) is water.     As of the census of 2000, there were 7,814 people, 3,077 households, and 2,229 families residing in the county. The population density was 10 people per square mile (4/km²). There were 3,744 housing units at an average density of 4 per square mile (1/km²).
