Bhajarangi 2 is an Indian Kannada-language fantasy film directed and co-produced by S.S. Rajamouli and produced by his father, C.R. Prasad. It is the sequel to the 2009 film Bhajarangi, and stars Yash Chopra, Samantha Ruth Prabhu and Raghuvaran in lead roles. The film was announced in May 2017 and began production in June 2017 in Mysore, Karnataka. The film was released on 20 November 2018 worldwide on Disney’s India subsidiary Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures.  Bhajarangi 2 tells the story of Prince Rama (Yash Chopra), who has to banish the demon king Ravana (Raghuvaran) from Earth after he kidnaps Sita (Samantha Ruth Prabhu). Meanwhile, Sita’s brother Lakshmana (Ravi Teja) sets out to find her with the help of his friend Hanuman (Darshan Jariwala).
