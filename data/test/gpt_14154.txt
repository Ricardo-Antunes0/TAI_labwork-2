I Remember Nelson is a British historical television series which aired on BBC Two from 20 January to 4 March 2014. The series is written and directed by David Nicholls, and stars Dominic West as Arthur Wellesley, Duke of Wellington, and Ruth Wilson as Lady Sarah Lennox.  The series tells the story of the Anglo-Irish War of 1803-1814, and focuses on the final years of the Duke of Wellington's life.
