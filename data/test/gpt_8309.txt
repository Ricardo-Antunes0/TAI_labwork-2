Sounds of Punjab was released on November 25, 2009 in India. The album consists of nine tracks and features the vocals of singers such as Rahat Fateh Ali Khan, Kailash Kher, and Kumar Sanu.  Punjabi music is a popular form of music found in the state of Punjab in northern India. The region is known for its distinctive vocal style and the music reflects this. Many popular singers and bands hail from Punjab, including Rahat Fateh Ali Khan, Kailash Kher, and Kumar Sanu. Sounds of Punjab is a compilation album featuring some of the best Punjabi music ever recorded.
