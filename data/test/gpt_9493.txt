Whittier is a neighborhood within the Powderhorn District of Minneapolis, Minnesota. The neighborhood is bordered by the University of Minnesota on the east, Lyndale Avenue on the north, Cedar Lake on the west, and 25th Street on the south.  The Powderhorn District is a historic district in Minneapolis that was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1978. Whittier is a small neighborhood within the district and is one of the few historic neighborhoods in Minneapolis that was not developed as part of downtown. The neighborhood's oldest house was built in 1881 and its most recent was built in 1987.
