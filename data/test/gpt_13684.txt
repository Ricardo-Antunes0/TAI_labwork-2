The first official meeting between the two sides took place on 18 October 1892 in Munich and since then, the two teams have played each other at least once every season, usually in the last game of the season. The most recent meeting between the two teams was on 15 May 2019.      The first Bavarian football derby was played between Bayern Munich and TSV 1860
