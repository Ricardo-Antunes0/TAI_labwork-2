Banipal is an independent literary magazine dedicated to experimental writing, visual art and music. It was founded in 2000 in Brooklyn, New York by editors Tariq Ali, Akram Khan and Parvez Sharma.  Banipal is devoted to the publication of new writing and art that challenges conventional wisdom and explores the boundaries of expression. The journal publishes poetry, short stories, essays, visual art, music and video.
