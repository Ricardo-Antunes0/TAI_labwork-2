The M12 Force Application Light Reconnaissance Vehicle, or Warthog, is a heavily armed ground vehicle used by the UNSC in the Halo universe. The Warthog was designed as a versatile transport and reconnaissance vehicle, able to carry a variety of weaponry and support a sizable infantry squad.  The Warthog first appeared in the first game of the Halo series, Halo: Combat Evolved, serving as the game's main vehicle for movement and combat. In subsequent games in the series, the Warthog has been updated with new features and capabilities, most notably in Halo 2 and Halo 3 where it can be equipped with a variety of vehicles and explosives. The Warthog also appears in the 2010 video game Halo: Reach as a main vehicle for multiplayer gameplay.
