The Hepparays was an Australian rock n' roll band that formed in Sydney in the early 1960s. The group consisted of lead singer and songwriter Dick Hepparay, guitarists Ron Starr and Garry Beers, bassist Ross Wilson and drummer Geoff Collins.  The Hepparays achieved national success in the early 1970s with their singles "I'm a Believer" (1972), "The Night Has a Thousand Eyes" (1973) and "Doctor Doctor" (1974). The group disbanded in 1975.  Dick Hepparay died on 17 February 2016, aged 74.
