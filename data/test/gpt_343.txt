Petoskey High School is a high school located in Petoskey, Michigan. It was founded in 1875, and is the oldest continuously operating public high school west of the Great Lakes. The current building was completed in 1938.  Petoskey High School has a rich history, and has produced many notable alumni. These include business executives, politicians, academics, and athletes. The school's mascot is the Pirates, and the school colors are blue and gold.
