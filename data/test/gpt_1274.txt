The Cadillac Three, originally known as The Raconteurs, are an American rock band from Columbus, Ohio. Formed in 2004, the group comprises Jack White (guitar, vocals), John Paul Jones (bass, vocals), and Jared Followill (drums).  The band's debut album, Broken Boy Soldiers, was released in 2006 and received critical acclaim. The follow-up album, Consolers of the Lonely, was released in 2009 and produced three singles that charted in the United States: "The Devil's Share", "Out of My Mind", and "I'm Waiting on a Song". The band's third album, El Camino, was released in November 2014 and debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart.
