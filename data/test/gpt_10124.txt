Michael H. Corbin (born 1960) is a Canadian psychologist who has focused on the study of personality, psychopathology and addiction.   He is currently a Professor at the University of Ottawa in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. He has authored or co-authored over 200 journal articles and book chapters, as well as the book "Personality Disorders: Theory, Research and Treatment" (Wiley, 2003).  He was elected a Fellow of the American Psychological Association in 2006, and a Fellow of the Royal Society of Canada in 2009.
