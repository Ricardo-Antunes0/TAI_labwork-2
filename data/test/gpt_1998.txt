Bigfoot is a monster truck. The original Bigfoot was a Chevrolet C10 truck that was modified by Jay Lynch and built in 1976. Lynch created the Bigfoot name and the truck was featured in an episode of the TV show "Monster Squad". The current Bigfoot is a 15-foot, 6-inch-tall, 1,500-pound monster truck with a 12-cylinder engine, four-wheel drive, and a suspension that can handle a 50-foot drop.
