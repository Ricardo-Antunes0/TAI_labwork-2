Evan Fong (born 31 May 1992), known by his stage name VanossGaming, is a French professional video game player who specializes in first-person shooters.  VanossGaming was founded in December 2013 by Evan Fong, who is also the team's captain. The team is composed of players from France, Belgium, the Netherlands, and Spain. They are notable for their consistent placing in the global rankings of professional video game tournaments, including first place at the Electronic Sports World Cup 2016 and the third place finish at the Halo World Championship 2016.  VanossGaming also produces their own video games, such as Vanoss III and Reload, which they release on Steam and other digital platforms.
