Kulimoe'anga Stone Maka, is an interdisciplinary artist and educator based in Hawai'i. She is the author of Stone Maka: A Hawaiian Earth Artist (University of Hawai'i Press, 2016), and the founder and director of Stone Maka Center for Art and Education, which is located in Honolulu.  Stone Maka has been working with materials found on the earth, such as rocks, shells, coral, and sand, for over forty years. She integrates these natural materials into her artwork to create sculptures and installations that explore the beauty and power of nature. Her work has been exhibited in galleries and museums around the world, including the Honolulu Museum of Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Museum of Fine Arts in Boston.
