Energetický a průmyslový holding, a.s. (EPH) is a multinational company, headquartered in Prague, Czech Republic.   EPH is the largest Czech company in terms of both revenue and employees. The company’s core businesses are the production and sale of energy products and services. EPH also offers a wide range of other services, such as engineering and construction, financial services, and information technology.   EPH has operations in more than 20 countries around the world. The company’s products are sold in more than 100 countries. EPH is a leading player in the Czech energy market and one of the largest suppliers of energy products and services in Europe.
