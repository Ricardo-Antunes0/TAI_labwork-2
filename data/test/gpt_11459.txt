Desamuduru ( Super Mischief [Not lit.]) is a Telugu film released on 14 August 2007. It is produced by Allu Aravind and directed by S. S. Rajamouli.  The film stars Venkatesh, Trisha and Soundarya in lead roles with music composed by M. M. Keeravani. The story is based on the novel of the same name by Kannada author R. K. Narayan.  The film received mixed reviews from critics, but was commercially successful and became a blockbuster at the box office, becoming the highest-grossing Telugu film of all time.
