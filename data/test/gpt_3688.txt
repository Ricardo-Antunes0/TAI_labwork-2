Jose Alexander Cora (born October 18, 1975) is a Dominican-born American professional baseball manager and former player. He has served as the manager of the Boston Red Sox of Major League Baseball (MLB) since December 2015.  Cora was drafted by the Philadelphia Phillies in the first round of the 1993 MLB draft and played for them for six seasons before being traded to the Houston Astros in 2000. He spent four seasons with the Astros before being traded to the Boston Red Sox in 2004. Cora played for Boston for 10 seasons, winning three World Series championships (2007, 2013, and 2017). After retiring as a player, Cora served as a coach for the Red Sox from 2010 to 2015. He was named manager of the team in December 2015.
