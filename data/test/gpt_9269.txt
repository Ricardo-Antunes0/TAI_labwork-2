Ronald Kreer (born 10 November 1959 in Heidelberg) is a German songwriter and singer.  He is the son of the composer and conductor Werner Kreer. After studying musicology, he began his career as a songwriter in the early 1980s. Kreer has written songs for both German and international artists, including Sandra, Celine Dion, Michael Jackson, and Julio Iglesias. His most successful singles include "Es gibt nichts (Ich liebe Dich)", "Das Glück", and "Ich hätte dich gern". Kreer has won three Grammy Awards, four Golden Globe Awards, and an Academy Award for Best Song. He has also been nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor for his role in the film Die Traumschwester.
