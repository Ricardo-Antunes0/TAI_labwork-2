American actor and comedian Robin Williams (1951–2014) has appeared in many films and television shows throughout his career. He is known for his roles in films such as Dead Poets Society (1989), Good Will Hunting (1997), The Fisher King (1991), Awakenings (1990), and Mrs. Doubtfire (1993). Williams has also starred in television shows such as Mork & Mindy (1978–1982), Good Morning, Vietnam (1987–1989), Dead Like Me (2003–2004), and Arrested Development (2003–2006).
