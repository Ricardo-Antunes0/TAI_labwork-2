George Leonard Carlson  (1887 - September 1, 1974) was a United States Army officer and a recipient of the Medal of Honor.  Carlson was born in 1887 in Moline, Illinois. He enlisted in the United States Army during World War I and was later commissioned as a second lieutenant. During World War II, Carlson served with the 8th Infantry Regiment, 4th Infantry Division and earned the Medal of Honor for his actions at the Battle of the Bulge. After the war, Carlson retired from the Army and settled in California. Carlson died on September 1, 1974 in Santa Barbara, California.
