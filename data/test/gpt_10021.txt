Gorkhmaz Abysh oglu Eyvazov (; born 1 March 1955) is an Azerbaijani politician and former Minister of Defense of Azerbaijan. He served as the head of the Republican Party of Azerbaijan from 2009 to 2017.  Eyvazov was born in Baku, Azerbaijan SSR. He graduated from the Azerbaijan State Medical University in 1978. He served as deputy head of the military hospital in the Baku Military District from 1981 to 1984. From 1984 to 1992, he was a deputy commander of the Azerbaijani army. In 1992, he became commander of the Azerbaijani army's Armoured Forces Division. He served as a Deputy Prime Minister of Azerbaijan from February to October 1995. From October 1995 to March 1998, he was Minister for Defense of Azerbaijan. From March 1998 to August 1999, he was General Commander of the Azerbaijani Armed Forces. In August 1999, he became head of the State Security Service (FSB) of Azerbaijan.  Eyvazov was appointed Minister for Defense on 23 October 2009, replacing Colonel General Rustam Ismayilov. On 18 January 2017, Eyvazov resigned from his post as Minister after being accused by President Ilham Aliyev of corruption and nepotism related to his family's business interests.
