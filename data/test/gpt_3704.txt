Alyson Kay Wagner (born August 10, 1980) is an American actress and singer. Wagner is known for her acting roles in the films ThePerks of Being a Wallflower (2012), Me and Earl and the Dying Girl (2015), and Dunkirk (2017). She has also released two albums, Ride (2012) and Heartworm (2015).  Born in Los Angeles, California, Wagner began her acting career at the age of 10, appearing in the television series My Family and Other Aliens, before appearing in the film Me and Earl and the Dying Girl. In 2015, she starred in the independent drama film Dunkirk, for which she was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress. In 2017, she starred in the horror-thriller film It.
