Richard Dean "Dick" Parsons (born April 4, 1933) is an American business executive and philanthropist. He served as the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Citigroup from 1984 to 2003 and was the founder of Parsons Corporation, a holding company.   Parsons was born in Chicago, Illinois, United States. He attended Yale University, where he was a member of the Skull and Bones society. He later earned an MBA from Harvard Business School.
