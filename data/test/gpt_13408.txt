FabricLive.15 is a DJ mix compilation album by English electronic music producer and DJ Paul Oakenfold. It was released on 24 January 2015 through Ministry of Sound.  The album features a selection of Oakenfold's own original tracks and remixes, as well as covers of songs by artists such as Kaskade, Fatboy Slim, and Swedish House Mafia.  The album has received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 73, based on 13 reviews.
