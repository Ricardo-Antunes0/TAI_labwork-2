Toy & Wing were an American tap house and bar located in the West Village of Manhattan, New York City.  Founded by restaurateurs Jesse Karmazin and Erik Bruner, Toy & Wing was a popular spot for beer, wine, and cocktails in the West Village. The menu featured small plates and pizzas as well as a range of sandwiches. The bar also had a large selection of spirits.  In November 2018, it was announced that Toy & Wing will be closing its doors after 18 years in business.
