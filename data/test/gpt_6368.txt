Darwin Airline SA was a Swiss regional airline that operated scheduled services from Zurich to destinations in Europe and Africa from its base at Zurich-Halle Airport. The airline ceased operations on 15 September 2009.  Darwin Airline was founded in 2002 and started operations in 2003.
