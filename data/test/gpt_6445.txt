The 2014 Tamil film Lingaa, despite being generally well-received by audiences and critics, has been met with criticism for its loss issue.  Lingaa is a 2014 Tamil film directed by S. Shankar and produced by K.V. Anand. The film stars Rajinikanth and Aishwarya Rai in the lead roles with Sathyaraj, Vivek, Dinesh and Radhika Apte in supporting roles. The story is based on the legend of the Tamils warrior king, Lingaa.  The film's music was composed by AR Rahman and the cinematography was done by Gopi Sundar. The film was released on 5 August 2014 to positive reviews from critics, who praised the visual effects and action sequences. However, the loss issue received widespread criticism, with many viewers alleging that it played a pivotal role in ruining the film's overall appeal.
