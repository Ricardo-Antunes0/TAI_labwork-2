The AECOM Building, formerly known as the AT&T Building and the Sony Building, is a skyscraper located in Midtown Manhattan, New York City. Completed in 1984, it is the fourth-tallest building in New York City and the 31st-tallest building in the United States.  The AECOM Building is a Class A office building located on Fifth Avenue between 40th and 41st Streets in Midtown Manhattan. The tower has 44 floors and rises 527 feet (161 m) above ground level. It is the fourth-tallest building in New York City and the 31st-tallest building in the United States. The tower was designed by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill and built by Beyer Blinder Belle.
