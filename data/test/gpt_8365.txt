Brandon Mychal Smith (born May 29, 1989) is an American actor, comedian and writer. He is known for his roles as Dylan McKay on the CW television series Teen Wolf, Asher Millstone on the Netflix series Stranger Things, and Rick Grimes on the AMC television series The Walking Dead.  Brandon Mychal Smith was born in Los Angeles, California. He is of Jamaican descent. He has two older brothers. Smith started acting at the age of four, appearing in a number of television commercials. After high school, he moved to New York City to study at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute. While there, he appeared in a number of off-Broadway productions.  In 2011, Smith landed his first major role on the CW television series Teen Wolf. The series aired for six seasons before ending in 2015. In 2017, he co-created and starred in the Netflix series Stranger Things. The series was met with critical acclaim and has been nominated for several awards, including a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Drama Series. In 2018, he reprised his role as Rick Grimes on the seventh season of The Walking Dead.
