Ephraim Island is luxury residential development located in the heart of the city of Dubai. Ephraim Island is a private development comprising of high-rise apartments, villas and penthouses, offering breathtaking views of the Dubai skyline and the Arabian Gulf.  Ephraim Island is one of the most prestigious luxury residential developments in Dubai and has become a favourite spot for City residents and tourists alike. The island is only a short walk from all the action in Dubai and provides an escape from the bustle of everyday life.   The luxurious apartments, villas and penthouses at Ephraim Island come with all the amenities you could wish for, including state-of-the-art security systems, stunning views and plenty of leisure facilities. There are also plenty of shops, restaurants and cafes on site to take advantage of, making it the perfect destination for a relaxing break.
