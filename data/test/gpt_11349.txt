Michael Mullan is an English-American researcher in the field of folklife. He is the author of several books on the subject, including The Folklife of Ireland (1980), England's Folk Heritage (1986), and The Irish Traditional Music Book (1996).  Michael Mullan was born in England in 1936. After studying at Cambridge University, he emigrated to the United States in 1958. Mullan has been a professor at the University of California, Santa Barbara since 1978, and he also served as president of the American Folklore Society from 1999 to 2001. He has received numerous awards, including a Guggenheim Fellowship and a MacArthur Fellowship.
