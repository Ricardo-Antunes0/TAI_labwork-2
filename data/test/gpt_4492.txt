Champions of Hell is a comic book series written by Brian Wood and illustrated by Riccardo Burchielli. The series is published by Image Comics.  The series tells the story of a group of monsters who have been exiled from Heaven and are now struggling to survive in the world of man. The monsters are forced to fight for survival against all kinds of humans, including corrupt politicians, criminals, and even religious zealots.  The series has been praised for its dark and gritty atmosphere, its well-developed characters, and its unique take on the genre.
