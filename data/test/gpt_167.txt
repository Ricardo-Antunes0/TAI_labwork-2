The Homestead Harmonizers are a chorus created by the American folk singer Pete Seeger in 1941. The group sing traditional folk songs and harmony arrangements of popular songs like "The Battle Hymn of the Republic" and "Yankee Doodle". They are most well-known for their 1940 recording of "This Land Is Your Land", which was inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame in 2012.
