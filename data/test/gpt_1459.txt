She has provided voice acting for characters in shows such as Attack on Titan, Sword Art Online, and Naruto Shippūden. She is also known for her work in the Fate/Apocrypha and A Silent Voice soundtracks.  Mikoi Sasaki was born on July 15, 1991 in Saitama Prefecture, Japan. After graduating from high school, Sasaki started working as a part-time announcer for a local FM station. In 2010, she debuted as a voice actress by providing the voice of Akari Akatsu in the anime series Free! Iwatobi Swim Club. From then on, she has been featured in a number of roles in various anime and video games, including those in Attack on Titan, Sword Art Online, Naruto Shippūden, Fate/Apocrypha, and A Silent Voice.
