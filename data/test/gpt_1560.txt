Stephen Crawley (born 16 September 1962 in Kingston upon Hull) is an English former professional footballer who played as a striker.  Crawley began his career at Hull City, making his debut in 1981. He played for the club for eleven seasons, scoring 86 goals in 278 appearances. He then moved to Sheffield United, where he played for two seasons. He then moved to Manchester City, where he played for six seasons. Crawley then moved to Norwich City, where he played for three seasons. He then retired from football.  Crawley has also been a coach at Sheffield United and Manchester City.
