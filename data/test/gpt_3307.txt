Beat Shazam is an American television musical drama series created by Peter Safran and Robert Carlock. The series is set in the fictional Rockwell City, a working-class suburb of Los Angeles, California, and follows the daily lives of its residents.  The series stars Jack Dylan Grazer, Fionn Whitehead, and Anthony Ramos as school friends turned music entrepreneurs Freddy, Darcy, and Pete, who use their innovative skills to start up a music company in their hometown.   On May 12, 2019, it was announced that Fox had cancelled the series after three seasons.
