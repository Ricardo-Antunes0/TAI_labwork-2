The Atos Worldline FASTticket system is a system used by Atos to create electronic tickets for events and appointments. It is a paperless system that allows users to submit their requests and receive their tickets via email or the Atos Worldline website. The system is based on the use of RFID technology, which allows users to access their tickets without having to carry them around.
