In Hinduism, the goddess Tara  is the manifestation of the divine feminine principle. She is the goddess of love, compassion, and wisdom. Tara is the mother of the god Hanuman, and the wife of Lord Rama.  Tara is considered one of the most important goddesses in Hinduism. She is worshipped as a protector and guide, and is often depicted with a bow and arrow. Her temples are located throughout India and Sri Lanka, and her festivals are celebrated throughout the year.
