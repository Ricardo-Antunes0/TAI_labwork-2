The Alley is a restaurant and piano bar in the Merchant's Exchange district of Manhattan, New York City. The Alley is known for its eclectic music, creative cocktails and its "signature" chicken and waffles.  The Alley was founded by restaurateurs Cathy O'Brien and her husband, John. The Alley opened in 2006 and quickly became a popular spot for New Yorkers looking for a unique dining experience. The Alley features live music every night, with a wide variety of performers ranging from jazz to blues to funk to alternative rock. In addition to the regular menu, The Alley offers a variety of creative cocktails made with house-made syrups and mixers. The Alley's signature dish is its chicken and waffles, which are served with a variety of toppings including maple syrup and bacon.
