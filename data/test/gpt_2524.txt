Christopher Richard Borrett (born 2 December 1979) is an English actor and musician. He is known for his roles as Dr. George O'Malley in the BBC medical drama series "Holby City" and as Alfie Enoch in the ITV soap opera "EastEnders".  Borrett was born in Liverpool, Merseyside, England. He trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA).  Borrett started his career on stage, appearing in productions of "The Seagull" and "Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Dead". He made his television debut in 2006 with a guest appearance in the BBC series "Life on Mars". In 2007, he portrayed Dr. George O'Malley in the BBC medical drama series "Holby City". He also played Alfie Enoch in the ITV soap opera "EastEnders" from 2010 to 2014. Borrett has also appeared in films such as "The Final Destination", "The World's End" and "Don't Tell Mom the Perks of Being a Wallflower".
