Babu is a common given name in India, meaning "son of the father". Notable people with the name include:  • Babu Jagjivan Ram, 10th President of India • Babu Jagannath, a noted Hindu deity  • Babu, a fictional character in the TV series Sherlock  • Babuji, a character in the novel Devdas by Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay
