Tesada'a Bemeen  (English: Who Do You Love?) is a 2002 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film written and directed by K. S. Ravikumar. The film stars Vijay, Sneha and Manoj Bajpayee in the lead roles, with Raghubir Yadav, Kushboo and Vijayakumar in supporting roles.  The film follows the story of an estranged couple who are brought together by chance when they are both involuntarily involved in a car accident. Despite their differences, the two find that they still love each other.   Tesadaa Bemeen received mixed reviews from critics. However, it was a box office success, becoming one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of 2002.
