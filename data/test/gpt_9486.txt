Mental Floss (stylized as mental_floss) is an American magazine, published six times a year, that covers "all things mental" in a humorous and entertaining way. It is aimed at a general audience and has articles on everything from history to science to pop culture.  The magazine was founded in 1997 by Dave Kelleher and John Flansburgh of the band The Bouncing Souls. Mental Floss has since expanded to include a website, an app, and an international edition. As of 2018, it had an estimated circulation of 1.5 million.  Mental Floss is headquartered in New York City.
