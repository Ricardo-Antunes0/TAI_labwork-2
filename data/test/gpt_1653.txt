The Alvarado wrestling family, also referred to as the Alvarado wrestling dynasty, is a family of professional wrestlers who are currently performing in the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) developmental territory NXT. The dynasty comprises of four brothers: Rey Mysterio Jr., El Hijo del Rey Mysterio, Prince Puma and Rush.  The Alvarado wrestling family is one of the most successful families in professional wrestling. The four brothers – Rey Mysterio Jr., El Hijo del Rey Mysterio, Prince Puma and Rush – have collectively held eighteen championships between them, including eleven world titles. Rey Mysterio Jr. is the most successful member of the family, having won eleven world championships, seven tag team championships and three Intercontinental championships.
