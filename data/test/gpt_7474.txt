Suraj Prakash (lit. "The Light of the Morning") is an Indian film actor and model who predominantly appears in Bollywood films. He has also done some work in Telugu and Kannada cinema.  Born in Pune, Maharashtra, Suraj Prakash started his career with small roles in various Marathi and Hindi films. He made his Bollywood debut with the 2003 film "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge". He then starred in successful films such as "Love Story 2050" (2004), "Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani" (2013), and "Sanju" (2018).  He has also appeared in Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada films. He was appointed the brand ambassador of the state of Maharashtra for the year 2018.
