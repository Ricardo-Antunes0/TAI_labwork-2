Kill Decision is a science fiction novel by American author Daniel Abraham. It was first published in hardcover in the United States on January 2, 2019 by Tor Books.  The novel tells the story of a future world where governments have become dependent on kill decisions, or orders to kill any and all inhabitants of a target city in order to prevent it from falling into the hands of the enemy. In this future, the only way to guarantee a kill decision is to have someone inside the government who can provide reliable information about the enemy's plans.  The novel has been praised for its well-drawn world, its compelling characters, and its suspenseful plot.
