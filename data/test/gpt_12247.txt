PIMEX is one of the so-called video game console manufacturers in Japan. Formed in 1981 as a joint venture between Nintendo and Sony, the company became a wholly owned subsidiary of Sony in 1996.  PIMEX produces video game hardware, software, and accessories for the Sony PlayStation 2 and Nintendo GameCube platforms. The company has also produced systems for the Microsoft Xbox, including the Xbox 360 and Xbox One.  As of March 31, 2018, PIMEX had sold over 59 million units globally.
