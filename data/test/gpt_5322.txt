Paisley Currah is a political scientist and author who has written extensively on feminist theory and political economy. She is known for her work on understanding how economic structures impact women's lives and for her contributions to the field of Critical Political Economy. Currah has also written extensively on environmental justice, race and ethnicity, and globalization. She currently teaches at the University of California, Davis.
