Grégoire Solotareff (born in Alexandria, Egypt in 1943) is a contemporary French painter.  Grégoire Solotareff was born in Alexandria, Egypt in 1943. He studied at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Beaux-Arts in Paris, France from 1963 to 1968. He has exhibited his work in France, Italy, Spain, Belgium, Switzerland, Sweden and the United States of America. His work is in the collections of the Musée d'Orsay, the Museum of Modern Art in New York City and the Centre Georges Pompidou in Paris.
