"Frank's Tribute" is the 16th episode of the fourth season of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and is written and directed by Joss Whedon.  After the death of Frank, the Scooby Gang tries to come to terms with their loss. Xander finds comfort in his old friend's journal, but the gang is disturbed when they find out that Frank had been planning to kill them all. When Willow tries to resurrect him, they are met with resistance from his undead body.
