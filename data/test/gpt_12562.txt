Shamanism is in practice in Ayyavazhi right from the very inception of the faith. The term 'shaman' has been used in a broad sense in Ayyavazhi, encompassing not only traditional healers, but also those who have access to spiritual power or knowledge.   Ayyavazhi is a religion with a strong belief in spiritualism and ancestor worship. Shamanism is an important part of the faith and there are many Shamans in Ayyavazhi who are responsible for mediating between the living and the spirits. They play an important role in healing and imparting wisdom to people.
