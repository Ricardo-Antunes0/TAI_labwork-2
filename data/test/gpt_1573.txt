Born in Nuremberg, Bavaria, Antun Bauer was ordained a priest in 1879. He served as a pastor in Germany before coming to the United States in 1892 to serve as the rector of St. Bernard's Seminary in Cincinnati. He was made the Archbishop of Cincinnati on March 3, 1902. Bauer died in office on December 10, 1916.
