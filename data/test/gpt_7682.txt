Allen Pearson was the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency from January 20, 1973 to February 15, 1975.  Allen Pearson was born in 1924 in Vineland, New Jersey. After graduating from college, Pearson served in the United States Marine Corps during World War II. He then worked for the Federal Bureau of Investigation before joining the Central Intelligence Agency in 1961. Pearson served as the Director of the Central Intelligence Agency from January 20, 1973 to February 15, 1975.
