The Cotocollao Parish is a parish in the canton of Tena in the Ecuadorian province of Cotopaxi. It is located in the Andes, on the eastern slopes of the Cotopaxi Volcano.  The parish covers an area of and has a population of 1,815 (2005). The main economic activity is agriculture; there are also small-scale mining and forestry activities. The primary cultural tradition is Quechua.
