Already Platinum is the debut album by American rapper Meek Mill. It was released on May 6, 2010, by Maybach Music Group and Atlantic Records. The album was preceded by the release of the single "In My Feelings" in August 2018.  Already Platinum received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 73, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, selling 287,000 copies in its first week. It has sold 1.5 million copies in the United States as of November 2018.
