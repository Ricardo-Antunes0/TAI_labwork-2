Christopher Frank Capuano (born August 19, 1978) is an American professional baseball pitcher. He has played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Boston Red Sox, Oakland Athletics, and Arizona Diamondbacks.  Capuano attended Penn State University, where he played college baseball for the Nittany Lions. He was drafted by the Red Sox in the second round of the 2000 MLB Draft. He made his MLB debut with the Red Sox in 2001. In 2003, he was traded to the Athletics, and he spent three seasons with them before being traded to the Diamondbacks in 2007.
