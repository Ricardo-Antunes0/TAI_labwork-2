Brown's Park or Browns Park, originally called East Park, is a public park located in the city of Buffalo, New York. The park was conceived in 1876 and originally consisted of a lake, a bandstand, and several other features. It was renamed Brown's Park in honor of Mayor George S. Brown in 1902.  The park currently contains a basketball court, a children's play area, a football field, and several other features. It is also the location of the Buffalo Zoo.
