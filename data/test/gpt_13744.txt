Count Carl Johan Gustaf Snoilsky (8 September 1787 – 4 November 1865) was a Swedish general and politician. He served in the army, the navy and the Swedish Senate, and was Governor of Uppsala County from 1835 to 1841.  Snoilsky was born in 1787 in Treriksröset, Uppsala County. He began his military career in the Army in 1802 and became a lieutenant colonel in 1815. He served as adjutant general from 1831 to 1835 and as governor of Uppsala County from 1835 to 1841. He was a member of the Riksdag from 1830 to 1840 and from 1849 to 1850. Snoilsky died in 1865 in Stockholm.
