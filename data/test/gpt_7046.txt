Darius Goes West: The Roll of his Life is a documentary film by British filmmaker Asif Kapadia, released in 2017. The film covers the life and work of the British photographer, artist and filmmaker Richard Avedon.  Darius Khondji is a British photographer, artist and filmmaker who has worked extensively in the fashion industry. He has been named as one of the "100 Most Influential People in London" by TIME magazine.  The film covers the life and work of Darius Khondji from his childhood in Kent, England, to his rise to international fame as a fashion photographer and artist. The film highlights his collaborations with well-known fashion designers such as Yves Saint Laurent, Balenciaga, Michael Kors and Prabal Gurung.
