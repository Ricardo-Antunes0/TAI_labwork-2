The  is a Japanese Shinkansen high-speed train type operated by Japanese National Railways (JNR). The first sets were introduced in 1964. The N700S series trains are based on the earlier N700-0 series trains and are equipped with a new head-end control system.  The N700S series trains are designed for high-speed services on the Kyushu Shinkansen and Hokkaido Shinkansen lines. They are capable of speeds of up to 350 km/h (217 mph) on the Kyushu Shinkansen and 300 km/h (186 mph) on the Hokkaido Shinkansen. The trains have a maximum capacity of 810 passengers.
