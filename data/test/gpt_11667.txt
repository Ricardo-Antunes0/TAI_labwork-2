NGC 7742 also known as Fried Egg Nebula is an emission nebula located in the constellation Pisces. The nebula is about 12,000 light years from Earth. It was discovered by English astronomer William Herschel on March 19, 1784. NGC 7742 is a H II region and features an open cluster, NGC 7742, as well as a few loosely associated stars. The central regions of the nebula are brightly glowing due to the emission of ionized atoms and molecules by the hot young stars within the open cluster.
