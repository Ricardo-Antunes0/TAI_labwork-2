Admirals (previously referred to as Admiral Markets) is a financial technology company that connects investors and traders in the global capital markets. The company's mission is to make the global capital markets more open, efficient, and fair.   Admirals was founded in 2007 by Michael Novogratz and former Goldman Sachs executive David Swensen. The company has since raised more than $1 billion in venture capital. Admiral is headquartered in New York City and has offices in London, Singapore, and Hong Kong.   Admirals provides access to its platform to institutional investors and traders in the global capital markets. The company's clients include sovereign wealth funds, family offices, investment banks, hedge funds, and other market participants. Admiral also operates a proprietary trading desk.
