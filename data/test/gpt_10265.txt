BAZ-2215 "Dolphin" (models made in Chernihiv are designated "Dolphin-C") is a Russian unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) developed by the Central Scientific Research Institute of Machine Building (TsNIIMash) and Rostec Corporation.  The Dolphin is a medium-sized UAV, with a length of 5.5 m and a wingspan of 8.2 m. The UAV is powered by two engines, each providing up to 1,500 kg of thrust, which give it a payload capacity of up to 1,000 kg. The UAV can fly at a maximum speed of 220 km/h and can stay in the air for up to 30 hours.
