Ashley Derek Vincent (born 26 May 1985) is an English footballer who plays as a midfielder for League Two club Swindon Town.  Vincent began his career with Manchester City, but made only one appearance for the reserve team before being released in 2003. He then moved to West Bromwich Albion, where he made over 100 appearances and scored six goals. In 2008, he signed for Swindon Town, where he has since made over 100 appearances. He has also represented England at youth level.
