"Mars University" is episode eleven of the science fiction television series "Doctor Who". The episode is set on the fictional Mars and was first broadcast on BBC One on 25 November 2009.  The Doctor, Romana and K9 arrive on the red planet to find that a university has been built on the site of an ancient archaeological site. The university is presided over by Professor Lazarus, a mad scientist who is experimenting on humans. The Doctor and Romana try to stop Lazarus, but are captured and taken to his laboratory.   Lazarus experiments on them, trying to find a way to increase his intelligence. Romana is able to free the Doctor and K9 and they escape back to Earth. The Doctor realises that Lazarus was only trying to help humanity, and that the university was only a cover for his experiments.
