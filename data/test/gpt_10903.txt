Kavilok (Gujarati: કવિલોક), a publication of Kavilok Trust, is a quarterly journal of Gujarati literature.  Kavilok was established in 1984 as an outlet for new and experimental Gujarati poetry. Since its inception, the journal has been committed to championing the work of emerging and mid-career writers from Gujarat. Kavilok has published work by such well-known poets as Anand Mehta, Raghuvir Sahni, Jignesh Mewani, Farhatullah Babar and Rajeshwari Sachdeva.
