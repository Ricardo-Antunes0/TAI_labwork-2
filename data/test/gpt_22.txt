Jesús Jiménez Barbero (born 1960, in Madrid) is a Spanish artist who works in sculpture and installation.  Barbero's work is characterized by its deliberate, minimalist approach to sculptural form, as well as its use of materials that are often recycled or found objects. His pieces often explore the relationship between man and his environment, and often reference traditional Spanish craftsmanship.  Barbero was born in 1960 in Madrid, Spain. He studied sculpture at the Escuela Superior de Arte de San Fernando in Madrid, where he was influenced by the work of Hans Arp and Tony Smith. He has exhibited his work throughout Spain and in Belgium, France, Italy, Germany, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and the United States. He is currently based in Paris.
