Jacques Labouchere (born July 4, 1978) is a French-born Canadian professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with HC Bochum of the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL).  Labouchere was born in France, but grew up in Canada. He played junior hockey with the Rouyn-Noranda Huskies of the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League (QMJHL) before moving to the NHL with the Tampa Bay Lightning in 2001. After four seasons in Tampa Bay, Labouchere was traded to the Calgary Flames in 2006. Labouchere played two seasons with Calgary before being traded to HC Bochum of the DEL in 2009.
