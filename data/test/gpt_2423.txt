The Lassus Mountains  are a large mountain range in central Nevada. The range is named for the French composer Jean-Philippe Rameau, who was born in the town of Lassus, near the mountains.  The Lassus Mountains are a large mountain range in central Nevada. The range is named for the French composer Jean-Philippe Rameau, who was born in the town of Lassus, near the mountains. The mountains are located about southeast of Reno and about west of Las Vegas.
