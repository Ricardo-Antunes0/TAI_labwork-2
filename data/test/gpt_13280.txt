Data exhaust or exhaust data is the process of extracting valuable information from large data sets.  Data exhaust can be used for a variety of purposes, such as mining for patterns or insights, indexing and searching, or creating new models. It can also help reduce the time and effort needed to process large data sets.  Data exhaust is often used in conjunction with data mining techniques, which are used to identify patterns and insights in data. Data mining can be used to find new information or to improve the accuracy of existing data.
