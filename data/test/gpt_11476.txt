Kesklinn (Estonian for "City centre") is one of the ten local government districts of Tallinn, the capital of Estonia. It has a population of 133,500 (as of 1 January 2012).[2]  The district was established on 1 July 2009, when the City of Tallinn was divided into ten local government districts.[3] Kesklinn is located in the southern part of the city. It includes the neighborhoods of Laagri, Kõpu, Mäetaguse, Mustamäe and Ülemiste.  Kesklinn is home to Tallinn's largest shopping mall, Kesklinna Teater, as well as the city's main railway station and bus terminal. The district is also home to Tallinn University of Technology, as well as several other schools and institutions.
