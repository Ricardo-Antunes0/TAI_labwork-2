Nice Girl? is a 1941 American musical comedy film directed by Norman Taurog and starring Judy Garland, Mickey Rooney, and Don Ameche. The screenplay was written by Moss Hart and Ira Gershwin.  The film tells the story of a young girl who is mistakenly identified as a 'nice girl' by her friends, who then set out to prove her 'niceness' to the boy of her dreams. The film is noted for its Technicolor production and Garland's performance.  Garland won the Academy Award for Best Actress for her performance in Nice Girl?
