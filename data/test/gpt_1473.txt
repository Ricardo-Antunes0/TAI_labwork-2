Macdonald "Mac" Benson (born June 30, 1930) is a Canadian retired ice hockey player and coach.  Benson played in the National Hockey League for the Boston Bruins, Chicago Black Hawks, and Detroit Red Wings. He also played for the St. Louis Blues and Toronto Maple Leafs in the World Hockey Association. As a player with the Bruins, he was a part of the team that won the Stanley Cup in 1960. Benson was also head coach of the Flyers, and then the Canadiens. He retired as a player in 1977 and became a color commentator for CBC Sports. He was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1980.
