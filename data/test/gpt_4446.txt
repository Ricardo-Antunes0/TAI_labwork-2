The sooty grunter (Hephaestus fuliginosus), also known as the black-chinned grunter, is a species of fish in the family Haemulidae. It is endemic to the Amazon River basin in Brazil, Bolivia, and Peru.  The sooty grunter is a small fish, up to 8 cm long, with a stout body and a short caudal peduncle. It is black on the upperparts with a white belly and a prominent white chin. The eyes are yellow and there are dark spots on the sides of the head. The sooty grunter feeds on small crustaceans and insects.
