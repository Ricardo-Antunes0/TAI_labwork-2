Zhanar Dugalova (, Janar Dūğalova; born 17 January 1989) is a Bulgarian tennis player.  Dugalova has a career high singles ranking of world No. 319, achieved on 9 October 2017. She also has a career high doubles ranking of world No. 177, achieved on 2 February 2018.  Dugalova has won one singles and nine doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. She reached her best singles ranking of world No. 316 in October 2017 and her best doubles ranking of world No. 177 in February 2018.  Dugalova made her WTA main draw debut at the 2017 St Petersburg Ladies Trophy, where she lost in the first round to Vera Zvonareva.   Dugalova represented Bulgaria at the Fed Cup in 2018, playing three ties. She teamed up with Elitsa Kostova to win their first ever doubles title at the 2018 European Games, defeating Svetlana Kuznetsova and Ekaterina Makarova in the final.
