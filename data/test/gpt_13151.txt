Lévite Thériault (May 14, 1837 – December 1, 1916) was a Canadian physician and medical researcher who is best known for his work in dermatology.  Thériault was born in Saint-Léonard, Quebec on May 14, 1837. He received his medical degree from the University of Montreal in 1863 and subsequently practiced medicine in Quebec City. In 1887, he was appointed professor of dermatology at the University of Ottawa. He served as president of the Canadian Dermatological Association and the American Academy of Dermatology. Thériault died in Ottawa on December 1, 1916.
