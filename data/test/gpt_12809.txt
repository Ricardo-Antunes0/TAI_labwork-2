Aim in Life is a Bangladeshi drama film directed by Javed Sheikh and produced by Shafiqul Islam. It stars Zahid Hasan, Humayun Ahmed, and Ferdous Ahmed in the lead roles. The film was released on 14 October 2017.  Aim in Life is a heart-wrenching drama film set against the backdrop of political turmoil and social unrest in Bangladesh during the early 1990s. It tells the story of a young couple, Shamsunisa and Mohsin, who are struggling to make ends meet during a period of financial hardship and social upheaval. As their relationship begins to unravel, Shamsunisa starts to question her purpose in life, while Mohsin begins to doubt his own abilities. Ultimately, their fragile relationship is tested as they are forced to confront the challenges of life - and each other - head-on.
