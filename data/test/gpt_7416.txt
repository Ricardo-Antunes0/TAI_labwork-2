Golagrama is a village or region in the Shimoga district of Karnataka, India.  Golagrama is a village or region in the Shimoga district of Karnataka, India. The village is located near the Gadag-Shimoga highway, about south of Gadag and about northwest of Shimoga. The population of Golagrama was about 2,000 in 2001.  The village is known for its granite boulders, which are used for building houses and other objects in the village. The boulders are also used for grinding flour and other grains. The village also has a temple dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva.
