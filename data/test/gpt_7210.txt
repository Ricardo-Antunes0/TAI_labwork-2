"'But Love Me'" is a song written by Hank Williams and recorded by him in 1951. The song is about a man who is in love with a woman but is unsure if she feels the same way.  "But Love Me" was Hank Williams' first major hit and remains one of his most popular songs. It has been covered by a number of country and rock artists, including Patsy Cline, Willie Nelson, Ray Charles, and The Beatles.
