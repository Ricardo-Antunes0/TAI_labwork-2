Vishwakarma Jayanti is a day of celebration and remembrance of the architect, craftsman and sculptor Vishwakarma.  The day is celebrated in India on the fourth day of the waxing moon in the month of Chaitra. It is also observed in Nepal and Bangladesh, where it is known as Vishwakarma Puja.  Vishwakarma is considered to be the architect of the universe and is said to have created everything that exists. He is particularly revered for his creative work in sculpture, which is said to be unmatched. The puja ceremony in his honor is a time for worshipping him and thanking him for his creations.
