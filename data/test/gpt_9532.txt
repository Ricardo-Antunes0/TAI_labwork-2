PR Senthinathan (born 1968) is a politician and former bureaucrat who was the Chief Secretary to the Government of Tamil Nadu from March 2011 to September 2014. He is a member of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) political party.  Senthilnathan was born in 1968 in Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu, India. He holds a Bachelors degree in Economics from Madras University. He began his career with the Indian Administrative Service in 1990, and served as an officer in the Tamil Nadu cadre of the service until he was appointed as Secretary to the Government of Tamil Nadu in March 2011. He served as Chief Secretary to the Government of Tamil Nadu from March 2011 to September 2014. On 31 August 2014, he resigned from his post as Chief Secretary following allegations of corruption made against him by the DMK's senior leader M K Stalin. He has denied any wrongdoing.
