Panduleni Filemon Bango Itula (born 2 August 1969) is a retired Liberian footballer and current football manager. Filemon played as a striker for clubs in Liberia, Ivory Coast, and Spain.  Filemon started his career with hometown club Mighty Barrolle Liberia in 1988. He then went on to play for ASEC Mimosas of Ivory Coast, FC Barcelona of Spain and Panionios of Greece. He retired from professional football in 2006.  Filemon was appointed manager of LSL side Real Monrovia in January 2016. He left the club in December 2017.  Filemon was appointed head coach of the Sierra Leone national football team on 6 March 2018.
