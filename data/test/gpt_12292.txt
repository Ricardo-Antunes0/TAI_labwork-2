Edu or EDU may refer to: People: Eduard Bernstein (1902-1970), German sociologist   Education, a form of training or instruction in the skills necessary for living, typically acquired in institutions of higher education. In the past, education was often restricted to members of certain socioeconomic classes, but today it is available to everyone through schools and universities.  In business, Edu is a reference to the electronic data interchange standard, EDI. Academic institutions may also use Edu as a metonym for their online presence and learning resources.
