The Kipengere Range, also known as the Kipengere Hills, is a mountain range located in the Rift Valley Province of Kenya. The range extends for approximately 150 kilometers and is made up of a series of hills, ridges and peaks. The highest peak in the range is Mount Kenya, which rises to an elevation of 5,095 meters.   The Kipengere Range is home to a number of species of animals, including lions, leopards, elephants, and hyenas. The range is also popular among hikers and mountain climbers, who use it to explore the surrounding areas.
