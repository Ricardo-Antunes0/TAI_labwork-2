Buvik is a former municipality in the county of Oppland, Norway. The municipality existed from 1838 until its dissolution in 1963. The administrative centre of the municipality was the village of Buvik.  Buvik is located in the eastern part of the county of Oppland, about north of the town of Ås and about east of the county town of Hamar. The municipality encompassed an area of . The population (2013) was 1,841.  The municipality was established on 1 January 1838 (see formannskapsdistrikt). On 1 January 1863, it was divided into Buvik and Åmot. On 1 January 1964, Buvik was dissolved and its individual settlements were merged with the municipalities of Åmot and Hamar.
