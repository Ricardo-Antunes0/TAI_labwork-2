Joanne Morgan is an Australian netball player. She was a member of the Australia women's national netball team that won the gold medal at the 2008 Beijing Olympic Games.  Joanne Morgan was born in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia on 30 October 1985. She played her junior netball for the Melbourne Tigers and then transferred to the Sydney Swifts in 2002. She made her national debut in 2003 and went on to play 134 matches for Australia, including the 2007 World Netball Championships and the 2008 Beijing Olympic Games. She retired from international netball in 2010. Morgan is currently the head coach of the Australian Women's Netball League (AWNBL) team the Northern Mystics.
