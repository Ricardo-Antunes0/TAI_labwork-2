Nicholas Kraemer (born 7 March 1945, in Wiesbaden) is a German ethnomusicologist, musicologist, and composer. He is best known for his work on the music of the Baroque period.  Kraemer studied musicology and ethnomusicology at the University of Cologne, where he earned his doctorate in 1975. From 1975 to 1987 he was a professor at the University of Cologne. Since 1987 he has been a professor of musicology at the Hanns Eisler School of Music and Theater in Berlin.  Kraemer's work centres on the music of the Baroque period, which he has studied extensively.
