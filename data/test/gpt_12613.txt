Strike Fighter Squadron 146 (VFA-146) also known as the "Black Knights" is an American naval aviation squadron based at Naval Air Station Lemoore, California. The squadron's mission is to provide air superiority for the United States Navy in support of carrier battle group operations.  VFA-146 was commissioned on October 1, 1992, and is the first squadron to be designated as a "Strike Fighter Squadron." The squadron's insignia features a black knight on a white field. The black knight is derived from the heraldic symbol of England and represents the squadron's commitment to its country and its mission.  The Black Knights are currently assigned to Carrier Air Wing Eleven (CVW-11) and operate out of Naval Air Station Lemoore, California.
