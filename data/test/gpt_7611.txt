The Pretender: Rebirth is the first novel in the Pretender series. It was originally published in 2006 by Random House.  The Pretender: Rebirth is the first book in the Pretender series, and tells the story of a young man who is discovered to be a clone of the original Pretender, who has been missing for over a decade. The clone, known as "The Pretender", is forced to resume his role as the leader of the resistance against the government and its forces.
