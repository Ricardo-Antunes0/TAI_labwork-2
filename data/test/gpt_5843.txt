Drafting tools may be used for measurement, drafting, or creating sketches or diagrams. They can be used with a variety of media, such as paper, film, or computer screens.  There are many types of drafting tools, each with its own specific uses. Some common tools include rulers, compasses, straightedge, calipers, and protractors. Each has its own advantages and drawbacks, so it is important to choose the right one for the task at hand.
