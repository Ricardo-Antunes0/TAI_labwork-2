The Oakdale Affair is a short contemporary mystery novel by Australian author Kerry Greenwood. It is the sixth book in the Inspector Lynley series and was published in 2006.  The novel tells the story of Inspector Lynley and his new assistant, Sergeant Havers, as they investigate the murder of a young woman, Evelyn Oakdale, in Oakdale Manor, Oakdale, Hampshire. The victim had recently been engaged to be married to a man named Simon Barnaby. The motive for the murder remains unknown, but the police believe that it may have been a crime of passion.
