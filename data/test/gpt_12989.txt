Vienna  is a city in Johnson County, Illinois, United States. The population was 25,868 at the 2010 census. Vienna is the county seat of Johnson County. It is part of the Springfield, Illinois Metropolitan Statistical Area.  Vienna was founded in 1836 and became a railroad town in the late 19th century. It developed as a manufacturing center for machinery and other industrial products. The city's economy has diversified in recent years, with health care and education becoming more important sectors.
