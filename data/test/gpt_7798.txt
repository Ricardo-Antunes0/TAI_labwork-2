MasterChef Israel  is an Israeli reality cooking show which premiered on 10 January 2012 and airs on Yes TV. The show is hosted by Ilanit Hazan and judges are Yotam Ottolenghi, Alex Levac and Ayelet Waldman.  The show is a competition between amateur cooks from all over Israel who compete in various challenges to win a place in the final round. The final round consists of a four-course meal which the contestants must prepare in under an hour. The winner is awarded a prize of 100,000 shekels and the title of MasterChef Israel.
