Hilltop Park was the nickname of a baseball stadium in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The stadium was located on the southwest corner of Broad and Locust Streets and it was the home of the Philadelphia Athletics of the American League from 1901 to 1954. The Philadelphia Phillies played their first game at Hilltop Park on April 18, 1901.  The Athletics moved to Kansas City in 1955, and the stadium was demolished in 1962. The site is now occupied by the Philadelphia Convention Center.
