NationsUniversity, also known as Nations University or simply Nations, is an American private research university in San Diego, California. The university was founded by Dr. George Lundberg and his wife, Evelyn. It has a total enrollment of 10,000 students and an annual budget of $100 million.   The university offers undergraduate and graduate programs in business, education, engineering, health sciences, law, nursing, pharmacy, and public policy. The university also has a center for international studies and a medical school affiliated with Scripps Health. Nations University has been ranked among the top 25 universities in the United States by "U.S. News & World Report" for eight consecutive years.
