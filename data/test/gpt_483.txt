Maartje Alexander Köster (born 3 March 1975) is a Dutch actress and television host. She is best known for her roles as Inge in the television series Mijn Hart Ga Hopen op Me and Liesbeth de Vries in the television series De Telegraaf Verloofd.   Maartje Alexander Köster was born on 3 March 1975 in Amsterdam, Netherlands. She began her acting career as Inge in the Dutch television series Mijn Hart Ga Hopen Op Me (1996–1998). She then starred in the television series De Telegraaf Verloofd (2002–2005), for which she was awarded the Nederlandse Film Award for Best Actress in 2004. In 2006, she hosted the Dutch version of Dancing on Ice.
