The Wesel Convention was a secret gathering of Nazi officials, diplomats, and military leaders that took place from 9 to 12 July 1944 in Wesel, Germany. The goal of the meeting was to discuss the future of the Nazi regime and to plan the next steps in the Nazi war effort. Several high-ranking Nazis, including Adolf Hitler, Heinrich Himmler, and Joseph Goebbels attended the meeting.
