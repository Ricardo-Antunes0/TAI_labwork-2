The series will be produced by the studio A-1 Pictures and directed by Kenichi Suzuki. The main cast includes Yūki Kaji as the protagonist Sōsuke Akagi, Saori Hayami as Yuri Tachibana, and Hiroyuki Yoshino as Fumito Uchimura. The show will premiere in Japan on January 8, 2018 on Tokyo MX and other networks.
