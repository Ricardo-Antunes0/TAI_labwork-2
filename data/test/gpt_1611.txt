Leon Charles Constantine (born 24 February 1978) is a British actor, most notable for his roles in the television series Midsomer Murders and its spin-off, Constantine.  Constantine was born in London, England. He began his acting career in 2001, appearing in several television roles including the mini-series The Bill and the 2003 BBC drama serial Dancing on the Edge. In 2006, he starred as the title character in the ITV detective series Midsomer Murders. He has since starred in the spin-off series Constantine, which aired from 2013 to 2017. Constantine has also appeared in films including Edge of Tomorrow (2014),xXx: Return of Xander Cage (2017), and The Fate of the Furious (2017).
