John bin Huwai , also spelled JohnBinHuwai, (born July 21, 1957, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia) is a Malaysian politician. He is the Member of Parliament for the constituency of Kota Kinabalu in Sabah, Malaysia.  John bin Huwai was born on July 21, 1957 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. He is the eldest son of Huwai Mohd. Ali bin Huwai and Haji Siti Hajar binti Ismail. He completed his secondary education at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Kuala Lumpur (SMKL) in 1975 and then went on to study at the University of Malaya where he obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Biological Science in 1979.  After completing his university studies, John bin Huwai started working as a research officer at the National Microbiology Laboratory (NMBL) in 1981. He later moved to the National Parks and Wildlife Department (NPWD) where he served as a senior research officer from 1985 to 1988. In 1988, John bin Huwai ventured into politics and was elected as an MP for the Kota Kinabalu parliamentary constituency. He has been re-elected for three consecutive terms since then.  John bin Huwai is currently the Minister of Rural Development and Local Government in the Malaysian government.
