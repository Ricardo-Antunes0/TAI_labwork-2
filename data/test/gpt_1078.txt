MuggleNet is the Internet's oldest and largest Harry Potter fan site, with over seven million registered users and over fifty thousand articles.  MuggleNet was founded on December 10, 1997, by a group of Harry Potter fans who wanted to create a site where fans could share information and connect with one another. Since its inception, MuggleNet has grown to be the largest Harry Potter fan site on the Internet, with a large community of users from all over the world.  MuggleNet is a comprehensive resource for Harry Potter fans of all levels of experience. The site offers news, commentary, feature articles, movie reviews, and more. MuggleNet also has an active forum where fans can discuss all things Harry Potter.  MuggleNet is dedicated to providing its users with the best possible experience.
