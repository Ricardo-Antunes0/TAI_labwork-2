Sérgio Miguel Moreira Paulinho, ComIH (born 26 November 1987), commonly known as Sérgio Paulinho, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Guangzhou Evergrande Taobao in the Chinese Super League.  Sérgio Paulinho started his career at hometown club Corinthians. He made his senior debut in 2006 and won the Campeonato Paulista in 2007. He was signed by Barcelona in 2008 and spent three seasons with the club, winning La Liga in his final season. He was loaned to Milan in 2011, where he won Serie A and the Coppa Italia. He joined Guangzhou Evergrande Taobao in 2013.  Paulinho has represented Brazil at the 2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup, the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup, the 2011 Copa América, and the 2014 FIFA World Cup.
