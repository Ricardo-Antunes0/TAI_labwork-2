A zero-carbon city is a type of city that runs entirely on renewable energy, and is designed to be completely self-sufficient in terms of energy. Zero-carbon cities are becoming increasingly important as the world moves towards reducing greenhouse gas emissions. By using renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, zero-carbon cities can help reduce the impact of climate change.
