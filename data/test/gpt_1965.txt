Haugner Church  is a parish church in the city of Bern, Switzerland.  The parish was founded in 1248 and the church was consecrated in 1261. The current building was erected in the 13th century on the site of an older church. The tower was added in the 15th century. The church was extensively restored between 1902 and 1904.  The church is dedicated to Saint Arnold of Brescia. The interior features a gilded altarpiece from 1647 and a carillon from 1892.
