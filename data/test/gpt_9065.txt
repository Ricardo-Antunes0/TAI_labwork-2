The Toyota Aurion (XV40)  is the fifth generation of the Toyota Aurion car. It was launched in March 2006 and is based on the Toyota Camry platform. The XV40 is available as a four- or five-door sedan, as well as a convertible.  The XV40 is available with either a 3.5-liter V6 or a 4.0-liter V8 engine. Both engines produce 268 horsepower (198 kW). The XV40 comes standard with all-wheel-drive and a six-speed automatic transmission. The XV40 also features a number of safety features, including side airbags, Stability Control, and traction control.
