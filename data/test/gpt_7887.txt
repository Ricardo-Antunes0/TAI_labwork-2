Hard Eight (originally titled Sydney) is a 1996 American crime thriller film directed by Peter Segal and written by Cameron Crowe. The film stars Matthew McConaughey, Ving Rhames, and John C. Reilly.  The film is about a group of friends who are out to make some easy money by robbing a cargo ship. However, things quickly go wrong and they are forced to fight for their lives.  The film was generally well-received by critics, who praised its plot, performances, and cinematography. It was also nominated for the Academy Award for Best Screenplay.
