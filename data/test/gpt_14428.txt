The war in Donbas, or the Donbas conflict, is a military conflict in the Donbas region of eastern Ukraine. The war started on 12 April 2014 and has since lasted more than two years, with reported casualties of over 10,000 people.  The war is fought between the government of Ukraine and pro-Russian separatists supported by Russia. The separatists have been fighting for independence or greater autonomy for the Donbas region, which is populated mainly by ethnic Russians. The Ukrainian military has been fighting with support from NATO and the United States.
