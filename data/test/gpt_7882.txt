Alexander Fedorovich Taran (born 1951), also known as Taran, is a Russian sculptor.  Taran was born in 1951 in the city of Leningrad. In 1967 he graduated from the Leningrad Institute of Art and Design. He has been a member of the Union of Russian Artists since 1978. Taran's sculptures are based on traditional Russian folk art and often depict fantastic or heroic subjects. His work has been exhibited in Russia, Germany, the United States, France, Spain, Italy, and Japan.
