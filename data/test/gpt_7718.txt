The 2008 Sharpie 500 was the twenty-fourth race in the 2008 NASCAR Sprint Cup Series. It was held on October 14, 2008 at Chicagoland Speedway in Joliet, Illinois.  Kyle Busch led all but one of the first 53 laps and held off a hardcharging Kasey Kahne to win his first race of the season. Clint Bowyer finished second, two seconds behind Busch, and Jimmie Johnson came in third.  This was the first win for Kyle Busch in 2008 and the first for Hendrick Motorsports.
