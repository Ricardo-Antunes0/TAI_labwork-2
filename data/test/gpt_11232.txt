Linden Park, officially known as Park of the Linden Trees, is a public park located in the borough of Queens in New York City. The park occupies a total area of 9.1 acres and has a documented history dating back to the early 19th century. It is currently managed by the New York City Department of Parks and Recreation.  Linden Park features a variety of recreational activities, including an outdoor pool, a basketball court, a playground, a cricket field, and a roller skating rink. The park also has a number of notable facilities, including the Linden Museum, which is dedicated to the history of Queens, and the Linden Library, which is one of the largest public libraries in New York City.
