Millionaires is the eighth studio album by American singer and songwriter Mariah Carey. It was released on November 15, 2016, by Columbia Records. The album is a follow-up to Carey's 2013 album Me.  The album was preceded by the release of the lead single "Emotions" on August 25, 2016. The song topped the charts in multiple countries and became Carey's eleventh number one single on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart. The album's second single, "I Don't Wanna Cry", was released on October 7, 2016. The song topped the charts in multiple countries and became Carey's fifteenth number one single on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart.  "Millionaires" received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 66, which indicates "generally favorable reviews".  Carey promoted the album with the Millionaires World Tour which started on June 8, 2017 and ended on December 13, 2017.
