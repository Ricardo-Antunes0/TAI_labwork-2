Retlaw Enterprises, originally Walt Disney Miniature Railroad, is a miniature railroad company based in Buena Park, California. The company was founded in 1976 by Walt Disney's nephew Roy E. Disney, and is now run by his son Roy E. Disney Jr.   Retlaw Enterprises operates three miniature railroads: the Disneyland Railroad (originally opened as the Walt Disney Miniature Railroad), the California Adventure Railroad, and the Frontierland Railroad at Disney's Magic Kingdom Resort in Orlando, Florida. The company also operates a miniature railroad at the Tokyo Disney Resort in Japan.
