Occasional Wife is an American sitcom which aired on ABC from September 22, 2009 to May 17, 2012. The show was created by Chuck Lorre and produced by Warner Bros. Television.  The show stars Christina Applegate as Kelly Kapoor, a working-class woman who becomes a stay-at-home mom after her husband (Josh Gad) leaves her for a younger woman. Applegate's character then must navigate the ups and downs of being a stay-at-home mom while trying to maintain her relationship with her son.
