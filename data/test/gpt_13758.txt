Gemini is a 2002 Indian Tamil-language crime thriller film, written and directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Ajith Kumar, Jyothika, and Annamalai in the lead roles. The story revolves around a group of criminals who are forced to take on a new identity after being wrongly accused of a murder.  Gemini is a gripping crime thriller set in Chennai. A group of criminals, wrongly accused of a murder, find themselves with no other option but to take on a new identity and start afresh. Their only hope is to clear their names before the statute of limitations runs out.
