The group has released six albums since its inception in 1996, including their most recent effort, "The Bones of What You Believe", in 2016.  Formed in 1996, Chilliwack is a Canadian rock band based in Chilliwack, British Columbia. The group has released six albums, including their most recent effort, "The Bones of What You Believe", in 2016. Chilliwack has toured nationally and internationally, selling over 300,000 records.
