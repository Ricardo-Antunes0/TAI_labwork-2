{{Infobox radio station | name      | image = | frequency = | location = | owner = }} KTRN is a radio station broadcasting at 102.9 FM in Ketchikan, Alaska. The station is owned by Ketchikan Broadcasting Company, LLC. It airs a classic rock format branded as "102.9 The Rock".  The station was founded in 1977 by Ralph and Jeanne Aleshire as KTKN-AM, and originally broadcast on 1020 kHz with 250 watts of power. In 1992, the station changed its call letters to KTRN and increased its power to 5,000 watts.
