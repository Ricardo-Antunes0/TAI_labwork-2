Pizza II: Villa is a 2013 Indian Telugu-language romantic horror film written and directed by S. S. Rajamouli. The film stars Allu Arjun, Charmy Kaur and Nandita Das in the lead roles.  The film is a sequel to the 2008 Tamil-language film Pizza. It is also the second part in the "Pizza" series, preceded by the 2010 Kannada-language film Pizza. The story is set 10 years after the first film, with a new generation of teenagers trying to cope with the aftermath of a supernatural event that happened ten years ago.  The film began production in February 2013 and was released on 17 December 2013 to mixed reviews. However, Arjun's performance received widespread acclaim and he was nominated for Best Actor at the 64th Filmfare Awards.
