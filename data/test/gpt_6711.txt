Kunsthalle Detroit is a non-profit 501(c)(3) art gallery located in Midtown Detroit, Michigan. The gallery was founded in 1974 by Walter and Jean Steiner, artists and art collectors.  Kunsthalle Detroit is home to a permanent collection of over 12,000 pieces of art, including work by Georges Braque, Pablo Picasso, and Andy Warhol. The gallery also hosts exhibitions of new and contemporary art, as well as retrospectives of significant artists.
