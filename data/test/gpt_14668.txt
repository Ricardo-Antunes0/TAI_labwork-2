Joseph Riddick "Rick" Hendrick III (born July 22, 1951) is an American businessman who is the co-founder, chairman and president of Hendrick Motorsports, a NASCAR team. He also owns the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League.  Hendrick was born in Nashville, Tennessee. He grew up in a family of racecar drivers and started his own business in car sales at the age of 17. Hendrick Motorsports was founded in 1984 and has since become one of NASCAR's most successful teams, winning 114 races and five Cup championships. Hendrick also owns the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League.
