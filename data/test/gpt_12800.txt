Lymm High School is a secondary school in the town of Lymm, Cheshire, England.  The school has approximately 1,000 pupils and was founded in 1892. It is a comprehensive school and has a sixth form. The school's headteacher is Mrs Jacqueline Smith.  The school has a sports hall which can hold up to 800 people, as well as an astroturf playing field. In 2006, the school gained Academy status.
