Fort Senneville is one of the outlying forts of the Saint-Maurice sector of the Maginot Line. It was built between 1930 and 1932 as part of the preparations for the Second World War. The fort is located in the commune of Saint-Maurice, in the Territoire de Belfort.  The fort is made up of an artillery battery and a number of casemates. It is also equipped with a number of anti-tank guns. The fort is now used as a military training centre.
