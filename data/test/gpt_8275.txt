Hayley Joann Williams (born in Cheshire, England) is a Welsh singer, songwriter and actress. Williams rose to prominence in the late 1990s as the lead singer of the pop-punk band Hayley Williams & the Prophets of Rage. After leaving Hayley Williams & the Prophets of Rage in 2002, she embarked on a successful solo career.  Williams has released five studio albums, two of which reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. She has also won two Brit Awards, an MTV Video Music Award, an American Music Award, and a Grammy Award. In 2013, she was made a Dame by the Queen in recognition of her services to music.
