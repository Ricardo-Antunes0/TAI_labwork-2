Pembina is a primarily commercial neighbourhood in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. It is bounded by 96 Street to the west, 101 Avenue to the north, 127 Avenue to the east and 137 Avenue to the south.   The neighbourhood was established in 1912. It was named after the Pembina River which flows through it. The primary commercial artery in the neighbourhood is 118 Avenue which features a number of small businesses and restaurants. The neighbourhood is also home to a number of large corporate buildings including TD Tower and the Meridian Centre.
