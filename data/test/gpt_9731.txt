Momir Bulatović (; 21 September 1956 – 4 October 2012) was a Serbian writer, translator, editor and critic.  Born in Belgrade, Bulatović attended the University of Belgrade Faculty of Philology and worked as a journalist for various magazines before publishing his first novel, "The Book of Blossoms", in 1998. Bulatović's work has been translated into over 20 languages. He died in 2012 after a long battle with cancer.
