HPE Service Activator is a service provisioning tool for HPE systems. It enables administrators to centrally manage service activations for their HPE systems, including deploying new services and managing existing services. Service Activator also keeps track of the status of deployed services, and provides proactive notifications when services fail or need to be updated.
