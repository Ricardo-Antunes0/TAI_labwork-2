Alexia González-Barros González (7 March 1971 - ) is a Spanish actress and singer.  Alexia González-Barros González was born in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain on 7 March 1971. She began her career as a child, appearing in theater productions before making her screen debut in the TV series El matadero (1991). From there, she appeared in several films and television programs before achieving widespread recognition for her role as Elena in the 1999 TV series La casa de al lado.  Since then, she has appeared in a number of films and television programs, most notably in the role of María del Carmen in the 2002 film Andalucía. More recently, she has starred in the soap opera La que se acaba de casar and has also released several albums.
