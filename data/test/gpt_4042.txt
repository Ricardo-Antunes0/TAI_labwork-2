Dieter Wolke (born 19 March 1957) is a German composer, musician and sound designer.  Wolke was born in Berlin and studied composition with Karlheinz Stockhausen at the University of Cologne. He has also worked with Pierre Boulez, Luciano Berio, John Cage, and Iannis Xenakis. Wolke's work often employs unusual sonic techniques, such as playing two pianos simultaneously or using electronic sound generators to create unusual sounds.
