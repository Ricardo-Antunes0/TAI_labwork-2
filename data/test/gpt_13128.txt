Vimala is a 1960 Indian Telugu-language swashbuckler film produced and directed by K. V. Narayanan. The film stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Savitri in the lead roles and music composed by Saluri Rajeswara Rao.     Plot:  Vimala is the story of a brave young man, Vimala Ramaswamy, who sets out to avenge his father's death at the hands of bandits. After a long and dangerous journey, he meets a beautiful princess, Savitri, and they fall in love. They are pursued by the king's guards but eventually manage to escape and return to the princess' kingdom. There they face many challenges, including the king's attempts to marry Savitri off to another prince, but they eventually overcome them all and are able to live happily ever after.
