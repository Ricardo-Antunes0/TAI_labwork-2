Beaver, a wingman, played his first league game for Melbourne against Essendon at the Melbourne Cricket Ground on 9 May 1922. He played 22 games for Melbourne before being transferred to Werribee in 1925. He returned to Melbourne for the 1928 season and finished his career with Melbourne in 1930.
