She is best known for her 1987 hit single "Aloha Ia O Waiana", which reached number two on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and stayed on the chart for 17 weeks. The song won the 1987 Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance. Kanakuze also recorded the ballad "Where Have All the Rainbow People Gone?" which reached number 11 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart in 1988.
