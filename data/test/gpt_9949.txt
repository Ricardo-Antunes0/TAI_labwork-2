The Beaumont-Wilshire  neighborhood of Portland, Oregon, is a rapidly gentrifying area that has been experiencing rapid population growth since the 1990s. The neighborhood is located in the northeast corner of Portland, bordered by Northeast Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard on the north, Burnside Street on the east, Taylor Street on the south, and Fremont Street on the west.  The Beaumont-Wilshire neighborhood is made up of a mixture of single-family homes, apartments, and condos. The majority of homes in the neighborhood are occupied by middle- and upper-middle class families. The neighborhood has a large number of restaurants, bars, and retailers, as well as a number of parks and green spaces.
