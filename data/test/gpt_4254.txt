Ostrogozhsk (; Ukrainian: Острогозьк, romanized: Ostrohozk) is a city located in the Lviv Oblast (region) of Ukraine. The city has a population of 122,219 as of the 2001 Ukrainian Census.  Ostrogozhsk is a significant industrial center in the region and is home to several major corporations including the Lviv Oblast Administration, the Lviv Polytechnic National University, and the Lviv Commercial Bank. The city is also known for its historical architecture and its annual Ostrogozhsk Opera and Ballet Festival.
