The X Games are an annual extreme sports event, produced by ESPN. They were first held in 1995 in Aspen, Colorado. The format has changed many times since then, but the core of the event has always been extreme sports competitions on a variety of terrain and in a variety of conditions.  The X Games are one of the most prestigious events in extreme sports. Competitors from around the world come to compete in a range of events, from skiing and snowboarding to BMX and skateboarding. The event has helped to popularize a wide range of extreme sports, and has also helped to create new stars of the sport.
