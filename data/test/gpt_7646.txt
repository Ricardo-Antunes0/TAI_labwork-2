Manuel Payno (Mexico City, 1810 – San Francisco, 1878) was a Mexican painter.  Manuel Payno was born in Mexico City in 1810. He studied painting with José Joaquín de Herrera and Manuel María Morelos y Pavón. He exhibited his work at the National Exhibition of Fine Arts in Mexico City in 1837 and at the Exposition Universelle in Paris in 1855. He died in San Francisco in 1878.
