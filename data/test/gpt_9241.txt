The game was first announced at the Facebook Developers Conference in March 2011. Empires & Allies was a social network game where players competed to build the most powerful empire through strategic gameplay. Players could interact with friends and other players through the game's social networking features, as well as participate in global events and challenges. Empires & Allies was discontinued on October 17, 2014.
