Lieutenant General Manvel Sektori Grigoryan (Armenian: Մանվել Սերգի Գրիկում) (born 12 October 1962) is a retired Armenian Army general and former Defence Minister of Armenia. He previously served as the Chief of General Staff of the Armed Forces of Armenia from 2011 to 2013.  Grigoryan was born in Yerevan, the capital of Armenia, on 12 October 1962. He graduated from the Military Academy of Internal Affairs in 1985. He served in various positions in the Armenian Army, including Deputy Chief of Staff for Operations from 1998 to 2002, Chief of Staff from 2002 to 2005, and Deputy Defence Minister from 2005 to 2011. He also served as a military observer at several peacekeeping missions.  In September 2011, Grigoryan was appointed Chief of General Staff by President Serzh Sargsyan. Grigoryan retired from the army on 31 December 2013. In November 2014, he was appointed Chairman of the Board at SOCAR.
