Turkish pop singer Sertab Erener's discography consists of eighteen studio albums, two compilation albums, two live albums, seventy-eight singles, eleven video albums, and fifty-six music videos. Erener has sold over forty million records worldwide.  Sertab Erener was born on October 10, 1966 in Istanbul, Turkey. She began her career in the late 1980s as a backing vocalist for various Turkish pop acts before releasing her debut album, Gelişim in 1991. Erener's first solo hit came the following year with "Sen," which became a major international success and earned her a nomination for the Best Female Pop Vocal Performance at the 1993 Grammy Awards. After releasing several successful albums throughout the 1990s and early 2000s, Erener achieved international success with her 2006 album Özgür Günler. The album spawned several hits including "Yalan" and "Bir Gün." Erener has since released several more successful albums including 2014's Kırmızı Sonbahar and 2018's Yeni Hayat.
