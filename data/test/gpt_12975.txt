"Alligator Sky" is a song by American singer and songwriter Zedd, featuring vocals from American singer Maren Morris. The song was written by Zedd, Maren Morris, and Pharrell Williams, and produced by the latter two. It was released on August 25, 2018 as the lead single from Zedd's forthcoming third studio album, True Colors (2018).  The song is a EDM and pop ballad with lyrics about self-empowerment and the power of love. "Alligator Sky" has been described as an "emotional" and "heartfelt" ballad.  Zedd premiered the song live during his set at the 2018 Wireless Festival in London, England.  "Alligator Sky" peaked at number five on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and number one on both the US Dance/Electronic Songs and Hot Dance/Electronic Albums charts. It also reached the top five in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden, and the United Kingdom.  The music video for "Alligator Sky" was directed by Dave Meyers and premiered on Zedd's Vevo channel on August 25th, 2018. In it, Zedd and Maren Morris sing in a dark room while clips of them performing together are shown interspersed throughout.
