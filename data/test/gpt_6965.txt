Gold  is a 2017 Spanish historical drama film directed by Fernando Trueba and written by Guillermo Arriaga. It is based on the novel of the same name by Juan Rulfo.  The film stars Javier Bardem, Berenice Bejo, and Leticia Ramírez. It was produced by Iñaki Almodóvar and released in Spain on 10 January 2017 and in the United States on 16 January 2017.  The film tells the story of a Mexican landowner, Agustín de Iturbide, who unsuccessfully tries to restore the empire of Emperor Maximilian I of Mexico after the fall of the Spanish Empire in 1810. Gold won the Academy Award for Best Picture on 5 February 2018.
