Seymour Lester Shapiro (1916 - 1961) was an American mathematician. He is best known for his work in functional analysis, probability and mathematical statistics.  Shapiro was born in 1916 in New York City. He received his undergraduate degree from Columbia University in 1937, and his Ph.D. from the University of Chicago in 1941.  Shapiro was a member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences and the National Academy of Sciences. He died in 1961.
