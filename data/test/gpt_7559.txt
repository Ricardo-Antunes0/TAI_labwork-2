The film is about a group of people who are trapped in a small town after a severe storm causes the local dam to break. The town's residents are possessed by evil spirits that have taken over their bodies, and they begin to slaughter each other. The only person who can stop the massacre is the town's sheriff, who must fight his way through the townspeople to find the source of the evil.
