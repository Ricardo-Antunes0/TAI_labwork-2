Orquestra Imperial is a Brazilian Big Band formed in the early 1960s by Stan Getz, João Donato, Herbie Hancock, and Ron Carter. The ensemble is known for its innovative and virtuosic use of the jazz horn and its arrangements of Brazilian popular music.  Formed in the early 1960s, Orquestra Imperial was one of the first big bands to feature jazz horns prominently. The group's arrangements of Brazilian popular music helped to introduce that genre to a wider audience. In addition to its own recordings, Orquestra Imperial has appeared on albums by other artists, including Stan Getz's collaborations with João Donato and Herbie Hancock. The ensemble has toured extensively both in Brazil and abroad, playing at major festivals such as the Montreux Jazz Festival and the Newport Jazz Festival.
