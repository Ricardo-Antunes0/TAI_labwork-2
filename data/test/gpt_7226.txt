Punchbuggy was a Canadian pop punk band from Montreal, Quebec. The band was formed in 2003 by vocalist/guitarist Kevin Murphy and bassist Darryl "Duke" Perkins. The band released two albums, "Punchbuggy" (2005) and "The Great Southern Trendkill" (2008), before disbanding in 2009.
