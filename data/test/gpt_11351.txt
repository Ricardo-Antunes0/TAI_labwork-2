Sarutahiko Ōkami  is a deity of the Shinto religion in Japan. He is the son of Izanagi and Izanami, and the brother of Kaguya-hime. He is also known as Fudo Myo-o, and Takeshiko.  Sarutahiko Ōkami is considered to be the god of war and agriculture, as well as the patron deity of the provinces of Izumo and Shimane. He is also worshipped in the city of Kyoto. The primary festival associated with Sarutahiko Ōkami is the Kumano Festival, which is held every year in July.
