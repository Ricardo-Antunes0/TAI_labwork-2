The Trouble-Makers  is a 2003 Hong Kong action film directed by Benny Chan and starring Chow Yun-fat and Michelle Yeoh.  The film follows two separate groups of criminals who, while planning a heist, become embroiled in a battle with one another. The first group is led by Chow Yun-fat's character, Wo Shing, who is a skilled thief and smuggler. The second group is led by Michelle Yeoh's character, Lin Feng, who is a skilled martial artist and assassin.
