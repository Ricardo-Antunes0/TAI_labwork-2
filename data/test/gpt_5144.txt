Joseph Paul Torre (; born July 18, 1946) is an American baseball manager and former player. He has managed the Los Angeles Dodgers, New York Yankees, and Chicago Cubs. Torre was inducted into the Baseball Hall of Fame in 2011.  Torre played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for 21 seasons, most notably with the Dodgers, Yankees, and Cubs. He won two World Series championships (with the Yankees in 1996 and the Cubs in 2016), an American League (AL) pennant (with the Dodgers in 1988), and five National League (NL) pennant (with the Dodgers in 1981, 1982, 1986, 1988, and 1992). He also won two Gold Glove Awards (with the Yankees in 1992 and with the Cubs in 2003).
