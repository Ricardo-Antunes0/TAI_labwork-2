It is the third largest city in the state of Bahia, after Salvador and Feira de Santana respectively. Vitória da Conquista is known as simply "Conquista" by locals in order to differentiate it from the city of Vitória in the state of Espírito Santo. It was named after the colony of Vitória da Conquista.
