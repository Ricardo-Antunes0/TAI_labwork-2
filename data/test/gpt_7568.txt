Mike Brooks aka Mikey Brooks, Prince Michael Brooks, or simply Mike, is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. He is best known as the frontman and lead vocalist of the rock band Brooks & Dunn.  Brooks was born in Pineville, Louisiana, on October 20, 1971. He attended Pineville High School and Louisiana State University before dropping out to pursue a career in music. He first came to prominence as the frontman of Brooks & Dunn, a country-rock band that achieved massive success in the 1990s. The band's albums sold over 20 million copies worldwide and they were nominated for five Grammy Awards. Brooks also released several solo albums and has appeared on several other artists' albums. He retired from touring in 2009 but continued to write and produce music. Brooks has two children.
