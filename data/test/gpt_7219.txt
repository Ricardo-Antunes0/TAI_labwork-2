The Hildyard Baronetcy, of Patrington in the County of York, is a title in the Baronetage of the United Kingdom. It was created on 10 October 1892 for the industrialist and Liberal politician Sir George Hildyard. The first Baronet sat as Member of Parliament for Leeds North from 1892 to 1906. The second Baronet served as Lord Mayor of London in 1958. As of 2014 the title is held by the third Baronet, who succeeded his father in 1978.
