Bully for Bugs is a 1953 Warner Bros. animated short film directed by Robert Clampett and produced by Chuck Jones.  The cartoon's plot revolves around a grasshopper who is bullied by a group of flies. The grasshopper eventually rallies his friends to stand up to the flies, and they are soon defeated.  Bully for Bugs was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film but lost to Flowers and Trees.
