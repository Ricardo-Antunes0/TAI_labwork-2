Jonathan Richard "Jono" Pryor is a New Zealand rugby union player who currently plays for the Leicester Tigers in the English Premiership Rugby. He previously played for the Auckland Blues, Northampton Saints and the Leicester Tigers. He has also represented New Zealand at international level, playing 67 times for the All Blacks between 2008 and 2017.  Pryor was born in Auckland, New Zealand on 4 March 1988. He attended Wesley College in Auckland before moving to England to study at Loughborough University.  Pryor made his debut for Auckland in 2004 against the Crusaders. In 2006 he moved to Northampton Saints and made 73 appearances for them before signing for Leicester Tigers in 2011. At Leicester, Pryor has won three Premiership titles and two European Cups. He was also named as the 2016 Premiership player of the year.  Pryor has also represented New Zealand at international level, playing 67 times for the All Blacks between 2008 and 2017. His most successful season with the All Blacks was in 2013 when he won a Tri-Nations title and a World Cup medal.
