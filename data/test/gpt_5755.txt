Sebastian Virdung (born c. 1465) was a German painter, primarily of religious subjects. He is considered one of the first masters of the Early Netherlandish style.  Sebastian Virdung was born in about 1465, in Nuremberg, Germany. He is considered one of the first masters of the Early Netherlandish style, which emphasised realism and naturalism in painting. He worked in Munich, Augsburg and Vienna before he died in 1505.
