Vitalii Mykhailovych Markiv (, born August 16, 1984) is a Ukrainian football midfielder who currently plays for FC Metalurh Donetsk.  Markiv began his career in his home town club FC Metalurh Donetsk. He made his professional debut for the club in 2002 and has since become one of the most experienced and important players in the team. In 2006, he was part of the Metalurh Donetsk side that won the Ukrainian Premier League championship. With Metalurh Donetsk, he has also played in the UEFA Champions League, UEFA Cup, and UEFA Europa League. In total, he has made over 220 appearances for the club.  In January 2016, Markiv joined FC Shakhtar Donetsk on a two-and-a-half year contract. He made an immediate impact at the club, helping Shakhtar to win the Ukrainian Premier League title and reach the 2016 UEFA Champions League Final.  Markiv was born in Kharkiv, Ukraine. He is married and has two children.
