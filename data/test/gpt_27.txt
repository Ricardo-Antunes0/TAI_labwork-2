Villain is a 2017 Indian Malayalam-language action-crime film written and directed by Nishikant Kamat. The film stars Manoj Bajpayee, Jackie Shroff, and Sunny Deol in lead roles. It is produced by Dileep and Kamal Haasan. The music is composed by A. R. Rahman.  Villain tells the story of a Mumbai based journalist, Rahul (Manoj Bajpayee), who is investigating the illegal activities of a powerful politician, Vinayakan (Sunny Deol). When Rahul's girlfriend, Pooja (Jackie Shroff), is threatened by Vinayakan, Rahul comes to her rescue. In the process, Rahul uncovers a corruption scandal that could bring down the government.
