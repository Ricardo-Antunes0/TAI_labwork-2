Steve Buckley (born 1956) is an American journalist who has been a staff writer for The New York Times since 1984. He has also been a columnist for The Wall Street Journal, and a senior editor at Newsweek.  Buckley was born in 1956 in the Bronx, New York City. He graduated from Brown University in 1978 with a degree in political science. After graduating from Brown, Buckley worked as a reporter for the Providence Journal and then for the Philadelphia Inquirer.  In 1984, Buckley joined The New York Times as a staff writer. He has written columns for The Wall Street Journal, and has been a senior editor at Newsweek. In 2007, he was named one of the ten most influential journalists in America by the National Press Foundation.
