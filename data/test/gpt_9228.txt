Orobanche uniflora, commonly known as one-flowered broomrape, is a perennial plant in the family Orobanchaceae. It is native to Europe and Asia and has been naturalized in North America.  This plant is a herbaceous, annual or perennial plant growing up to 150 cm tall. The stem is erect and branched, and the leaves are alternate, simple, and entire. The flowers are small and white, with a few yellow or pink petals. The fruit is a capsule.
