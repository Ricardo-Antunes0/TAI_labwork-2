Mirko Castillo (born August 30, 1962 in Fort Worth, Texas) is an American former professional basketball player. He played college basketball at Southern Methodist University and was drafted by the Philadelphia 76ers in the second round of the 1984 NBA draft.  Castillo played for the Philadelphia 76ers, Golden State Warriors, and Milwaukee Bucks of the NBA. He also played for BC Minsk of the Soviet Union and Saski Baskonia of Spain. He averaged 8.3 points and 3.1 rebounds per game in his career.
