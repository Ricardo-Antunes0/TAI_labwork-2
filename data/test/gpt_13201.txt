Calle Erik Steén (born May 16, 1980) is a Norwegian professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with the Frölunda HC of the Swedish Hockey League (SHL).  Steen was drafted by the Ottawa Senators in the third round, 78th overall, of the 2000 NHL Entry Draft. He has also played for the Colorado Avalanche, Tampa Bay Lightning, and Calgary Flames.  Steen has represented Norway at two Winter Olympics (2006 and 2010), as well as one World Cup of Hockey (2009).
