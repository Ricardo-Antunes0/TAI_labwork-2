Medical Independent is a medical newspaper aimed at practicing doctors. It is published weekly and has a circulation of about 25,000.  Medical Independent was founded in 1985 by Dr. Lawrence S. Schwartz, a cardiologist from New York City. The publication's stated mission is to "provide the practicing doctor with the latest medical information and insights."  Medical Independent is distributed free of charge to doctors throughout the United States and Canada. The newspaper features articles on a variety of medical topics, as well as columns written by doctors and other medical professionals.  Medical Independent also maintains a website, which features additional content not found in the newspaper edition. This includes blog posts, video tutorials, and interactive tools.
