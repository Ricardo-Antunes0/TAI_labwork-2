Soda Drinker Pro is a video game for the Nintendo Entertainment System released in 1995. It was developed by Hudson Soft and published by Nintendo.  The objective of the game is to guide a character, who drinks soda, through a series of levels, consuming as many cans as possible in the process. Along the way, the player must avoid obstacles and enemies, and collect bonus items.  The game received mixed reviews upon release, with some reviewers praising its humor and gameplay, while others found it unenjoyable due to its repetitive nature. Nevertheless, it has since gained a cult following among retro gamers.
