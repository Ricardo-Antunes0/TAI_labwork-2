Operation Libelle ("Dragonfly" in German) was an air operation of the Allied invasion of Normandy, code-named Operation Overlord, in World War II. The goal of the operation was to destroy the German V-2 rocket launcher sites in Occupied France.  The operation commenced on D-Day, 6 June 1944, and ended on 12 August. By the end of the operation, about half of the V-2 sites had been destroyed. The cost of theoperation was high, with 2,846 Allied casualties including 982 dead.
