Shanti  is an Indian Kannada language romantic drama film written and directed by Dinesh Babu. Produced by Aravind Agarwal and Dinesh Babu under the banner of Sri Venkateshwara Creations, it features an ensemble cast that includes Shruti Haasan, Rana Daggubati, Ramya Krishnan and Sudeep in pivotal roles. The film is based on a novel of the same name by R. K. Narayan.  Shanti tells the story of an unrequited love between a young man and a woman who are both married to other people. The film explores their relationship over a period of time, depicting their interactions as it evolves from friendship to love to tragedy.  Shanti was announced in October 2014, with Dinesh Babu directing and producing the film with his brother Aravind Agarwal. The cast was revealed in January 2015, with Shruti Haasan and Rana Daggubati playing the lead roles. Ramya Krishnan was later added to the cast in the role of the woman's sister-in-law.
