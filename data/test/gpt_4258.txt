Neon Hitch (born 25 May 1986) is an Australian singer and songwriter. She is the lead vocalist and guitarist for the rock band The Paper Kites.  Neon Hitch was born in Melbourne, Australia on 25 May 1986. She was raised in a musical family, with her mother playing the piano and her father playing the guitar. Neon Hitch began playing the guitar at the age of six, and began composing her own songs at the age of thirteen.  In 2006, Neon Hitch formed the rock band The Paper Kites with her brother Daniel and friend Alex Milburn. The Paper Kites released their self-titled debut album in 2009, and followed it up with the EP "The Chronicles of Fear" in 2010. In 2013, Neon Hitch released her second album "The Bones of What You Believe".  Neon Hitch has toured throughout Australia, Europe, and North America, and has shared stages with notable artists such as The Paper Kites, Saosin, Architects, and Halestorm. In 2017, she formed a new band called Darlings with Australian singer-songwriter Holly Throsby. Darlings released their debut EP "No One Is Lost" in 2018.  Neon Hitch is currently working on her third album.
