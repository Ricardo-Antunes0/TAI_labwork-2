Arly Golombek is a professional horse rider. He has competed in the FEI World Cup, the Grand Prix d'Amérique, and the Pan American Games.  Golombek was born on December 2, 1984, in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador. He began riding at the age of four, and first competed in horse racing at the age of eight. Golombek has since become a successful horse rider, winning numerous competitions around the world. He is particularly well-known for his riding abilities in the Grand Prix d'Amérique, where he has won four times. Golombek also competed in the FEI World Cup in 2010 and 2011, finishing fifth and fourth respectively. In 2013, he won a silver medal at the Pan American Games in the team eventing event. Golombek currently resides in Oakville, Ontario.
