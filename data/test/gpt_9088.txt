Vincent Baron was a French Dominican theologian who served as the Archbishop of Paris from 1974 to 2002.  Baron was born on 31 July 1921 in Bordeaux, France. He entered the Dominican Order in 1944 and studied at the University of Paris and the Pontifical Gregorian University. He was ordained a priest in 1951 and served as a professor at the Dominican School of Theology in Paris from 1952 to 1974. He was named Archbishop of Paris by Pope Paul VI in 1974. He retired from his position in 2002. Baron died on 3 June 2006.
