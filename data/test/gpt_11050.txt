He is perhaps best known for his roles in Neon Genesis Evangelion, Death Note, and Attack on Titan.  Koyasu was born in Kanagawa Prefecture on July 12, 1967. After graduating from high school, he entered a college of music in Tokyo. However, after a year, he dropped out and began working as a voice actor.  In the early 1990s, Koyasu began working on anime productions such as Maison Ikkoku and Vision of Escaflowne. His breakout role came in 1995 when he played Shinji Ikari in the TV series Neon Genesis Evangelion. The series was a huge success, and Koyasu reprised his role in the movie trilogy released between 1997-2000. He also voiced Lelouch Lamperouge in the 2003 series Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion, and played the title character in Death Note from 2006-2007. In 2011, he voiced Gintoki Sakata in the television series Gintama. In addition to voice acting, Koyasu is also a stage actor and has appeared in productions such as Taming of the Shrew and The Merchant of Venice.  Koyasu married actress Sakura Tange in 2002. The couple have two children.
