Joaquin Ridge is a ridge in the Santa Lucia Mountains, in Ventura County, California. It is located east of the main range of the Santa Lucia Mountains, and rises to a height of 6,811 feet (2,075 m).  The ridge is traversed by the Ventura County Line Road, which connects the town of Ojai to the east with the town of Fillmore to the west. The ridge is also traversed by the Pacific Crest Trail, which runs north-south along its eastern slope. The ridge is part of a larger area known as the Santa Lucia Range.
