Newbury Rugby Football Club is a rugby union club based in Newbury, Berkshire, England. The club was formed in 1896 and plays in the RFU Championship.  The club has a long and proud history, with many famous players having represented the club, including Billy Wright, Bill Beaumont, Geoff Cooke, Mike Tindall and Jonny Wilkinson. The club's home ground is The Lawns and it is also affiliated to the Berks & Bucks Rugby Union.
