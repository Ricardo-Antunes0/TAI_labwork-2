Mk III LCU class vessels were follow-on ships to the Mk II LCUs which were introduced in the early 1960s. They were designed to operate in a variety of environments including coastal and inshore waters, rivers, and the offshore oil and gas industry.  The Mk III LCU class vessels had a maximum operating speed of 17 knots and a range of 1,500 nautical miles. They were equipped with a range of weaponry including 40mm cannons, 25mm cannons, and machine guns.
