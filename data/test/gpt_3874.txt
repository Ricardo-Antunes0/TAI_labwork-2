Papuwa (stylized as PAPUWA)  is a Japanese pop group formed in 1998. The group consists of four members: vocalist and songwriter Yūki Ōshima, guitarist Yasutaka Nakata, bassist Naoto Watanabe, and drummer Tatsuya Kawasaki.
