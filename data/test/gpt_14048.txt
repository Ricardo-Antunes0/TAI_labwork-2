Hydroxyapatite, also called hydroxylapatite (HA), is a mineral composed of calcium, hydrogen and oxygen. It is the most common mineral in the Earth's crust, making up about 5% of the weight of the Earth's solid mass. It is also the most common mineral in human teeth and bones.
