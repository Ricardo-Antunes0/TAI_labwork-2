Valery Kritskov is a Russian conductor who has been the Music Director of the Saint Petersburg Philharmonic since 2004.  Kritskov was born in Leningrad, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union on 25 October 1963. After graduating from the Leningrad Conservatory in 1986, he worked as a conductor with various regional orchestras before being appointed Music Director of the Kirov Orchestra in Saint Petersburg in 2004. Kritskov has also held posts with the Royal Liverpool Philharmonic (1993-1996), the Berlin State Opera (1996-1998), and the Vienna State Opera (2003-2004). He has made several commercial recordings, most notably of Mahler's Symphony No. 9 with the Berlin Philharmonic and Vladimir Feltsman.
