Meddling Kids is a 2017 horror-comedy novel written by Nick Cutter.  When the new kid in school, Jace, stumbles upon a Ouija board in his old friend’s basement, he has no idea what he’s getting himself into. With the help of his friends and the board, Jace begins to communicate with the dead, but soon realizes that something sinister is following him. Soon, Jace is on a wild adventure to stop the meddling kids before they get him killed.
