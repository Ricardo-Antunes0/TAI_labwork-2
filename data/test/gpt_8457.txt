Ben Paton (born 19 October 1998) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship club Derby County.  Paton started his career with Derby County, making his professional debut in the EFL Cup against Tottenham Hotspur on 2 October 2016. He made his first league appearance for the club on 3 January 2017, coming on as a substitute in a 1-0 win over Queens Park Rangers.
