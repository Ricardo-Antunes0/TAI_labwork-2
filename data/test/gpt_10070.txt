Death with Interruptions, published in Britain as Death on an Oasis, is a 2003 novel by English author Elizabeth George.  The book is set in the fictional town of Beckworth in Suffolk, England, in the years leading up to and following World War II. It tells the story of three women – Rosie, Emma, and Nell – who are all connected to death in some way.  Death with Interruptions was awarded the Orange Prize for Fiction in 2003.
