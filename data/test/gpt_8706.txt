Hagoromo Bungu  was a Japanese office worker who, in 1984, became the first person to be cryogenically frozen.   Hagoromo Bungu was born in 1934 in the city of Osaka. After completing high school, he began working as an office worker. In 1984, Bungu became the first person to be cryogenically frozen and stored at a facility in Russia. He died in 2002.
