Firelight is a 1997 period romance film written and directed by Peter Weir. The film stars Nicole Kidman and Daniel Craig and tells the story of a young woman, Margaret (Kidman), who is married to an older, wealthy man (Craig) in 18th century Australia. When he dies, she is left with a large estate and must choose between fulfilling her husband's last wishes or taking advantage of the opportunities presented to her.   Weir shot the film in Tasmania and Victoria, using a combination of period sets and local locations. The film received mixed reviews from critics, but was a box office success.
