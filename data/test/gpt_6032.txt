"Walk This Way" is a song by the American rock band Aerosmith. Written by Joe Perry and Steven Tyler, the song was released as the lead single from the band's 1973 album, Toys in the Attic.  "Walk This Way" is a hard rock song with a driving beat and heavy guitar riff. The song was written as a tribute to the streetwalkers of New York City, and features lyrics about walking on the "mean streets of New York".  "Walk This Way" was a major hit in both the United States and United Kingdom, reaching number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and number two on the UK Singles Chart. The single has been certified triple-platinum by the RIAA.
