Defeater is an American melodic hardcore band from Springfield, Massachusetts. The band was formed in 2004 by vocalist and guitarist Matt Barlow, bassist/vocalist J.D. Richards, and drummer Nate Johnson.  Defeater has released six full-length albums, two split albums, and one EP. The band's last album, "The Color of Violence", was released in 2018. The band has toured extensively since its inception, performing across the United States and Europe.
