Whore is a 1991 American drama film written and directed by Paul Schrader. The film stars James Woods, Patricia Arquette, and Kimberly Quinn.  The film tells the story of a Los Angeles street prostitute, Candy (Arquette), who is addicted to cocaine and heroin and has a daughter with her pimp, Harry (Woods). Candy is upset with her life and tries to get clean, but drugs and Harry always find a way to tempt her back. One night, Candy gets into a fight with Harry and tries to run away, but he catches up to her and rapes her. Candy ultimately decides to go to the police and tell them what happened.
