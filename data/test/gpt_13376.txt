"Slime" or "Mire"  is an 1886 short story by British author Arthur Machen. Set in the rural English countryside, it tells the story of a young woman, Eveline, who is tormented by an alien life form that takes over her body and mind.  The story was first published in "The Strand Magazine" and has been anthologized numerous times. It is considered a classic of British horror literature.  The protagonist, Eveline, is a young woman who is plagued by an alien life form that takes over her body and mind. The creature forces Eveline to engage in grotesque behaviors and causes her to believe that she is a monster. The story ends with Eveline's death and the revelation that the alien was only attempting to help her become more self-aware.
