LT PGB is an abbreviation for a low-temperature polycrystalline boron nitride material, which is a type of ceramic material.  Low-temperature polycrystalline boron nitride (LT PGB) is a type of ceramic material that contains small, polycrystalline grains of boron nitride. LT PGB has a wide range of applications, including use in electronic components, thermal management materials, and armor and weapon systems.
