Countdown is a British game show involving contestants, generally two, competing to answer a series of questions in order to win a cash prize. The show is hosted by Nick Knowles and features regular guests, with the final round being a celebrity-assisted game.  Countdown was first broadcast on BBC1 on 20 September 2002, and has since been aired in over 100 countries. The show has been praised for its unique gameplay, which combines elements of quiz shows and memory games. The show has won a number of awards, including Best Entertainment Programme at the 2003 British Comedy Awards and Best Game Show at the 2005 BAFTA Awards.
