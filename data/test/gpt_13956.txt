Minju Kim (born ) is a South Korean actress. She made her acting debut in the cable television series "Reply 1988" in 2008.  Kim made her debut in the cable television series "Reply 1988" in 2008, and has since starred in numerous television dramas, including "You're Beautiful", "Secret Garden", "Good Doctor", and "A Gentleman's Dignity". In 2013, she starred in the romantic comedy film "My Sassy Girl" and the crime thriller film "The Good Wife". In 2017, she starred in the period drama "Queen In Hyun's Man" and the biographical drama "The King's Face".
