Super heavyweight is a weight class in professional boxing. It is the heaviest class in professional boxing, and is between the heavyweight and cruiserweight classes. The super heavyweight class was created in 1991 by the World Boxing Council, with their inaugural champion being Roy Jones Jr.   There are currently ten super heavyweight world champions, with Jones Jr. holding the title for the most time, followed by WBC champion Deontay Wilder and IBF champion Anthony Joshua.
