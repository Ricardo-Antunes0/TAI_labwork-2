Randy Borum is a Professor and Coordinator of the Master of Fine Arts Program in Creative Writing at Southern Illinois University Carbondale.  Randy Borum has published widely, both fiction and non-fiction. His work has appeared in The Iowa Review, The Southern Review, and Harper's Magazine, among other journals. He is the author of three novels and a non-fiction book about Appalachian life.  Borum lives with his wife and two children in Carbondale, Illinois.
