Rockford Rage is a women's flat track roller derby league based in Rockford, Illinois. The league was founded in 2008 and is a member of the Women's Flat Track Derby Association (WFTDA).  Rockford Rage is one of the newer leagues in the Women's Flat Track Derby Association. The league was founded in 2008 and has since expanded to include six teams. Rockford Rage's home track is the Gold Standard Skating Rink.  Rockford Rage is led by President Tracy Dolan and Vice President/League Manager Melissa Pfanner. The league is coached by skaters Sharon Jones, Ashley Lomax, and Tracey Kudrna.
