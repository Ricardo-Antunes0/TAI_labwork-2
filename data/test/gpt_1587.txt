"Two Times Blue" is a song recorded by American country music artist George Strait. It was released in February 1995 as the fourth and final single from his album of the same name. The song was written by Strait and his then-wife, Melinda McBride.  "Two Times Blue" is a ballad about a man who is heartbroken over the death of his wife. The song has been described as having a "soulful" feel.  The song reached number one on the "Billboard" Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart and number two on the "Billboard" Hot 100 chart. It also reached number one on the Canadian Country Chart and number three on the Australian charts.
