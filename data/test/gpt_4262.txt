Pylaemenes is a genus of stick insects in the family Phasmidae. There are about 80 described species in Pylaemenes.  Most Pylaemenes species are found in the Neotropics, with a few species in Asia and Australia. They are small insects, typically less than 1 cm long, with a long abdomen and a long antennae. They mainly eat dead plant material, but some species are known to feed on other insects.
