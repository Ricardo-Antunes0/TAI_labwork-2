Jerry Angelo (born c. 1949) is a retired American football player. He played college football at the University of Notre Dame and was drafted in the third round of the 1973 NFL Draft by the Buffalo Bills.  Angelo played for the Bills for eleven seasons, from 1973 to 1983. He was a member of the team that won the Super Bowl in January 1984. In his career, Angelo played in 128 regular season games, recording 511 tackles and two interceptions.
