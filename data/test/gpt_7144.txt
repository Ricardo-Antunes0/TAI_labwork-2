"Benderama" is the seventeenth episode of season one of the American animated television series Futurama, and the first episode of the show's seventh season. It aired on the Fox network in the United States on September 25, 1999. The episode was written by Ken Keeler and directed by Bruce Timm.  The crew of the Planet Express ship find themselves stranded on a planet of giant walking Bender dolls, who are ruled by a tyrannical leader named Bender Bending Rodríguez (voiced by Billy West). The crew must work together to overthrow Bender Bending Rodríguez and return peace and happiness to the planet.  "Benderama" was well received by critics, who praised its humor and storytelling. It acquired a Nielsen rating of 9.4, making it the highest-rated episode of season one. The episode was nominated for an Emmy Award for Outstanding Animated Program (but lost to "The Simpsons").
