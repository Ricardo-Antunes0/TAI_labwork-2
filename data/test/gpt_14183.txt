is a Japanese magical girl manga series created by Atsuko Ishida. The manga has been adapted into a anime series produced by Studio Pierrot and Aniplex, which aired from October 6, 2007 to March 26, 2013. A second season, subtitled New Magical Girl Site, aired from October 3, 2009 to March 25, 2010. A third season, subtitled The Final Stage, aired from October 6, 2011 to March 25, 2012. Aniplex of America has licensed the series for an English language release.  The story follows the adventures of Yûko Sumikawa, a high school student who discovers she is a magical girl after being sent on a mission by her school's president. Together with her friends Tsubasa Hanekawa and Akari Kamiya, Yûko fights against wicked magical beings known as witches and their minions in order to protect the people of Japan.
