Yasuda was born in the city of Kurume in Fukuoka Prefecture on July 25, 1953. He made his professional sumo debut in January 1971, and reached the highest rank of Yokozuna in 1988. He retired from sumo after finishing his nineteenth tournament in September 1993.  After his retirement, Yasuda became a coach at his former stable, Isegahama stable. He also became a commentator for Japanese television networks, and has written a book about his sumo career. In 2006, he was inducted into the Japan Sumo Association Sumo Hall of Fame.
