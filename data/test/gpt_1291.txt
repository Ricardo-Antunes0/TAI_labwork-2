Laxmi Sharma is a Nepali entrepreneur. She is the founder of the shoe company Laxmi Footwear, which manufactures and sells women's footwear.  Laxmi Sharma was born in Kathmandu, Nepal in 1978. She studied economics at Tribhuvan University. In 2001, she founded Laxmi Footwear, a women's footwear company that manufactures and sells shoes online. The company has since become one of the largest women's footwear retailers in Nepal. In 2006, Sharma was named Entrepreneur of the Year by the Federation of Nepalese Chambers of Commerce and Industry. She has also received several other awards for her business success. Sharma is also a member of the advisory board for the National Centre for Entrepreneurship Development.
