Serendipity is a 2001 American romantic comedy-drama film directed by Peter Chelsom and written by Eric Roth and Robin Swicord. The film stars John Cusack, Kate Beckinsale, Dan Aykroyd, and Jim Breuer.  The film tells the story of two city dwellers who are randomly reunited in the wilds of upstate New York and must find a way to reintegrate into society.  Serendipity was released on October 25, 2001, received positive reviews from critics, and was a box office success. It was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Picture.
