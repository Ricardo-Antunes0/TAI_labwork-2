The Irish Family (later The Irish Family Album) is a album by The Clancy Brothers and Tommy Makem, released in 1961. It was their first album together since the early 1950s.  The album consists of 12 traditional Irish ballads, most of them arranged by Tommy Makem. The album reached number two in the Irish Albums Chart and remained in the chart for forty weeks. It also reached number one in the Scottish Albums Chart.  The album was reissued on CD in 2004 with five bonus tracks.
