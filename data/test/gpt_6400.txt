The 1954 NSWRFL season was the forty-seventh season of the National Rugby League. Eight teams competed from Australia and New Zealand for the championship. The Sydney Roosters won the premiership and made it their tenth consecutive title.     This season was also the first time that a team from Queensland, the Brisbane Broncos, competed in the premiership.     The 1954 NSWRFL season was also notable for the debuts of two of rugby league's greatest players, George Best and John Charles.
