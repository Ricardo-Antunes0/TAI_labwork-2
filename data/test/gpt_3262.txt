Nathalie Ronvaux (born 1977) is a Luxembourgish artist who lives and works in Berlin.   Ronvaux's practice focuses on the investigation of the formal properties and dynamics of images. Her work often deals with the representation of time and space, as well as their fictional and synthetic derivations. She has exhibited at venues including the Museum of Modern Art, New York; Centre Pompidou, Paris; and La Cambre, Luxembourg.
