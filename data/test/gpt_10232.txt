Sid Meier's Pirates! is a video game developed by Firaxis Games and published by 2K Games. It is the fourth game in the Sid Meier's Civilization series and was released on October 17, 2012.  The game puts players in the role of a pirate captain seeking to plunder treasure and sail the seven seas in search of new ports of call. Players must manage their crew, trade goods, and battle hostile ships in order to become the most powerful pirate captain of all time.  The game has received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised its graphics and gameplay. It was nominated for Best Strategy Game at the 2013 Golden Joystick Awards, but lost to XCOM: Enemy Unknown.
