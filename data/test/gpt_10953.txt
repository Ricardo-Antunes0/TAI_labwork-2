Dangerous Curve Ahead is a 1921 American silent drama film directed by George Melford and starring Barbara Stanwyck, Walter Huston and Lionel Barrymore.  The film tells the story of a young woman (Stanwyck) who marries a man (Huston) only to discover that he is a gambler and alcoholic. She leaves him and begins a new life with her sister (Barrymore). However, Huston tracks her down and tries to win her back.
