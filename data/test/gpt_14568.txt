Giuliana Attenni was an Italian film editor.  Born on October 5, 1934 in Rome, Attenni began her career as an apprentice editor at the age of 18, working on films such as "The Battle of Algiers" (1965). She went on to work on a variety of notable films, including "A Special Day" (1970), "The Godfather" (1972), "The Conformist" (1970), and "The Leopard" (1963). Attenni was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Film Editing for her work on "The Conformist". She died on March 7, 2009 in Rome.
