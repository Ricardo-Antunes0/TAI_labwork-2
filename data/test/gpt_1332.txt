The Wild Thornberrys is an American animated television series created by Jay Ward. The show originally aired on ABC from September 12, 1998 to May 9, 2002.  The show is about a family of wild animals who live in a forest near the town of Rockville. The Thornberrys are a family of four: their parents, Thornberry Sr. (Ward), an anthropomorphic black bear, and Mrs. Thornberry (Suzy Nakamura), a human; their children, Rudyard (Ward), a jaguar; and their daughter, Kip (Rhea Perlman), a red panda. The family's primary antagonist is the evil lion named Timmy Turner (voiced by Tony Anselmo).
