Jeunesse Sportive de Kabylie (Kabyle: Ilemẓiyen inaddalen, French pronunciation: ​[jɛ̃swa sɔpti f kabyle], commonly known as JS Kabylie or JS Kabyles, is a French football club based in the city of Kabyles, Algeria. The club was formed in 1966 and plays in the Ligue Nationale 2.  JS Kabylie played its first season in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 2 in the 1966–67 season. JS Kabylie finished second in their group and advanced to the round of 16 where they were eliminated by ASO Chlef. JS Kabylie finished their first season in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 2 with a record of six wins, one draw, and one loss.  In the 1970–71 season, JS Kabylie finished second in their group and advanced to the round of 16 where they were eliminated by ES Sétif. JS Kabylie finished their first season in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 2 with a record of eight wins, two draws, and two losses.
