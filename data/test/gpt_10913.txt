WWFW is an FM radio station located in Fort Wayne, Indiana. Owned by iHeartMedia, it broadcasts a country music format.  WWFW first went on the air in 1953 as WKSW-FM. It was owned by the Knox family and broadcast a classical music format. In 1969, the station changed its call sign to WFWA and became a Top 40 station. In 1974, it switched to its current country music format.
