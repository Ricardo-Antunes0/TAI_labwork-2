Periyachi (Tamil: பெரியாச்சி, ) is a ferocious Tamil deity who is worshipped in the state of Tamil Nadu in southern India. He is also known as Amman, Periya Puranam and Periya Kaal.  Periyachi is one of the most popular Hindu deities in Tamil Nadu. He is the lord of forests, animals and fertility. He is also the guardian deity of the city of Madurai. Periyachi is often depicted with a conch shell in his hand, indicating his role as a god of marine life.  Periyachi is also one of the Navagrahas, or nine celestial deities, who are worshipped in India for their beneficial effects on health and wealth.
