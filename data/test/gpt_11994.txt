Macroglossum aesalon is a moth of the family Sphingidae. It is known from the Philippines.  The wingspan is about 110Â mm. It is similar to "Macroglossum costaricense" but the forewing upperside is more uniform brown, without any conspicuous markings and the hindwing upperside has a smaller discal spot and a less prominent postmedian line. The underside of the hindwing upperside is paler than that of "Macroglossum costaricense".
