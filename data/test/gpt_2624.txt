Karen Greenlee (born 1956) is an American artist who has explored a wide range of media, including painting, sculpture, video, and performance.  Greenlee was born in 1956 in New York City. She received her BFA from the School of Visual Arts in 1978 and her MFA from the Yale School of Art in 1985. She has exhibited her work extensively, including at the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Guggenheim Museum. Greenlee is also the recipient of a Guggenheim Fellowship and a MacArthur Fellowship.
