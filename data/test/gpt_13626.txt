A. S. Valentine was an early professional American football player. He played for the Pittsburgh Alleghenys from 1883 to 1884.  Valentine was born in 1848 in Allegheny County, Pennsylvania. He attended the University of Pennsylvania and played football for the Quakers. After graduating from college, Valentine played for the Alleghenys in 1883 and 1884. He was a member of the Pittsburgh team that won the 1883 American Football Association championship.
