Safe Haven is the debut studio album by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on October 24, 2008, by Big Machine Records. The album was produced by Nathan Chapman and Swift.  The album has been noted for its lyrical and musical simplicity, with most of the tracks being written in just two or three takeovers.
