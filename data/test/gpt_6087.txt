The Ngcobo Police Killings are a series of murders of seven police officers which took place on the night of 1 December 2008 in Ngcobo, a township in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The officers were shot dead while on duty at a police station in Ngcobo township. The motive for the killings is unknown, but they are suspected to have been carried out by the Zulu Nationalist Movement (ZNM).
