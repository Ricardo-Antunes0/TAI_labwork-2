Chevron Lubricants Lanka PLC is a manufacturer, supplier and distributor of lubricants in Sri Lanka. The company was founded in 1978 and is based in Colombo.  Chevron Lubricants Lanka PLC manufactures lubricants for a wide range of applications including automotive, industrial, agricultural and construction. The company's lubricants are marketed under the Chevron, Rexel, GTL and Castrol brands.  Chevron Lubricants Lanka PLC is a privately owned company with a workforce of around 200 people. The company has a product range that includes synthetic and natural oils, greases and waxes. The company's products are sold through distributors and retailers across Sri Lanka
