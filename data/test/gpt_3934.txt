Kenneth Napper (born July 14, 1933 in Mt. Juliet, Tennessee) is a former American football player and coach. He played college football for the University of Tennessee under head coach Johnny Majors, and was drafted in the first round of the 1955 NFL Draft by the Baltimore Colts. Napper served as the head coach at his alma mater, the University of Tennessee, from 1967 until 1977, compiling a record of 49–32–2. He was inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame as a coach in 1988.
