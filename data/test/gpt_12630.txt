The Merrill Accolades American Express Card, previously known as the Merrill Lynch American Express Card, is a credit card issued by Merrill Lynch.  The Merrill Accolades American Express Card is a rewards credit card that offers its cardholders a variety of benefits and rewards including airfare and travel insurance, free access to airport lounges, and special offers from select merchants. With a $450 annual fee, the Merrill Accolades American Express Card is one of the more expensive cards on the market. However, its rewards program makes it worth considering for those looking for a premium rewards card.
