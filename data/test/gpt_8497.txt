Gingko is a UK-based charitable foundation created in 2004 to support the promotion and diffusion of information and knowledge. The Gingko Library is an online library that provides free access to a wide range of academic journals, books, e-books and other resources.  The Gingko Library was founded in 2004 with the aim of promoting and diffusing information and knowledge. The library provides free access to a wide range of academic journals, books, e-books and other resources. The library's online platform makes it easy for users to find the information they need regardless of where they are in the world.  The Gingko Library is a valuable resource for students, researchers and anyone interested in learning more about the latest trends in their field. The library offers a unique collection of resources that can be accessed from anywhere in the world.
