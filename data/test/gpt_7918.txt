Jumber Patiashvili  (born January 5, 1940) is a retired Georgian professional football player.  Patiashvili played club football for FC Tbilisi, FC Dinamo Tbilisi, FC Zestafoni, FC Dynamo Tbilisi and FC Rustavi Batumi. He made 176 appearances in the Soviet Premier League, scoring 33 goals. He also played for the Georgian national team, appearing in the 1966 FIFA World Cup and the 1974 FIFA World Cup.
