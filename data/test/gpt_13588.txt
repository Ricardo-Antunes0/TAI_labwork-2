Jordan Taylor Clarkson (born June 7, 1992) is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the Missouri Tigers.  Clarkson was born in Columbia, Missouri, and attended school at Massorette Valley High School in Chesterfield, Missouri. As a senior at Massorette Valley, he averaged 28.7 points, 10.0 assists, and 6.7 rebounds per game while shooting 50% from the field and 42% from 3-point range. After his high school career ended, Clarkson attended Missouri where he played for the Tigers from 2011 to 2013. As a freshman at Missouri, he was a member of the SEC All-Freshman Team and was a member of the SEC All-Defensive Second Team as a sophomore. Clarkson helped Missouri reach the Final Four of the NCAA Tournament as a junior in 2013 before losing to Kentucky.  Clarkson was selected by the Lakers with the 22nd overall pick in the 2013 NBA Draft. He made his debut for the Lakers on November 4, 2014 against the Dallas Mavericks. Clarkson averaged 5.5 points and 2.6 assists in 17 minutes per game over his first three seasons with Los Angeles before becoming a full-time starter in 2017–18.
