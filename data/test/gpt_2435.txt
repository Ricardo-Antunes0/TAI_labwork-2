Biyan Pian or Bi Yan Pian   Biyan Pian is a Chinese classical pianist and composer. He was born in Shanghai in 1952. He studied music at the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, where he graduated with a degree in composition in 1977.   Biyan Pian has performed throughout China and abroad, and has made recordings of both his own compositions and works by other composers. In 1984 he founded the Beijing Biyan Pian Quartet, which has since toured extensively throughout China and abroad. He also teaches at the Beijing Conservatory of Music.
