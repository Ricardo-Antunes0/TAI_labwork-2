Born in Hiroshima Prefecture, Miyashita made her television debut in 1992 with a role in the drama "Kimi no Na Wa". She continued to appear in supporting roles throughout the 1990s, but received her breakout role in the 2001 drama "Inuyasha". The following year, she played the lead role in the acclaimed mystery series "Nanatsu no Taizai", which helped her win the Best Actress award at the Japan Academy Prize. Miyashita continued to appear in leading roles in films throughout the 2000s and 2010s, including "Tokyo Ghoul" (2014), "The Cat Returns" (2002), and "2046" (2013).  Miyashita has also worked as a noted stage actress, appearing in productions of Shakespeare's plays such as "Romeo and Juliet" and "Macbeth". In 2019, she starred in the NHK drama series "Tsugaru Taniguchi" as Kaede.
