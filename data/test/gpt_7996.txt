The Kingpin (Wilson Grant Fisk) is a fictional supervillain appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics, usually as an enemy of the superhero Spider-Man. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #129 (May 1965).  Fisk is a wealthy businessman and crime boss who became the head of the criminal underworld in New York City after killing his father. He uses his wealth, power, and connections to ensure that he remains one of the most powerful people in the city. Fisk has been married three times and has had several children.
