She later became a regular on variety shows of the 1950s, appearing alongside such comedians as Red Skelton and Dick Van Dyke. Bell made her Broadway debut in 1964 in the musical comedy Bye Bye Birdie. She won a Tony Award for her performance in the play. In 1977, she starred in the Broadway revival of The Sunshine Boys, for which she received another Tony Award nomination. Bell also appeared in several films throughout her career, including The Apartment (1960), Theres Something About Mary (1998), What Women Want (2000) and Sisters (2002).  Bell was married to actor Tim Conway from 1951 to 1957. They had one child, daughter Kelly Conway-Bell. She later married journalist/author Robert Novak in 1988. Bell died on October 14, 2018 at the age of 92.
