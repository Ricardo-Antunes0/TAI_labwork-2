Idea Bank Spółka Akcyjna (currently Bank Polska Kasa Opieki SA) is a Polish bank with the largest credit and debit card market share in the country. It was founded on January 26, 1991 in Warsaw.  Idea Bank is one of the largest banks in Poland with a market share of over 30%. The company offers both personal and business banking products and services. Idea Bank has over 1,500 branches in Poland and employs over 35,000 people. The company is a subsidiary of Bank Polska Kasa Opieki SA.
