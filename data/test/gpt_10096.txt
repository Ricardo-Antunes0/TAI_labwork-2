The HTC One S (codenamed Ville) is a high-end Android smartphone developed by HTC and released on November 10, 2011. It is the successor to the HTC One and is powered by Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich.  The HTC One S features a 4.7-inch WVGA Super LCD 2 display with a resolution of 1280x720px, a 1.5GHz dual-core Snapdragon S4 processor, 1GB of RAM, 16GB of internal storage (plus a microSD card slot), a 5MP rear-facing camera with LED flash and a 1.3MP front-facing camera, an aluminum unibody design, and runs on HTC's Sense 4 user interface.
