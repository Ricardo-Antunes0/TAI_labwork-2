Gogo Inc. is a provider of in-flight Internet service, which allows passengers to access the Internet, email, and other online services while in flight. The service is offered on a variety of commercial aircraft, including airplanes from major airlines such as American Airlines, Delta Air Lines, United Airlines, and British Airways. Gogo was founded in 2002 by The Carlyle Group and has since been funded by several venture capital firms. The company has more than 3,000 employees and operates in more than 60 countries.
