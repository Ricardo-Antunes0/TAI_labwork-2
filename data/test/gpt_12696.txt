Karine Jean-Pierre (born August 13, 1977) is a Canadian actress, model, and singer.  Karine Jean-Pierre was born in Ottawa, Ontario to parents of Haitian descent. She has two sisters and an older brother. Jean-Pierre began her acting career in the late 1990s, appearing in films such as "The Score" (2001), "The Prestige" (2006), and "Inglourious Basterds" (2009). Jean-Pierre has also appeared in television series such as "Warehouse 13" (2008), "Nikita" (2010), and "Kim Possible" (2012). In 2015, she starred in the drama film "Pete's Dragon".
