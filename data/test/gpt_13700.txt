Captain Caution is a 1940 American adventure film directed by Michael Curtiz and produced by Hal B. Wallis. The screenplay was written by James Kevin McGuinness, from a story by Curtiz and Charles Lederer.  The film is about a treasure hunter, played by Errol Flynn, who is captured by pirates and held for ransom on a tropical island. He is rescued by a friendly naval officer, played by Don Ameche, who takes him to the United States to seek justice.  The film was shot on location in the Virgin Islands, and has been described as one of the most popular adventure films of its era.
