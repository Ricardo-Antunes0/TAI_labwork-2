Zachary Borovay is a media designer specializing in motion graphics, animation and interactivity. He has worked on projects for MTV, The New York Times, and Nike.  Borovay was born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada in 1984. After graduating from the Emily Carr University of Art and Design with a degree in media design in 2006, he moved to New York City to work as an animator on projects for MTV. In 2009 he moved back to Montreal to work as a motion graphics designer at Nike. Borovay has also worked as a freelance graphic designer since 2007.
