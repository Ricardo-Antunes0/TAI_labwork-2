Matt Marcy (born 1976, Los Angeles, California) is an American artist. He lives and works in Brooklyn, New York.  Marcy's early work was based in video and performance, but he has since shifted his focus to painting and sculpture. His work often features appropriated images from pop culture and advertising, as well as references to biblical stories and mythology. His paintings and sculptures have been exhibited at venues including the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Museum of Modern Art, the Tate Modern, and the Venice Biennale.  Marcy was named the recipient of the 2018 MacArthur Fellowship, one of the world's most prestigious awards for artists.
