The Velopark is a motorsport venue and amusement park in Baku, Azerbaijan. The amusement park has a capacity of 350,000 people and opened in 2006. It is home to the Baku World Circuit, a racing track which has hosted several Formula One races. The Velopark also has a zoo, a waterpark, and a theme park.
