Van Schaik is a Dutch toponymic surname. Notable people with the surname include:  1) Jan van Schaik (1576–1641), a Dutch astronomer and mathematician  2) Cornelis van Schaik (1621–1684), a Dutch painter, etcher and printmaker  3) Johan van Schaik (1632–1711), a Dutch physician and natural scientist  4) Pieter van Schaik (1650–1721), a Dutch painter
