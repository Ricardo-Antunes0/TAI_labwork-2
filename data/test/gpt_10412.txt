Antara (or Antaragram) is a mental health hospital located in Delhi, India. It is one of the oldest and largest psychiatric hospitals in Asia. It has a capacity to accommodate 1,200 patients.  The hospital was established in 1895 by the Government of India as the Central Mental Hospital. It was renamed as Antara in 1978. The hospital is affiliated to the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi.
