Arthur Tashko (1901-1994) was an Albanian born American painter who specialized in landscapes and seascapes.  Arthur Tashko was born in 1901 in Tirana, Albania. After studying at the Academy of Fine Arts in Vienna, he moved to the United States in 1930, where he began painting landscapes and seascapes. He became a naturalized citizen in 1941. Tashko died in 1994.
