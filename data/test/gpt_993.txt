Delivery Dudes is a Florida-based on-demand delivery service.  Delivery Dudes was founded in 2014 by two young entrepreneurs, Chase Williamson and Trenton Wooten. The company operates as a on-demand delivery service, providing customers with local and national delivery services. Delivery Dudes offers a variety of delivery options, including same-day and next-day delivery. In addition to on-demand delivery, the company also provides delivery consulting services to businesses.   Delivery Dudes has quickly grown to be one of the leading on-demand delivery companies in the United States. In 2016, the company received $5 million in venture capital funding. Delivery Dudes has also received awards for its innovative delivery methods, including the prestigious 2017 Fast Company 50 Award for Best New Company and the 2017 South by Southwest (SXSW) Innovator Award.
