Stephanie Ann Miner (born April 30, 1970) is an American actress, model and television personality. She is best known for her role as Donna Pinciotti on the ABC sitcomStephanie (1996–2002).  Miner was born in Topeka, Kansas, and moved to Los Angeles when she was twelve years old. She started her career as a model and appeared in several commercials. Miner was cast in the role of Donna Pinciotti on the ABC sitcomStephanie (1996–2002), which won her two Golden Globe Awards. She also appeared in the films Coyote Ugly (2000), The Omen (2006), and The Stepford Wives (2004). In 2006, Miner starred in the independent filmThe Burning Plain. Since 2007, she has appeared as a judge on America's Next Top Model.
