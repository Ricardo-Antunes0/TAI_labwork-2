Avnede Church is a Gothic church located in the town of Avnede, in the province of Aarhus, Denmark. Avnede Church is a Gothic church located in the town of Avnede, in the province of Aarhus, Denmark. The church was built between 1220 and 1250 and it is the only remaining Medieval Gothic church in Jutland. The church is situated on a small hill and it has a rectangular nave with side aisles and a transept. It has a tower with three stages and a spire. The exterior walls are decorated with frescos, which date from the 14th century.
