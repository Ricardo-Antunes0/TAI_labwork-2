Sophronia primella is a moth of the family Geometridae. It is found in Nova Scotia and Maine.  The wingspan is about 25 mm. The forewings are uniform light brown, but there is a broad median band of light brown from the base to one-third, edged anteriorly with dark brown and posteriorly with white. There is a series of black spots along the costa, including one at one-fourth and another beyond the middle, and a short streak from the dorsum before the tornus to the costa at one-third. The hindwings are light brown, but darker towards the apex.
