Raj Singh Chaudhary is an Indian actor, producer and director who has worked in Hindi, Telugu, Malayalam and Tamil films. He made his acting debut in the 2003 film Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge. He received critical acclaim for his performances in the films Mohabbatein (2010), Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge 2 (2012), and Chennai Express (2013). In 2018, he starred in the biographical war drama Sairat.  Raj Singh Chaudhary was born in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, on 15 December 1979. He is the son of actor-director Rajesh Khanna and writer-singer Parveen Chaudhary. He has two sisters: Riddhi and Saachi. When he was five years old, his family moved to Mumbai to pursue a career in cinema; his father had earlier appeared in several Hindi films.  Chaudhary made his acting debut in the 2003 film Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, playing the role of Jagdish Verma. The film was a critical and commercial success, and Chaudhary was praised for his performance. He subsequently appeared in several other commercially successful Hindi films, including Mohabbatein (2010), Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge 2 (2012), Chennai Express (2013) and Jab Tak Hai Jaan (2017).
