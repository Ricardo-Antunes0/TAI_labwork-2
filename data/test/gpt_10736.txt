The Battle of Charasiab was fought on October 26, 1839, as part of the Sikh Wars. The British East India Company and Sikh forces led by Ranjit Singh clashed against Afghan forces led by Ahmad Shah Durrani. The battle ended in a decisive British victory, thereby suppressing the Sikh movement and wiping out the last major threat to British Raj in the subcontinent.
