Sir David Mackenzie Wilson (born 30 October 1948) is a British Conservative Party politician who has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for North West Durham since 1997. He served as Minister of State for Transport from 2010 to 2012, and was Parliamentary Under Secretary of State at the Department for Transport from 2012 to 2014.  Wilson was born in Durham, North East England, and was educated at Durham School and Corpus Christi College, Oxford. He worked as a journalist before being elected to Parliament in the 1997 general election as the MP for North West Durham. He served as Parliamentary Private Secretary to Kenneth Clarke from 2001 to 2002, and then became a Junior Minister at the Department for Transport under Michael Howard.
