Tahereh Eybod (Persian: طاهره ایبد) (born 24 January 1989) is an Iranian actress and model.  Eybod was born in Tehran, Iran. She started her acting career in 2007 with a small role in the Iranian film "The Station" directed by Mohsen Makhmalbaf. Eybod later appeared in several other Iranian films and television series, notably "The City", "Narges" and "The Throne".  In 2012, she moved to Germany to continue her acting career. She appeared in several German films, including "The Son's Room" and "Crazy Love". In 2015, she starred in the psychological thriller "Kill Me Lucky" directed by Stefan Koch.  Eybod has also modelled for various campaigns, including the French fashion house Chloé.
