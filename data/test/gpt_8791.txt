Anthony "Tony" Stonem is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character is a member of the X-Men, who first appeared in X-Men #129 (August 1969), and was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby.  Tony is a blue-skinned, black-haired mutant with the ability to project powerful energy blasts from his eyes. He is also a skilled hand-to-hand combatant and possesses superhuman strength.  Tony has been a member of the X-Men since their inception, and has been featured in numerous comic book storylines and arcs. He has also starred in several animated television series and video games.
