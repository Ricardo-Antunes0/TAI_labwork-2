Martha Dübber was a German film editor. She was the editor for over fifty films, including "The Nun's Story" (1959), "The Flight of the Phoenix" (1965), and "The Exorcist" (1973).  Dübber was born in Berlin, Germany, on January 10, 1922. She started working as an editor after completing her studies at the Berlin Film School in 1945. She worked for the German film company UFA from 1945 to 1949, before moving to the United States to work for MGM. Dübber edited films such as "The Nun's Story" (1959), "The Flight of the Phoenix" (1965), and "The Exorcist" (1973). She died on March 4, 1986.
