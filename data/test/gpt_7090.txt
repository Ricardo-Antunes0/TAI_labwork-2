Devin Terrell Clark (born April 12, 1990) is an American mixed martial artist and professional boxer who competes in the Light Heavyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship. A professional competitor since 2008, Clark has also formerly competed for Strikeforce, Tachi Palace Fights, and Bellator.  Clark was born in Mobile, Alabama. He is married to fellow mixed martial artist and former UFC fighter Jessica Eye. They have one daughter.  Clark has primarily fought in the United States, with a single fight in Canada. He has also fought outside of North America twice. His first foreign fight was against Toni Milos in Croatia in December 2013. Clark won the fight by unanimous decision.   Clark became a professional boxer in 2009 after being turned down for a MMA fight due to his boxing experience. He compiled a record of 18-0 with 14 stoppages before signing with the UFC.
