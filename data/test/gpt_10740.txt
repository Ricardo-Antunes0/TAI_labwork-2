"American Honey" is a song written by Sia and Greg Kurstin, and produced by the latter. It was released as the lead single from Sia's sixth studio album, 1000 Forms of Fear (2017).  "American Honey" is an uptempo electropop song with a folk-tinged sound. It features Sia's falsetto vocals and Kurstin's production, which is characterized by its crisp beat and layered synthesizers. The song has been noted for its catchy hook and dance-floor appeal.  The song was well received by music critics, who lauded its catchy melody and Sia's vocals. It reached number one on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and the UK Singles Chart. It was also nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Pop Song.
