It's Breaking News is a Bollywood movie about a young woman named Kavya who is a reporter for a news channel. She is passionate about her work and is determined to make a name for herself in the industry. Kavya's life takes a turn when she is assigned to cover the story of a politician who has been accused of sexual misconduct. Kavya is forced to face her own demons as she investigates the allegations and tries to get to the bottom of the matter.
