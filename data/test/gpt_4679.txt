{{about|the silent film|the Pee-wee's Playhouse episode|List of Something to Do episodes}}  "Something to Do" is an American live-action sitcom that aired on CBS from September 25, 1985 to May 20, 1987. The series was created by Garry Marshall and starred Michael J. Fox as Mike Brady, a high school graduate who becomes the manager of the fictional Amusement Park Playland.
