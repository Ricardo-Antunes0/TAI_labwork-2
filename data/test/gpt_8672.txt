Casualty is a British medical drama television series that aired on BBC One from 1998 to 2003. The show is set in the fictional Holby City hospital and follows the lives of the staff and patients who work and visit there.  The show's main characters are the doctors and nurses who work at the hospital, as well as the patients and their families. The show has been praised for its realistic portrayal of medical procedures and its strong cast of characters.
