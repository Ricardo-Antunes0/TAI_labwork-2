Beatriz Villacañas (born 1954 in Toledo, Spain) is a Spanish artist who lives and works in Barcelona. She studied at the Escuela Superior de Bellas Artes de San Fernando in Madrid and at the Rhode Island School of Design, where she received her BFA in 1977.  Villacañas has exhibited her work extensively, including solo exhibitions at the Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofía in Madrid (2003), the Palais de Tokyo in Paris (2008), and the Museum of Modern Art in New York (2009). Her work is included in numerous public collections, including the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the Tate Gallery.  Villacañas was awarded the Premio Princesa de Asturias de Artes y Letras in 1998, the Premio Nadal in 1999, and the Premio Medalla del Rey en 2003.
