Skyway Monte Bianco is a cable car system in the Dolomites region of northern Italy. The system is operated by the company Skyway S.p.A. and began operations on 15 July 2002.   The system is composed of two cable cars, each with a capacity of up to 80 passengers. It runs from the valley of the river Sella to the summit of Monte Bianco, reaching a height of 3,036 metres (10,046 ft). The journey takes about 25 minutes and is operated using a single car per cable.
