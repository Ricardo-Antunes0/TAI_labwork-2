Colkatay Columbus is a 2016 Bengali comedy film directed by Soumitra Chatterjee and produced by Anjan Dutt. The film stars Rajat Kapoor, Prosenjit Chatterjee, Tapas Pal and Rupesh Ojha in the lead roles.  The film is about a middle-aged man named Columbus (Rajat Kapoor) who is diagnosed with a terminal illness. He decides to spend his last days with his sons in the city he never really appreciated while he was alive. His sons are not only shocked but also unwilling to let their father go. As the days pass, they start to understand what their father means to them and how much he has taught them. They also come to terms with the fact that their father is not just a great man but a great father as well. The film ends with a touching and heartwarming climax.
