Julia Mary Black  (born 29 January 1973) is an English musician, singer, songwriter and record producer. She is the lead singer and primary songwriter of the rock band La Roux.  Black was born in London, England. She started her musical career as the lead singer of the band La Roux, which she formed in 1999 with Claude Lelouch and Patrick Mckenna. The band released their debut album, "Deux", in 2001 and their follow-up, "La Roux", in 2004. Black has also released three solo albums: "Black Box" (2006), "In My Dreams" (2009), and "Jagged Little Pill" (2012).
