Garbanotas (The Curly One) is a Lithuanian rock band formed in 2001. The band's name is a reference to the character Garbanzos from the animated series "The Simpsons".  Garbanotas Bosistas is a Lithuanian rock band formed in 2001, named after the character Garbanzos from the animated series "The Simpsons". The band currently consists of Vytautas Bačiūnas (guitar and vocals), Saulius Juodagalvis (bass guitar), Mindaugas Černiauskas (drums) and Artūras Šimkus (keyboards).
