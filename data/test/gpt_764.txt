HD 220689 is a single star in the constellation of Perseus. It has an apparent visual magnitude of 4.74 and is located about 216 light years from Earth.  HD 220689 is a white-hued main sequence star that is about twice the mass of the Sun and has a temperature of about 5,700 K. It is radiating about 116 times the solar luminosity from its photosphere at an effective temperature of around 7,500 K.
