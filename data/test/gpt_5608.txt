He studied at the University of London, where he obtained his BA in English Literature in 1987. After returning to his home country, he worked as a journalist and translator. He also worked as a columnist for several Arabic-language newspapers.  Dana's poetry is characterized by its lyricism and its exploration of traditional Palestinian themes. His work has been translated into several languages and has been acclaimed by both critics and readers alike.
