Sandtorg is a former municipality in Troms county, Norway. The municipality existed from 1838 until its dissolution in 1964. The administrative centre of the municipality was the town of Sandtorg.  The municipality was established on 1 January 1838 (see formannskapsdistrikt). On 1 January 1964, the municipality was dissolved and divided among neighboring municipalities. The northern and eastern parts of the municipality were combined with Båtsfjord Municipality to form the new Båtsfjord/Sandtorg Municipality. The southern and western parts of the municipality were combined with Holt Municipality to form the new Holt/Sandtorg Municipality.
