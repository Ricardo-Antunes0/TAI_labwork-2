John M. (Sean) Cadogan is  Professor of  Economics at Trinity College Dublin and a Research Fellow in the Department of Economics, Trinity College, Dublin. He has also been a Visiting Professor at the University of California, Los Angeles and at the University of Geneva.  He is the author of over 70 academic papers in the fields of economic theory and analysis, international economics, public finance, and economic development.  He has served on the editorial boards of several academic journals and has been a reviewer for various journals.  He was elected a Fellow of the Royal Irish Academy in 2009.
