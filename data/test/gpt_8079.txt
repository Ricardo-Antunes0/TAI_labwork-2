Roy Sproson (23 September 1930 – 24 January 2013) was an English professional footballer who played as a winger.  Born in Sheffield, Sproson initially played for his hometown club Sheffield United, before moving to Manchester United in 1952. He spent ten seasons at the club, making 283 appearances and scoring 58 goals. He won the FA Cup and the European Cup in 1963 and 1968, respectively. After leaving Manchester United, Sproson played for Racing Santander and Barcelona before retiring from football in 1975. He later worked as a football coach in Spain.  Sproson died on 24 January 2013 at the age of 83 after a long illness.
