LeCharles Vernon Bentley (born November 7, 1979) is an American football coach and former player who is the current head coach of the University at Buffalo. Bentley played college football at Florida, where he was a two-time consensus All-American. He was drafted by the Jacksonville Jaguars in the first round of the 2003 NFL Draft and played for the team from 2003 to 2007. He has also played for the Baltimore Ravens, Philadelphia Eagles and Detroit Lions.  Bentley has been a head coach since 2011, when he was hired as the offensive coordinator and quarterbacks coach at Division II Tuskegee University.
