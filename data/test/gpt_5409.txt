Extraordinary Rendition is a 2007 drama film directed by Kathryn Bigelow and written by Mark Boal. The film stars Jake Gyllenhaal, Ralph Fiennes, and Michael Shannon. It tells the story of a group of American intelligence officers who attempt to capture or kill Al-Qaeda leader Osama bin Laden.
