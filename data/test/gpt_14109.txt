Mildred Milliea (1930-2019) was a Canadian First Nations artist and musician. She is best known for her large paintings of traditional First Nations scenes, as well as for her music, which has been described as "gorgeous, melancholic, and soulful".  Mildred Milliea was born in 1930 in the Nahanni Valley of the Northwest Territories, Canada. She was raised by her grandparents on the Nahanni River, and began painting and making music at an early age. Milliea's paintings depict traditional First Nations scenes with a distinctive combination of realism and abstraction. Her music is often described as gorgeously melancholic and soulful.  Milliea died on January 26, 2019 at the age of 90.
