Anthony "Tony" Gervin Oettinger (born 29 March 1971) is a British Conservative Party politician who has been the Member of Parliament (MP) for Basildon and Billericay since the general election of 2015.  Oettinger was educated at the University of Bristol, where he read business studies. He worked as a corporate lawyer before being elected as a councillor in Basildon in 2003, serving until his election to Parliament. He is a former chairman of the Conservative Party's youth wing, the Conservative Future.
