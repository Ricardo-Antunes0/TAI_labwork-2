is a Japanese anime OVA produced by Sunrise and directed by Kenichi Matsubara. It was originally broadcast in Japan on TV Asahi from July 2nd to September 27th, 1995. The show is based on the manga of the same name written and illustrated by Hiroyuki Imaishi.  Mazinkaiser is set in a future world where wars have ceased, and humans and robots coexist as equals. The story follows the exploits of a team of robots, led by their human pilot, who fight to protect humanity from an evil empire led by a powerful General.
