Franz Burgmeier (born 7 April 1982) is a German professional football player who last played for Rot-Weiß Oberhausen.  Burgmeier began his career with FC Köln, making his Bundesliga debut in 2001. He moved to Hansa Rostock in summer 2005 and played for the club until 2010. Burgmeier then transferred to Rot-Weiß Oberhausen.
