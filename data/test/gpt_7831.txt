Apaches is a Spanish drama television series created by Emilio Aragón and directed by Antonio de la Torre. The series is based on the novel of the same name by Arturo Pérez-Reverte. It premiered on 26 January 2019 on TV3.  The series tells the story of a group of Apaches who, after being betrayed by their own, are forced to survive in the harsh and dangerous environment of the Border Country.
