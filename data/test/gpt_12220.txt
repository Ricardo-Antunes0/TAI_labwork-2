He then worked as a law clerk in Christiania from 1833 to 1836, before becoming an attorney in 1836. He served as deputy judge in Christiania from 1840 to 1841, and as county governor of Buskerud from 1847 to 1850. He also served as a member of the Norwegian Parliament from 1848 to 1850, representing the constituency of Drammen. He later served as Minister of Justice from 1870 to 1872. Riise died in October 1884.
