Victory at Sea is a documentary television series that was produced by the History Channel in 2001. The series is a documentary history of World War II, focusing on naval engagements in the Pacific Ocean.  The History Channel commissioned the series to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the end of World War II. The series was filmed between 1998 and 2001 and is narrated by actor Alec Baldwin.  The series is divided into eight episodes and covers the battles of Pearl Harbor, Midway, Guadalcanal, Iwo Jima, Okinawa, Leyte Gulf, and the battle of the Atlantic. Each episode covers a different naval engagement in the Pacific Ocean.
