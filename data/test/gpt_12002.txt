Nano seed priming in botany and agriculture is the application of a very small quantity of nano seed to the soil surface before seeding. The objective is to increase the rate of germination, growth and yield of plants.  Priming is most commonly used in botany and agriculture to increase the rate of germination, growth and yield of plants. The objective is to increase the number of viable seeds that will grow into plants. Nano seed priming is a process that uses a very small quantity of nano seed to the soil surface before seeding. This can help to increase the rate at which the seeds germinate, grow and produce crops.
