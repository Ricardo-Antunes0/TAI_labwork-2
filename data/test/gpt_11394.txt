The Lucerne Festival Strings is one of the most important classical music festivals in Europe. It is held every two years in Lucerne, Switzerland.  The Lucerne Festival Strings was founded in 1950. The festival has been held every two years since then, except for 1978 and 1980. The festival is usually held in July.  The programme of the Lucerne Festival Strings typically includes a variety of classical music from the Baroque era to the present day. Past performers have included such famous names as Antonio Vivaldi, Johannes Brahms, and Gustav Mahler.
