Gabriel Boric Font (; born 11 February 1986) is a Chilean politician, former student leader and president-elect of Chile, having won the 2021 presidential election run-off vote on 19 December 2021. He is currently serving as a member of the Chamber of Deputies representing the Magallanes Region's 60th district from 2014 to 2018 and the 28th district from 2018 to 2022. He will continue to serve in the Chamber of Deputies until his inauguration as president on 11 March 2022. Boric studied in the Faculty of Law at the University of Chile, and was the president of the University of Chile Student Federation from 2011 to 2012. Boric was twice elected to the Chamber of Deputies representing the Magallanes and Antarctic district, first as an independent candidate in 2013 and then in 2017 as part of the Broad Front, a left-wing coalition he created with several other parties. He is a founding member of Social Convergence, which was formed in 2018 and is one of the constituent parties of Broad Front.
