Old Airport (; also referred to as Doha International Airport, Hamad International Airport, or Qatar International Airport) is an airport in Qatar. It is the busiest airport in the world by passenger traffic, and the ninth busiest by aircraft movements.  The airport was built in 1974 and has been expanded several times since then. It is now the world's largest airport by capacity, with a total area of over 2,000 hectares (5,200 acres). In terms of passenger traffic, it is also the world's busiest airport.  The airport has been praised for its efficient and spacious terminals, as well as its wide range of services and amenities. It has been ranked as one of the best airports in the world by several international organizations.
