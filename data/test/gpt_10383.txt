Bratsky (; masculine), Bratskaya (; feminine), or Bratskoye (; neuter) is a rural locality (a settlement) in Krasnoyarsk Krai, Russia, located on the left bank of the Yenisei River, south of Krasnoyarsk, across from the village of Borovaya. Population:   The name of the settlement derives from the Russian word bratsky (meaning "fisherman"), as the area was formerly a fishermen's colony.
