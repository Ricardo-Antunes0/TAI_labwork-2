Girls' Generation-TTS (, also known as TTS, Girls' Generation-TTS 2nd Generation, or GG TTS) is a South Korean girl group formed by the SM Entertainment label in 2016. The group consists of 9 members: Seohyun, Yuri, Sooyoung, Taeyeon, Sunny, Tiffany, Yoona, and Jiyeon.  Girls' Generation-TTS made their debut on August 11, 2016 with the single "Mr. Taxi".
