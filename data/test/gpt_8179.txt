Andre Eric Proctor (born June 9, 1991), better known by his stage name Dre Moon, is an American rapper and songwriter. He is the co-founder of the record label Strange Music, and has released two studio albums, "Dre Moon" (2013) and "The Good, The Bad & The Dre" (2017).  Moon was born in Flint, Michigan, and grew up in Detroit. He began rapping at the age of 16 and met fellow rapper Eminem while they were both attending high school. Moon released his debut mixtape "The Good, The Bad & The Dre" in December 2017.
