The Miss Bahamas 2010 pageant was held on Saturday, October 11, 2010 at the Atlantis Resort and Casino in Nassau, Bahamas. The winner was Mandy Moore of the United States. Moore won the competition by scoring the highest number of points from the judges based on her presentation skills and interview responses. She will represent her country in Miss Universe 2010 later this year.
