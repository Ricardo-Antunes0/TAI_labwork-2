The Guild Inn, or simply The Guild, is a tavern in the city of Baldur's Gate. The inn is owned and operated by Jarlaxle, a powerful drow mercenary and thief.  The Guild Inn is a popular meeting place for adventurers and traders in Baldur's Gate. Jarlaxle, the proprietor, is always willing to make deals and offer information on the city. He also has a soft spot for the ladies, and often provides them with free drinks in exchange for favors.
