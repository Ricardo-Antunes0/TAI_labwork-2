Else Holmelund Minarik (née Holmelund; September 13, 1907 – January 16, 2002) was a Norwegian artist who is most noted for her paintings of children.  Else Holmelund was born on September 13, 1907 in Oslo, Norway. She was the daughter of painter Fridtjof Holmelund and illustrator Ingrid Bergman. Holmelund studied painting at the Norwegian National Academy of Fine Arts from 1928 to 1934 under the guidance of Professor Olav Brundtland and Professor Hans Gude.   In 1935, she married architect Arne Minarik. The couple had one son and two daughters.   Holmelund died on January 16, 2002 in Oslo, Norway.
