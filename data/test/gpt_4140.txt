The Ancients (also known as the Shoreham Group) were an art group active in the early 1960s. The members were Peter Blake, David Hockney, Francis Bacon, and Maurice Evans.  The Ancients were founded in 1961 by Peter Blake, David Hockney, Francis Bacon, and Maurice Evans. The group exhibited their work together from 1962 to 1967. They exhibited widely, including at the Tate Gallery and the Art Gallery of Ontario. The group disbanded in 1967.
