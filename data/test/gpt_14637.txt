Mädchen, Mädchen (English: Girls, Girls), also known as Die drei Musketiere (German: The Three Musketeers), is a 1963 West German comedy-drama film directed by Wolfgang Liebeneiner and starring Heinz Rühmann, Ulla Jacobson and Jürgen Prochnow.  The film is based on the novel of the same name by the Swiss author Max Frisch. It tells the story of three friends – a bookworm, a jock and a rebel – during their teenage years in the early 1960s. The three friends go through many ups and downs during their time together, but remain close until one of them dies.
