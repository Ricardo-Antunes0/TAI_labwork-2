Chu Jinzhao (; born 11 January 1993) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays for Liaoning Whowin in the Chinese Super League.  Chu started his football career with his local club Liaoning Youth in the China League Two in the 2012 season. He made his senior debut for Liaoning in the 2014 Chinese Super League season. He scored his first senior goal for Liaoning in the 2016 Chinese Super League season.  In January 2019, Chu transferred to Liaoning Whowin.
