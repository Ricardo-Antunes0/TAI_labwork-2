The Museo Mitre (Spanish) (Mitre Museum) in Buenos Aires is a museum of Argentine art. It is housed in a neoclassical building on the corner of Caseros and Belgrano in the neighbourhood of Retiro.  The Museo Mitre was founded by the Society of Jesus in 1887. The collection focuses on Argentine art from the 17th to the 20th centuries, with a particular emphasis on painting, sculpture, and decorative arts.
