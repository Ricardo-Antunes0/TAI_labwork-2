Hongsa  is a town in northwestern India. The town is located in the Himachal Pradesh state, about 190 kilometres southwest of Shimla and 25 kilometres northwest of Dharamsala.  Hongsa is a small town nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. It is known for its scenic beauty and its Tibetan Buddhist monasteries. The town is also home to an ancient Hindu temple, which was built during the reign of the Raja of Jammu and Kashmir.
