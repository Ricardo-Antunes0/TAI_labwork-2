Summer Sundae is a discontinued soft-serve ice cream cone which was introduced by Ben & Jerry's in 1988. The cone is made with a layer of fudge on the bottom, and then a layer of chocolate ice cream. It is then topped with a layer of whipped cream, and finally a layer of cherry sauce.
