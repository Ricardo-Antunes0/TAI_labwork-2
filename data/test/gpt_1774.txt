Gordon Fitzell (born 1968 in Portage la Prairie, Manitoba) is a Canadian artist who lives and works in Winnipeg.  Gordon Fitzell was born in 1968 in Portage la Prairie, Manitoba. He studied at the University of Manitoba, where he earned a BFA in 1993. In 1998, Fitzell moved to Winnipeg, where he has been based ever since.  Fitzell's work explores the relationship between people and their environment. His paintings are often site-specific and explore themes of isolation and loneliness. His work has been exhibited across Canada and in Europe, and has been featured in publications such as the Globe and Mail and Artforum.
