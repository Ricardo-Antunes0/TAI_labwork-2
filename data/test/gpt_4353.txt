Pleione (pronounced  or ) is a blue-white hued star in the constellation of Lyra. It is the brightest star in that constellation and one of the faintest stars visible to the naked eye.  Pleione is about 25 light years from Earth and is around 220 times more luminous than the Sun. It is a member of the spectral class A0V and has the traditional name Alnitak, meaning "the hand of Akhet". Pleione is thought to be a young star that has recently exploded as a supernova.
