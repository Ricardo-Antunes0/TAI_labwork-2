Wikindx is a free bibliographic and quotations/notes database that can be edited by anyone with internet access. Wikindx is powered by the Wikimedia Foundation, a non-profit organization.  Wikindx was created in 2006 by Jimmy Wales, who was then the founder and CEO of Wikipedia. Wikindx is an extension of Wikipedia, which allows users to add text, images, and links to sources. Wikindx is freely available under the GNU General Public License.
