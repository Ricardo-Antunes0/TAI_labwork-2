Renato Bruson (born 13 January 1936) is an Italian film critic and historian. He has written extensively on Italian neorealist cinema, and is considered a leading authority on the topic.  Bruson was born in Milan, Italy. He studied at the University of Milan before becoming a critic for the newspaper "Il Giorno" in 1964. In 1978 he founded the magazine "Cinema Nuovo", which he edited until 1985. He has also been a professor at the University of Bologna and at the University of Rome La Sapienza. Bruson was made an honorary member of theAccademia Nazionale dei Lincei in 2006.
