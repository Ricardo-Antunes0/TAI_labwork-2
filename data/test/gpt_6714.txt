Armscor (stylized as ARMSCOR), the Armaments Corporation of South Africa, is a South African weapons manufacturer. It is headquartered in Pretoria, and has a manufacturing plant in the town of Secunda, Gauteng Province.  Armscor was established in 1966 as a joint venture between the South African government and United States arms manufacturer Colt Industries. The company manufactures firearms and ammunition, as well as military vehicles, aircraft parts and equipment. Armscor is one of the largest arms manufacturers in the world and has been involved in several controversial collaborations with governments in Africa and elsewhere.
