Sports Center Rožaje (SC Rožaje; Montenegrin and Serbian: Sportni centar Rožaje) is a sports complex located in Rožaje, Montenegro. It was opened in 1968.   SC Rožaje is home to the Montenegrin basketball team, FK Budućnost Podgorica and the Serbian handball club Budućnost Belgrade. The complex also features an indoor arena, a football pitch, and a track and field area.
