The HTR-PM (球床模块式高温气冷堆核电站) is a small modular reactor (SMR) design by the China Nuclear Engineering and Construction Corporation subsidiary, CNNEC. It is a successor to the HTR-PM prototype reactor.[1]  The HTR-PM is a small modular reactor designed by CNNEC subsidiary, the HTR-PM. The reactor is a successor to the HTR-PM prototype reactor and is slated to enter commercial operation in the mid 2020s. The design features passive safety systems and uses a fully automated containment system.
