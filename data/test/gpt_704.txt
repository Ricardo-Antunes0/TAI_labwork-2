Holly Near (born June 6, 1949) is an American actress, singer and writer. She is known for her roles as Daphne Blake on the ABC comedy-drama series "The Office" (2005–2013), for which she was nominated for an Emmy Award, a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award; and as Dr. Juliet Burke on the CBS medical drama series "Blue Bloods" (2012–present).  Near was born in Chicago, Illinois, to advertising executive Harry Near and actress-singer Ruth Etting. She has two sisters, Ellen and Joanne. Near began her acting career in the late 1960s, appearing in various stage productions before making her television debut in the NBC sitcom "The Partridge Family" (1970). After guest appearances on various shows, she landed her first regular role on the ABC comedy-drama series "The Office" in 2005.  Near's most notable role to date has been as Daphne Blake on the ABC comedy-drama series "The Office". For which she was nominated for an Emmy Award, a Golden Globe Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award. She also starred as Dr. Juliet Burke on the CBS medical drama series "Blue Bloods" from 2012 to present.
