Sindlesham is an estate village in the English county of Hampshire. The village is located about 4 miles (6.4 km) south of the town of Alton and about 1 mile (1.6 km) east of the town of Basingstoke.  Sindlesham is a small village, with a population of just over 1,000, situated in the Hampshire district of Eastleigh. It is a popular tourist destination due to its location in the New Forest National Park, and is also known for its annual Sindlesham Forest Fire Festival, which attracts thousands of visitors each year. The village has a single pub, the Sindlesham Arms, and a small amount of retail activity.
