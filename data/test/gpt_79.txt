Burçak Tarlası (literally "Vetch Field") is the name given to an area located in the Çanakkale Province of Turkey, just south of the city of Çanakkale. The Burçak Tarlası is a fertile valley, with a population of around 6,000.  The Burçak Tarlası was once covered in vineyards, but today the area is mostly used for agriculture. The Burçak Tarlası is also popular for its natural scenery and for its hot springs.
