African Political Systems is an academic anthology that covers a wide range of African political systems. The book has been written by a team of experts from various disciplines, such as history, sociology, anthropology, politics and law.  The African Political Systems book provides an overview of the variety of African political systems. It provides an understanding of the history and development of these systems and the factors that have influenced them. The book also covers the key factors that may affect the functioning of African political systems, such as economic development, social tensions and international relations.
