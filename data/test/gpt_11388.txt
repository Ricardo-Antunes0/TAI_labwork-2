Settlement Music School is a community music school located in the heart of downtown Toronto. Established in 1994, Settlement Music School is committed to providing quality music education to the local community.  Settlement Music School offers a variety of music programs for students of all ages and abilities. We offer beginner, intermediate, and advanced level classes for singers, instrumentalists, and dancers. Our faculty is comprised of experienced music teachers who are passionate about teaching and sharing their knowledge with students.  Our school is located in the heart of downtown Toronto, within walking distance of many popular tourist destinations. We offer a wide variety of music classes and workshops that are open to the public. If you're looking for a quality music education that's convenient to your home or office, Settlement Music School is the perfect place for you!
