Malika Favre (born 1 December 1982) is a French actress and singer.  Malika Favre was born in Paris on 1 December 1982, the daughter of Moroccan parents. Favre started her career as a model, and made her acting debut in a 2002 short film called "Le Bossu". She became known for her roles in the films "Un Hiver à Paris" (2004), "L'Enfant" (2005), and "Les Misérables" (2007). In 2008, she starred in the drama film "The Class". Favre has also appeared in several French television series, including "Les Liaisons dangereuses" and "Nikita". She has released two albums, one of which, "Je t'aime", was certified platinum in France. Favre is married to French actor Romain Duris.
