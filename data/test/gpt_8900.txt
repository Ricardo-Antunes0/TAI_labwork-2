Jean Beaudry (born 1947 in Trois-Rivières) is a Canadian artist whose work deals with the relationship between art and life.  Beaudry was born in 1947 in Trois-Rivières, Quebec. He studied at the Université du Québec à Trois-Rivières and at the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston. He has exhibited his work in Canada, France, Italy, Spain, Belgium, Switzerland, the United States and Japan. Beaudry is a member of the Royal Canadian Academy of Arts.
