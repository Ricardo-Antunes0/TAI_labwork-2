There are about 1.5 million speakers of Tai Lue, making it the tenth most spoken language in the world.  Tai Lue is one of the seven principal Tai languages, and is classified as a southern Tai language by many linguists. It is not mutually intelligible with any other Tai language.  Tai Lue is the national language of Taiwan and Vietnam. In Taiwan, it is also an official language of the Republic of China.
