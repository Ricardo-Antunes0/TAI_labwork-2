Kumbhalgarh Wildlife Sanctuary is located in the Kumbhalgarh district of Rajasthan, in the north-western region of India. The sanctuary is situated at an elevation of 1,522 meters above sea level.   The sanctuary encompasses an area of 5,000 hectares. It is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including tigers, leopards, elephants, rhinos and several other species of animals. The sanctuary is also a popular tourist destination.
