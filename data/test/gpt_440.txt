The International 2017 (TI7) was the seventh annual Dota 2 International tournament. Held in Seattle, Washington, United States from August 3 to August 7, 2017, it was the largest international tournament to date, featuring 8 teams from around the world.  The International is one of the premier international tournaments of the Dota 2 game. Held annually in a different city, TI is a chance for teams from all over the world to compete for a share of a $24 million prize pool.  TI7 was held at the KeyArena in Seattle, Washington and was broadcast by Valve Corporation. The main event featured 8 teams, with the first round robin group stage consisting of 4 teams, followed by a double-elimination bracket.
