Mohja Kahf (, born 1967 in   Tehran, Iran) is a contemporary Iranian artist.   Mohja Kahf was born in Tehran in 1967. She completed her undergraduate studies at the University of Tehran in 1991 and subsequently continued her studies at the Royal College of Art in London, England, where she obtained her MA in 1995. She has exhibited her work extensively both in Iran and internationally, including solo exhibitions at the Museum of Contemporary Art, Tehran (2007), the Mus√©e du Louvre, Paris (2008), and the Centre Pompidou, Paris (2010). She also participated in group exhibitions such as "Toward a New Era: Contemporary Iranian Art" at the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston (2006), "Perspectives: Contemporary Iranian Painting" at the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, New York (2008), and "Women Artists from Iran" at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art (2010).
