Boogeyman 2 is a 2007 American horror film directed by David Slade and written by Patrick Melton and Marcus Dunstan. The film stars Shawn Ashmore, Danielle Harris, and Oliver Platt.  The film tells the story of a family who move into a new home and soon start to fear the boogeyman that they believe is lurking in the shadows.   The film was released on October 4, 2007, to mixed reviews from critics. However, it was commercially successful, grossing over $105 million worldwide.
