Booches is a bar, restaurant, and pool hall located at 2136 North Central Expressway in Dallas, Texas. The bar is open Monday through Friday from 3 p.m. to 2 a.m., Saturday from 12 p.m. to 2 a.m., and Sunday from 12 p.m. to 1 a.m.; the restaurant is open Monday through Thursday from 11:30 a.m. to 11 p.m., Friday and Saturday from 11:30 a.m. to 12 a.m., and Sunday from 11:30 a.m. to 11 p.m.; the pool hall is open Monday through Thursday from 8 p.m. to 2 a.m., Friday and Saturday from 8 p.m. to 3 a.m., and Sunday from 8 p.m. to midnight
