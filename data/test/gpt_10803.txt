Character.org is a worldwide network that empowers people to create and share their stories about characters in fiction and nonfiction. We provide resources and tools to help people create and share their character stories, and we work to promote understanding and appreciation of characters in literature, television, film, and other forms of storytelling.  Our mission is to create a space where people can share their stories, learn from others' experiences, and build a community of character enthusiasts. We offer a variety of tools and resources to help people write, share, and connect with others who share their interests in characters.  We welcome everyone who wants to learn more about character and storytelling, regardless of experience or level of expertise. Our goal is to make Character.org a resource for everyone who loves characters and wants to explore the power they have to engage audiences around the world.
