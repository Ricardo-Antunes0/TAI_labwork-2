Ishikawa 2nd district (石川[県第]2区, Ishikawa-[ken-dai-]ni-ku) is a district located in Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan. The district has an area of 181.22 square kilometers and a population of 207,460. The district office is located in the town of Matsumoto.  The district is located in northeastern Ishikawa Prefecture, on the border with Yamanashi Prefecture. The district includes the town of Matsumoto and the villages of Ayabe, Itoigawa, Nakagawa, Nishino-machi, Takasaki and Tomioka.  The main industries are agriculture, forestry and paper production. Tourism is also a major source of income for the district. The district also has a large number of small businesses.
