Ovidiu Cotruș (February 24, 1926–September 12, 1977) was a Romanian critic, translator, and essayist.  Born in Bucharest, Cotruș attended the University of Bucharest Faculty of Letters and received his degree in 1951. He was a professor at the University of Bucharest from 1965 until his death in 1977. Cotruș was also a literary critic for the daily newspaper "Jurnalul National" from 1958 until his death. His essays and translations have been published in various anthologies, including "Poezii și proze" (1962), "Românii de la Platon la Eminescu" (1971), "Ovidiu Cotruș: studii și texte" (1976), and "Ovidiu Cotruș: o istorie literară" (1991).
