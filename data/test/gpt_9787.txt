Paul S. Riebenfeld was a political scientist and public servant. He served as the United States Ambassador to Sweden from 1984 to 1988.  Riebenfeld was born on September 26, 1928, in New York City. He received his bachelor's degree from Yale University in 1950 and his doctorate in political science from Harvard University in 1954.  Riebenfeld began his career as a professor at the University of Minnesota in 1956. He then served as a professor at the University of Maryland from 1960 to 1973, where he was also the director of the Institute for Advanced Study from 1971 to 1973. From 1973 to 1984, he served as the United States Ambassador to Sweden.  Riebenfeld died on January 10, 2017, in Chevy Chase, Maryland.
