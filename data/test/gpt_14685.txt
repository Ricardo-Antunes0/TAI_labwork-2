"The Debarted" is the thirteenth episode of the first season of AMC's The Walking Dead. It is written by Scott M. Gimple and directed by Greg Nicotero.  Rick and the group come across a group of survivors led by a man named John who tells them about a safe haven known as the Debarted. However, when they arrive, they realize that the place is already occupied by walkers.  Meanwhile, back at the prison, Carol is trying to come up with a plan to get out with the help of her fellow inmates. However, when she finally succeeds and leads them on a daring escape attempt, they are quickly surrounded and forced to fight for their lives.  The episode ends with Rick and his group finally making it to the Debarted, where they find that it's not as safe as they thought it would be.
