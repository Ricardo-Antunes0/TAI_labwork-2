Brita, "the wife of Jöran Pipare" (floruit 1290-1315?), was a Norwegian noblewoman. She is best known as the wife of Jöran Pipare, who served as the Bishop of Bergen from 1290 until his death in 1315.  Brita was the daughter of Hakon Lavard and Margrete Friis. She married Jöran Pipare in 1290. The couple had three children: Odd, Oda, and Tora. Brita died in 1315.
