The so-called Saardom (full name: "Ecclesia Parochialis Saardom";  English:  Church of the Parish Saardom) is a church body in Estonia, which was founded in 1919 by the Estonian National Awakening movement. The Saardom is the only ecclesiastical body in Estonia that has a nationwide jurisdiction. The church has about 3,000 members.  The Saardom has its seat in Tallinn and is a member of the World Council of Churches.
