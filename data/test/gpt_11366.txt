In cotidianis precibus  is a motu proprio issued by Pope Francis on September 24, 2016, to create a new Secretariat for the Promotion of Christian Unity.     Pope Francis issued "In cotidianis precibus" on September 24, 2016, creating a new Secretariat for the Promotion of Christian Unity. The Secretariat will be responsible for fostering Christian unity through prayer, dialogue, and action. The Pope called for the Secretariat to be "a place where the Church's many voices can be heard and where all can contribute to the building of a more peaceful and just world."
