Bob Ong is the pseudonym of a Singapore-based author who writes about Southeast Asian history, culture, and politics. Ong's work has been published in "The New York Times", "The Wall Street Journal", "The Australian", and other international journals and magazines.  Prior to becoming a full-time author, Ong was a reporter and editor with several Singapore newspapers, including the Straits Times and The Business Times. He is also a former executive producer of the television news program "Asia Broadcast News".  Ong was born in Singapore in 1962. He graduated from the University of Singapore with a degree in political science and history.
