The  is a high-speed Shinkansen railway line in Japan. The line runs from Shin-Osaka in the south to Hakata in the north, and has a total length of 467.5 km. The line is operated by JR East.   The Hokuriku Shinkansen opened on March 15, 1964, connecting Shin-Osaka with Hakata. At the time, it was the fastest railway in the world. The line was extended north to Nagoya in 1986, and to Tokyo in 2001.
