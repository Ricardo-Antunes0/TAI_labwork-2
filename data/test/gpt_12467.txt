Adcote School is a non-selective independent day and boarding school in the village of Adcote, near Harlow, Essex, England. It was founded in 1892 by Revd. W.H.R. Rivers and his wife, and takes pupils from Nursery to Year 12.  The school is a member of the Headmasters' and Headmistresses' Conference and the Association of Independent Schools (AIS).  The current headmaster is Dr Simon Dutton. Adcote School has an enrolment of about 210 pupils aged 3 to 18 years old, including 50 boarders. The school has a roll of 52 staff.
