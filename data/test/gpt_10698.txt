Wolf Solent is a novel by John Flanagan, published in 2006. It is the first book in the series known as The Ranger's Apprentice.  The story is set in the fictional kingdom of Araluen, on the shores of the Wolf River. After the death of his father, young Will Stanton is apprenticed to the Ranger-Captain Araluen. He must learn to use his natural gifts as a ranger to protect Araluen from its many enemies.
