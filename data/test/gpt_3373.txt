Vasanthakala Paravai  is a 1991 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film directed by S. Shankar and produced by K. Balachander. The film stars Kamal Haasan and Sarika in the lead roles with Rajinikanth, Sathyaraj, and Vadivelu appearing in supporting roles.  The film tells the story of Vasanthakala (Kamal Haasan), a playboy who falls in love with Parvathy (Sarika), a poor girl who is working as a maid in his family. When Vasanthakala's family learns of his true identity, they try to break their relationship, but Vasanthakala stands by Parvathy no matter what. The film culminates with Vasanthakala and Parvathy getting married.
