Lana Bastašić is a Bosnian and Herzegovinian singer, songwriter and actress. Bastašić rose to prominence in the early 2000s, when she began recording music for Bosnian and Herzegovinian films. She has since released several albums, as well as appeared in a number of television and film roles. Bastašić is also a vocal advocate for women's rights and has worked to raise awareness of violence against women in her country
