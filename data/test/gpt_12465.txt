Surinder Pal Dhammi, better known as Surinder Shinda, is a Canadian comedian and actor. He is best known for his work on the Canadian television series Mr. D and its spin-off series The Surinder Shinda Show.  Shinda was born in Toronto, Ontario to parents of Punjabi descent. He attended Thorncliffe Park Secondary School and then studied at York University, where he received a degree in English literature.  Shinda began his career as a stand-up comic in Toronto. In 2003, he appeared as a contestant on the Canadian reality television show Mr. D, which helped launch his career in television. He later starred in the Canadian sitcom The Surinder Shinda Show, for which he was nominated for a Golden Globe Award in 2015.  Shinda has also acted in films, including the Canadian comedy Kopi Kapoi.
