Delta Pictoris, Latinized from Î´ Pictoris, is an orange-hued star located about 163 light years from Earth in the Pictor constellation. The spectral type of this star is A5III.  Delta Pictoris is a K-type main sequence star that is about 2.6 billion years old. It is approximately 170,000 times the size of the Sun and has about 1.1 times the mass. The star is radiating about 120 times the Sun's luminosity from its outer atmosphere at an effective temperature of about 6,000 K.
