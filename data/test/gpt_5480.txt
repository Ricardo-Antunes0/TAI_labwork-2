Enrique Mari Bacay Gil V (; born January 9, 1965) is a Mexican politician affiliated with the PRI. He currently serves as Deputy of the LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress representing Aguascalientes.  Enrique Gil was born on January 9, 1965 in Aguascalientes, Mexico.
