Steve White is an English musician and songwriter, best known as the lead guitarist for the rock band Whitesnake.   White was born in Redditch, Worcestershire, England, and started playing guitar at the age of 12. He attended Redditch College and played in a number of local bands before being signed to Geffen Records in 1984. He recorded two albums with Whitesnake before leaving in 1987 to form his own band.  White has since released six solo albums, one of which, "Before I Forget", reached number one in the UK Albums Chart. He has also appeared as a guest guitarist on albums by Ozzy Osbourne, David Gilmour and Angra. In 2009, he formed a new Whitesnake with former Deep Purple bassist Roger Glover and drummer Ian Paice.
