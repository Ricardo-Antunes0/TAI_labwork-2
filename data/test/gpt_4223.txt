Gregorio Bermann (1894–1972) was an Argentine psychiatrist, psychoanalyst and researcher. He was the first president of the ArgentinePsychoanalytic Association.  Bermann was born in Buenos Aires on December 10, 1894. He received his medical degree from the University of Buenos Aires in 1916 and began his psychiatric residency at the Hospital de Clínicas de la Universidad de Buenos Aires in 1919. He served as a military doctor during World War I, and returned to the Hospital de Clínicas to continue his residency in 1922.  Bermann was appointed director of the psychiatric service at La Plata Hospital in 1936, and served as president of the Argentine Psychiatric Association from 1954 to 1955. He died on November 7, 1972.
