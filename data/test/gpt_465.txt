Thomas Peckett (or Preskett) Prest (probable dates of birth and death unknown; died 1692) was an English writer, printer and publisher.  Prest was born in the town of Thrapston, Northamptonshire, in about 1635. He became a printer in London around 1660, and published several books. He was also a printer of the Declaration of Rights (1689), and is said to have been the first person to print the Bible in English.  Prest died in London in 1692.
