It was released as the fourth single from Nakajima's fifth studio album "Sakura Hitohira" on November 21, 2016.  "Sakura Hitohira" is a story about a girl who is searching for her lost love. The song is written from the perspective of the lost love, and tells the story of how they met, how they fell in love, and what happened after that.  The song was well-received by music critics. They praised its heartfelt lyrics and refreshing soundscape. "Sakura Hitohira" debuted at number one on the Oricon Singles Chart, becoming Nakajima's sixth consecutive number-one single. The song has sold over 260,000 copies in Japan as of February 2018.
