Happy Ghost (released in the Philippines as Naughty Ghost) is a 2010 Philippine horror comedy-drama film directed by Gil Portes and produced by Star Cinema. It is based on the Taiwanese film of the same name, which was also directed by Portes.  The film stars Ruffa Gutierrez, John Lloyd Cruz, and Piolo Pascual. It premiered on October 14, 2010 at the Metro Manila Film Festival.  It tells the story of a nun who moves into aHaunted house with her convent sisters, only to find out that the ghost of their former abbess still haunts the place.
