William Paul Michals is an American stage and screen actor. He is known for his roles in the films The Village, The Omen, and The Exorcist.  Michals was born in Hollywood, California, to actors parents. His father, Paul Michals, was a stage and television actor and his mother, Judith Nathan, was a singer and actress. Michals attended the University of Southern California, where he studied theatre.  Michals began his career in the early 1980s, appearing in television roles before making his feature film debut in the 1985 thriller The Village. He went on to star in the critically acclaimed horror films The Omen (1976) and The Exorcist (1973), as well as the comedy-drama Dead Poet's Society (1989). In recent years, Michals has starred in such films as Letters from Iwo Jima (2006) and We Are Marshall (2009).
