Ahmed Ouyahia (; 2 July 1952 – 25 October 2010) was a French Algerian politician who was the Minister of Territorial Administration from 2004 to 2006 and was President of the Regional Council of Bouches-du-Rhône from 2004 to 2007.  Born in 1952 in Villeneuve-lès-Avignon, Ouyahia was a member of the Union for a Popular Movement (UMP) and was elected to the French National Assembly in 1986, representing the Gard department. He became Minister of Territorial Administration in 2004, serving until 2006. He then served as President of the Regional Council of Bouches-du-Rhône from 2004 to 2007.  Ouyahia died on 25 October 2010 in Marseille, aged 61.
