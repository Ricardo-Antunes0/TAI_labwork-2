Dancing Mary is a 2019 Japanese fantasy film directed by Kinji Fukasaku and written by Nobuaki Mori. It is based on the novel of the same name by Yoshiki Tanaka.  The film stars Rinko Kikuchi, Masahiro Higashide, and Eriko Sanada. It was released in Japan on July 20, 2019.  The film tells the story of a young woman who is cursed with a dancing spirit that takes her on a wild journey across Japan. Along the way, she meets a variety of colorful characters and must fight to save her loved ones from an evil force.
