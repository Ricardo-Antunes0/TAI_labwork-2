David Effron is an American conductor and musicologist.  Effron was born in 1950 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He studied at the Oberlin Conservatory of Music and the University of California, Berkeley, where he received his doctorate in musicology in 1978. He has been conductor of the Orchestre Philharmonique de Radio France since 1984 and the Cleveland Orchestra since 1997. Effron has also been the music director of the Vancouver Symphony Orchestra since 2005.  Effron has made numerous recordings, including a complete cycle of Beethoven symphonies with the Cleveland Orchestra.
