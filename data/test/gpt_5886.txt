Hitron Technologies  is a Taiwanese company that specializes in telecommunications and networking products. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Hsinchu, Taiwan.  Hitron is a leading provider of wired and wireless broadband access solutions for the enterprise, government, education, and home markets. The company's products are used to connect people and devices across the globe. Hitron's products are sold through a network of sales and support partners around the world. Hitron Technologies is a member of the Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) family of companies.
