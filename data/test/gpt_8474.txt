Muscle is an American sitcom television series that aired on NBC from October 24, 2015 to May 19, 2017. The series was created by Kevin Biegel and directed by Jon Cassar. The show stars Jay Baruchel as an ordinary guy who discovers he has a gift for bodybuilding and becomes a professional bodybuilder.  Muscle is a new comedy about Jay Baruchel, an ordinary guy who discovers he has a gift for bodybuilding and becomes a professional bodybuilder. In the show, Jay moves to LA to pursue his dream of becoming the best in the world, but quickly learns that being famous and rich isn't everything it's cracked up to be. In each episode, Jay must balance his career and personal lives while also dealing with his family, friends and occasionally crazy fans.
