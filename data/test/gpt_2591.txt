Studio X  (formerly known as Bad Boy Recording Studio) is a recording studio located in the West Village of Manhattan, New York City. It is owned and operated by American music producer and songwriter Dr. Luke.  Studio X was originally opened in 2004 by Dr. Luke as a small recording studio in the West Village of Manhattan. It quickly gained a reputation for being one of the most respected and successful recording studios in the music industry, working with a wide variety of artists including Katy Perry, Bruno Mars, Britney Spears, and Justin Bieber. In February 2018, it was announced that Dr. Luke had sold Studio X to investment group WME | IMG for an undisclosed sum.  The current owner and operator of Studio X is American music producer and songwriter Dr. Luke.
