Johnathan Hildred Wall Jr. (born September 6, 1988) is an American professional basketball player for the Washington Wizards of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Previously, Wall played for the Kentucky Wildcats and was drafted with the first overall pick in the 2010 NBA draft.  Wall has won three consecutive Eastern Conference Player of the Month awards, and was named to the 2013–14 All-NBA First Team. He was also named to the 2014–15 All-NBA Second Team. In February 2018, Wall was traded to the Washington Wizards in a deal that sent Bradley Beal to the Orlando Magic.
