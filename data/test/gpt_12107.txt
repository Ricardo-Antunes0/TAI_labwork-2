KOLR, virtual and VHF digital channel 10, is an NBC-affiliated television station licensed to Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States and serving the Tulsa television market. The station is owned by the Hearst Television subsidiary of Hearst Corporation. KOLR's studios are located on North Lake Tulsa Boulevard in northeast Tulsa, and its transmitter is located in southeast Tulsa.  KOLR signed on the air on September 12, 1953 as KTVT-TV, the first television station in Tulsa. The station was founded by the Cox Communications Company, which also operated KVOO-TV (channel 4) in Albuquerque, New Mexico and KTXA-TV (channel 5) in Austin, Texas. Cox sold KTVT to the Hearst Corporation in 1987. In 1995, KOLR became an NBC affiliate.
