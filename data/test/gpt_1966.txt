Orazio Attanasio (born 31 October 1959, in Catania) is an Italian writer, journalist and literary critic.  Attanasio wrote his first novel, Il segreto di Gionata, when he was just seventeen years old. He has since written several other novels, including La donna nell'ombrello (1994), L'amante invisibile (1996), and Lo scandalo della signora X (2005), as well as a collection of short stories, L'altra faccia della Luna (1994). His work has been translated into twenty-four languages.   Attanasio has also written extensively on literature and journalism, and has been a regular critic for the Italian newspaper La Repubblica. In 2004 he was awarded the Strega Prize.
