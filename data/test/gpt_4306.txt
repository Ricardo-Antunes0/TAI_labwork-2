The Darbandikhan Dam  is a multi-purpose dam located in the Darbandikhan Valley, Tajikistan. It was built between 1984 and 1988 by the Soviet Union as part of the Aral Sea project. The purpose of the dam is to produce hydroelectric power and irrigation. The dam has a total height of 164 m, a length of 1,294 m and a width of 48 m. The reservoir it creates, known as the Aral Sea reservoir, has a capacity of 10,000 m3.
