Park City Center is a shopping mall located in Park City, Utah. Opened in 1976, it is the largest shopping mall in Utah, and the 18th largest mall in the United States. With 1,070,000 square feet (102,000 m2) of retail space, it is also the largest mall in Summit County.  The mall was built on the site of a former ski resort and is anchored by Saks Fifth Avenue, Nordstrom, Macy's, Sears, JC Penney and Dick's Sporting Goods. The mall features a variety of restaurants and retailers including a department store that has been open continuously since 1976.
