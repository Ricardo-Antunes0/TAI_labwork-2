David Michael Pascoe (born March 2, 1967) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player who played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Vancouver Canucks, Detroit Red Wings, and Calgary Flames. Pascoe was drafted by the Canucks in the second round, 38th overall, of the 1987 NHL Entry Draft.  Pascoe was born in Toronto, Ontario. He played junior hockey for the Oshawa Generals of the Ontario Hockey League (OHL) before being drafted by the Vancouver Canucks. Pascoe made his NHL debut with the Canucks in 1991. He played for Vancouver until 1999, when he was traded to the Detroit Red Wings. Pascoe spent four seasons with Detroit before being traded to Calgary in 2003. Pascoe played six seasons with Calgary before retiring in 2007.
