The glass blue-eye is a species of cichlid endemic to Lake Tanganyika. It is a medium-sized fish that can reach a length of up to SL. It is most commonly found in areas with rocky substrates and subdued water clarity. This fish feeds on small crustaceans, insects, and other invertebrates.
