Sekkemo Church  is a parish church in the municipality of Odda in Hordaland county, Norway. It is located in the village of Sekkemo, about east of the town of Odda. The church is part of the Sekkemo parish in the Sunnhordland deanery in the Diocese of Bjørgvin. The white, wooden church was built in a long, narrow style in 1883 by the architect Gustav Lindberg. The church has been renovated several times over the years, most recently in 2002-2003. Services are held on Sundays at 10:00 and 17:00, and also on special occasions such as Christmas and Easter.
