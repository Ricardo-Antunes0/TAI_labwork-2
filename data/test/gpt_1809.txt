Jean-Pierre Maransin (20 March 1770 in Lourdes – 1 February 1855 in Paris) was a French author, playwright, and journalist.  Jean-Pierre Maransin was born on 20 March 1770 in Lourdes. After studying at the lycée of Toulouse, he went to Paris in 1792 and began his career as a journalist. He was the author of many comedies, including "Le mariage de Figaro" (1809), "Le mariage de Monsieur Jourdain" (1812), and "Le conseiller d'État" (1815). He also wrote dramas, including "Le père Goriot" (1835) and "Thésée" (1848).
