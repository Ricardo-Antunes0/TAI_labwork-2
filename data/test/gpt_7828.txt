Jennie Thornley Clarke (c. 1866 - ?) was an English artist and author who specialized in watercolour and pastel. She was a member of the Royal Society of Painter-Stainers and the Royal Watercolour Society.  Clarke was born in London in 1866, and studied at the Slade School of Art. In 1893 she became a member of the Royal Society of Painter-Stainers, and in 1906 she became a member of the Royal Watercolour Society. She exhibited her work at the Royal Academy, the Royal Institute of Painters in Watercolours, and the Paris Salon. Clarke died in London in 1934.
