Timur Gareyev (sometimes spelled Gareev; born March 2, 1988) is a Russian professional football player. He last played for FC Krylia Sovetov Samara in the Russian Premier League.  Gareyev started his career in FC Krylia Sovetov Samara's youth system, and made his senior debut in the 2002 Russian Premier League season. In 2009, Gareyev transferred to FC Terek Grozny. He helped Terek win the 2009–10 Russian Cup and the 2010 Russian Super Cup. The following season, Gareyev re-joined Krylia Sovetov Samara.  In July 2014, he moved to FC Rostov on a two-year contract. Rostov finished third in the Russian Premier League, and consequently qualified for the 2015–16 UEFA Europa League. In May 2017, Gareyev signed with FC Krylia Sovetov Samara on a two-year contract.
