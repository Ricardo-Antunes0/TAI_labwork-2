Sidiki Kaba (born 21 August 1950 in Togo) is a French-born Togolese journalist and author. He is the editor-in-chief of the Togolese newspaper L'Observateur.  Kaba was born in 1950 in Togo. He studied at the University of Paris, where he obtained a degree in law. He then worked as a lawyer before becoming a journalist. He has been the editor-in-chief of the Togolese newspaper L'Observateur since 1993.  Kaba has written numerous books, including La République togolaise (1995), Un an après l'indépendance (1998), L'ombre du pouvoir (2000), Les enjeux économiques du Togo (2002), and La guerre civile au Togo (2005).
