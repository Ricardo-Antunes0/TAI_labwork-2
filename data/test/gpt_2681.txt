Only Love, L is the fifth studio album by French singer-songwriter Mylène Farmer. It was released on 17 October 2007 by Warner Music Group. Recorded in Paris and Los Angeles, the album is a departure from the singer's earlier work, incorporating funk and soul influences into her pop sound.  Only Love, L debuted at number one in France, and has been certified platinum by the Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique (SNEP). The album has also charted in Belgium, Denmark, Finland, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Norway, Sweden and Switzerland.  Only Love, L includes the singles "Si la vie est cadeau" (a collaboration with English singer Sting), "Un homme et une femme" (a duet with Richard Marx), "Aimer" and "Je t'aime".
