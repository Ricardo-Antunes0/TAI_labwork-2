Widerøes Flyveselskap AS, trading as Widerøe, is a Norwegian airline with its head office in Oslo. The airline was founded on 10 July 1924 as A/S Widerøe, a seaplane ferry company. It is the oldest extant airline in the world. The airline operates scheduled passenger services to more than fifty destinations in Europe, North America, Asia and the Caribbean.     Widerøe is a member of the Oneworld alliance, and operates a fleet of more than sixty aircraft. The airline has been ranked as the ninth best European airline by Skytrax for six consecutive years.     In 2018, Widerøe was voted Norway's most popular company.
