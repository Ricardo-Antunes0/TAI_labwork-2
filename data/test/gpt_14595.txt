The Iowa–Iowa State rivalry is an American college football rivalry between the Iowa Hawkeyes and Iowa State Cyclones. The rivalry is the fourth-largest in college football.  The Iowa–Iowa State rivalry was first played on November 5, 1892. The Cyclones lead the all-time series 47–37–2. The rivalry has been played annually since 1903, with the exception of 1917 due to World War I and 1943 due to World War II. The two schools have met for a total of 116 games, with Iowa winning 56 games and Iowa State winning 54 games.  The Cyclones lead the all-time series 47–37–2. Iowa won their first game against Iowa State 29–6 in 1892. Iowa State won their first game against Iowa 29–6 in 1892.
