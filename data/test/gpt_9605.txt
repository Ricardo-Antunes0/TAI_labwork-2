Tsotne Bakuria (born December 22, 1971 in Gaborone, Botswana) is a retired middle-distance runner from Botswana. He represented his country at the 1996 and 2000 Summer Olympics.  Bakuria finished fourth in the 1500 metres at the 1995 World Championships, but was disqualified for a false start. He competed collegiately for the University of Utah.
