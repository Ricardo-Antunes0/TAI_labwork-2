Contagious is the third full-length studio album by Moroccan singer Sitti. It was released on 9 February 2019 by Sony Music France.  The album is a follow-up to her 2016 debut album, L'Aube, and was produced by the French producer Christophe "Coco" Muller. It contains a mix of Arabic and French lyrics, as well as collaborations with artists such as Nabil Ayouch and Cheb Mami. Upon its release, Contagious received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Sitti's vocals. The album has sold over 15,000 copies in France according to Nielsen SoundScan.
