Montague Marsden Glass (born July 23, 1877 in Montague, Massachusetts) was an American glass manufacturer.   He founded the Montague Glass Company in 1907 with his brother-in-law, and became one of the largest producers of leaded glass in the United States. In 1937, he merged his company with the Pilkington Glass Company to form Pilkington-Montague Glass Corporation. He served as chairman of the board of directors from 1938 until his death in 1946.
