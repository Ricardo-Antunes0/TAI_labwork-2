"Lift Yourself" is a song by American hip hop recording artist Kanye West. It was written by Kanye West, Noah Goldstein, and Max Martin and produced by the latter two. The song was released as the lead single from West's sixth solo studio album, "The Life of Pablo" (2016).  "Lift Yourself" is a soulful and upbeat song that features a prominent sample of the Ray Charles song "I Got A Woman". The song has been compared to West's earlier work with The Black Eyed Peas, particularly their album 2010's "The E.N.D.".  The song was originally meant for Rihanna, but she declined to feature on the track. After the release of West's fifth album, "The Life of Pablo", "Lift Yourself" charted on several Billboard charts, including the Hot 100 and the UK Singles Chart. The song has been certified triple platinum in the United States, and double platinum in the United Kingdom.
