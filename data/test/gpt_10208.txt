Yuelu District  is one of six districts of the prefecture-level city of Chongqing, China. The district has an area of 9,697 square kilometers and a population of 1,836,000 as of 2010. It is located in the south of the municipality.  The district administers 4 towns and 1 township. These are:   Towns:   Yuelu  Dajie  Jinzhai  Xinzhou
