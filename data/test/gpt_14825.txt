Penelope is a bird genus in the family Strigidae. It contains the following four species:  - Penelope colombiana: The Penelope's Falcon, a large falcon found in much of South America.  - Penelope jacaranda: The Jacaranda Penelope, a brightly coloured parrot found in Brazil, Paraguay and Argentina.  - Penelope troyana: The Troia's Penelope, a small forest bird found in parts of Central America and North America.  All four species are considered to be threatened or endangered.
