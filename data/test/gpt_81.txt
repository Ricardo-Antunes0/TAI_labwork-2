The 6th Magritte Awards ceremony, presented by Magritte Awards, was held on December 10, 2016 at the Théâtre des Champs-Élysées in Paris, France. The ceremony honored the best in French and Belgian cinema of 2016.   The nominees were announced on November 27, 2016. The winners were announced on December 10, 2016.   The nominees were:  Best Film: "The Lobster"  Best Director: Yann Demange  Best Actor: François Cluzet  Best Actress: Isabelle Huppert  Best Screenplay: Yann Demange and David Farrands  Best Cinematography: Philippe Le Guay  Best Editing: Bernard Kouchner
