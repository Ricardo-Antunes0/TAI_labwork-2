The Hohnekamm or Hohne Kamm is a mountain in the Swabian Jura, southern Germany. It is part of the Albuchalpen range.  The Hohnekamm rises to a height of 1,877 metres above sea level and is located in the municipality of Bad Urach. The mountain is part of a nature reserve and is accessible by hiking trails. The summit can be reached by ascending the Klettersteig Hohnekamm, which starts at the Lauterbrunnen Valley.
