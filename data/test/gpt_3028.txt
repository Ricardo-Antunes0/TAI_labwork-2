The Guanyinyan Dam is a gravity dam on the Guanyinyan River in Hebei Province, China. It is the largest concrete gravity dam in the world. The dam was built between 1958 and 1964 and has a height of 305 metres (1,000 feet). It is used to generate hydroelectric power.
