Pennsylvania Route 532 (PA 532) is a state highway in the U.S. state of Pennsylvania. The route runs from PA Route 9 in the community of Belmont to PA Route 611 in the community of Uniontown.  PA 532 was designated in the 1930s as a renumbering of PA Route 12, which had been established in 1922. The route originally ran from PA Route 9 in Belmont southwest to PA Route 611 in Uniontown. In the early 1990s, PA 532 was extended southeast to its current terminus at PA Route 9.
