Houghton College is a private Christian liberal arts college located in Houghton, New York, United States. The college offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in more than 40 majors.  Houghton College was founded in 1887 by the Methodist Episcopal Church as a women's college. It became coeducational in 1969 and began admitting men in 1976. The institution has an enrollment of 1,000 students.
