James Winfield Lyons (August 26, 1878 – October 22, 1937) was an American football player and coach. He served as the head football coach at the University of Michigan from 1903 to 1905, compiling a record of 3–4. Lyons was also the head basketball coach at Michigan from 1901 to 1903 and 1905 to 1906, tallying a mark of 66–43.
