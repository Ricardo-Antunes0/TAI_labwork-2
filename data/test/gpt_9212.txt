Era mio Fratello (Translation: He was my brother) is a 2006 Italian drama film directed by Gianluca Savino. It was screened in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2006 Cannes Film Festival.  The film tells the story of a brother and sister who are struggling to cope with the death of their mother. The sister, played by Valentina Ricci, is an artist who is struggling to find her place in the world. The brother, played by Toni Servillo, is a successful lawyer who is trying to find his way after their mother's death.
