Corythomantis greeningi, occasionally called Greening's frog, is a species of frog in the family Centrolenidae. The specific name, greeningi, is in honor of British herpetologist Roy Greening.  It is endemic to the eastern slopes of the Andes in Ecuador and Peru. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forests and subtropical or tropical high-altitude shrubland.
