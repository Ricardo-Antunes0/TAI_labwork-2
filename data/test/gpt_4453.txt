Tyla Flexman (born July 16, 1986 in Los Angeles, California) is an American actress and model.  Flexman was born in Los Angeles, California on July 16, 1986. She is of Russian and Jewish descent. Flexman began her modeling career at the age of 14, when she participated in an L.A. modeling contest. She later appeared in campaigns for Abercrombie & Fitch, Ralph Lauren, and Marc Jacobs.  In 2006, Flexman starred as Vicky in the horror film "The Faculty". She also appeared in the films "The Air I Breathe" (2008), "The Back-Up Plan" (2010), and "A Haunted House 2" (2011). In 2009, Flexman starred in the short film "The Girlfriend Experience". In 2011, she starred in the comedy film "We're the Millers".  In 2012, Flexman starred as Sasha on the CW series "Arrow". She reprised her role for the show's fourth season in 2013. In October 2013, it was announced that Flexman had been cast in a lead role on the ABC Family series "Young & Hungry". The series premiered on January 8, 2014. In November 2014, it was announced that Flexman had been cast in a lead role on the NBC series "The New Normal". The series premiered on September 25, 2014.
