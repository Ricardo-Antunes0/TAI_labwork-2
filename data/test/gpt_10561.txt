{{Infobox character | name = Luke Skywalker | series = Star Wars | gender = Male | image = Luke Skywalker.jpg | creator = George Lucas | birth_date = April 17, A.D. 40 | death_date = December 25, A.D. 33 }}  Luke Skywalker is a fictional character in the Star Wars franchise. He is portrayed by actor Mark Hamill in the original trilogy, and by actor Hayden Christensen in the prequel trilogy. Introduced in the 1977 film Star Wars, Luke is a farm boy who joins the Rebel Alliance and becomes a Jedi Knight to oppose the Empire's tyranny. In Return of the Jedi, he defeats Darth Vader and helps destroy the Death Star. He is also involved in the rebellion against the Empire during the original trilogy, and the New Republic during the prequel trilogy.
