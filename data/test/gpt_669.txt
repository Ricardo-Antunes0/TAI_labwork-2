Game of Outlaws (Thai: เกมล่าทรชน, RTGS: Kem Lai Toon) is a Thai-American action-adventure television series that premiered on RTGS on November 25, 2017. The series is produced by Fox 21 and is based on the Outlaws motorcycle club from the movie of the same name.  The series follows the Outlaws as they fight against their rivals, the Bandits, for control of the town of Sundown. The show also features appearances by real life motorcycle clubs such as the Devil's Highway Riders and the Iron Riders.  Premiere On November 25, 2017, Game of Outlaws premiered on RTGS.
