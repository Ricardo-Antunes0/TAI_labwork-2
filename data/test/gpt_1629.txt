John Patton (Colonel) was born 1745 in what is now Warren County, Kentucky. Patton moved with his family to the Northwest Territory in 1776 and fought in the American Revolution. After the war, Patton settled in what is now Illinois and became a prominent figure in politics and business. He served as a governor of Illinois and as United States Senator from Illinois.
