Abdul Meniem Al-Toras is the former Commander of the Egyptian Navy.  Abdul Meniem Al-Toras was born on 5 January 1958 in Alexandria, Egypt. He attended the Egyptian Military Academy, and graduated in 1976. He then attended the Naval War College in Newport, Rhode Island, United States, and graduated in 1981.  Al-Toras served as the commander of the Egyptian Navy from 2000 to 2002. He has also served as the deputy chief of general staff of the Egyptian Army and as an adviser to the Minister of Defense.
