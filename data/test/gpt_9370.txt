Birger Sevaldson (born 1953, Bærum, Norway) is a Norwegian artist who lives and works in Oslo.  Birger Sevaldson was born in 1953 in Bærum, Norway. Sevaldson completed his studies at the Academy of Fine Arts, Oslo (1975-1979) and at the Royal College of Art, London (1978-1979). He has exhibited extensively both nationally and internationally, including solo exhibitions at the Secession, Vienna (1986), Kunsthalle Hamburg (1991), Museum Folkwang Essen (1996), Centre Georges Pompidou, Paris (2007), and MoMA PS1, New York (2009). Sevaldson was awarded the Norwegian National Prize for Painting in 2002.
