Arvind Singhal (born 1962) is an Indian-born academic and the Dean of the Faculty of Management Studies at the Warwick Business School, University of Warwick. He is also a senior research fellow at the Oxford Martin School.  Born in 1962 in India, Arvind Singhal received his BBA from the University of Mumbai in 1985, and his MA and PhD degrees in Business Administration from the University of California, Berkeley, in 1988 and 1991 respectively. He has been a professor at Columbia Business School (1995-1998), the University of Arizona (1998-2000), and the Warwick Business School (since 2000).  Arvind Singhal's research interests lie at the intersection of marketing, management, and organizational behavior. His published work has focused on topics such as innovation, product development, customer segmentation, organizational design, and corporate social responsibility. In 2016, he was elected a fellow of the Academy of Management.
