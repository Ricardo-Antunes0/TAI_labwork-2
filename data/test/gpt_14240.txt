Jalaram Bapa  popularly known as Bapa Maharaj Ji is a saintly figure in the Sikh religion. He was born on November 5, 1926 in Amritsar, Punjab, India. He is best known for his healing and spiritual powers which he has demonstrated throughout his life.  Jalaram Bapa was born into a family of religious scholars. At an early age, he showed signs of spiritual talent and began to receive guidance from his parents and other religious leaders. In 1944, at the age of seventeen, he arrived in Amritsar to study under the tutelage of his father, a well-known scholar and teacher.  Bapa Maharaj Ji quickly became renowned for his spiritual gifts. He was able to heal the sick and perform other miracles. By the early 1950s, he had attracted a large following of followers who traveled from all over India to hear him preach and learn from him.  Bapa Maharaj Ji continued to demonstrate his spiritual powers throughout his life. In 1961, he was able to stop a train from crashing by using his powers of concentration. In 1969, he was able to walk on water as part of a spiritual ceremony in Rishikesh.  Bapa Maharaj Ji passed away on June 15, 1984 at the age of 73 after a long battle with illness.
