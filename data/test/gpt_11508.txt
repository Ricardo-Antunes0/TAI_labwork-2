Black Emperor.  13 & God is a collaboration between two production teams, 13th Floor and Godspeed You! Black Emperor. The album was produced by 13th Floor and mixed by Godspeed You! Black Emperor. The music is an expansive and ambitious work that incorporates elements of post-rock, electronica and black metal while exploring themes of religion, spirituality and the afterlife.  The album was released on October 5, 2013 through Constellation Records. Critical response to the album has been positive, with many reviewers praising its ambitious soundscape and unique style.
