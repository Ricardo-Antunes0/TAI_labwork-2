Copperfields Mine, originally known as Temagami Mine, is a copper and gold mine located in Temagami, Ontario, Canada.   The mine has produced copper and gold since 1881. Copperfields was the first commercial copper mine in North America, and it is one of the world's largest open-pit copper mines. The mine has an annual production capacity of 1.1 million tonnes of copper and 49,000 ounces of gold. The mine employs around 800 people. Copperfields is owned by Barrick Gold Corporation.
