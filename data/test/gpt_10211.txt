David Beazley is an American software engineer. He is best known for his work on the Java language, where he was a principal architect of the JVM.  Beazley was born in New York City in 1966. He received a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of California, Santa Cruz in 1988 and a M.S. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University in 1992.  He worked as a software engineer at Sun Microsystems from 1992 to 1997, where he worked on the Java programming language. He then worked at IBM from 1997 to 2003, where he was a principal architect of the IBM JVM and the IBM Rational software development platforms. He has since been a software engineer at Google Inc.
