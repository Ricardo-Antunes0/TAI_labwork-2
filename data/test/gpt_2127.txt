Eric Samuel Heffer (12 January 192227 May 1991) was an English cricketer. He was a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-pace bowler.  Heffer made his first-class debut for Hampshire in 1946 against Oxford University. Heffer played for Hampshire until 1956, making a total of 238 appearances for the county. Heffer also played for the Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC) in 50 first-class matches between 1948 and 1956. Heffer was awarded his county cap in 1948.  Heffer made his List A debut for Hampshire in the 1946 Minor Counties Championship against Wiltshire. Heffer played Minor counties cricket for Hampshire from 1946 to 1955, making a total of 29 appearances. Heffer also played for the MCC in 5 List A matches between 1948 and 1951. In his 5 List A matches, Heffer scored 58 runs at an average of 10.50, with a high score of 25 not out. Heffer died in 1991 at the age of 78.
