Chiloglottis platyptera, commonly known as the winged kelpfish, is a species of fish in the family Chilodontidae. It is endemic to the Hawaiian Islands, where it is found in the Pacific Ocean near reefs at depths of up to 30 m. The winged kelpfish is a brightly colored fish with a long, slender body and a long, thin dorsal fin. Its scales are arranged in irregular rows and its dorsal fin has 12 to 14 rays.
