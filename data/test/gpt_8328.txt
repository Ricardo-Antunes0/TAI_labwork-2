"Fortune Faded" is a song by the American rock band Linkin Park. It was released as the third and final single from their sixth studio album, "One More Light".  The song was written by Linkin Park members Mike Shinoda and Brad Delson, along with additional writing from Rob Bourdon. The song is about Shinoda's experiences with addiction and his recovery.  "Fortune Faded" was well received by music critics, who praised its heavy guitars and lyrical content. The song debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart, behind the Ed Sheeran single "Shape of You". It has since peaked at number one. The song also reached the top 10 in Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, New Zealand, Portugal, Spain and Sweden.  Linkin Park performed "Fortune Faded" at the 2017 American Music Awards and the 2017 MTV Video Music Awards.
