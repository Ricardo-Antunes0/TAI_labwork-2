Market Forces is a science fiction thriller novel by American author Robert Charles Wilson. It was first published in 1987.  The novel tells the story of a future society in which the market has replaced the government as the main source of authority. The story follows the efforts of a group of rebels to restore democracy to the society.  The novel has been praised for its innovative ideas and for its well-executed plot. It has also been criticised for its lack of realism, its focus on economic rather than political themes, and its datedness.
