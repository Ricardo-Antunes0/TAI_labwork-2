Gertrude Carter, who was also known as Georgia Carter, was an American blues singer, pianist, and songwriter.  Carter was born in Memphis, Tennessee, on July 2, 1900. She began performing in clubs in her early twenties and made her recording debut in 1930 with the Vocalion label. She became a popular blues performer, touring widely and recording dozens of songs over the course of her career. Carter died on October 30, 1978.
