Sonia Livingstone  is a professor of political science at the University of Toronto. She specializes in international relations and has written extensively on the politics of Africa.  Born in London, England, Sonia Livingstone received her undergraduate degree from the University of Cambridge and her graduate degree from Columbia University. She taught at the University of Toronto from 1981 to 2002, when she was appointed a professor. Livingstone has written extensively on the politics of Africa, with a focus on the continent's relations with Europe and the United States. Her books include "The Politics of African Development" (1986), "Africa Confronts World Politics" (1994), and "The Quest for Democracy in Africa" (2007).
