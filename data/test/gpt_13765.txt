Harold "Hal" Glen Borland (May 14, 1900 – September 12, 1973) was an American football player and coach. He served as the head football coach at the University of Michigan from 1937 to 1939, compiling a record of 5–4. Borland played football for the Michigan Wolverines from 1922 to 1925 and was captain of the 1924 team.
