KLIN (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Lubbock, Texas, it serves the Lubbock area. The station is currently owned by Cumulus Media.  KLIN first went on the air in 1969, and was originally known as KGNC. It became KLIN in 1978 when the station was purchased by Larry and Barbara Stowell. The Stowell family sold the station to Cumulus Media in 2009.
