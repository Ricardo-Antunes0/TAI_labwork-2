Hope is a 1995 spy novel by Deighton, his twentieth novel. It is the sequel to The IPCRESS File and is set in the fictional British intelligence service MI6.  The novel tells the story of John Preston, a senior MI6 officer who is forced to deal with the aftermath of a failed operation which has left him discredited and out of favour. Preston is also in love with his boss's daughter, Tessa.  The book was adapted for television by the BBC in 2002.
