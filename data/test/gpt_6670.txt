Marion County Airport  is a county-owned public-use airport located two nautical miles (3.7 km) southwest of the central business district of Dunnellon, a city in Marion County, Florida, United States. The airport covers an area of 1,760 acres (730 ha) at an elevation of 5 feet (1.5 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 9/27 with an asphalt surface measuring 4,000 by 75 feet (1,219 x 23 m).
