WWE Slam City is an American animated television series created by Eric Schaeffer for the WWE Network. The series is a reimagining of the classic 1980s wrestling show Super Star Wrestling and is set in Slam City, a fictional wrestling city. The show is produced by Titmouse, Inc. and directed by Dave Willis.  The show is set in Slam City, a fictional wrestling city where the most popular show in town is Super Star Wrestling. When the city's most popular wrestler, Stone Cold Steve Austin, retires, his protégé Triple H must take on the mantle of city champion. In addition to the main characters from Super Star Wrestling, Slam City features new characters, including Austin's son Stone Cold Steve Austin, Jr., and WWE Hall of Famer Mick Foley as referee.
