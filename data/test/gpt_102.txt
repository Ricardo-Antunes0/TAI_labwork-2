Fjære Church   is a parish church in the municipality of Fjære in Trøndelag county, Norway. It is located in the village of Fjære. The church is part of the Fjære parish in the Nord-Trøndelag deanery in the Diocese of Nidaros. The white, wooden church was built in a long, narrow style in 1868 by the architect Christian Heinrich Grosch. It has a long nave and a small chancel. The altarpiece is from 1892 and it features a sculpture of "The Madonna and Child". The church seats about 300 people.
