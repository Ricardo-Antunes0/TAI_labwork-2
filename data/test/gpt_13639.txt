Darren A Douglass Reed (born October 16, 1980) is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Atlanta Hawks of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Reed played collegiately for the University of Michigan and was a two-time consensus first-team All-American. He was drafted second overall in the 2001 NBA draft by the Portland Trail Blazers. Reed has also played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Memphis Grizzlies, and Phoenix Suns.  Reed is a versatile player who can play both point guard and shooting guard.
