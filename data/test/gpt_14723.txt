"Too Many Walls" is a song by American recording artist and songwriter Ariana Grande from her second studio album, Dangerous Woman. The song was written by Grande, Max Martin, and Shellback and produced by the latter two. "Too Many Walls" is a pop ballad with an electronica sound that speaks to the isolation that Grande feels from society.  The song was released as the album's lead single on May 20, 2016, through Republic Records. The accompanying music video was directed by Nabil Elderkin and premiered on Grande's official YouTube channel on May 25. It features Grande as a woman confined to an isolated room, where she contemplates her relationship with someone who is distant from her. The song has been met with acclaim from critics, who praised its emotional content and production. "Too Many Walls" became Grande's first number-one single in the United Kingdom and Ireland, as well as Australia and New Zealand. The song has been certified quadruple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for sales of 4 million copies in the United States.
