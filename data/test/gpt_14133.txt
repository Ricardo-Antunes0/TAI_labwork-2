The Bled is a self-titled EP by American indie rock band The Bled. It was released on October 21, 2012 through Wichita Recordings.  The Bled is an American indie rock band from Wichita, Kansas. The band consists of vocalist and guitarist Alec O'Hanley, drummer Adam Koeppen, and bassist Dan McMahan. Formed in 2009, the band released its debut EP, "The Bled", in 2012.
