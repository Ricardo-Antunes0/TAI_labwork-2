Fiannamail ua Dúnchado was a king of Munster in the late 6th century.  Fiannamail ua Dúnchado was the son of Dúnchad mac Cerbaill and grandson of Fergus mac Roich. He succeeded his father as king of Munster in 584, and ruled until his death in 589. He is chiefly remembered for his efforts to bring peace to Munster after a period of civil war.
