WIMN-CD, virtual channel 20 and UHF digital channel 23, is an educational television station licensed to Monroe, Louisiana, United States. The station is owned by the Louisiana State University System, and operated as a satellite station of LSU's flagship station, WLAF-TV (channel 59) in Baton Rouge.  WIMN-CD's programming is devoted to public affairs programming and educational programming from LSU and other universities in the LSU System. The station also airs occasional locally produced programs.   The station's transmitter is located near Wilkinson in West Baton Rouge Parish.
