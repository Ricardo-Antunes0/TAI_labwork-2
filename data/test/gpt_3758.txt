Viña Seña is a small, family-owned winery located in the Stags Leap District of the Napa Valley. The winery was founded by a group of friends who met while working at various vineyards in the area. After years of working together and learning from each other, they decided to form their own winery. Seña wines are made from 100% Sonoma County Cabernet Sauvignon grapes.  The winery has a small tasting room and produces around 2,000 cases of wine per year. Viña Seña wines are available for purchase online and in select stores throughout California.
