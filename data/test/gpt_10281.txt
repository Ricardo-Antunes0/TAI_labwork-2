"Manhattan Vigil" is the fifth episode of the first season of the American television series "Breaking Bad".  The episode was written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Michelle MacLaren. It aired on AMC in the United States on July 15, 2008.  After the death of his partner, Hank Schrader (Dean Norris) sets out to find the man responsible: Tuco Salamanca (Ray Romano). Meanwhile, Walt Whitman (Bryan Cranston) and Skyler White (Anna Gunn) are trying to make ends meet by cooking meth. Matters become further complicated when Hank gets a call from his old partner, who tells him that he knows where Tuco is.   The episode was critically acclaimed, with many reviewers praising Romano's performance.
