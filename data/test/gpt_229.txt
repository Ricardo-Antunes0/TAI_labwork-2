Ken Webster (born July 29, 1957 in Los Angeles, California) is an American film and television director.  Webster directed episodes of the TV series "The West Wing" (2000), "Boston Legal" (2004), and "Damages" (2007-2011). He also directed the 2009 feature film "The Banker". Webster's other films include the Sundance Film Festival award-winning drama "The Kids Are All Right" (2010), the political thriller "The East" (2013), and the family comedy "Big Hero 6" (2014). Webster is also a frequent guest director on TV shows such as "Law & Order", "NCIS", and "NCIS: Los Angeles".
