The Dinkey Creek Bridge, also known as the Coal Creek Bridge, is a historic bridge located near Boone, Boone County, West Virginia. It was built in 1908, and is a single-span steel arch bridge with a concrete substructure. It has a roadway width of 36 feet and a total length of 164 feet. The bridge is crossed by Coal Creek Road (TR 460).  The bridge was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
