Todd James (born 1969) is a contemporary artist, musician and composer. He is known for his dark, haunting and often surreal paintings and sculptures, which explore the boundaries between reality and fiction.  James was born in 1969 in Sydney, Australia. He studied Fine Arts at the University of Technology, Sydney, and then moved to New York City in 1991 to study at the School of Visual Arts. In 1994 he moved to Berlin, Germany, where he continued his studies at the Kunstakademie Berlin.  In 2001 James returned to Sydney and resumed painting and sculpting. His work has been exhibited in Australia, Europe, North America and Asia. In 2006 he was awarded the Australian Cultural Award for Lifetime Achievement.
