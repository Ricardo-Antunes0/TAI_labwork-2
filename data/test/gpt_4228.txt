Bill Koeb is an American  painter, sculptor and installation artist. He specializes in painting, drawing and printmaking, with a focus on depictions of natural and urban landscapes.  Koeb was born in 1953 in Trenton, New Jersey. Koeb has exhibited his work throughout the United States, Europe and Asia. His paintings are in the collections of the Museum of Modern Art, the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Philadelphia Museum of Art, and the National Gallery of Victoria in Melbourne. He is also the recipient of a Guggenheim Fellowship (1989) and a Pollock-Krasner Foundation Grant (1998).
