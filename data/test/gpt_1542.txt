Tarzan the Fearless is a 12 chapter book written by Edgar Rice Burroughs and published in 1912. It tells the story of Tarzan's first encounter with the ape-man, Kerchak, and the subsequent adventures of Tarzan, Jane, and their infant son, Tarzan Jr.  Tarzan is a man of the jungle who was raised by apes. He is strong, fast and skilled in the use of weapons and primitive tools. One day he met a man-ape named Kerchak who was much larger and stronger than Tarzan. Kerchak wanted to capture and eat Tarzan. Tarzan fought back and eventually won.
