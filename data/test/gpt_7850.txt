Silviu Craciunas (in Romanian: Crăciunaș; 1914–1998) was a Romanian poet and literary critic.  Born in 1914 in Iași, Romania, Craciunas graduated from the University of Iași in 1936. He worked as a teacher and journalist before becoming a full-time poet in the 1950s. He was a founding member of the Romanian Writers' Union and the Romanian Academy. He won the 1977 National Prize for Literature.
