Atlas is a survival MMO video game developed by Techland and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. It was initially announced at E3 2019 as part of the DC Universe Online expansion, and is currently in development by the Warsaw studio of Techland.  Atlas is set in a post-apocalyptic world inhabited by mutated creatures and players must scavenge for food and resources, build shelters, and defend themselves from hostile mutants in order to survive. The game is based on the "Atlas" comic book series written by David Lapham and illustrated by Piotr Kowalski.
