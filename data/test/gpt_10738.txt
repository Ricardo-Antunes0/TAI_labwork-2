David Norbrook (born 1 June 1950) was an English jurist who served as a Justice of the Court of Appeal from 1998 to 2003. He was also a Professor of Law at the University of Cambridge from 1990 to 1998.  Norbrook was born in 1950 in London, England. He was educated at Westminster School and then read law at Oxford University. After being called to the bar in 1973, he worked at the law firm of Herbert Smith and then became a lecturer at Cambridge University. He was appointed a Justice of the Court of Appeal on 10 January 1998, and retired on 31 August 2003.
