The Renault Twizy is a two-seat electric vehicle manufactured by the French automaker Renault. It was first shown to the public at the Paris Motor Show in September 2010 and went on sale in Europe in early 2011.   The Twizy is a compact two-seater, with a length of 4,540 mm and width of 1,850 mm, and a low weight of just 680 kg. It has a top speed of 75 km/h (47 mph), and can reach a range of 100 km (62 mi) on a single charge using the standard European CCS charging network.
