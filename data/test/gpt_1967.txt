The 2020 Rally Finland (also known as Rally Finland 2019) will be the ninth round of the World Rally Championship season. The rally will take place over 16 stages between 18 and 23 June 2020.  The rally is based in the Lapland region of Finland, and starts and finishes in Rovaniemi.
