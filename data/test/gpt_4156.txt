KAAM, known as "K-Double-A-M", is an AM radio station licensed to serve the community of Ithaca, New York. The station is owned by Townsquare Media and broadcasts a Christian radio format.  KAAM's broadcast license is held by Townsquare Media, LLC. The station's studios are located at the intersection of Cayuga and Seneca Streets in Ithaca, and the transmitter is located in town near Cornell University.  KAAM broadcasts on the frequency of 1100 kHz with a power of 5,000 watts.
