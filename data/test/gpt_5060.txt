Røros Church or Bergstadens Ziir  is a parish church in Røros municipality in Trøndelag county, Norway. It is located in the village of Bergstaden, about east of the town of Røros. The church is part of the Røros parish in the Indre Nordmøre deanery in the Diocese of Nidaros.  The present church was built in a Romanesque style in the 12th century. It was enlarged and rebuilt in a Gothic style in the 14th century. The present tower was built in 1653. The church was consecrated on 26 October 1654 by Bishop Peder Andersen Strand. The current rector is Ingvar Borge.  The church seats about 650 people. It is decorated with carved woodwork and paintings from the 17th century. There is also a baptismal font from the 12th century and a pulpit from 1706.
