CP3 is a disability sport classification specific to wheelchair basketball. Athletes in this classification compete using a wheelchair and must have a minimum muscle strength of 35 kg.  Wheelchair basketball is a sport for athletes with disabilities that uses a wheelchair. Athletes in this classification compete using a wheelchair and must have a minimum muscle strength of 35 kg. CP3 is the third most common disability sport classification in the world. There are currently over 1,000 athletes competing in CP3 worldwide.
