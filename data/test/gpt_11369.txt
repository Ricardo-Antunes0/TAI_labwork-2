Catalog is a "best of" album by The Cure, released in 1992. It consists of tracks from their earlier albums, remastered with new sound effects and music.  The Cure is an English rock band formed in London in 1976. The band's original line-up consisted of Robert Smith (vocals and guitar), Simon Gallup (bass), Roger O'Donnell (guitar) and Michael Dempsey (drums). Smith is the only remaining original member. The Cure has released thirteen studio albums, three compilation albums, thirty-six singles and twenty-one videos.
