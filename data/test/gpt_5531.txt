George Pusenkoff (; born 1953 in Krasnapolle), is a Russian-born artist who lives and works in Berlin, Germany.  Pusenkoff was born in 1953 in Krasnapolle, a small town in the Russian SFSR. He studied at the Moscow State University of Art and Design from 1972 to 1976. In 1978 he moved to Berlin, Germany. His work is held in major public collections including MoMA, the Tate Gallery, and the British Museum.  In 2006 Pusenkoff was awarded the prestigious Hans Ulrich Obrist Prize.
