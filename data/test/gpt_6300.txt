"See You Again" is a song by the American pop rock band the Beach Boys, released on September 16, 2018, as the lead single from their twenty-fifth studio album, "Chemtrails over America" (2018).  The song was written by Brian Wilson, Mike Love, and Al Jardine and produced by Mike Love. It is a return to the pop-rock sound of the Beach Boys of the 1960s and 1970s. The song was released as a digital download and served as the opening track to the band's live album "Live in London" (2019).  The song reached number one on the UK Singles Chart, making it the band's eighteenth number-one single in the United Kingdom. It also reached number one in Australia, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden, and Switzerland.  Reviewing the song for "Rolling Stone", Chris Richards described it as a "happy blast of mid-'60s revival pop." He also praised Wilson's vocal performance, writing that he sounded "incredibly youthful and inspired."   The music video for "See You Again" was directed by David Dobkin and premiered on October 2nd on Vevo. In it, Wilson and Jardine perform the song at various locations around Los Angeles.
