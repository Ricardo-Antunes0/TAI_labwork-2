Dingwall Designer Guitars is a manufacturer of handmade acoustic guitars in Dingwall, Ross-shire, Scotland.  Dingwall Designer Guitars was founded in 2006 by brothers Graeme and Ian Dingwall. The company produces handmade acoustic guitars using traditional methods and materials. The guitars are made to order and are each unique.  The Dingwall Designer Guitars line of guitars includes a range of instruments from classical and flamenco-style guitars to modern models inspired by rock and blues styles. All of the guitars are handcrafted using high-quality materials, including exotic woods such as koa and mahogany.  The Dingwall Designer Guitars line of acoustic guitars has received acclaim from musicians and music journalists around the world. In 2013, the company was awarded a bronze medal at the prestigious British Guild of Craftsmen Awards for its innovation and craftsmanship in acoustic guitar production.
