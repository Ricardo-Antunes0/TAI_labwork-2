Alien is the debut studio album by the British electronic music duo Beam. It was released on 25 January 2009 through Alien8 Recordings.  The album was preceded by the single "Leave Me Alone" on 10 December 2008. The album peaked at number 20 on the UK Albums Chart.  The album received mixed reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has an average score of 54, based on 14 reviews.  Ben Davis of "NME" awarded the album a 7 out of 10, and said "Beam are a beguiling new duo with a great deal to say, as well as some nifty tunes to play." Greg Kot of "Chicago Tribune" gave the album 3 out of 4 stars, and wrote that "Beam sound like they've been playing together for years." Robert Christgau of "The Village Voice" gave the album a two-star honorable mention, writing that "Beam's debut is a polite but chilly electronic-pop record with too much tinkering, not enough emoting."
