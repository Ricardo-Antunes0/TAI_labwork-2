84C MoPic (also known as 84 Charlie MoPic) is a 1984 American romantic comedy film directed by John Hughes, and written by Hughes and Erin Cressida Wilson. The film stars Emilio Estevez, Demi Moore, Judd Nelson, and Charlie Sheen.  The film tells the story of high school student Mike O'Brien (Sheen), who is applying to colleges and falls in love with older woman Jodie Foster (Moore). When Foster begins an affair with O'Brien's friend, Mike must choose between his love for Foster and his friendship with his friend.  The film received mixed reviews from critics but was a commercial success, grossing over $173 million at the worldwide box office. It was nominated for three Academy Awards: Best Original Screenplay, Best Actress in a Leading Role (Foster) and Best Supporting Actor (Nelson).
