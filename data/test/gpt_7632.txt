Gargoyle Mountain is a  mountain summit located in the Catskill Mountains of New York, in the United States. The mountain is located in the town of Hunter, and rises to an elevation of 2,583 feet (853 meters).   The mountain is composed of Precambrian gneiss and is the highest summit in the Catskill Mountains south of Mount Marcy. The mountain is part of the Taconic Mountains and drains into Cold Brook, which flows into the Hudson River north of Kingston.
