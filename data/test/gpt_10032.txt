The Royal Radar Establishment was a research and development establishment located in Chelmsford, Essex, England. Established on 1 October 1934 as the Royal Aircraft Establishment (RAE), it became the Royal Radar Establishment in 1946. It was merged with the University of London's School of Mines and Metallurgy in 1959 to form the University of London Institute of Science and Technology.
