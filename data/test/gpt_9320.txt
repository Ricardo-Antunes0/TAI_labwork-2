Maximiano Valdés Subercaseaux (23 September 1908 – 24 November 1992) was a Spanish lawyer, jurist and politician. He was the third president of Spain from 1969 to 1974.  He was born in Madrid to José Valdés Subercaseaux and Juana de la Cruz. He studied law at the Universidad Complutense de Madrid and then pursued a career in the judiciary. In 1951 he became a judge of the Audiencia Nacional, and from 1959 to 1969 he served as its president.  In 1969 he was elected president of Spain, replacing Francisco Franco. He retired in 1974, after only two years in office. He died in 1992 at the age of 84.
