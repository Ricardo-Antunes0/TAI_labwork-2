Filip Dewulf (born 15 March 1972) is a Belgian footballer who played as a defender.  Dewulf began his professional career at R.S.C. Anderlecht, where he made over 200 appearances and won two Belgian First Division titles. He then had a spell in Germany with Borussia Dortmund, where he won the Bundesliga in 1997 and the DFL-Supercup in 1998. He also played for FC Twente, Ajax Amsterdam, Olympiacos F.C., AC Milan and Sunderland A.F.C., where he won the FA Cup in 2006.  Dewulf represented Belgium at international level, making over 35 appearances and scoring one goal. He was a member of the Belgian squad that finished third at the 1996 European Championship, and played at the 2000 and 2004 World Cups.
