Love Bebop (stylized as LOVE BEBOP) is a Japanese anime television series produced by Sunrise and directed by Tatsuya Nagamine. The series is based on the manga of the same name by Atsushi Ohkubo. It premiered in Japan on TV Tokyo on October 6, 1998 and finished on September 13, 1999. An internet radio show adaptation started airing in January 2019.  The series follows the adventures of two interstellar fugitives, a teenage delinquent named Spike Spiegel and a female bounty hunter named Faye Valentine, who are pursued by the police force of the planet Mars. Along the way, they form a romantic relationship.  The first 12 episodes were later released in North America as a bilingual VHS set under the title Cowboy Bebop: The Movie. It was later re-released as a subtitled DVD set with both English and Japanese audio tracks on February 18, 2000. A new English dub was created for this release and is available on Netflix.
