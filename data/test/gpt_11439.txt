Empire: Total War is a turn-based strategy game developed by Creative Assembly and published by Sega. The game was released in 2001 for the PC and later ported to numerous other platforms.  Empire: Total War is a turn-based strategy game set in the historical period of the Napoleonic Wars, covering the campaigns of Napoleon Bonaparte and his armies in Europe and Russia. The game features a large number of units, extensive real-time battle dynamics, and a modding community that has produced many additional mods.
