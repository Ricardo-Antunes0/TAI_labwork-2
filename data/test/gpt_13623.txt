The Submarines are an American indie rock band from Los Angeles, California. Formed in 2009, the band consists of singer and songwriter Matt Berninger, guitarist Brent Knopf, bassist Bryan Devendorf, and drummer Aaron Dessner.  The Submarines have released two albums, "I Bet on Sky" (2011) and "No One Is Lost" (2013), both to critical acclaim. Their debut album was named one of the best records of 2011 by publications such as The Guardian, NME, Q, and Rolling Stone. In early 2014, the band released their second album "No One Is Lost" to widespread acclaim once again.   The Submarines are currently in the process of recording their third studio album.
