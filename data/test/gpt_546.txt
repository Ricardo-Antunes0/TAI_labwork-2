Neil McIntosh (born 1942) is a British paediatrician. He is a retired Professor of Paediatrics at the University of Liverpool, having been appointed in 1991.  McIntosh's research has focused on paediatric respiratory diseases, particularly asthma and bronchitis, and he has made significant contributions to the understanding of these conditions. He is also known for his work on the prevention and treatment of childhood obesity.  He has been awarded honorary degrees from several universities, and in 2009 was awarded the prestigious Royal College of Physicians' prestigious Lindsell medal.
