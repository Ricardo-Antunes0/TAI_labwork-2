Ratchaburi railway station is a railway station in Ratchaburi, Thailand. It is on the Bangkok–Chiang Mai Railway and is served by trains to both destinations.   The station opened in 1925 as part of the original route of the Bangkok–Chiang Mai railway. The current station building was completed in 1984. In 2006, a new station building was completed adjacent to the old one. The new building has a total floor area of .   Ratchaburi railway station is a major passenger hub for travel between Bangkok and Chiang Mai. The facility includes a ticket office, a post office, a food court, and several passenger terminals for long-distance trains. There are also several taxi stands outside the station.
