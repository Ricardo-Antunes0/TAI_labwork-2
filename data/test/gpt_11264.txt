Paradise Point is a coastal suburb in the City of Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. The suburb is bordered by Woolloongabba to the north-east, Milton to the south-west and the Brisbane River to the east.  The area was originally part of a large sugar plantation and since the early 1900s it has been developed as a residential suburb. It is now one of Brisbane's most popular suburbs with spacious streets, parks and gardens, and excellent access to the city. Paradise Point is also well known for its cafes, restaurants and nightlife.  Paradise Point is served by the Paradise Point railway station on the Ipswich Line of Queensland Rail.
