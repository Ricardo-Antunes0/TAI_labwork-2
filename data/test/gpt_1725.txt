Heroes & Thieves is the third album by American rock band Twenty One Pilots. It was released on March 9, 2019, through Fueled by Ramen and Interscope Records. The album was preceded by the single "Heathens" in February 2019.  The album was preceded by the single "Heathens" in February 2019.   The album's lead single is "Trees". The song also topped the charts in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom. The second single from the album is "Jumpsuit".
