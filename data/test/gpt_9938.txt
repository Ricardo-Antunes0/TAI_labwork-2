The Unidale Mall is a shopping mall located in Unidale, a suburb of the city of Sydney, in the state of New South Wales, Australia. Opened in 1976, it is one of the largest shopping centres in New South Wales and is currently managed by Westfield Corporation.  The Unidale Mall has over 120 stores and eateries, including flagship stores for Big W, Myer, Kmart and Target. The mall also features a cinema complex with six screens, a bowling alley and a multi-purpose arena. The Unidale Mall is also home to the Unidale Carnival which is one of Sydney's largest annual festivals.
