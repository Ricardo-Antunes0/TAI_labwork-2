Born at Heidelberg, Victoria, Gwynn was a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium pace bowler. He made his first-class debut for Victoria in 1902-03, and played one further first-class match for the state in 1904-05. His sole Test appearance came against England in 1903-04, and he scored 10 runs in the first innings of the match, and 2 not out in the second. Gwynn died at Kensington, Victoria, aged 70.
