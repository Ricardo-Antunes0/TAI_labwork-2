"Enemigos" (Spanish for "Enemies") is a song by Spanish artist José José. The song was written by José José, Fernando Guerra and produced by the latter. It was released as a single in Spain in December 1985 and peaked at number one on the chart.  The song is a duet between José José and Argentinian singer Silvio Rodríguez, with backing vocals by Conchita Bautista. It tells the story of a young man who has been betrayed by his friends and is now out to seek revenge. The song has been described as an "anthemic" ballad with "powerful vocals" and "a stirring message".
