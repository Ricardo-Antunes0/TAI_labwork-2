Root Into Europe is an ITV comedy-drama set in the heart of the European Union. The show follows the everyday lives of working class Britons Shazia and Rufus, who are forced to uproot their lives and move to the heart of the EU in order to provide for their young daughter.   Root Into Europe is a hilarious and heartwarming portrayal of life in the EU, with Shazia and Rufus struggling to adjust to a new life while trying to maintain their British traditions. The show is an excellent way to learn about the workings of the EU, and to see how everyday people from all over Europe live their lives.
