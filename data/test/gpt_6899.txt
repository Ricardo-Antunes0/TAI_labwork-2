Anaheim Street or Anaheim is an at-grade light rail station on the Orange Line of Los Angeles Metro's Metro Rail system. The station is located at the intersection of Anaheim Boulevard and Katella Avenue in the city of Anaheim, California. Anaheim Street is a major transportation hub for the surrounding communities and is served by several bus lines as well as the Orange Line.  Opened in 2000, Anaheim Street is one of two new stations on the Orange Line, serving an area that had been underserved by transit. The station features a large concourse that provides access to all platforms, as well as a park-and-ride lot. It is also adjacent to the Angel Stadium of Anaheim and Honda Center, two of the three major sports venues in Southern California.
