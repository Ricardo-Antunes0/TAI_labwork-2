Montaque "Monty" Brown (born April 13, 1970) is a retired American professional basketball player who played in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He was a 6'7" forward-center who played for the Golden State Warriors, Houston Rockets, Toronto Raptors, and Milwaukee Bucks.  Brown was selected by the Golden State Warriors with the 38th pick in the 1992 NBA draft. He played with the Warriors from 1992 to 1997, averaging 8.5 points and 5.6 rebounds per game. Brown then signed with the Houston Rockets in 1998, where he averaged 14.7 points and 6.5 rebounds per game. He was traded to the Toronto Raptors in 2000 and then to the Milwaukee Bucks in 2001.
