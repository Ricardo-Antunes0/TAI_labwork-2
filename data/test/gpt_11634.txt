Ninewells Hospital is a large teaching hospital, located in Dundee, Scotland. It is the largest hospital in the city and has a total of 1,000 beds. The hospital was founded in 1851 and has been named after Ninewells Hospital, a hospice in Edinburgh which was founded by William Ninewells in 1796.  Ninewells Hospital is known for its specialist services including cancer care, cardiology, neurology, orthopedics and general surgery. In 2013, it was announced that Ninewells had been awarded the prestigious Queen's Award for Excellence in Patient Care.
