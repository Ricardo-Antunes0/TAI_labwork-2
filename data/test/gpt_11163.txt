Phungyo Baptist Church is a Baptist Church in Seoul, South Korea. The church was founded in 1908 by a group of Christian converts from the United States.  The Phungyo Baptist Church is the oldest Baptist Church in Seoul and the largest Baptist congregation in South Korea. The church has a membership of over 4,600 people and operates fifteen ministries, including five missions in the surrounding area. The church is also involved in community service and participates in various social welfare programs.
