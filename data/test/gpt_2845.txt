Najat Kaanache is the host of AMC's "Najat Kaanache: Arab Style" which airs on Sundays at 10pm ET. Kaanache is a Lebanese-born television personality and author who has been living in the United States since the mid-1990s.  Najat Kaanache was born in Beirut, Lebanon in 1969. She moved to the United States in the mid-1990s and began her television career as a weather anchor for WUSA in Washington, D.C. In 2002, she joined CNN as a correspondent and later became a correspondent for Al Jazeera America. In 2006, Kaanache created and hosted her own show, "Najat Kaanache: Arab Style", which aired on AMC. The show focused on modern Arab culture and fashion. Kaanache retired from television in 2013 but continues to write and lecture. She is married to American actor and singer Tony Shalhoub and they have two children.
