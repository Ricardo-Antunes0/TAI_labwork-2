Caira Moreira-Brown (born June 11, 1996) is an American soccer player who plays as a forward for the Seattle Reign FC in the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL).  Moreira-Brown played college soccer at the University of North Carolina. She was a member of the Tar Heels’ 2015 NCAA Championship team and was named to the All-ACC First Team and ACC Tournament MVP.  Moreira-Brown also played for Houston Dash in the NWSL and made her debut in 2016. She was named to the NWSL Second Team in 2017 and 2018.  On January 10, 2019, Moreira-Brown announced her retirement from professional soccer.   Moreira-Brown was born in Chapel Hill, North Carolina to parents of Brazilian descent.
