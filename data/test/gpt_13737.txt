Molly Patricia Schaus (born July 29, 1988) is an American actress and model. Schaus began her acting career in 2001, appearing in minor roles in films such as Rent and The O.C. She gained wider recognition for her starring role as Margot Tenenbaum in the television series The Office, which aired from 2005 to 2013.  Schaus also appeared in a number of feature films, including Life As We Know It (2010), The Five-Year Engagement (2012), Neighbors (2014), and Captain America: Civil War (2016). In 2017, she starred in the Hulu series The Path.
