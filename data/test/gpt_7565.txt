Yuriy Ovcharenko  is a Ukrainian retired football player.  Ovcharenko made his professional debut in the Soviet Second League in 1988 for FC Znamya Truda Orekhovo-Zuyevo. Ovcharenko played in Russia for FC Torpedo-BelAZ Moskva, FC Spartak Moscow, FC Krylia Sovetov Samara, FC Krylia Sovetov Samara again and FC Terek Grozny. Ovcharenko played in Ukraine for FC Metalurh Donetsk, FC Shakhtar Donetsk and FC Oleksandriya Kharkiv. Ovcharenko also played for the Ukrainian national team, making his debut in 1998. He participated in the 2002 FIFA World Cup and UEFA Euro 2004. Ovcharenko retired from football in 2006.
