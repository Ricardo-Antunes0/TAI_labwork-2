Juan Bielovucic (30 July 1889 – 14 February 1971) was a Croatian football striker who played for Yugoslavia, Italy and Spain.  Bielovucic started his career with Yugoslav side Partizan. He then moved to Italy to play for Genoa, where he won the 1934–35 Serie A championship. In 1936, he joined Spanish side Valencia, where he won two La Liga championships and the 1938 Copa del Generalísimo. He also played for the Croatian national team, appearing in the 1938 World Cup. After his retirement from football, Bielovucic worked as a football coach. He died in 1971 at the age of 80.
