Santa Clarita Diet is an American horror-comedy television series created by Victor Fresco and produced by The Asylum. The series is set in the fictional town of Santa Clarita, California, and follows a group of people who are forced to live out their days after a deadly virus has wiped out most of the population.  The show stars Drew Barrymore, Timothy Olyphant, Eric Bana, and Nick Offerman. It premiered on Netflix on August 25, 2018.
