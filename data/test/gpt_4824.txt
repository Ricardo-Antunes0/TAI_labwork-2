The Luminous Dead is a 2019 sci-fi horror film directed by David Slade and written by Luke P. Smith. It is a sequel to the 2016 film The Witch and is set in the same world.  The film stars Nicholas Hoult, Katherine Parkinson, and Rob Delaney. It was released on 8th October 2019.  The Luminous Dead follows the return of a group of witches who have been living in seclusion for over fifty years. When they discover that their home has been burned down and their children taken, they set off on ahunt for their children.
