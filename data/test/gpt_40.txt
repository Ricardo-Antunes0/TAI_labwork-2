Madison Packer (born June 25, 1991) is an American model, actress and television personality. She is the daughter of Hollywood producer Robert Packer and fashion model Peggy Siegal.  Madison was born in Los Angeles, California and raised in Beverly Hills, California. She has two older sisters, Rebecca and Elizabeth. Packer's parents divorced when she was three years old and she and her sisters were raised by their mother.  Packer started modelling at the age of five, appearing in campaigns for brands including Guess, Nike and Lululemon Athletica. In 2009, she appeared in the music video for Nelly Furtado's single "Promiscuous". That year, she also made her acting debut in the Nickelodeon series "iCarly".  In 2010, Packer signed with Wilhelmina Models. In 2011, she appeared in the music video for Pitbull's single "I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho)". That year, she also appeared in the independent film "The To Do List". In 2012, she starred in the film "The Five-Year Engagement" as Lacey Chabert's sister. Packer also appeared on the cover of "Tess" magazine that year.   In 2013, Packer appeared in the music video for Ariana Grande's single "Problem."
