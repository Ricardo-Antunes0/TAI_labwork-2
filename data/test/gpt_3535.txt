Trevor John Linden  (born April 11, 1971) is a Canadian computer scientist, game designer and entrepreneur. He is the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Linden Lab, a social media and virtual world company, as well as the founder of Second Life, an online 3D virtual world.  Linden was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He earned a BSc from the University of Waterloo in 1994 and his MSc from the University of Cambridge in 1997.  Linden Lab was founded in 2003 by Linden and his then- classmate Michael Abrash. Linden Lab's first product was "Second Life", which became one of the world's most popular online social worlds with over 2 million users by 2006. In December 2009, Linden Lab announced it had agreed to be acquired by Ebay for US$2 billion. The acquisition was completed on February 8, 2010.
