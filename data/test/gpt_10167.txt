Tang Shu-wing  is a theatre director, playwright, and actor who has been active in the Chinese-speaking world since the early 1990s.  Born in Shanghai, Tang Shu-wing graduated from the Academy of Dramatic Art in 1993. Her early theatre work was primarily in Mandarin, but she has since branched out into other languages, including English. She is perhaps best known for her directing work, which has included productions of plays by renowned playwrights such as Tian Zhuangzhuang and Cao Yu. In 2016 she was awarded the prestigious Peking Prize for her lifetime achievements in theatre.
