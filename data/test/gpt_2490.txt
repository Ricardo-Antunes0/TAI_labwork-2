Percy Isenia (born 25 December 1976 in Waikato) is a New Zealand rugby union player. He plays as a flanker and has since 2003 played for the Waikato Chiefs in the ITM Cup.  Isenia made his debut for Waikato in 2001 and has since played 83 games for the Chiefs, including the 2005 and 2007 ITM Cup championships. He also represented New Zealand in the 2003 World Cup, playing against England and Fiji.   In 2009, Isenia was signed by French side Tarbes Pyrénées-Atlantiques on a two-year contract. He left Tarbes at the end of the 2011 season.  Isenia has been playing for Waikato since 2001 and has scored over 70 points in that time.
