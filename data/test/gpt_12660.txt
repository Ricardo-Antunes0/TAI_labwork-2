Thiruvathigai Veerateeswarar Temple is a Hindu temple located in Thiruvathigai, near Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, India. The temple is dedicated to the Hindu god Veeraraja.  The temple has a granite chariot drawn by four horses and an image of Veeraraja seated on a peacock throne. The temple has three shrines - the first one is dedicated to Muruga, the second to Veeraraja, and the third to Lakshmi. The temple has two entrances - one facing the east and the other facing the west.
