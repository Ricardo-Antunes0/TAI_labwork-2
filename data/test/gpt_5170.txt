Kalabaland Glacier of Himalaya is situated in the north-east of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is located on the left bank of the river Indus, about 20 km south of the Chamba town. It is one of the longest glaciers in India and it has a length of about 58 km. The glacier has a surface area of about 197 sq km and a thickness of about 650 m. It is in a state of active retreat and its recession has been monitored since the late 1970s.   Kalabaland Glacier is one of the longest glaciers in India.
