V Graham Norton is a British chat show host, comedian and television personality. He is best known for his work on the BBC show "The Graham Norton Show" and for his regular appearances on the British television series "The Friday Night Project".  Norton was born in Hammersmith, London, to Irish parents, and was brought up in Dublin. He began his career as a stand-up comedian in the early 1990s. He first came to prominence in 1996, when he won the prestigious Perrier Award for Best Newcomer. In 2000, he moved to the United Kingdom and began hosting "The Graham Norton Show", a BBC2 chat show which ran for six series. The show became popular in the United States when it was aired on Comedy Central from 2005 to 2007. From 2007 to 2009, Norton co-hosted "The Paul O'Grady Show" on ITV with O'Grady.  Since 2009, Norton has hosted "The Graham Norton Show" again on BBC2. The show has been nominated for several awards, including two BAFTAs (2006 and 2007) and a Golden Globe (2007). In February 2013, it was announced that Norton would be joining forces with American talk show host Conan O'Brien on a new project called "Conan", which aired its first episode in April 2013.
