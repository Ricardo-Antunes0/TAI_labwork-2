"Red Cold River" is a song by American country music artist George Strait. It was released in May 1984 as the third and final single from his album of the same name. The song reached number one on the U.S. country chart and number six on the Billboard Hot 100.  The song is about a man who is trying to find his way home after becoming lost in the desert. He sings about the red cold river that he is following, and how it is helping him to stay warm.
