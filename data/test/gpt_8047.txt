"Trust" is the tenth episode of the second season of the American television series "Breaking Bad". It is the twenty-second episode of the series and was written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Michelle MacLaren.  Walter White Jr. (Bryan Cranston) urges his brother-in-law, Hank Schrader (Dean Norris), to come clean with their DEA agent, Marie Schrader (Alycia Debnam-Carey), about the methamphetamine they cooked in their previous job. Hank is hesitant, fearing that Marie will push to have them arrested.   Meanwhile, Skyler White (Anna Gunn) becomes suspicious when her husband begins spending a lot of time with their new employer, Todd Alquist (Jesse Plemons). She follows him to a meeting at Alquist's office only to find out that he is representing a company that her husband is thinking of investing in. Todd tells her that he has no involvement with the company other than to act as an intermediary, but Skyler is not convinced.   Later, Walt and Jesse visit a scrap yard where Walt has arranged for them to meet Tuco Salamanca (Raymond Cruz), a powerful drug lord who is also looking for meth supplies. Tuco is unimpressed with Walt's product, but agrees to deal with him if he can produce more quickly. Back at home, Walt tries to sell shares in his company to Skyler but she is still skeptical.
