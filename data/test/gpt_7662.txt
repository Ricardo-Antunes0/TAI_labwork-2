The Knightsville Meetinghouse (also known as Knightsville-Franklin Meetinghouse) is a historic building located at Franklin in Lewis County, New York. It was built in 1814 and is a -story, rectangular, gable-roofed frame structure on a stone foundation. It features a two-story, projecting entrance pavilion with a pedimented gable roof and six fluted Ionic columns.  The Knightsville Meetinghouse was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
