Hrudaya Geethe  is a 1989 Indian Malayalam film, directed by Kamal and produced by S. Radhakrishnan. The film stars Mohanlal, Shobana, Sukumari and Innocent in lead roles. The film had musical score by Gireesh Puthenchery.  The film tells the story of Krishnan (Mohanlal), a poor man who is falsely accused of murdering his wife. Krishnan is jailed and spends years in prison. After his release, he tries to find out who framed him and why.
