Rattle and Hum is a hybrid live/studio album by the English rock band the Rolling Stones, released on 2 October 2003.  The Rolling Stones released Rattle and Hum in 2003, a live album and studio album hybrid. The studio album was recorded at the Stadthalle in Vienna, Austria during the band's 2002 tour. It features covers of songs by Bob Dylan, Neil Young, and Chuck Berry. The live album was recorded at the Oakland-Alameda County Coliseum in Oakland, California during the band's 2002 tour.
