Palencia is a province of northern Spain, in the Basque Country. Its capital is the city of Palencia. The province has an area of 2,768 km², and a population of 277,996 in 2009.  The province is bordered by the provinces of Burgos, León, Cantabria, Castile and León, and the autonomous community of La Rioja. Palencia is subdivided into 34 municipalities.  The province has a humid subtropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The main agricultural products are wine, cereals, cattle and poultry.
