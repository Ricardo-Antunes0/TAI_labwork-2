Combat Missions was a one-hour-long reality TV series that aired on the Syfy channel from 2005 to 2006. The show followed a group of professional military personnel as they completed combat missions in various locations around the world.  Combat Missions was one of the first reality TV shows to focus exclusively on military personnel, and it helped to pioneer the genre. The show was well-received by viewers, and it won several awards during its run. However, after six seasons, Combat Missions was canceled in 2006.  Today, Combat Missions is largely forgotten by television audiences. However, the show has been cited as an influential factor in the development of reality TV as a genre. It is also worth noting that many of the participants in Combat Missions went on to successful careers in the military or other fields.
