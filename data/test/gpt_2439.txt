Kanchanaburi Stadium or Kleeb Bua Stadium ,   is a multi-purpose stadium in Kanchanaburi Province, Thailand. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home stadium of Kanchanaburi United. The stadium has a capacity of 15,000 people.  The stadium was built in 1998 and originally had a capacity of 10,000 people. It was upgraded to its current capacity in 2007.
