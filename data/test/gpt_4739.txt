Stephen Lawrence Morgan (born 1971) is a United States District Judge of the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia.  Morgan was born in Norfolk, Virginia, in 1971. He received a B.A. from the University of Virginia in 1993 and a J.D. from the University of Virginia School of Law in 1996. He was an associate at the law firm of Hunton & Williams from 1996 to 1999, and an assistant U.S. attorney for the Eastern District of Virginia from 1999 to 2004. He was a judge on the Norfolk City Court from 2004 to 2006, and a judge on the General District Court for Norfolk County from 2006 to 2009.  On September 29, 2009, Morgan was nominated by President Barack Obama to a seat on the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia vacated by Judge John A. Gibbons III. Morgan was confirmed by the United States Senate on November 16, 2009, and received his commission on November 17, 2009.
