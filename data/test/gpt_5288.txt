Leeann Chin is a Bloomington, Minnesota-based Asian-American artist and educator. She has been recognized for her multimedia installations, paintings, and drawings, which explore the intersections of race, culture, and identity. Chin has exhibited her work in numerous U.S. and international galleries, including the Minneapolis Institute of Arts, the University of Minnesota Art Gallery, and the Columbus Museum of Art.  Leeann Chin was born in Bloomington, Minnesota in 1965. She is a Bloomington-based artist and educator who explores the intersections of race, culture, and identity in her work. Chin has exhibited her work in numerous U.S. and international galleries, including the Minneapolis Institute of Arts, the University of Minnesota Art Gallery, and the Columbus Museum of Art. She received her BFA from Rhode Island School of Design in 1988 and her MFA from Yale University School of Art in 1993.
