Eldest is the second novel in the Eldest trilogy by Australian author Christopher Paolini. It is preceded by Eragon and followed by Brisingr.  The story follows Eragon, a young man who is searching for his missing father, and Arya, a young woman who is looking for her brother. They are joined by Saphira, a dragon who Eragon trains to be his companion. Together they explore the land of Alagaesia and fight against the evil Galbatorix and his forces.
