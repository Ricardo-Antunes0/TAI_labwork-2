Jamie Jilynn Chung (born April 10, 1983) is an American actress, model, and singer. Chung began her career as a child model and appeared in print advertisements and on television. Chung made her acting debut in the CBS soap opera The Young and the Restless in 2001. She has since appeared in films such as Panic Room (2002), The Family Stone (2005), and Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle (2003). Chung has also starred in the Fox series Alcatraz from 2009 to 2011. In 2012, she starred in the film Kung Fu Panda 3 as Po.
