 is 1958 Indian Tamil-language film directed by T. R. Ramachandran and produced by K. Balachander. The film stars Sivaji Ganesan, Nageswari, K. A. Thangavelu and T. M. Soundararajan in lead roles with music composed by M. S. Viswanathan.  The film tells the story of an illegitimate child, who is raised by a widower and his daughter in a small town in Tamil Nadu. The child, who is christened Athisaya Thirudan, grows up to become a powerful forest chief and clashes with the local villagers over their exploitation of the forest resources.
