Michael Blake (born 31 October 1951) is an English composer and multi-instrumentalist. He has written music for stage, film and television, and has worked with a wide range of performers, including the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra and the London Symphony Orchestra.  Blake was born in 1951 in London, England. He studied music at the University of Cambridge, where he was awarded a Master of Music degree in 1976. He continued his studies at the University of Oxford, where he was awarded a Doctor of Music degree in 1984.  Blake has worked as a composer for stage, film and television.
