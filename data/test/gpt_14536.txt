Bear Shooters is a 1930 Our Gang short comedy film directed by Edward Laemmle.  The gang go on a bear hunting trip and get into all sorts of predicaments. The most notable is when Spanky gets his rifle caught on a tree and has to free it with his bare hands.   The film was released on October 15, 1930 and was a critical and commercial success. It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Short Subject (Cartoon).
