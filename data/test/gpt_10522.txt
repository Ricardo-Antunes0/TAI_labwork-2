He played in the National Hockey League with the Philadelphia Flyers and the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim.  Born in Moscow, Tsygurov played in the Soviet Union with HC Lokomotiv Moscow, CSKA Moscow, Metallurg Novokuznetsk, HC Dynamo Moscow and HC Sochi. He also played in Germany with ERC Ingolstadt and the Mighty Ducks of Anaheim.  Tsygurov died in a car accident on September 4, 2006.
