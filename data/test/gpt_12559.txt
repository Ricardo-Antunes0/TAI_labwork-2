Dana S. Coin (born December 10, 1949) is an American businesswoman who is the Founder, Chairman, and CEO of the Coinstar Corporation, a Fortune 500 company.  Coinstar is a leading provider of automated retail solutions, including coin and currency processing and redemption services for consumers and small businesses. The company also produces physical and digital media products, including DVDs and downloadable products. In addition, Coinstar operates the Cash & Check Cashing business.  Coinstar was founded in 1994 by Dana S. Coin and her husband, Gary W. Karr. The company went public in 1996 and has since grown to be one of the largest providers of automated retail solutions in the world. In 2013, Coinstar was ranked 263rd on the Fortune 500 list and generated $5.4 billion in revenue.  Dana S. Coin was born on December 10, 1949, in Arlington County, Virginia. She received her bachelor's degree in mathematics from George Mason University in 1971 and her MBA from Boston University in 1976.
