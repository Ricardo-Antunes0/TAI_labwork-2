Baltazar Martínez Montemayor (born August 31, 1966) is a Spanish composer, musicologist, and professor of contemporary classical music at the University of Murcia. His compositions are predominantly for chamber ensemble, including several works for string quartet.  Born in Valencia, Montemayor studied composition with Manuel de Falla at the Madrid Royal Conservatory and with Heinz Holliger at the University of Basel. He has also received instruction from Olivier Messiaen, Iannis Xenakis, and Krzysztof Penderecki. He has been awarded several prizes, including the Prince of Asturias Award in Music in 2002 and the Premio de la Fundación Juan March in 2006.
