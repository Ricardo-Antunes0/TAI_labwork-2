Karthik Calling Karthik is a 2010 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film written and directed by K. S. Ravikumar. Produced by Udhayanidhi Stalin and A. R. Rahman, the film features Vijay and Karthika in the lead roles along with Anushka Shetty, Trisha Krishnan and Sathyaraj in supporting roles.  The story focuses on Karthik, a call center employee who falls in love with Sanchita, a woman he meets through his work. When Sanchita's family rejects her love for Karthik, he decides to leave his job to be with her. The film culminates with their marriage at a beachside ceremony.  The film received mixed reviews from critics but was commercially successful, becoming one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of 2010. It was later dubbed into Telugu as Veera Prathapulu and into Hindi as Dabangg3.  Karthik (Vijay) works in a call center and falls in love with Sanchita (Karthika), a woman he meets through his work.
