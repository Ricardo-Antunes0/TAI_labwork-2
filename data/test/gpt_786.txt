Baku Metro  is a rapid transit system serving the city of Baku, Azerbaijan. Opened on May 12, 2012, the Metro is the first underground metro system in Azerbaijan. The system has a total length of 66 km and has 4 lines. As of 2018, the system has a ridership of over 1 million passengers per day.   The Baku Metro is operated by Azerbaijan Railways. The project was financed mainly by the European Union, with additional contributions from the Azeri government and international partners. The construction cost was estimated at $1.5 billion.
