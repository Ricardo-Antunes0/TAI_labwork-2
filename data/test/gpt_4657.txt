Serkan Özkaya (1973) is a Turkish-American conceptual artist and musician.  Born in Ankara, Turkey in 1973, Özkaya immigrated to the United States with his family at the age of six. He studied painting at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and music at Indiana University Bloomington. Özkaya has exhibited his work internationally, including solo exhibitions at the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago, the Centre Pompidou in Paris, and the Museum der Moderne in Vienna. He was awarded the prestigious Guggenheim Fellowship in 2006.
