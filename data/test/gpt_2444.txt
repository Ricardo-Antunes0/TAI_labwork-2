The Life Nature Library is a series of encyclopedic works on natural sciences and mathematics, edited by American naturalist Herbert Spencer. The first volume, published in 1864, was a collection of essays by Spencer on a variety of scientific and mathematical topics. The series has been continued by different editors, and the present edition is the thirty-fourth volume.
