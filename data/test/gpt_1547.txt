"Q Who" is the 16th episode of the fourth series of the British science fiction television series Doctor Who. Written by Steven Moffat and directed by Rachel Talalay, it was first broadcast on BBC One on 7 October 2013.  In the episode, the Doctor (Matt Smith) and his companions Clara (Jenna-Louise Coleman) and Danny (Tom Hiddleston) investigate a mysterious group of people known as the Q, who are linked to a space event that has caused a temporal rift.  The episode received mixed reviews from critics. However, it was praised for its action sequences and Moffat's writing.
