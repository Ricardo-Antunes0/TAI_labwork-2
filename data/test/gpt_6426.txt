Lee Lawrence McKinley (August 22, 1906 – March 12, 1974) was an American football player and coach. He played college football for the University of Alabama and served as the head football coach at the University of Kansas from 1941 to 1945 and at the University of Minnesota from 1946 to 1949, compiling a career college football record of 54–27–5. McKinley was also the head baseball coach at Kansas from 1941 to 1945 and at Minnesota from 1946 to 1949, tallying a career college baseball record of 82–41.
