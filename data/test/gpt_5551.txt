Andrei Derevianko is a Sara Louise Hartman Award-winning children's author and illustrator.  Andrei Derevianko was born in Toronto, Ontario in 1975. He studied illustration at Sheridan College and the Ontario College of Art and Design. He has been a children's book author and illustrator since 2001. His books have been published in Canada, the United States, and Australia. He has won the Sara Louise Hartman Award for his book A Day with Santa (2004), the Alberta Library Association Award for Best Illustrated Book (2008), and the Regina Public Library's Children's Choice Award (2012).
