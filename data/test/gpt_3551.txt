Steven Terner Mnuchin ( ; born December 12, 1975) is an American banker and businessman who is the Secretary of the Treasury in the Trump administration. Mnuchin has served in executive positions at several banks, including Goldman Sachs and OneWest Bank. Mnuchin was nominated to be Secretary of the Treasury by President Donald Trump on November 17, 2017.  Mnuchin was born in 1975 in Brooklyn, New York City to Jewish parents. His father was a real estate developer and his mother is a former teacher. Mnuchin attended Yale University, where he received his undergraduate degree in economics. After graduating from Yale, he worked at Goldman Sachs for four years. He then started his own bank, OneWest Bank, which was later bought by CIT Group. Mnuchin is married to Louise Linton and they have two children.
