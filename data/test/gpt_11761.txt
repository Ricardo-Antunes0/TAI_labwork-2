Michael Joseph Madigan (born April 19, 1942) is an American politician who has served as the Illinois Governor since 1983, and Speaker of the Illinois House of Representatives since 1987. He is the longest-serving current speaker in the history of the United States House of Representatives.  Madigan was born in Chicago, Illinois, to a working-class Irish Catholic family.
