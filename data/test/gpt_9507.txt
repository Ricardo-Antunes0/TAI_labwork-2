Burnt Hill is a hamlet in Berkshire, England. It is situated in the parish of Winkfield, about 1 mile (1.6 km) northeast of Winkfield Broad Oak and 4 miles (6.4 km) southeast of Reading.  Burnt Hill is a small, quiet hamlet with a population of around 60 residents. It is a popular spot for camping and hiking, and has a number of pubs and restaurants. The hamlet is also home to Burnt Hill Windmill, which is open to the public for tours.
