Project Panther Bidco Ltd. is a British-American multinational conglomerate, headquartered in the United States. The company was founded in 1992 by three former executives of British Petroleum: Lord Browne of Madingley, David Richards, and James Crosby. The company has headquarters in the United States and is principally engaged in the oil and gas business, although it also has interests in mining and other industries. The company has a primary focus on Africa, where it has invested in oil and gas projects, as well as other businesses.
