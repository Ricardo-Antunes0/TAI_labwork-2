HMAS Yunnan (FL-151) was a 2,812-ton former Royal Australian Navy (RAN) minesweeper that served in the Royal New Zealand Navy (RNZN) from 1951 to 1970.  The vessel was destroyed by fire on 26 February 1970 while docked at the Auckland Naval Base.  The Yunnan was originally built as a Royal Australian Navy minesweeper in 1941. The vessel served in the Royal New Zealand Navy from 1951 to 1970. In 1968, the Yunnan was transferred to the RAN's 17th Mine Sweeping Squadron, based in Sydney. The Yunnan was destroyed by fire on 26 February 1970 while docked at the Auckland Naval Base.
