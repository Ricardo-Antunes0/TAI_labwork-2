Ludi Lin  (born November 11, 1987) is an American singer and songwriter.  Lin was born in Los Angeles, California. She began her career in the music industry as a backing vocalist for recording artist Travis Scott and rapper Kanye West. She later signed to Scott's label, GOOD Music, and released her debut album "Blonde" in 2018. The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, making her the first female artist to debut at the top spot of the chart with a solo album.  Lin has won several awards including Best New Artist at the 55th Annual Grammy Awards and Best New Artist at the 2018 American Music Awards.
