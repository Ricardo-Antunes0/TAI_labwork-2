Akmal Shaikh (5 April 1956 – 29 January 2017) was an Indian cricketer. He played for the country in two Tests and 21 ODIs from 1978 to 1984.  Akmal Shaikh was born on 5 April 1956 in Hyderabad, Sindh, Pakistan. He played first-class cricket for Hyderabad and Karachi between 1978 and 1984. He made his Test debut against New Zealand in 1978 at the age of 22, and his final appearance came against England in 1984. Shaikh scored 1,847 runs at an average of 26.75, with a high score of 111 not out against Australia in 1983–84. He also took eleven wickets at an average of 30.07.
