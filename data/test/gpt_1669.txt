Asylums: Essays on the Condition of the Insane in England and Wales is an edited volume of essays on the history, sociology and politics of asylums in England and Wales. It was published in 2002 by Routledge.  The book assembles a range of essays on the history, sociology and politics of asylums in England and Wales. The essays are written by a variety of scholars from a variety of disciplines, including history, sociology, psychiatry, law and literature. The book is divided into three parts: the first part covers the history of asylums from ancient times to the early nineteenth century; the second part looks at developments in nineteenth-century asylum policy; the third part examines contemporary asylum systems.
