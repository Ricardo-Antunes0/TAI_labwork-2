Avengers: Endgame (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) is the soundtrack to the 2019 Marvel Cinematic Universe film Avengers: Endgame. It was composed by John Williams and features music from the film's score, as well as songs from various Marvel films and television shows. The album was released on April 26, 2019, by Walt Disney Records.  The album debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, selling 132,000 copies in its first week. It has since sold over 1.5 million copies in the US. The album also topped the charts in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Sweden, and the United Kingdom.  Avengers: Endgame is the culmination of 22 films spanning six phases in the MCU and features an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy Renner, Don Cheadle, Tom Hiddleston, Danai Gurira, Brie Larson, Josh Brolin, Anthony Mackie and Paul Rudd. The film is set after the events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and features a new cast of characters who join forces to stop Thanos from assembling all six Infinity Stones and using them to inflict his will on reality.
