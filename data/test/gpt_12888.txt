Forgiven is the fourth single from Christian rock band Skillet's sixth album The Beautiful Letdown. The song was released on April 21, 2018.  "Forgiven" is a song about overcoming past mistakes and moving on. It was written by band members John Cooper and Aaron Gillespie, and produced by Kevin Churko. Lyrically, the song speaks to the idea that forgiveness is something that should be given freely, not demanded or taken.  "Forgiven" is Skillet's first single in five years, and their first release since their departure from Atlantic Records. The song was well-received by music critics, who praised its catchy melody and lyrical depth. It debuted at number six on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, making it Skillet's seventh consecutive top ten hit on that chart. "Forgiven" has also reached the top five in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Norway, Sweden and the United Kingdom.
