Blackmail is a 1973 Bollywood thriller film directed by K. A. Abbas and produced by S. S. Vasan. The film stars Rekha, Dharmendra, Mala Sinha and Nirupa Roy in lead roles. The music is scored by Laxmikant-Pyarelal.  The film tells the story of a woman (Rekha) who is blackmailed by her ex-husband (Dharmendra) into giving him money. She is forced to move to another city with her children, and the only way she can make ends meet is to work as a maid in a rich man's house. She starts to investigate her husband's past, and discovers that he has been blackmailing other people for money.
