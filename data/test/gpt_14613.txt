The Archdeacon of Totnes or Totton is an ecclesiastical officer within the Anglican Church in the United Kingdom. The current Archdeacon is David Mowat.  The archdeaconry of Totnes is one of the thirteen archdeaconries within the Diocese of Exeter, and covers the parish of Totnes and the towns of Barnstaple and Bideford. The archdeaconry includes the churches of All Saints, Barnstaple; Christ Church, Bideford; Holy Trinity, Buckland; St Michael & All Angels, Dawlish; and St John the Evangelist, Ilfracombe.
