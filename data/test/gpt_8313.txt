Ivan Paunović (Serbian Cyrillic: Иван Пауновић, born 27 September 1983) is a Serbian footballer who plays as a defender for French club Saint-Étienne.  Ivan Paunović was born in Sombor, Serbia. He started his career with local side FK Sombor, before moving to Dutch club Feyenoord Rotterdam in 2002. He made his Eredivisie debut on 10 September 2002 in a 3–2 away win over Heracles Almelo.  In the summer of 2004, Paunović moved to Serie A side Lazio. He made his Serie A debut on 24 September 2004 in a 2–1 home win over Udinese Calcio. He spent three seasons at the Stadio Olimpico, helping Lazio reach the UEFA Cup semi-finals in 2006–07 and the UEFA Champions League semi-finals in 2007–08.  In July 2009, Paunović signed for English club Portsmouth for an undisclosed fee. He made his Portsmouth debut on 22 August 2009 in a 3–0 home win over Scunthorpe United. In November 2009, he scored his first goal for the club in a 2–1 away win over Walsall Town Hall.  Paunović left Portsmouth after making 70 appearances and scoring four goals during his time there.
