Dubuque YMCA Building, also known as the YMCA of Dubuque and the Dubuque Civic Center, is a historic building located in Dubuque, Iowa, United States. The building was designed by the architectural firm of McKim, Mead & White and was completed in 1917. It is considered to be one of the finest examples of Classical Revival architecture in the Midwest. The building has been listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1984.
