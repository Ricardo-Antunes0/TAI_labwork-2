The Hurting is the debut studio album by American rock band The Used. It was released on October 22, 2002, through Vagrant Records. The album was produced by the band's drummer Joel Madden and recorded at The Bakery in Hollywood, California.  The Hurting received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's hard rock and punk influences. It peaked at number four on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, selling over 148,000 copies in its first week. The album has since been certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
