Group Captain Thomas Gerard Hetherington CBE (1932-1998) was an English aviation officer and Royal Air Force commander.  Hetherington was born in 1932 in London, England, and joined the Royal Air Force in 1950. He served in a variety of roles throughout his career, most notably as a pilot and squadron leader with the RAF's Transport Command. He was appointed a Commander of the Order of the British Empire in 1986 for his service in Bosnia. Hetherington retired from the RAF in 1988 and died in 1998.
