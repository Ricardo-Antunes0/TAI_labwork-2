Kim Hyun-ah (born June 6, 1992), better known by her stage name Hyuna, is a South Korean singer and actress. She is a member of the K-pop girl group Girls' Generation.  Hyuna was born in Gwangju, South Korea on June 6, 1992. When she was three years old, her family moved to Seoul and she began taking singing and dancing lessons. Hyuna made her debut as a member of the girl group Girls' Generation in 2007. In October 2009, Hyuna married actor Lee Seung-gi. The couple divorced in March 2016.
