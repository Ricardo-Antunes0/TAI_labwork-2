Paulo Carcasci (born 7 January 1964 in Palermo) is an Italian criminal and mafia boss. He is the son of Antonio Carcasci, a member of the Sicilian Mafia.  Carcasci was a member of the Palermo crime family and was one of the most feared Mafia bosses in Italy. He is also known as "The Beast" or "The King of the Mountains". He is currently serving a life sentence for his involvement in a major Mafia conspiracy.
