"Gotta Move On" is a song by American recording artist Miley Cyrus. It was written by Cyrus, Max Martin, Shellback and Ali Payami, with production handled by the latter two. The song was released on November 18, 2014, by RCA Records as the lead single from her fourth studio album Bangerz (2014). "Gotta Move On" is a dance-pop and electropop song that lyrically discusses the protagonist trying to move on from a past relationship. Critical reception to "Gotta Move On" was mixed, with some reviewers deeming it as an improvement over Cyrus' previous efforts, while others found it unoriginal.
