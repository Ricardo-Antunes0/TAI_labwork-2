"The Calusari" is the twenty-first episode of the fourth season of the American science fiction television series "Battlestar Galactica".  The Cylons have finally found Earth and the Colonial Fleet. The humans mount a desperate defence, but they are outmatched. As the Cylons bring their battle machines down on the planet, Apollo and Starbuck lead a daring mission to destroy their mother ship. But even that may not be enough to save humanity.
