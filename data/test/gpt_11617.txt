Thomas Eastchurch (? - January, 1678) was an English politician who sat in the House of Commons from 1660 to 1678.  Eastchurch was the son of Sir Robert Eastchurch, 1st Baronet and his wife Elizabeth Molyneux, daughter of Sir Edward Molyneux. He was admitted at Lincoln's Inn on 13 June 1636 and became a barrister. In 1660, he was elected Member of Parliament for Hampshire. He was re-elected MP for Hampshire in 1661 and 1665. In 1668, he was appointed High Sheriff of Hampshire. He died at the age of 73 and was buried at Eastleigh on 9 January 1678.
