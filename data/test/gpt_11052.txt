The Hazelden Foundation is an American non-profit organization that helps people facing addiction and mental illness. The foundation was founded in 1949 by Dr. Bob Hazelden.  The Hazelden Foundation provides a variety of programs to help people with addiction and mental illness. These programs include residential treatment, outpatient treatment, support groups, and clinical research. The foundation also provides education and training for professionals who work with these populations.  The Hazelden Foundation is headquartered in Northbrook, Illinois. The foundation has offices in more than 40 countries around the world.
