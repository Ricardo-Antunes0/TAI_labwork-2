Beard attended the Professional Children's School in Manhattan before making her stage debut at the age of twelve in a production of Annie Get Your Gun.  After appearing in several Broadway productions during the 1940s, including The Girl Who Came to Supper and Peter Pan, Beard made her film debut in the role of Dorothy Gale opposite Mickey Rooney in the 1955 classic Oklahoma!
