HMS Pegasus was a 28-gun  sixth-rate ship of the line of the British Royal Navy, launched on 5 May 1779 at Woolwich Dockyard.   The ship participated in the Battle of Ushant on 27 July 1781 during the French Revolutionary Wars and was captured by the French. She was recaptured by the British on 16 August 1782. She was wrecked off the island of Ischia in 1803.
