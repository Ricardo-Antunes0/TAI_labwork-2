DeLundon (born DeLundon Jimel Spearman on July 2, 1988) is an American rapper and singer from Detroit, Michigan. He was signed to Interscope Records in 2013 and released his debut studio album, Take Care, in 2015. DeLundon has since toured extensively, supporting acts such as The Weeknd, Chance the Rapper, and Bryson Tiller.  DeLundon was born DeLundon Jimel Spearman on July 2, 1988, in Detroit, Michigan. He began rapping at the age of 12 and started making music in his spare time while attending high school. He met producer Knuckleboi at a local high school party and they quickly formed a songwriting partnership. In 2010, DeLundon self-released his first mixtape, The Shooter Mixtape. After hearing positive feedback from friends and family, DeLundon decided to send the mixtape to various record labels and received a publishing deal with Motown Republic Records in early 2013. Shortly after signing with Interscope Records, DeLundon released his first single "Good Life" featuring Rick Ross. The song peaked at number 38 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). DeLundon's debut studio album Take Care was released on September 25, 2015. The album peaked at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified gold by the RIAA.
