Kayhan Mortezavi ( ; born 9 June 1985) is an Iranian professional football player who currently plays for Naft Tehran in the Persian Gulf Pro League.  Mortezavi started his career with Saipa in the Azadegan League. He then transferred to Esteghlal Ahvaz and played in the Iran Pro League. In July 2009, he moved to Turkish club Gaziantepspor. In August 2010, he moved to Dutch club Excelsior Rotterdam. In January 2013, he transferred to Iranian side Sepahan. He was part of the Iran national football team at the 2014 FIFA World Cup and the 2015 AFC Asian Cup.
