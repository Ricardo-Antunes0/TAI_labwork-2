Maria Cristina Falls is a waterfall of the Santa Catarina River in the city of Joinville, in the Brazilian state of Santa Catarina. With a height of 74 metres (239 ft), it is the tallest waterfall in Brazil. The falls are named after Maria Cristina, second wife of King John VI of Portugal.
