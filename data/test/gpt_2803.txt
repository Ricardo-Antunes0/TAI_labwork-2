Midd-West School District (MWSD) is a midsized, public school district in north-central Ohio, serving the communities of Middlefield, Napoleon, and Wayne, as well as portions of Delta, Monroe, and Williams counties.  The Midd-West School District has a population of approximately 10,000 students and 1,000 employees. The district operates 11 schools with an enrollment of 3,600 students.  The Midd-West School District was formed in 1956 by the merger of the Napoleon and Wayne school districts. The districts had been established in 1913 and 1885 respectively.   MWSD is a member of the Ohio School Boards Association, the National School Boards Association, and the Ohio Association of Elementary Schools.
