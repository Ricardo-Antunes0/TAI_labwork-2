John Rankin Urquhart (29 May 1921 – 5 March 1995) was an Australian cricketer who played first-class cricket for Tasmania from 1945 to 1958. He was also a member of the Australian cricket team that won the 1954–55 Ashes series in England, and he played in all six Tests of that series.  Urquhart was a right-handed batsman and a right-arm off-break bowler. He was born in Launceston, Tasmania, and died in Hobart.
