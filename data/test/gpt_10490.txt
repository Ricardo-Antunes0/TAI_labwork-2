Balfron High School is a secondary school located in Balfron, a town in the North-East of England. It is a comprehensive school with around 1,600 students. The school was founded in 1875 and has undergone several name changes over the years:   Balfron High School was originally called Balfron County School, and was founded in 1875. The original school building was on School Lane, but in 1889 it moved to new premises on Lowergate. In 1912, the school became a grant-aided school. In 1965, the school became a comprehensive school.  In 1992, the school merged with Balfron Junior High School to form Balfron High School and Junior High School. In 2006, the school became an academy.
