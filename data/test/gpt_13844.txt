Mike Bushell is an English sports presenter and journalist. He is currently the main presenter of BT Sport's football coverage.  Born in Stevenage, Bushell began his broadcasting career at local radio station LBC in 1997, before moving to BBC Radio 5 Live in 2000. He became a regular pundit on the network's football coverage, and also presented its rugby programme. In 2007, he joined Sky Sports as a football reporter, and later became a co-presenter of their Premier League coverage.  In January 2014, Bushell was appointed as BT Sport's main presenter of football coverage, succeeding Colin Murray. He has since presented the Champions League, Europa League, FA Cup and League Cup finals, as well as the UEFA Euro 2016 and 2018 World Cup qualifiers.
