The Cybernetic Grandma (Czech: Kybernetická babička) is a Czech science fiction comedy film from 1990. It was directed by Jiri Menzel and stars Josef Kostelny, Irena Sedláčková and Vlastimil Brodka.  The film is set in the future, when elderly people are replaced by cyborgs. The main character, Jan, is a disturbed young man who becomes obsessed with tracking down his old cybergrandma, only to find out she has been replaced by a real one.
