Sir John Helier Le Rougetel  (19 November 1624 – 10 October 1693) was a Welsh nobleman and soldier who served as Governor of Jamaica.  John Le Rougetel was born in 1624, the son of Sir John Helier Le Rougetel and Anne Pryce. He served in the English army during the Second English Civil War, rising to the rank of captain. In 1660, he was appointed Governor of Jamaica, where he remained until his death in 1693. He was knighted in 1661 and made a baronet in 1672.  Le Rougetel married Lady Elizabeth Pierrepont, daughter of Edward Pierrepont, 1st Earl of Kingston-upon-Hull, in 1658. They had three children: John Le Rougetel, 2nd Baronet (1660–1712), Lady Elizabeth Le Rougetel (1662–1736), and Lady Catherine Le Rougetel (died young).
