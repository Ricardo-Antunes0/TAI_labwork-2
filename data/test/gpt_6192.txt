Carmilla is a Canadian single-frame web series created by Jessica Martin and produced by her company, Red Flamingo. The series is based on the 1871 novella Carmilla by Josef von Sternberg.  The show is set in a small town in Ontario in the early 1900s, and follows the lives of the Hunter family: father Joseph, mother Martha, daughter Laura, and son Charlie. The family is plagued by Carmilla, an enigmatic femme fatale who seems to be haunting them.   The series was first announced in February 2016, and premiered on YouTube on March 10, 2016.
