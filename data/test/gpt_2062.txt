Willard Harvey Wattenburg (February 9, 1936 – August 21, 2012) was an American film and television composer.  Wattenburg was born in Los Angeles, California. He began his career as a drummer and session musician before becoming a composer for film and television. His best-known works include the original score for the 1980s sitcom Happy Days, as well as the music for the popular animated series The Simpsons. He died at the age of 78 after a long illness.
