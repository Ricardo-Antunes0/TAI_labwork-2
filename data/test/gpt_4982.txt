Greenwich Heritage Centre was a museum and tourist attraction in Greenwich, London, England. The museum was closed on 31 March 2013.  The Greenwich Heritage Centre was founded in 1984 as a private museum by volunteers and operated on a trust basis until it was taken over by Greenwich Council in 2007. In 2013, the council announced that the museum would close at the end of the year due to budget cuts. The museum's collection focused on the history of Greenwich and Royal Navy Dockyards.
