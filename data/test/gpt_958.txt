Motithang Takin Preserve, located in the Motithang Valley of the Annapurna Conservation Area in Nepal, is one of the largest and most significant protected areas in the country.  The Motithang Valley is a remote and pristine region, east of the Annapurna Circuit, with undulating hills, deep gorges and meandering rivers. The area is home to a diversity of endangered wildlife including the snow leopard, blue sheep, musk deer and Himalayan tahr.
