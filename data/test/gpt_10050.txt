Ōikari Tsuyoshi (born 16 June 1972 as Tsuyoshi Ōikari in Kōchi Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese artist who specializes in installation art.  Ōikari Tsuyoshi was born on June 16, 1972 in Kōchi Prefecture, Japan. He is an artist who specializes in installation art. He graduated from the Tokyo National University of Fine Arts and Music in 1997.   Ōikari Tsuyoshi's works have been exhibited at the Mori Art Museum, the Yomiuri National Museum of Art, the Hiratsuka Museum of Art, and the Tokyo Metropolitan Art Museum. In 2006, he was awarded the prestigious Kyoto Prize for his art.
