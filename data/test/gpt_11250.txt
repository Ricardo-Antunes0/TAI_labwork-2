Balwaan  is a 1992 Indian Hindi-language romantic drama film directed by R. D. Burman. The film stars Aamir Khan, Madhuri Dixit, and Sanjay Dutt in lead roles. It was produced by Dutt's father and actor-director Sunil Dutt.  The film tells the story of a young man (Aamir Khan) who falls in love with an older woman (Madhuri Dixit). They are forced to part ways when Madhuri marries another man. A decade later, they meet again and fall in love again, but this time their relationship is not accepted by Madhuri's family. They eventually run away from home and marry in a private ceremony.  The film was well received by critics, who praised the performances of the cast and the screenplay. It became one of the highest-grossing Hindi films of 1992. Balwaan won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Hindi and the Filmfare Award for Best Screenplay.
