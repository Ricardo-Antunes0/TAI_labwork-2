Ryan Patrick Vail (born March 19, 1986) is an American professional ice hockey center who is currently playing with HC Davos of the National League (NL) of Switzerland.  Vail was drafted in the first round, 12th overall, by the Colorado Avalanche in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He played college hockey at the University of Denver.
