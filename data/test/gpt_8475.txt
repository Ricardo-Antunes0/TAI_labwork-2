Black Oak Conspiracy is a 1977 American Techno-thriller film directed by Michael Crichton. The screenplay was written by Richard Price, who also stars in the title role.  The film tells the story of a group of scientists who develop a technology that allows them to travel through time. They use this technology to attempt to prevent a terrorist attack that will take place on the U.S. Capitol Building. However, their actions inadvertently create a future in which the United States is ruled by a tyrannical government.
