The Pearls Naval Brigade was formed on December 12, 1914, in San Francisco, California, as a volunteer naval unit composed of local men eager to participate in the American war effort. The brigade was initially assigned to the Pacific Division of the United States Navy, and served in a variety of roles on both land and sea during World War I.
