Marzi was a Pakistani drama serial that aired on Geo TV from August 2, 2013 to October 22, 2013. The show is written by Bushra Ahmed and directed by Sohail Najmi.  Set in a rural valley in the early 1990s, Marzi follows the lives of the inhabitants of the small town of Marzi. The show is a drama about secrets, love, family, and loyalty.
