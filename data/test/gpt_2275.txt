Trowbridge Cricket Club is a cricket club located in Trowbridge, Wiltshire, England. The club was founded in 1872 and has played in the Minor Counties Championship since 1936.  The club has played at a number of grounds throughout its history, most notably Trowbridge Cricket Club Ground which has been its home since 1896. The ground has changed hands several times and currently holds 2,000 spectators. The club also fields a second team which plays in the West Wiltshire League.
