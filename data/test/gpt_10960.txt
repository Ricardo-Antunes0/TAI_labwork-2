Dr. Brown's is a brand of soft drinks that was founded in 1892 by Dr. John Brown. The company is headquartered in Springfield, Missouri and manufactures products under the Dr. Brown's, Schweppes, Squirt, and RC Cola brands. The company has a long history of producing popular soft drinks, including its most well-known product, Dr. Brown's Bottled Water. In addition to its soft drink line, the company produces fruit juice and energy drinks.
