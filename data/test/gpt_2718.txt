He was a member of the Bulgarian tennis team which competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris, France. He and his team-mate Ivan Todorov won the bronze medal in the men's doubles competition. Laxa also competed in the singles event, but did not progress past the first round. He died in 1940.
