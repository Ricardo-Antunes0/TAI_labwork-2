John Kannenberg (Milwaukee, WI) is a multimedia artist and educator.  Kannenberg's work addresses a wide range of subjects, from video art to performance art to spatial installations. He has exhibited throughout the United States and Europe, and has been the recipient of numerous awards, including a MacArthur Fellowship.  Kannenberg is currently the Thomas R. Kuhn Professor of Art at the University of Michigan, where he also serves as Director of the Center for Interdisciplinary Studies in Art and Media.
