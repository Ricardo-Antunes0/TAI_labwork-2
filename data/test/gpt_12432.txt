"Tails" is a horizontal-scrolling shooter that tasks the player with flying a spaceship through a series of enemy-filled levels. The player can fire both forward and backward, and can use weapons such as lasers and missiles to destroy enemies. The game's two-player mode allows players to battle each other cooperatively.  "Tails" was developed by Japanese game developer Tatsumi Kimishima, who had previously worked on the shooter game "Virtua Cop". "Tails" was published by Sega in 1995, and was the first game in the "Sega Saturn Super Hang-On" series. It was later ported to the Dreamcast in 2000, and to the PlayStation 2 in 2001.
