Albert "Ankles" Bennett (16 July 1944 – 2 January 2018) was an English professional footballer who played as a defender. He made more than 350 appearances in the Football League for Darlington, Bradford City, Sheffield United, Portsmouth and Coventry City. He also played in the NASL for the Atlanta Chiefs and the Canadian Soccer League for the Toronto Metros-Croatian club Scarborough SC.  Bennett was born in Darlington, County Durham, and began his career with hometown club Darlington. He moved to Bradford City in July 1966 for a fee of £10,000. He made his Bradford debut on 19 August 1966 in a 3–2 victory over Rotherham United. He went on to make 333 appearances for Bradford, scoring nine goals.  Bennett joined Sheffield United on loan in November 1969. He made his Sheffield United debut on 18 November 1969 in a 2–1 victory over Tranmere Rovers. He went on to make 33 appearances for the Blades, scoring one goal.  He joined Portsmouth in February 1971 for £35,000. He made his Portsmouth debut on 17 February 1971 in a 1–0 victory over Tottenham Hotspur. He went on to make 206 appearances for Portsmouth, scoring six goals.  He joined Coventry City in May 1974 for £25,000. He made his Coventry City debut on 16 May 1974 in a 1–0 victory over Plymouth Argyle. He went on to make 248 appearances for Coventry City, scoring eight goals.
