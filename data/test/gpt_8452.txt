Skänninge Abbey, also known as St. Ingrid's Abbey, is a former nunnery in Skänninge Municipality, Sweden. The abbey was founded in 1150 by Birger Jarl and his wife Ingrid of Roslagen. The present church was completed in 1495. The monastery was suppressed in 1536 and the property sold to the crown.   The abbey is a popular tourist attraction and the site of a yearly music festival.
