Ida Ekblad (born 1980) is a Norwegian artist.  Ekblad was born in Oslo, Norway in 1980. She grew up in the city of Trondheim and studied at the Norwegian National Academy of Fine Arts from 1999 to 2003. Ekblad has exhibited widely, including at the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Centre Pompidou, and the Museum of Modern Art in New York City. Her work has been featured in publications such as The New Yorker, Artforum, and The Paris Review.
