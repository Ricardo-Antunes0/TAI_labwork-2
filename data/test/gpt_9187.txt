Zoomerang is the name of a boomerang-shaped amusement ride located at Lake Compounce in Bristol, Connecticut, United States. It is a patented ride that spins riders around in a loop.  Zoomerang opened to the public on July 4, 2008. It was manufactured by Dutch manufacturer KMG Rides and is the flagship ride of the company's American operation. The ride is rated for a maximum rider weight of up to 275 pounds.
