Revive & Restore is a California-based nonprofit organization that specializes in restoring and maintaining historic buildings.  Revive & Restore was founded in 1994 by a group of architects, historians, and preservationists who were passionate about the preservation of historic buildings. The organization has since restored and maintained more than 60 historic buildings across California.  Revive & Restore is dedicated to providing quality restoration services to meet the specific needs of each individual building. The organization uses a variety of restoration techniques, including stabilization, restoration of original materials and finishes, and rehabilitation.  Revive & Restore is a member of the National Trust for Historic Preservation and the American Institute of Architects.
