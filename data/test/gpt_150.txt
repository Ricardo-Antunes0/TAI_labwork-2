HMS Bounty, also known as HM Armed Merchant Cruiser Bounty, was a Royal Navy 50-gun fifth-rate frigate, launched on 12 November 1759 at Deptford Dockyard. She served in the French and Indian War and the American War of Independence, capturing several prizes. She was sold in 1815.
