Jonas Paulavičius (1898–1952) was a Lithuanian carpenter, sculptor, and architect.  Born in 1898 in the town of Kaunas, Jonas Paulavičius began his career as a carpenter. In 1926 he moved to Vilnius, where he started sculpting and became known for his simple, geometric designs. He also started designing and building houses, public buildings, and furniture. In 1935 he was appointed head of the Vilnius City Planning Department. He died in 1952.
