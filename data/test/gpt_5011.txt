Stanislav Igolinsky (born 1954, in Saratov) is a Russian artist who lives and works in Moscow. Igolinsky is noted for his sculpture, painting and video art.  Stanislav Igolinsky was born in Saratov, Russia in 1954. He studied at the Saratov State Academy of Fine Arts from 1972 to 1978. Igolinsky has exhibited his work internationally, including at the Museum of Modern Art, New York; the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, New York; the Centre Georges Pompidou, Paris; the Museum of Contemporary Art, Chicago; and the National Gallery of Victoria, Melbourne. In 2002 he was awarded the prestigious Turner Prize.
