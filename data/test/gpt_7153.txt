New Legends is a 2002 action video game developed by Crystal Dynamics and published by Eidos. It is the sequel to the 1998 game Tomb Raider and is the fourth installment in the Tomb Raider series.  The game is set in the fictional island of Yamatai, which is home to a race of advanced humans known as the Lara Croft Species. The player assumes the role of Lara Croft, who must defeat a group of enemies in order to save her friends and return home.  The game received mixed reviews from critics, who praised its graphics but criticized its repetitiveness and storyline. It was commercially successful, selling over five million copies worldwide. A remastered version, titled Lara Croft and the Temple of Osiris, was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in January 2019.
