Vitória Futebol Clube do Riboque (Forro language: Riboque Olivesa Futebol Clube) is a Portuguese football club based in Riboque, a municipality within the district of Aveiro. The club was founded on 4 November 1928.  The club's stadium is the Estádio Municipal de Riboque, which has a capacity of 2,000 spectators. Vitória currently plays in the Terceira Divisão (Third Division), the fourth tier of Portuguese football.
