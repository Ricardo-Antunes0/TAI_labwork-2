Bright Angel Lodge is a hotel complex located in the Bright Angel National Monument in the Grand Canyon National Park, Arizona. The lodge is operated and managed by the National Park Service as a concessionaire.  The Bright Angel Lodge complex includes the Bright Angel Lodge, Bright Angel Campground, and the Phantom Ranch. The lodge was built in 1933 as a concessionaire operation of the Grand Canyon National Park and is one of the oldest continuously operated hotels in North America. The lodge is a popular tourist destination for visitors to the Grand Canyon and features a restaurant, bar, store, and guest rooms.
