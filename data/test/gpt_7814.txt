Arad International Airport  is located  in the city of Arad, in the north-western part of Romania. It is operated by Arad Airport Holding S.A., a subsidiary of the Government of Romania. The airport has been in operation since 1972 and has a total area of 1,500 hectares. In 2015, it handled 4.8 million passengers and 6.5 million tonnes of cargo.  Arad International Airport is one of the busiest airports in Romania, with a 12-year passenger growth rate of over 20%. The airport ranks first in terms of passenger traffic in the region and hosts flights to international destinations such as Frankfurt, Istanbul, Moscow, Vienna and Warsaw. It is also a major hub for Romanian carriers such as Tarom, Blue Air and Alitalia.
