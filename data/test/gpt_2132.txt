Kentucky Route 402 (KY 402) is a  state highway in the U.S. state of Kentucky. The highway runs from an intersection with KY 60 east of Pikeville to an intersection with KY 92 north of Prestonsburg.  KY 402 was designated in the early 1970s as a replacement for KY 22, which had been decommissioned in 1969. The highway initially ran from KY 60 east of Pikeville to KY 92 north of Prestonsburg. The route was extended to its current location in the early 1990s.
