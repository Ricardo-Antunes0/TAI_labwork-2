128 001, as registered at Deutsche Bahn, is a German standard class of electric locomotive. It is used on passenger trains in Germany and Austria.  The DB Class 128 was designed in the 1970s as an upgrade of the earlier DB Class 117. The locomotives are equipped with DBAG's modular traction system, which allows for easy replacement of individual components. They are also capable of running on both conventional and high-speed track.
