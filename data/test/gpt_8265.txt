Dumkibas  is a village development committee in Ramechhap District in the Janakpur Zone of south-eastern Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 4,513 people living in 785 individual households.   Dumkibas is located about 12Â km from Ramechhap town on the Ramechhap-Koteshwor highway. It has a population of about 4,500 people, mostly farmers and traders. The village is well connected with other parts of Nepal through road and rail networks. The village has a primary school, a health clinic and a post office. There are also temples and mosques in Dumkibas.
