Inetimi Timaya Odon  (born 15 August 1988) is a Nigerian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Italian club A.C. Milan and the Nigeria national team.  Timaya was born in Zaria, Nigeria on 15 August 1988 to a family of footballers. His father, former Nigerian international, Onyeka Onyeka, was his coach from an early age and helped him develop his skills.  After spending time playing for several Nigerian youth teams, Timaya made his senior debut for Enyimba International in the Nigerian Premier League in 2006. He then signed for Portuguese side Benfica in 2007 and spent two seasons there, before joining A.C. Milan in 2009.  Timaya has won several trophies with Milan, including the Serie A title in 2011 and the Coppa Italia in 2013. He has also represented Nigeria at various international tournaments, including the 2010 World Cup and the 2014 African Cup of Nations.  On 25 March 2016, Timaya was banned from playing for six months after testing positive for a banned substance during UEFA Champions League play against FC Copenhagen.
