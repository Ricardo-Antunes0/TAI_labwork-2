Matisse & Sadko is a Russian DJ and production duo consisting of Matisse (born Mikhail Sadko) and Dj Pasha (born Pavel Pashkovsky).   Matisse & Sadko's music is a mix of Eurodance, house and techno, with the duo frequently using elements of IDM and dubstep. They have released two studio albums, "Fly" (2005) and "In Motion" (2008), as well as several singles. Their song "Axe" was featured in the video game FIFA 09.
