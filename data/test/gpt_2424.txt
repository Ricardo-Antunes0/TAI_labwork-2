Wainwright Bank was a nationally recognized socially responsible bank in the United States.  The bank was founded in 1909 by Preston Wainwright, Sr. and operated until its acquisition by BB&T in 1998. Wainwright Bank was a pioneer in the field of community banking and was known for its environmental and social responsibility initiatives.  The bank's legacy includes its support of numerous charitable organizations, its investments in sustainable businesses, and its dedication to providing quality financial products and services to its customers.  Today, Wainwright Bank is part of BB&T, one of the nation's largest banking institutions. The bank continues to focus on its social responsibility mission and provides financial products and services that help improve the lives of local communities.
