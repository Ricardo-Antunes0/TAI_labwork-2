Travis Smith (born February 26, 1970) is an American painter and sculptor.  Travis Smith was born on February 26, 1970, in Reading, Pennsylvania. He was raised in the small town of Lewistown, Pennsylvania, and attended Lewistown Area High School. After high school, Smith studied at the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts in Philadelphia, graduating in 1994.   Smith's early works were figurative paintings and sculptures that explored the relationship between people and the environment. He later began making abstract paintings and sculptures that use natural materials such as stone and wood. His work has been exhibited in galleries across the United States and in Europe. In 2013, Smith was awarded a Guggenheim Fellowship.
