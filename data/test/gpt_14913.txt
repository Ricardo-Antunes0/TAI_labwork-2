The Kibaale Community Schools are Christian schools located in the Kibaale District of the Western Province of Uganda.     The schools were founded in 1984 by the Anglican Church of Uganda, and offer primary, junior secondary, and senior secondary educational programmes.     The schools are divided into five campuses, and have a total enrollment of 1,200 students.   The Kibaale Community Schools are known for their strong academic performance, and have produced many successful students who have gone on to pursue higher education degrees in universities across Uganda.
