Ndabezinhle Sibanda Sigogo was a Zimbabwean writer, playwright, and political activist.  Sigogo was born in Mberengwa, Zimbabwe in 1933. She studied at the University of Zimbabwe, where she obtained a degree in English literature. Sigogo began her writing career in the 1960s, publishing her first novel, The Great Zimbabwe, in 1966. Sigogo's work addresses themes of colonialism and post-colonialism, as well as the challenges faced by women in African societies. She has also written plays, poetry, and children's books. Sigogo served as president of the Zimbabwe Writers Association from 1978 to 1982 and was a member of the Zimbabwean Academy of Literature. She died in Harare in 1998.
