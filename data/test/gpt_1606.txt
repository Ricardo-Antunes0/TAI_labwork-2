William Workman (born February 4, 1940, Valdosta, Georgia) is an American opera baritone. Workman was born in Valdosta, Georgia, the son of Jeannette (n√©e Peck) and William Workman, a career soldier. He attended the University of Georgia where he was a member of the Phi Kappa Psi fraternity. He began his professional career as a baritone with the Atlanta Opera in 1965. He has since appeared at most of the leading opera houses in the United States and Europe, including the Royal Opera House, Covent Garden, La Scala, Teatro alla Scala, Teatro San Carlo in Naples, Royal National Theatre and Deutsche Oper Berlin. He has also made numerous recordings both for commercial labels and on his own label, William Workman Productions.   Workman has been married twice. His first marriage was to soprano Patricia Racette in 1968; they divorced in 1978. In 1984 he married mezzo-soprano Joyce DiDonato, with whom he has two children.
