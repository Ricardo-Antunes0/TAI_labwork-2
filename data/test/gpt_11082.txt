The Albany Academy is an independent college preparatory school located in Albany, New York, United States. It was founded in 1816 and has a current enrollment of approximately 1,000 students in grades 9-12.  The Albany Academy was founded in 1816 by the Presbyterian Church as an educational institution for young men. The Academy has been continuously operated by the same family for over 175 years and is now one of the largest independent college preparatory schools in the United States. The Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes the development of critical thinking, problem solving, communication, and leadership skills.  The Albany Academy is a selective school that accepts only students who have achieved a high academic standard. The Academy's admission process is based on an application process and a admissions interview. The Academy's student body is composed of students from throughout New York State and from around the world.
