The Winston Wildcats were an indoor football team based in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. The Wildcats played in the Professional Indoor Football League (PIFL) from 2001 to 2003.  The Winston Wildcats were one of the original eight franchises in the PIFL. The Wildcats were led by head coach Rick Majerus and played their home games at the Wake Forest University Sports Center.  The Wildcats finished second in the league standings in their first season, but they struggled in their second season, finishing with a record of 4-10. However, they rebounded in 2003, winning nine games to finish second in the league standings. However, they were unable to win the league championship, losing to the eventual champion Columbus Lions in the championship game.  The Winston Wildcats folded following the 2003 season.
