National Testing Agency (NTA) is an Indian government-owned organization, responsible for conducting national level examinations in various subjects. The NTA was established on 1 October 2002, through the National Testing Agency Act, 2002.  The NTA is a statutory body under the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. It has its headquarters at New Delhi and a regional office at Mumbai.  The NTA conducts examinations in various subjects such as mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, economics, political science and English language. These examinations are used to measure the learning and development of students across the country. The NTA also provides training to examinees and conducts research on examination reforms.
