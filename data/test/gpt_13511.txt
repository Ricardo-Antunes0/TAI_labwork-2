Ilus Vay (born Ilona Horváth; February 1923 – October 25, 2006) was a Hungarian actress.  Ilus Vay was born in Budapest, Hungary in February 1923. She started her career as a child actress and appeared in over 100 films before retiring in the early 1980s.  Vay died on October 25, 2006 in Budapest, aged 84.
