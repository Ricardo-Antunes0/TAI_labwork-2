"Duet" is the seventeenth episode of the fourth season of the American television series The Flash, and the ninety-ninth episode overall. It aired on October 12, 2017 on The CW.  In this episode, Barry Allen (Grant Gustin) and Kid Flash (Keiynan Lonsdale) team up to take on an alien threat.  After a failed attempt to reach out to his old friend Eddie Thawne (Rick Cosnett), Barry Allen reaches out to his former mentor Dr. Harrison Wells (Tom Cavanagh) for help in tracking down an alien threat. Wells agrees to help but warns Barry that the speed force may not be enough to stop the aliens.   Barry and Kid Flash team up to take on the alien threat and are successful in stopping them. However, Barry realizes that he may have permanently damaged the Speed Force and is now unable to travel at super speeds anymore.
