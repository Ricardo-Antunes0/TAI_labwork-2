Shadow Force is a documentary television miniseries that aired on the History Channel in the United States from July 5 to July 11, 2009. The miniseries consists of 10 episodes and is based on the novel Shadow Force by David Morrell. The series tells the story of a team of Special Forces soldiers who are sent into battle against an insurgent group led by a mysterious figure known only as "The Ghost".
