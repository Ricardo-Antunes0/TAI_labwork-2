"Fustani"  is a song recorded by Pakistani singer-songwriter Shahid Khanzada. It was released on 20 October 2018 as the lead single from his upcoming album "Inshallah".  "Fustani" is a mid-tempo ballad about the love between two people. The song was written by Shahid Khanzada and produced by Ali Noor.   The music video for "Fustani" was directed by Ali Noor and premiered on YouTube on 20 October 2018.
