Abigail Elizabeth Smith, (born January 16, 1995) better known by her stage name Yebba, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. She released her debut mixtape, The Life of Yebba, in 2016. The following year, she released her first studio album, I Am Yebba.  Yebba was born in Atlanta, Georgia on January 16, 1995. She is of Ethiopian and Cuban descent. She began singing and writing songs at a young age and began performing as a solo artist in 2015. That year, she released her debut mixtape, The Life of Yebba. The mixtape featured guest appearances from Chance the Rapper, Quavo, and Lil Uzi Vert.  In 2016, Yebba released her first studio album, I Am Yebba. The album features guest appearances from Chance the Rapper, Quavo, and Lil Uzi Vert. It was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).  Yebba has performed at festivals such as Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival and Lollapalooza. In 2018 she appeared on the season finale of the reality television series Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta. That year she also released the single "In My Feelings".
