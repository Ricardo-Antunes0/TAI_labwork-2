Manoj Kumar Jha is an Indian politician who is currently a member of the Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Indian Parliament. He represents the Jharkhand constituency in the state of Jharkhand.  Jha was born on 6 February 1962 in Giridih, Jharkhand. After completing his schooling from Giridih College (now Giridih University) and a B.A. from Ranchi University, he pursued an LL.B. from the University of Delhi. He started his legal career as an advocate in 1987 and became a judge in the Jharkhand High Court in 2002.  Jha was first elected to the Rajya Sabha in 2009 as a candidate of the Indian National Congress. He has served as the chairperson of various parliamentary committees, including those on Labour and Employment, Coal and Steel, Personnel, Public Accounts Committee, Law and Justice and Home Affairs.
