Portela (Ret.), (born November 3, 1941) is a retired United States Army officer who served as the Deputy Commanding General for Training from January 2006 to September 2007.  Portela received his commission through the Officer Candidate School at Fort Benning and subsequently completed Infantry and Armor ranger training at Fort Benning and The Infantry School at Fort Benning. He served in a number of staff positions, including Operations Officer and Chief of Staff for the 1st Infantry Division (Mechanized), before assuming command of the 2nd Brigade, 1st Infantry Division (Mechanized) in 1995.  He was then appointed Deputy Commanding General for Training, serving in that position until his retirement in 2007. During this time, he also served as the commander of the Combined Arms Center and School at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas.
