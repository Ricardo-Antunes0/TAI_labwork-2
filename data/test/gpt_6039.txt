A Pell Grant is a subsidy the United States Government provides to students of higher education. The maximum Pell Grant award is $5,550 for the 2009–10 academic year. Pell Grants are distributed by the Department of Education to students who have demonstrated financial need.    The Pell Grant program was created in 1965 as part of the Higher Education Act of 1965.
