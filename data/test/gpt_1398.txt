Kill It Kid are an English alternative rock band from Manchester. The band was formed in late 2007 by lead singer and guitarist Jamie Urwin, drummer Anthony Foley, and bassist James Davenport.  Kill It Kid's debut self-titled album was released in 2009 and reached number one on the UK Albums Chart. The album was later certified Platinum by the British Phonographic Industry. The band's second album, "KILL", was released in 2011 and reached number 10 on the UK Albums Chart. In 2012, Kill It Kid supported Muse on their UK tour.   In 2013, Kill It Kid announced they were working on their third album. The album, titled "The Dream", was released in September 2014 and reached number 8 on the UK Albums Chart.   Kill It Kid have supported Muse again on their 2015 tour of the UK and Ireland. In October 2015, they announced they were working on their fourth studio album. The album, which is titled "The Awakening", was released on 7 October 2018.
