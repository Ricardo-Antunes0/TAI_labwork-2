The Luoyang–Zhanjiang railway or Luozhan railway , is a railway line in Henan province, China. The line runs from Luoyang to Zhanjiang. The total length of the line is 427 kilometres. The railway opened in December 2009.  The Luoyang–Zhanjiang railway is a important transportation infrastructure linking the provinces of Henan and Zhejiang, and has great potential to promote economic development in both provinces. The railway will help reduce travel time between Luoyang and Zhanjiang from three hours to one hour, and provide easier access for tourists and businessmen travelling between the two provinces.
