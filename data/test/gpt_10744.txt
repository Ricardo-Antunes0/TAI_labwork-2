The Subaru Vivio is a kei car made by the Japanese car manufacturer Subaru. The Vivio was first shown at the Tokyo Motor Show in 1999 and was released in Japan in 2000. It is based on the Subaru Impreza platform and is available as a five-door hatchback, four-door sedan, or two-door coupe. The Vivio was facelifted for the 2007 model year, with a new front end and interior design. A convertible version, called the Outback Sport, debuted in 2011.
