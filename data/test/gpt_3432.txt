Palmbomen is the stage name of Amsterdam, Netherlands-based DJ and producer Tom Kaulitz.  As a teenager, Kaulitz listened to house and techno music, which deeply influenced his sound as a DJ and producer. He started making his own music in 2002, and released his first EP in 2006. In 2009, he released his debut album, which reached number one on the Dutch album chart. His second album, "Release," was released in 2014 and reached the top spot on the Dutch album chart.  Kaulitz has toured extensively throughout Europe, North America, and Asia, playing at major festivals such as Amsterdam Dance Event (ADE), Tomorrowland, Ultra Music Festival, and Creamfields.
