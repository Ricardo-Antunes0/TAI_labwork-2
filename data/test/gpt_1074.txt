"Wasted Youth Brew" is a compilation album by American rapper and producer MF DOOM. It was released on December 12, 2014, by Lex Records. The album features guest appearances from Pharoahe Monch, Mos Def, Earl Sweatshirt, Charlie Brown, and Conway The Vamp.   The album was announced in November 2014 and was preceded by the single "Contraptionz".  "Wasted Youth Brew" received generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 73%, based on nine reviews. Ben Ratliff of "The New York Times" stated that "the beats are uniformly spooky and inventive", and praised the guest appearances from Pharoahe Monch and Mos Def. Jon Caramanica of "The New York Times" said that the album "shows a more expansive side of MF DOOM" than his previous releases.
