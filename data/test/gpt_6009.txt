Murderers & Robbers is the first compilation album by the American heavy metal band Megadeth. It was released on November 15, 1992, through Capitol Records. The album features material from the band's first four studio albums: Killing Is My Business... and Business Is Good! (1988), Rust in Peace (1990), Peace Sells... but Who's Buying? (1992), and So Far, So Good... So What! (1993). It also includes two new tracks, "Trust" and "Fear".  The album was remastered and reissued on December 11, 2009, through Capitol Records. It featured six bonus tracks, including three live tracks from the 1991 Peace Sells... but Who's Buying? Tour, as well as a cover of the Judas Priest song "Hell Bent for Leather".  The album has been certified Platinum by the RIAA.
