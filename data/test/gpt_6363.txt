Colin Murray (born 10 March 1977) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Murray began his career with Dundee United, making over 200 appearances for the club before moving to Rangers in 2002. He spent two seasons at Ibrox, before joining Aberdeen in 2004. Murray spent four seasons with the Dons, winning the Scottish Premier League and Scottish Cup twice, before leaving in 2008 to join Kilmarnock.
