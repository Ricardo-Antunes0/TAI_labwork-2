He attended the University of Ghana, Legon, where he earned a degree in political science in 1975. Kafui worked as a journalist for several Ghanaian newspapers before founding the influential weekly newspaper "The Guardian" in 1989. He served as its editor-in-chief until his death in 2014. Kafui also authored several books, including a biography of Kwame Nkrumah.
