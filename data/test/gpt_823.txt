ESPN NBA 2K5 is a 2004 basketball video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It is the fifth installment in the ESPN NBA 2K series.  The game was released on November 4, 2004 for the PlayStation 2, Xbox, and Microsoft Windows. It was also released on the GameCube as part of a compilation with NBA Live 2004 and NBA Street 2004. The game features updated graphics and commentary from Rick Majerus, Hubie Brown, and Jeff Van Gundy.  Following the release of "NBA 2K5", Electronic Arts announced that it would be releasing a re-release of "NBA Live 2004" with updated graphics and commentary from Majerus, Brown, and Van Gundy.
