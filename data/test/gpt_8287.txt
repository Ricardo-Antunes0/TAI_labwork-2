Daryl McMahon (born 10 October 1983) is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Scottish Premiership club Hearts.  McMahon started his career with Scottish Premier League side Celtic, before moving to Rangers in 2003. He spent three seasons with the Ibrox club before joining Derby County in 2007. He spent two seasons with Derby, before joining Portsmouth in 2010. He spent two seasons with Portsmouth, before joining Hearts in 2013. McMahon has made over 190 appearances for Hearts, scoring 16 goals.
