Andebu is a village in Sandefjord municipality, Norway. The village is located on the southeastern shore of the Oslofjord, about northeast of the city of Sandefjord. The population (2013) was 511.  The village is named after the old Andebu farm, which was first mentioned in 1593. The farm was originally located at the mouth of the Andebu River, but it later moved upriver to its current location.  Andebu Church is located in the village. It dates from 1874 and it is a wooden church.
