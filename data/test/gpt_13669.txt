Jefferson Starship is an American rock band that formed in San Francisco in 1971. The band's original lineup consisted of singer/guitarist John Paul Jones, bassist/vocalist Grace Slick, drummer Skip Spence and keyboardist/vocalist David Freiberg.  Jefferson Starship's first album, "Jefferson Airplane!", was released in 1969 and featured the hit single "White Rabbit". The band went on to release eight more albums before splitting up in 1984. Jefferson Starship reunited in 1997 and has since released three more albums.
