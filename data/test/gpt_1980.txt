Johann von Böber or Boeber or Johann Boeber (fl. 1516-1590) was a Swiss author, printer and publisher.  He was born in Böbingen, in the canton of Thurgau, and died in Basel. He was a grandson of Johann von Böbingen, and a nephew of Ulrich von Böbingen.  He was educated at the University of Basel, where he studied law. He then became a printer and publisher, first in Basel and then in Zürich. He is best known for his 1615 book "NOVA et VIRGINIA", a history of Switzerland written in Latin.
