The River is an extended play written and recorded by English singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran. It was released on September 24, 2018, through Atlantic Records.   The recording sessions for the album took place between March and July 2018 in various locations in London, England. The album's lead single, "River", was released on June 8, 2018. "Castle on the Hill" and "Shape of You" were released as follow-up singles.  "The River" received positive reviews from music critics, who praised Sheeran's vocals and lyrical content. It debuted at number one on the UK Albums Chart and became his sixth consecutive number one album in the country. The album has sold over 1 million copies in the UK as of December 2018.
