Rocco's Dinner Party is an American reality television series that premiered on January 8, 2018, on the Bravo network. The series chronicles the lives of Rocco DiSpirito, his wife Lisa and their four children as they host a dinner party for their friends and family.  Rocco DiSpirito is an American chef and restaurateur who has been the head chef at several notable New York City restaurants, most notably La Grenouille and DiVine. He is best known for his televised cooking shows, which have aired on the Food Network since 2002.  Lisa DiSpirito is an Italian-born actress and model who has appeared in several television series and films. She is married to Rocco DiSpirito and together they have four children.
