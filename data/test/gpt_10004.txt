Bathukamma is a flowers festival celebrated predominantly in the Indian state of Kerala. The festival is also observed in parts of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. The word "bathukamma" is a Malayalam word meaning "eight days of flowers".   The festival commemorates the victory of the goddess Parvati over the demon Mahishasura. On the final day of the festival, a ceremonial procession is held in honour of the goddess. The participants carry pots of water on their heads and throw flower petals at the statue of the goddess.
