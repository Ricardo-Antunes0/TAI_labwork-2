Isla Bella and her sister ship Perla are two of the largest privately owned islands in the world. The Isla Bella was purchased by billionaire Carlos Slim in 2002 and he began extensive renovations and renovations which were completed in 2006. The Isla Bella is a luxury resort with over 100 rooms, 6 restaurants, a private beach, and 5 pools.   The Isla Bella is also home to the Carlos Slim Foundation, which focuses on fighting diseases in developing countries. The Isla Bella has made significant contributions to AIDS research and efforts to improve healthcare in Mexico.
