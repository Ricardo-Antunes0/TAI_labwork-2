Korn Chatikavanij (, , born 19 February 1981 in Bangkok, Thailand) is a Thai former professional boxer who held the IBF super middleweight title from 2007 to 2009.  Korn Chatikavanij was born on 19 February 1981 in Bangkok, Thailand. He began boxing at the age of 12 and won a bronze medal at the Southeast Asian Games in 1998. After turning professional, Chatikavanij won the WBC interim super middleweight title in 2002 and then won the IBF super middleweight title in 2007 by defeating Juergen Braehmer. He defended the title twice before losing it to Lucian Bute in 2009. After the Bute fight, Chatikavanij retired from boxing.
