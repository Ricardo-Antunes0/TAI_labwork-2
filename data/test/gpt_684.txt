Stella Heinsheimer Freiberg (November 29, 1862 - October 15, 1938) was a German physician and the first woman to receive a doctorate in medicine from the University of Berlin.  Stella Heinsheimer Freiberg was born on November 29, 1862 in Freiberg, Saxony-Anhalt, Germany to Jewish parents. She studied medicine at the University of Berlin and received her doctorate in 1885. She worked as a doctor in Berlin and Dresden before moving to Vienna in 1893. She returned to Berlin in 1906 and retired in 1932. She died on October 15, 1938 in Berlin.
