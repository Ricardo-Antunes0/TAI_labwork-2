Chris Caruana (born 18 March 1971) is a Maltese chess grandmaster. He has won six international tournaments, including the 2002 European Chess Championship.  Born in Valletta, Malta, Caruana played in his first international tournament at the age of twelve, and achieved his first grandmaster title at the age of twenty-one. He has since won six international tournaments, including the 2002 European Chess Championship, and finished second twice (2000 and 2004). He represented Malta in eleven Chess Olympiads, from 1992 to 2006.
