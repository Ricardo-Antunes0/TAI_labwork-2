Cryptolechia diplosticha is a moth in the Depressariidae family. It was described by Edward Meyrick in 1907. It is found in Peru.  The wingspan is about 17 mm. The forewings are light grey with the costal edge suffused light fuscous, the basal third more suffused dark fuscous. There is a small light grey spot near the base, another near the middle and a third at three-fifths, these marked with blackish scales. The hindwings are pale grey.
