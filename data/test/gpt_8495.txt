Portogloboviridae is a family of DNA viruses.  Portogloboviridae is a family of DNA viruses. They are notable for their ability to infect both plant and animal hosts. The family is divided into two genera, Portoglobovirus and potexviruses. Portoglobovirus is the more widespread genus, containing the majority of known viruses. Potexviruses are only known from a few species of flowering plants.
