The Challenge Tour is a series of professional golf tournaments played in Europe. It was founded in 1989 and is organised by the European Tour. The tour consists of five tournaments, three of which are played in the United Kingdom and two in Continental Europe. The Challenge Tour events are played on courses that are either designated or co-designated as Challenge golf courses by the European Tour.  The Challenge Tour features a number of high-quality events that provide opportunities for players to earn ranking points and qualify for the European Tour's biggest tournaments, including the PGA Championship and The Masters. In 2018, the top five players on the Challenge Tour Rankings earned invitations to play in The Open Championship, one of golf's four major championships.
