"iSpy" is the 14th episode of the 5th season of the American sitcom "Modern Family", and the 87th episode overall. It was aired on October 5, 2014.  In this episode, Phil (Ty Burrell) and Claire (Julie Bowen) work on a new project with their kids, while Jay (Ed O'Neill) and Gloria (Sofia Vergara) take a vacation in Mexico to reconnect. Mitchell (Jesse Tyler Ferguson) tries to find new ways to entertain his daughter Lily (Aubrey Anderson-Emmons), while Alex (Eric Stonestreet) and Cam (Eric Stonestreet) continue their search for a new place to live.  The episode was written by Steven Levitan and directed by Beth McCarthy-Miller.
