Greererpeton burkemorani ("crawler from Greer, West Virginia") is an extinct genus of neoceratopsian dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period, about 76 to 73 million years ago. It was a small, lightly built dinosaur with a long neck and tail. It had a small head and relatively large eyes.
