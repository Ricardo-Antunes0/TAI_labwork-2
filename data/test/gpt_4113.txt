The Iowa Barnstormers are a professional indoor football team based in Des Moines, Iowa. The team is a member of the Indoor Football League (IFL).  The Iowa Barnstormers were founded in 2009 and played their inaugural season in 2010. The team plays its home games at the Wells Fargo Arena in Des Moines.  The Iowa Barnstormers are one of the most successful franchises in indoor football. The team has made it to the IFL playoffs eight times, including five appearances in the IFL championship game. The Barnstormers have also won five division championships and three conference championships.
