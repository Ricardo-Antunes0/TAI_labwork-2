Gunnar Sønstevold (26 November 1912 – 18 November 1994) was a Norwegian jurist and politician for the Labour Party.  He served as Minister of Justice in the Cabinet of Einar Gerhardsen from 1955 to 1957, as Minister of Defence from 1957 to 1961, and as Minister of Social Affairs from 1961 to 1965. He also served as a member of the Standing Committee on Justice and Civil Affairs from 1959 to 1975.  Sønstevold was born in Kristiania, and graduated from the University of Oslo with the cand.jur. degree in 1934. He started his career as a lawyer in Oslo, and was also involved in politics there. He chaired the Oslo city council from 1951 to 1953, and was deputy mayor from 1951 to 1953. He served as a deputy representative to the Norwegian Parliament from Oslo during the term 1945–1949.
