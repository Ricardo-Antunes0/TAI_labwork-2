Ada Christen, also known as Ada Carla, (born November 27, 1952, in Antwerp, Belgium) is a Belgian singer and actress.  Christen started her career as a singer in the early 1970s in the Belgian group Los Dakotas. She later moved to France, where she enjoyed some success as a pop singer. In 1984 she released her first solo album, which spawned the hit single "Je t'aime". The following year she starred in the French film adaptation of the Anne Rice novel "Interview with a Vampire".
