KKOY-FM (105.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Kingman, Arizona, United States, it serves the Kingman area. The station is currently owned by Cumulus Media.  KKOY-FM first went on the air in 1978 as KKOY-FM 105.5. The station was owned by Arizona Broadcasting Company and was originally a Top 40 station playing hits from the 1970s through the 1990s. In 1998, the station changed its format to classic rock and became known as "The Rock of Kingman".
