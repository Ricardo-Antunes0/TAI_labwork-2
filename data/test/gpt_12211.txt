Northern Tool + Equipment (commonly referred to as Northern Tool), is a Canadian-owned and operated manufacturer of hand and power tools, specializing in the construction, forestry, mining, automotive, manufacturing and agricultural industries. The company has production facilities in Edmonton and Red Deer, Alberta.  Northern Tool was founded in Edmonton in 1935 by Ludwig Friesen and his brother-in-law Erich Koenig. The company became publicly traded on the Toronto Stock Exchange in 1978. In 1984, Northern Tool acquired the Canadian division of The Stanley Works. In 1996, Northern Tool acquired the Australian division of Schuler Tools Ltd. In 1997, Northern Tool acquired the U.S. division of AEG Manufacturing Co., Ltd.  Northern Tool has over 6,000 employees across Canada, the United States and Australia.
