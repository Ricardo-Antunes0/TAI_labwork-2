The Time Tunnel is an American color science fiction television series that aired on ABC from 1967 to 1969. The show was created by Rod Serling, and was based on the short story "The Time Tunnel" by H.G. Wells.  The series follows a team of scientists who travel through time using a portal created by Professor George T. Wilbur. The team is usually tasked with saving humanity from various dangers, often using the knowledge and technology that Wilbur has acquired in his travels.  The show received mixed reviews from critics, but it was popular with audiences and has since been re-aired numerous times. It has also been released on DVD several times.
