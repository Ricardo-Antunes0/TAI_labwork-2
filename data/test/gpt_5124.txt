RedruM 781 (born Jermaine Carter) (July 22, 1978) is an American rapper, singer and songwriter. He first gained attention as a member of the rap group Black Moon, with whom he released two critically acclaimed albums, 1998's The Unseen and 2001's Enta Da Stage. In 2006, he began his solo career with the release of his debut album, O.Y.G., which spawned the singles "I'm Different" and "Put It On Me". His second album, Street King Immortal, was released in 2009 and featured the hit singles "Boom Boom" and "Murder Was The Case".
