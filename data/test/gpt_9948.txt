Pestilence is a fictional supervillain appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He has a blue skin color, insectoid features, and is usually depicted as wearing a cloak and mask.  Pestilence first appeared in The X-Men #101 (March 1971), and was created by Chris Claremont and John Byrne.  Pestilence is one of the most frequently appearing X-Men villains, typically teaming up with other villains such as Apocalypse, Magneto, and Sabretooth. He has also been a member of other teams such as the Hellfire Club and the Morlocks.  One of Pestilence's most common methods of attack is via his ability to emit a deadly virus that can kill almost anyone within seconds. He has also been known to generate powerful gusts of wind, fire blasts from his fingertips, and project powerful waves of energy.
