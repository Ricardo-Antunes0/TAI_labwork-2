In certain sports, a magic number is a specific number or combination of numbers that is believed to be essential to achieving a desired result. In American football, for example, the magic number is 7. In basketball, it is 15. In hockey, it is 3. As such, these numbers are often spoken about and obsessively pursued by teams and players alike.  The magic number has been credited with influencing the outcomes of sporting events for centuries. And while its true effect is often debated, there is no doubt that it has had an impact on the game of sport.
