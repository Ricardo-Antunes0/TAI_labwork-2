Beardsley School District, in Bakersfield, California, traces its history back to 1872 when the first school was built on the current site. The district has grown steadily since then, adding new buildings and annexations as its population and needs have increased.  Today, the Beardsley School District serves more than 3,000 students in pre-kindergarten through eighth grade, making it one of the largest districts in Kern County. The district's nine schools provide a comprehensive education for students ranging in socioeconomic backgrounds, with a focus on preparing them for college and career success.  The Beardsley School District is committed to providing a quality education for all of its students. In addition to offering a wide range of programs and services, the district emphasizes instruction that is challenging and engaging for pupils of all ages. The district's staff members are highly qualified and dedicated to providing a quality education for their students.
