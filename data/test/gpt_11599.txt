John Christopher Antonio "JC" Intal (born November 13, 1975) is a Filipino-American retired professional basketball player who last played for the Rio Grande Valley Vipers of the NBA Development League. Intal played college basketball for the University of San Francisco.  Intal was originally drafted by the Toronto Raptors in the second round of the 1997 NBA draft but he never played in the NBA. He played in Spain, Italy, and Greece before signing with the Vipers in 2009. Intal has also played for the Philippine national basketball team.
