Keiko Fukuda was born on October 2, 1954, in Tule Lake, California. She began training in taekwondo at the age of six, and became the first woman to achieve a black belt in taekwondo in 1977. Fukuda was also the first woman ever to be inducted into the International Taekwondo Federation Hall of Fame. Fukuda died on February 15, 2014, at the age of 65.
