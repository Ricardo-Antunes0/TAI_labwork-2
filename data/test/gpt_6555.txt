Hans Wiegel (; born 16 July 1941) is a German politician and Member of the European Parliament (MEP) from Germany. He is a member of the Christian Democratic Union, part of the European People's Party.  Wiegel was born in Freiberg, Saxony-Anhalt. He has been a member of the Christian Democratic Union since 1972 and has been an MEP since 1989. He has been president of the German branch of the EPP since 2006.
