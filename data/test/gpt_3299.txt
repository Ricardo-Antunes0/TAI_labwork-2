Compaq SLT was a line of laptop computers produced by Compaq from 2002 to 2006. The models in the line were the S series, the L series, and the XL series.  The Compaq SLT line was aimed at business users and was based on the Intel Core 2 Duo processors. All models featured a 15 inch screen, a DVD-RW drive, and a digitizer touch screen.
