ASTR is the solo project of American musician Christopher Bear. He is best known as the frontman and primary songwriter of the bands Grizzly Bear and Midnight Organ Fight, as well as a frequent collaborator with David Byrne.  ASTR first emerged in early 2005 as a solo project of Christopher Bear. After releasing a self-titled album in 2007, Bear released his second album, The Golden State, in 2009. It was followed by the EP Fading Frontier in 2010 and the full-length album Nocturne in 2011. In 2013, he released the album Plains, which was his first release on 4AD. The album was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album. In 2015, he released the album Black Mile Highway, which was his first release on Modular Recordings. The album was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album.
