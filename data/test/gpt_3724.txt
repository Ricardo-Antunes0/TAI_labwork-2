Narayana Swamy (born 28 December 1943), known professionally as Srinath or Srinath Raghavan, is an Indian film actor and producer who has appeared in over 250 films in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Hindi. He is best known for his roles as a villain in Tamil and Telugu film industries.  Srinath was born in a family of actors and started his career as a theatre artist. He made his acting debut in the 1979 Tamil film "Nirupama", directed by K. Balachander. He has since appeared in over 250 films in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada and Hindi. He received the National Film Award for Best Actor for his performance in the 2007 Telugu film "Nuvvu Naaku Nenu".
