Cogent Partners is a boutique, private equity-focused venture capital firm. The firm invests in technology and software startups, with a focus on companies that are solving pressing global problems.  Cogent Partners was founded in 2006 by a group of experienced venture capitalists. The firm has invested in over 50 companies, and has backed some of the leading technology and software startups in the world. Cogent Partners is headquartered in San Francisco and has offices in London, Berlin, Paris, and Tokyo.
