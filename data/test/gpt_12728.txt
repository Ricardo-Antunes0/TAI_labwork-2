Oh Manapenne!  is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy film written and directed by P. S. Ravikumar. Produced by S. S. Rajamouli under his banner Sri Venkateswara Creations, the film features Nani, Rakul Preet Singh, and Roma in the lead roles. The music is composed by A. R. Rahman.  The film is about two friends, Ravi and Venkat, who are in their twenties and aim to start their own business. They fall in love with the same girl, but their families don't approve of their relationship.
