Children's Park is a 2019 Indian Malayalam-language psychological thriller film directed by A. R. Rahman and produced by A. R. Rahman and Devi Sri Prasad. The screenplay was written by Salim-Javed. The film stars Anushka Shetty, Dileep, Randeep Hooda and Tabu in lead roles.  The film tells the story of three children who are stranded in a park after their parents disappear. They must find a way to get home before the park closes.
