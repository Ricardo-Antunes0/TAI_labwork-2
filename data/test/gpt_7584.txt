The Sardars of Vahali were historical jagirdars (local rulers) of the Sardar tribe in the Vahali region, in present-day Gujarat, India. The Sardars of Vahali were one of the most powerful and influential tribes in Gujarat.  The Sardars of Vahali trace their ancestry to the legendary hero Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, who is credited with unifying Gujarat and creating the modern state of Gujarat. The Sardars of Vahali were also instrumental in the independence movement in India, and many of them served in various capacities in the Indian government. The Sardars of Vahali are still one of the most influential and powerful tribes in Gujarat, and they continue to play an important role in Indian politics.
