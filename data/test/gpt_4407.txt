Stylidium ensatum is a dicotyledonous plant that belongs to the genus Stylidium and family Stylidiaceae. It is endemic to Fiji.   Stylidium ensatum is a small, herbaceous, succulent plant that grows up to 10 cm tall. The leaves are round and succulent, with a pronounced papery sheath. The inflorescence is a cluster of up to six flowers, each with five white petals and a greenish-yellow disc.
