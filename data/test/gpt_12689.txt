Kyle Collinsworth (born October 3, 1991) is an American football wide receiver for the Pittsburgh Steelers of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Temple.  Collinsworth was drafted by the Steelers in the seventh round of the 2013 NFL Draft. He has also been a member of the Houston Texans and Baltimore Ravens.  Collinsworth was born in Freeport, Illinois, to Lisa and Kyle Collinsworth. He has two sisters, Lindsay and Casey. Collinsworth attended Freeport High School, where he played football and was a linebacker on the varsity team his senior year. He was rated as a three-star recruit by Rivals.com and ESPN. Collinsworth attended Temple University where he played wide receiver for the Temple Owls football team from 2009 to 2012. As a freshman in 2009, he recorded 9 receptions for 148 yards and two touchdowns in nine games. In 2010, he had 50 receptions for 677 yards and six touchdowns, earning first-team All-American honors from College Sports Journal and second-team All-American honors from ESPN. As a junior in 2011, he had 83 receptions for 1,076 yards and 10 touchdowns, earning first-team All-American honors from both College Sports Journal and ESPN. In 2012, he had 79 receptions for 1,171 yards and 12 touchdowns, earning first-team All-American honors from both College Sports Journal and ESPN again.
