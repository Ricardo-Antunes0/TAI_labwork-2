His best finish on the Tour was second place in the 1941 PGA Championship. He won three times on the PGA Tour in his career, including the 1942 U.S. Open. He also won twice on the PGA Tour Champions, in 1936 and 1937. Coy died in 1980 at the age of 83 in Palm Springs, California.  Coy was inducted into the World Golf Hall of Fame in 1974.
