Nooralhaq Nasimi (born 1967) is a former Iranian actress.  Nooralhaq Nasimi was born in Tehran in 1967. She graduated from the acting program at the Islamic Azad University, Tehran. Her first role was in the 1988 short film "The Tables Are Turned". She then appeared in several television series and movies, most notably "The White Balloon" (1996), "The Sun and the Moon" (1998), "The Apple" (1999), "The Wings of Desire" (2002), and "Empire of Dreams" (2005).
