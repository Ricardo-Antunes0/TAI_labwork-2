Axiomatic is the third studio album by American rock band My Chemical Romance. Released on October 3, 2006, the album was produced by Bob Rock and was recorded at locations in New York City and Los Angeles.  The album debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 with first-week sales of 143,000 copies. It has since sold over 1.5 million copies in the United States. The album achieved widespread critical acclaim, with many reviewers praising its heavier sound and lyrical themes. It was nominated for a 2007 Grammy Award for Best Rock Album.
