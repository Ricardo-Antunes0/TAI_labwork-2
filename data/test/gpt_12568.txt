Estelle Laura Johnson (born 21 July 1988) is an English actress who has appeared in a number of television and film roles. Johnson was born in London to Jamaican parents. She has an older sister, former model and actress Amala Ratna Zandile Johnson, and a younger brother, actor Steffan Johnson. Johnson started her career as a model before making her acting debut in the 2006 British comedy-drama film Starter for 10. Her breakthrough role came in the 2010 British romantic comedy-drama film Love Actually, in which she played the role of Anna. Johnson has since appeared in a number of roles in television and film productions, most notably in the 2013 British period drama series Downton Abbey as Lady Sybil Crawley, and in the 2014 American science fiction action thriller film Ender's Game as Peebee Aten.
