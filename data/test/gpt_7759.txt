"Message to Michael" is a song written by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift and penned by Max Martin and Shellback. It was released as the lead single from Swift's fourth studio album, "1989" (2015).  The song is a ballad that speaks to the unrequited love of a young man for an older man. The lyrics are written from the perspective of the young man, who tells his older lover that he still loves him and misses him, despite their separation.   "Message to Michael" was met with acclaim from music critics, who praised its emotional depth and catchy melodies. It became a top ten hit in many countries, including Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, Sweden and the United Kingdom. The song was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Original Song at the 88th Academy Awards.  Taylor Swift has stated that "Message to Michael" is about her personal experience with Michael Jordan.
