Paramount Global (doing business as Paramount) is a motion picture production and distribution company founded in 1916. The company is headquartered in Hollywood, California. Paramount is one of the Big Six major American film studios. Paramount's current president and CEO is Jim Gianopulos.  Paramount's film library includes some of the most iconic and popular films in history, including "Casablanca", "The Godfather", "Star Trek", and "Jurassic Park". The studio has produced some of the biggest box office hits of all time, including "E.T.", "Jaws", "Pirates of Caribbean", and "Mission: Impossible". Paramount has also been involved in creating some of the most successful franchises in cinema, such as Transformers, Star Wars, and James Bond.
