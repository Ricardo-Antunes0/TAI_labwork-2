Wulf Konold (29 June 1946 – 24 January 2017) was a German composer and conductor.  Born in Würzburg, Konold studied composition with Gerhard Oppermann and conducting with Hans Werner Henze at the Hochschule für Musik in Frankfurt am Main. He won the ARD Prize for New Music in 1982 and the Kranichsteiner Musikpreis in 1989. Konold has been a professor of composition at the Hochschule für Musik Mainz since 1987.
