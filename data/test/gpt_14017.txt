Malcolm Edward Osborne Brown (born 19 August 1986) is an English cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-fast bowler. He was born at Cheltenham, Gloucestershire.  Brown made his debut for Gloucestershire in the 2nd round of the 2006 County Championship against Northamptonshire. Brown has played Minor counties cricket for Wiltshire since 2006, making his first-class debut for the county against Durham in 2009. He has also played for the England Lions.  He made his One Day International (ODI) debut for England against Sri Lanka at The Oval on 9 April 2010. He made his Twenty20 International (T20I) debut for England against South Africa at The Oval on 15 July 2010. Brown has also represented Worcestershire in two List A matches. His first List A match came against Hampshire in the 2011 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy, while his second came against Somerset in the 2013 Cheltenham & Gloucester Trophy. In both of these matches, he took one wicket each.
