Pikin Santi, sometimes spelt as Pikien Santi, is a Filipino folk dance that is usually performed by women. It is a procession dance with complex steps and a lively rhythm.   The origins of Pikin Santi are unknown, but it is thought to have originated in the Visayan region. The dance is believed to have originally been used as a means of communicating between people during celebrations such as weddings or religious ceremonies.   Today, Pikin Santi is popular throughout the Philippines, and it can be seen performed at various festivals and community events. It is also sometimes used as an entertainment option for weddings or other special occasions.
