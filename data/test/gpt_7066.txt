Farris Hassan (born July 30, 1989) is an American professional basketball player for the Turkish club Fenerbahçe Ülker. He played college basketball for the Illinois Fighting Illini.  Hassan was born on July 30, 1989 in Chicago, Illinois. He attended Lane Technical College in Chicago, where he played college basketball for the Illinois Fighting Illini. Hassan averaged 10.2 points and 5.4 rebounds per game as a junior and led the team to the NCAA Tournament in his final season.  After going undrafted in the 2012 NBA draft, Hassan joined the Miami Heat for the 2012 NBA Summer League. In September 2012, he signed with the Italian team Pallacanestro Reggiana for the 2012–13 season. He moved to Turkey for the 2014–15 season, signing with Fenerbahçe Ülker. He was named the Turkish Cup MVP in 2017.   Hassan has also played for the United States national team at the AfroBasket 2013 and 2017 tournaments, winning a bronze medal at AfroBasket 2017.
