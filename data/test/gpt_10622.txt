Cameron Pearce Norman (born 12 October 1995) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship club Leeds United.  Cameron Norman was born in Leeds, West Yorkshire on 12 October 1995. He started his career at Leeds United, signing a professional contract in February 2014. Norman made his first-team debut in the FA Cup against Norwich City on 21 September 2014, coming on as a substitute in the 85th minute.
