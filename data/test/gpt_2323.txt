30 is the fourth studio album by British singer Adele. It was released on 25 November 2015, by XL Recordings. The album was produced by Adele and Paul Epworth.   The album contains the singles "Hello" and "Love Song". Upon its release, 30 received critical acclaim, topping the charts in over 30 countries. It has been certified Platinum by the British Phonographic Industry (BPI) and has sold over 1.5 million copies in the UK.  In July 2014, Adele announced that she would be releasing her fourth studio album in 2015. In October 2014, she released the first single from the album, "Hello". The song became a viral hit and peaked at number one in over 20 countries. In December 2014, it was announced that "Love Song" would be the second single from the album. The song was released in February 2015 and peaked at number two in the UK. In May 2015, it was announced that "Rolling in the Deep" would be the third single from the album. The song was released in June 2015 and peaked at number one in 18 countries. In September 2015, it was announced that "Set Fire to the Rain" would be the fourth single from the album. The song was released in October 2015 and peaked at number one in 15 countries. "Hello" and "Love Song" were both nominated for Grammy Awards in 2016.
