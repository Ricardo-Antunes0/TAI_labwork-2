Vuguru is an American independent multi-platform studio, founded in 2007 by brothers John and Michael Zalewski. The company is headquartered in Cambridge, MA.  Vuguru specializes in developing and publishing casual games for mobile platforms, with a particular focus on iOS and Android devices. Vuguru has released over 60 games since 2007, including the popular franchises Zombies vs. Ninjas, Stickman Golf, and Farm Heroes Saga. The company has also released several critically acclaimed console titles, including the critically acclaimed puzzle game Cut the Rope and the hit platformer Angry Birds.  In December of 2017, Vuguru was acquired by GameStop Corporation.
