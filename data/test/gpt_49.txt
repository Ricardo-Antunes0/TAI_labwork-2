St Athan  is a village and community in the Vale of Glamorgan, south Wales. The population of the village at the 2011 census was 1,878.  The village lies on the A4120 road about north-west of Cardiff, north-east of Swansea and south-west of Newport.  The parish church, dedicated to Saint Athan, is a Grade II* listed building. The present building dates from 1856 and replaced an earlier church which had been destroyed in a fire in 1568.  The village has a primary school, a post office and a number of shops. There are also a pub and a hotel/restaurant.  The community also contains St Athan Golf Club, which was founded in 1892. The club's course has been designed by J.H. Taylor and is considered to be one of the best golf courses in Wales.
