Sven Jonas Gerhardsson Hafström (born 27 February 1986) is a Swedish professional football player who plays for AIK in Allsvenskan. He has also played for Östersunds FK in the Swedish Superettan and Blackburn Rovers in the Premier League.  Hafström started his career with local side IFK Göteborg, before moving to Dutch side PSV Eindhoven in 2004. He made his first-team debut for PSV on 15 November 2004 in a 3–1 away win against Vitesse Arnhem. At the end of the 2006–07 season, he signed for Blackburn Rovers in England, for a fee of £1 million.  Hafström made his Blackburn Rovers debut on 20 August 2007, coming on as a substitute for Gary McAllister in the 71st minute of a 1–0 home victory over Watford. He scored his first goal for the club on 20 November 2007, scoring the winner in a 2–1 home victory over Birmingham City. He made 47 appearances and scored six goals for Blackburn Rovers before being transferred to AIK on 31 January 2010.  Hafström has represented Sweden at under-21 level and made two appearances for the Sweden national football team.
