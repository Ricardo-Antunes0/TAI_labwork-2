The R355 is a Regional Route in South Africa that connects the town of Vereeniging in the Gauteng province with the city of Johannesburg.  The R355 begins at the intersection of the R103 and the R81 in Vereeniging. The route heads southeast, crossing the Vaal River into the Johannesburg metropolitan area. The highway continues through Johannesburg's inner-city, intersecting major arterial roads such as Main Road and Parktown Road. It then turns northeast, becoming a freeway near Soweto. The freeway ends at an interchange with the M5, which provides access to Johannesburg International Airport and Gauteng's major industrial areas.
