Samantha "Sam" Carter, USAF is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The character was created by Jim Lee and Danny Madsen and first appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #252 (January 1990).  Carter is the daughter of Dr. Thomas and Marian Carter, and the older sister of Alex. After her parents were killed in a car accident, Sam was raised by her Aunt May and Uncle Ben in Smallville, Kansas. As a teenager, she attended Clark Kent High School and became romantically involved with Peter Parker. After graduation, she enlisted in the United States Air Force and served as a flight officer.  Carter first appeared as an Avenger in The New Avengers #1 (October 2006) and subsequently joined the team full-time. She has since been a regular member of the team, acting as its field leader during the Siege of Asgard. In 2013, Carter was ranked number 12 on IGN's list of "The Top 25 Avengers".
