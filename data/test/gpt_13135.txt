Northeaster is a painting by American painter Edward Hopper. The work was created in 1954 and currently resides in the Museum of Modern Art in New York City. Northeaster depicts a desolate landscape with a single, snow-covered house in the center. The painting is noted for its use of deep blues and greens, as well as its stark, angular lines.
