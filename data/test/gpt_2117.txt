Edda Manga (born 1969) is a Swedish manga artist and illustrator.   Edda Manga was born in 1969 in Sweden. She studied at the University of Arts Stockholm and graduated with a degree in graphic design.   Manga career began in 1995 with the publication of her first manga, "Kuroshitsuji". Her work has been published in magazines such as "Animage", "Weekly Shonen Jump" and "Dengeki Daioh". She has also collaborated on several projects, including "Soul Eater" and "Attack on Titan". In 2015, she won the prestigious Eisner Award for Best New Artist.
