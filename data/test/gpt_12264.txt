Le Mobile is a remote recording truck, manufactured by JBL. It is a converted Ford E-350 van with a mobile recording studio in the back. The truck has two microphones and two decks, allowing it to record both audio and video simultaneously.  The truck was created in response to the changing music industry. The van allows artists to record anywhere they want, without having to rent a studio or hire a crew. Le Mobile is also capable of recording high-quality audio and video, making it an ideal choice for recording music festivals or concerts.
