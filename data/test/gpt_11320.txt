is a third-person shooter developed by Cavia and published by Sega for the Sega Saturn in Japan on December 9, 1996 and in North America on January 6, 1997. The game was ported to the PlayStation 2 on October 10, 2000 and to the GameCube on November 21, 2001. A spin-off game, titled "Bullet Witch 2", was released for the PlayStation Portable on September 1, 2009 in Japan and on October 13, 2009 in North America.  The game is set in a world of witches and warlocks who use firearms to combat each other. The player controls the titular Bullet Witch as she travels across various landscapes firing her gun at various targets.
