Blaster Master Zero is an action-adventure platform game for the Nintendo 3DS handheld system and Wii U home console, developed and published by Inti Creates. The game was announced during Nintendo's E3 2013 media briefing and is Inti Creates' first game for a home console in over eight years.  The game is a spiritual successor to the 1992 arcade game Blaster Master, and features a similar gameplay mechanic where the player controls a protagonist who must traverse stages while defeating enemies using a variety of weapons. The game also features an open world environment with side-quests and environmental puzzles.
