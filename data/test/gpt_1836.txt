Middle Class Madhavan is a 2001 Indian Tamil film written and directed by S. Shankar. The film stars Vijayakanth and Trisha in lead roles. The film had musical score by Ilaiyaraaja.  The film tells the story of a man, Madhavan (Vijayakanth), who is a middle-class Tamilian who is forced to migrate to Chennai from his hometown due to financial difficulties. Madhavan befriends an alcoholic millionaire, Thangarajan (Sathyaraj), and eventually falls in love with his daughter, Dhanushkodi (Trisha). However, Dhanushkodi is already married to an affluent man, Thangamani (Rajinikanth). Madhavan tries to win her over, but fails. In the end, Madhavan is able to return home and reconciles with his family.
