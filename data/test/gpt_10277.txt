Leroy Jetta (born 6 July 1988) is a German footballer who plays for FC Ingolstadt 04.  Jetta started his career with SC Pfullendorf before joining 1860 Munich in 2005. He made his Bundesliga debut for 1860 Munich on 8 August 2006 in a game against Borussia Mönchengladbach. Jetta was loaned out to 1. FC Nürnberg in February 2007, and then to 1. FC Kaiserslautern in July 2007. He returned to 1860 Munich at the end of the 2007-08 season, where he became a first-choice centre back. Jetta made his international debut for Germany on 11 February 2009 in a friendly against Brazil, and was part of Germany's UEFA Euro 2012 squad.
