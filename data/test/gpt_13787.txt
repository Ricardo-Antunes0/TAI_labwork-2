Kooyoora State Park is a state park located in the Murray-Darling basin, in the state of Victoria, Australia. The park is located about north-west of Melbourne and contains the world's second largest stand of Eucalyptus regnans (Kooyoora) trees. The park is open for day use only and includes a visitor centre, camping and caravan sites, a swimming pool and a playground.   Kooyoora State Park was proclaimed in 1955.
