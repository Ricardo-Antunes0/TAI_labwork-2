Brendon Bolton (born 18 April 1979) is an English football coach and former player who is the manager of Bolton Wanderers.  Bolton started his playing career at Manchester United, where he made over 200 appearances, before moving to Bolton Wanderers in 2004. He spent six years at the club, winning the League Cup in 2006 and reaching the UEFA Cup final in 2007. He rejoined Manchester United in 2009, but left in 2012 to become Bolton's manager.  Bolton was appointed manager of Bolton Wanderers on 9 May 2012 after leaving Manchester United.
