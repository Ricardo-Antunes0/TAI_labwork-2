Star Trek is an American science fiction television series created by Gene Roddenberry that originally aired on NBC from 1966 to 1969. The show is set in the future and follows the adventures of the crew of the starship Enterprise, which is led by Captain James T. Kirk and First Officer Spock.  The series has been revived twice on television, once in 1987 and again in 2009, both times with new cast members. The original series was released on home video in the 1990s, and a new series, Star Trek: Discovery, debuted on CBS in 2017.
