Lykling Church  is a parish church in the municipality of Lykling in Sogn og Fjordane county, Norway. It is located in the village of Lykling, about north of the village of Vik and about northeast of the town of Fjørdal. The church is part of the Viken prosti (deanery) in the Diocese of Bjørgvin.  The church is built on a high hill above the village, and it has a long nave that is divided into three aisles by two Tuscan columns. The interior features several paintings by noted Norwegian artist Hans Gude. The altarpiece was painted by Ole Bull in 1892, and it features a depiction of Christ on the cross surrounded by angels. The church was consecrated on 10 September 1905.
