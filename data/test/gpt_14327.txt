Harry Steenbock was born in 1886 in Charlestown, Wisconsin. He received a bachelor's degree from the University of Wisconsin in 1908 and a master's degree from the University of Illinois in 1911. Steenbock worked as an engineer for several companies before becoming the president of the Wisconsin Alumni Research Foundation in 1946. He served in this position until 1951, when he retired. Steenbock died in 1957.
