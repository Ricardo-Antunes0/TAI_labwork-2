Sesh Chithi is a Bengali drama film directed by Rituparno Ghosh and jointly produced by the Ghosh family and the Shaw brothers. The film stars Soumitra Chatterjee, Rituparno Ghosh, Kiran Banerjee and Prosenjit Chatterjee in lead roles.  The film tells the story of a family that is torn apart by their patriarch's death. Young son Gogol (Soumitra Chatterjee) must deal with his loss, while his sister-in-law (Kiran Banerjee) takes on the role of caretaker to their mother (Prosenjit Chatterjee). Gogol's brother (Rituparno Ghosh) battles alcoholism while their father (Prosenjit Chatterjee) tries to find his way.
