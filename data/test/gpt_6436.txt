Loucks Grove Church (Wahtawah Christian Church) is a historic church located in rural Monroe County, Illinois, United States. The church was built in 1872 and is a two-story, rectangular, frame building with a gable roof. The facade features a two-story Romanesque Revival-style entrance tower and round-arch window.
