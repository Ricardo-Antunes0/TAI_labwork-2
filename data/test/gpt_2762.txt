The BM-30 Smerch (, "tornado", "whirlwind"), 9K58 Osa, is a Ukrainian man-portable air-defense system (MANPADS) jointly developed by the Yuzhmash plant in Dnipropetrovsk and the Ukrspetsexport company. It fires the 9M337M1 missile, which is a copy of the RIM-174A missile used by the United States Army's MIM-104 Patriot Advanced Capability 3 (PAC-3) system.
