Pecking Order  is a card game, typically for two players, in which players attempt to score points by playing cards of the same rank. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.  The game is said to have originated in India and was introduced to Europe in the late 18th century. It became popular in Britain during the Victorian era and has been played there ever since. There are several variations of the game, but the most common is Pecking Order, in which players attempt to score points by playing cards of the same rank.
