The 2019 All Out was the inaugural edition of the professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event produced by WWE. It took place on February 17, 2019 at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, California.  The card consisted of ten matches, including one championship match, which was a rematch of the 2018 WWE Royal Rumble match between AJ Styles and Shinsuke Nakamura.
