Darong class transport (AP)/troopship (APT) is a class of transport ship designed by South Korean shipbuilders Daewoo Shipbuilding and Marine Engineering. The class is based on the design of the Daewoo Musang class transport ships, but with a new hullform and a larger displacement.  The Darong class transports are intended for roles such as military transport, humanitarian aid, and disaster relief. They have a capacity of up to 2,000 troops or 60 tons of cargo. The ships are armed with a single 3-inch gun and two 20mm cannons.
