Maria Antonia is a feminine Portuguese given name. It may refer to:   Maria Antonia of Austria (1752-1790), Empress of the Holy Roman Empire and Queen of Portugal, mother of Maria I of Portugal;   Maria Antonia Walewska (1807-1849), wife of Napoleon III, mother of Napoleon II;   Maria Antonia von Habsburg (1957-), daughter of Otto von Habsburg and former wife of Prince Ernst August von Hannover;   Maria Antonia Menocal (born 1967), Mexican author.
