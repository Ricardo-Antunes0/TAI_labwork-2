Andrea Chung (born 1978) is an American journalist and television personality. She is the co-anchor of ABC's Good Morning America, as well as a correspondent for the network. Chung has also been a news correspondent for NBC News and CBS News, and has co-hosted the newsmagazine show Dateline NBC.  Chung was born in 1978 in Garden Grove, California, to immigrant parents from Taiwan. She is a graduate of Garden Grove High School and the University of California, Berkeley. Chung began her career as a reporter for the Orange County Register in 2001. In 2003, she was hired by ABC News as a correspondent for World News Tonight with Peter Jennings. Chung became co-anchor of Good Morning America in September 2013, succeeding David Muir.
