Dillwynia ramosissima, commonly known as bushy parrot-pea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae. It is endemic to south-central Australia.  Dillwynia ramosissima is a bushy, perennial herb with a stem up to 130 cm long, and a scattering of green, leathery leaves. The inflorescence is a raceme of up to 100 flowers, each with five white petals and a bright purple centre.
