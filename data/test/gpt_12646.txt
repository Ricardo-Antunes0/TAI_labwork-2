Amy Polumbo was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Polumbo's television roles include guest appearances on shows like CSI: Crime Scene Investigation and ER, as well as recurring roles on the WB series Gilmore Girls and the ABC drama Lost. She has also appeared in commercials for companies like Sprint and GEICO.  Polumbo married actor and musician Nolan Gould in May 2011.
