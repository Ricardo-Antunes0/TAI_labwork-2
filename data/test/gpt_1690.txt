"When Christmas Comes" is a song by American singer and songwriter Kelly Clarkson. The song was written by Clarkson, Bonnie McKee, and Hillary Lindsey and produced by Greg Kurstin. It was released as the lead single from her fifth studio album, "Christmas" (2013).  "When Christmas Comes" is a pop ballad with a gospel-inspired arrangement. Its lyrics are about Clarkson's excitement for the holiday season. The song was well received by music critics, who praised its composition and lyrical content. It reached the top five in several countries, including Australia, Canada, Ireland, and the United Kingdom. "When Christmas Comes" became Clarkson's seventh number-one single on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and her fourth number-one on the UK Singles Chart. The accompanying music video was directed by Declan Whitebloom and features Clarkson alongside various children in a snow-covered town singing along to the song.
