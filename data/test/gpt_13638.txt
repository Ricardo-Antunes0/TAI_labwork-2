Jonathan Rigby is an English actor and musician who has appeared in a number of television programmes and films. He is best known for his roles as Alex in the BBC soap opera EastEnders and as PC Andy Milligan in the ITV police drama Midsomer Murders.  Rigby was born in South London, to parents who were both teachers. He studied at the University of East Anglia, where he began his career as a theatre actor. Rigby's television appearances began with a role in the 2000 BBC drama series Casualty. From 2002 to 2003, he starred as Alex in the BBC soap opera EastEnders, for which he was nominated for a British Television Award. In 2004, he played PC Andy Milligan in the ITV police drama Midsomer Murders, for which he was nominated for a BAFTA Award and won an International Emmy Award for Best Actor. Rigby has also appeared in the films Young Adam (2003), Death Sentence (2007), and A Single Man (2009).
