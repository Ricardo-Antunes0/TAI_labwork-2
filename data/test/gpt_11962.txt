Javouhey is a town in northwest French Guiana, located on the Amazon River. It is located in the commune of Maroua.  The town is located on the left bank of the Amazon River, northeast of Maroua. It has a population of 4,000. Javouhey is situated on a plateau at an altitude of 1,000 metres. The terrain is flat, with a few small hills. The town has a primary school, a dispensary, and a market. There are also some small businesses that deal in woodworking and agriculture.
