JW Marriott Chennai is a five-star luxury hotel located in the heart of the city, next to the Chennai International Airport. The hotel offers a range of facilities and services that guests can enjoy, such as an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, a sauna and a spa.  JW Marriott Chennai was opened in December 2013 and is managed by JW Marriott International. The hotel has 296 rooms and suites that feature a variety of amenities, including an indoor pool, a spa and a business centre. The hotel also has a restaurant, which serves international cuisine.
