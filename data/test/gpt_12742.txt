Primo Filmes, led by Joana Mariani, Matias Bergara and Pablo Fuiña, is a film production company based in Lisbon, Portugal.  Primo Filmes was founded in 2004 by Joana Mariani, Matias Bergara and Pablo Fuiña. The company has produced a number of award-winning films including the critically acclaimed drama "The Act of Killing" (2012), the biographical drama "The Missing Picture" (2013), the comedy-drama "A Fantastic Woman" (2017), and the documentary "Three Identical Strangers" (2018).
