Bane is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics, commonly as a member of the Batman villainous organization the Dark Knight Rises. Bane is played by Tom Hardy in the 2016 film Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice.  In the original comic book story, Bane was conceived as an intelligent and physically superior mercenary who breaks out of Arkham Asylum, intending to destroy Gotham City with the help of fellow inmates Scarecrow, Penguin and Two-Face. Batman foils his plan, but Bane escapes and returns to Arkham, where he plots his revenge against Batman. In subsequent stories, he becomes a member of Catwoman's organization the Fearsome Five and battles the superhero Superman.  Bane first appeared in comic books published by DC Comics in 1992. He was created by artist Tom Grummett and writer Chuck Dixon.
