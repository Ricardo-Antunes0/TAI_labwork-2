Niederbrechen station is a station on the Berlin–Hamburg railway. It is located in the town of Niederbrechen, in the district of Spandau, Berlin. The station is served by S-Bahn line S 9 and Regionalbahn line RB 42.   The station was opened on 14 November 1847 as part of the Berlin–Hamburg railway. The line was upgraded to a high-speed railway in 2007.
