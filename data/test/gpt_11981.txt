Kyle Noke (born 18 March 1980) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for League One side Burton Albion.  Noke began his career in the Football League with Shrewsbury Town, before moving onto Cheltenham Town, where he made over 150 appearances. He then had spells with Darlington and Rotherham United, before signing for Burton Albion in January 2013. He has since made over 150 appearances for the club, scoring 10 goals.  Noke has also represented England at under-21 level.
