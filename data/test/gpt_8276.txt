Them Thar Hills is a 1934 American Pre-Code Western film directed by George O'Brien and written by Albert Hackett and Frances Goodrich. The film stars Roy Rogers, Gene Autry, Smiley Burnette, and Trigger.  The film is about a group of ranchers who are threatened by a group of outlaws led by the fearsome Bronc Buster. The ranchers must band together to overcome their enemies and save their land.
