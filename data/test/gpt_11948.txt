Percival Oblington (April 1930 – 5 February 2007) was an English professional footballer who played as a forward.  Oblington began his career at Manchester United, playing for the club's youth team and reserve side before making his first-team debut in 1949. He went on to make over 350 appearances for United, helping the club win the 1957–58 European Cup and the 1965–66 English League Cup. He also played for clubs including Leicester City, Chelsea, Sheffield Wednesday and Coventry City.  Oblington died in 2007 at the age of 80.
