Kimberly Prause is an American voice actress and singer. She is known for her work in the English-language dubs of anime and video games.  Prause was born in Baltimore, Maryland, to an American father and Jamaican mother. She attended college at the University of Maryland, College Park, studying music performance. She began her voice acting career in 1997, doing the English-language dub of the anime series Wolf's Rain. Since then, she has voiced a number of characters in anime and video games, including Cardcaptor Sakura's Sakura Kinomoto in the English dub and Shinji Ikari in Neon Genesis Evangelion in both the original Japanese and English versions.
