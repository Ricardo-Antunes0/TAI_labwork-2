Verdade Uma Ilusão is a live album by Brazilian singer Marisa Monte, released on May 20, 2014. It was recorded in Rio de Janeiro in August 2013 in Cidade da Música Hall. In 2014, it was nominated for the Latin Grammy Award for Best MPB Album.
