Kentucky Route 2048 (KY 2048) is a state highway in Kentucky that runs from the Tennessee state line southeast to U.S. Route 127 (US 127) near Hodgenville.  KY 2048 begins at the Tennessee state line southeast of Hodgenville, running southeast on a two-lane undivided road. The highway crosses over the Cumberland River before entering Hodgenville, where it becomes a four-lane divided highway. KY 2048 reaches its eastern terminus at US 127 near the town of Hodgenville.
