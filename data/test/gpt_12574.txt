Mo Chhu is a major river in the Himalayas. It is a tributary of the Sutlej River and has a length of 432 kilometres.  The Mo Chhu valley is one of the most scenic areas in the Himalayas. The river flows through a deep canyon before entering the plains of Himachal Pradesh. The valley is flanked by steep, snow-capped mountains and contains a number of glaciers. The river is popular for rafting and kayaking.
