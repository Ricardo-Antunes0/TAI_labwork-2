Jalin Jacob Turner (born 18 May 1995) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Championship club Derby County.  Turner started his career at Arsenal, where he made one appearance in the Football League Trophy before joining Derby County on loan in February 2016. He made his senior debut the following month and went on to make 54 appearances for the " Rams", scoring five goals. In May 2018, Turner signed a new contract with Derby County, running until 2023.
