Irina Stolyarova (Russian: Ирина Столярова; born 14 May 1979) is a Russian actress. She has appeared in more than fifty films since her debut in 2001. Born in Leningrad, Stolyarova trained at the Vakhtangov Theatre and then at the Moscow Art Theatre School. She made her film debut in 2001 in the comedy film Moscow Life. Her breakthrough role came in 2003 when she starred as Yelena in the comedy film Stalingrad. After appearing in several successful films, including The Intouchables (2011), House of Sand and Fog (2013), and The Gambler (2015), Stolyarova starred as Yevgenia Morozova in the historical drama Anna Karenina (2012).
