Robert Jahrling (born 14 February 1974) is an American professional golfer.  Jahrling was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota. He attended the University of Arizona, where he was a member of the golf team and won the NCAA Division I Men's Golf Championship in 1998. Jahrling turned professional in 1999 and played on the PGA Tour from 2000 to 2007. He finished in the top-10 on the Tour five times and won three tournaments, the 2004 New Orleans Open, 2006 Honda Classic, and 2007 Zurich Classic of New Orleans. Jahrling has also played on the European Tour and has two career wins on that tour, the 2006 Abu Dhabi HSBC Championship and 2007 Samsung Galaxy Championship. In 2008, Jahrling joined the PGA Tour's developmental tour, the Nationwide Tour. In 2009 he left the Nationwide Tour to join the European Challenge Tour where he won two events.
