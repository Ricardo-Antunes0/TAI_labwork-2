Dreamchild is a 1985 British drama film directed by Maurice Pialat. It tells the story of a mother and daughter who are estranged, and of the daughter's attempt to reconnect with her mother.  The film was entered into the 1985 Cannes Film Festival, where it won the Prize of the Ecumenical Jury. It was also nominated for the Palme d'Or.  The film has been described as a "sensitive and thoughtful" drama, dealing with themes of alienation and estrangement.
