William John Storey (born 13 September 1978) is an English professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for League One side Wycombe Wanderers.  Born in Northampton, Storey started his career at Southampton, making his first-team debut in 1998. He spent six years with the club, making over 150 appearances before joining Coventry City in 2003. He spent three years with the club, making over 100 appearances, before moving to Rotherham United in 2006. He spent two seasons with the club, making over 50 appearances, before joining Wycombe Wanderers in 2009. He has made over 150 appearances for Wycombe and helped the club win promotion to League One in 2014.
