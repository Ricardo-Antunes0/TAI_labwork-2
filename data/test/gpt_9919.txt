Trens Intercidades (TIC) is a four line poem by the Spanish poet Federico García Lorca. The poem is about a young man's love for a woman who is away from him.   Trens Intercidades is a four line poem by the Spanish poet Federico García Lorca. The poem is about a young man's love for a woman who is away from him. The speaker describes his feelings in simple, direct language. He expresses his hope that the woman will return to him soon, and his longing for her company.
