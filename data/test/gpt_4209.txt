TNA Today was an online professional wrestling news and discussion website operated by Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). The site was founded by Josh Mathews in September 2007, and featured news, results, opinion, and interviews from TNA employees and independent wrestlers. On January 5, 2016, TNA announced that TNA Today would be closing its doors effective immediately.  Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA) is an American professional wrestling promotion, formerly known as World Championship Wrestling (WCW). It is the oldest active wrestling promotion in the United States and the largest wrestling organization in the world. TNA is headquartered in Nashville, Tennessee.
