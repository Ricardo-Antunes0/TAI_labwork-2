Open is the debut extended play by American singer-songwriter Halsey, released on August 3, 2018, by Astralwerks. The EP was preceded by the release of the single "Gasoline" on July 12, 2018.  Open is a collection of five songs written and recorded by Halsey in 2017 and 2018. "Gasoline" was released as the lead single from Open on July 12, 2018.
