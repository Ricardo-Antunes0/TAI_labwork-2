2004 Incomparable Concert  is the second live album by the Japanese rock band B'z. It was released on July 29, 2004, and was recorded at the Tokyo Dome in Tokyo, Japan on October 24 and 25, 2003. The album reached number one on the Oricon chart.  The album was compiled from two concerts held at the Tokyo Dome in October 2003. The first concert was a joint performance with Japanese pop singer Kumi Koda, and the second concert featured B'z only. The album features nine songs from their studio albums "Deeper Blue" (1999) and "The Best of B'z" (2002), as well as three previously unreleased tracks.   The album reached number one on the Oricon chart, selling 627,000 copies in its first week. It has since sold over 1.5 million copies in Japan.
