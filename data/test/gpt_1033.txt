The Runner Stumbles is a 1979 American mystery film directed by Michael Crichton and written by him. It stars James Garner, Diane Lane, Jason Robards and Richard Widmark.  Set in the fictional town of Carthage, California, the film tells the story of a private detective, John McFee (Garner), who is hired to find a missing woman, but discovers that she has been kidnapped by a group of criminals led by her ex-boyfriend (Robards). McFee stalls for time by spending more time with the woman's daughter (Lane) than he does with the woman herself, but eventually he is forced to confront the criminals head on.
