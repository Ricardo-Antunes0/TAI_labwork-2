Shooter is an American drama television series that aired on Fox from January 12, 2019 to May 18, 2019. The series is created and written by Jason Blum and is executive produced by him, showrunner Matt Miller, and Jordan Levin.  The series stars Stephen Moyer, Trevante Rhodes, Wes Chatham, Lili Simmons, Afton Williamson, and Denis O'Hare.  Shooter follows the lives of the people working at a small-town newspaper in West Texas during the early days of the internet.   On May 18th, 2019, Fox cancelled the series after one season.
