Meriden Mall (formerly Meriden Square and Westfield Meriden) is a shopping mall located in Meriden, Connecticut. Opened in 1978, it is the second largest shopping mall in Connecticut after The Mall at Quaboag Valley.  The mall features over 170 stores, including anchor stores Macy's, JCPenney, Sears, and Target, as well as regional chain stores like Boscov's and ShopRite. It also has a movie theater, food court, and two ice skating rinks.
