Malaysian Tamil (Tamil: மலேசியத் தமிழ் மொழி) (Tamil: மலேசியத் தொழி) is a dialect of Tamil spoken in the Malaysian state of Peninsular Malaysia and the Malaysian state of Sabah.  The Malaysian Tamil dialect has many features in common with the Tamil dialects spoken in South India, Sri Lanka, and Singapore. However, there are also important differences. For example, Malaysian Tamil has a number of loanwords from Malay and English. Additionally, Malaysian Tamil has its own set of words and expressions that are unique to the Malaysian context.
