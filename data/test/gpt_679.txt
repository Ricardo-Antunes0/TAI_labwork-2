The 2019–20 Elite One was the 60th season of the Elite One League, the top level professional ice hockey league in Asia. Eight teams contested the league, with the defending champion, the Seoul Dynasty, entering as defending champions.  The season commenced on October 5, 2019 and concluded on May 25, 2020.  The KLH Cup was the secondary playoff tournament to determine the sixteenth and final team to qualify for the 2020–21 Elite One League season. The tournament was contested by the top six finishers of the Elite One League regular season. The winner of the KLH Cup qualified for the 2020–21 Elite One League season.  Shanghai Dragons won the KLH Cup after they defeated the Kunlun Red Star 3–1 in the final. This qualified Shanghai Dragons for the 2020–21 Elite One League season.
