Rodger Kamenetz (born 1950) is an American composer, pianist, and music educator. He is the creator of a unique compositional style which he has termed "phantasmagoria". Kamenetz's music is heavily inspired by the works of Erik Satie and Igor Stravinsky.  Kamenetz was born in 1950 in New York City. After studying at the Juilliard School of Music, he began his professional career as a pianist and composer. He has written pieces for orchestra, chamber ensemble, solo piano, and voice. His music has been performed throughout the United States, Europe, and Asia. He is currently the Glenn J. Neuman Professor of Composition at Rutgers University.
