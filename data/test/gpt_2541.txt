Kateřina Tučková (born 31 October 1980) is a Czech actress. Tučková was born in the town of Písek, and started her career in 2002. She has appeared in a number of Czech films, including The Unbearable Lightness of Being, A Good Old Fashioned Orgy and The Intouchables. In 2013, Tučková starred in the comedy-drama film Anna Karenina opposite Keira Knightley.
