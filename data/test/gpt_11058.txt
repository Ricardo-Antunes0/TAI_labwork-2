Lunde Church  is a parish church in the municipality of Lunde in Agder county, Norway. It is located in the village of Lunde. The church is part of the Lunde parish in the Vest-Agder prosti (deanery) in the Diocese of Agder og Telemark. The white, wooden church was built in a long church style in 1878 by the architect Ole Johannes Larsen. The church seats about 330 people.
