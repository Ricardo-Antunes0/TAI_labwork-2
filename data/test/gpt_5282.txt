The Country Bears is a 2002 American animated comedy film produced by Amblin Entertainment and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The film features the voices of John Goodman, Cheech Marin, Tara Strong, and Tom Kenny.  The film is about a family of bears who live in a forest near the edge of civilization. They are constantly harassed by humans, who try to steal their food and destroy their homes. One day, the bears learn that they can use their dance moves to hypnotize humans and make them do whatever they want. Using this power, they soon become rich and powerful.
