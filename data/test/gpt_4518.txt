Irish singer and songwriter Róisín Murphy has released six studio albums, one compilation album, two extended plays and thirty-eight singles.  Murphy first came to prominence in 2006 with the release of her debut album "The Dreaming", which reached number two on the Irish Albums Chart and was certified platinum by the Irish Recorded Music Association. Her follow-up album, "Róisín", was released in 2009 and reached number one on the Irish Albums Chart, becoming her first album to be certified gold by the IRMA. Murphy's third album, " baptism", was released in 2012 and peaked at number two on the Irish Albums Chart. Her fourth album, "Cardigan", was released in 2015 and reached number one on the Irish Albums Chart.   Murphy has also released three singles from her fourth album: "Baptism", "Notting Hill" and "God knows". Her fifth album, "Bloody Valentine", was released in 2018 and peaked at number one on the Irish Albums Chart.
