Chika Anadu is a Nigerian filmmaker best known for her feature film debut, the critically acclaimed drama film, "The Wedding Party".  Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, Chika Anadu began filmmaking at a young age, directing short films and making music videos. After completing her secondary education in London, she moved back to Nigeria to pursue a career in filmmaking. Her first feature film, "The Wedding Party", was released in 2016 to critical acclaim, earning her numerous accolades including the African Academy of Film and Television Arts (AFTAs) Award for Best First Feature Film and the Berlin International Film Festival's Silver Bear Award for Best Feature Film.
