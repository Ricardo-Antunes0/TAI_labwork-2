Helmond Sport is a professional association football club based in Helmond, Netherlands. The club was formed in 1899 and plays in the Eerste Divisie.  The club was founded on 15 January 1899, and played its first competitive match on the following day. In 1904, Helmond Sport merged with Helmondia to become Helmondia Helmond Sport. The club played in the Hoofdklasse from 1917 to 1929, when it was relegated. In 1936, the club merged with SV Heteren to form Heteren-Helmond Sport.  In 1948, Heteren-Helmond Sport merged with SV Kampen to form SC Helmond Sport. The club played in the Tweede Divisie from 1949 to 1951, when it was relegated to the Derde Divisie. In 1954, SC Helmond Sport merged with SV Noordwijk to form Noordwijk-SC Helmond Sport. The club played in the Eerste Divisie from 1955 to 1962, when it was relegated to the Tweede Divisie.  In 1966, Noordwijk-SC Helmond Sport merged with FC Twente to form FC Twente-SC Helmond Sport. The club played in the Eredivisie from 1967 to 1972, when it was relegated to the Hoofdklasse.
