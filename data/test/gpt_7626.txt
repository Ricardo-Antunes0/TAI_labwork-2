The , signed as Route K1, is a hiking trail in the mountains of eastern Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan. It is one of the most popular trails in the prefecture, and has been designated as a National Route.  The trail starts from the town of Yokohama and heads eastward, following the ridge of the Eastern Yamanashi Mountains. The route passes through a variety of ecosystems, including deciduous forests, subalpine meadows, and alpine tundra. It is well known for its stunning views of Mount Fuji and other peaks in the area. The Yokohane Route is also popular for its easy walking and abundant wildlife.
