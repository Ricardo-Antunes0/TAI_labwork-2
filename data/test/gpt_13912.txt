Sulabh International is an India-based social service organisation with a focus on the eradication of poverty and the promotion of human welfare. It was founded in 1975 by Mahatma Gandhi's grandson, Arun Gandhi, and operates in more than 60 countries.  Sulabh International is one of the world's largest and most successful social service organisations, working in more than 60 countries to fight poverty and promote human welfare. With a focus on sanitation and hygiene, the organisation runs programmes to improve access to clean water, education and health care. Sulabh International is also known for its work promoting gender equality and empowering women.
