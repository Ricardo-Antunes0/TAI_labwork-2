The Fujian Fleet was founded in 1605 and was one of the three fleets of the Chinese Maritime Customs Service. It played an important role in China's early maritime history and was abolished in 1949. The fleet mainly operated along the coastal regions of Fujian and Zhejiang provinces, protecting their maritime interests and guarding the coast against pirates.
