Vaklush Tolev (Vaklush Tolev Zapryanov, Bulgarian: Ваклуш Товлев, born 18 October 1986) is a Bulgarian football midfielder who plays for Ludogorets Razgrad.  Vaklush Tolev started his career in his hometown club Blagoevgrad, before moving to Levski Sofia in the summer of 2006. After two years with Levski, he signed with Turkish side Çaykur Rizespor. However, he only spent one season with the club and returned to Bulgaria to join Ludogorets Razgrad in the summer of 2008.  In his first season with Ludogorets, Tolev helped the team to win the Bulgarian Cup and reach the final of the UEFA Europa League. He also played in all six of Ludogorets' UEFA Europa League matches, helping them to reach the group stage for the first time in their history.  Tolev represented Bulgaria at under-21 level and has also played for their national team at full international level. He made his debut on 27 March 2007 against Montenegro and has since earned a total of four caps, scoring one goal.
