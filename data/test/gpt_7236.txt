The Burning City is a fantasy novel by American author Glen Cook, the first book in his Chronicles of the Black Company series. It was first published in 1983.  The Burning City is the first book of the Chronicles of the Black Company series, written by American author Glen Cook. The novel is set in a world of sword and sorcery, and follows a group of mercenaries hired to protect a princess from a rival kingdom.  The novel was first published in 1983 and has been praised for its intricate plotting and characterization. It has also been compared to works by George R. Martin and J. R. R. Tolkien. The Burning City was adapted into a television series, which aired on the USA Network from 1998 to 2000.
