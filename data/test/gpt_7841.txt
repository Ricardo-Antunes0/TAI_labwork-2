Mandalay Bodaw  is one of the oldest cities in Myanmar.  Mandalay is located in central Myanmar and is the former royal capital. The city is surrounded by a wall and has a population of around 1 million people.  Mandalay is known for its pagodas, markets, and beautiful architecture. The city is home to many religious sites, including the Mandalay Palace, which was built by the king of Mandalay in the 15th century.
