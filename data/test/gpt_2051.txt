Katie Goodman is a comedian, author, and actress. She is known for her work on the Comedy Central series Reno and for her appearances on shows such as The Office, Parks and Recreation, and Brooklyn Nine-Nine.  Katie Goodman was born in Los Angeles, California, on October 9, 1978. She attended Santa Monica College before transferring to the University of California, Santa Barbara, where she studied theater and comedy.  Goodman started her career as a stand-up comedian in 2002. She appeared on the Comedy Central series Reno from 2006 to 2007 and on the Fox comedy series The Office from 2007 to 2013. She also appeared in the films I Love You, Man and The Hangover Part III.  Goodman wrote and starred in the comedy web series Katie Goodman's Crazy Life from 2013 to 2014. In 2017, she co-created and starred in the HBO series Crashing.
