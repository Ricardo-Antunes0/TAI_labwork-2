Bonjour paresse (Hello Laziness) is the title of a painting by Jean-Honoré Fragonard. The painting is in the collection of the Louvre in Paris.  The painting is a oil on canvas, measuring 101 cm high by 73 cm wide. It shows a young woman sitting at a desk lazily painting with a brush. She wears a blue dress and a white bonnet, and her feet are rested on a stool.   The painting was first commissioned by the Duke of Orléans and painted in 1764. It is considered to be Fragonard's most famous work and is one of the most popular paintings in the Louvre.
