Nagaram Nidrapotunna Vela   is a star in the constellation Orion. It has an apparent magnitude of 3.92 and is about 390 light years from Earth.  Nagaram Nidrapotunna Vela is a giant star that is about twice the size of the Sun. It is about 950 times more luminous than the Sun and has about the same mass. The star is about 4.5 billion years old and is spinning rapidly on its axis.
