Pullmantur Cruises was a cruise line headquartered in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The company operated passenger ferries and cruises between Brazil and foreign ports.  The company was founded in 1984 by two brothers, Marcelo and Oswaldo Pullmantur. The company was initially known as Cruzeiro do Sul, and operated cruises between Rio de Janeiro and Uruguayan ports. In the early 1990s, the company began operating cruises to South America. In 2003, the company changed its name to Pullmantur Cruises.  In 2012, Pullmantur Cruises filed for bankruptcy protection in Brazil. The company ceased operations in 2013.
