Liolà  is an Italian stage play by the dramatist Pietro Mascagni. It was first performed at the Teatro Apollo in Rome on 20 October 1892.  Liolà is a tragic play about a young girl, Lola, who falls in love with a married man, Renato. When Renato discovers Lola's true identity, he rejects her and she commits suicide. The play is based on the true story of Lola Montez, a 19th-century actress who was famous for her role in the opera Pagliacci.
