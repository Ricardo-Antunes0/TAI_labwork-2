Debra Oswald  (born 1959) is an Australian actress, best known for her roles in soap operas.  Debra Oswald was born in 1959 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. She began her acting career in the early 1980s, appearing in television series such as "The Young Doctors" and "Police Rescue". Oswald subsequently gained recognition for her roles in soap operas, including appearances on "Neighbours", "Home and Away", and "All Saints". In 2002, she guest starred on the Australian drama series "Breaker Bay". Oswald has since retired from acting.
