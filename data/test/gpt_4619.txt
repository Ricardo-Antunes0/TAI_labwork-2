Founded in 1994 in Spain,  El Compromiso is a non-profit organisation that provides humanitarian aid to victims of conflict and natural disasters.   El Compromiso has worked in the Balkans, Colombia, Haiti, Japan, Macedonia and Sri Lanka. The organisation has also helped refugees in France, Germany, Italy and Spain.   El Compromiso does its work through a network of local partner organisations. It employs a team of field workers and volunteers who provide assistance to those in need.   The organisation is funded by donations from individuals and foundations.
