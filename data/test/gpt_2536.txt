Idol Tour  is a 2017 South Korean reality television series. The show is divided into two parts: the first part, which aired from January 8 to January 15, 2017, followed the contestants as they prepared for their debut on the show "K-Pop Star" and the second part, which aired from January 22 to January 29, 2017, followed the contestants as they performed on the show.  The show was created by JTBC and produced by SPEED. The executive producers are Yeo Jin Woo and Kim Changwan.  The show was met with critical acclaim and became one of the most popular shows of 2017. It was also nominated for Best Reality / Variety Program at the 38th Golden TV Awards.
