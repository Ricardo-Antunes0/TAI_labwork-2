Marharyta Aljakandrauna Fjafilawa ( also written as Marharyta Fefilava or Fefilava Marharyta; born September 9, 1988) is a Samoan rugby union player. She represented Samoa at the 2010 Women's Rugby World Cup.  Fefilava made her international debut for Samoa in a 2010 Women's Rugby World Cup match against France. She played one further match for Samoa at the tournament, against England.  Fefilava currently plays for the USA Eagles women's national rugby union team. She made her debut for the team in a 2014 test match against Australia.
