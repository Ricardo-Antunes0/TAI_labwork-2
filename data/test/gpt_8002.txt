Nilesat 201, is an Egyptian communications satellite, launched in 2009. It was built by Orbital ATK.  Nilesat 201 is a high-throughput communications satellite that provides enhanced coverage of the Middle East, North Africa, Europe and parts of Asia. It is designed to provide enhanced television and radio services to broadcasters, business customers and government organizations across the region.   Nilesat 201 is equipped with 48 Ku-band transponders and 12 C-band transponders to provide high-capacity satellite communication services. The satellite is also equipped with a Ka-band payload for maritime communications.
