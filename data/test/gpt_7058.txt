John Mayor (1735-1817) was an English brewer who is best known for his recipe for London Porter.  John Mayor was born in 1735 in the village of Whitechapel, London. He started working in a brewery in 1755 and became head brewer in 1787. His recipe for London Porter was first published in 1796 and became popularised due to its popularity with the British Army. He died in 1817 and is buried in St Paul's Cathedral.
