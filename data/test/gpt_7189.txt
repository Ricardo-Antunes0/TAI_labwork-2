DO-242A is an aviation system standard published by the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) in 1996. It defines a communication protocol for air traffic control services in air traffic management systems.  The DO-242A is an ICAO standard for aviation communication protocol used in air traffic management systems. The standard defines a communication protocol for use between air traffic control units and other components of an air traffic management system. It was published in 1996 and has been updated several times since then.
