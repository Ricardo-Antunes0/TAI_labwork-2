Thomas de Lanouguère (1644 – May 1678) was a French Roman Catholic cardinal.  He was born in Auch, France, the son of a nobleman. He studied law at the University of Poitiers and then theology at the University of Paris. He was ordained a priest in 1668.  He served as Bishop of Mende from 1668 to 1678. He was created cardinal in 1675. He died in Rome in 1678.
