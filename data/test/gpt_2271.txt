The Crazymen or Crazymen is a punk rock band from Montreal, Quebec, Canada. The band was formed in 1985 by vocalist, guitarist and songwriter Paul Cauthen, bassist and vocalist John Kastelic and drummer Pat Lajoie.   The Crazymen's music is often described as "anthemic punk rock", with lyrics that explore social and political issues. The band has released six albums and has been featured on several compilation albums. The Crazymen have toured extensively across Canada and the United States.
