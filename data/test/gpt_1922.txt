Rock-A-Bye Baby is a 1958 American musical comedy film directed by Robert Lewis and starring The Three Stooges. The Stooges play two vaudeville criminals who are released from prison and must find a way to make ends meet. They live with their old lady (Moe Howard's mother) and try to keep out of trouble.
