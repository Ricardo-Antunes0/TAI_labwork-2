Fatih (Arabic: فَاتِح fātiḥ) is both an Arabic and Turkish given name, meaning "father of victory". The feminine form of the name is Fatima. The name may refer to:  • Fatih Sultan Mehmet, also known as "the Conqueror" and "the Grand Sultan", the Ottoman sultan who ruled from 1451 to 1481 • Fatih Sultan Mehmed II, also known as "the Conqueror" and "the Grand Sultan", the Ottoman sultan who ruled from 1512 to 1520
