Wickremesinghe first entered politics in the early 1950s, when he was appointed the head of the youth wing of the United National Party (UNP). In 1960, he was elected to the Sri Lankan Parliament as an MP for Kandy. He served as Leader of the Opposition from 1966 to 1971, and later served as Minister of Justice from 1971 to 1972.
