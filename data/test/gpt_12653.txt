The Pakistan Press Foundation (PPF) was established in 1988 as a not-for-profit, non-governmental organization with the aim of strengthening and promoting the media in Pakistan. The PPF has played an important role in the development of journalism in Pakistan, working with media organizations to promote standards, professionalism and accuracy in the news reporting process. In addition to its work with the media, the PPF also provides training and education opportunities to journalists and media professionals.
