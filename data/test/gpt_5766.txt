Leavitt Farm is a historic farmstead at 105 Northfield Road in Northfield, Massachusetts. The property includes a main house, barn, and associated outbuildings, dating from the mid-19th century. The main house is a 2-1/2 story wood frame structure, with a gabled roof, clapboard siding, and a central chimney. The property was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
