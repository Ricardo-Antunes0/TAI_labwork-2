The Pyŏlgigun, Byeolgigun, Gyoryeonbyeongdae, or Waepyeolgi (Korean: 화이트, 波銃, 韓式銃, 輪掌推軸式銃, 완벽기, 비즈기) is a Korean semi-automatic rifle. It was developed in the early 1990s by the Korean military in response to the Soviet AK-74 and is based on the AR-15 design. The rifle is used by the South Korean military and police.
