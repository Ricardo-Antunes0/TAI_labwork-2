Bratislav "Bata" Anastasijević (April 18, 1936, Niš, Serbia – May 11, 2006, Los Angeles, California) was a Serbian-American jazz and blues guitarist, composer and bandleader.  Anastasijević was born in Niš, Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. In 1958 he emigrated to the United States and settled in Los Angeles. He played with many noted musicians of his time, including Miles Davis, John Coltrane, Cannonball Adderley, Sonny Rollins, Freddie Hubbard and Yusef Lateef. He also recorded several albums as a leader. Anastasijević died from cancer in 2006 in Los Angeles at the age of 70.
