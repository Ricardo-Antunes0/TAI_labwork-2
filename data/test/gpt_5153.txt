Steven D'Wayne Townsend is an Associate Professor of Psychology at the University of Utah. Townsend's research focuses on social cognition and emotion, with a particular focus on the role that emotions play in social interaction. He has also published work on the relationship between creativity and emotions, and the role that emotions play in self-regulation.  Townsend's work has been featured in journals such as "Personality and Social Psychology Review", "Journal of Experimental Social Psychology", and "Journal of Personality and Social Psychology". He has also been interviewed by various media outlets, including "The New York Times", "The Washington Post", and " TIME Magazine".  Townsend was born in Salt Lake City, Utah, in 1970. He received his undergraduate degree from Brigham Young University in 1992, and his graduate degree from the University of Utah in 1997.
