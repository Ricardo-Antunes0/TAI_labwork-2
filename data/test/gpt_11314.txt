Artful Dodger is a UK garage duo, consisting of brothers Russ and Ross Smith. The duo's debut single "I'm a Freak" was released in 2001 on the London-based label, Big Dada Records. The song reached number one on the UK Singles Chart and was followed up by their debut album, "I'm a Freak... and That's Good", which was released in 2002.   The duo disbanded in 2003, but reunited in 2007. They released their second album, "Garage Days", in 2009. The album reached number four on the UK Albums Chart. In 2010, they released their third album, "In the Shadows". The album reached number eight on the UK Albums Chart.
