Alexandra Ana Maria Agiurgiuculese (born January 15, 1983 in Chişinău) is a Moldovan singer, songwriter, and actress. Agiurgiuculese represented her country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2008 in Belgrade, Serbia, with the song "I Believe in Love". She placed eighteenth with 102 points.  Agiurgiuculese was born into a musical family. Her father is a bass guitarist and her mother is a pianist. She started singing at age five and performed in various talent shows before making her professional debut in 2001. She has since released three albums and has appeared in several films and television shows.  In 2008, Agiurgiuculese represented Moldova at the Eurovision Song Contest with the song "I Believe in Love". She reached the eighteenth position with 102 points.
