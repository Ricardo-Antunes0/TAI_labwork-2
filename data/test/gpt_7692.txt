War of Legends was a massively multiplayer online game developed by Riot Games. The game was released in China on March 7, 2013, and internationally on May 10, 2013. The game is set in the world of Valoran, a world of magic and intrigue.  War of Legends is a three-dimensional online battle arena game that pits teams of five players against each other in a series of five matches, each lasting up to 20 minutes. Players use a variety of champions to battle it out on the Fields of Justice, using their unique strengths to strategically outmaneuver and defeat their opponents.  The game is free-to-play, with optional microtransactions that can be used to purchase in-game items such as champions, skins, or boosts. As of July 2017, War of Legends has over 150 million active players.
