The Salt Lake Theatre was a 1,500-seat theater in Salt Lake City, Utah. Constructed in 1912, it was the first theater in the city and was used mainly for live performances. The theater closed in 1978 and was demolished in 1985.  The Salt Lake Theatre was constructed in 1912 and was the first theater in the city.
