Hollyoaks Later (originally Late Night Hollyoaks) is a spin-off show that aired on Channel 4 in the United Kingdom from 10 October to 24 November 2016. The show is a continuation of the soap's 19th series and is set in the fictional town of Hollyoaks after dark.  The show stars new cast members including Jessica Forrest, Harrison James, and Connor Swindells as well as returning cast members including Preeya Kalidas, Jamie Lomas, and Daniel Kilkelly.  The show follows the storylines of characters who have been locked away in police cells, nightclubs and other locations after the sun has set. The show is produced by Hollyoaks Productions and is written by Paul Marquess.
