Krishnan Marriage Story is an Indian Kannada-language romantic drama film written and directed by debutante Nagarjuna Akkineni. Produced by Akkineni's father Nagarjuna Sr. and uncle K. S. Raghava Rao, the film features Akkineni in the titular role, with debutante Jyothika and an ensemble cast including Darshan, Rangayana Raghu, Anupam Kher, Upendra and Shruti Haasan.  The film is based on the novel of the same name by author Mahadeva Bhatt. The story revolves around Krishnan (Akkineni) who falls in love with Radha (Jyothika), but is forced to marry her sister Geetha (Shruti Haasan). Radha's love for Krishnan proves to be stronger and they eventually get married against everyone's wishes. The couple faces many difficulties in their relationship, but eventually find happiness together.  The film was announced in January 2017 and began production in February that year. It was released on 15 September 2017 to positive reviews from critics.
