"Ama Figo"  is a song by Brazilian singer Zeca Pagodinho. It was released in 1966 as the lead single from his album Zeca Pagodinho.  "Ama Figo" is a ballad about a lover who is indifferent to the singer's affections. According to Zeca, the song is about a woman who is not interested in him and prefers another man.
