The Elk River is a tidal tributary of the Susquehanna River in the U.S. state of Maryland. It is approximately long and flows through Cecil, Harford, and Anne Arundel counties.  The Elk River is home to a number of parks and recreational areas, including North Point State Park, Elkridge Landing State Park, and the Elk River Wildlife Management Area. The river also provides drinking water to portions of the Baltimore metropolitan area and serves as a major transportation corridor for goods and people.
