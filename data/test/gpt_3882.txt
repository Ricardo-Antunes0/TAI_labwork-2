Évry Cathedral (; "Évry Cathedral of the Sacred Heart") is a Roman Catholic church and former cathedral in Évry, France. It was designed by the architect Paul Viollet-le-Duc and completed in 1895.  The cathedral has been the seat of the Bishop of Évry since 1962. The current bishop is Pierre-Marie Morel.  Évry Cathedral was declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 1983.
