Lien-Ju Anne Chao  is a Taiwanese American businesswoman and political strategist who is the current Secretary of Labor in the Trump administration. Chao is also the founder and president of The Chao Group, a consulting firm.  Chao was born in Taiwan in 1964 to parents who emigrated from China. She moved to the United States with her family when she was four years old, and grew up in Southern California. After graduating from Stanford University with a degree in engineering, Chao worked as a engineer for Silicon Graphics before founding her own business, The Chao Group, in 1998. The company specializes in strategic consulting and marketing services.  Chao served as chairman of the Republican National Committee from 2011 to 2013, and she was an adviser to Mitt Romney's presidential campaign in 2012. She was nominated for Secretary of Labor by President Donald Trump on November 13, 2017, and was confirmed by the Senate on December 8, 2017.
