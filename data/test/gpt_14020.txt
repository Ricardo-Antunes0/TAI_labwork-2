She was noted for her natural acting and screen presence.  Sumitra Devi was born on 22 July 1923 in the town of Gobindpur in the Bengal region of British India to a Bengali Hindu family. After completing her schooling, she worked as a stenographer before starting her career in cinema in the early 1950s. She began her career with small roles and gradually worked her way up to becoming one of the most popular actresses in Hindi cinema. Her most notable films include "Aan" (1955), "Deewana" (1960), "Kati Patang" (1972), and "Sati" (1977). Devi died on 14 September 2010 at the age of 89 after a long illness.
