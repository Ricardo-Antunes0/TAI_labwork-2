My Little Airport is a Hong Kong-based low-cost carrier with its head office in the Central Business District of Hong Kong. The airline was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in the Central Business District of Hong Kong.  The airline offers flights to a number of destinations in Asia, including Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur, Phnom Penh, Ho Chi Minh City and Yangon. It also offers flights to Europe, including Barcelona, Lisbon and Rome. My Little Airport is a member of the Oneworld airline alliance.
