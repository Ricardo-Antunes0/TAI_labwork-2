Midsumma is Victoria's premier queer arts and music festival. Held annually in mid-September, Midsumma celebrates diversity, inclusivity and creativity through music, art and activism. The festival has something for everyone, with performances from up and coming artists as well as internationally acclaimed performers. Midsumma is a safe space for all people and provides a unique opportunity to experience Victoria's vibrant queer community.
