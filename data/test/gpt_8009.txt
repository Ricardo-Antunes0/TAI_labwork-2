Pyotr Nikolayevich Wrangel (; April 25, 1928), also known as Petrov-Wrangel, was a Russian general and politician who served as the Defense Minister of the Soviet Union from 1985 to 1988.  Pyotr Wrangel was born in what is now Krasnodar Krai, Russia in 1928. He graduated from the Frunze Military Academy in 1951 and became a lieutenant colonel in the Soviet Army. He fought in the Korean War, becoming a major general in 1970. He served as deputy defense minister from 1975 to 1985. He was appointed defense minister in 1985, serving until 1988. During his time as defense minister, he oversaw the military response to the Chernobyl disaster.   Wrangel retired from active duty in 1990 and returned to civilian life. He died on July 17, 2008, aged 80.
