Beautiful People is a 1971 album by the American rock band the Eagles. The album was released on Asylum Records and produced by Leon Russell.  The Eagles' 1971 LP "Beautiful People" is one of the most underrated and overlooked albums of their catalog. The album features some stellar music by the band, highlighted by singles "Take It Easy", "Peaceful Easy Feeling", and "New Kid in Town". The album also has some excellent tracks that didn't make the singles mix, including "You Can't Take it with You" and the title track.
