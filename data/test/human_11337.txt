Raja Saleem Akhtar (Punjabi, )                                                                                                   (8 September 1930 - 22 April 2004) was a former Pakistani cricketer who played for Multan and Sargodha. His sons Wasim Raja and Rameez Raja played for the Pakistan cricket team, while Zaeem Raja played first-class cricket. Early life and career He was a retired civil servant and former cricketer.
