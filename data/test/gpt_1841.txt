"Je te survivrai"  is a 1989 French-language song written and performed by Celine Dion. It is the title track of her album of the same name. The song was produced by Jean-Claude Vannier.  The song tells the story of a love that survives despite difficulties. It has been noted for its strong emotional content.  "Je te survivrai" was released as the lead single from Dion's album "Je te survivrai" on 7 July 1989. It became a commercial success, topping the charts in France, Belgium, Switzerland and Germany and reaching the top ten in Austria, Denmark, Finland, Italy, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Norway, Spain and Sweden. The song won Dion several awards, including the 1989 Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance and the 1990 Golden Globe Award for Best Female Pop Performance.
