Eric Semborski (born June 1, 1993) is an American professional baseball pitcher for the Kansas City Royals of Major League Baseball (MLB). He made his MLB debut in 2015 with the Royals.  Semborski attended Gadsden High School in Gadsden, Alabama, and played college baseball for the Florida Gators. He was drafted by the Royals in the seventh round of the 2013 MLB draft.
