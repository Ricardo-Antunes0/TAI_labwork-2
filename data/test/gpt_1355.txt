Tiago Salazar (born 1972) is a Portuguese artist, sculptor, and installation artist. He currently lives and works in Lisbon.  Salazar was born in 1972 in Lisbon, Portugal. After completing his secondary education at the Lisbon School of Fine Arts, Salazar studied sculpture at the University of Fine Arts in Vienna, Austria, from 1994 to 1997.
