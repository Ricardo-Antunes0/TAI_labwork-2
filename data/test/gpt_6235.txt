L.A. Symphony is an independent hip hop group from Los Angeles, California, United States of America. Formed in 2003, the group is composed of rappers/singers Saul Williams, Jarobi White, and Cardiak.  The group's music is influenced by classical, jazz, and funk music. They have released three albums: "Sound & Color" (2005), "A Brief History of LA Symphony" (2007), and "Imani World" (2012).
