Martim Mércio da Silveira (2 March 1911 – 15 September 1984) was a Brazilian football player who played for Brazil and Santos. He also coached both clubs.  Silveira played for Santos from 1930 to 1936, winning the Campeonato Brasileiro in 1934 and 1935. With Brazil, he won the silver medal at the 1936 Olympic Games. He also played at the 1938 World Cup, where Brazil were knocked out in the semi-finals.  After retiring as a player, Silveira became a coach with Santos. He also managed Atlético Mineiro and Flamengo, both of Brazil. He was inducted into the Brazilian Football Hall of Fame in 1978
