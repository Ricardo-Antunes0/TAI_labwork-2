He is best known for his work on the laws of inheritance, and for his editing and writing of authoritative works on the subject.  Khansari was born in Jerusalem, into a family of rabbis. After completing his religious studies, he served as a rabbi in several communities in Israel. In the 1970s he began to focus exclusively on scholarly work, publishing several books on Jewish law and kabbalah. He died in 2002.
