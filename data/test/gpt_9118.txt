Stephen James Merchant (born 24 November 1974) is an English actor, writer, and comedian. He is best known for his roles as Graham in the British television sitcom Graham Norton Show (2005–2013), Tim in the mockumentary film The Office (2005–2013), and Max in the American television series Parks and Recreation (2009–2015).  Merchant was born in Reading, Berkshire, England. He attended the University of Bristol, where he studied English Literature. Merchant began his career as a stand-up comedian, performing at various venues across the United Kingdom. In 2005, he was cast in the British sitcom Graham Norton Show, for which he wrote and starred. He also appeared in the mockumentary film The Office, as Tim. Merchant co-created and starred in the American television series Parks and Recreation, for which he was nominated for an Emmy Award in 2013. He has also appeared in films such as Get Him to the Greek, Monsters University, and War Horse.
