He directed such classic crime films as The Prowler (1951), The Servant (1963), and Accomplice (1968).  Losey was born in Brooklyn, New York, the son of a preacher. He began his film career as a writer and producer for RKO Pictures in the 1940s. He directed his first feature film, The Prowler, in 1951. His films received critical acclaim and he was nominated for two Academy Awards. In the 1970s he directed two films that were poorly received, but later received critical reappraisal: Accomplice and The Servant. He died from prostate cancer in 1987 at the age of 76.
