Fusarium wilt is a common vascular wilt caused by the fungus Fusarium oxysporum. It is a serious disease of corn, soybeans, popcorn, and other warm season crops. The fungus penetrates the plant through its roots and fungal hyphae grow throughout the plant vascular system, eventually killing it.  Fusarium wilt is caused by a fungus called Fusarium oxysporum.
