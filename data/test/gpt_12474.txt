Wicked Weasel Pty. Ltd. is an Australian software company specialising in the design and development of web applications. Wicked Weasel was founded by Garry Knight in 2002.  Wicked Weasel is a privately owned company based in Melbourne, Australia. The company has a team of experienced developers who have developed a range of web applications for clients such as Telstra, Woolworths, and News Limited. Wicked Weasel's flagship product is the Wicked Weasel Platform, which allows users to create and manage their own web applications.
