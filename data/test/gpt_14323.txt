"End Game" is a song by American rapper Eminem from his eighth studio album Kamikaze (2018). The song was written by Eminem, Boi-1da, Scott Storch and Mike Elizondo and produced by the latter two.  End Game was first teased on the official Kamikaze website on September 5, 2018, with a 30-second video clip featuring Eminem and SZA. The song was officially released as the album's fourth single on December 7, 2018.   "End Game" is a fast-paced, anthemic track that features guest vocals from SZA. The song is about Eminem's experience with addiction and his battle to overcome it. It has been described as "a propulsive ode to self-reliance" and "an intense sonic assault".  "End Game" debuted at number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming Eminem's 43rd number one single in the United States. It also became his sixth number one single on the Billboard Hot 100 in 2018. It also topped the charts in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand and Sweden.
