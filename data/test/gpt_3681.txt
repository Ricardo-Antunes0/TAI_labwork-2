Lindsay McAllister M "The Doctor" McDougall (born March 2, 1965) is a Canadian actor, comedian and television host.  McDougall was born in Toronto, Ontario. He has starred in many Canadian and American television series, including "The Kids in the Hall", "Second City Television", "The Simpsons", "King of the Hill" and "The Office". He also starred in the Canadian series "Heartland" and the U.S. series "The New Adventures of Old Christine". He has hosted Canadian TV shows "Saturday Night Live" and "Jimmy Kimmel Live!", as well as U.S. shows "Chelsea Lately" and "The Tonight Show with Jay Leno".  In 2010, McDougall published a memoir, "Laughing Out Loud: A Life of Fun and Friendship".
