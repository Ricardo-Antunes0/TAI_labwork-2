Nicholas (II) from the kindred Budmér, also known as Nicholas von Budmér, was a Hungarian nobleman, who served as the first Palatine of Hungary from 1390 to 1402.   Nicholas Budmér was the son of Nicholas I and Margaret of Bavaria. He became the Palatine of Hungary in 1390, and held this office until his death in 1402. He was also Count of Transylvania from 1393 to 1402.
