Cinelerra is a video editing and composition software for the Linux and Unix operating systems. It has been developed by the Cinelerra project, a collaborative effort of developers from the free and open source software community.  Cinelerra is a powerful video editing and compositing software with a modular architecture. It supports a wide range of file formats, including AVI, MPEG, DV, FLV, and MOV. It has a feature-rich timeline manager and a powerful compositing engine that allows users to create complex video compositions. Cinelerra also has advanced audio editing capabilities, including support for multi-channel audio files and advanced effects such as noise removal and echo cancellation.  Cinelerra is released under the GNU General Public License.
