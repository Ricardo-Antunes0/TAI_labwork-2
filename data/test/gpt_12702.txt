Biarritz Olympique Pays Basque , usually known simply as Biarritz, is a French professional football club based in the city of Biarritz. The team plays in Ligue 1, the top division of French football. Biarritz Olympique was founded on 12 September 1907 and played its first game on 14 September 1907. The club's home stadium is the Stade Biarritz-Bayonnais, which has a capacity of 26,000 spectators.  The club has won the Coupe de France twice, in 1932 and 2014. Biarritz also reached the final of the European Cup in 1978 and 2002. The team has also finished as runner-up in the Coupe de France twice, in 1928 and 2006. In European competition, Biarritz Olympique has reached the UEFA Cup semi-finals twice, in 1974 and 2007.
