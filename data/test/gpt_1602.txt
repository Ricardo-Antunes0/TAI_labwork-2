The Loening C-1 Air Yacht was an American air yacht that was designed by A.H. Loening and built by the Loening Aircraft Corporation in New York City.  The C-1 was a single-seat, single-engine biplane that could carry up to six passengers. It was first flown in 1934 and remained in production until 1941. The C-1 was used by celebrities and wealthy individuals for recreational flying and cruising.
