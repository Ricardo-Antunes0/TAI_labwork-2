The Belgrade printing house was a printing house in Belgrade, Serbia. It was founded in 1811 by Ignazije Mitrinović. It ceased operations in the early 20th century.  The Belgrade printing house was one of the oldest printing houses in Serbia. It was founded in 1811 by Ignazije Mitrinović, and operated until the early 20th century. The printing house published a variety of books and magazines, including "Novi list", "Istorija", "Glasnik izdavačke kuće", "Zbornik radova na nastavnici učiteljstva obrazovanja" and "Književni index".
