"Tweedlee Dee" (also "Tweedly Dee" or "Tweedle Dee") is a character in the nursery rhyme "Two Little Kittens".  Tweedlee Dee is a small white kitten with black spots. He is described as being "full of fun" and "lively as a lark". According to the rhyme, Tweedlee Dee lives with his sister Twee Pie in a tree. The two kittens love to play together and jump around.
