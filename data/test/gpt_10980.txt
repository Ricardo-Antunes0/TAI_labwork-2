The 89 North Bergen-Hoboken is a bus line that operates in Hudson County, New Jersey. The line runs between the North Bergen terminal in North Bergen and the Hoboken Terminal in Hoboken.  The 89 North Bergen-Hoboken was originally established as the 79th Street Crosstown Line in 1939. The line was extended to its current terminus at the Hoboken Terminal on November 21, 1966. The line has been operated by the New Jersey Transit Corporation since that time.  Today, the 89 North Bergen-Hoboken is one of the busiest bus routes in Hudson County. The route carries an average of over 9,000 passengers each weekday.
