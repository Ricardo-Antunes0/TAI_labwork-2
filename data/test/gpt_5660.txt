Build-a-lot is a 2007 casual video game for the Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3 video game consoles. It is a spin-off of the popular Build-a-lot construction game series.  In Build-a-lot, the player constructs a small town from a selection of pre-made buildings, then helps their community grow by hiring workers, building new businesses, and attracting tourists. Players can also interact with other players online to trade items, build together, or compete in challenges.  The game was developed by Blue Tongue Entertainment and published by Microsoft Game Studios and Sony Computer Entertainment Europe.
