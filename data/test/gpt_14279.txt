Ahmed White (born September 5, 1970) holds the world record for the most consecutive blindfolded back-to-back summits of Mount Everest, having reached the summit of Everest eight times in a row from May 2002 to May 2005.  Ahmed White was born in Cairo, Egypt on September 5, 1970. He is blind. Ahmed White became the first person ever to summit Mount Everest eight times consecutively. He first summited Mount Everest in May 2002 and completed his eighth summit on May 5, 2005. Ahmed White is a member of the Sherpas Tenzing Norgay Base Camp Association and was sponsored by Mountain Hardwear.
