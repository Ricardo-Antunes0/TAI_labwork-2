Cops (stylized as COPS) is an American police procedural television series that originally aired on the Fox network from 1985 to 1994. The show typically follows the daily activities of a fictional police department in Los Angeles, California.  Created by John Langley and Stephen J. Cannell, the show starred Tom Selleck as Vietnam War veteran turned police officer Andy Taylor and Don Johnson as his partner, Rick Hendrix. Other regulars on the show included Tony Scotti (Detective Bobby Simone), Ronny Cox (Detective Sergeant Ernesto Rodriguez), Stacy Keach (Captain Phil Vickers), and Larry Wilmore (Officer Sherman "Butter" Daniels).
