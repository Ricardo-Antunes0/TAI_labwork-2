Vice Admiral Sir Roy Russell Dowling,  KCB, CB, DSO (1909-1991) was a senior officer in the Royal Navy. He served during the Second World War and was appointed Deputy Chief of the Naval Staff in 1958. He was promoted to the rank of Vice Admiral in 1962 and served as Flag Officer, Scotland and Northern Ireland from 1967 to 1971. He was made a Knight Commander of the Order of the British Empire in 1970.  Born on 1 December 1909, Roy Dowling joined the Royal Navy in 1927. He served during the Second World War, initially as a gunnery officer on submarines before becoming Deputy Chief of Staff to the Commander-in-Chief, Home Fleet in 1944. After the war he served as Chief of Staff to the Commander-in-Chief, Mediterranean Fleet until 1951.  Dowling was promoted to the rank of Vice Admiral in 1962 and served as Flag Officer, Scotland and Northern Ireland from 1967 to 1971. He was made a Knight Commander of the Order of the British Empire in 1970. Dowling retired from the Royal Navy in 1971 and died on 22 January 1991.
