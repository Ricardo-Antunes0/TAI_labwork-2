Petric is a Canadian country music band formed in Toronto, Ontario in 2006. The band consists of lead singer and songwriter Petric, guitarist and backing vocalist Steve Kijak, bassist AJ MacLeod, and drummer Andrew Friesen. Their second album, "Life's a Highway", was released in 2009 and produced by Dann Huff. The album was nominated for a 2009 Canadian Country Music Association Award for Album of the Year.  The band's third album, "Mountains on the Horizon", was released in 2011. The album was nominated for a 2011 Canadian Country Music Association Award for Album of the Year.  The band's fourth album, "Distant Star", was released in 2013. The album was nominated for a 2013 Canadian Country Music Association Award for Album of the Year.  The band's fifth album, "Roots & Branches", was released in 2015. The album was nominated for a 2015 Canadian Country Music Association Award for Album of the Year.
