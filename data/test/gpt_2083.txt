Bakerloo (previously The Bakerloo Blues Line) was a British blues rock band formed in London in 1975.  The band was formed by ex-members of The Animals, including vocalist Robert Plant, guitarist John Paul Jones, bass player John Bonham and drummer Kenney Jones. The group released two albums, "Bakerloo" (1976) and "Ride on the Wild Wind" (1978), before breaking up in 1979. Since their break-up, Plant has released six solo albums, while Jones has collaborated with a range of artists, most notably with Led Zeppelin's Jimmy Page.
