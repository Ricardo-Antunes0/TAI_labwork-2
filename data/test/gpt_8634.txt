is a tactical role-playing game for the PlayStation Portable and PlayStation 3, developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment.  The game is a sequel to the 2003 game "Yggdra Union", and was announced at the 2010 PlayStation Media Showcase in Japan. It was released in Japan on March 26, 2011, in North America on April 15, 2011, in Europe on April 22, 2011, and in Australia on May 5, 2011.  The game is set 1000 years after the events of the original game. The player assumes the role of either Yggdra or Ludger, one of the two main protagonists from the original game, who must stop a group of dark elves led by a powerful elf named Arianrhod from destroying all of humanity. The game features a new battle system that allows players to control multiple characters simultaneously in order to take down enemies.  "Yggdra Union" was met with mixed reviews from critics. However, many praised its graphical design and unique battle system.
