Grant M. DePorter (born November 7, 1964) is an American film and television actor. He is best known for his roles as Dr. Max Jenkins in the NBC medical drama ER, and as Deputy Sheriff Roy Purdy on the TNT crime drama series Major Crimes.  DePorter was born in Hollywood, California, the son of actress Joanne Woodward and director and producer Roger DePorter. He has two sisters, actress Annette DePorter and producer Lisa DePorter. He attended The Buckley School in La Jolla, California and then studied at the University of Southern California.  DePorter began his career in television as a regular on the short-lived ABC drama series Darkwing Duck before landing his breakthrough role as Dr. Max Jenkins on the NBC medical drama ER from 1994 to 1998. He also appeared as Deputy Sheriff Roy Purdy on the TNT crime drama series Major Crimes from 2006 to 2009. In 2013, he starred as Dr. David Addison in the Fox medical drama series House M.D. for one season.
