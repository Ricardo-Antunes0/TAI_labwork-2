The Lincoln Stars are a Tier I junior ice hockey team based in Lincoln, Nebraska. The Stars play in the United States Hockey League (USHL) and are members of the Western Conference.  The Lincoln Stars were founded in 1988 as a member of the Central Junior Hockey League. The Stars won the CCHL championship in 1993, 1998 and 2006. In 2007, they joined the USHL as an expansion team and finished first in their inaugural season. The Stars have appeared in the USHL Championship Game twice, in 2003 and 2006.
