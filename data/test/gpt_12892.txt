The  form one of two branches of the Ryukyuan languages, which is a group of languages spoken in the southern Ryukyu Islands. The other branch is the Northern Ryukyuan languages. The Southern Ryukyuan languages are spoken on the main islands of Okinawa, Kerama Retto, Yaeyama and the Miyako Islands. There are about twenty six Southern Ryukyuan languages, which are all mutually intelligible.
