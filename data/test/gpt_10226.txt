Operation Bribie (17â€“18 February 1967), also known as the Bribie Island Raid, was a commando raid on the Australian island of Bribie. The raid was conducted by the Australian Army's 2/6th Commando Squadron, supported by elements of the Royal Australian Navy. The objective of the raid was to capture or kill Harold Holt, the Prime Minister of Australia.  The operation began on 17 February 1967 at 2:00am local time and ended two hours later. Three members of the unit were killed, four were wounded, and Holt was not captured or killed. The operation has been described as a complete failure.
