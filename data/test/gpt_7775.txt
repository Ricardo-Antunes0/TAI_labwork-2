Wang Bing (; born 1967) is a Chinese film director.  Wang Bing was born in 1967 in Beijing, China. He studied at the Beijing Film Academy and made his feature debut with the film "A Woman Called Golda" (1998). Wang's subsequent films have been noted for their poetic and often abstract visual styles.  Wang Bing was awarded the Best Director Award at the 2007 Asia-Pacific Film Festival for his film "The World".
