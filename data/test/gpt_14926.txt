Operation Candid: Protection of the Royal Family of Monaco   On 11 November 2005, the Principality of Monaco was the target of a terrorist attack. A group of gunmen dressed as police officers stormed the palace and shot at the Prince and Princess of Monaco, wounding them both. The attack was unsuccessful and all but two of the gunmen were killed.  The Principality of Monaco is a sovereign state located in Southern Europe, on the Mediterranean Sea. It is one of only two landlocked countries in the world, the other being Liechtenstein. The principality is surrounded by France and has a population of just over 39,000. The principality's capital is Monte Carlo, and it is a member state of the European Union, the United Nations, and the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development.
