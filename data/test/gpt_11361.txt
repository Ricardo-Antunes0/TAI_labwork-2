ViziApps is an online mobile application platform for businesses.  The ViziApps platform provides businesses with an easy way to create, manage and publish mobile applications. With ViziApps, businesses can quickly and easily build and launch mobile apps that are tailored to their specific needs and customers.  ViziApps was founded in 2013 by CEO Brooke Hargrave and CTO Matt Fisher. The company has since raised over $3 million in funding from investors including 500 Startups, Andreessen Horowitz, and Redpoint Ventures.
