Allan Hawco is a Canadian writer, actor, and comedian. He is known for his work on the television series Corner Gas, The Newsroom, and Bull.  Allan Hawco was born in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, on July 21, 1967. He began his career as a writer and actor on the Canadian television series Corner Gas, for which he was nominated for a Gemini Award for Best Performance by an Actor in a Comedy Series. He later starred in the U.S. television series The Newsroom and its Canadian spin-off The Newsroom Canada. He has also appeared in films such as Dead Man's Shoes, I'm Not There, and Young People Fucking.  Hawco is married to actress Lisa Hawco and they have two children.
