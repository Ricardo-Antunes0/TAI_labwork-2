Pramodhawardhani (also known as Çrī  Kahulunnan) is a village in the Palghar district of Maharashtra, India.  The village is located on the National Highway 4, around 50 km from Mumbai. Pramodhawardhani has a population of around 2000 people.  The village is known for its handloom and cotton production. The village also has a small agricultural sector which produces fruits, vegetables and dairy products. The villagers also engage in cottage industry such as manufacturing handicrafts and garments.  Pramodhawardhani has a school and a health center which provide basic healthcare services to the villagers. The village also has a small market which provides essential goods and services to the villagers.
