"Crackerbox Palace" is the ninth track on the third album by the British rock band Queen, released in 1981. It was written by the band's frontman Freddie Mercury and producer Roy Thomas Baker.  "Crackerbox Palace" is a rock ballad with a slow tempo and a heavy guitar riff. The lyrics describe the protagonist's disillusionment with society and his desire to retreat into a world of fantasy and escape. The song has been praised for its poetic lyrics and Mercury's vocal performance.
