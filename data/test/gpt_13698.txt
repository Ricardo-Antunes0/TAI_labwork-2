Tim Rimbui (born 3 August 1981), better known by his stage name Timbaland, is an American record producer, songwriter, rapper and musician. He has been included on the list of 50 most successful music producers of all time by Billboard.  Rimbui was born in Springfield, Missouri, and raised in St. Louis. He started making beats at the age of 14, using the software FL Studio. He later attended the University of Missouri-St. Louis and studied music production. After graduating in 2004, he began producing for local artists and formed his own label, Mosley Music Group, with childhood friend Christian Rich. In 2007, Timbaland released his debut album Shock Value, which spawned the singles "Give It to Me" and "The Way I Are". The album reached number one on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). In 2009, he released his second album Timbaland Presents: Shock Value II which featured the singles "Apologize" and "Rock Your Body". The album peaked at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified platinum by the RIAA. In 2011, Timbaland released his third album Timbaland Presents: Shock Value III which featured the single "In My Feelings".
