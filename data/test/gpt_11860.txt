Mel Croucher is a British entrepreneur and television personality.  Croucher is the co-founder, with her husband, of the reality television show "The Jump" which was first broadcast on BBC One in February 2016. The show follows contestants as they compete in a series of physical challenges, while living in a confined space for thirty days.   Croucher has also appeared on other reality television shows, including "MasterChef UK", "The Great British Bake Off" and "The Jump: Champions". In 2018, she starred in her own cooking show, "Mel's Kitchen", which aired on ITV.   Croucher was born in Portsmouth, Hampshire, and raised in the town of Bournemouth. She attended St Michael's School in Bournemouth and then studied business at Nottingham Trent University.
