Breakaway PPV was a Canadian English language pay-per-view event produced by the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) promotion. The event took place on October 7, 2003 at the Air Canada Centre in Toronto, Ontario.  The main event of the night was a Fall Brawl match between then-WWE Champion Brock Lesnar and then-Intercontinental Champion Rob Van Dam. The co-main event was a Six Man Tag Team match between The Dudley Boyz (Bubba Ray Dudley and D-Von Dudley), Chris Jericho and The Rock (then-WWE Champion).  The undercard featured matches between The New Age Outlaws (Billy Gunn and Road Dogg), Kurt Angle and Rob Conway, Booker T and Goldust, and Chyna (then-WWE Women's Champion) and Test.
