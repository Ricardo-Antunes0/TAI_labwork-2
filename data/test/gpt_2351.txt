Glory Road is a 2006 American sports drama film directed by Peter Berg and written by Will Smith and Akiva Goldsman. The film tells the story of an inner-city high school basketball team from Detroit, Michigan, as they attempt to win the state championship.  The film stars Will Smith, Terrence Howard, Taraji P. Henson, Nick Cannon, Omar Epps, and Rosario Dawson. It was released on June 27, 2006 in the United States by Universal Pictures. The film received positive reviews from critics and grossed over $236 million at the international box office.
