Dukes Meadows is a riverside park in eastern Northampton, England. The park is situated on the River Nene, between the towns of Northampton and Wellingborough.  The park covers an area of 2.5 hectares and has a wide range of facilities, including a children's play area, a cricket ground, a football pitch, a skatepark, a BMX track and a tennis court. It is also home to the Dukes Meadows Golf Club, which was founded in 1892. The park is used by locals and tourists for recreation and relaxation.
