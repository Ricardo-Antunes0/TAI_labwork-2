Southern Blood: New Australian Tales of the Violent South is a collection of eleven short stories by Australian authors, edited by Shaun Tan. It was published in paperback in Australia on 13 October 2010.  The stories are set in the fictional town of Salinas in the Australian state of Victoria. They depict a society where violence is endemic and where the boundaries between right and wrong are often blurred.  The book has been well received by critics, who have praised its atmosphere and its author's talent for writing violent scenes convincingly.
