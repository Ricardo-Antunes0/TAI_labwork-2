"This Is Halloween" is a song from the Halloween soundtrack. It was written by John Carpenter, Alan Howarth, and Gary Sherman.  The song was originally recorded for the 1978 movie of the same name but was not used in the final cut. It was released as a single in 1979, peaking at number 25 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. The song has been covered by a number of artists, most notably The Scissor Sisters and Oingo Boingo.
