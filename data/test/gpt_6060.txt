Joann Kagan Downey (born October 31, 1966) is an American actress, comedian, and writer. She is known for her roles as Karen Walker on the NBC sitcom Will & Grace and Margo Martindale's daughter on the CBS sitcom The Good Wife.  Downey was born in Los Angeles, California, the daughter of actress Joanna Carlisle and comedian Dick Downey. She has two sisters, Christine and Catherine. Downey began her career as a stand-up comic in the early 1990s. Her breakthrough role came in 2003 when she was cast as Karen Walker on the NBC sitcom Will & Grace. The show became one of the most successful television series in history, ending in 2006 with total ratings of over 18 million viewers. Downey starred as Margo Martindale's daughter on the CBS sitcom The Good Wife from 2009 to 2017.  Downey has appeared in a number of feature films throughout her career, including 2004's Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle and 2007's I Now Pronounce You Chuck and Larry. She has also written several books, including a memoir entitled Blonde Ambition: A Memoir of Living My Dream (2015). Downey is married to actor Greg Kinnear, with whom she has two children.
