Yang Se-jong (born ) is a South Korean politician who has been the seventh president of South Korea since 2013. He was first elected to the National Assembly in 2000, and served as the Minister of Education from 2008 to 2013. On 19 February 2013, he was nominated by President Park Geun-hye to be the seventh president of South Korea. He was elected to a four-year term on 9 May 2013.    Yang is a member of the Democratic United Progressive Party (DUPP), and served as its chairman from 2009 to 2013. He also served as a member of the National Assembly's Education Committee from 2001 to 2003 and from 2008 to 2013.
