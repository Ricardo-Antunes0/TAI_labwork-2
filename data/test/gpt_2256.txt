Richard Kearney (; born 1954, Cork, Ireland) is an Irish journalist and writer. He is the author of nine books, including the memoirs "The Fall: A Memoir of the Celtic Tiger Years" (2010) and "The Lost Boys: A Story of the Irish Revolution" (2016).  Kearney was educated at Presentation Brothers College in Cork, and Trinity College, Dublin, where he took a degree in History. He began his career as a journalist with the "Irish Times", before moving to London to work for "The Observer". He returned to Ireland in 1995, and has since written for "The Sunday Times", "The Irish Independent", "The New York Times", "The Guardian", and other publications.  Kearney is a Distinguished Professor at University College Dublin. In October 2010 he was appointed a Member of the Order of Australia for his services to journalism.
