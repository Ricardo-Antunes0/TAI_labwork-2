Olkhovsky (; masculine), Olkhovskaya (; feminine), or Olkhovskoye (; neuter) is an inhabited locality in the Central Federal District of Russia. Population:   Olkhovsky is located near the north-western border of the Central Federal District, on the left bank of the Olkhov River, in the Kirovsky District. According to the 2010 Census, the population of Olkhovsky was 1,502 inhabitants, down from 1,826 recorded in the 2002 Census. The Olkhov River forms the border between Olkhovsky and Kirovsky Districts. The settlement was first mentioned in 1549 as "Olykaevo".
