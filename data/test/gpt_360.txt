Ryde Arena (previously ‘Planet Ice Isle of Wight’) is a 5,000 seat ice hockey arena located in Ryde, Isle of Wight, England. The arena was opened in 1987 and has been the home of the Isle of Wight Islanders of the British National League since 1991.  Ryde Arena is the home of the Isle of Wight Islanders of the British National League. The arena has seen many famous hockey players pass through its doors including; Ian Turnbull, Andy Cherry, Dave Lowry, Colin McDonald and Phil Esposito. The arena also played host to the Isle of Wight Festival of Ice in 2007-2008 which saw over 10,000 spectators attend over two weekends.
