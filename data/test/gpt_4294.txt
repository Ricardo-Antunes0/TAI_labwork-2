The Save Darfur Coalition was an advocacy group formed in 2006 to end the Darfur genocide in Sudan. The group was composed of representatives from over 50 non-governmental organizations, and its stated purpose was to pressure the Sudanese government to end the violence and protect the civilians in Darfur.
