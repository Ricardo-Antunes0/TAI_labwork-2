The Ford F-Series is a series of trucks manufactured by Ford. The series includes a wide range of vehicles, from the smallest pickup trucks to the largest superduty trucks. The F-Series was originally designed as a replacement for the Ford Bronco, and has been sold in North America since the 1960s.  The F-Series is one of the most popular truck lines in the world and has been sold in more than 170 countries. The F-Series is popular for its versatility, durability, and fuel efficiency. The series is also known for its strong sales performance and numerous industry awards.
