{{Infobox album | name     = Corazón latino  | image = albumcover.jpg | lyrics =  Corazón latino is the sixth studio album by Puerto Rican singer-songwriter Gilberto Santa Rosa. It was released on April 21, 2011, by Sony Music Latin. The album was produced by Santa Rosa and Alex Lipsky and features collaborations with Mexican singer-songwriter Juan Gabriel, Colombian singer-songwriter Andrés Calamaro, and Brazilian singer-songwriter Caetano Veloso.   The album's lyrical content deals with love and heartache, and its musical style incorporates traditional Latin music with contemporary elements. Critical reception to the album was mixed, with some reviewers praising its innovative production and Santa Rosa's emotive vocals, while others found its eclectic mix of genres and lyrics unappealing.
