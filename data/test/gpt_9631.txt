"Grown-Up Christmas List" (sometimes titled "My Grown-Up Christmas List") is a article in the December 2006 issue of The Oprah Magazine, written by actress and author Reese Witherspoon.  In the article, Witherspoon reflects on the various things she would like for her own Christmas list, whether it's material possessions or experiences that she would like to have during the holiday season. She also offers advice to those who are planning their own Christmas gifts for loved ones.
