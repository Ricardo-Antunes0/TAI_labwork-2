John Ferneley College is a mixed college located in the town of Wokingham, Berkshire, England.  The college was founded in 1974 as a co-educational comprehensive school for pupils aged 11 to 18. In 1987 it became a secondary school and in 2002 it became an all-male independent school. In September 2006 it merged with The Wokingham School to form Wokingham College.  John Ferneley College has a range of facilities, including a sports hall, a library, an art room and a music room. It also has a canteen, a common room and a gym. The college has a total of 1,000 students.
