Darova (;  or Kranichstätten) is a small town in the district of Graz-Umgebung in Styria, Austria. It is situated on the Darß River, 10 km northeast of Graz. As of 2011, it has a population of 2,023.  Darova was first mentioned in a document from 1226 as "Dorf auf dem Darß". In 1938 it was elevated to town status.
