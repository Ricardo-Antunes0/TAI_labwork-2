Huang Junhua  (; born 29 December 1985) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Hebei China Fortune.  Huang Junhua started his professional football career with Beijing Guoan in the 2002 Chinese Super League. He then transferred to Shanghai Shenhua in 2006 and helped the club win the league title in 2007. Huang transferred to Hebei China Fortune in January 2013.  Huang Junhua has made one appearance for the Chinese national team. He made his debut for the national team in a friendly match against Thailand on 5 March 2010.  Huang Junhua was born in Beijing, China. He has two younger brothers, Huang Dongpeng and Huang Chengliang, both of whom are footballers.
