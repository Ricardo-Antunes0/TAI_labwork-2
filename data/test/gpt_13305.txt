Sigourney Public Library is located in Sigourney, Iowa, and has been in operation since 1871. It is the only public library in Sigourney and serves the residents of Sigourney, the surrounding rural communities, and parts of New Richmond and Mount Pleasant. The library has a collection of more than 30,000 items, including books, music, movies, and video games. It also offers programming for adults and children, including classes on creative writing, photography, and history.
