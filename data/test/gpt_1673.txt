Zevri Abseitov  along with Remzi Memetov, is the founder and director of the Samarkand-based NGO "Watching the Moon". In 2010, Abseitov and Memetov were awarded the prestigious Sakharov Prize for their work on freedom of expression.  Zevri Abseitov was born in Samarkand, Uzbekistan, in 1978. He obtained a degree in history from Moscow State University in 2001. After graduation, he worked as a researcher at the Russian Academy of Sciences' Institute of Oriental Studies. In 2007, he founded the NGO "Watching the Moon", which works to promote freedom of expression and human rights in Uzbekistan. In 2010, Abseitov and Memetov were awarded the prestigious Sakharov Prize for their work on freedom of expression.
