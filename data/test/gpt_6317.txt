The Phillips Haymakers football team represented the Phillips University in NCAA Division II college football. The team played its home games at the Kline Field on the campus of Phillips University in Enid, Oklahoma. The team was a member of the Heartland Conference.  Phillips Haymakers football began play in 1974. The team competed as an independent until 1992 when it joined the Heartland Conference. The Haymakers made their first appearance in the playoffs in 1994 and have appeared in every playoff since then. In 2007, the team made its first trip to the Division II Football National Championship game where they lost to West Texas A&M. In 2012, the Haymakers won their first conference championship and advanced to the Division II Football Championship game where they lost to North Dakota State.
