Neelkanth  is a feature film based on the Hindu epic poem, the Mahabharata. The film is directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali and produced by Bhansali Productions.  The story is about a young man, Rishi Neelkanth, who is destined to be the next king of Hastinapur. However, Rishi is ostracized by his father and the kingdom's elite because he is not a warrior like them. Rishi must prove himself to be worthy of the throne and save Hastinapur from destruction.  The cast of Neelkanth includes Deepika Padukone as Rishi Neelkanth, Ranveer Singh as Arjun, Priyanka Chopra as Draupadi, and Shahid Kapoor as Prince Dhritarashtra.
