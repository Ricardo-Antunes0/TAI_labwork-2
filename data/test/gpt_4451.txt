Caroline Barker is a British sports journalist who has written for The Guardian, Independent, and the Observer.  Barker was born in London in 1972. She read English at Cambridge and began her career as a journalist in 1994 at The Observer. In 2000 she moved to The Guardian, where she has been a sports correspondent since 2004. She has also written for the Independent, and is a regular contributor to BBC Radio 4's Today programme.  Barker is the author of two books: A Question of Sport: A Life in Pursuit of Justice (2004) and The Outsiders: Eight Unconventional Lives in a World of Sports (2010). She has also written for the BBC's Panorama programme, and is a contributing editor to the London Review of Books.
