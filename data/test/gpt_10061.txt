Anthony Shey Tarke (born May 27, 1998) is an American professional basketball player for the Chicago Bulls of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the Duke Blue Devils.  Tarke was born in Indianapolis, Indiana. He attended Lawrence Central High School in Indianapolis, where he played high school basketball for the Lawrence Central Raiders. As a senior, he averaged 18.4 points, 9.4 rebounds and 3.3 assists per game, leading Lawrence Central to the Class 4A state championship game. He was also a two-time Indiana Mr. Basketball selection.  Tarke played college basketball for the Duke Blue Devils. As a freshman, he averaged 10.2 points, 4.5 rebounds and 1.8 assists per game in 33 games (32 starts). As a sophomore, he averaged 16.5 points, 7.8 rebounds and 2.1 assists per game in 36 games (35 starts). As a junior, he averaged 21.1 points, 8.3 rebounds and 2.8 assists per game in 37 games (36 starts). As a senior, he averaged 25.9 points, 11 rebounds and 2.9 assists per game in 37 games (36 starts). Tarke was named first-team All-ACC and ACC Player of the Year as a junior and senior, respectively.  On March 30, 2019, Tarke was selected with the fifth overall pick in the 2019 NBA draft by the Chicago Bulls.
