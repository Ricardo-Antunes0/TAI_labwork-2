Jonathan Bruce Hancock is the author of the novel, The Sacred Lies of Minnie Driver. He is also the co-creator and host of the popular podcast, The Memory Palace. Hancock was born in Long Beach, California in 1971. After graduating from high school, he studied at the University of California, Santa Cruz before transferring to the University of Southern California. Hancock's first novel, The Sacred Lies of Minnie Driver, was published in 2006.
