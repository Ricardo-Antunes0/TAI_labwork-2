Saw IV: Original Motion Picture Score is the soundtrack to the fourth installment of the Saw franchise, Saw IV. The album was released on October 16, 2009, by Sumthing Else Music Works and entered the Billboard 200 chart at number 158.  The score for Saw IV was composed by Michael Giacchino and was his first score for a Saw film. The album features a total of thirty-six tracks, with eleven tracks written by Giacchino, five tracks written by Jigsaw and one track written by Corey Burton.  The score received mixed reviews from music critics. Some praised the composer's ability to create suspenseful and atmospheric music, while others dismissed the score as being generic and unoriginal. Despite this, Giacchino was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Original Score for the film.
