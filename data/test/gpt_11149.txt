Ishq-e-Laila is a 1957 Pakistani Urdu language romantic drama film directed by Sheikh Sani Khan and produced by Muhammad Ali. The film stars Mehboob Khan, Noor Jehan, and Mumtaz Mahal in lead roles. The screenplay is written by Wajid Anwar.  The film tells the story of two lovers - a rich nobleman, Sahir, and a poor girl, Laila - who are separated by circumstances. Sahir is imprisoned for a crime he did not commit and Laila is forced to marry another man. Years later, Sahir is released from prison and seeks out Laila. He finds her married to another man but still loves her. Sahir finally manages to win Laila's heart and they live happily ever after.
