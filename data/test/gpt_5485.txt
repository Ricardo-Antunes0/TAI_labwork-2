Mao Zedong 1949  is a 2019 biographical drama film directed by Zhang Yimou and written by Tian Zhuangzhuang. The film is based on the life of Mao Zedong, the founder of the People's Republic of China.  Zhang Yimou stated in an interview with Chinese news outlet CGTN in December 2018 that "Mao Zedong 1949" is his best work to date.  The film began principal photography on January 22, 2019 and was shot in the provinces of Hebei, Henan, Shaanxi, Gansu and Liaoning.  "Mao Zedong 1949" was theatrically released in China on October 18, 2019. It has grossed over $80 million at the Chinese box office.
