Oleg Petrovych Fisunenko (14 November 1930 - 30 May 1995) was a Ukrainian scientist, who made notable contributions to the fields of laser physics and spectroscopy.  Fisunenko was born in Kiev, then in the Soviet Union. He studied at the Leningrad Polytechnic Institute, where he received his doctorate in 1954. From 1954 to 1958 he was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. In 1958 he joined the faculty of Kiev State University, where he remained until his death.   Fisunenko was a member of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences and Arts, and the Russian Academy of Sciences.
