Robert Wayne "Bob" Burcham, Sr. (August 22, 1922 – February 1, 2007) was an American football player and coach. He served as the head football coach at the University of Mississippi from 1957 to 1965, compiling a record of 38–52–5. He was also the head coach at Louisiana Tech from 1966 to 1969, compiling a record of 25–25.
