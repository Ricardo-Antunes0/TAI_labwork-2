Thampanoor is the geographic center (CBD) of Ernakulam district, in the Indian state of Kerala. It is located about 8 kilometres from the city center of Ernakulam.  Thampanoor is one of the most important religious and cultural centers in Kerala. The first church in Thampanoor was built in 1612. The main deity of the church is St. Thomas the Apostle. Thampanoor is also known for its traditional silk industry.
