No Business is the tenth album by the American rock band The White Stripes. It was released on October 15, 2011, through Iamsound and Third Man Records.  The White Stripes' tenth album, No Business, was recorded in early 2011 at Third Man Studios in Nashville, Tennessee with producer Jack White. The album was announced on July 8, 2011, and its first single, "Seven Nation Army", was released on July 11.  No Business has a minimalist aesthetic; the cover art features a white stripe against a black background. The album's lyrics cover a wide range of topics including love ("I Just Don't Know What to Do with Myself"), death ("Guillotine"), and paranoia ("Just One Kiss"). Critical response to the album has been generally positive, with many reviewers calling it The White Stripes' best work in years.
