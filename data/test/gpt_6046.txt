Charlie Nicholes Holmberg (born April 4, 1988) is an American professional ice hockey defenseman who is currently playing with the Hershey Bears in the American Hockey League (AHL).  Holmberg was drafted by the Chicago Blackhawks in the second round, 52nd overall, of the 2006 NHL Entry Draft. He played three seasons of college hockey with the University of Minnesota-Duluth from 2005 to 2007, amassing 64 points in 119 games.
