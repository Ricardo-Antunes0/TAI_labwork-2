Bob van Asperen (born 8 October 1947, in Eindhoven) is a Dutch astronomer and Professor at the University of Groningen.  He has been active in researches on the motion and evolution of stars, galaxies and extragalactic objects, as well as on the physics of black holes. In 2002 he was awarded the Spinoza Prize, the highest scientific award in the Netherlands.  In 2012 he was elected a Fellow of the Royal Society.
