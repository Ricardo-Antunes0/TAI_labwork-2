Steadfast Football Club is a Ghanaian professional football club based in the city of Kumasi. The club competes in the Ghanaian Premier League.  Steadfast Football Club was founded in 1988. Steadfast made their debut in the Ghanaian Premier League in 2000 and have competed in every season since then. The club has won the Ghanaian Premier League twice, in 2003 and 2005. They also reached the final of the CAF Champions League in 2004, but were defeated by Al-Ahly of Egypt. Steadfast also reached the semi-finals of the CAF Cup in 2007 and 2009.  The club plays its home matches at the Richard Mofolo Stadium, which has a capacity of 16,000 spectators.
