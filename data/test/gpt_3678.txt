Mina Shum (born 1966) is an independent curator and writer based in Berlin.  Shum has exhibited her work internationally and her recent exhibitions include "Mina Shum: A Selection" at the Tanya Bonakdar Gallery in New York, "Mina Shum: A Retrospective" at the Kunsthaus Bremen, and "Sensation" at the Benaki Museum in Athens.   Her writing has been published in "The New York Times", "Artforum", " Frieze", "Art in America", and other magazines. She is the author of the book "Private Views: The Collecting of Contemporary Art" (2009).
