"The Buys" is the third episode of the fourth season of the American television drama series "Breaking Bad". It is written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Michelle MacLaren.  The episode originally aired on AMC in the United States on August 11, 2013.  Breaking Bad returns with its fourth season with "The Buys." Walt (Bryan Cranston) and Jesse (Aaron Paul) team up with a new partner named Mike (Jonathan Banks) to sell methamphetamine. The only problem is that they are broke and need money fast. They decide to rob a drug cartel boss named Tuco (Raymond Cruz) who is known to be ruthless and has a large cash stash. However, things do not go as planned and they end up getting caught in the middle of a deadly battle between the cartel and a rival gang.   "The Buys" was written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Michelle MacLaren. It features guest appearances by Jonathan Banks, Raymond Cruz, Rhea Seehorn, Patrick Fabian, and Michael Mando.
