Four ships of the Royal Navy have borne the name HMS Bellerophon. The first was a 64-gun third-rate ship of the line launched at Woolwich Dockyard on 9 March 1759 and destroyed by fire in 1793.   The second was a 36-gun fifth-rate ship of the line launched at Portsmouth on 21 January 1798 and wrecked on the Goodwin Sands on 12 October 1802.   The third was an 80-gun fourth rate ship of the line launched at Portsmouth on 15 October 1814 and sold for breaking up in 1836.   The fourth was a 60-gun fourth rate ship of the line launched at Portsmouth on 5 November 1856 and sold for breaking up in 1865.
