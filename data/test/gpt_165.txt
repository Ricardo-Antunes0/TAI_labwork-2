Bellefield Boiler Plant, also known as "The Boiler Lady", is a historic boiler plant located at Bellefield, Lawrence County, Missouri. The plant was built in 1916, and is a 2 1/2-story brick building with a hipped roof. It has a single front facade, and features a central entrance with double doors flanked by pilasters and topped by a dentillated cornice. It originally housed three boilers and several pumps.  Bellefield Boiler Plant was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982.
