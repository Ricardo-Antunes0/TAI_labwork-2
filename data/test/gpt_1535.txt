World of Warships is a free-to-play naval combat simulator video game developed and published by Wargaming.com. It was released on July 12, 2013 for Microsoft Windows and OS X. The game was announced at the Gamescom 2013 media conference and shown for the first time at the July 7, 2013 closed beta.  World of Warships is a naval combat simulator video game that allows players to control ships from the World War I, World War II, and Korean War eras in battles against other players or AI-controlled ships. Players can command different types of ships, engage in team play with friends, and engage in realistic naval warfare.
