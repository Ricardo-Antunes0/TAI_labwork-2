, abbreviated as Hizakurige and known in English as The Tokaidōchū Hizakurige, is a long-running manga series written and illustrated by Itō Katsuhiko. It tells the story of Miyamoto Musashi, a legendary swordsman from the Sengoku period, and his travels across the country.  The manga was first published in Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine in 1972, and has been collected in over 30 volumes. An anime adaptation produced by AIC and directed by Konaka Yūji aired from October 4 to December 20, 2009, and was simulcast by Crunchyroll. A second season, subtitled "Kimi no Na Wa" (meaning "Your Name") aired from January 8 to March 22, 2010.
