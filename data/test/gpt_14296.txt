BlindArt is a British charity which was founded in 1989. It provides a range of services to people who are blind or partially sighted, including access to art galleries and museums.  BlindArt was founded in 1989 by a group of volunteers who felt that there was a need for accessible art activities for people who are blind or partially sighted. The charity currently provides a range of services to its beneficiaries, including access to art galleries and museums. BlindArt also runs workshops and educational programmes which aim to help individuals develop their creativity and understanding of art.
