Johan Landsberg (born 30 December 1974) is a Swedish film and television actor. He is best known for his roles as Martin Beck in the Swedish series Beck and as Henrik Vanger in the American series Villingarna. Landsberg has also starred in the films The Cider House Rules, The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, and Let the Right One In.  Landsberg was born in Stockholm, Sweden, to a Swedish father and Norwegian mother. He grew up in Trondheim, Norway, before moving to Sweden when he was ten years old. He studied at the National Academy of Dramatic Art in Stockholm, graduating in 2000.
