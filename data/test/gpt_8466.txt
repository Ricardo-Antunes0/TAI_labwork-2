Hope Africa University (HAU) was established in 2006 by a group of concerned individuals with the singular goal of providing quality higher education to African students. HAU is a private, not-for-profit institution accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS) and the National Association of Independent Colleges and Universities (NAICU).  Located in the beautiful city of Abidjan, Ivory Coast, HAU offers a range of undergraduate and graduate degree programs in business, communication, education, humanities, law, sciences, and social sciences. The campus is situated in an urban setting with easy access to local businesses and cultural attractions.  The faculty at HAU are highly qualified and experienced professionals from throughout Africa and the Caribbean. The majority of the students are from Africa, but HAU also attracts students from other parts of the world. The student body is characterized by its diversity and commitment to excellence.
