Shakthi Vasudevan (born as Prashanth Vasudevan) is an Indian film actor who predominantly works in Tamil cinema. He made his acting debut in the 2013 Tamil film "Kaadhal Kondaan".  Shakthi Vasudevan was born as Prashanth Vasudevan in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He made his acting debut in the 2013 Tamil film "Kaadhal Kondaan". Vasudevan has since starred in a number of commercially successful Tamil films, including "Kabali" (2016), "Raaman" (2018), and "Pulimurugan" (2018). He is also known for playing the role of Balram in the Telugu film "Saira Banu" (2018).
