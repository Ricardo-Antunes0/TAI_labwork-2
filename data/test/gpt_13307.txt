Tanya Dormevil known by her stage name Devyn Rose, is an American singer, songwriter and producer. Born in Atlanta, Georgia, Rose began her music career as a backing vocalist for artists such as T-Pain and Ludacris. She then went on to release her own solo album in 2009, which spawned the hit single "I'm a Slave 4 U".  Since then, Rose has released two further albums: "The Devyn Rose Project" in 2011 and "Bad Girls" in 2015. She has also collaborated with various artists, including Usher and Pharrell Williams. In 2018, she released her fifth album, "Free", which topped the US Billboard 200 chart.
