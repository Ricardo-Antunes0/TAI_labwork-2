Yeshiva Ohel Torah-Baranovich, commonly referred to as Baranovich Yeshiva, is a yeshiva located in the town of Baranovich, Belarus. The yeshiva was founded in 1883, and is one of the largest and most prestigious yeshivas in Belarus.  The Baranovich Yeshiva was founded in 1883 by Rabbi Yitzchak Meir Alter, who served as its first rosh yeshiva. The yeshiva became well-known for its Talmud scholarship and has produced many notable rabbis and scholars, including Rabbi Aryeh Leib Steinberg, Rabbi Yaakov Yitzchak Ruderman, and Rabbi Yosef Shalom Eliashiv. The Baranovich Yeshiva is also known for its famous kollel, which offers post-graduate studies in Torah learning.
