The Intouchables , also known as Untouchable or the Disabled One, is a 2009 French comedy-drama film directed by Olivier Nakache and Eric Toledano. The film is about a quadriplegic man who is hired by a wealthy Parisian family to be their caretaker.  The Intouchables was released in France on 6 December 2009 and in the United States on 12 December 2009. It has grossed over $230 million worldwide. The Intouchables won the Academy Award for Best Picture and Best Actor (Jean-Pierre Bacri) at the 81st Academy Awards. It also won four BAFTAs, including Best Film and Best Director (Olivier Nakache and Eric Toledano).
