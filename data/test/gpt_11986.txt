Vang Church  is a parish church in the municipality of Vang, in Oppland county, Norway. It is located in the old Vang village, about south of the village of Haukelifjell. The church is part of the Vang parish in the Indre Østfold deanery in the Diocese of Bjørgvin.   The first church on this site was probably built around 1300. The present church was built in a long church style in 1881. It was designed by A.D. Nilsen, and it is a wooden cruciform church with a square bell tower. The altarpiece is from 1915 and it is painted by Edvard Munch.   The church has been refurbished several times, most recently in 2005-2006. The current pastor is Håkon Martinsen.
