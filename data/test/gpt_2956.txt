Mostafa Karkhaneh (born May 24, 1959, in Tehran) is an Iranian composer, musician and singer. He has released five albums.  Karkhaneh was born in Tehran on May 24, 1959. After completing his studies at the Faculty of Music in Tehran, he started his music career in the early 1980s. He has since released five albums, including "Invisible Hands" (1992), "A Different Way" (1996), "The Traveller" (2002), "The Journey" (2005), and "Passing Moments" (2009). His work has been praised by critics and audiences alike for its unique blend of traditional Iranian music with contemporary elements. Karkhaneh has also collaborated with numerous artists, including Shahram Nazeri, Mohammad Reza Shajarian, Hossein Alizadeh, and Mahsa Vahdat. In 2009, he was awarded the title of Commander of the Order of Arts and Letters by the French government.
