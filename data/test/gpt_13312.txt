Phillip Reyes was a member of the band Deftones. He was born in Sacramento, California, on January 3, 1981. Phillip Reyes was a versatile and talented musician. He played the guitar, bass, keyboards and drums. He also sang and wrote lyrics. Phillip Reyes played in Deftones from 1997 to 2006. In 2006 he left the band to pursue a solo career. His first solo album, "Phobos", was released in 2007.
