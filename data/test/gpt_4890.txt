Mr. Church is a 2016 American drama film written and directed by Sean Baker. The film stars Michael Fassbender, Carey Mulligan, and Domhnall Gleeson.  The film tells the story of an enigmatic gay man who runs a Miami church and attracts the interest of the city's mayor (Mulligan).   Baker first announced the project in 2013, and shooting began in Miami in March 2016. The film had its world premiere at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival. It was released in the United States on June 24, 2017, by A24.   "Mr. Church" received mixed reviews from critics, but was praised for its acting and cinematography. Fassbender was named Best Actor at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival.
