The Glamorgan Yeomanry was a Yeomanry regiment in the British Army.  Formed in 1794 as the Glamorgan Militia, the regiment served in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars before being disbanded in 1815. It was reformed in 1832 as the Glamorgan Yeomanry and served in the Crimean War and Indian Mutiny before being amalgamated with the Welsh Regiment of Yeomanry to form the Welsh Yeomanry in 1908. The regiment was disbanded in 1922.
