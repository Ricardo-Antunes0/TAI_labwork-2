The Wedgwood Institute is a large red-brick building in Burslem, Stoke-on-Trent, England. It is the home of the pottery school of the University of Stoke-on-Trent, and was opened in 1969 by Princess Alexandra.  The Wedgwood Institute has a wide range of facilities for teaching potters, including classrooms, kilns, a potters' laboratory, a painting and decorating studio and a printmaking studio. There is also a retail shop and a cafe.
