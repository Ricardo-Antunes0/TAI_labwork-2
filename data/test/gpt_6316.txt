Mark Coeckelbergh (born 1975) is a Belgian artist who lives and works in Amsterdam. He is known for his sculptural installations and paintings.  Mark Coeckelbergh was born in 1975 in Kortrijk, Belgium. He studied at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Antwerp, graduating in 2000. He currently lives and works in Amsterdam, where he has exhibited his work at venues such as the Stedelijk Museum, the Museum of Modern Art, and the EYE Film Institute Amsterdam. Coeckelbergh's sculptural installations often feature everyday materials such as cans, bottles, and pieces of metal twisted into unconventional shapes. His paintings typically depict scenes from everyday life or introspective portraits of himself.
