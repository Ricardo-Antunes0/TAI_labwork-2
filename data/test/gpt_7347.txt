Jarid Lukosevicius is a Canadian ice hockey player who is currently playing for HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL). Lukosevicius was drafted by the Montreal Canadiens in the third round, 78th overall, of the 2010 NHL Entry Draft.  Lukosevicius made his professional debut with the Windsor Spitfires of the Ontario Hockey League (OHL) in the 2009â€“10 season. He scored his first career OHL goal on October 10, 2009, against the Owen Sound Attack. Lukosevicius played with Windsor until he was traded to the London Knights in February 2013. In 54 games with London, he scored 14 goals and 27 assists for 41 points.  Lukosevicius signed a three-year contract with HC Sochi of the Kontinental Hockey League on July 2, 2013. He played in his first game for HC Sochi on September 26, 2013 against HC Dynamo Minsk. In 46 games with HC Sochi, he scored 10 goals and 17 assists for 27 points.
