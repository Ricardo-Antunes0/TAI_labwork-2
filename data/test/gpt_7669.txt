Weathermaker Music is the record label owned by the American singer and songwriter Norah Jones. It was founded in 2002 and has released six albums, including her most recent, Come Away With Me, in 2014.  Norah Jones is an American singer and songwriter. Born on September 25th, 1978 in New York City, she began her music career as a backing vocalist for artists such as Rufus Wainwright and the Fugees. She released her first album, Come Away with Me, in 2002, to critical acclaim and went on to win several awards including three Grammy Awards. Her subsequent albums have also received widespread acclaim, with 2006's Feels Like Home earning her a nomination for the Brit Award for Best International Female Artist.
