Linda Carroll Hamilton (born September 26, 1956) is an American actress who has starred in popular television series such as The A-Team, ER, and Star Trek: The Next Generation. Hamilton was nominated for two Emmy Awards for her role as Dr. Elizabeth Corday on the prime time soap opera General Hospital and won a Golden Globe Award for her performance in the television movie A Beautiful Mind. She is also noted for her voice acting work, including her roles as Aloy in the video game Horizon Zero Dawn and as General Elinor Hale in the animated series Rick and Morty.
