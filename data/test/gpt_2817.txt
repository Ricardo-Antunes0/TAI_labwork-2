Gemory (also Gremory, Gamori, Gaeneron, Gemon, Gemyem) is a fictional kingdom in the "Dungeons & Dragons" role-playing game. It is based on the medieval kingdom of Gaeta, located in Southern Italy.  The kingdom is ruled by a monarchy and its capital is the city of Gemory. Gemory is home to a large number of Wizards, who are able to use powerful magic to protect their kingdom and its citizens. The kingdom also has a strong military force, which is used to fight against enemies from outside the kingdom.
