The condom challenges are two viral Internet challenges in which participants are asked to complete various challenges with condoms, such as eating a condom in one minute, putting a condom on a banana in one minute, or wrapping a condom around a pen.  The first condom challenge was created in 2014 by YouTuber Cassy Athena and was called the "Condom Jenga Challenge". The challenge involved stacking as many condoms as possible on a tower of Jenga blocks. As of February 2018, the challenge has been completed over 1.5 million times.   The second condom challenge was created by YouTube personality Ryan Higa and was called the "Condom Twister Challenge". The challenge involves wrapping a condom around a pen. As of February 2018, the challenge has been completed over 2.5 million times.
