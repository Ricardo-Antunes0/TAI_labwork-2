Nerello is a name given to two different wines made from the Trebbiano grape:  The Nerello di Montepulciano, also known as Nerello Mascalese, is a red wine made from the Montepulciano grape and produced in the Mascalese region of Tuscany. It has a fruity, floral flavor with notes of cherry and raspberry.  The Nerello Cappuccino is a white wine made from the Trebbiano grape and produced in the Cappuccino region of Abruzzo. It has a fruity, floral flavor with notes of strawberry and peach.
