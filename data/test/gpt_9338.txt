Kawakita played for Japan from 1996 to 2003 and was a member of the national team that reached the 2002 World Cup. Kawakita was also a participant at the 2001 Confederations Cup.  In 2006, Kawakita moved to Saudi Arabia to play for Al-Ittihad but left after two months. He then played for Eintracht Frankfurt in Germany, before returning to Japan in 2009. He retired in 2014 after playing for Sanfrecce Hiroshima.
