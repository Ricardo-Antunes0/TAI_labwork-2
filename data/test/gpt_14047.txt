The Caleb Baldwin Tavern is a historic tavern located at 210-212 Maiden Lane in Newark, New Jersey. It was built in 1768 and was added to the National Register of Historic Places on October 20, 1976.  The Caleb Baldwin Tavern is a two-story, seven bay, Georgian style building on a raised basement. The front facade is five bays wide, with a center entrance flanked by pilasters and a transom window. The roof is pierced by two chimneys. The interior features wood paneling and Federal period flooring.
