7800Â° Fahrenheit is the second studio album by American rock band Kansas. It was released in 1978 on their own label, Turnabout Records. The album produced five singles, all of which charted in the top 40 on the "Billboard" Hot 100: "Carry On Wayward Son", "Dust in the Wind", "In the beginning", "Rumble (At The Moon)". and "The Light that Guides You Home". "Carry On Wayward Son" has been certified Diamond by the RIAA and is the best-selling single of all time.  The album reached number one on the "Billboard" album chart and has been certified Platinum by the RIAA. It has been reissued several times, most recently in 2007 as a two-disc set with their debut album, Kansas.
