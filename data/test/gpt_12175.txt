Flesh Gordon is a 1974 American sexploitation film directed by John Waters and starring Divine, Joe Spinell, and Margaret Cho. It is based on the cult comic-book series of the same name created by Frank Frazetta and Harvey Kurtzman.  The film tells the story of Gordon (Divine), a depraved and narcissistic young man who is transported to the planet Gorilla-5 after committing a heinous act. There, he becomes the slave of the King (Joe Spinell) and Queen (Margaret Cho) of the planet, who use him for their sexual gratification. Gordon eventually escapes and returns to Earth, where he is pursued by the police.
