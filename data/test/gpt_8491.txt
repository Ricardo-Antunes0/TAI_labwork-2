The Stark Reality was an American jazz-rock band, active from 1971 to 1976. The band was formed in Los Angeles by saxophonist and composer Charles Lloyd, keyboardist and composer Keith Jarrett, bassist and composer Ron Carter, and drummer Tony Williams.  The band's sound was based on improvisation, free jazz, classical music, and funk. They recorded six albums before breaking up in 1976. Their most famous songs include "Naima", "Inner Urge", "Stark Reality", and "Way Out West".
