Shutter Island is a 2010 American neo-noir thriller film directed by Martin Scorsese and written by Laeta Kalogridis and Michael Lebold. It is based on the novel of the same name by Dennis Lehane. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Mark Ruffalo, Ben Kingsley, and Michelle Williams.    "Shutter Island" tells the story of U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels (Leonardo DiCaprio) who is sent to Shutter Island to investigate the disappearance of patients from a mental institution. The island's erratic and enigmatic doctor, Dr. John Shutter (Mark Ruffalo), refuses to let Daniels leave until he has solved the mystery.
