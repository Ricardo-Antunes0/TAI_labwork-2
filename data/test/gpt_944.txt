Doug Drexler (born in New York City) is an American retired professional basketball player. He played in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Houston Rockets, Portland Trail Blazers, and Los Angeles Lakers. Drexler was the 1992 Most Valuable Player (MVP) of the NBA Finals.  Drexler was a versatile player who could play both the shooting guard and small forward positions. He was known for his prolific scoring ability from outside the arc and was also a very good passer and defender. He was a six-time All-Star and won two championships with the Rockets in 1995 and 1997. Drexler was also named to the NBA All-Defensive First Team three times.  Drexler retired from professional basketball in 2001 after playing 18 seasons in the NBA. He has since started a career as a television commentator for NBA games.
