Funny as Hell is a Canadian television series created by Judd Apatow and produced by his company, Funny or Die. The series is a mockumentary-style comedy show that parodies reality TV shows.  The show is set in the fictional town of Pawnee, Indiana and follows the everyday lives of its residents. The show has been praised for its use of strong female characters and its subversive humor.  The series aired its first season on Comedy Central in the United States from April 27, 2010 to May 22, 2010. The second season premiered on August 1, 2011 and concluded on December 12, 2011. A third season was announced on February 26, 2012 and began airing on October 1, 2012. A fourth season was announced on February 5, 2013 and began airing on September 26, 2013. A fifth season was announced on January 10, 2014 and began airing on August 22, 2014. Season six premiered on April 16, 2015 and concluded on November 12, 2015. A seventh season was announced on March 2, 2016 and began airing on September 28, 2016.
