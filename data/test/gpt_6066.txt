The Lockville Historic District is a historic district in Lockville, New York. The district was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979.  The district includes a mix of residential, commercial, and institutional buildings dating from the mid-19th century to the early 21st century. It includes a variety of architectural styles, including vernacular Victorian, Gothic Revival, and Romanesque Revival. The district is significant for its architecture and its role in Lockville's history.
