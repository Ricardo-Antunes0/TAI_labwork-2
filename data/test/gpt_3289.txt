Shilpa Shetty (born Ashwini Shetty; 8 June 1975) is an Indian film actress and former model. After making her screen debut in the 1996 Hindi film Lagaan, Shetty became one of the leading actresses in Bollywood. She has appeared in over 150 films in Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam language films.  Shetty is noted for her unconventional acting style, and has been compared to actresses such as Marilyn Monroe and Elizabeth Taylor. She has won three National Film Awards, two Filmfare Awards South, two Filmfare Awards East and two International Indian Film Academy Awards. In 2007, Shetty was listed among the world's 100 most powerful women by Forbes magazine.  Shilpa Shetty was born on 8 June 1975 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India to Ashwini Kumar and Rajeshwari Devi. She has an elder sister named Rajeshwari (born 1970). Growing up, she wanted to be a model like Twiggy and worked as a child model for Pepsi and Coca-Cola. Her first acting role came when she was cast in the 1996 Hindi film Lagaan alongside Aamir Khan. The film was a major success and gave Shetty recognition in the industry.
