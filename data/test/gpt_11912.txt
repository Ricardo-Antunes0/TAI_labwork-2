Photisarath (also spelled Phothisarath, Phothisarat, or Potisarat, Thai:                Phothisa Sarath, born November 9, 1978) is a Thai singer and actress.  After completing her studies in graphic design at Bangkok University of Technology, Photisarath started her music career as a backup singer for various Thai singers. She made her acting debut in the 2001 film "The Return of King Naresuan". In 2006, she starred in the successful comedy film "Barney". She has since starred in several successful films, including the 2008 comedy "The King and I" and the romantic thriller "The Unbreakable Law" (2012).  Photisarath was awarded Best Actress at the Thailand National Film Awards for her performance in "The Unbreakable Law".
