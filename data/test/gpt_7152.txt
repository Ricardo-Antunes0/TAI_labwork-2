Mended Hearts is a United States-based charity that provides humanitarian aid to communities in need. The organization was founded in 2006 by Caren Blake and her husband, Scott Blake.  Mended Hearts works to restore dignity and hope to marginalized communities by providing emergency relief and long-term developmental assistance. The organization's programs focus on HIV/AIDS, malaria, refugee and internally displaced persons, women and girls, children in conflict or crisis, and community-based organizations.  Mended Hearts is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
