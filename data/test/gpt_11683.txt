LM5: The Tour was the sixth concert tour by Canadian singer Celine Dion. The tour began on May 15, 2019 in Montreal, Quebec and ended on November 3, 2019 in Perth, Australia.  The tour was announced on February 7, 2019 and was produced by Live Nation. The tour is in support of Dion's eleventh studio album "Loved Me Back to Life" (2018).  The tour's setlist is predominantly based around songs from Dion's eleventh studio album "Loved Me Back to Life" (2018), with a few tracks from her previous albums also included. The tour will also feature select songs from Dion's back catalogue.   Dion initially announced that she would be embarking on a world tour in support of her eleventh studio album "Loved Me Back to Life" (2018). The tour was officially announced on February 7, 2019 and is set to commence on May 15, 2019 in Montreal, Quebec and end on November 3, 2019 in Perth, Australia.   Along with the announcement of the tour came the news that production company Live Nation has been appointed as the exclusive global touring partner for LM5: The Tour. The tour marks the fifth time that Live Nation has partnered with Dion for a global tour; their previous tours include the Taking Chances World Tour (2011-2012), The Power of Love Tour (2012-2013), My Love: The Tour (2015-2016) and The Classic Celine Dion Tour (2017).
