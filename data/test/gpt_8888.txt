Raguf Orujov   (12 July 1972, Skopje) is a Macedonian musician and composer.  Raguf Orujov was born in Skopje, Republic of Macedonia. He studied music at the Academy of Music in Skopje, where he graduated in 1995. Raguf has since been a member of the Macedonian Radio Orchestra, and has worked as a composer and musician for theatre, film and television. In 2002, he released his debut album, entitled "Naletili smo se u magli". Raguf's music is often influenced by classical and jazz styles, and he has collaborated with various performers, including Nikola Vrančić and Goran Kovačević.
