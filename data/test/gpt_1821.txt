M. Bala Subramanion (Tamil: மு. பாலா சுப்பிரமணியன்; born 3 November 1963) is an Indian film director and screenwriter. He made his directorial debut with the Tamil language film Naan Sigappu Manithan (2011), for which he was nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Director.  Subramanion was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He completed his engineering degree from Madras University and then worked as an engineer for a few years before turning to filmmaking. He began writing scripts in 2002 and directed his first film, Naan Sigappu Manithan, in 2011. The film was well-received by critics and won several awards, including the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Tamil. Subramanion has since directed two more films, Ezhil (2013) and Thuppakki (2015), both of which received critical acclaim.
