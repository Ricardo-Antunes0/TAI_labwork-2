NSS College, Nemmara is a college of Nursing Education located in Nemmara, a town located in the southwestern part of the state of Tamil Nadu, India.  NSS College, Nemmara was established in the year 2006. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in nursing. The college is affiliated to the University of Madras.   The college is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).   The college has a well-equipped library that houses a collection of books and journals in nursing. The college also has an audio-visual room that can accommodate up to 100 people. The college has a computer lab that is equipped with latest software and hardware.   The college has a well-equipped canteen that serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. The college has a playground that is suitable for children.
