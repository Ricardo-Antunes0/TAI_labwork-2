Isabel Suckling (born 24 March 1998) is an English actress. She is known for playing Jessica in the 2016 ITV series Jessica Jones, for which she was nominated for a BAFTA Television Award for Best Actress.  Suckling was born in London, England, to a mother who is a barrister and father who is a television commercial producer. She has one older brother. She attended The Latymer School in London and then studied acting at the Drama Centre London. Suckling began her acting career in theatre productions, including roles in A Midsummer Night's Dream at the Old Vic Theatre and The Crucible at the Royal National Theatre.  In 2016, Suckling was cast in the role of Jessica Jones in the series Jessica Jones, which premiered on Netflix. For her performance in the series, she was nominated for a BAFTA Television Award for Best Actress.
