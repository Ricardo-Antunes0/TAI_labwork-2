Trials Rising is a 2.5D fault-based racing game for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One platforms, developed by RedLynx and published by Ubisoft. It is the sequel to the 2011 game Trials HD.  The game is set in an open world environment, where the player can explore and race on various islands, circuits and challenges. The game features cooperative multiplayer for up to four players, as well as a singleplayer campaign where the player can choose from a variety of characters to race.   The game was announced at E3 2018, and is set for release in 2019.
