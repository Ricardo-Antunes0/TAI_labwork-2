Nikolay "Niki" Kanchev  (born 26 December 1988) is a Bulgarian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Cherno More Varna.  Kanchev started playing football with local team Cherno More Varna. He made his professional debut in the 2006–07 season and played in eight league matches.  In the 2007–08 season, Kanchev played in all 30 of Cherno More Varna's league matches and scored his first professional goal in a 3–1 home victory over Lokomotiv Plovdiv. He also helped his side win the Bulgarian Cup, scoring in the final against Litex Lovech.  In the 2008–09 season, Kanchev played in all 30 of Cherno More Varna's league matches as they finished third in the league and reached the Bulgarian Cup final again. He also played in both legs of the UEFA Champions League play-off round against Rangers, which Cherno More Varna lost 1–0 on aggregate.  In the 2009–10 season, Kanchev made 31 appearances in all competitions as Cherno More Varna finished fourth in Bulgaria and reached the Bulgarian Cup final for a third time. He also played in both legs of the play-off round against FC Barcelona, which ended in a 2–2 draw and meant that Barça would play Malmö FF instead of Cherno More Varna in the 2010 UEFA Champions League group stage.
