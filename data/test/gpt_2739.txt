General  (abbreviated as GEN) is a monthly Pakistani magazine published by Geo TV. The magazine is aimed at the general public and covers a broad range of topics including politics, international relations, economy, defense, culture, sports and lifestyle.  General was first published in March 1976 as a general interest magazine. It was originally owned by the Pakistan Broadcasting Corporation (PBC) and was later acquired by the then-newly founded Geo TV in 1996. The magazine is currently published monthly and has a circulation of approximately 150,000.
