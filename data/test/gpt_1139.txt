Desde El Principio is the twelfth studio album by Mexican singer-songwriter Juan Gabriel. It was released on November 25, 2018.  The album was produced by Gabriel, along with Gustavo Santaolalla, Ariel Rechtshaid, and Daniel López. It features collaborations with artists like Demi Lovato, Carlos Vives, and Maluma.  "Desde El Principio" is Juan Gabriel's first album of new material in six years. He announced the album in September 2018 and released the first single, "Por Qué Te Vas", which peaked at number two on the Mexican charts.
