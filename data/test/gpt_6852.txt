Crying Girl is the name of two different songs by American singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey. The first, titled "Crying Girl", was released as the lead single from her debut studio album, Born to Die (2013). The second, titled "Honeymoon", was released as the second single from her second studio album, Lust for Life (2017).  The songs are written about Del Rey's experiences with love and heartbreak. "Crying Girl" lyrically speaks of a girl who is in love but is not accepted by her loved one. "Honeymoon" is about a woman who has finally found love and is overjoyed, but she soon realises that the man she is with is not good for her.
