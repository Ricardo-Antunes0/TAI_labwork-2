"Su-zakana" is the eighth episode of the first season of the American television series Hannibal. It was written by Bryan Fuller and directed by Michael Rymer.  The title translates to "Suicide Hunter" in English. It is a story about Will Graham's (Hugh Dancy) attempt to find a new serial killer who is copying the methods of the cannibalistic Hannibal Lecter (Mads Mikkelsen).  The episode features a guest appearance by Caroline Dhavernas as Dr. Alana Bloom, a psychiatric consultant who helps Will in his investigation.
