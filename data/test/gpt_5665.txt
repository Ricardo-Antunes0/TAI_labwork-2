Sweet Rendezvous is the first EP by American rock band The Head and the Heart. It was released on March 3, 2015 through the band's own label, Hi-Fi Records.  The Head and the Heart are an American rock band from Louisville, Kentucky. Formed in 2009, the group consists of Casey Crescenzo (vocals, guitar), Ryan McMahon (guitar, backing vocals), Brent Knopf (bass guitar), and Joe Kwon (drums). Crescenzo is the only remaining original member of the group.  Sweet Rendezvous is their first EP release. The EP was recorded at locations in Louisville, Kentucky and Nashville, Tennessee during 2014 and 2015. The album's first single, "Boys Don't Cry", was released on February 26, 2015.
