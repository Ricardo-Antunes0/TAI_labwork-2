The canton of Thoissey is a former French administrative division. It was disbanded in 1790. Its territory corresponded to the present-day department of Saône-et-Loire.  The canton of Thoissey was created in 1790 from the former canton of Clermont-Ferrand. Its territory corresponded to the present-day department of Saône-et-Loire. The canton was disbanded in 1790, when it was merged into the newly created department of Saône-et-Loire.
