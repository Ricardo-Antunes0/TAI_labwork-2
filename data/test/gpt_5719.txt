Candy Buttons, Candy Dots, or Pox are small, hard candies that are most commonly found in the United States. They come in a variety of colors, including red, green, yellow, and blue.  Candy Buttons are made from sugar, cornstarch, and butter. They are coated with a sweet syrup and then polished off with a hard candy shell. Candy Buttons have been around since the late 1800s and are most popular in the United States.
