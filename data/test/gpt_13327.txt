"The 23rd Psalm" is the tenth episode of the fourth season of the American television series "Breaking Bad". It is also the forty-third episode of the series overall.  In this episode, Walt Whitman's "Song of Myself" is quoted in a scene where Skyler White reads it to her son, Hank.
