Warhammer 40,000: Conquest was a Living Card Game developed by Fantasy Flight Games and released in October 2018. The game is a 3-player deckbuilding game set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe.  Warhammer 40,000: Conquest is a three-player card game set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. In the game, players compete to build powerful decks of cards and battle against each other using those decks to defeat their opponents.  Players control powerful factions from the Warhammer 40,000 universe, such as the Space Marines, Orks, and Eldar. They will use these factions to attack enemy planets, capture resources, and build up their armies in order to claim victory.  Warhammer 40,000: Conquest is a Living Card Game that uses specially designed cards. Each card has both a physical and digital component. The digital component contains all the game data, including the faction's stats, abilities, and cards that can be played with it. The physical component is the card itself, which players will use to play the game.
