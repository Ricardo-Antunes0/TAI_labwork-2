Norm Benning Racing is an American professional stock car racing team that currently competes in the NASCAR Xfinity Series. The team is owned by Norm Benning and his son, Austin Benning.  Norm Benning Racing was founded by Norm Benning in 1981. The team made its debut in the Busch Series that year and has since competed in a number of different NASCAR series. In 2000, the team made its debut in the Cup Series and has competed there ever since. In 2007, Norm Benning Racing became the first African American-owned and operated NASCAR team. In 2015, Norm Benning Racing was sold to Austin Benning. The team currently competes in the NASCAR Xfinity Series.
