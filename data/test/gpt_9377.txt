Matt Finish are an Australian rock band from Sydney, formed in 2009. The band consists of vocalist/guitarist Matt Finish, drummer John Lawless, bassist/backing vocalist Luke Bulla, and guitarist/backing vocalist Tim Farrant.  The band's sound has been described as "melodic hard rock" with influences from classic rock, heavy metal and punk. They have released two EPs and one full-length album, "The Winter of Mixed Drinks" (2014).
