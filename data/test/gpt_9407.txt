Yuvan Yuvathi is a 2011 Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by A. R. Rahman. The film stars Nagarjuna, Kajal Aggarwal, Madhavan and Aadukalam Naren in the lead roles with music composed by Rahman. The film's production company is Cloud9 Motion Pictures while the distribution rights are held by Prakash Jha's Red Chillies Entertainment. Principal photography began on 6 October 2010 in Coimbatore and continued in Chennai and Bangalore during early 2011.  The film was originally titled "Kadal" (translated to "Crush"). However, the original title was changed after actor Madhavan objected to it since he had a similar title in an earlier project of his, "Kadal Seiveeran". The film was released on 12 January 2011 to positive reviews from critics. It became a blockbuster at the box office and won several accolades including the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Tamil, which was Nagarjuna's fourth consecutive win in this category.
