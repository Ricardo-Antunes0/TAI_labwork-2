David Holton (born 1946) is a British artist who has worked in painting, sculpture, installation and film.  Holton was born in 1946 in Manchester, England. He attended the University of Manchester, where he studied Fine Art. Holton's first solo exhibition was at the Whitechapel Gallery in London in 1976. He has exhibited at the Tate Gallery, the Museum of Modern Art in New York City, the Centre Pompidou in Paris, and the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum in New York. Holton's work is included in the collections of the Tate Gallery, the Whitney Museum of American Art, and the National Gallery of Victoria in Melbourne, Australia. He lives and works in London.
