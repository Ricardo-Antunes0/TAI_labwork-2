Sericomyia carolinensis (also called the Two-spotted Pond Skater) is a species of skater in the family of dragonflies. It is found in North America.  The Two-spotted Pond Skater is a small dragonfly that can be found across much of North America. They inhabit many different types of water bodies, from slow-moving streams and marshes to larger bodies of water like lakes and ponds. They are often seen skimming the surface of these bodies of water, looking for prey.
