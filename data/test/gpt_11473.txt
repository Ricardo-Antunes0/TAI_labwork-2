"Sledgehammer" is a song recorded by Barbadian singer Rihanna for her fifth studio album, "Loud" (2015). The song was written by Rihanna, Pharrell, and Benny Blanco, with production handled by the latter two. The song was released as the album's lead single on February 24, 2015.  "Sledgehammer" is a uptempo and dance-pop song with a heavy electronic production. Lyrically, the song talks about the power of love and its ability to make things happen. Critical response to "Sledgehammer" was positive, with some reviewers deeming it as one of the standout tracks from "Loud". The song peaked at number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 and became Rihanna's ninth number-one single in the United States. It also debuted at number one in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom.  The music video for "Sledgehammer" was directed by Dave Meyers and filmed in late 2014 in Los Angeles. It features Rihanna as a cyborg warrior battling her enemies while sporting futuristic clothing and makeup. The video has received positive reviews from critics who praised its visual effects and choreography.
