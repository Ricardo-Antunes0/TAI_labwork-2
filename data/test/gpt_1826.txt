He began studying the piano at the age of six with his mother and at the age of eleven he entered the conservatory in Frankfurt am Main. After two years of studies at the conservatory he entered the University of Frankfurt to study musicology and composition with Rudolf Serkin. In 1972 he won first prize in the International Tchaikovsky Competition in Moscow and subsequently toured extensively throughout Europe and Russia.  Wilfried Feldenkirchen was killed in a car accident on February 12, 1984, at the age of 36.
