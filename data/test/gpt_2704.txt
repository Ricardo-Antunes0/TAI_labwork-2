"Mutt" is a song by American rock band Radiohead. It was written by the band's lead singer Thom Yorke, and recorded by the band in January 1994 at their Oxfordshire home studio.  "Mutt" was released as the third single from Radiohead's second studio album "The Bends" in March 1994. The song reached number one on the UK Singles Chart and stayed there for four weeks. The song has been described as an "epic" and "mesmerizing" ballad.  "Mutt" has been covered by numerous artists, including The Paper Kites, who released a electropop version of the song in 2003. In 2007, the song was used in an advertising campaign for Jaguar cars.
