Jangdan County (read as Jangdan-gun in the native language) is a county in North Gyeongsang Province, South Korea. Jangdan County is bordered by Chungcheongbuk-do to the north, Chungcheongnam-do to the east, and Gimhae County to the south. The principal city of Jangdan County is Changwon. The total area of the county is 5,811.58 square kilometers. The population of the county was 245,853 as of 2009. Jangdan County is famous for its oranges and pears, and also has a number of hot springs.
