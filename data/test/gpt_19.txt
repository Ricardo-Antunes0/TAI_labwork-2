"The Darkling Thrush" is a poem by Alfred, Lord Tennyson. Written in 1842, it is one of the most popular and widely anthologized poems in the English language.  The Darkling Thrush is a bird of the thrush family, Turdidae. It is found in Europe, Asia, and North America. The Darkling Thrush is a migratory species, wintering in southern Mexico and Central America.
