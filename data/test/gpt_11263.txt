Spilna Sprava  is a political party in Slovenia. The party was founded in 2006 as a split from the Slovenian Democratic Party.  Spilna Sprava is a centrist party which advocates for liberal economic policies and social liberalism. The party also supports decentralization and the promotion of regionalism. The party has been critical of the Slovenian government's implementation of austerity measures and its support for the European Union.
