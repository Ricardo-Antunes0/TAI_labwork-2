Karosa B 832 is a low-floor bus manufactured by the Czech manufacturer Karosa from the Czech city of Brno between 1978 and 1990. A total of 1,636 buses were produced. It is one of the last buses produced in the Czechoslovakia before its breakup in 1993.  Karosa B 832 is a popular bus for tourists in the Czech Republic, where it is commonly seen on routes connecting smaller towns and villages with larger centres such as Prague, Brno and Ostrava. The bus has been in production for over thirty years, and retains much of its original design and functionality.
