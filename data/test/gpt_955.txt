Mohanlal Viswanathan (born 21 May 1960), known professionally as Mohanlal, is an Indian actor who has appeared in over 150 films in a career spanning more than three decades. He has received two National Film Awards, four Filmfare Awards and two Kerala State Film Awards.  Mohanlal was born on 21 May 1960 in a Tamil-speaking family in Palakkad, Kerala. After completing his schooling from the Government Arts College, Thiruvananthapuram, Mohanlal began his acting career by appearing in small roles in films. In 1984, he starred in the Malayalam thriller film "Namma Vaaram". His performance in the film won him critical acclaim and he was subsequently offered more leading roles. He has since appeared in a number of commercially successful films, including the romantic comedy "Pulimurugan" (1993), the action thriller "Devi" (2002), and the psychological thriller "Aparajito" (2010). He has also starred in several critically acclaimed films, including the biographical drama "The Legend of Lakshmi Prasad" (2003), the social drama "Ek Tha Tiger" (2007), and the romantic drama "Kaala" (2016). Mohanlal has been nominated for various awards, including two National Film Awards, four Filmfare Awards and two Kerala State Film Awards.
