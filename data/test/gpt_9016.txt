"Kidz" is a song by British pop duo The Saturdays from their fifth studio album, Living for the Weekend. The song was written by the duo and produced by Xenomania. "Kidz" is a synthpop and dance-pop song with a tempo of 100 beats per minute.  The song was released as the album's lead single on 11 May 2013 and peaked at number two on the UK Singles Chart. It also reached number one in Ireland, number three in Sweden and Switzerland, and number five in Australia.  An accompanying music video for "Kidz" was filmed in Los Angeles and directed by Colin Tilley. It features the group performing in a warehouse filled with children who are dancing to the song.
