The  Inman Diaries is an opera in two acts by composer John Adams. The work was commissioned by the Santa Fe Opera in 1987 and completed in 1990.  The Inman Diaries is a dramatic work about the American Civil War and Reconstruction era, set in the fictional town of Inman, Texas. The opera tells the story of a love triangle between a Confederate soldier, a Union sympathizer, and the woman they both love.
