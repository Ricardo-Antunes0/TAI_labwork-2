Tyurya, sometimes known as murtsovka, is a traditional Ukrainian dish made of rice, beef, and onions cooked in ajvar.  Tyurya is a traditional Ukrainian dish made of rice, beef, and onions cooked in ajvar. It is similar to dumplings in that the ingredients are boiled before being formed into small balls and then fried. Tyurya is often served as an appetizer or main course.
