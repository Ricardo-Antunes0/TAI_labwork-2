Kerry R. McCoy (born August 2, 1974, in Poughkeepsie, New York) is an American professional wrestler, better known by his ring name Kerry McCoy. He is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the Raw brand.  McCoy made his professional wrestling debut in 1995, working for various independent promotions across the United States. He made his WWE debut in 2002, under the ring name K-McCoy, and won the WWE Cruiserweight Championship in 2006. He has also competed in Japan's New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) promotion, where he held the IWGP Junior Heavyweight Championship twice. McCoy was part of the first ever WWE Tag Team Champions, The New Age Outlaws.
