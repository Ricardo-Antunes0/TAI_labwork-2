Pascal Bedrossian (born 28 November 1974) is a French actor, comedian, voice actor and singer.  Born in Paris, he is the son of actress Annie Bedrossian and actor Philippe Bedrossian. He is of Armenian descent.  Bedrossian began his career in stand-up comedy in the early 1990s and has since appeared in several films and television series. He is most notable for his work on the French comedy show Les Guignols de l'info and his starring role as Georges dans the television series La Vie en rose. He also voiced Pascal in the French-language version of Pixar's The Incredibles.  In 2017, Bedrossian released his first album, L'Été prochain.
