Rorden Michael E. C. Wilkinson FAcSS FRSA (born 31 October 1948) is a British sociologist and emeritus professor at the University of Kent. He is known for his work on social stratification and social mobility, and for his contributions to the sociology of knowledge.  Wilkinson was educated at Eton College and Trinity College, Cambridge, where he was a distinguished scholar in the Cavendish Laboratory. He held a D.Phil from Oxford University in 1976 for a thesis on 'Class, Status, and Education'.  Wilkinson has been a professor at the University of Kent since 1987, and has been the university's Vice-Chancellor since 2006. He has also been a visiting professor at universities in Germany, Spain, Italy, Sweden, Denmark and the United States of America. In 2009 he was awarded an honorary doctorate by the University of Lapland.  Wilkinson is a Fellow of both the Royal Society of Arts (FRAcSS) and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences (FAcSS).
