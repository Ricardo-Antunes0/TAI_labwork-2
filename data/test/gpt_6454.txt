Hooray (foaled 17 March 2008) is a British-bred Thoroughbred racehorse and sire. After winning two minor races as a two-year-old, Hooray finished third in the Group 1 2000 Guineas and second in the Epsom Derby. He was retired from racing at the end of the year and spent time as a breeding stallion in Europe.  Hooray is best known for his victory in the 2017 Epsom Derby, becoming only the third horse to win both the Derby and the St Leger Stakes in the same year.
