Mountbellew Agricultural College or Franciscan Brothers College is a coeducational secondary school in Mountbellew, County Clare, Ireland. It is located on the outskirts of the town, adjacent to the Franciscan Friary.  The school was founded in 1842 as Mountbellew National School by the Society of Jesus (Jesuits). It was renamed Mountbellew Agricultural College in 1948. The present day school buildings were opened in 1959. The college has a primary section which was established in 1988.   The college has a roll of about 650 pupils and 50 staff. The current Principal is Mr. Dermot O'Leary.
