Lemon Andersen (born Andrew Andersen; April 21, 1985) is an American actor, comedian and musician.  Andersen was born in St. Louis, Missouri, the son of Tina (née Gallup), a homemaker, and Andrew Andersen, an accountant. He has Norwegian and Danish ancestry. He grew up in Webster Groves, a suburb of St. Louis. After graduating from Webster Groves High School in 2003, Andersen attended the University of Missouri–St. Louis, where he studied theater arts.  Andersen's first major role was as host of the short-lived MTV show "The Andy Show" (2005). He also appeared in supporting roles in films such as "Juno" (2007) and "The Hangover" (2009). In 2010, he starred in the comedy film "Superbad" as Seth Bluth. In 2012, Andersen starred as Ted Mosby on the CBS sitcom "How I Met Your Mother".  Andersen released his debut album, "The Lemon Sessions", in October 2013. The album features appearances by artists such as Snoop Dogg and Fitz & The Tantrums.
