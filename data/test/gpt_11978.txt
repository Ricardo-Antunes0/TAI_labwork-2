"Love" is a song recorded by Romanian singer Inna. It was released on 25 October 2014 as the lead single from her sixth studio album, Smile. The song was written by Inna, Mihai Pop and produced by the latter two under their own record label, PlayBox Music.  "Love" is a pop ballad with elements of soul and R&B. Lyrically, it is about the power of love and its ability to make people happy. The song received positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and Inna's vocal performance. It debuted at number two on the Romanian Top 100 chart and reached the top spot in its second week.
