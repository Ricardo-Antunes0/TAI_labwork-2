Liu Wei-ping 劉渭平 (1915 Beijing – 2014) was a Chinese diplomat and politician.  Liu Wei-ping was born on 1915 in Beijing, China. He graduated from the Peking University School of Law in 1937. Liu served in the Chinese Nationalist Army during the Second Sino-Japanese War, and then worked as a lawyer before joining the Chinese Foreign Service in 1948. He served as the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia (1953–1957), Czechoslovakia (1957–1961), and East Germany (1968–1979). He also served as the Chinese Ambassador to France (1981–1983), and the Chinese Ambassador to the United States of America (1983–1985). Liu retired from the Chinese Foreign Service in 1985.
