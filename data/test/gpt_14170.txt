The 1977 Tangerine Bowl was an American college football bowl game played on December 21, 1977 at the Tangerine Bowl in Orlando, Florida. It was the third edition of the Tangerine Bowl and featured the Miami Hurricanes (10–2) and the Wyoming Cowboys (10–2).  The Cowboys, led by first-year head coach Eddie Robinson, upset the third-ranked Hurricanes 20–14. The Hurricane offense was held to just 267 yards of total offense while Miami's defense allowed just 283 yards of total offense.    This was Miami's first bowl loss since 1964.
