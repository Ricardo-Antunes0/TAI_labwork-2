Trisha Baga (born 1985 Venice, Florida) is an American artist who primarily creates sculptures and drawings.  Trisha Baga was born in 1985, in Venice, Florida. She received her BFA from the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston in 2006. Her work is primarily sculpture and drawings. Baga has exhibited at the Whitney Museum of American Art, the Carnegie Museum of Art, and the Hirshhorn Museum and Sculpture Garden.
