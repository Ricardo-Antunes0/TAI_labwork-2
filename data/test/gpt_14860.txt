Ike Reilly (born Michael Christopher Reilly) is an Irish musician, singer-songwriter and actor.  Reilly was born in Dublin, Ireland, and grew up in the city's north inner-city. He began his musical career as a member of the folk band The Mothball Men, before breaking out as the frontman of the alternative rock band The Paper Kites. Reilly has since released four solo albums, as well as collaborations with a range of artists including Hozier, Damien Rice and The Script. He has also appeared in films such as "The Guard" and "The Young Offenders".  Reilly is currently based in London.
