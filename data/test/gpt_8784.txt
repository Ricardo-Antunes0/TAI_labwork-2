Barry James Harper (30 October 1938 – 2 June 2018) was an English professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Born in Birmingham, Harper started his career with local side Aston Villa. He made his first-team debut in 1957, and went on to make over 350 appearances for Villa, scoring 53 goals. He also played for Birmingham City and Stoke City, before ending his career with non-league Tamworth in 1980. Harper won two League Cups with Aston Villa, and also represented England at international level. He died in 2018 at the age of 82.
