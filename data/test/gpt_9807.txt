"The Pet Goat" (often erroneously called "My Pet Goat") is a webcomic created by Matthew Inman and produced by The Oatmeal. It ran from February 2, 2006 to December 20, 2017.  The comic follows the life of an average guy named Mike who, due to a freak accident, is forced to take care of a pet goat named Bill. Each comic typically consists of four panels with a text box in the middle, as well as occasional stick figures. The humor typically revolves around Mike's struggles with taking care of Bill, as well as his interactions with other characters in his life.
