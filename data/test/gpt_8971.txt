Blaise Castle is a folly built in the shape of a castle by the 5th Earl of Abergavenny in 1815. The estate has been owned by the National Trust since 1951.  The 5th Earl of Abergavenny, George Edmund Blaise, built Blaise Castle in 1815 as a folly in the shape of a castle. The estate has been owned by the National Trust since 1951. Blaise Castle is located near the village of Tretower, north-west of Abergavenny, Wales. The castle is made of red sandstone and is surrounded by a wall.
