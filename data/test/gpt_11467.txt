Antonia Mary Prebble (born 6 June 1984) is an English actress and singer. She is known for her roles in the ITV series Midsomer Murders (2002–2013), the BBC series Broadchurch (2013–2015), and the Netflix series The Crown (2016–present). In 2017, she starred in the musical Rock of Ages at the London Palladium.  Prebble was born in Northampton, Northamptonshire, England, to a Northern Irish mother and an English father. She has two sisters. Prebble attended Northampton School for Girls and then studied acting at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.
