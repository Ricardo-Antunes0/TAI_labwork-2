The D.E.Y. is a Latin hip hop duo from Miami, Florida, consisting of rappers D-Lo and Young Scooter. The duo first gained recognition in 2006 for their single "Laugh Now Cry Later", which reached number one on the US Billboard Hot Rap Tracks chart. Their debut album, The D.E.Y. Project, was released in 2007 and peaked at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.
