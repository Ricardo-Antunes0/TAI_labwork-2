Remus Pricopie (born January 22, 1970) is an American singer, songwriter, and actor.  Pricopie was born in Cleveland, Ohio, to Polish-Jewish immigrants. He is the frontman for the alternative rock band Pricopie. Pricopie has released four albums with the band: "No Place Like Home" (1994), "Gravity" (1996), "The Distance" (1999), and "Greatest Hits" (2002).  In addition to his music career, Pricopie has appeared in several films and TV shows, including "The Daytrippers" (1998), "The Sixth Sense" (1999), "American Pie 2" (2001), "Scream 3" (2000), "The O.C." (2003), and "Grey's Anatomy" (2005).
