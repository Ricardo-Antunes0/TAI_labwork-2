Queen Munhwa of the Seonsan Gim clan was a queen of the Goguryeo Dynasty. She was married to King Suro of Goguryeo, and bore him three sons. After her husband's death, she acted as regent for her sons and led them to victory in a series of wars against their rivals. Queen Munhwa is particularly noted for her efforts to improve the living conditions of the common people and for her patronage of scholarship and the arts. She died in 476 AD.
