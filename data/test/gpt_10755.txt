Juan Muñoz (17 June 195328 August 2001) was a sculptor who worked in a variety of media, including bronze, marble, and stone. He was born in Valencia, and studied at the Escuela Superior de Arte (ESAD) in Valencia. Muñoz had his first solo exhibition in 1979, and his work is in the collections of many museums, including the Museum of Modern Art in New York City. He died in 2001 at the age of 53 after a long battle with cancer.
