Folliott Sandford was born on 26 April 1891 in Hampstead, London, England. He was educated at Christ Church, Oxford where he read Literae Humaniores. He ordained in 1926 and began his career as a curate in the Diocese of London. From 1937 to 1962 he was the Vicar of St James's, Regent's Park. He then became Archdeacon of London. In 1965 he was made a Canon Residentiary of St Paul's Cathedral and retired in 1971. He died on 8 July 1976.
