"Queeg" is the fifth episode of science fiction sitcom "Red Dwarf", first aired in the UK on BBC2 on 18 May 1988.  The crew of the "Red Dwarf" find themselves stranded on a strange and hostile planet after their ship is damaged in a battle with a space monster. They are saved by a group of aliens who guide them back to their own world.  The episode was written by Rob Grant and Doug Naylor, and directed by Ed Bye.  The cast includes Chris Barrie as the ship's captain, Craig Charles as the engineer, Danny John-Jules as the science officer, and Norman Lovett as the navigator.
