The Sierra Juárez is a range of mountains located in the Mexican state of Oaxaca. The range runs between the states of Chiapas and Veracruz, and is part of the Sierra Madre Oriental. The highest peak in the range is San Martín at 3,566 meters (11,552 ft).   The Sierra Juárez is an important source of water for the region, and it is also home to a number of important ecological areas. The range is popular for hiking and mountain biking, and it is a popular destination for tourists.
