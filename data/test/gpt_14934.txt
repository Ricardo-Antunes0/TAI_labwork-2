"American Ride" is a song written by Bruce Springsteen and released in 1984 as the first single from his album Born in the U.S.A. The song is about the journey of an American soldier returning home from Vietnam.   The song reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and spent five weeks at the top. It also reached number one in Canada and number two in Australia. The song has been certified quadruple platinum by the RIAA.
