Samuel Prophask Asamoah (born 1981) is a Ghanaian-American professional soccer player. He currently plays for Columbus Crew in Major League Soccer.  Asamoah was born in Accra, Ghana, in 1981. In his youth, he played for local clubs Atletico Madrid and Chelsea Boys Club. He attended the University of South Florida, where he played for the Tampa Bay Mutiny and captained the team to two NCAA Division I Final Four appearances.
