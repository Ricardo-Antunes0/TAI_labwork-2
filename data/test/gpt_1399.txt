{{Infobox rugby team | teamname     = Ebbw Vale RFC  | founded     = 1886  | country     = Wales  | region     = Mid Wales  | headquarters     = Ebbw Vale  | playing colors    = Blue and White  Ebbw Vale RFC is a Welsh rugby union club based in the town of Ebbw Vale. The team competes in the Welsh Rugby Union Division 2 East. The club was founded in 1886 and played its first ever match in 1892. Ebbw Vale RFC has a long and proud history, having won the Welsh Cup on three occasions (1926, 1932, 1963), as well as the Division Two East championship on four occasions (1933, 1935, 1938, 1956). The club also competed in the Anglo-Welsh Cup on one occasion (1962).
