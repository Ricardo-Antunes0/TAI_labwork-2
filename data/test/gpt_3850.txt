Novomessor ensifer is a species of ant in the genus Novomessor. It is found in Central America and North America.  Novomessor ensifer is a small, slender ant with an elongated and robust body. The head and mesonotum are black, and the rest of the body is reddish-brown. Workers are about one-half the size of queens, and have a conspicuous yellow clypeus. The queen is much smaller than the workers, and has a narrower waist.
