Sopocachi is a neighborhood in La Paz, Bolivia. It is located in the district of Santa Cruz and has a population of about 6,000. Sopocachi is known for its market, which is one of the largest in Bolivia. The market is also known for its street food, which includes llapingachos (a type of sandwich made from boiled maize pancakes filled with shredded meat or cheese), chicharrones (fried pork rinds), and anticuchos (a skewered meat dish).
