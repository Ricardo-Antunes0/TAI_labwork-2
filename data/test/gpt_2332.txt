Laura Michele Fortino (born January 30, 1991) is an American actress, model, and singer. Fortino began her acting career as a child in theater productions before making her television debut in the 2007 sitcom Scrubs. She has since appeared in a variety of roles on television and in feature films, becoming known for her roles in the romantic comedy-drama film The Fault in Our Stars (2014), the biographical drama film Stronger (2017), and the crime thriller film Annihilation (2018).   Fortino has also released two solo albums, the self-titled Laura Fortino in 2013 and Unforgettable in 2017.
