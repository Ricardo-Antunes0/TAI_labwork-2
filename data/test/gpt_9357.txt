The RD-855 (GRAU Index 8D68M), also known as the RBS-85, is a Russian anti-tank missile. It entered service with the Russian Army in 1985. The missile is fired from a portable launcher and has a range of 5,000 meters.  The RD-855 is a sophisticated weapon, featuring a semi-automatic launch system and a hit probability of 98%. The missile is capable of penetrating up to 760 millimeters of armor at a range of up to 5,000 meters.
