Villa 69 (Arabic: فيلا ٦٩) is a luxury hotel located in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.  Founded in 1969 by Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, Villa 69 is one of the world's leading luxury hotels. With its sweeping Arabian gardens and imposing white marble architecture, the hotel is a popular destination for celebrities and businesspeople.   The hotel has a total of 1,172 guest rooms, including 69 suites and 60 villas. The hotel's signature restaurant, Le Jardin, offers a range of international cuisine. Other facilities include a spa, a fitness centre, and a pool.
