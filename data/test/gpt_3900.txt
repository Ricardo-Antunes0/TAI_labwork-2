Tom Paterson is a Scottish comic artist, best known for his work on The Beano.  Tom Paterson was born on September 4th, 1951 in Edinburgh, Scotland. After studying at the Edinburgh College of Art, Paterson began his career in the late 1970s working for the satirical magazine, The Sporting Life. In the early 1980s, he began working for The Beano where he has remained a regular contributor ever since, collaborating with a wide range of writers and artists. His work has been celebrated for its anarchic sense of humor and its distinctive style which features exaggerated facial features and exaggerated body movements. Paterson has received numerous awards for his work including an OBE in 2002 and a CBE in 2007.
