Andrew "Andy" Parrish is a retired American professional wrestler and referee who is best known for his work in the World Wrestling Federation (WWF) from the early 1990s until the company's closure in 2001. He also refereed matches for the World Extreme Cagefighting (WEC) organization from 2002 until its closure in 2009.  Parrish was born on December 26, 1961, in Dallas, Texas. He began his wrestling career as a referee in the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA) during the early 1980s. In 1990, he moved to the World Wrestling Federation (WWF), where he worked as a referee and commentator. He also became a regular wrestler, appearing on WWF programming such as "Saturday Night's Main Event", "WWF Superstars", and "Raw". In 2000, Parrish retired from professional wrestling.
