John Stape is a fictional character from the Australian soap opera Neighbours, played by actor Marc Reid. He made his first screen appearance in the episode broadcast on 5 October 1985.  John Stape is the husband of former Neighbours actress Lesley Ash and father to her two children, Scott (Craig McLachlan) and Tasha (Elise Neal). He also has a half-sister, Georgie (Sue Jones) from his mother's previous marriage. In March 2002, it was announced that Reid had decided to leave the show and his last appearance was on 30 June 2002.  Reid's final scenes saw John reveal that he is gay. Following the storyline's conclusion, Stape embarked on a new career as a travel writer.
