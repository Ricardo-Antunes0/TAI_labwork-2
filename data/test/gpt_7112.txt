Davorin "Davo" Karničar (October 26, 1962 – February 16, 2016) was a Croatian professional basketball player. He was a 6'8" (2.03 m) center.  Karničar played for the Yugoslavia national basketball team at the 1988 European Championship and the 1990 FIBA World Championship. He also played for Zalgiris Kaunas, Virtus Bologna, Panathinaikos Athens and Caja Laboral.  Karničar was diagnosed with colon cancer in late 2015 and died from the disease on February 16, 2016 at the age of 59.
