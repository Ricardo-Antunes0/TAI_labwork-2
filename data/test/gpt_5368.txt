Finelines is the debut studio album by American singer and songwriter SZA. It was released on June 8, 2018, by Top Dawg Entertainment and Interscope Records. The album features guest appearances from Kendrick Lamar, Travis Scott, Pharrell Williams, and Isaiah Rashad.  The album's production was handled by SZA herself alongside a range of producers including Hit-Boy, Pharrell Williams, Ryan Tedder, and more. Upon its release, Finelines received generally positive reviews from music critics who praised its cohesive sound and SZA's vocals. The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, selling 116,000 album-equivalent units in its first week.  SZA was born in Atlanta, Georgia to a mother of African American and Native American descent and a father of Jamaican descent. She began writing music at the age of 12, after she was inspired by artists such as Lauryn Hill.
