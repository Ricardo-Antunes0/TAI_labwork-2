Goodenia grandiflora, commonly known as large-flowered goodenia, is a species of orchid native to eastern Australia.  Goodenia grandiflora is a large, showy orchid with flowers up to 50 centimetres (20 in) long and 20 centimetres (8 in) wide. The flowers are borne on a stout stalk up to 30 centimetres (12 in) tall. The flower petals are green and the labellum is red, white or yellow.  Goodenia grandiflora is found in eastern Australia, from the Cape York Peninsula, Queensland, to the Hunter Valley, New South Wales. It grows in moist to wet areas, near streams and rivers.
