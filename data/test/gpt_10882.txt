When We Rise is an eight-part American documentary miniseries directed by Ava DuVernay and executive produced by Oprah Winfrey. The series is based on the book of the same name by Dr. Maya Angelou.  The series premiered on PBS on February 19, 2017, and was released on Netflix on May 17, 2017.  The series tells the story of American history through the lens of African Americans. It covers topics such as racism, civil rights, gender inequality, and HIV/AIDS.  The series has been praised for its historical accuracy and its depiction of complex issues. However, some viewers have criticized it for its preachy nature and its lack of emotional impact.
