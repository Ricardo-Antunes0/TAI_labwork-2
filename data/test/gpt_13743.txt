Nki National Park (Parc national de Nki, in French) is a National Park in the Republic of the Congo. The park is located in the Ituri Forest, near the border with Rwanda.  Nki National Park was established in 1994. The park is home to a wide variety of animals, including elephants, gorillas, and lions. The park is also home to a variety of plant life, including bamboo and palm trees.
