Wonder Pets! is an American educational animated television series created by Paul Germain and Joe Ansolabehere, who also served as executive producers along with Jeffrey Katzenberg. The series is produced by DreamWorks Animation Television and distributed by Netflix. The show premiered on Netflix on August 22, 2019.  Wonder Pets! is a series about animals who use their imaginations to solve problems. Each episode features a different animal and its friends solving a problem together.
