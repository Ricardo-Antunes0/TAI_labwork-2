Liparit I Dadiani (; died 1470) was a Georgian prince of the Bagrationi dynasty. He was a son of King George V of Georgia by his wife Tamar, and a brother of George VI.  Liparit I Dadiani was born in 1436, probably in the town of Kutaisi. He succeeded his father as ruler of Imereti in 1470. Liparit I died in 1470, leaving no children. After his death, Imereti was divided between his two brothers, George VI and Constantine VIII.
