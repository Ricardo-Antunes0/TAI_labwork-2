Tetronarce californica also known as the Pacific electric ray, is a species of ray-finned fish in the family Torpedinidae.  The Pacific electric ray is a medium-sized ray-finned fish found in the eastern and central Pacific Ocean, from California to Japan. It is the only extant member of its genus Tetronarce. This species grows to a length of about .  The Pacific electric ray feeds mainly on small crustaceans and fishes, but also eats molluscs. It is an active hunter, swimming slowly through the water using its pectoral fins to propel itself forward.
