Harry Alfred Rée, DSO, OBE (15 October 1904 – 31 October 1990) was an English actor and theatrical producer.  Rée was born in Manchester, England, the son of Flora (née Cadman) and Alfred Rée. He attended Manchester Grammar School and then studied at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art before making his stage debut in 1924.  Rée made his first film appearance in 1929, and over the next few decades appeared in a variety of roles in British and American productions. He achieved prominence on stage in the late 1940s, appearing in a number of successful productions for the Royal Shakespeare Company. He also appeared in a number of television productions during this time.  Rée retired from acting in the late 1960s and later became a theatrical producer. He died at the age of 85 in 1990.
