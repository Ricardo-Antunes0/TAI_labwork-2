Trespassing is the second studio album by American heavy metal band Slipknot. The album was released on September 24, 2008, through Roadrunner Records. Produced by Slipknot and Ross Robinson, the album was noted for its more experimental sound, which diverged from the band's previous thrash metal style.  Upon its release, Trespassing received generally positive reviews from music critics. It debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart and has sold over 500,000 copies in the United States. The album won a Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance in 2009.
