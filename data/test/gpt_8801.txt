Andrei Angouw is a Chinese-Indonesian politician for the Indonesian Democratic Party of Struggle (PDI-P).  Andrei Angouw was born in Surabaya, East Java, on 8 December 1961. He is a lawyer by profession.   Angouw joined PDI-P in 1988 and has been a member of the party's Central Committee since 1993. He has served as the party's Secretary General since 2013.   Angouw has been a Member of the Indonesian Parliament since 2009, representing the East Java regency of Garut. In the 2014 election, he was elected to the House of Representatives as a PDI-P candidate.
