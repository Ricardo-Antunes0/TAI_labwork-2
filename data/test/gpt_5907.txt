The game was released in Japan on December 3, 2002, in North America on October 26, 2003, and in Europe on October 30, 2003.  The game is a spin-off of the "Pop'n Music" series and stars magical creatures known as pop idols who must help prevent an evil wizard from enslaving the world. It is set within a cartoon-style world and features remixes of songs from the "Pop'n Music" series.  "Magical Pop" was met with mixed reviews from critics. However, it was popular with gamers and sold well. A sequel, "Magical Pop'n Tour", was released for the PlayStation Portable in 2006.
