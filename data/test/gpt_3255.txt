Thiotricha rhodopa is a moth of the family Gelechiidae. It was described by Meyrick in 1913. It is found in India (Assam).  The wingspan is about 20Â mm. The forewings are light ochreous, with a slender streak of blackish suffusion from the base along the costa to one-fourth, and a blackish dot near the base of the costa. There is also a blackish streak from one-fourth of the costa to the dorsum before the tornus, and a terminal black dot. The hindwings are light grey.
