Datuk Seri Panglima Md Salleh bin Md Said Keruak (Jawi: سليم بن مدرع سعيد خروق; born 7 February 1944) is a Malaysian politician who is the current Speaker of the Parliament of Malaysia. He is also the Member of Parliament for the seat of Melaka, and a member of the United Malays National Organisation (UMNO) political party.  Salleh Said Keruak was born in Sungai Petani, Melaka on 7 February 1944. He received his early education at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Sungai Petani and then continued his studies at Universiti Teknologi MARA in Kuala Lumpur where he obtained his bachelor's degree in mathematics in 1966.   He subsequently commenced his studies at the University of Cambridge, England, where he obtained a master's degree in mathematics in 1968. He also holds a diploma in business administration from Henley Business School, England.  Salleh Said Keruak began his career as an officer with the Malaysian Armed Forcesin 1966. He later served as an officer with the Malaysian Civil Service from 1967 to 1982, holding various positions including Deputy Secretary-General of the Prime Minister's Department from 1981 to 1982 and Acting Secretary-General from 1982 to 1983.
