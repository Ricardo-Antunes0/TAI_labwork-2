Heartland is a comic book one-shot published by Image Comics in 1992. The comic was written and drawn by Mark Millar and was published in a limited run of 2000 copies.      Heartland is set in the fictional town of Heartland, Illinois, which is a thinly veiled reference to the real-life city of Chicago. The story revolves around the inhabitants of Heartland and their interactions with the outside world.  The comic has been praised for its dark and satirical elements, and has been cited as an influence on the development of the graphic novel form.
