The Maximato is a term used by historians to describe a period in the history of the Roman Empire lasting from 69 to 96. It began with the accession of Vespasian to the imperial throne and ended with the death of Domitian. The Maximato is characterised by significant political, military, and social changes which altered the course of Roman history.
