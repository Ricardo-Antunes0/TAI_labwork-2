Hear the Silence is a 2003 semi-fictional mystery novel written by Diane Setterfield.  Flora Gardener is an artist who has long struggled to find inspiration for her paintings. When she learns that her aunt, who she hasn't seen in years, is dying, Flora decides to take a trip to Scotland to visit her. While there, Flora meets a man named John who tells her about a legend of a woman who can hear the silence. Flora is intrigued and decides to stay longer to find out more about the legend. Eventually, Flora discovers that the woman in the legend is herself and she begins to hear the silence surrounding her. This leads her into difficult and dangerous situations, but Flora persists in seeking out answers.
