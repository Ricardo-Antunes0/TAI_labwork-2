Ernesto Lamagna ( born 1945) is a notable Italian artist. He is known for his paintings and sculptures which explore the relationship between man and nature.  Lamagna was born in 1945 in Naples, Italy. He studied at the University of Naples Federico II and the Academy of Fine Arts, Rome. Lamagna has exhibited his work in many countries, including Italy, France, Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Poland, and Spain. He has been awarded several prizes, including the Premio Nazionale di Sculpture in 1991 and the Premio Internazionale di Sculpture in 1998. Lamagna is currently a professor at the Academy of Fine Arts in Naples.
