Kondraty was born in Moscow on 1815 and died there in 1847. He learned to paint from his father, Dmitry Mikhaylovich Efimovich, who was a successful portrait painter in the Moscow School of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture.  Kondraty exhibited his works at the Russian Academy of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture from 1830 to 1846, when he died at the age of 27. He is best known for his portraits, but also produced landscapes and still lifes.
