Zamudio: Lost at Night is a Chilean drama television series created by Matías Piñera and directed by Claudio Soto. It stars Rodrigo Santoro as Zamudio, a man who returns home to Chile after being in exile for many years, and must deal with the consequences of his past actions.  The series premiered on TVN on March 16, 2018, and concluded on May 18, 2018.
