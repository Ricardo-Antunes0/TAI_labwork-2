The Sony Xperia Z2 is an Android-based phone released in October 2014. It is the successor to the Sony Xperia Z1 and uses the same Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 processor.  The Xperia Z2 has a 5-inch 1080p display, and is available in black, white or gold. It has a waterproof design and a 20MP rear camera with dual-LED flash, and 5MP front camera.  The Xperia Z2 has 3GB of RAM and 32GB of storage, which can be expanded using a microSD card. It runs on Android 4.4 KitKat, with Sony's proprietary user interface overlay.
