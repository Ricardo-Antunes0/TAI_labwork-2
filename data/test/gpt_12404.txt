Reginald Alan MacAdam (born March 16, 1952) is a Canadian retired ice hockey player. He played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Minnesota North Stars, Philadelphia Flyers, and Quebec Nordiques.  MacAdam was born in Sudbury, Ontario. He was drafted by the Minnesota North Stars in the fourth round of the 1971 NHL Amateur Draft. He played his first NHL game on October 11, 1973, against the Philadelphia Flyers.
