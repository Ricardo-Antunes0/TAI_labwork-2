Tallaght  is a stop on the Luas Red Line in Tallaght, Dublin. The stop is located on the north side of Tallaght Road, between the Tallaght roundabout and the Tallaght Shopping Centre. The stop has two platforms and four entrances.  The Luas stop opened on 4 August 2004, as part of the extension of the line to Tallaght. The station was designed by Hopkins Architects.
