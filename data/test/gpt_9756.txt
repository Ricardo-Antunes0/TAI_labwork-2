Chittagong College is a government college in Bangladesh. Established in 1914, it is the second oldest college in the country after Dhaka College.  Chittagong College is located in the city of Chittagong. The college has an area of 156 acres (61 ha). It has a population of about 20,000 students and 1,000 faculty and staff.  Chittagong College offers undergraduate programs in arts, sciences, commerce, law and medical sciences. The college also offers postgraduate programs in arts, sciences and commerce. The college has a library that has about 200,000 volumes. The college sports ground has facilities for cricket, football, badminton and basketball.
