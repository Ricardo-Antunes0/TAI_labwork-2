Dance music is music composed specifically to be danced to. It has evolved over time, incorporating different styles of music and becoming more complex. Many dance songs are inspired by current events or popular culture.  Dance music began as early as the late 17th century in England. During the 18th century, it became popular in France and Germany. In the early 19th century, it spread to Italy and Russia. In the early 20th century, dance music became a popular genre in North America. It became popular throughout Europe during the 1970s and 1980s.   Today, dance music is enjoyed throughout the world. It is sometimes used to promote social activism or as part of a concert experience. Dance music is often used in nightclubs, bars, and other venues.
