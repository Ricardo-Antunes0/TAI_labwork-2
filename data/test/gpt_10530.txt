Pedro Tapia, O.P. (March 1582 – 25 September 1652) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Santiago de Compostela (1640–1652).  Pedro Tapia was born in Salamanca, Spain in 1582. He was ordained a priest in the Order of Preachers on July 4, 1612. On July 27, 1640, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Urban VIII as Bishop of Santiago de Compostela. He served as Bishop of Santiago de Compostela until his death on September 25, 1652.
