Always B Miki (foaled 2011) is a British Thoroughbred racehorse and broodmare. She was undefeated in three races as a juvenile, winning the Group One Fillies' Mile at Ascot Racecourse and the Group Two Pretty Polly Stakes at Newmarket Racecourse. In the following year she finished second to the colt Golden Horn in the Group One Breeders' Cup Mile. In 2016 she was runner-up in both of her races, first to the Japanese-owned Takarazuka Kinen and then to Al Mahaab.  Always B Miki is a bay mare with a white blaze and two white socks bred in the United Kingdom by her owner Khalid Abdullah. She was sent into training with John Gosden.  She made her racecourse debut in a six furlong maiden race at Doncaster Racecourse on 25 May and won by half a length from the filly Lady Elizabeth. On 8 June she contested her first Group One race, the Fillies' Mile at Ascot Racecourse and started 3/1 favourite against seven opponents. Ridden by Gosden, she raced close to the leaders before taking the lead in the last quarter mile and winning by one and a half lengths from Princess of Wales (later to win the Irish 1000 Guineas).
