Damir Siraciev (,  Damir Siraziev, born 12 February 1984) is a Macedonian footballer who currently plays for FK Vardar. He also holds Turkish citizenship.  Siraciev started his career in his hometown club FK Metalurg Skopje, before moving to Turkish side Bursaspor in 2005. After two seasons at the club, he moved to Greek side AEK Athens, where he played for two seasons. In July 2009, Siraciev transferred to Bulgarian side Ludogorets Razgrad. He helped Ludogorets win the Bulgarian Cup in his first season with the club, and also made twelve appearances in Bulgaria's First League. He then moved to Romanian side FC Steaua București on a three-year contract in July 2011. Siraciev made his debut for Steaua on 10 August 2011 in a 1–0 away victory over ACS Poli Timișoara.  Siraciev was part of the Macedonian national team that competed at the 2006 FIFA World Cup and the 2007 UEFA European Championship.
