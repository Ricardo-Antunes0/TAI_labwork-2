Camp Allen, is a small United States Army post located in the city of Allen, Texas. It was formerly known as Camp Robinson and was named for General James Allen, a Union general during the American Civil War. The post is currently home to the 1st Cavalry Division (Airmobile).  Camp Allen was originally established in 1917 as Camp Robinson, after General James Allen, a Union general during the American Civil War. The post was renamed in honor of General Allen in 1959. The 1st Cavalry Division (Airmobile) is currently headquartered at Camp Allen.
