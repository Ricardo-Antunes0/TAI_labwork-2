Leduck Island is an island in the Louisiade Archipelago, part of the Solomon Islands. The island is situated in the Eastern Solomon Sea, about northeast of Guadalcanal and northwest of Santa Isabel Island. Its area is .  Leduck Island is home to approximately 150 people who are subsistence farmers and traders. It has a small airstrip that is used primarily for the transport of goods to and from the island. There is no permanent population on Leduck Island.
