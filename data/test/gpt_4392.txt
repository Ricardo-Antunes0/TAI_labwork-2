Peter John Winter (born 17 January 1971) is a retired Australian athlete who specialised in the 400 metres. He represented Australia at the 1996 Summer Olympics and finished fourth in his heat, failing to advance to the semifinals. Winter also competed at the 2000 Summer Olympics and finished sixth in his heat, again failing to advance to the semifinals. Winter's personal best time in the 400 metres is 48.48 seconds, achieved in July 1998 in Melbourne.
