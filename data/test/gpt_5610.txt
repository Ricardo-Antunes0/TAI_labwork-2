Spartacus is an American television series produced by Starz and written by Steven S. DeKnight. It premiered on the premium cable network on January 10, 2014 and concluded on June 12, 2014 with a total of 60 episodes. The series is set in the late Roman Republic and follows the life and struggles of Spartacus, a Thracian slave who leads a rebellion against his Roman masters.  The series received generally positive reviews from television critics. It was nominated for nine Primetime Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Drama Series, but lost all nine to Game of Thrones.
