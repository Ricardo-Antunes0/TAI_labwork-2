Enel Green Power S.p.A. is an Italian energy company. The firm was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Milan. It has operations in Italy, France, Spain, Germany, Austria, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania and Slovakia.  Enel Green Power is one of the largest producers of renewable energy in the world. The company's products include electricity, renewable heat and green power. It has a portfolio of over 30 wind and solar power plants with a total installed capacity of more than 12 gigawatts (GW). In addition, the company sells electricity and renewable energy products to corporate customers and municipal authorities in over 20 countries.
