Mystery in Mexico is a 1948 crime film directed by Edward Dmytryk and starring Dana Andrews, Raymond Burr, and Loretta Young.  The film is about a US Army officer, Major Ben Ryan (Dana Andrews), who is stationed in Mexico City. Ryan's wife (Loretta Young) has recently died and he is trying to come to terms with his grief. One night, Ryan's house is broken into and his wife's necklace is stolen. Ryan begins to investigate the theft and soon finds that it may have been connected to a series of murders that have been happening in the city.
