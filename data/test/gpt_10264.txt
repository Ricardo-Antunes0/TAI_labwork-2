Riverbend Apartments was an infamous 600-unit apartment complex in Memphis, Tennessee that was the scene of a horrific fire on February 8, 2007. The fire killed almost two dozen people, making it the deadliest in U.S. history.  The complex was built in the 1970s and was known for its poor safety record and shoddy construction. The complex was also plagued by code violations and had been the subject of several lawsuits.
