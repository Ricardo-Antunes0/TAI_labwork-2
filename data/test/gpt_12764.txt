The Guillotines is a 2012 Chinese-Hong Kong action-thriller film directed by Johnnie To and starring Tony Leung Chiu Wai, Zhang Ziyi and Donnie Yen.  The Guillotines is a gripping, edge-of-your-seat thriller set in the days of the French Revolution. When a group of revolutionaries attempt to assassinate the tyrannical leader of France, they unwittingly unleash a vengeful spirit that will stop at nothing to punish them. Facing impossible odds, the rebels must use all their ingenuity and courage to survive.
