Wisteria Lane is the name of a street in the fictional town of Springfield, Massachusetts. It is the location of the home of the Simpson family, which is owned by Homer and Marge Simpson.  The street originally appeared in the first episode of the animated television series "The Simpsons" which aired on October 17, 1987. The name was chosen by Dan Castellaneta, who voices Homer Simpson. It is an allusion to the song "Wisteria Lane" by The Beatles. The street is also mentioned in "The Simpsons Movie" (2007).
