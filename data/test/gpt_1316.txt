Music On Console (MOC) is an ncurses-based music player for various console platforms, such as the NES, SNES, Genesis, Game Boy Advance, and PlayStation. It has a custom skinned interface and supports a variety of music formats, including MP3 and OGG.  Music On Console was written by Daniel Robbins in 2000. It was inspired by the Amiga Music Player, which he used to listen to music on his Commodore Amiga. The program is free and open source software released under the GNU General Public License.  MOC has been praised for its user-friendly interface, support for a variety of music formats, and ability to keep track of playlists. However, some reviewers have noted that it is not as feature-rich as other music players available on different console platforms.
