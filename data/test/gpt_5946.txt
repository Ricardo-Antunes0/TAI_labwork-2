Schwabacher Landing is a boat landing located in the Town of Westfield on the western shore of Lake Ontario. The landing is adjacent to the Schwabacher Marina and offers access to both the lake and its many waterways. The landing is also the location of Schwabachers Island, a privately owned island that is part of the Town of Westfield.  Schwabacher Landing was originally a landing for logging boats that provided access to the numerous waterways in and around Westfield. The landing gradually developed into a popular spot for fishermen, boaters, and birdwatchers. In recent years, the landing has become popular among tourists who visit Schwabachers Island and the surrounding areas.
