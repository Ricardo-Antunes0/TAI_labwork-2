Paweł Wocial  is a Polish installation artist who lives and works in Warsaw.  Paweł Wocial was born on March 28th, 1976 in Łódź, Poland. After getting his degree in Fine Arts from the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw in 2000, he started working as an installation artist. In 2003 he founded the Warsaw-based performance art collective "Krakowskie Przedmieście" with Michał Witkiewicz and Maciej Kuciak.   Wocial’s installations are conceptual and often involve the use of light and sound. He has exhibited at venues including the National Museum in Warsaw, the Museum of Contemporary Art in Krakow, and the Centre Pompidou in Paris. In 2013 he won the Wolf Vostell Prize.
