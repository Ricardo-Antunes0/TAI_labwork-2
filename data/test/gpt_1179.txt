Word Ways: The Journal of Recreational Linguistics is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes research on the nature and use of words. The journal has been published since 1978 and is currently edited by James D. McCarty (University of Massachusetts Amherst).  Word Ways is devoted to the study of language in all its forms and manifestations, from individual words to entire language systems. It covers both theoretical and applied work, with an emphasis on the investigation of language in natural settings. Word Ways welcomes submissions on all aspects of word use, including morphology, syntax, semantics, phonology, pragmatics, discourse analysis, corpus linguistics, and other methodsologies.
