Wooden language has been used in different parts of the world for centuries, and it is still commonly used today.  There are a variety of reasons why people might choose to use wooden language. Some people may find it more comfortable to communicate in a vague way, while others may prefer to use idioms because they sound more natural. Regardless of why someone chooses to use wooden language, it is an interesting and unique form of communication that is worth exploring.
