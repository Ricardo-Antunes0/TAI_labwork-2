HackFwd was a German pre-seed company started in Berlin in March of 2016 by Florian Windhorst and Rafal Mleczko.  HackFwd is a German pre-seed company that helps startups to accelerate their development by providing them with the resources they need to get their products to market as quickly as possible.  The company operates out of Berlin and offers access to its resources to startups around the world. Its mission is to help startups achieve their goals and disrupt the industry.
