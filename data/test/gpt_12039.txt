Aaron A. Brooks, also known as, Aaron Brooks, is an American comedian, actor, writer, and producer. He is known for his work on the Comedy Central series Reno 911!, where he played Deputy Andy Brooks for six seasons from 2003 to 2009. Brooks has also starred in the films Swingers and I Love You, Man.  Brooks was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, to a Jewish family. After graduating from high school, Brooks moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in comedy. He began performing at open mic nights and soon found himself working as a stand-up comedian on the Tonight Show with Jay Leno and The Late Show with David Letterman.  In 2003, Brooks was cast as Deputy Andy Brooks on Comedy Central's hit series Reno 911!. The show ran for six seasons and featured Brooks' trademark brand of off-the-wall humor. In addition to his work on Reno 911!, Brooks has starred in the films Swingers and I Love You, Man. He currently stars in the Fox sitcom The Last Man on Earth.
