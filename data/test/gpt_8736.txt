Wolf Totem  is a 2004 Chinese-American neo-noir mystery film directed by Hong Kong filmmaker Wong Kar-wai.  The film is set in the city of Los Angeles, and tells the story of a detective, played by Jack Nicholson, investigating the murder of his partner. The film also features a cameo appearance by actress Sharon Stone.  The film has been well received by critics, with praise going to its visuals and Nicholson's performance. It won the Palme d'Or at the 2004 Cannes Film Festival and was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Screenplay.
