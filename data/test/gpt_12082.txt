The MX2 is a carbon fiber, two seat, single engine, jet aircraft designed and produced by MX Aircraft. The aircraft is supplied complete and ready-to-fly.  The MX2 was designed to provide an affordable jet aircraft for personal use or as a platform for light tourism. The aircraft has a single engine in pusher configuration and can be flown either as a solo or dual flight model.  The MX2 is made from carbon fiber which gives the aircraft a strong and lightweight construction. The aircraft is capable of carrying up to two people and has a range of up to 500 kilometers.
