Robin Galloway (born 18 December 1961) is a Scottish former professional footballer who played as a midfielder.  Galloway began his career with Airdrieonians before moving to Rangers in 1981. He won the 1983 Scottish League Cup with Rangers, and also played for Aberdeen, Dundee United, Motherwell and Hamilton Academical.  In 2006, Galloway was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer and died in October 2006 at the age of 50.
