Jānis Puriņš was a Latvian Rifleman and a decorated veteran of World War II. He was also a noted artist and sculptor.  Jānis Puriņš was born in 1919 in the town of Ventspils, Latvia. He served in the Latvian Army during World War II, and after the war he studied at the Riga Art School. Puriņš worked as an artist and sculptor until his death in 1995. His work is notable for its realism and its use of traditional Latvian folk art techniques.
