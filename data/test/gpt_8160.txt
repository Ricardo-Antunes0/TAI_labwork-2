Clara de noche (Clara at night), is a Mexican telenovela produced by Carlos Sotomayor for Televisa in 1980.  Clara de noche is a Mexican telenovela produced by Carlos Sotomayor for Televisa in 1980. It is the story of Clara, a poor girl who is forced to become a prostitute to support her impoverished family. A rich man, Diego, falls in love with Clara and begins a campaign to save her from her life of degradation.
