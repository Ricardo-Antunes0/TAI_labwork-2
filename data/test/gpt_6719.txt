"Chemicals React" is a pop rock song written by John Lennon and credited to The Beatles. It was recorded on 5 November 1965 during the sessions for the Beatles' album Help! and released as a single on 10 November 1965.  The song is about a boy who is upset because his friends have all gone to see the love of their lives, while he has to stay at home and do chores. He laments that chemicals always react and he wishes they would just stop reacting and let him be happy too.
