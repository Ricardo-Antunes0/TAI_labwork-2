Episode 1094 is the opening episode of the third season of the American science fiction television series, "Stargate SG-1."  The Atlantis expedition arrives at the planet P3X-797, where they find a Goa'uld mining operation. SG-1 investigates and discovers a way to destroy the mine, but they are attacked by the Goa'uld. In the ensuing battle, Teal'c is injured and is taken back to Atlantis. Colonel Jack O'Neill and Major Samantha Carter return to Earth with a new weapon to defeat the Goa'uld.
