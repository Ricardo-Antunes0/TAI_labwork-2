Righeira were an Italian Italo disco duo formed in 1979.  The duo consisted of brothers Mario and Salvatore Righeira, who were originally from the town of Cremona. They started their career as backing vocalists for various Italian pop and rock bands, before releasing their own single, "I'm in Love", in 1979. The song reached the top 10 on the Italian Singles Chart and was followed by a string of successful singles. In 1982, they released their debut album, "Righeira". The album was certified platinum and spawned several hit singles, including "Body Dance", "Chocolate Cake" and "Disco Lady". The duo disbanded in 1984 but reunited in 1995 to perform at the Eurovision Song Contest 1995.
