Suddenly Susan is an American television sitcom that aired on ABC from September 24, 1988, to May 20, 1989. The show starred Valerie Bertinelli as Susan Boyle, a down-on-her-luck singer who is thrust into the national spotlight after appearing on an amateur talent show. The show was cancelled after one season.  Susan Boyle is a Scottish singer who became famous after appearing on the UK television talent show "America's Got Talent" in 2009. The show aired on NBC in the United States and was a major hit, winning Boyle a record deal and leading to her appearance on the UK version of "The X Factor" in 2011.
