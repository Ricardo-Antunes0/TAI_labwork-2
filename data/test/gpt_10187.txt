He made his stage debut in 1932 in the role of Friedrich in Max Reinhardt's play "Die Geschwister Scholl". Schmidt was frequently involved in both stage and screen productions during the Nazi period. After World War II, he resumed his career and appeared in numerous films, often playing supporting roles. He also appeared in several television productions. Schmidt died in Berlin aged 63.
