The Tiwest Joint Venture was a joint venture between Tiwest and Canopy Growth Corporation that operated in the Canadian province of Newfoundland and Labrador. The venture was established in 2014 and had a combined market share of 37% in the province by July 2017. Tiwest was responsible for cultivation, production, and sales while Canopy Growth Corporation provided operational support, including management, marketing, and branding. In July 2017, Tiwest announced that it would be exiting the joint venture due to regulatory pressures.
