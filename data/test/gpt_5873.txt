Dirk Geeraerts (born 24 October 1955) is a Belgian painter, sculptor and performance artist.  Dirk Geeraerts was born in Antwerp on 24 October 1955. He studied painting at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Antwerp, Belgium, from 1973 to 1978. In 1981, he moved to New York City, where he continued his studies at the School of Visual Arts.   Geeraerts has exhibited his work internationally since the early 1980s. His work is in the collections of the Museum of Modern Art, New York; the Centre Pompidou, Paris; and the Musée d'Art Moderne de la Ville de Paris. In 2001, he was awarded the Grand Prix de la Ville de Liège.  Geeraerts lives and works in Antwerp.
