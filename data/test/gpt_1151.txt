Koili Devi was born in 1929 in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. She enrolled at the Allahabad University in 1949 and received her law degree from the University of Allahabad in 1955. Koili Devi began her career as an advocate in the Allahabad High Court in 1963. In 1974, she became the first woman to be appointed a judge of a High Court in India, and the first woman to serve as the Chief Justice of a State High Court. She served as Chief Justice of the Rajasthan High Court from 1985 to 1988 and as Chief Justice of the Madhya Pradesh High Court from 1988 to 1995. Koili Devi retired from active service in 1995. In 2006, she was awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian honor. Koili Devi died on 10 November 2007 at her home in Jaipur, India.
