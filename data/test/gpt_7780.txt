Rockstar London Limited is a British video game developer and publisher, based in London, England. The company was founded in 1997 by brothers Sam and Dan Houser.  Rockstar London is known for its work on the Grand Theft Auto series, as well as the Bully, Max Payne and Manhunt franchises. The company has also developed games for other platforms, including the Wii, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360.
