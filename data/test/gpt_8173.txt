Eni Jurišić (born 22 June 1990), known as Eni or Juri, is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Serie A club Udinese.  Jurišić started playing football with his local team NK Slaven Belupo, before moving to Dinamo Zagreb in 2007. He made his first-team debut in 2009 and went on to make 137 appearances for the club before joining Udinese in 2016.  Jurišić has represented Croatia at youth level and made one appearance for the senior side.
