Manfred Amerell (born 25 February 1947 in Münster) is a German actor.  Amerell was born in Münster, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. After studying acting at the Berliner Schauspielhaus, he made his screen debut in the television movie Der letzte Mohikaner (1979). He has since appeared in a number of films, including Der neue Mönch (1981), Die Welle (1983), and Der Untergang (2004). Amerell has also starred in several successful stage productions, including Die Rolle des Galileo Galilei (1982), Der Freischütz (1995), and Der Kaufmann von Venedig (2003).
