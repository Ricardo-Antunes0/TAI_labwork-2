97 Express Kelowna RapidBus or 97 Okanagan RapidBus is a bus rapid transit system in the Okanagan Valley region of British Columbia, Canada. The system has six routes that serve Kelowna, Penticton, Vernon, West Kelowna and Peachland.  The 97X Kelowna RapidBus was approved by the Regional District of Central Okanagan in 2013 and began operating on October 15th of that year. The system is operated by the Regional District of Central Okanagan and uses a fleet of Gillig buses.   The 97X Kelowna RapidBus is unique in that it is the only BRT system in the Okanagan Valley. It has been praised for its reliability and speed, with riders travelling between Kelowna and Penticton in 25 minutes or less.
