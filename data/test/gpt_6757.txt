Bálint László (Serbian: Balint Laslo; born January 12, 1988) is a Serbian professional basketball player for FMP of the Bulgarian League. Standing at 6'11" (2.11 m) tall, he plays as a center.  Bálint László was born in Zrenjanin, Serbia on January 12, 1988. He started playing basketball at the age of six, with local team Partizan. After spending four years playing in the junior ranks of Partizan, László made his debut with the senior team in 2004–05 season. In his first season with Partizan, he averaged 8.5 points and 5.8 rebounds in 25 games played, helping them to win the Serbian Cup championship.  In the 2005–06 season, László improved his averages by averaging 14 points and 10 rebounds per game. He also helped Partizan win the Serbian League championship and reach the semifinals of the Adriatic League championship. In 26 games played in the 2006–07 season, László scored 21 points and grabbed 11 rebounds on average.  László left Partizan in 2007 to sign with Crvena zvezda of the Serbian Super Liga. In his first season with Crvena zvezda, he averaged 16 points and 11 rebounds per game.
