Bruno Sroka (born July 9, 1976 in Krakow, Poland) is a Polish-born French professional basketball player. He currently plays for ASVEL Basket of the LNB Pro A.  Sroka played college basketball at the University of Connecticut, where he was a two-time All-American and two-time Big East Player of the Year. He was selected by the Atlanta Hawks in the first round of the 1999 NBA draft. He also played for the Philadelphia 76ers, Milwaukee Bucks, and Golden State Warriors.  Sroka has also played for the Polish national team, and was a part of the Polish team that won a silver medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics.
