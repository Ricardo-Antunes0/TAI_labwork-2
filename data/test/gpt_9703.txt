Robert ÆOLUS Myers was an American composer, songwriter, musician and producer.     His works have been described as "highly melodic", "catchy", and "memorable".     Myers has written and produced songs for artists such as Britney Spears, Backstreet Boys, Aerosmith, N Sync, Whitney Houston, Christina Aguilera, Justin Timberlake and Celine Dion.  Robert ÆOLUS Myers was born on November 10, 1958 in Tulsa, Oklahoma. He began composing and producing music at a young age and achieved early success writing songs for artists such as Britney Spears, Backstreet Boys, Aerosmith and N Sync. Myers has written and produced songs for over 150 albums, selling over 150 million records worldwide. He is also known for his work on the Disney movies The Little Mermaid and Beauty and the Beast. In 2018 he received a Grammy Award nomination for his contribution to the album D'Angelo & The Vanguard: A Tribute to Duke Ellington.
