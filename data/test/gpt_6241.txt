Project Horizon was an ambitious study undertaken in 1959 to assess the feasibility of sending a manned mission to Mars. The study's goals were to gain a better understanding of the space environment and how humans wouldreact in space, as well as to investigate possible methods of achieving a manned mission. The study's conclusions were that such a mission was technically achievable, but would require significant advances in technology.
