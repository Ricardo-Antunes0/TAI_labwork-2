Shirley Washington the first Miss Black America was born on July 8, 1914 in Tuskegee, Alabama. She started modeling at the age of sixteen and later became a popular actress in Hollywood. Washington died on May 13, 2008.   Shirley Washington was the first African American to be crowned Miss America and to win an Oscar. She was also the first black actress to be nominated for an Academy Award for Best Actress. Additionally, she has been honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and has been inducted into the Television Hall of Fame.
