My Brother is the biography of Muhammad Ali Jinnah, the founder of Pakistan. The book tells the story of Muhammad Ali Jinnah's life from his early years in India to his political career and eventual role in founding Pakistan.  The book is written by his younger brother, Hasan Ali Jinnah. The book provides a unique and comprehensive account of Jinnah's life and his contribution to the development of Pakistan. It is an excellent source of information for students and researchers interested in the history of Pakistan and the Muslim world.
