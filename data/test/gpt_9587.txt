Clémentine (pronounced ) was a 1985 French romantic comedy film directed by Claude Lelouch and written by Luc Besson and Jean-Paul Rappeneau. The film stars Juliette Binoche, Denis Lavant, and Gérard Depardieu.  The film tells the story of a woman who moves from Paris to the small town of Saint-Tropez to start a new life with her new husband. She soon discovers that the life she imagined is not the same as the one she is living.
