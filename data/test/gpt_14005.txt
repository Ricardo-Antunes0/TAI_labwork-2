Warm Leatherette is the fourth studio album by American rock band The Killers. It was released on November 10, 2010, through Island Records. The album was produced by Dave Sardy and the band's regular producer Jacknife Lee.  The album features a more stripped-down sound than their previous releases, with the band citing influences such as The White Stripes, The Clash and Neil Young. "Warm Leatherette" has received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's return to their rawer roots.
