Circus is the sixth studio album of Argentine rock band Argent. It was released on January 12, 2018, through Sony Music Argentina.  Argent began working on the album in October 2017, shortly after the release of their fifth album, "Era". The band worked with producers such as David Kahne and John Feldmann, who have previously worked with acts such as Blink-182 and Green Day. Circus is Argent's first album in six years and their first with new singer Gustavo Cerati.  The album's lead single, "Matador", was released on December 1, 2017. The song has been praised for its raw energy and its similarity to the band's earlier work. The second single, "Borracho", was released on January 12, 2018.
