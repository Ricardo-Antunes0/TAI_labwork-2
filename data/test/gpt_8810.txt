Henry Tang Ying-yen  (; born 6 December 1964) is a Hong Kong business magnate, investor and philanthropist.  Henry Tang was born on 6 December 1964 in Hong Kong. He is the son of Tang Shiu-kai, who was a lieutenant general in the Chinese People's Liberation Army and a former member of the National People's Congress. Tang earned a degree in business administration from the University of Southern California.  Tang is the founder and CEO of Henderson Land Development, one of the largest property developers in Hong Kong. He has also been involved in other businesses, including real estate, finance, and telecommunications. In October 2009, he was ranked 83rd on "Forbes" magazine's list of the world's billionaires, with a net worth of US$4.5 billion.  Henry Tang has donated millions of dollars to charity, including donations to establish the Henry Tang Ying-yen Foundation, which focuses on education and health care initiatives in Hong Kong and around the world. He has also been involved in volunteer work with organizations such as United Way Worldwide and the Red Cross.
