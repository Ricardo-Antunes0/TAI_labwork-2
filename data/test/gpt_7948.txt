Solid (Korean: 솔리드) is a South Korean boy group formed in 2014 by YG Entertainment. The group consists of four members: Zion.T, Crown, Daehyun, and Jaehyo.  The group first debuted on August 5, 2014, with the single "Don't Cry". Solid released their first EP, "Solid", on September 3, 2014. The album peaked at number two on the Gaon Album Chart. In December 2014, the group won the 'Best New Artist' award at the Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA).
