Reboot Camp is an American satirical comedy web series that premiered on March 22, 2019 on YouTube. The show is created and written by Ben Wexler and stars Brian Huskey, Dan St. Germain, and Hannah Simone.  Reboot Camp is a satire about the youth culture of the early 21st century. The series follows a group of kids who are forced to attend a Reboot Camp to restore their moral values.
