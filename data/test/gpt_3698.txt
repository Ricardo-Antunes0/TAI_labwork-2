BHEL Township, Hyderabad is  a suburb of Hyderabad in the Indian state of Telangana. It is located at .  BHEL Township was originally established as a township in 1964 and was later upgraded to a municipality in 1984. The township is home to the headquarters of the Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), one of the largest conglomerates in India. The township also has several educational institutions, including a private medical college and a government technical college.
