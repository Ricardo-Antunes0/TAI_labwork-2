Yılmaz Gruda (born 14 July 1930) is a Turkish actor and singer. He is best known for his role as Süleyman in the TV series İslamiye.  Gruda was born in Istanbul on 14 July 1930. He began his career as a singer and actor in the 1950s. He is best known for his role as Süleyman in the TV series İslamiye, which aired from 1968 to 1977.
