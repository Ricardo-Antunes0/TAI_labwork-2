Dieppa is a surname. Early Origins of the Dieppa surname include the French place name Dieppe, from the Latin dieppa meaning "a rocky cliff overlooking the sea". The place name is now a municipality in the Seine-Maritime department in the Normandy region of north-western France.  The Dieppa surname is derived from the French place name Dieppe, from the Latin dieppa meaning "a rocky cliff overlooking the sea". The place name is now a municipality in the Seine-Maritime department in the Normandy region of north-western France. The Dieppa surname was first found in Normandy where they held a family seat as Lords of Dieppe.
