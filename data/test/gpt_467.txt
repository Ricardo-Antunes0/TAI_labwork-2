Richard the Stork (Released in North America as Richard Scarry's Stork Tales) is an American children's animated television series produced by The Fred Seibert Company and distributed by Warner Bros. Television. The series aired on PBS from 1987 to 1992, and then on Fox Family from 1992 to 1995.  The show is based on the characters created by Richard Scarry, who also provided the original storyboards and direction. The show features a cast of colorful characters, as well as educational content about the natural world.
