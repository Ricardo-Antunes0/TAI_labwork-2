Sihanoukville Autonomous Port (; , PAS) is an autonomous port located in Sihanoukville, Cambodia. The port is located on the Gulf of Thailand and is operated by the Sihanoukville Port Authority.   The Sihanoukville Autonomous Port was established in 1994 to provide an alternative to the Phnom Penh International Port. The port was initially used to transport goods to and from Vietnam, but has since expanded its operations to include container shipping, bulk cargo, and cruise ships. The port has a capacity of handling 1.5 million tons of cargo per year. In 2006, the port handled 1.1 million tons of cargo.  The Sihanoukville Autonomous Port is also a major tourist destination in Cambodia. The port has a number of restaurants, hotels, and other attractions that are popular with tourists.
