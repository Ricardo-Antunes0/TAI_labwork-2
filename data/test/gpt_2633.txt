Steve David (born 11 March 1951 in Liverpool) is an English singer-songwriter best known for his work with the band Humble Pie.  David was born in 1951 in Liverpool, England. He started his career as a songwriter and vocalist with the band Humble Pie, which formed in 1969. The band released several albums before David left to pursue a solo career in the early 1980s. His first solo album, "The Steve David Show", was released in 1983. David has since released several more albums, and has been featured on several others. He has also performed live concerts throughout the world.
