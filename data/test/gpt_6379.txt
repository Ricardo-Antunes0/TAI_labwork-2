The Export Promotion Council of Kenya (EPC) was established in 1984 to promote exports and generate foreign exchange. The EPC is a private sector-led organisation that works with the government to develop export strategies, promote exports, and provide support to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the export sector.  The EPC has a membership of over 200 companies from the private, public and non-profit sectors. Its mission is to promote exports and generate foreign exchange for the country, through its activities including:  -Creating and implementing export promotion programmes; -Providing consultancy services to small and medium-sized enterprises in the export sector; -Coordinating promotional events; -Strengthening trade links with key countries and regions; -Publishing industry reports and white papers.
