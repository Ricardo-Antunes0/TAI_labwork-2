Major General Duncan Edward Lewis  (born c. 1951) is a retired Canadian Army officer who served as Chief of the Defence Staff from November 2009 to January 2013.  Lewis was born in Toronto, Ontario in 1951. He joined the Canadian Army in 1971, and served in a variety of infantry and artillery positions before being commissioned as an officer in 1985. Lewis has held a number of senior command and staff roles, including Deputy Commander Land Force Command, Commander British Columbia Land Force and Commander Joint Task Force Kandahar. He became Chief of the Defence Staff in November 2009. Lewis retired from the Canadian Army in January 2013.
