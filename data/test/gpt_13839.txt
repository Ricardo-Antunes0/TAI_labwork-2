RushTera is software as a service, that helps businesses streamline their operations by automating repetitive tasks.   RushTera was founded in 2013 by two entrepreneurs, who saw a need in the business world for a software that could help streamline their operations. RushTera offers a suite of software that automate tedious and time-consuming tasks, such as sending email, organizing files, and tracking customer data. The company has received significant investment from venture capitalists and has raised over $5 million to date.
