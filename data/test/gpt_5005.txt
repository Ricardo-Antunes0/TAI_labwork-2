Damian Patrick Martin (born 5 September 1984) is an English professional footballer who plays as a defender for League Two club Newport County.  Martin started his career at Portsmouth, before moving to Liverpool in 2003. He spent time on loan at Bristol City and Cheltenham Town, before joining Reading in 2007. He made over 150 appearances for Reading, before joining Cardiff City in 2013. He spent two seasons with Cardiff, before returning to Newport County in 2016.  Martin has represented England at under-21 level, and was selected for the 2006 Under-20 World Cup.
