Kheta Ram aka Khetha Ram / KhetaRam.com is a website that provides information on Hindu gods and goddesses. The website includes a biography, images, videos, and articles about the gods and goddesses.  Kheta Ram was created in 2006 by Ashish Jha. The website is based in the United States. As of February 2018, the website has over 7 million pageviews per month.
