TeleMessage is an Israeli software company based in Tel Aviv, Israel. The company develops a messaging app for Android and iOS devices.  TeleMessage was founded in 2013 by Amir Sarid and Oren Hazan. The company's first product was a messaging app for Android devices. The app was later released for iOS devices. The company has raised $1.5 million in venture funding from investors including Kala Capital and Bessemer Venture Partners.  TeleMessage's messaging app is available in 16 languages and supports text, photos, and videos. The app has been downloaded more than 5 million times globally.
