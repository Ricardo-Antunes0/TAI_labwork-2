Shararat (English: Mischief) is a 2002 Bollywood thriller film directed by Prakash Jha and produced by Karan Johar. The film stars Aishwarya Rai, Abhishek Bachchan, and Kareena Kapoor in pivotal roles.  The film tells the story of a family torn apart by the clash of cultures and ideologies in contemporary India. Wife Shabana (Aishwarya Rai) tries to protect her husband Nand (Abhishek Bachchan), a respected civil servant, from the political pressure that is mounting on him. Daughter-in-law Rani (Kareena Kapoor) supports her husband in his work, but is also pressured to conform to traditional Indian values. Young son Sanjay (Aarav Bedian) idolizes his father and is torn between his love for his mother and his loyalty to his father's beliefs. As the pressure mounts on Nand, he is forced to make a difficult decision that could jeopardize his career.
