Aric Cushing (born September 26, 1973) is an American actor, voice actor, and comedian.  Cushing was born in Buffalo, New York, the son of Lois (n√©e Horowitz), an interior designer and art consultant, and Bruce Cushing, an artist and sculptor. His father is Jewish and his mother is of Irish Catholic descent. He has two sisters, Stephanie and Jennifer. Cushing attended the North Tonawanda Central School District.  Cushing first came to prominence as a cast member on the sketch comedy show " MADtv ". He played the role of "Drew Carey's Sidekick" from 1997 to 2000. In 2002, he starred in the short-lived sitcom "Just Shoot Me!" with his real-life friend and " MADtv " co-star Will Sasso.  Cushing has also appeared in films such as "The 40 Year Old Virgin", "Date Night", "The Day After Tomorrow", "Kiss Kiss Bang Bang", "The King's Speech" and "Thor: The Dark World". In 2013, he starred as Dr. Stephen Strange in Marvel's film adaptation of the comics superhero Spider-Man: 2013 and reprised his role in the sequel, Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017).   In 2018 he portrayed Captain Kirk's father George Kirk in the Star Trek sequel series Star Trek: Discovery.
