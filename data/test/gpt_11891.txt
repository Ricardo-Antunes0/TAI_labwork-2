Andrea Barzagli  (; born 8 May 1981) is an Italian footballer who plays as a defender for Napoli and the Italy national team.  Andrea Barzagli was born in the small town of Bari, in the Apulia region of southern Italy. He started his career at his local club, Atalanta, before moving to Milan in 2001. He spent six seasons with Milan, winning two Serie A titles and one Coppa Italia. In 2007, he moved to Juventus, where he won two more Serie A titles and two more Coppa Italia titles. In 2013, he moved to Napoli. With Napoli, he has won the Coppa Italia twice and the Italian Super Cup.  Barzagli has been capped 74 times for Italy, scoring four goals. He played at the 2006 World Cup and Euro 2008, and was a part of the 2010 World Cup-winning squad. He represented Italy at the 2012 Olympics.
