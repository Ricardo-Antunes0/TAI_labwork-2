North Buxton is a dispersed rural community in western Oxford County, Ontario, Canada. It is located in the township of North Buxton and the district of Parry Sound.  The community is situated on the north bank of the Credit River, about northeast of Barrie and northwest of Toronto. It has a population of approximately 750 people. The primary economic activity is agriculture. The community also has a post office, a church, a general store, and a restaurant. North Buxton is also home to the North Buxton Conservation Area, which protects an area of forest and wetlands.
