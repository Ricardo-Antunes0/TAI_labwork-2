Eldad Tarmu is a vibraphonist, composer, and improviser born in Haifa, Israel in 1957.  Tarmu's music is built on a foundation of traditional Israeli folk and classical music, with an improvisational edge. He has toured extensively throughout Europe, North America, and Asia and has recorded seven albums. His most recent album, "The Art of the Vibraphone", was released in 2014.   Eldad Tarmu is also a teacher and lecturer, having given master classes and lectures around the world. In 2005, he was appointed Professor of Vibraphone at the Royal Conservatory of The Hague in the Netherlands.
