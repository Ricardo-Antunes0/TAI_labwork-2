Arneberg Church  is a parish church in the city of Lund, Sweden. It is situated in the district of Lundvall.  The church was built in a Romanesque Revival style in 1881-82 by the architect Carl Fredrik Adlersparre. The church seats about 1000 people.   The church features a rose window and a carved pulpit. The organ was built by the organ builder Wilhelm Heinrich Schulz in 1896.
