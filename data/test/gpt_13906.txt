Souhaila Sami Andrawes Sayeh ( born 28 October 1988) is an Egyptian actress.  Born in Cairo, Andrawes started her acting career at the age of 12, appearing in a number of TV series and movies. She rose to prominence for her performance as Sulaymana in the Egyptian drama series Al-Bernameg (2011), for which she won the Best Actress Award at the 11th Annual Cairo International Film Festival.   She has since starred in a number of other successful dramas, including Laila Walied Ibrahim (2013), Manal (2014), and Yara Shahid (2015). In 2017, she starred in the Egyptian comedy film Khosara.   Andrawes has also appeared in several commercials and been a presenter on several television shows. In 2018, she starred in the romantic drama Khaled el-Wakil.
