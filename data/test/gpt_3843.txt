Elliot Knight is an English actor. He has appeared in films such as The Social Network, Black Swan, and Interstellar.  Born and raised in London, Knight began his acting career in 2004 with a small role in the film The Village. He went on to star in the films The Social Network, Black Swan, and Interstellar, for which he was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Actor. In 2018, he starred in the thriller Annihilation.
