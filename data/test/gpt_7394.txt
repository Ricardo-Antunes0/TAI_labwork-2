Elpida Hadzi-Vasileva (born 1971, Kavadarci, North Macedonia) is a Macedonian contemporary artist.  Born in 1971 in Kavadarci, North Macedonia, Elpida Hadzi-Vasileva is a contemporary artist who incorporates painting, sculpture and installation into her work. Influenced by both traditional Macedonian art and modern Western art, Hadzi-Vasileva's paintings depict natural landscapes and rural life with a mystical and dreamlike quality. Her sculptures are often made from recycled materials, and her installations often incorporate light and sound elements. Hadzi-Vasileva has exhibited her work in Macedonia, Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Belgium, Italy and Greece, and has received several awards, including the First Prize at the 2006 Skopje Biennial.
