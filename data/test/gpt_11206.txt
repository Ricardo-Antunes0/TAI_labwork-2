Beram Kayal (or Biram Keyal, , ; born June 26, 1978) is an Israeli professional football manager and former player.  Kayal played as a defender for Beitar Jerusalem, Maccabi Haifa, Hapoel Tel Aviv and Ironi Kiryat Shmona. He also played for the Israel national team, making 28 appearances between 1997 and 2006. After retiring from playing, Kayal became a manager, taking charge of Liga Leumit side Hapoel Petah Tikva in 2013.
