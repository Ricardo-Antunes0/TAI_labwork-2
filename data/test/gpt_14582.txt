Majaa Talkies (Kannada: ಮಜಾ ಟಾಕೀಸ್) is an upcoming Indian Kannada language film directed by Rajesh Ramachandra and produced by G. V. Prakash Kumar. The film stars Uppaluri Suman, Raghavendra Rao, Yagavathi and Srinath in the lead roles.  The film is based on a story of a woman who falls in love with a man who is married to someone else.
