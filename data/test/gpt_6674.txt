Leon was born in 1935 in Milan, Italy. After studying law at the University of Milan, he started his film career as an assistant director on the films of Valerio Zurlini. He made his debut as a director with the film "Il giorno della civetta" (1960). His films have been noted for their strong visual and narrative style, and for their exploration of human relationships. Leon died on 11 November 2016 at the age of 85.
