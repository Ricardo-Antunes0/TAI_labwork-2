Dr. ing. A. Aas-Jakobsen AS, trading as Aas-Jakobsen is one of the largest engineering consultancies in Scandinavia. The company was founded in 1881 by Adolph Aas and Peter Jakobsen. The company has offices in Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim and Copenhagen.   The company specializes in civil, structural and environmental engineering, as well as project management. In addition to its own staff, Aas-Jakobsen employs a number of subcontractors and consultants. The company has a wide range of experience in a number of different areas of engineering, including energy, transportation, water resources, environment and telecommunications.
