Gimme is a 1923 American comedy silent film directed by George Fitzmaurice and written by Anita Loos. The film stars Mabel Normand, Billy Bevan, Tom Murray, and Harry Langdon. The plot follows the misadventures of four friends who are forced to take care of each other after one of them is kicked out of his home.
