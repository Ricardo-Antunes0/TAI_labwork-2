Joseph Aldège Albert "Baz" Bastien (August 29, 1978 – March 5, 2007) was a Canadian professional ice hockey player. He was drafted in the second round, 53rd overall by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft.  Bastien played in the National Hockey League (NHL) with the Edmonton Oilers, Florida Panthers, Detroit Red Wings and Dallas Stars. He also played in the American Hockey League (AHL) with the Springfield Falcons and Worcester Sharks.  Bastien's death was announced on March 5, 2007 after he suffered a heart attack while playing in a game with the Dallas Stars. He was 34 years old.
