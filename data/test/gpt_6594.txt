He moved to New Netherland in 1674, where he became one of the earliest settlers in the colony of New York. Cuyler is best known for his paintings of Native Americans and for his prints and sculptures. He also created portraits and still lifes.  Cuyler was a member of the New York Society of Artists and the Royal Academy of Painting and Sculpture in Amsterdam. He died in Albany, New York.
