Still - William is the fifth book in the Harry Potter series. It was written by J.K. Rowling and published on July 16, 1997.  The story follows Harry and his friends Ron and Hermione as they travel to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to try and stop Lord Voldemort from gaining power. Along the way, they face many obstacles, including the evil Dolores Umbridge, the giant spiders Aragog and Acromantula, and the dark Forest of Dean.  The book was very well-received by both critics and readers, becoming one of the most popular entries in the series. It has been translated into over 30 languages and sold over 150 million copies worldwide.
