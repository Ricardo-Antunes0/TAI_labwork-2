Pchum Ben (,  , lit. "Ancestor's Village") is a village in the Mae Sot District of in northern Thailand.  Pchum Ben is located on the eastern slope of the Doi Saket mountain range, about southwest of the town of Mae Sot. The village has a population of about 1,000 people.  The traditional economy of Pchum Ben is agriculture, with produce including bananas, coconuts, palm oil, and rice. There is also a small handicrafts industry.  Pchum Ben has a school and a health clinic. The village has access to electricity and water.
