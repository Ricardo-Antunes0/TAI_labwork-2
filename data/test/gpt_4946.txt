Arun Rai (born 1963) is an Indian artist who is known for his paintings and drawings. He has exhibited his work internationally and has been awarded several prizes, including the prestigious Zee Art Award in 2006.  Rai was born in 1963 in the Indian state of Maharashtra. After completing his schooling from St. Xavier's College, Mumbai, Rai studied fine arts at the National School of Fine Arts, New Delhi, from where he received a BFA in 1985. He then moved to London to continue his studies at the Royal College of Art (RCA), where he received a MA in 1990. Rai has exhibited his work internationally, most notably in the United States, Canada, Japan and the United Kingdom. He has also been awarded several prizes, including the prestigious Zee Art Award in 2006. Rai currently lives and works in Mumbai.
