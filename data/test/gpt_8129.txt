Dynasty Warriors Next is a beat 'em up video game for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One platforms developed by Omega Force and published by Koei Tecmo. It is a successor to Dynasty Warriors 9 and is the eighteenth installment in the Dynasty Warriors series.  The game is set in the Three Kingdoms period of China and follows the story of an unnamed protagonist, who joined forces with other warriors to fight against the forces of the enemy kingdom. Players can command a team of up to three characters on-screen at once, and use a variety of weapons and abilities to defeat enemies.   Development of Dynasty Warriors Next began in late 2013, shortly after the release of Dynasty Warriors 9. The game was officially announced at the PlayStation Media Showcase during Sony's E3 2017 press conference, and was released in Japan on September 20, 2017, and in North America on October 6, 2017.
