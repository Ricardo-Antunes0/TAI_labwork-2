Édouard Chambon (Valence), born 19 August 1986, is a French footballer who plays for Valencia CF as a defensive midfielder.  Chambon began his career at hometown club Valence, making his senior debut in the 2004–05 season and becoming a regular starter over the next few years. In 2007, he signed with FC Lorient where he spent four seasons before moving to Premier League club Sunderland in 2012. He spent one season at the Stadium of Light before joining Valencia in 2015.  Chambon has made over 100 appearances for France, scoring one goal. He was part of the French squad that won the UEFA Euro 2016 tournament.
