Apple Jam is a bonus disc included with the iTunes Store version of the album "The Black Keys" by the American rock band the Black Keys. It was released on November 10, 2010, exclusively for the iTunes Store and was made available for purchase for a limited time. The bonus disc includes five exclusive tracks that were not included on the standard edition of the album.  The Black Keys are an American rock band formed in Akron, Ohio in 2001. The band consists of Dan Auerbach (lead vocals, guitar), Patrick Carney (guitar, backing vocals), and Ben Harper (drums). The group's first major-label release was the 2003 album "The Big Come Up". Their second album, "El Camino", was released in 2005 and produced by Danger Mouse. The Black Keys released their third album, "Brothers", in 2009.
