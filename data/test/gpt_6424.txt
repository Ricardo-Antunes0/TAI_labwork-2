Yvonne McCain (1948 - 2011) was the wife of the U.S. Senator from Arizona, John McCain. She was born in 1948 in Chicago, Illinois and raised in the town of Whiting, Indiana. After graduating from Indiana University with a degree in political science, she married John McCain in 1977. The McCains have four children: Meghan, James, Jack, and Kelly.  Yvonne McCain was known for her work on behalf of veterans and for her support of the arts. She served as chair of the Arizona Veterans Memorial Fund and as president of the John S. McCain Foundation for International Leadership. She also served on the boards of directors of the Mayo Clinic and The Children's Hospital of Phoenix. Yvonne McCain died on October 25, 2011 after a long battle with brain cancer.
