"Salsa Tequila" is a novelty song by American rapper Flo Rida. The song was released on June 2, 2014, as the lead single from his sixth studio album, My House. The song was produced by DJ Frank E and Flo Rida himself.  The song is about a man who is trying to impress a woman by buying her expensive gifts, but she only wants to spend time with him if he can dance well. He gets frustrated and decides to take salsa lessons in order to impress her.   "Salsa Tequila" debuted at number eighty-six on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and became Flo Rida's thirty-seventh top ten single on the chart. The song has been certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
