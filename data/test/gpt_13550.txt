Hoàng Thị Thùy or  Hoàng Thùy Hồng (born December 11, 1981) is a Vietnamese singer, actress and model.  Hoàng Thùy was born in Hanoi, Vietnam on December 11, 1981. She started her musical career in 2002 as a member of the girl group Nhạc Việt. The group released two albums and disbanded in 2003.   In 2004, Hoàng Thùy starred in the Vietnam Television Series "Kim Lục Vương" as Thu Lan. The series became a huge hit and won her several awards.   In 2007, she starred in the film "Sau Đó" alongside Dương Văn Cẩm and released her first solo album that year. The album reached number one on the Vietnamese charts and spawned several successful singles such as "Ngọc Lâm" and "Hai Đài". In 2009, she starred in the film "Thúy Yêu" and released her second solo album that year.   In 2010, she starred in the film "Chuyện Riêng Tôi" and released her third solo album that year.
