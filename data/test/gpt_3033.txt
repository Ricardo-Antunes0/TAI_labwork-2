Jennifer Montoya Betancourth (b. January 31, 1985) is an American actress. She is known for her roles as Dr. Gina Torres on the CBS medical drama series "Code Black", and as Sara in the Amazon Studios television series "The Man in the High Castle".  Montoya was born in Los Angeles, California, to Mexican parents. She first appeared on television at the age of four, in an episode of the NBC series "The Cosby Show". She later studied acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in New York City.  Montoya began her career appearing in minor roles on television shows before landing her breakout role as Dr. Gina Torres on the CBS medical drama series "Code Black". The show began airing in March 2015 and ran for six seasons until May 2019. In 2018, she starred in the Amazon Studios television series "The Man in the High Castle", based on the novel of the same name by Philip K. Dick. The show received positive reviews and has been renewed for a second season.
