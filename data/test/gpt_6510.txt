Odessa Rose Jorgensen, known mononymously as Odessa, is an American singer, songwriter and musician. Born in Seattle, Washington, Odessa rose to fame in 2010 after the release of her critically acclaimed debut studio album, O.   Odessa has since released two further studio albums, both of which reached the top 10 of the US Billboard chart. She has also achieved a number one single on the US Billboard Hot Dance/Electronic Songs chart with her 2015 single "Walking on Sunshine". Odessa has been nominated for five Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist and Best Pop Vocal Album.
