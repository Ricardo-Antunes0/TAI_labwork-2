Doug Carpenter (born July 1, 1942, in Cincinnati, Ohio) is an American composer, orchestrator, and conductor.   Carpenter holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Music from the University of Cincinnati, where he studied with Hugh Martin. He also holds a Master of Arts degree from the University of Michigan.  Carpenter has worked in film and television since the early 1960s. His credits include scores for films such as Invasion of the Body Snatchers (1978), The Dead Zone (1983), and WarGames (1983), as well as television series such as The A-Team, Star Trek: The Next Generation, and Dallas. He has also written music for Broadway productions such as Rocky (1976) and Donny Osmond: The Music Man (1977).  Since 1990, Carpenter has been the conductor laureate of the Santa Fe Symphony Orchestra.
