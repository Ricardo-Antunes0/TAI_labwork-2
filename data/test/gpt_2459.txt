"Feker Libi"  is a song recorded by American singer and songwriter Ariana Grande for her fourth studio album, Dangerous Woman (2018). It was written by Grande, Pharrell Williams, and Max Martin and produced by the latter two. "Feker Libi" is a mix of Arabic and Italian music with a dancehall beat.  Grande premiered the song on Beats 1 on August 3rd, 2018, two days prior to its release as the album's lead single. "Feker Libi" was well-received by critics, who praised its production and deemed it an improvement over Grande's previous work. The song topped the charts in several countries, including Australia, Canada, Denmark, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Portugal, Sweden and Switzerland.
