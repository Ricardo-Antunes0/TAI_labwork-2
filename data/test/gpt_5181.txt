Tysnes Church  is a parish church in the municipality of Tysnes, Norway. It is located in the village of Skogna. The white, wooden church was built in 1892 by the architect Jacob Aall. The church seats about 250 people.   The first record of a church at Tysnes dates back to 1070. The present church was built in 1892 by the architect Jacob Aall. The church was consecrated on 24 October 1893. The painter Johannes Vigeland decorated the interior of the church with his paintings. The altarpiece is from 1897 and it is made of wood and gold.
