Human's Dust is a live performance DVD by the rock band Radiohead. It was recorded at the Palais des Sports in Paris on 7 July 2007 and released on 17 November 2007.  The DVD consists of three sets, each lasting around two hours. The first set is a performance of Radiohead's standard repertoire, including songs from their albums "Kid A" (2000), "The Bends" (1995), "Amnesiac" (2001) and "Hail to the Thief" (2003). The second set is a selection of covers, including covers of The Velvet Underground's "Sweet Jane", Neil Young's "Heart of Gold" and The Beatles' "Let It Be". The third set consists of two new tracks, "Nude" and "Fitter Happier".  Human's Dust was met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has received an average score of 79, based on 10 reviews.   IGN awarded the album 9 out of 10, calling it an "energetic performance that captures the spirit of their live show." Pitchfork Media awarded the album 8.5 out of 10, writing that it is a "solid document of their current form."
