Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo, trading as Trans Asia, is a Sri Lankan owned hotel chain with properties in Colombo, Kandy and Mirissa.   Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo was founded in 2002 by Mr. Rohan Gunasekera and Mrs. Nirosha Gunasekera. It is a multi-brand hospitality company with properties in Colombo, Kandy and Mirissa. The company has over 70 employees and operates under the Trans Asia brand.   The company was founded with the aim of providing quality accommodation and services at reasonable prices to the travelling public. Cinnamon Lakeside Colombo properties are located in prime tourist destinations and are ideally suited for travellers looking for comfortable, affordable and convenient accommodation.
