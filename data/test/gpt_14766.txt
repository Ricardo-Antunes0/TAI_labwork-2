Wideawake is an American alternative rock band from Los Angeles, California. The band was formed in 2006 by vocalist and guitarist Wes Borland, bassist and backing vocalist Troy Sanders, and drummer Josh Freese.  Wideawake has released two studio albums, "Wideawake" (2007) and "The Clearing" (2010), both of which reached the top 40 on the "Billboard" 200 chart. They have also released two extended plays, "The Distance" (2007) and "Burn the Witch" (2008). Wideawake has toured throughout the United States and internationally, supporting acts such as Muse, Muse, The Black Keys, The Killers, and Halestorm.
