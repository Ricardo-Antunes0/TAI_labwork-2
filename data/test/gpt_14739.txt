Solar road studs are flashing solar cell road markings that can be used to indicate solar-powered pathways in landscapes. They were first developed by the United States Department of Transportation in 2007 as a way to increase the efficiency of solar energy use on roads. Solar road studs are made from a durable, weatherproof material and are designed to last for many years.
