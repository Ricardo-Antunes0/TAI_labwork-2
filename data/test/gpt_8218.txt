Idiophantis soreuta is a moth of the family Gelechiidae. It is found in the Russian Far East (Amur, Primorskiy Kray, Khabarovsk Krai, and Sikhote-Alin), Korea, Japan, Taiwan and China. The forewings are light ochreous-brown with a small dark mark on the costa at one-fourth, a triangular dark mark on the costa at three-fourths and a small mark at two-thirds. There are two very small dark marks on the dorsum before the tornus. The hindwings are pale greyish-white.
