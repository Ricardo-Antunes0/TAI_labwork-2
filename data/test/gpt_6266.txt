Quidditch Benelux is the informal organization of quidditch teams in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. The organization was founded in 2015, and currently has four teams.  Quidditch Benelux was founded in 2015 by members of the quidditch communities in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. At its inception, the organization had only two teams: the Belgians and the Dutch. In 2016, the organization added a Luxembourg team.   The goal of Quidditch Benelux is to promote quidditch in its respective countries and to create a competitive league that allows teams from all three countries to compete against each other. Quidditch Benelux also aims to provide support and resources to its teams, and to raise awareness of quidditch among the general public.
