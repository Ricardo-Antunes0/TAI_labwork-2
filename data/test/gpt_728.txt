Gregory Ralph Kingsley (born July 28, 1980) is a Canadian actor and comedian. He has appeared in films such as The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy and Flight of the Conchords.  Kingsley was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, the son of actress Joanne Kingsley and author and former politician Peter Kingsley. Kingsley has English, Scottish, Irish, and Portuguese ancestry. He attended Thornhill College in Ontario, Canada.  Kingsley began his career as a stand-up comedian in Toronto. He has since appeared in films such as The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy and Flight of the Conchords, as well as television shows such as Trailer Park Boys, Veep, and Silicon Valley. In 2015 he starred in the comedy filmdumpster fire alongside Zach Galifianakis and Kristen Wiig.
