Poole Stadium is a speedway and former greyhound racing stadium in Poole, Dorset, England. It is the home of the Poole Pirates speedway team and was first used as a greyhound racing track in 1928.  Poole Stadium hosted speedway events from 1928 until 1987 when it was closed due to safety concerns. The stadium reopened in 2004 and has since played host to various other events including concerts, motorcycle races, car shows and family entertainment. In 2018, it was announced that Poole Stadium would be the home of the 2019 British Speedway Grand Prix.
