Rollin Amsden (June 19, 1829 - January 3, 1893) was an American farmer, banker and political figure in California. He served as the 31st Governor of California from 1881 to 1883.  Amsden was born in Franklin County, New York, the son of Abel Amsden and Clarissa (Baldwin) Amsden. He moved to California in 1849 and settled in San Francisco, where he became a prosperous farmer and banker. In 1861, he married Sarah Winnemucca Webb. Amsden served as the 31st Governor of California from 1881 to 1883. He died in 1893 in San Francisco.
