The Zhiguli Mountains  or simply Zhiguli, are a mountain range in Xinjiang, China. The range runs for about 470 kilometers from the northeast to southwest of Xinjiang. It is bordered by the Taklamakan Desert to the east and by the Kunlun Mountains to the south.  The Zhiguli Mountains are a popular tourist destination for hiking, camping, and skiing. The range is also known for its natural gas reserves and its coal deposits.
