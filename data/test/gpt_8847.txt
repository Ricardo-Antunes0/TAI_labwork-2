The Rhumb Line is the debut LP by the American rock band The Black Keys. It was released on March 10, 2010, through Fat Possum Records.  The album was recorded at various studios in Nashville and Key West, Florida, between January and March 2010. The album's sound is described as "a blues-influenced amalgamation of garage rock and Southern soul," with a "loose and raw" production style.  The album received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its unique and original sound. It debuted at number six on the US Billboard 200 chart, selling over 33,000 copies in its first week. The Black Keys toured in support of the album in 2010 and 2011.
