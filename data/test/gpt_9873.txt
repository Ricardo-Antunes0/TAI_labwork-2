Coccorchestes is a genus of South Pacific seabird in the family Alcidae.  The genus Coccorchestes contains the following six species:   1. Coccorchestes melanoleucus - The white-faced storm petrel  2. Coccorchestes dux - The black-faced storm petrel  3. Coccorchestes solitarius - The wandering albatross  4. Coccorchestes harrisii - The buller's storm petrel  5. Coccorchestes antarcticus - The Antarctic storm petrel  6. Coccorchestes australis - The Australian storm petrel
