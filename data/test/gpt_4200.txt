The Koenigsegg TFG is an Inline-3 engine. It is a twin-turbocharged and intercooled V8 engine that produces a total of 940 horsepower (680 kW) and 1,106 Nm (848 lb-ft) of torque. It has an eight-speed automatic transmission. The TFG was first shown as a concept at the 2007 Geneva Motor Show. The production version was unveiled at the 2009 Geneva Motor Show.
