Akakichi no Eleven is a manga series about Takao Akakichi, a second-year high school student who has an unusual ability: he can see spirits. One day, he meets a girl named Aoi who is also able to see spirits, and the two of them become friends. Takao soon finds out that Aoi is possessed by a spirit known as "The White Witch," and she needs his help to stop her from harming people.
