"Mi Chico Latino" (English: "My Latin Boy"), is a Mexican telenovela produced by Televisa for Telesistema Mexicano in 1956.  The telenovela, starring Adalberto Santiago and Silvia Pinal, tells the story of a young man who falls in love with a woman from a wealthy family.   Despite the obstacles that come their way, the couple is able to overcome all adversities and are ultimately able to find happiness together.
