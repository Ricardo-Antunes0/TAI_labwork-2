"Woo Baby" is a song by American soul singer Alicia Keys from her fourth studio album, "The Element of Freedom" (2009). It was written by Keys, Jermaine Dupri, and Pharrell Williams, and produced by Dupri. The song was released as the album's lead single on September 25, 2009.  "Woo Baby" is a soulful ballad about a love interest who makes Keys feel free. The lyrics are about a man who makes her feel like "everything is cool" and provides emotional support when she needs it. The song has been compared to Keys' earlier work with the Neptunes, and has been described as an "emotional roller coaster".  The song reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming Keys' fifth number-one single on the chart. It also reached number one in Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, the Netherlands, Belgium, Sweden and Switzerland. "Woo Baby" has sold over six million copies worldwide.  An accompanying music video for "Woo Baby" was directed by Dave Meyers and features Keys performing the song in various settings.
