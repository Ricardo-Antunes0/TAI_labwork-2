Antónia Rodrigues (Aveiro, Portugal, 1572-1641), was a Portuguese writer and gilder of the Baroque period.  Antónia Rodrigues was born in Aveiro, Portugal in 1572. She married a nobleman, and had two children. In 1616, she moved to Lisbon, where she began writing. Her works mainly focused on gilding and painting. She died in 1641.
