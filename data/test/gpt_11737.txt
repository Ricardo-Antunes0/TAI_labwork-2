Dream is a 2012 Telugu-language psychological thriller film written and directed by Srihari and produced by S. S. Rajamouli under the banner of Sri Srimurala Films. The film stars Venkatesh, Shruti Haasan, and Rao Ramesh in the lead roles.  The film's story revolves around an unmarried woman, Nandini (Shruti Haasan), who has a powerful dream that haunts her every night. She begins to suspect that the dream may be related to her past, which she cannot remember. Nandini meets an old friend of her father (Rao Ramesh), who tells her that she may be able to find answers to her questions if she travels to her ancestral home in Andhra Pradesh. Nandini travels to Andhra Pradesh and begins to piece together her past. She learns that she was abandoned by her parents as a baby and was raised by a foster family. She also discovers that the reason for her dream is connected to her past.
