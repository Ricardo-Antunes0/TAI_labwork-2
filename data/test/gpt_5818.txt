Mikhael is a 2019 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film directed by P. A. Backer and written by Ranjith Sankar. Produced by Ranjith Sankar, the film stars Mohanlal, Manju Warrier, and Jagathy Sreekumar in the lead roles.  The film tells the story of an ordinary man who gets caught up in a web of deceit and murder.
