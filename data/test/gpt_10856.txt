Notes from Below is a UK-based digital magazine that publishes investigative journalism and short fiction.  Notes from Below was founded in 2013 by journalist and fiction writer James Smyth. The magazine's editorial focus is on investigative journalism, with a particular focus on stories that challenge the status quo. Smyth has said that Notes from Below "aims to be as rigorous as possible in its pursuit of the truth, while also being entertaining and engaging."  Notes from Below has published work by authors including Hanif Kureishi, Lenny Abrahamson, Hilary Mantel, and Sarah Waters.
