The 19th Filipino Academy of Movie Arts and Sciences Awards Night was held on March 15, 1971 at the Manila Hotel.   This year's event was marked by a record number of participants with 118 entries from studios all over the country. The major categories were Best Picture, Best Actor, Best Actress and Best Director.   The FAMAS Awards are considered as the most prestigious film awards in the Philippines. They are also considered as the most prestigious awards in the Filipino-American community.
