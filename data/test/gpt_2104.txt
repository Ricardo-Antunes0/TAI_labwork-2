Waheeda Rehman (born 3 February 1938) is an Indian actress who has appeared in over 150 films in a career spanning more than four decades. She received the National Film Award for Best Actress for her performance in the 1983 film Neelum Neelum.  Rehman was born in 1938 in Hyderabad, Sindh, British India to a Muslim family. She studied at Islamia College, Hyderabad and then pursued an acting career in Mumbai. She made her film debut opposite Rajesh Khanna in the 1967 film "Aan". She has since appeared in several critically acclaimed films, including "Kya Kehna" (1970), "Zanjeer" (1982), "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" (1995), and "Neelum Neelum" (1983). Her performance in the latter earned her the National Film Award for Best Actress.  Rehman married actor Hareesh Saeed in 1972; the couple divorced in 1978. She has since remarried to businessman and industrialist Zahid Hussain.
