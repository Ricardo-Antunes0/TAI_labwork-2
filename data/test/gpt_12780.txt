River City Ransom, later released as Street Fighter II: The World Warrior, is a beat 'em up game released for the arcades in 1987. It was developed by Capcom and published by Capcom. The game was ported to various home systems over the years.  In River City Ransom, the player controls a character who has been imprisoned in a mysterious tower. The objective of the game is to escape the tower and rescue the princess. Along the way, the player encounters various enemies, collects items, and solves puzzles.  River City Ransom became popular due to its graphics, which were at the time considered advanced for an arcade game. The game has been praised for its tight controls and its challenging levels. It has also been credited with popularizing the beat 'em up genre.
