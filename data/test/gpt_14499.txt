Olivia "Liv" Boeree (born 18 July 1984) is a Dutch former professional tennis player who played on the WTA tour from 2000 to 2013.  Born in Amsterdam, Boeree has played on the professional tour since 2000. She reached her highest singles ranking of World No. 7 in August 2009 and her best doubles ranking of World No. 1 in July 2009.  She has won seven singles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit and one title on the WTA Tour, both in 2006. She also won six doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit and two titles on the WTA Tour, both with Alisa Kleybanova.  Boeree retired from professional tennis in 2013. She currently works as a commentator for Eurosport and is also a coach for the Dutch national women's team.
