Vida Parayum Munpe is a 1981 Malayalam film directed by Sibi Malayil. The film stars Mohanlal, Jayabharathi, Kaviyoor Ponnamma and Sukumari in lead roles.  The film centres around the life of a poor family, led by patriarch Ayyappan (Mohanlal), who are struggling to make ends meet. Ayyappan's eldest son Kunjappan (Jayabharathi) is a hard-working and determined young man, while his daughter Vidyappan (Kaviyoor Ponnamma) is a carefree young woman who loves spending time with her friends. When Kunjappan falls in love with Aparna (Sukumari), Vidyappan's friend, she disapproves of the relationship and tries to dissuade her brother from marrying Aparna. However, Kunjappan is adamant about getting married and eventually wins Vidyappan over. The couple marry and start a family of their own.
