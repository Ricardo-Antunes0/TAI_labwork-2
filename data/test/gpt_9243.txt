Simply Eva is the eighth posthumous album by American singer and songwriter Eva Cassidy. It was released on November 10, 1995, by Columbia Records. The album was produced by John Lee Hooker and features backing vocals from Sheryl Crow and Lenny Kravitz.  Cassidy had completed recording of the album before her death in May 1995 at the age of just 36. The album was released posthumously to generally positive reviews, with some reviewers commenting that it showcased Cassidy's "astonishing" vocal range. It peaked at number 13 on the "Billboard" 200 chart and has since been certified platinum.
