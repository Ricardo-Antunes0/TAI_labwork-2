Gerard Timothy McLauchlan (born 8 March 1989) is a Scottish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Scottish Premiership club Kilmarnock.  McLauchlan began his career at Celtic, where he developed into a first-team regular and won the Scottish League Cup in 2012. He transferred to Sunderland in 2013, where he made over 100 appearances and won the FA Cup in 2015. He moved to Kilmarnock in January 2017.
