Fleet Week is a United States Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard celebration that occurs each year in late May or early June. It is a time to commemorate naval history, to showcase the maritime capabilities of the United States Armed Forces, and to thank the public for their support.  Fleet week celebrates the US Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard with parades, ceremonies, and exhibitions. It began in World War I as an effort to build morale and thank the public for their support. Fleet week is now a nationwide event that takes place in late May or early June.
