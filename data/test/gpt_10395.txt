Genaro (from the Latin Januarius, meaning "devoted to Janus", the god of gates) is a Spanish and Italian given name, often used in Portugal. The name is derived from the Roman cognomen Gennaro, meaning "characteristics of Gennaro", which was derived from the Latin name Gennaeus.  People with the name Genaro include:   Genaro (footballer) (born 1978), Argentine footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for RCD Espanyol
