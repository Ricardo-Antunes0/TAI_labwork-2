"Thanksgiving Orphans" is the ninth episode of the first season of the American television series Breaking Bad.  The episode was written by Vince Gilligan and directed by Michelle MacLaren. It aired on AMC in the United States on November 26, 2008.  In this episode, Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul) and Walter White (Bryan Cranston) team up to cook and deliver a Thanksgiving meal to an orphanage. They face unexpected challenges along the way.
