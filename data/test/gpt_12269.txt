Mohnyin Thado was born in 1379 to King Narapati II and Queen Ummagami. After the death of his father in 1428, Mohnyin Thado ascended to the throne of the kingdom of Ava. Mohnyin Thado's reign was marked by a series of military campaigns against neighbouring kingdoms, most notably against the kingdom of Toungoo. Mohnyin Thado died in 1439 and was succeeded by his son, Narapati III.
