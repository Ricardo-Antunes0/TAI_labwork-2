Nove is an Italian general entertainment cable and satellite television channel owned by Mediaset. The channel broadcasts a variety of programs including soap operas, reality shows, talk shows, and documentaries. Nove was launched on December 1, 2003.  Nove was originally known as Italia 1, and was one of the first general entertainment channels in Italy. The channel was sold to Mediaset in 2009. Nove currently has a viewership of approximately 2.5 million subscribers.
