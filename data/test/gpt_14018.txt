Urmas is an Estonian masculine given name. It is derived from an Old East Slavic word "urma", meaning "bear".[1]  The name Urmas is most popular in Estonia, where it ranks as the eighth most common name for males. It is also relatively common in Latvia and Lithuania, where it ranks as the fourth and fifth most common names for males, respectively. Outside of these three Baltic countries, the name Urmas is not particularly well known.
