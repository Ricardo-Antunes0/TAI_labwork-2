Phạm Văn Quyến  (born April 29, 1978) is a Vietnamese actor and singer. He is perhaps best known for his roles in the television series Love Letters and as the lead singer of the Vietnamese pop group F4.  Quyến was born in Đà Nẵng, Vietnam on April 29, 1978. After completing his primary and secondary education in Đà Nẵng, Quyến studied at the National Academy of Performing Arts in Hanoi, Vietnam. He made his acting debut in the television series Love Letters in 2001. He has since starred in a number of successful television series and films, including A Gentle Monster (2006), The Phantom Lover (2007), The Uninvited (2009), and The Legend of Tarzan (2013).  In 2006, Quyến joined the Vietnamese pop group F4. The group achieved significant success in Vietnam and abroad, releasing six albums and receiving numerous awards. In 2013, Quyến released his solo album entitled "Không Lên Tiền". He has since starred in several successful films, including The Bestseller (2015), The Adventurer (2016), and Uncle Boonmee Who Can Recall His Past Lives (2018).  Quyến is married to actress Huynh Thi Ngoc Loan. They have one child.
