"Duppy Freestyle" is a song by Canadian rapper Drake, released as the fourth single from his fifth album, "Views" (2016). The song was written by Drake, Noah "40" Shebib, and Jimmy Harry.  The song is a freestyle rap over the instrumental of "One Dance" by DJ Khaled. Lyrically, the song features Drake rapping over a beat that sounds like it was influenced by 1970s funk music. The song has been compared to Drake's earlier work such as "Forever" and "Hotline Bling".  "Duppy Freestyle" was leaked on July 28, 2016, ahead of its release on August 5. The song was later released as the fourth single from "Views".  The song debuted at number 55 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart, making it Drake's 43rd top-ten hit on that chart. It also debuted at number one on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. It also became his first number-one hit in Australia, where it debuted at number two on the ARIA Charts.
