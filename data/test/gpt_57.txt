Dunlap is an unincorporated community in Fresno County, California. Dunlap is located on the east side of the San Joaquin River about 2 miles north of Kingsburg.  Dunlap was founded in 1884 by Charles Dunlap and named for him. A post office was established at Dunlap in 1886, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1957.
