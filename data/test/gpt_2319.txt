Geir Eriksen (born 15 May 1965), former Norwegian footballer who played as a midfielder. He is best known for his time at Manchester United, where he made over 300 appearances in all competitions.  Geir Selvik Malthe-Sørenssen was born in Oslo, Norway on 15 May 1965. He started his footballing career at local side Lyn and then joined Manchester United in 1987. He made his debut for the club in a League Cup match against Coventry City and went on to make over 300 appearances in all competitions for the club. Geir Selvik Malthe-Sørenssen retired from football in 2001 and later became a football coach at Manchester United's academy. In 2006, he was appointed head coach of Norwegian side Molde FK, but he was sacked after only six months in charge.
