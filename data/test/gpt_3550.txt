The 1900–01 FA Cup was the 30th season of the FA Cup. The competition was won by Sheffield United, who defeated Arsenal 2–1 in the final.  The 1900–01 FA Cup was the 30th season of the world's oldest association football knockout competition. The cup was first played in 1871–72 and was open to both professional and amateur clubs. The final was the first time that an English club had won the cup, and they did so against Arsenal, who had finished as runners-up in both the 1899–1900 and 1898–99 seasons.
