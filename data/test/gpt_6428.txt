The Hale Koa Hotel, which means House of the White Pine in Hawaiian, is located at 3300 Ala Moana Boulevard in Honolulu, Hawaii. The hotel was opened in 1927 and was designed by Wallace K. Harrison.  The hotel has 738 rooms and suites, a 18-hole championship golf course, a spa, and a pool. The hotel also features a restaurant, bar, and lounge. The Hale Koa Hotel is a AAA five-diamond hotel.
