Lovejoy Matare (born 13 September 1991), popularly known by his stage name L kat, is a Ghanaian recording artist.  L kat started his music career in 2009 with the release of his first single "Kokua" (meaning "Help"). The song was well received and helped him gain popularity in the local music scene. He followed up with several successful singles including "Kofi", "Dua", "Boom Boom" and "Wetin". In 2013, he released his debut album, titled "L. Kat". The album was well received and featured the hit singles "Kokua", "Dua", and "Boom Boom".  L kat is signed to Mango Music and has toured extensively throughout Ghana, Nigeria, Togo, Benin, Burkina Faso, Mali, Senegal, Gambia and The Ivory Coast. He is also scheduled to tour the United States later in 2019.
