The Littlemore Priory scandals took place between 1332 and 1336, when the priory was in the hands of the de Montfort family. The priory was accused of financial improprieties, including embezzlement and fraud. Many of the monks were arrested and tried, but all were eventually acquitted. The scandal ruined the reputation of the de Montfort family and led to their downfall.
