The Dionysus mosaic found in Samatya, Istanbul, is one of the most important and well-preserved examples of Hellenistic mosaic art. It was created in the 2nd century AD, and depicts Dionysus in a chariot drawn by panthers, with Thalia and Maia on his left and right sides, respectively.
