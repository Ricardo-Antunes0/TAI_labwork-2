Kenneth Dagnall (30 January 1921 – March 2014) was an English professional footballer who played as an inside forward in the Football League for Leicester City and Watford.  Dagnall began his career with Leicester City, making his debut in the Second Division on 15 October 1941 in a 1–1 draw with Northampton Town. He went on to make 142 league appearances for the "Foxes", scoring 38 goals, before joining Watford in May 1950 for a fee of £4,000. He made just nine league appearances for the Hornets, before retiring in December 1951 due to injury.  He later worked as a football coach at various clubs, including Peterborough United and Rotherham United. Dagnall died in March 2014 at the age of 95.
