Mayilsamy (born October 2, 1965) is an Indian film actor and playback singer who predominantly works in Tamil cinema.  Mayilsamy was born in October 2, 1965 in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. He made his acting debut with a supporting role in the 2001 Tamil film "Kaala". In 2002, he played the lead role in the film "Kadhal Kottai". He has since starred in a number of successful films, including "Kadhal Kottai 2", "Puli", and "Thirumaal Thirupu". He has also sung for several films, including the soundtrack for "Kaala" and "Kadhal Kottai 2".
