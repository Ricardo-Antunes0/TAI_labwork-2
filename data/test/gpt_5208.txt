STS-134 (ISS assembly flight ULF6) was the 134th flight of Space Shuttle Discovery, the 30th shuttle mission to assemble the International Space Station (ISS) and the fifth flight of Discovery to the ISS. The mission began on July 8, 2009, and ended on July 20, 2009.  The STS-134 crew consisted of Commander Mark Kelly and Pilot Scott Kelly, both of NASA; and Russian Cosmonauts Aleksandr Skvortsov, Oleg Kononenko, and Mikhail Tyurin.
