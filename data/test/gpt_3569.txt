Longterm Mentality is the debut studio album by American rapper 21 Savage, released on August 24, 2018, by Savage Squad Records and Interscope Records. The album was produced primarily by Metro Boomin, with additional production from DJ Mustard, Southside, Harry Fraud and Pharrell Williams.  The album has been met with generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised the album's production and 21 Savage's lyricism. It debuted at number two on the US "Billboard" 200 chart with first-week sales of 97,000 copies.  21 Savage was born on October 7, 1995 in Atlanta, Georgia. He began rapping at the age of 10 and started producing music at the age of 13. He released his first mixtape in 2013 and his first extended play (EP) in 2014. In 2016, he released his second EP, "Issa Tourist". On June 20, 2018, 21 Savage announced that his debut studio album would be titled "Longterm Mentality", and it was scheduled to be released on August 24.  The album has been met with generally positive reviews from music critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album has an average score of 74 based on 11 reviews.
