Hood Billionaire is the seventh studio album by American rapper and actor Tyga. It was announced on March 10, 2019, by Tyga and his label Cash Money Records. The album was produced by the label's head, Birdman, alongside the likes of Metro Boomin, Southside and Harry Fraud.
