Tyreese Williams is a fictional character from the American post-apocalyptic horror television series "The Walking Dead", based on the comic book series of the same name by Robert Kirkman. He is portrayed by Chandler Riggs.  Tyreese was originally a prisoner at the series' beginning, but later becomes one of the show's most well-known and popular characters. He is a Hilltop member and a survivor of the outbreak, and his character arc throughout the series focuses on his relationships with other characters, particularly Rick Grimes and Michonne.  Tyreese has received critical acclaim for his portrayal, with some reviewers praising him for his development as a character and noting that he provides a strong emotional link between the show's early episodes and later ones. His death was highly anticipated by viewers, and was ranked as one of the show's most memorable scenes.
