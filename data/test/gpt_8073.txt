Martin Retov (; born 5 May 1980) is a Slovenian journalist, photographer and film director.  Retov was born in Ljubljana, Slovenia. He studied photography at the Academy of Fine Arts in Ljubljana and film directing at the Film Institute of the Czech Republic. He has worked as a photojournalist for the daily newspaper Dnevnik and for magazines such as Luft, Forum and Revija. He directed his first feature film, "The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou" (2004), which was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Picture. Retov's second feature film, "A Serious Man" (2009), was also nominated for an Academy Award for Best Picture.
