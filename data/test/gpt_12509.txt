"Shimmy" is a song by Swedish singer Robyn, taken from her fourth studio album, Body Talk (2005). It was written by Robyn and Stefan Glaumann, and produced by the latter. The song was released as the album's lead single in Sweden on 5 September 2005 and in the United Kingdom on 11 September 2005.  The song is a dance-pop number with a eurodance beat. It is lyrically about wanting to get down and party, and its accompanying music video, directed by Dave Meyers, features Robyn dancing around a warehouse. "Shimmy" reached the top ten in Finland, Germany, Sweden and the United Kingdom, and charted within the top twenty in Austria, Denmark, Ireland, Italy, Norway and Switzerland.
