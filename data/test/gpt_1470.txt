The Lamar Cardinals baseball team represents Lamar University in NCAA Division I college baseball. The Cardinals play their home games at Provost Umphrey Stadium.  The Lamar Cardinals have been a member of the Southland Conference since their inception in 1984. Lamar has made four trips to the NCAA Division I College World Series, advancing as far as the championship game in 1993. The Cardinals have also won three Southland Conference titles and one NCAA Regional Championship.  Lamar University first fielded a varsity baseball team in 1911.
