Qurram Hussain is a Pakistani Canadian musician, composer and producer. He was born in Karachi, Pakistan in 1971 and raised in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada. He is best known as the founder, lead vocalist and primary songwriter for the progressive metal band Rishloo.  Hussain has released six studio albums with Rishloo, as well as one live album and one compilation album. His work with the band has been praised by music critics, who have called him a "master of progressive metal". He has also released two solo albums, one of which was certified gold by the Canadian Recording Industry Association. In addition to his musical career, Hussain has also worked as a record producer and music video director.
