The Mostafa Kamel Museum is a museum in Cairo, Egypt, dedicated to the life and work of Mostafa Kamel, an influential Egyptian architect and sculptor. The museum is located in the Garden City district of Cairo.  The Mostafa Kamel Museum was inaugurated on 5 November 1988. The museum has an area of 10,000 square meters. The collection of the museum contains more than 10,000 pieces, including sculptures, paintings, and manuscripts. The museum offers exhibitions of painting, sculpture, and architecture.
