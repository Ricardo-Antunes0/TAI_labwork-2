Johanna Maria (Joke) de Kruijf (born 22 June 1961) is a Dutch comedian, actress, singer and writer.  De Kruijf was born in Amsterdam. She is the daughter of the late Dutch comedian Kees de Kruijf and actress Jacqueline de Kruijf. She has been married to comedian and actor Bram van der Vlies since 1997. They have two daughters.  She began her career as a stand-up comedian in 1987 and has since appeared in numerous films and television shows. Her breakthrough role came in the sitcom Nummer 28, which ran from 1995 to 1998. De Kruijf has also released several albums, including the comedy albums Nummer 22 (1997) and Joke de Kruijf 2 (2002).
