The Spring Standards is an independent three-piece rock band from Orlando, Florida. Formed in 2009, the band has since released two full-length albums and an EP, as well as touring nationally and internationally.     The band is influenced by a variety of bands and styles, including punk, alternative rock, and post-hardcore.     Their debut album, "The Spring Standards", was released in April 2013 and has received positive reviews from music critics. The band has also toured nationally and internationally to support the album.
