Two Point Hospital is a 2018 business simulation video game developed by British studio Bluepoint Games, published by Sony Interactive Entertainment and developed in collaboration with several other studios.  Two Point Hospital is a business simulation video game set in a fictional hospital. The game is played from a first-person perspective and the player controls the actions of medical staff and patients, as well as managing finances and resources. The game was designed to be more challenging than other business simulation games, and to provide a more immersive experience.  The game was announced at the PlayStation Experience event in December 2017, and was released worldwide on 17 May 2018 for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. A Microsoft Windows version was released on 12 August 2018. A mobile version of the game was released on 25 September 2018 for iOS and Android devices.  "Two Point Hospital" received "mixed or average reviews" on all platforms according to review aggregation website Metacritic.
