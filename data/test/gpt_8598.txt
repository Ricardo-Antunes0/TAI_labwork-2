Discopath  is a 2013 Canadian slasher film written, produced and directed by David Bruckner. It stars Mira Sorvino, John Leguizamo, and Tony Shalhoub.  The film is set in a small Canadian town, where a serial killer is stalking and killing local residents. The police are unable to solve the case, until one of their own turns out to be the killer.
