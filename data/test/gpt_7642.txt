Jeff Olson (born July 14, 1962) is an American musician and composer.  Olson was born in Riverside, California. He studied music at the University of California, Davis, and at the University of Pennsylvania. He has worked as a session musician, playing with artists such as Sheryl Crow, John Mayer, Norah Jones, and Dave Matthews Band. Olson has released three albums: "The Art of Possibility" (2001), "The Blue Room" (2004), and "Shadows on the Sun" (2009).
