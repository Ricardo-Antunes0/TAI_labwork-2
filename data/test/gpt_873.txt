Lia Gal Barkai (; born 5 April 1983) is an Israeli actress and model.  Barkai was born in Tel Aviv to a Jewish family. She started her career as a model, appearing in campaigns for L'Oreal, KFC, Coca-Cola and others. In 2006, she made her acting debut in the Israeli drama series "Hatufim" and since then has appeared in several other television series and films. In 2013, she won the Israeli Best Actress award for her role in the film "The Boy".
