The Daffodil Festival is a regional festival which takes place in the town of Llandaff, Cardiff, England. The festival celebrates the arrival of spring with a range of events and activities, including a flower show and market.   The Daffodil Festival is held every year in late April or early May. It is a popular event with tourists and locals, and attracts over 1,000 people each year. The main events of the festival are the flower show and market, which are both open to the public. There are also a number of smaller events which take place throughout the day, such as music performances and craft stalls.
