Juan Claudio González Calderón (born 6 October 1975) is a Mexican politician from the Institutional Revolutionary Party. He has served as Deputy of the LXI Legislature of the Mexican Congress representing the State of Mexico.  González Calderón was born in Cuernavaca, Morelos, on October 6, 1975. He completed his secondary education at the Instituto Tecnológico de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey and his undergraduate studies at the Autonomous University of Ciudad Juárez. He also participated in a program to study at the London School of Economics and Political Science.
