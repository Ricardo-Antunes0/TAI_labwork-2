Shanthakumaran Nair Sreesanth (, born 6 February 1981) is an Indian cricketer who has played for the Kerala cricket team in domestic cricket and for the Indian national team. He is a left-handed batsman and a right-arm off-break bowler.  Sreesanth made his debut for Kerala in 2001, and played for the side until 2007. He then represented Tamil Nadu from 2008 to 2010, before returning to Kerala in 2011. In March 2013, he was arrested for match-fixing in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He was suspended from all cricketing activities by Cricket Australia and the International Cricket Council (ICC) pending trial, but his suspension was lifted in January 2014. In February 2015, he was found guilty of fixing matches and sentenced to six years' imprisonment. He was released from prison on parole in September 2018.   In December 2018, Sreesanth was arrested again for betting offences.
