Gotarzes II ( G≈çtarz) was king of the Visigoths from 589 to 612. He succeeded his father, Theudimer, in 589.  Gotarzes II fought a bloody and unsuccessful war against the Frankish kingdom of Clovis I in 602-603. In 612, he was defeated and killed by the Visigothic nobles at the Battle of Toulouse.
