Hare Forest Farm is a historic home and farm located near Rugby, Warwickshire, England. The house is a Grade II* listed building.  Hare Forest Farm was built circa 1620 by Sir Richard Newcourt and passed through the Newcourts, Earls of Warwick and Dukes of Beaufort before being sold in 1949 to the present owner.  The farmhouse is a three storey brick structure with a tiled roof and has been extended several times over the years. The property includes a Palladian style mansion house, a dovecote, several barns, two cottages and an old water mill.  Hare Forest Farm is open to the public for tours on certain days during the summer season.
