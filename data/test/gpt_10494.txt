The Australian 34th Brigade was an Australian Army brigade formation that served during World War II. The brigade was raised in Sydney in March 1942, and was initially assigned to the 2nd Australian Infantry Division. The 34th Brigade fought in the Battle of Buna–Gona, the Battle of Sanananda and the Battle of Borneo. After the war, the brigade was disbanded in 1946.
