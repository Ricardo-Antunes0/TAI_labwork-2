Child's Play (also known colloquially as  "Chucky") is a horror-comedy franchise centered on a doll possessed by the spirit of a serial killer, Chucky. The first film was released in 1988 and the franchise has since consisted of six sequels, two remakes, and four television films.  The franchise has been met with mixed critical reception, but has been commercially successful, spawning merchandise such as action figures, video games, and comic books. Chucky has been cited as an influential figure in the development of the slasher film genre.
