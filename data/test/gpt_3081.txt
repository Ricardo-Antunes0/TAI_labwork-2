Theo Thomson is a Malawian born singer, songwriter, and record producer. He has been based in London, England, since the early 1990s.  Thomson is best known as the frontman and primary songwriter for the band The Paper Kites, which he formed in 2003. The Paper Kites have released three studio albums and one EP.  Thomson has also released two solo albums, both of which were released in 2014. His debut solo album, "The North", was nominated for the Mercury Prize. His second album, "Theo Thomson", was nominated for the British Independent Music Awards Album of the Year.
