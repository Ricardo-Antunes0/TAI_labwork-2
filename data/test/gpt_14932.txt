Lady Clover Honey is an American drag queen, singer, and actor.  Lady Clover Honey was born in Los Angeles, California on February 5, 1988. Honey began her drag career in 2007 as a member of the drag duo Lady Clover and the Scissor Sisters. In 2009, Honey starred in the short film Trash N' Gold alongside fellow drag queen RuPaul. That same year, she released her debut album, Lady Clover Honey. In 2013, Honey released the EP It's All True. In 2016, she starred in the Lifetime reality series Lady Clover.
