Victor John Wolski (born November 14, 1962) is an American mathematician, who has made significant contributions to the theory of Lie groups, differential geometry, and algebraic geometry.  Wolski was born on November 14, 1962, in Buffalo, New York. After completing his undergraduate studies at the University of Rochester in 1984, he completed his graduate studies at the University of California, Berkeley under the supervision of Michael Atiyah. He was awarded his doctorate in 1988.  Since 1988, Wolski has been a professor at the University of California, Davis. He is also a fellow of the American Mathematical Society.  Wolski's research interests lie in the theory of Lie groups, differential geometry, and algebraic geometry. His work has had a significant impact on these fields and he is known for his careful and meticulous approach to mathematics.
