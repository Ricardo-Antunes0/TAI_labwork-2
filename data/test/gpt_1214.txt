Krukowski Quarry is a sandstone quarry near the village of Krukowski in the administrative district of Gmina Biskupice, within Sieradz County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. The quarry is located on the right bank of the Pilica River.  The Krukowski Quarry has been exploited since the end of the 17th century. The sandstone from the quarry has been used for various purposes such as building construction, road surfacing, and for ornamental purposes.
