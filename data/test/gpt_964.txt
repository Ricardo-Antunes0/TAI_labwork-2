Musicology Live 2004ever was a concert tour by the British rock band Radiohead. The tour was their first since the release of their album " Hail to the Thief" in 2003. The tour commenced on 11 October 2004 in Barcelona, Spain and concluded on 15 December 2004 in Madrid, Spain. Each show was recorded and released as a live album.  As part of the tour, Radiohead performed with a number of special guests, including PJ Harvey, Erykah Badu, and Thom Yorke's brother Jonny Greenwood. The tour received positive reviews from music critics, who praised the band's performance and the variety of their songs.
