She left EARTH STAR Entertainment and became affiliated with Link Plan all in the same year of 2018. Biography Takao was born in Tokyo on September 10, 2002. Her older brother Sōnosuke is a pianist and composer. She learned to play the piano at an early age, and she won an international piano contest held in Milan, Italy, when she was 10 years old.
