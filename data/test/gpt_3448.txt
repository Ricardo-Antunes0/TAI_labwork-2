Juanita Suazo Dubray (born 1930) also known as "Queen of the Blues" is a Mexican-American blues singer and guitarist.  Dubray was born in San Antonio, Texas, in 1930. She started playing the guitar at an early age and became a proficient player. Her first performance was at the age of 14. She moved to Los Angeles in 1954 and began performing with various blues bands. Dubray was discovered by Muddy Waters in 1965 and recorded her first album the following year. She has since released numerous albums and has been praised for her unique style of blues singing.
