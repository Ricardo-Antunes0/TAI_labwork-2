MidSouthCon is an American science-fiction convention held annually in Memphis, Tennessee. The convention was founded in 1978 and typically has over 2,000 attendees.  MidSouthCon is the longest-running science fiction convention in the Southern United States. The convention has a strong focus on literature, music, and film. It also features a wide variety of gaming events and panels, as well as a dealers room and art show.
