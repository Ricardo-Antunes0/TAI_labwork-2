(video game)" is a Japanese adult visual novel developed by Leaf and published by D3 Publisher. It was released for Windows in Japan on January 25, 2017, and for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in Japan on March 3, 2017. It features an original story by Koichi Nakamura, with character designs by Haru Okumura.  The game's mechanics are based around the use of QTEs to advance the story; these are often used to decide the fate of the protagonist's love interest. "Happiness! (video game)" has been praised for its emotional storytelling and well-developed characters, although some reviewers have criticised its short length.
