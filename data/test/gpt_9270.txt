Leucospermum utriculosum is a lax, evergreen, upright shrub or small tree, up to 3.5 m tall. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, 2–3 cm long and 1.5–2 cm wide, shiny green on the upper surface and paler beneath, with a prominent midrib and smooth margins. The flowers are white, borne in clusters of up to 10, and are fragrant. The fruit is a drupe, dark purple when ripe, 2–3 cm diameter.
