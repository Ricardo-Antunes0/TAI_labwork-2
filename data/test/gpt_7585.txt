The Tide-class tanker (formerly the Military Afloat Tanker, or MANT) is a class of oil tanker built for the Royal Navy.  The Tide-class tanker was designed to replace the Royal Fleet Auxiliary's ageing fleet of the River class. The vessel has a displacement of 37,000 tonnes and can carry up to 120,000 tonnes of oil. The tanker is capable of travelling at a speed of 20 knots.
