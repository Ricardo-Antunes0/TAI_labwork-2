She is usually depicted holding a ceremonial mirror, which reflects her image to those who ask for it, and a ribbon to represent the water she personifies.  Ebisu is often considered the goddess of commerce and prosperity, as well as intelligence and wisdom. She is also known for her calm demeanor and ability to bring good luck. In some cases, she is seen as the guardian of travellers and the bringer of luck.
