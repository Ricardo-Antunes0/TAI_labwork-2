Vampire films have been a staple in the horror genre since the late 1800s. However, the vampire film genre really took off in the 1970s when Hammer Film Productions released "Dracula" which set the standard for all vampire films to come.   Today, there are a variety of vampire films available to audiences of all ages and interests. Whether you're a fan of classic vampire tales like "The Twilight Saga" or newer, more urbanized interpretations like "Vampire Diaries," there's sure to be a movie out there that appeals to you. So whether you're a diehard vampire enthusiast or just looking for a good scare, check out some of the best vampire movies on Netflix!
