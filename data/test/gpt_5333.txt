The 47th Academy Awards were presented Tuesday, March 27, 1977 at the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion in Los Angeles. The host was Bob Hope.  The nominees for the Academy Awards of 1977 were announced on February 7, 1977. The winners were announced on March 27, 1977.  Best Picture: "The Sting"  Best Actor: Paul Newman   Best Actress: Olivia de Havilland   Best Director: Sidney Lumet   Best Screenplay: Tony Bill and Maureen Stapleton   Best Cinematography: Freddie Young   Best Film Editing: Arthur Hiller   Best Sound: Dick Ebersol and Gary Geiger
