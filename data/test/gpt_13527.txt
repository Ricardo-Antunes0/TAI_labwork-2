Bournville Rugby Football Club is an English rugby union club based in Bournville, near Birmingham. The club was formed in 1875 and is one of the oldest clubs in the Midlands.  The club runs four senior sides, as well as a number of youth teams. In the 2007–08 season, Bournville won their first ever national cup, beating Coltishall 14–12 in the final. The side also reached the quarter-finals of the RFU Championship in the same season.
