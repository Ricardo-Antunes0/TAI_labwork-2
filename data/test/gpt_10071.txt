Goldworm was an American sportswear company that primarily manufactured running and fitness clothing. The company was founded in 1985 by Allen Gold and his wife, Fran Gold.  Goldworm was a leading manufacturer of performance running and fitness clothing, with a focus on technical fabrics and innovative design. The company's products were sold in specialty stores, athletic departments, online retailers, and select department stores. In 2009, Goldworm merged with rival company apparel company Spalding. As part of the merger, Allen Gold retired from the company he had founded.  Goldworm was headquartered in San Francisco, California.
