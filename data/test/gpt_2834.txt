Jessica Whitney Dubroff (May 5, 1988 – February 4, 2009) was an American fashion model. Dubroff was signed to Wilhelmina Models in 2006.  Dubroff was born in 1988 in Scottsdale, Arizona, the daughter of Lynda (née Whitney), a homemaker and real estate agent, and Gary Dubroff, a software engineer. She has two sisters and an older brother. Dubroff attended Chaparral High School in Scottsdale.  Dubroff started modeling at the age of 16, when she won a local beauty pageant. After appearing in various commercials and print campaigns, she was signed to Wilhelmina Models in 2006. Her debut show was at New York Fashion Week in September 2006. Dubroff walked for designers such as Prabal Gurung, Roberto Cavalli, and Tommy Hilfiger.  Dubroff died on February 4, 2009 after suffering a pulmonary embolism while on vacation in Acapulco with her boyfriend. She was only 26 years old.
