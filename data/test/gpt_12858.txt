The Belenski most (Беленски мост) or Byala Bridge is a bridge over the Byala River in Belgrade, Serbia. It connects the neighborhoods of Palilula and Zemun on the left bank with Banjica and Novi Beograd on the right bank. The bridge is around 1,500 meters long and has a width of 10 meters. It was built between 1897 and 1899 by the Austro-Hungarian Empire as a road bridge.
