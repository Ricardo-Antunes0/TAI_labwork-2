The dynasty was noted for its great military achievements, including the conquest of most of China by 1040, and its establishment of a centralized government.  The Song dynasty was a time of great economic prosperity and growth, with a rapidly growing population and active foreign trade. Its capital was located in Kaifeng, but eventually moved to Beijing. The Song period saw a number of scientific, technological, and artistic developments, including the invention of the movable-type printing press and the development of porcelain.
