The Ibanez S Series (also known as the S series) is a line of electric guitars manufactured by Ibanez. It was introduced in 2006.  The S series is a mid-priced line of Ibanez electric guitars. The guitars are made in Japan, and feature alder bodies with maple necks and fretboards, active electronics and Ibanez's signature "double cutaway" shape.  Ibanez S guitars are well-rounded instruments that can be used for a variety of styles, from rock to blues to country. They are also popular with metalheads and shredders, as their active electronics provide good sound and string tracking capabilities.
