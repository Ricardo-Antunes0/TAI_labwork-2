INVU  is the third studio album by Japanese singer-songwriter Fumiya Tanaka. It was released on March 16, 2019, by Sony Music Japan.  Fumiya Tanaka is a Japanese singer-songwriter. She released her debut album, "INVU", in 2017, and followed it up with the EP "INVU 2" in 2018. INVU is Tanaka's third studio album.  The album was produced by Tanaka herself, alongside the help of longtime collaborator Kenichi Matsubara. The album features collaborations with artists such as P-Money, Ami Suzuki and Kyary Pamyu Pamyu.  INVU received generally positive reviews from music critics. Many praised Tanaka's songwriting and instrumentation, while others found the album too experimental for mainstream success. However, Tanaka's distinctive voice was praised across the board.
