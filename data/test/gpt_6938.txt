Happy Hotel  is a 2012 Chinese romantic comedy film directed by Zhang Yimou. The screenplay was written by Zhang, along with screenwriter Niu Baolong. The film stars Li Bingbing and Chen Bolin.  The film tells the story of two hotel employees who fall in love and are forced to face the challenges of their families and work lives when their relationship is threatened.  Zhang Yimou's Happy Hotel premiered at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival where it won the Special Jury Prize. It was released in China on October 12, 2012, and in international territories on November 25.
