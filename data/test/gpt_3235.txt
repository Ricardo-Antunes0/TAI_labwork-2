Meisdorf House  is a schloss in the municipality of Meisdorf, district of Goslar, in Lower Saxony, Germany.  The castle was built from 1485 to 1493 by John the Fearless, Duke of Jülich-Cleves-Berg and his wife Elisabeth of Brunswick-Lüneburg. It is a three-winged stone building with a square plan. The entrance is on the south side. The castle is used today as a youth hostel.
