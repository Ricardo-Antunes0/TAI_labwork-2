Born in Los Angeles, California, Hamilton began his acting career in 1957, appearing in guest roles on television series. He made his Broadway debut in the role of Billy Bigelow in the original production of The Pajama Game (1957). Hamilton received Tony Award nominations for his performances in the roles of Young Tom Sawyer in the original production of The Adventures of Tom Sawyer (1960) and Cliff Arbogast in The Odd Couple (1969). He also starred as the title character in the original production of Promises, Promises (1982). Hamilton's film appearances include The Ghost and Mr. Chicken (1981), Hannah and Her Sisters (1986), My Best Friend's Wedding (1997) and The Visitor (2007).  Hamilton died of a heart attack at age 59 in Los Angeles, California.
