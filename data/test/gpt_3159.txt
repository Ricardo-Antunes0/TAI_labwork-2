Aryan Kaganof (born 1964 as Ian Kerkhof) is a British artist. He was born in London, England.  Kaganof studied painting at the Slade School of Fine Art in London from 1982 to 1985, and then at the Royal College of Art in London from 1986 to 1990. He has exhibited at the Royal Academy of Arts, the Guggenheim Museum Bilbao, and the Tate Gallery London. In 2002, he was awarded an honorary doctorate by the University of Portsmouth.
