Pierre Youssef El Daher (, born 4 March 1979) is a Lebanese-born French football coach and former player who is currently the head coach of Saudi club Al-Nassr. He has also been a head coach at French clubs Lille OSC, Marseille and Nantes.  Born in Beirut, Lebanon, El Daher played as a midfielder for a number of French clubs, most notably Marseille, Lille and Nantes. He made his international debut for Lebanon in 2001 and went on to make 59 appearances for his country. He retired from playing in 2012 after spending two seasons with Belgian club KV Mechelen.  El Daher started his coaching career with AS Monaco's youth setup in 2013, before moving to Belgium to take charge of KV Mechelen's academy the following year. In January 2016, he was appointed manager of Lille OSC, where he led the team to third place in the Ligue 1 table at the end of his first season in charge.  On 29 May 2018, El Daher was named the new head coach of Saudi club Al-Nassr.
