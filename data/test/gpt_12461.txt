Kande is an Indian Punjabi-language romantic action film written and directed by Gurinder Chadha and produced by his wife Kiran Chadha. The film stars Diljit Dosanjh, Anushka Sharma, and Deepak Dobriyal in pivotal roles.  The film tells the story of two young people from different backgrounds who find love and must face the challenges of life together. It is based on the novel of the same name by Gurinder Chadha. The film began production in April 2015 in Punjab, India and was released on 14 July 2016.  Kande received positive reviews from critics, with praise going to the performances of Diljit Dosanjh and Anushka Sharma, as well as the writing and directing. The film grossed $2 million during its opening weekend in India, becoming the highest-grossing Punjabi language film ever released. It has since been released in other countries including Canada, United States, United Kingdom, Australia and New Zealand.
