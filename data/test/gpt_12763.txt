The New York Skyliners were a soccer club based in New York City that played in the second division of the American Soccer League from 1967 to 1974.  Founded in 1967 as a member of the second division American Soccer League, the Skyliners finished the season in first place and were promoted to the first division. The club played their home games at Giants Stadium. In their two seasons in the first division, they finished seventh and eighth respectively. After finishing fourth in 1974, they were demoted back to the second division. The Skyliners folded after the 1975 season.
