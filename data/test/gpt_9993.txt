Choi Jun-hong (Hangul: 최준홍; born October 15, 1984), better known by his stage name Zeo (Hangul: 제엔), is a South Korean rapper, songwriter and record producer. He is a member of the South Korean hip-hop group Epik High.  Zelo was born on October 15, 1984 in Busan, South Korea. He began rapping at the age of 12, and released his first album in 2001. Zeo's biggest hit to date has been "Good Night", which peaked at number 2 on the Gaon Chart. Zeo has also produced for other artists such as Park Myung-soo, Brown Eyed Girls' Jeon So-yeon, 2NE1's CL and GFRIEND's Yerin.
