Delme Herriman (born 8 May 1973 in Auckland, New Zealand) is a musician and composer.  Herriman began playing the piano at the age of four, and studied music at the University of Auckland, graduating with a Bachelor of Music in 1995. He then moved to London, where he studied composition with Julian Lloyd Webber and Anthony Minghella at the Royal Academy of Music. He has since worked with many leading opera companies around the world, including Opera North and Glyndebourne.  Herriman's compositions include operas, ballets, chamber music, and choral works. His most recent opera, "The Fairy's Kiss", was commissioned by Opera Queensland and premiered in Brisbane in 2013.
