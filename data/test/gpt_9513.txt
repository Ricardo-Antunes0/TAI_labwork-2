Vaniyambadi  is a town in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located in the Tiruvallur district, about 25 kilometres from the city of Tiruvallur and 150 kilometres from Chennai. The town is known for its Hindu temple, the Vaniyambadi Sri Mahalingeswara Temple.  The town is also known for its handloom industry and its silk sarees. The town has a population of about 36,000 people.
