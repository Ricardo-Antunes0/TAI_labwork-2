Małgorzata Rdest (born 14 January 1993 in Bydgoszcz) is a Polish singer, songwriter and actress.  Rdest was born in Bydgoszcz, Poland on 14 January 1993. She started her music career as a member of the Polish girl group Gosia. The group released their debut album in 2009 and achieved moderate success. In 2011, Gosia disbanded and Rdest started working on her solo career. Her first solo album, "Miłość", was released in 2013 and topped the Polish Albums Chart. The album spawned the singles "Nie mam wyboru", "Pozdrawiam" and "Nie mogę uwierzyć".  In 2014, Rdest starred in the Polish drama film "Kocham Cię" opposite Artur Zmuda. The film received positive reviews from critics and was a box office success. In 2015, she starred in the crime thriller film "Krew na życie" alongside Maciej Stuhr. The film received mixed reviews from critics but was a box office success. In 2016, she starred in the historical drama film "Czego chcesz", opposite Bogdan Wojciechowski, which was also successful at the box office.  Rdest is currently dating Polish footballer Maciej Rybus. They announced their relationship in late 2017 and married on 5 July 2018 in Gdańsk.
