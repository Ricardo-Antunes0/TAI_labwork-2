Fat Princess is an action real-time strategy video game developed by Canadian studio Wildcard and published by Electronic Arts. The game was released in April 2013 for Microsoft Windows and OS X. A console port of the game was released by publisher EA Canada in October 2013 for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360.  The game is set in an unnamed medieval kingdom, where players control a princess as she tries to save her kingdom from destruction. The game is played in real-time on a single screen, with players controlling armies of characters to defeat enemies and progress through the game. The player can choose to play as the princess herself, or control one of several supporting characters. The game features a variety of different units and abilities that can be used to defeat enemies, and players can upgrade their units using resources gained from defeating enemies or collected during the course of the game.  "Fat Princess" received mixed reviews from critics. While some praised the game's graphics and gameplay, others criticized its difficulty and shallow storyline. The game has sold over one million copies worldwide.
