The College Tribune is a student newspaper published at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. The Tribune is the flagship publication of the Student Media Advisory Board, an organization that provides resources and support to student media organizations on campus.  The Tribune was founded in 1892 as the University of Illinois College Register and Journal. The paper was renamed the College Tribune in 1897 and has been published continuously since then. The Tribune is a member of the National Student Press Association and the Associated Collegiate Press.
