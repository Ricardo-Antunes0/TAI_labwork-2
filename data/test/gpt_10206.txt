Njerep (Njerup) is a Mambiloid language spoken in Cameroon.  Njerep is spoken by the Njerep people in the North-Eastern Region of Cameroon. It is also used as a lingua franca by other Mambiloid groups in the region. The Njerep language is closely related to the Bamileke, the Oti and the Bamoun languages.  Njerep is an agglutinative language with a VSO verb system. It has two genders, three numbers and four cases. Njerep has a rich lexicon with over 1,000 words.
