Caladenia praecox, commonly known as early caladenia, is a species of orchid found in eastern Australia. It has a single leaf and up to fifty greenish white flowers with a pink labellum.  The early caladenia is a terrestrial, perennial, deciduous, herb that typically grows to a height of and has a single leaf long and wide. The flowers are greenish white and have a pink labellum. They usually bloom from September to December.
