Diamond cutting is the practice of shaping a diamond by using a diamond saw. Diamonds are cut into rough diamonds, which are then polished until they have a flawless surface.  Diamond cutting is an important part of the diamond industry. Rough diamonds are cut into diamonds that are of the desired size, shape, and quality. The process of diamond cutting begins by using a diamond saw to create cuts in the rough diamond. The cuts are then polished until the diamond has a flawless surface.
