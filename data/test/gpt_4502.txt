Roger D. Nelson is an American scientist and the director of the Institute for Theoretical Physics at the University of California, Santa Barbara.  Nelson received his B.A. in physics from the University of California, Berkeley in 1972 and his Ph.D. in physics from Yale University in 1977. He was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of California, Santa Barbara from 1977 to 1978, and a professor of physics at the University of California, Irvine from 1978 to 1984. He was a professor of physics at UC Santa Barbara from 1984 to 2007, and the director of the Institute for Theoretical Physics at UC Santa Barbara from 2007 to 2016. Nelson is a fellow of the American Physical Society and the American Association for Theoretical Physicists.
