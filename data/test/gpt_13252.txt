Vike Church  is a parish church in Vestlandet, Norway. It is located in the village of Vike, about south of the town of Tromsø. The church is part of the Tromsø prosti (deanery) in the Diocese of Nord-Troms.  The first church on this site was built in 1848. The present church was built in 1905 by the architect Hans Chr. Sunde. The church seats about 350 people. The altarpiece was painted by Oddvar Smedal in 1957.
