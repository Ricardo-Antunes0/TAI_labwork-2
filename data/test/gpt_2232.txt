Valerie Mason-John (born 22 November 1962) is a British actress. She has appeared in a number of television and film roles, but is perhaps best known for her role as Dr. Laura Nelson in the BBC medical drama series Casualty.  Mason-John was born in 1962 in Wokingham, Berkshire, England to a family of doctors. She attended Eton College, where she was a member of the debating society, and then studied medicine at the University of Cambridge. After graduating, she worked as a doctor before beginning her acting career.
