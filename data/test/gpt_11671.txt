"Pilot" is the series premiere to the American television series "Devious Maids". The series is produced by Warner Horizon Television and stars Ruth Wilson, Eva Longoria, Eva Mendes, and Marc Anthony.  "Devious Maids" is a comedy-drama series that follows the lives of working-class women who become maids in Beverly Hills. The series centers on the maids' efforts to navigate the wealthy and demanding households of their clients while trying to maintain their own identities and relationships.  The pilot was filmed in Los Angeles in early 2014. The series premiered on Lifetime on April 20, 2015. On May 11, 2015, Lifetime cancelled the series after one season.
