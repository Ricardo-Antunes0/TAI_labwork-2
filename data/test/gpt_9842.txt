Chiranjeevulu  is a 1956 Indian Telugu-language film directed by K. V. Reddy and produced by M. G. Ramachandran. The film stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Savitri, Gemini Ganesan, Rajasthani in the lead roles and music composed by T. V. Raju.  Chiranjeevulu is a tale of two brothers – one good and one bad – set in the backdrop of the turbulent Telugu political scene in the 1940s. The good brother sacrifices everything for his people while the bad brother uses his power to line his own pockets. In the end, the good brother prevails and sets an example for all.
