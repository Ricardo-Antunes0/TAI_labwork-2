NALED Serbia, or National Alliance for Local Economic Development, is a non-profit organization which was founded in 2006. It is headquartered in Belgrade, Serbia. The organization's goal is to support the development of local economies by providing technical assistance and promoting the development of innovative local business models.   NALED Serbia is a member of the United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO), the Association of Southeast European Chambers of Commerce and Industry (ASECCI), and the European Union Local Development Fund (EULDF). It also has bilateral agreements with countries including Albania, Bulgaria, Croatia, Greece, Hungary, Macedonia, Montenegro, Romania, Russia, Serbia, Slovakia, and Turkey.
