The Beltrami County Courthouse is a historic courthouse located in Beltrami County, Minnesota, United States. It was built in 1904 and is a two-story, Richardsonian Romanesque style red brick building. It features a large round tower with a conical roof and is capped with a steeple. The building served as the county courthouse until 1966 when it was replaced by a new facility. The courthouse was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.
