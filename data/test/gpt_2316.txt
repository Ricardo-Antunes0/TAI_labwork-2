Pepper Dagenhart Culpepper (born 1 October 1968) is a Canadian journalist and author who specializes in investigative journalism.  Culpepper is a former reporter for the "Ottawa Citizen" and the "Toronto Star", where he won two National Newspaper Awards for investigative reporting. After leaving the "Star" in 2006, he became a freelance journalist, writing for the "National Post" and other publications. His work has focused on government corruption, police brutality, and the media. Culpepper was born in Toronto, Ontario.
