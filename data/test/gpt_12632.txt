Kahlil Lewis (born August 31, 1997) is an American football running back for the Los Angeles Rams of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Ohio State.  Kahlil Lewis was born on August 31, 1997 in Columbus, Ohio. He attended St. Ignatius High School in Cleveland, Ohio.  Lewis played college football at Ohio State. As a sophomore in 2018, he was a backup running back and averaged 2.9 yards per carry. As a junior in 2019, he was the starting running back and led the Buckeyes with 1,191 rushing yards and 12 touchdowns.   On May 12, 2019, Lewis signed with the Los Angeles Rams as an undrafted free agent.
