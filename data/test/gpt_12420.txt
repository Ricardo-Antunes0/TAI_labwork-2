Fredonia is a city in Louisa County, Iowa, United States. The population was 1,112 at the 2010 census.  Fredonia was founded in 1857 by John and Rebecca (Baker) Fredonia. The city was named for Frederick Augustus, Prince of Wales (later King George V of the United Kingdom). Fredonia was incorporated as a city in 1878.  Fredonia is located at (40.010516, -93.544416).
