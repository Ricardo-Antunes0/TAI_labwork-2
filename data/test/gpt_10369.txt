Chaturi Thalagalage is a Sri Lankan former first-class cricketer who played for the Tamil Union Cricket Club.  Chaturi Thalagalage was born on the 6th of October 1983 in Colombo. He played for Tamil Union Cricket Club from 2002 to 2004, and he made a single first-class appearance for the club, in 2003/04. Chaturi Thalagalage scored 6 runs in the match, which Tamil Union Cricket Club lost by an innings margin. He also played for the Sri Lanka under-19s team, and he made a single appearance for the team in 2002.
