Ronald William Howard (born March 1, 1954) is an American actor, director and producer. He is best known for his roles as an actor in the films Apollo 13 (1995), A Beautiful Mind (2001), and The Da Vinci Code (2006), as well as his directing work on the films Splash (1984), The Lost Boys (1987), Parenthood (1989), and The Ron Howard Film Foundation (1991).  Howard has also produced films such as Apollo 13, A Beautiful Mind, and The Da Vinci Code. He has won two Academy Awards, for Best Director for A Beautiful Mind and Best Picture for Apollo 13. He also received a Golden Globe Award, a British Academy Film Award, and a Screen Actors Guild Award for his acting work.
