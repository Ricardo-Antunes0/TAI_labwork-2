Hannah Nielsen (born 28 November 1987 in Copenhagen) is a Danish singer and songwriter. She has recorded three albums, most recently the self-titled Hannah Nielsen in 2016. Nielsen's music has been described as folk, soul and pop with a touch of country.  Nielsen was born in Copenhagen, Denmark to parents who are both musicians. She started singing at an early age and began her music career in 2006, when she won the Danish talent show "X Factor". After winning the show, Nielsen signed to Copenhagen Records. She released her debut album, "Soulmates", in 2009 and followed it up with "Heartbeats" in 2011. Her third album, "Hannah Nielsen", was released in 2016.
