She is perhaps best known for her roles in the British soap opera Coronation Street, as Audrey Roberts since 1990, and in the US television series Mad Men, as Peggy Olson since 2007.  Moss was born in Manchester, England. She trained at the Manchester School of Theatre. She has been nominated for five British Academy Television Awards and has won twice, for Best Actress in a Drama Series for her role as Audrey Roberts on Coronation Street and for Best Supporting Actress in a Drama Series for her role as Peggy Olson on Mad Men.
