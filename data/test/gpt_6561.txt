Elisa Aaltola (born 1976) is a Finnish artist. She has exhibited her work in galleries and museums in Finland, Sweden, Denmark, Germany, Italy and Spain.  Elisa Aaltola was born in 1976 in Jyväskylä, Finland. She studied photography at the University of Art and Design Helsinki, graduating with a degree in 2001. Her early work focused on the exploration of the process of photographic reproduction and the manipulation of image data. However, since 2006 her focus has shifted to painting. Her paintings are characterised by their delicate use of colour and light, as well as their often abstract forms.
