"Bat Country" is a song by Avenged Sevenfold. The song is about a character named Bat Country, who is "the man with the plan".  The song was released as the third and final single from their seventh album, "The Stage", on October 12, 2017. The song peaked at number 26 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart.  The song's video was directed by Joseph Kahn and premiered on September 25, 2017. In the video, Bat Country is a vigilante fighting crime in an alternate Gotham City.
