Thaneer Thaneer  is a 1981 Indian Malayalam film, directed by P. Bhaskaran and produced by S. B. Chacko. The film stars Mohanlal, Jayalalithaa, Kalabhavan Mani and Sukumari in lead roles. The film has musical score by M. K. Arjunan.  The story revolves around the life of a poor man, Siva Pillai (Mohanlal), and his struggles in life. He works as a barber in a small town and is always struggling to make ends meet. One day, he meets a woman named Lalitha (Jayalalithaa) who is a wealthy woman. She falls in love with him and decides to help him out financially. Siva Pillai is overjoyed and grateful to her. However, Lalitha has another plan in mind - she wants to marry Siva Pillai and take away all of his money. Siva Pillai is hesitant at first, but he eventually agrees to marry her. Lalitha then sets about making sure that everything goes according to her plan - she starts spending all of Siva Pillai's money on expensive clothes, jewelry and cars for herself and her friends. Siva Pillai is left with nothing, and he is forced to live in poverty once again.
