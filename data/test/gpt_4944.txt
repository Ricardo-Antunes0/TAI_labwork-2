The Rawlett School is a secondary school located in the town of Rawlett, in the English county of Hampshire. The school was founded in 1947 by Mrs. Eileen Rawlett, who was also its first headmistress. In 1997, it became a foundation school and gained academy status in 2010. The Rawlett School has a population of around 680 pupils aged 11-18, with around half of pupils coming from disadvantaged backgrounds. It has a strong performing and ambitious SEN department and has been recognised as a specialist science and mathematics school by the Government.
