Jesse Labelle is a Country singer-songwriter. In 1991, he was signed to Capitol Records and released his debut album, "Jesse". The album was certified platinum by the RIAA. Labelle's second album, "Blue Moon", was also certified platinum. In 1995, he released his third album, "Why Should I Cry Now?". The album was certified gold. In 1997, Labelle released his fourth album, "The Color of Me". The album was not as successful as his previous albums. In 2000, he released his fifth album, "Hillbilly Deluxe". The album was not as successful as his previous albums. He has released seven studio albums and one compilation album.
