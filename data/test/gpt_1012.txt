Kronans Droghandel Apotek AB, doing business as Kronans Apotek, is a pharmacy in the city of Gothenburg, Sweden. It is one of the largest pharmacies in Scandinavia and is also one of the largest in Europe. The pharmacy was founded in 1892 and is still family owned and operated. Kronans Droghandel Apotek AB has 550 employees.  Kronans Droghandel Apotek AB specializes in general pharmaceuticals, health foods and supplements, and medical equipment. The pharmacy also offers a wide range of services, including preventive health care, health education, and children's health care. Kronans Droghandel Apotek AB is a member of the Swedish Pharmacy Association (Svenska Pharmakistsamfundet) and the European Pharmacists Association (EUROPAS).
