Pullet Surprise is a 1997 7-minute Looney Tunes short directed by Chuck Jones and starring Daffy Duck.  Pullet Surprise is a Looney Tunes short directed by Chuck Jones and featuring Daffy Duck. The short is about Daffy's attempts to surprise a female guinea pig with a bouquet of flowers, only to have the guinea pig ambush him in her lair and force him to eat her eggs.
