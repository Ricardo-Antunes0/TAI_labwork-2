Christine Thorburn (born September 17, 1969 in Edmonton, Alberta) is a Canadian singer-songwriter. She is the daughter of country singer Ed Thorburn.  Thorburn started her music career in the early 1990s, releasing her debut album "The Evolution of a Lady" in 1992. She followed up with "Street Dreams" in 1994 and "Portraits" in 1996. In 2001, she released her third album, "The Best of Christine Thorburn". In 2004, she released her fourth album, "The Collection", which was nominated for a Canadian Juno Award. Thorburn's fifth album, "The Invisible Way", was released in 2009. Thorburn has also released two EPs and one live album. Thorburn has lived in Vancouver since the early 1990s.
