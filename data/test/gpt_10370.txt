Kevin Bolibruck (born February 8, 1977) is an American actor, musician, comedian and voice actor. He is known for his roles in the television series How I Met Your Mother and the animated series The Simpsons.  Bolibruck was born in Burbank, California. He is the son of Barbara (n√©e Bowles), a singer and actress, and John Bolibruck, a television producer and director. His maternal grandparents were Austrian-born actress Betty Bowles and Austrian-born musician George Bowles. Bolibruck has two younger sisters: Dana and Candace.  Bolibruck began his career as a musician, playing in various punk and hardcore bands before he began his acting career. He made his television debut in the 1999 ABC sitcom How I Met Your Mother as Barney Stinson's best friend Marshall Eriksen. He also appeared in episodes of Freaks & Geeks, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, ER, Monk, The West Wing and House. He portrayed Groundskeeper Willie on The Simpsons from 2003 to 2007. He starred as David Wallace in the Fox comedy series The Mindy Project from 2013 to 2015, for which he received an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Comedy Series.  Bolibruck was also a regular panelist on the game show Jeopardy! from 2004 to 2010. In 2011, he starred in the independent film The Overnight.
