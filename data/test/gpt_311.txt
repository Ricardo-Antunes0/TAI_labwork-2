Maels Rodríguez Corrales (born October 15, 1979) is a Spanish professional basketball player for BCM Gravelines of the French LNB Pro A. He has also played in the Spanish League and in the Italian Lega Basket.  Rodríguez started his professional career with CB Granada of the Spanish League. In 2000, he moved to PAOK of the Greek League, where he played until 2003. In 2004, he joined FC Barcelona of the Liga ACB, and helped them win the league title that season. Rodríguez then signed with Real Madrid for the 2005–06 season. He later moved to CSKA Moscow of Russia for two seasons before returning to Spain and signing with CB Cantabria. In 2014, Rodríguez signed with BCM Gravelines of the French LNB Pro A.  Rodríguez has won several accolades during his career, including the EuroLeague MVP Award in 2006, and was named Spanish Player of the Year in 2006 and 2007. He also won an Olympic gold medal with Spain's basketball team at the 2008 Summer Olympics.
