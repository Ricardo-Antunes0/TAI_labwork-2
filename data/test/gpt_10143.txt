Kenan Cahit Ece (born 24 December 1980) is a Turkish football player who currently plays for Konyaspor. He is a former captain of the Turkish national team.  Kenan Cahit Ece was born in 1980 in Ankara, Turkey. He started his professional career with Ankaragücü, making his debut in the 1998-99 season. He was transferred to Beşiktaş in 2002, where he would spend six seasons before moving to Konyaspor in 2006. With Konyaspor, he won the Süper Lig title in the 2009-10 season and the Turkish Cup in the 2013-14 season. He made his international debut for Turkey on 27 May 2003 in a friendly match against Qatar. He has represented Turkey at UEFA Euro 2004, the 2007 FIFA World Cup, and the 2010 FIFA World Cup.  Kenan Cahit Ece is married to Emine Şahin and they have two children: Kenan (born 2006) and Melis (born 2010).
