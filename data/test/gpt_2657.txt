HNLMS A (Dutch: Hr.Ms. A) was an unarmed trawler that was sunk by a German U-boat on 12 September 1944 while on a convoy patrol in the North Atlantic. Of the crew of 44, all but three were killed.  The trawler was commissioned into the Royal Netherlands Navy in 1929 and was used for fisheries patrols and to transport troops.
