Sye is a 2005 Indian Kannada-language action film written and directed by V. S. Raghavendra. The film stars Darshan, Sadna and Lokanath in lead roles. The music of the film is composed by Hamsalekha. The film was initially scheduled to be released in November 2004, but was delayed due to various reasons.  Sye is a story of two brothers who are left to fend for themselves after their parents' death. They have to fight against all odds to protect their land and their village from the greedy and corrupt officials.
