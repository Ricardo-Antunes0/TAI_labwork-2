Jones Dairy Farm is an American, privately owned dairy farm located in the town of West Lebanon, New Hampshire. The farm is operated by the descendants of Gilbert and Abigail (Jones) Perkins, who acquired the property in 1892. Gilbert Perkins was a native of Lyme, New Hampshire, who worked as a farmer before opening the farm. The Joneses continue to operate the farm today, producing milk, cheese, and yogurt.
