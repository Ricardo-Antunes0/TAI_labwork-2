Rahi Mohinder Singh better known as R. M. Singh, is an Indian chartered accountant and business executive. He currently serves as the Chairman of the Audit Committee and Treasurer of the Board of Directors at Wal-Mart Stores, Inc.  Born in India in 1960, Singh came to the United States in 1984 to study at the University of California, Berkeley. After completing his undergraduate studies, he worked as an accountant with Arthur Andersen until 1991, when he joined Ernst & Young as a partner. In 2002, he was named the Vice Chairman and Chief Financial Officer of PepsiCo International. In 2006, he was appointed the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Cadbury Schweppes plc.  In 2009, Singh joined Wal-Mart Stores, Inc., as its chairman and chief executive officer. At Wal-Mart, Singh has focused on improving the company's financial performance and increasing its international presence. As of 2017, Singh has been ranked as one of "The World's Most Powerful People" by Forbes magazine.
