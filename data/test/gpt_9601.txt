Western supremacy: triumph of an idea? is a book about the history of the idea of western supremacy, from ancient Greece and Rome to the modern world. The book is divided into three parts: the ancient world, the medieval world, and the modern world.  The idea of western supremacy has been central to international relations and world politics for centuries. It has been used to justify colonialism, imperialism, and war. The book argues that the idea of western supremacy is a false concept, and that it has led to many problems in the past.
