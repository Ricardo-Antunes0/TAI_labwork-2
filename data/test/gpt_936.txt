William Henry Chase (June 4, 1798 – February 12, 1872) was an American prelate of the Catholic Church. He served as Bishop of the Diocese of Cincinnati from 1835 to 1872.  William Henry Chase was born in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, United States, on June 4, 1798. He was ordained a priest in the Catholic Church in 1824. Chase served as a missionary in Ohio and Illinois before being appointed the bishop of the Diocese of Cincinnati in 1835. During his tenure, he helped to build many churches and schools in the Diocese. Chase died in Cincinnati on February 12, 1872.
