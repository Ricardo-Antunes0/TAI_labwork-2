Darren Shane Berry (born 10 December 1969) is a former English cricketer. Berry was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm medium pace. He was born at Gloucester, Gloucestershire.  Berry made his first-class debut for Gloucestershire against Nottinghamshire in 1989. He made four further first-class appearances for the county, the last of which came against Somerset in 1992. In his five first-class appearances, he scored 82 runs at an average of 9.50, with a high score of 34 not out. With the ball, he took six wickets at a bowling average of 36.50, with best figures of 3/39.  Berry also played one List A match for Gloucestershire, against Somerset in the 1992 NatWest Trophy. In this match, he scored a single run before being dismissed by IanBotham. He also took one wicket in this match, that of Martin Whiteley.
