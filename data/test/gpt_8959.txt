Angela Taylor (born 1971 or 1972) is an American retired professional basketball player who played in the Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA) from 1992 to 2001.  Taylor was born in New York City, where she attended and played high school basketball for the Manhattanville College Falcons. She then attended Louisiana State University, where she played for the LSU Lady Tigers women's basketball team. She was drafted by the Houston Comets in the second round of the 1992 WNBA Draft. She played for the Comets, San Antonio Silver Stars, Indiana Fever, and Seattle Storm during her career. In 2001, Taylor retired from playing professional basketball.  Since retiring from playing professional basketball, Taylor has worked as an assistant coach for her alma mater, Louisiana State University women's basketball team. In 2009, she was named head coach of the Louisiana Tech Bulldogs women's basketball team. She served in that position until she resigned in December 2017 after eleven seasons with the Bulldogs.
