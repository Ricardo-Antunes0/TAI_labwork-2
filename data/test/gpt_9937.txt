Vantha Rajavathaan Varuven is a remake of the 1990 Tamil film Vantha Rajavathaan. The film stars Ajith Kumar and Shriya Saran in the lead roles and was released on 14 August 2014. The story is about an unemployed man, played by Ajith Kumar, who is forced to take up a job as a chauffeur to a woman, played by Shriya Saran, after her husband is killed. The two form an unlikely alliance and start a new life together.
