Pavillion Agency is a domestic staffing agency headquartered in Dallas, Texas. It specializes in the placement of nurses, respiratory therapists, and physical therapists.  The Pavillion Agency was founded in 1984 by brothers Dan and Paul Pavillion. The company initially focused on the placement of electricians and plumbers. In 1993, the agency began providing staffing services for nurses and respiratory therapists. Today, Pavillion Agency is one of the largest domestic staffing agencies in the United States, with more than 2,000 employees located in more than 40 states.
