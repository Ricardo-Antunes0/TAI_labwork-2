The Cebu flowerpecker (Dicaeum quadricolor) is a species of bird in the Dicaeidae family. It is found in the Philippines.  The Cebu flowerpecker is a small but brightly coloured bird with a red head and breast, black wings and tail, and a white belly. It has a long, graduated bill and is very active in searching for food. It feeds mainly on insects, but also takes small reptiles and birds. The Cebu flowerpecker is found mainly in forests and plantations, but also occurs near human settlements.
