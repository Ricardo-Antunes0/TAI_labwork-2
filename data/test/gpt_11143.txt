BTS , also known as the Bangtan Boys or the Bullet Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy group formed in 2013 by Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of Kim Seung-hyun, Jin, Suga, Jimin, V, J-Hope, and Rap Monster.  The group gained significant popularity with their singles "No More Dream" (2015) and "DNA" (2018), both of which reached the top five of the South Korean National Chart. They were also awarded Best New Artist at the Mnet Asian Music Awards in 2016 and the Golden Disk Award for Best New Artist at the Seoul Music Awards in 2018.
