Joe Thornalley, known professionally as Vegyn, is a Welsh singer, songwriter and musician. He is the vocalist, guitarist and principal songwriter for the post-hardcore band Funeral for a Friend.  Vegyn was born in Swansea, Wales, on 25 March 1987. He started playing the guitar at the age of 12 and formed Funeral for a Friend in 2006 with his school friend Tim Lambesis. The band released their debut album, "The Black Parade", in 2007 and toured extensively throughout Europe and North America before releasing their second album, "The Great Fire", in 2009. In 2010 they released their third album, "The Black Crown", which charted in the UK at number 2.  Vegyn has also released two solo albums: "Distant Son" (2011) and "Live at the BBC" (2013). He has also collaborated with artists including Architects, Asking Alexandria, Bullet For My Valentine and The Silent Civil War.  Vegyn is married to British singer-songwriter Beth Ditto. They have one child together.
