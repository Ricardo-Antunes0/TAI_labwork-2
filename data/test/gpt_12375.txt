Ellsworth City Hall is the seat of government for the city of Ellsworth, Maine. The building was completed in 1897 and is located at the junction of Main Street and Franklin Street. It is a 2-1/2 story masonry structure, with a gable roof and a long expanse of north-facing windows. The building was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.
