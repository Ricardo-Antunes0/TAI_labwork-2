"Something There" is a song written by American singer-songwriter Rob Thomas and produced by Ryan Tedder for Thomas's third studio album, "A Thousand Miles" (2009). The song was released as the album's second single in the United States on September 26, 2009.  "Something There" is a ballad about a person who is searching for something, or someone, that they have lost. The song was well received by music critics, who praised its lyrics and melody. It reached number one on the US Adult Top 40 chart and peaked at number three on the "Billboard" Hot 100. Internationally, the song reached the top ten in Australia, Belgium, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand and the United Kingdom.  Rob Thomas wrote "Something There" while touring with Matchbox Twenty in support of their third album "Mad Love" (2008). Thomas had been working on an album of acoustic-based material and "[the Matchbox Twenty tour] gave him some real impetus to finish it". He completed the song while on tour in Europe in late 2008 and early 2009. Ryan Tedder produced the track and recorded it at his studio in Los Angeles.  The song was released as the second single from Thomas's third studio album "A Thousand Miles" on September 26, 2009. It was serviced to contemporary hit radio in the United States three weeks later on October 9. The accompanying music video was directed by Dave Meyers and premiered on VEVO on October 18.
