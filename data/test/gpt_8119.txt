CJBK is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 1010 kHz in Hamilton, Ontario. The station airs a hot adult contemporary music format branded as "1010 CJBK".  The station first went on the air on October 10, 1968, as CJFB. It was owned by the Hamilton Broadcasting Company, which also operated CJBK-FM. In 1977, the station was sold to the Yorkton Broadcasting Group, and adopted its current callsign in 1984. Yorkton sold CJBK and CJBK-FM to Bell Media in 1998. In 2007, Bell Media sold CJBK and sister station CKXR to Ottawa-based Astral Media.
