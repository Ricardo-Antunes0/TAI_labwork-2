He was a professor of English at Yale University and the author of several books on American writers, including Charles Reznikoff: A Biography (1983), The Life of Allen Ginsberg (1994), William S. Burroughs: A Biography (1997), and Joan Didion: A Biography (2003).  Howard Frank Mosher was born in the Bronx borough of New York City on June 2, 1942. He taught at Yale from 1967 to 1991, when he became a professor of English at Princeton University. Mosher died on March 20, 2017 at his home in Princeton, New Jersey.
