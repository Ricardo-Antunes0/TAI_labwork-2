The 1st Destroyer Flotilla, also styled as 1st Destroyer Squadron, was a naval unit of the Royal Navy that served during the First World War.  The 1st Destroyer Flotilla was formed in 1914 and saw service in the Battle of Jutland and in the Mediterranean during the War. The unit was disbanded in 1920.  The 1st Destroyer Flotilla consisted of eight destroyers: HMS Aboukir, HMS Acasta, HMS Active, HMS Achates, HMS Acheron, HMS Acorn, HMS Actaeon and HMS Adonis.
