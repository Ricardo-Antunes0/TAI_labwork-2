Ceredwen (pronounced ker-ED-wen) is a Celtic band from Wales. They play a mixture of Celtic, folk and rock music.  Formed in 1999, the band consists of lead vocalist Emyr Huws, guitarist Guto Pryce, fiddler Huw Lewis, bass player Ceri James and drummer Ioan James. Ceredwen have released two albums, "Ceredwen" (2002) and "Sing to the Moon" (2008), and have toured extensively throughout Europe.
