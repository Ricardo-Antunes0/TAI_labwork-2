vSide was an Internet-based 3D virtual world that allowed users to interact with each other in a 3D environment. vSide was a social network where users could communicate, socialize, and play together. The service was developed by vSocial and operated by vSphere, Inc.  On September 30, 2016, it was announced that vSide would be shutting down on October 15, 2016.  vSide was developed by vSocial, which was later acquired by VMware in 2013.
