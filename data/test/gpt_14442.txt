The Smoking Flowers are a rock duo from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The band was formed in 2007 by brothers Alex and Luke Kibbe, and drummer Jimmy LaValle.  The Smoking Flowers' sound is a mix of alternative rock, indie pop, and electronica. They have released two studio albums, "The Great Western" (2010) and "A Fever You Can't Sweat Out" (2015), as well as one EP, "In the Garden" (2013).
