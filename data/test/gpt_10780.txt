The 76th Indianapolis 500 was held at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway on Sunday May 25, 1992. The race was won by Emerson Fittipaldi driving a McLaren-Honda.  The Indianapolis 500 is one of the most prestigious automobile races in the world and is considered one of the greatest races in American racing history. Held annually at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway in Speedway, Indiana, it is the first of the IndyCar Series events of the year. The race is known for its high speeds, tight turns, and dramatic finish.  The Indianapolis 500 has been contested over a distance of 500 miles (800 km) since 1911.
