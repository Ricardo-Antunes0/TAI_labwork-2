Mahyuddin N. S. was born on 14 September 1947 in Tangail district of Bangladesh. He received his early education from Madrasa-e-Islamia Tangail and then completed his B.A. from the University of Dhaka in 1971. He then undertook an M.A. in Philosophy from the University of Oxford in 1974 and a D.Phil. in Philosophy from the University of Cambridge in 1978, both with first class honours.  Mahyuddin N. S. began his academic career as a lecturer at the University of Dhaka from 1974 to 1979 and then became a Professor of Philosophy there from 1979 to 1988. He served as Dean of the Faculty of Arts from 1988 to 1990 and Vice-Chancellor (Academic) from 1998 to 2001.  Mahyuddin N. S.'s main interests were in Islamic philosophy, Indian philosophy, ethics, and philosophy of religion. He authored or co-authored a number of books on these topics, including The Unity of Knowledge (1981), The Essence of Islamic Philosophy (1988), Ethics: An Islamic Perspective (1993), and A Guide to Islamic Thought (2002).
