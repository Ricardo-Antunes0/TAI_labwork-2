Alric ( December 1036), also known as Alberic II, was the Count of Asti from 1068 until his death. He was also the count of Novara and Piacenza.  Alric was born in 1036, the son of Count Henry I of Asti and Gisela of Burgundy. He succeeded his father as count in 1068. In 1075 he married Adelaide, daughter of marquis Conrad of Milan. He died in 1115, leaving three children: Henry II, who succeeded him as count; Adelheid; and Adelaide.
