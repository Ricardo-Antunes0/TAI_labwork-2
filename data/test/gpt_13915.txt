Sein Win  is a Burmese politician, currently serving as a Member of Parliament for Kyauktada Township in Mandalay Region.  Born on 17 October 1965 in Kyauktada Township, Sein Win completed his Bachelor of Arts degree from University of Southern California in 1988 and his Master's degree from University of California, Los Angeles in 1991. He then worked as a teacher in Mandalay Region before being elected to the House of Representatives in 2004. In 2010, he was appointed Minister for Social Welfare and Human Rights, a position he held until 2012. He was subsequently elected to the Pyithu Hluttaw, Burma's house of parliament, and re-appointed Minister for Social Welfare and Human Rights in 2016.
