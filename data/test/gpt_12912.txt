The 3rd season of the television series HaPijamot aired from October 5, 2009 to March 11, 2010 on Israel's Channel 10.  The season featured 15 episodes. The regular cast included Irit Shemer and Ohad Naharin as the title characters, Eyal Podell as Yossi, Shira Hadar as Lila, and Anat Hoffman as Tzipporah. New cast members in this season were Anat Hoffman as Tzipporah, and Tali Sharot as Nechama.  The season was released on DVD in Region 2 on December 12, 2009.
