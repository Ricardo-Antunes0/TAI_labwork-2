WBFY-LP is a low-power, community radio station licensed to serve the community of Burlington, Vermont. It is operated by Burlington Free Radio, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.  WBFY-LP broadcasts a community radio format consisting of music, news, and public affairs programming. The station also offers a variety of educational and cultural programming.  The station was founded in 2006 and began broadcasting from studios in downtown Burlington. In 2009, the station moved its operations to a new building on North Winooski Street.   WBFY-LP is available through digital audio broadcasting (DAB) and streaming on the web and via mobile apps.
