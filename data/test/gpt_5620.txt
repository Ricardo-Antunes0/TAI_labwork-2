Mukesh (born Mukesh Madhavan) is an Indian actor and producer who has appeared in over 200 films in both Tamil and Hindi. He is the son of actor-producer M. G. Ramachandran and actress Sarojini.  Mukesh made his acting debut in the 1992 Tamil film Thalapathi. He later starred in commercially successful Hindi films, including Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge (1995), Devdas (2002), Lagaan (2001) and Chak De India (2007). He has also appeared in several acclaimed regional films, including Kannada's Santosh Sivan's Vataliputra (1998) and Telugu's Naga Chaitanya's Nagarjuna (2002).
