Iris atropurpurea, the coastal iris (, Irus atropurpureus), is a species of Iris native to the coasts of North America from Alaska to Mexico. It is widespread in foggy coastal areas, where it grows in wet meadows and on rocky shores.  Iris atropurpurea grows to about one metre tall, with fragrant, purple flowers in early summer. The plant is hardy to Zone 4, but is not frost-tolerant.
