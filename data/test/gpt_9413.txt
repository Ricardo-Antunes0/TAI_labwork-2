Gerald Achee (November 15, 1952 - January 3, 1991) was an American singer, songwriter, and musician. Achee was the lead singer of the post-hardcore band Dead Kennedys and played guitar in the rock band Bad Brains.  Gerald Achee was born in New York City on November 15, 1952. He grew up in Long Island and attended high school in Huntington, NY. Achee started his musical career as the lead singer of the hardcore punk band Dead Kennedys. In 1980, he formed the rock band Bad Brains with drummer Darryl Jenifer and bassist J Mascis. The band released four albums before Achee's death in 1991.
