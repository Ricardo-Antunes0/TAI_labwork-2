Sergey Yevgenyevich Naryshkin (; born 27 October 1965) is a Russian politician and retired general of the Russian Armed Forces.  Born in the town of Naryshkin, Moscow Oblast, Naryshkin graduated from the Frunze Military Academy in 1987. He served in the Soviet Army from 1987 to 1991 and then in the Russian Armed Forces until 1999. He was elected to the State Duma in 1999, and served as a deputy minister of defence from 1999 to 2003. From 2003 to 2007 he served as the first deputy chairman of Russia's Federal Security Service (FSB), and then became its chairman from 2007 to 2012. In 2012, he retired from active service.
