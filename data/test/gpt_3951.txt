Volo is a French musical singing duo consisting of Tiphaine Panahi and Rachid Taha. The duo has released six studio albums, the most recent being "Le Retour" in 2017.   The two met in 2006 when Panahi was living in Paris and working as a backup singer for French singer Sakine Cissé. The two became friends and decided to form a duo, with Taha serving as the lead vocalist and songwriter. They released their first album, "Volo", in 2009 and followed it up with "Les Parfums" (2011), "Les Mots" (2013), "L'Odyssée" (2015), and "Le Retour" (2017).
