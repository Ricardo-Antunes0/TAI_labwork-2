Feminist is an adventure story arc of a Filipino webcomic written and illustrated by Filipino-American artist Nanjo Garcia. It is set in a world where the sexes are equal, but where sexism and gender discrimination still exist.   Feminist is a story about a young woman who, after growing up in a society that insists on treating women as second-class citizens, decides to fight for her place in the world. She becomes involved with the feminist movement, and finds herself in the middle of a battle against those who would keep her down.
