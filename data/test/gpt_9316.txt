Kuḻambu , is a common dish in Tamil Nadu, Kerala and Karnataka. It is a stew made with mutton, chicken or fish, and various vegetables. The spices used depend on the region where it is made.   The word "kuḻambu" comes from the Tamil words "kuḻam" (कुछ) meaning "a kind of meat", and "bu" (பூ) meaning "a kind of stew".
