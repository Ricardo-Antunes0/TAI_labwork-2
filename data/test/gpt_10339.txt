Arriva Rail North, branded as Northern by Arriva, is a British rail company operating in Northern England. The company is a subsidiary of Arriva Plc. The company headquarters are in York.  The company was formed on 1 April 2006, as a result of the merger of Regional Railways North East and Northern Rail. It operates the majority of services in County Durham and Northumberland, with a smaller presence in Cumbria and Lancashire. It also operates a small number of services in Yorkshire.   In January 2019, Arriva Rail North announced that it would be withdrawing all its services from South Tyneside due to low passenger numbers. The last service to South Tyneside departed on 3 January 2019.
