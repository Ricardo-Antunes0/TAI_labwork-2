Kususa is an electronic dance DJ and producer originally from Louisville, Kentucky. He has released tracks on labels such as OWSLA, Mad Decent, and Spinnin Records. Kususa has also collaborated with artists such as Diplo, Flux Pavilion, and Steve Aoki. In 2017, Kususa was one of the DJs selected to perform at the first-ever edition of Electric Daisy Carnival Las Vegas.
