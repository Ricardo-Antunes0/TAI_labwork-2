Every Girl is a studio album by American singer and songwriter Charli XCX. Released on October 12, 2014 by Atlantic Records, it was produced by XCX and John Hill and features guest contributions from Iggy Azalea, Charli XCX, and Snoh Aalegra.  The title of the album is derived from the 1987 song "Every Girl" by the British pop group Wham!.  The album received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and XCX's vocals. It debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart, selling 139,000 copies in its first week. The album has been certified Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
