Right Reverend Dr. Fidelis Lionel Emmanuel Fernando (born 13 May 1961) is a Nigerian prelate of the Roman Catholic Church, who has been the Bishop of Bayelsa since 9 January 2016.  Fidelis Fernando was born in May 1961 in Onitsha, Anambra State, Nigeria to the parents of Emmanuel and Theresa Fernando. He attended St. Augustine's College, Jos, where he obtained his secondary school certificate in 1979. He then attended the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, where he obtained a bachelor's degree in theology in 1984. He then entered the Pontifical Gregorian University in Rome to continue his theological studies and obtained a doctorate in theology in 1993.  On 19 June 1988, Fidelis was ordained a priest by Cardinal Francis Arinze at the Basilica of Our Lady of Peace, Lagos. From 1988 to 1990 he served as an assistant parish priest in Ibadan. From 1990 to 1992 he served as an associate pastor at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Parish, Ikoyi, Lagos. From 1992 to 1994 he served as an assistant parish priest at Our Lady of Fatima Parish, Ile-Ife, Lagos. From 1994 to 1996 he served as an associate pastor at Blessed Sacrament Parish, Ibadan.
