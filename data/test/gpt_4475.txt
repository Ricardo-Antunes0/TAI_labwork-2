Mr. Vampire (Chinese: 殭屍先生) is a 1985 Hong Kong action film directed by Gordon Chan and produced by Raymond Chow.  The film stars Jet Li as Wong Cho-wing, a vampire who starts to drink human blood in order to survive. He clashes with a group of humans led by Lo Wei (Yuen Biao) and tries to stop them from destroying vampires everywhere.  The film was a major success in Hong Kong and spawned two sequels, "Mr. Vampire II" (1989) and "Mr. Vampire III" (1991).
