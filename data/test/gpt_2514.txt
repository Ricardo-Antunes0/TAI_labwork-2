Zoe Slater is a fictional character from the Australian soap opera Neighbours, played by Stephanie McIntosh.  Zoe Slater was first introduced as the younger sister of established character Steph Slater (Caroline Flack). She made her first screen appearance during the episode broadcast on 29 July 2002. Zoe quickly became popular with viewers, and was given her own story arc in 2003. That year, she became involved with local mechanic Robbie Hunter (Scott Major), but their relationship was short-lived.  In 2004, Zoe began a relationship with Robbie's older brother, Scott (Mark Bazeley). The couple were forced to break up when Scott discovered that Zoe had been cheating on him with his brother. Later that year, Zoe began a relationship with Darren Osborne (Ashley Sargeant), but the pair ended their relationship in 2006.  Zoe returned to the show in 2010 and began a romance with Darren's cousin, Jason Robinson (Scott Major). The couple married in 2011, but their marriage ended in 2012. In 2013, Zoe began a relationship with Robbo Fisher (Ray Meagher), but the couple ended their relationship in 2014.  Zoe has since appeared on several occasions as a guest star on Neighbours. She made her final appearance on the show in May 2018.
