The Atlético Reynosa Fútbol Club, is a professional Mexican football club based in Reynosa, Tamaulipas. The team was founded on November 15, 1942 and play in the Liga MX. Atlético Reynosa plays its home games at the Estadio Reynosa.  Reynosa is a city located in the northeastern state of Tamaulipas, in Mexico. It is the capital of the municipality of Reynosa, and one of the most important commercial centers and industrial centers of northern Mexico. Reynosa has a population of over 1.5 million people. The city lies on the Rio Grande which forms its southern border with Texas.   Atlético Reynosa was founded on November 15, 1942 and has played continuously in Liga MX since its inception in 1970. The club has had several spells as champions of the league (1974, 1975, 1979, 1984, 1985, 2001), as well as runners-up (1976, 1981). Atlético Reynosa reached the quarterfinals of the CONCACAF Champions' Cup twice (1977 and 2004), but exited each time to more successful teams.
