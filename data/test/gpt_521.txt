Jammu and Kashmir State Land (Vesting of Right to Property) Act, 1973  The Roshni Act, 1973 is a law in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir that protects the right to property of women. The law ensures that women are equally entitled to own and inherit property, and provides for a fair and speedy trial in cases of property disputes between women. The act also prohibits discrimination against women in property dealings, and provides for the protection of women's sexual autonomy.
