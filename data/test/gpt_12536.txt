Nicholas James Loman (born February 1979) was an American man who committed suicide by jumping from the upper deck of the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco, California on January 13, 2010.  Nicholas James Loman was born in February 1979 in San Francisco, California to James and Patricia Loman. He had one younger sister. Growing up in the Richmond District of San Francisco, Nick was an avid reader and enjoyed spending time outdoors. He attended Skyline High School and studied business at SFSU.  After college, Nick moved to New York City to pursue his career in finance. He worked for several prestigious investment banks before leaving to start his own company. In 2007, he met and married his wife, Stephanie. The couple had been living in Los Angeles when Nicholas decided to move back to San Francisco to be with Stephanie and their young daughter.  On January 13, 2010, Nicholas took his own life by jumping from the upper deck of the Golden Gate Bridge. He was survived by his wife and daughter.
