Inge Auerbacher (born December 31, 1934, in Vienna, Austria) is an Austrian artist who is known for her paintings, sculptures, and installations. She is a pioneer in the field of video art and has been working in this medium since the early 1960s.  Auerbacher's paintings are characterized by their use of strong colors and bold lines, and often incorporate elements of performance art. Her sculptures are often large and abstract, and she has also created installations that combine sculpture, video, and sound. Auerbacher was born in Vienna to a Jewish family, but she has since become an internationally recognized artist who has exhibited her work in many countries around the world.
