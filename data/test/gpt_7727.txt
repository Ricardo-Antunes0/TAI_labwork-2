28S ribosomal protein S33, mitochondrial is a protein that in humans is encoded by the MRPS33 gene.  MRPS33 is a 28S ribosomal protein that in humans is encoded by the MRPS33 gene. This protein is located in the mitochondrial ribosome and is a component of the tRNA anticodon-binding site. This protein has been shown to interact with FKBP12.
