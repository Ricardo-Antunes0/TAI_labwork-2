Hannah Maud Hart (born November 2, 1986) is a Canadian comedian, writer and actress. She is best known for her YouTube channel, hannahhart.net, which has over 2.5 million subscribers.  Hannah Hart was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada to Pamela (n√©e Hart) and Mark Hart. She has two older sisters, Shannon and Breanne. Hart grew up in the suburb of West Vancouver. As a child, Hart was always interested in making people laugh. She started performing stand-up comedy at the age of 15 and quickly became known on the Vancouver comedy scene. In 2006, Hart moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in comedy.  Hart's first major break came in 2009 when she was cast as one of the stars of the FOX television series "The Mindy Project". The series aired for three seasons before being cancelled in 2013. In between seasons of "The Mindy Project", Hart released her first stand-up album, "Don't Start with Me". The album was well-received by critics and sold over 50,000 copies.  In 2014, Hart created and starred in the Comedy Central series "Hannah Hart: On My Block". The show was cancelled after one season. That same year, she released her second stand-up album, "Irresponsible". The album was also well-received by critics and sold over 30,000 copies.
