Oladotun Ojuolape Kayode (born April 8, 1984) better known by his stage name Do2tun, is a Nigerian rapper, songwriter, record producer and entrepreneur.  Do2tun is an accomplished musician and songwriter. He has released five studio albums, including the critically acclaimed 2005 album "The First Step". His other albums include "The Answer" (2007), "The Legacy" (2009), "Do2tun" (2011) and "One Night Only" (2015). He has also released several singles, including "One Day", "I Believe in You", "Ghetto Gospel" and "Feeling Good".  Do2tun has also produced music for other artists, including P-Square, Maleek Berry and Lil Kesh. In 2013, he founded the record label Do2tun Music Group. He has been nominated for several awards, including Best Newcomer at the 2005 Nigeria Entertainment Awards and Best Rap Album at the 2007 Nigeria Entertainment Awards.  Born in 1984 in Lagos State, Nigeria to parents of Yoruba descent, Do2tun began his musical career as a member of the hip hop group Da Mafia 6ix. In 2003, he released his first solo album, The First Step. He followed up with The Answer in 2007 and The Legacy in 2009. His most recent album, Do2tun, was released in 2011.
