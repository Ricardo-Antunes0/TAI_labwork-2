Kamtapur Progressive Party (now merged with Kamtapur Janata Dal) is a political party in Nepal. It was founded on 7 January 1991 by Ram Prasad Dhakal.  Kamtapur Progressive Party is registered with the Election Commission of Nepal. It has been a part of the Nepali Congress-led alliance since 1991. In the 2008 Constituent Assembly election, it won 2 seats. In the 2013 general election, it won 1 seat in the House of Representatives.
