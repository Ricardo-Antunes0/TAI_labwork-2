Ford Park Cemetery is a  cemetery located in the Ford Park neighborhood in the city of Detroit, Michigan. It is owned and operated by the Detroit Land Bank Authority. As of 2007, it had a total area of 5.6 acres (2.2 ha), and as of 2009, it had a total area of 6 acres (2.4 ha).  Ford Park Cemetery was founded in 1892 and is the second-oldest cemetery in Detroit. It is also the largest cemetery in the city, with a total area of 6 acres (2.4 ha). The cemetery has more than 51,000 interments.
