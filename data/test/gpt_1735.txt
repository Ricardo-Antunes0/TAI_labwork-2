William John Irvine (born 18 June 1943) is a Scottish former footballer who played as a midfielder. He was capped 66 times by Scotland, scoring six goals.  Born in Glasgow, Irvine played for Celtic, Aberdeen and Dundee United in a career that spanned sixteen years. He won the Scottish Cup with Celtic in 1967 and the Scottish League Cup with Aberdeen in 1978. He also played for Dundee United in the European Cup Winners' Cup in 1975 and 1976. After retiring from football, Irvine ran a hotel in Aberdeen before becoming a football coach. He was appointed manager of Aberdeen in January 2004, but was sacked after only eight months in charge.
