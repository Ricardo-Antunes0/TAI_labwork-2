Silver 'n Voices is an album by American rock band Nickelback. It was released on October 24, 2002 through Roadrunner Records. It is their fifth studio album and the follow-up to their 2002 debut album, Silver Side Up. It was produced by the band's then-manager, Shaun Morgan, and mixed by Michael Platt.  The album has sold over 10 million copies worldwide, making it one of the band's most successful albums. Its lead single, "How You Remind Me", became a major hit worldwide and received multiple awards and nominations. The second single, "If Today Was Your Last Day", became a top five hit in several countries and was nominated for a Grammy Award.   "Silver 'n Voices" received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised its production and the band's musical abilities. The album has been included in several publications' lists of the best albums of 2002 and 2003.
