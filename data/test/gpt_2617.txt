TalkSports is a user-generated sports site run by fans, for fans. We cover the latest news and scores from around the world of sports, while our community provides its own opinions and insights on the games and athletes they love.  TalkSports was created in 2006 by two friends who loved sports and wanted to bring fans together to talk about them. We've grown rapidly to become one of the leading sources for sports news and information on the web, with a user base that spans all corners of the globe.  We're a site built for fans, by fans. Whether you're a die-hard fan of your favorite team or just taking in the games on TV, we want to be there for you. So come join the conversation, and let's keep sports talking!
