Disgorge Mexico is the eighth album by the Norwegian black metal band Mayhem. The album was recorded in early 1995 and released the same year through Necropolis Records.  The album is a concept album, revolving around the biblical story of Samson and Delilah. The lyrics deal with themes of murder, revenge, and nihilism. The album has been cited as an important influence by both black metal and death metal bands.
