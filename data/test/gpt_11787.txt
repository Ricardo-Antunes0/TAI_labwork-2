Magnus Georg von Paucker   was a German historian who specialized in the history of the French Revolution.  Paucker was born in 1877 in Berlin, and studied at the universities of Berlin, Heidelberg, and Leipzig. He received his doctorate from the University of Leipzig in 1903 with a thesis on the history of the French Revolution. Paucker taught at the Universities of Kiel and Freiburg im Breisgau before joining the faculty of Cologne University in 1928. Paucker died in Cologne in 1944.
