Pierre Galet (28 January 1921 – 30 November 2007) was a French actor, director and screenwriter. He was born in Paris.  Pierre Galet began his acting career in the theatre, appearing in such productions as "Cyrano de Bergerac" and "The Threepenny Opera". He made his film debut in the French film "Une femme est une femme" (1955). He later appeared in roles in such films as "Le bonheur" (1959), "Le Père Noël" (1962), "Les Enfants du Paradis" (1945) and "Trois hommes et un couffin" (1970).  Galet made his directorial debut with the film "Les Amants du Pont-Neuf" (1973). He later directed films such as "Le Boucher" (1980), "Les Liaisons dangereuses" (1988), "L'Étranger" (1998) and "J'ai décidé de ne plus rien faire" (2006).  Galet was married to actress Marie-France Pisier from 1952 until their divorce in 1976. He later married actress Françoise Leroy. Pierre Galet died on 30 November 2007 at the age of 89.
