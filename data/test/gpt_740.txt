Impala Saracens is a Kenyan rugby club that was formed in the early 2000s. The club is based in the town of Kisumu and plays in the Kenyan Premier Division. Impala Saracens is one of the newest clubs to compete in Kenyan rugby, and has made considerable progress in its short history. The club's most successful season to date was in 2009 when it finished second in the Kenyan Premier Division. Impala Saracens also reached the semi-finals of the Kenya Cup that year, losing to eventual champions Nairobi RFC. Impala Saracens has also achieved some success in regional competitions, most notably winning the East African Club Championship in 2007.
