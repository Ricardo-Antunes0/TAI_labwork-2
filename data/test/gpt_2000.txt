Kim Davis, better known by her stage name, Choice, is an American rapper.  Choice was born in Atlanta, Georgia, in 1984. She began rapping at the age of 13 and began to gain recognition in the Atlanta rap scene in the early 2000s. Choice released her first album, Choice Time, in 2005. She has since released several more albums, including 2009's Choice Time 2 and 2013's Choice Time 3.  Choice is best known for her singles "Get It" and "Too Tight". "Get It" reached number 21 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 and number 1 on the US Rap Albums chart. The song was also nominated for a Grammy Award in the category of Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group. "Too Tight" reached number 36 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 and number 4 on the US Rap Albums chart.
