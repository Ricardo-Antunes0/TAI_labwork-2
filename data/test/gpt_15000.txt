Simone Stratigo (, Symeon Filippos Stratigos; ; born 1 July 1979) is a Greek professional footballer who plays for Aiginiakos as a midfielder.  Simone Stratigo started his career at Panionios. He was then transferred to AEK Athens in the summer of 2001. With AEK Athens, Stratigo won the Greek Cup in 2004 and the Greek Super Cup in 2006.
