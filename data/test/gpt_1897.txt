Conant is a pictish surname, and means "descendant of the black one". The name may refer to:  1. Benjamin S. Conant (1892-1962), American chemist and physicist who won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1943 2. John Conant (1632-1716), an American Puritan clergyman, scholar, and scientist 3. Samuel J. Conant (1775-1855), an American astronomer and educator 4. Rufus W. Conant (1888-1979), a United States Army general who was Chief of Staff of the United States Army during World War II 5. Kenneth A. Conant (1907-1988), an American chemist
