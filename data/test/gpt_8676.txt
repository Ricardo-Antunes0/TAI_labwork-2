Ha Dong-kyun (; born June 28, 1980), is a South Korean singer and actor. He is best known as a member of the South Korean boy band Shinhwa.  Ha Dong-kyun was born in Seoul, South Korea on June 28, 1980. He attended Yeouido Elementary School and later Yeouido High School. Ha Dong-kyun then studied at the University of Music and Theatre in Vienna, Austria, where he majored in Musical Theatre.  Ha Dong-kyun made his debut in 2001 with the South Korean boy band Shinhwa. The group achieved massive success throughout Asia, Europe, and North America and released eight albums before disbanding in 2014. Ha Dong-kyun has since released two solo albums and starred in several television dramas. In 2017, he released his first ever solo album in Japan.  Ha Dong-kyun is married to actress Lee Young-ah. They have one child together.
