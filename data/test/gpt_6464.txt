"Intuition" is a song by American singer and songwriter Taylor Swift from her fifth studio album, "1989" (1991). It was written by Swift, Max Martin, and Shellback. The song was released as the album's lead single on October 27, 1991, by Big Machine Records. "Intuition" is a ballad about the power of intuition.  "Intuition" received positive reviews from music critics who commended its production and lyrical content. The song reached the top 10 on the United States "Billboard" Hot 100 chart and peaked at number one for one week. It also topped the charts in Australia, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom. The accompanying music video was directed by Paul Haggis and features Swift performing in a white room with minimalistic props. The video has been viewed over 1.5 billion times on YouTube.
