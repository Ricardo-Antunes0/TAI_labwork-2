English Garden, released in North America as Garden, is the third studio album by Canadian indie rock band The Paper Kites. It was released on July 10, 2012, through Labrador Records.  The Paper Kites announced English Garden on their Facebook page on February 8, 2012. The album was recorded at Audio West in Vancouver, British Columbia with producer John Congleton and mixed by John Agnello at Electric Lady Studios in New York City.  "English Garden" is a collection of songs about love and loss, recorded during the band's time spent on tour in support of their second album "Sleep Well Beast". "The Observer" called it "a disarmingly beautiful album, full of delicate guitars and heart-wrenching lyrics".    The Paper Kites toured in support of English Garden throughout North America and Europe in 2012.
