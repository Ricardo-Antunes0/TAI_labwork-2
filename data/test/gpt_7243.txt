Didier Fassin, born in 1955, is a French sociologist and philosopher.  Fassin is known for his work on post-structuralism, critical theory, and social theory. He has written extensively on a range of topics, including the philosophy of history, the sociology of knowledge, and the theory of communication.  Fassin has been a visiting professor at various universities around the world, including Yale University, Stanford University, and the University of California, Berkeley. In 2014 he was awarded the prestigious Jean-Paul Sartre Prize.
