Stockfish Film Festival is an international film festival held annually in the city of Tallinn, Estonia.  The Stockfish Film Festival is a celebration of cinema, art and culture. The festival presents a diverse selection of films from around the world, with a focus on independent and experimental cinema. The festival also features a wide range of panel discussions, masterclasses and other events aimed at providing audiences with an insight into the latest trends in filmmaking.  The Stockfish Film Festival was founded in 2002 by Estonian film enthusiasts, with the aim of promoting Estonian and international cinema. Since its inception, the Stockfish Film Festival has grown into one of the leading festivals in Estonia, attracting filmmakers and film fans from around the world.
