Gazipur City Corporation ( - in short: GCC) is a local government organization in Gazipur City of Bangladesh. Gazipur City Corporation was established on 24 October 1984. The corporation covers an area of 386 km² and has a population of over 1.5 million people.  Gazipur City Corporation is responsible for public services such as water supply, sanitation, transportation, and waste management. The corporation also manages public parks, colleges, and hospitals. In addition, the corporation operates a number of businesses including a port and a power plant.  The corporation is led by a mayor who is appointed by the Bangladeshi government. The mayor is assisted by a municipal commissioner who is responsible for day-to-day operations.
