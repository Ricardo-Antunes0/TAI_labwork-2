Phil Tollestrup (born October 21, 1949) is a Danish film director and screenwriter. He has directed thirteen films since 1978, including the critically acclaimed drama The Hunt (1997), which was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film. His filmography also includes the comedies Lipstick on Your Collar (1990) and Bedtime Stories (1995), and the crime thriller The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo (2009). Tollestrup was born in Copenhagen, Denmark.
