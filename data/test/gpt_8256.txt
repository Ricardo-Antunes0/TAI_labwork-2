Général de division Louis, Comte Lepic (September 18, 1825 – January 1, 1895) was a French general who fought in the Franco-Prussian War of 1870–1871. He also served in the Second Opium War and in the Mexican–American War.  Louis Lepic was born on September 18, 1825, in Saint-Quentin, Aisne. He was educated at the École Polytechnique and the École des Ponts et Chaussées.  Lepic served in the Franco-Prussian War of 1870–1871 as a general de division and distinguished himself at the Battle of Gravelotte. After the war Lepic served in Algeria and Mexico before retiring in 1881. He died on January 1, 1895, in Paris.
