Wilhelm Arthur Vogel (2 April 1868 – 22 September 1943) was a German-born American photographer and film maker. He is best known for his portraits and landscapes.  Arthur Vogel was born in Breslau, Silesia, then part of Prussia, now Wrocław, Poland on April 2, 1868. He emigrated to the United States in 1887 and settled in New York City. Vogel became a naturalized citizen of the United States in 1909.  Vogel worked as a photographer and film maker. He is best known for his portraits and landscapes. His subjects included celebrities, politicians, and other dignitaries. Vogel died in New York City on September 22, 1943.
