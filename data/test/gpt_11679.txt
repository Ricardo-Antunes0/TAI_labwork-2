Clodine  is an unincorporated community in DeWitt County, Texas, United States. It is located on U.S. Highway 281 south of DeWitt.  Clodine was settled in the late 19th century by a group of Tennesseans. The community was named for one of the settlers, Clodine H. Boone. A post office was established in Clodine in 1893, and it operated until 1913. The community is now abandoned.
