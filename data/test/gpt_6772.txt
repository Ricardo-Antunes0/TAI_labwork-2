Trans Media is a business unit of a company that creates, produces and distributes electronic media products. Trans Media creates, produces and distributes electronic products that are used to entertain, inform and engage users.  Trans Media was founded in 1998 by entrepreneur and media executive, Michael Eisner. The company is based in Los Angeles, California.  Trans Media's products include video games, movies and television shows. The company's video games include the popular franchises "The Sims", "God of War" and "Call of Duty". Trans Media's movie releases include such blockbusters as "The Dark Knight" and "The Avengers". Trans Media's television shows include the critically acclaimed series "Lost" and "Breaking Bad".
