Lineage  is a medieval fantasy, massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by NCsoft Corporation. Lineage was released on October 12, 2003, in South Korea as Lineage II. The game has since been ported to multiple platforms including Microsoft Windows, OS X, iOS, Android, Xbox 360 and Playstation 3.  Lineage is a medieval fantasy MMORPG with action-based combat and a player-versus-player (PvP) system. Players can choose from one of the game's six races and choose from one of the game's nineteen classes, each with its own unique abilities and skills. The game features three continents and numerous cities that players can explore. Players can join guilds for social interaction or go solo to pursue their own goals.
