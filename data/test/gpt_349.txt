Kattula Kondaiah is a 1985 Telugu-language action film directed by K. Raghavendra Rao and produced by Suresh Babu. The film stars Rajinikanth, Sonia Tandon, Jagapathi Babu, Chiranjeevi and Mohan Babu in lead roles. The film has music composed by Ilaiyaraaja.  The film is about an honest police officer (Rajinikanth) who is falsely implicated in a crime he didn't commit and has to fight against the corrupt police force and the politicians to clear his name.
