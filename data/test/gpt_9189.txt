Betty Crocker is a brand and fictional character created by Helen Crocker in 1922, who appeared in advertisements until the brand was discontinued in 2000. The character is mentioned in the song "Betty Crocker's dream" by Rod Stewart.  Betty Crocker is the fictional creation of Helen Crocker, who first appeared in 1922 in a series of ads for her baking mix. In 1950, General Mills acquired the rights to the Betty Crocker name and created a separate company to market the product. General Mills discontinued the Betty Crocker brand in 2000.
