Karma yoga , also called Karma marga, is a system of Hinduism and yoga that emphasizes the practice of self-discipline and charity. It is based on the belief that karma is the cause and effect of one's actions, and that the accumulation of good deeds leads to happiness in this life and in future lives. The goal of karma yoga is to achieve balance and harmony between one's own desires and the wishes of others, as well as within oneself.
