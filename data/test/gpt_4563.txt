Jinx is a fictional character from the toyline and comics series G.I. Joe, co-created by Larry Hama and Bill Reinhold. She first appeared in the 1983 toyline as a member of the Joes' elite unit, Serpent Squad.  Jinx is an acrobatic and skilled fighter, and a valuable asset to the team. Her weapon of choice is a pair of sai. Her appearances in the comics have generally been set during periods of high-stakes conflict, and she has been involved in several high-profile battles against Cobra.  Jinx has also been featured in a number of spin-off comics, including G.I. Joe: A Real American Hero, G.I. Joe: The Movie, G.I. Joe: The Revenge of Cobra and G.I. Joe: Snake Eyes vs. Storm Shadow.
