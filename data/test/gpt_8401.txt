Jaya Bhattacharya is an Indian television actress. She is best known for her roles as Dr. Neelima in the soap opera Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, and as Janani in the crime drama series Kasautii Zindagii Kay.  Jaya Bhattacharya was born on 5 September 1985 in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. She graduated with a BSc degree in Zoology from St. Xavierâ€™s College, Kolkata in 2007.  Bhattacharya started her television career with a small role in the 2006 soap opera Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi. She played the role of Dr Neelima for four years before leaving the show in 2010 to focus on her new show Kasautii Zindagii Kay.  Bhattacharya has appeared in several television shows since then, including Ek Tha Tiger, Dabangg 2, and Baadshaho. In 2016, she starred in the Hindi supernatural thriller film Ghost Stories opposite Riteish Deshmukh and Amit Sadh.
