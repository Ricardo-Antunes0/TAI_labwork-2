Boblbee (also expressed as BOBLBEÂ·E when used as a first name) is an American brand of coffee and tea. It is headquartered in Dallas, Texas.  Boblbee was founded in 1984 by brothers Vernon and Glenn Boblbee. The company's first product, Boblbee Coffee, was a ground coffee packaged in individual-sized foil pouches. The Boblbee Coffee brand is now owned by Nestle.  Boblbee's other products include bottled iced tea, energy drinks, and flavored teas. The company's slogan is "Fuel Your Day."
