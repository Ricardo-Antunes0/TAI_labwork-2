Alejandro Rosales Castillo (born November 26, 1998) is an American-born Mexican male criminal who has been convicted of six counts of first-degree murder.  Alejandro Castillo was born in San Diego, California, to Mexican immigrant parents on November 26, 1998. Castillo's family relocated to Tijuana, Mexico, when he was just a few months old. Castillo eventually moved back to the United States, where he resided in Brownsville, Texas. In early 2018, Castillo was apprehended in Brownsville for possession of a .40 caliber handgun and ammunition. Castillo later confessed to six murders that he had committed in the area over the past two years. Castillo has been positively identified as the perpetrator of all six murders and is currently awaiting trial.
