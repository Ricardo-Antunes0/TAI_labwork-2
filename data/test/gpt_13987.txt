Kate Fodor is an American playwright and screenwriter. She is the founder of the experimental theater company, Primary Stages, and the author of the play, The Coast of Utopia.  Fodor was born in New York City in 1955. She studied playwriting at Yale University and the University of California, Berkeley. Her first play, The Coast of Utopia, was produced in 1985. Fodor has written for both stage and screen, including writing the screenplay for the film, Donnie Darko. She has also written documentaries and children's books.
