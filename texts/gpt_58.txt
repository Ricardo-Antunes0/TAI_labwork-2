Elon Reeve Musk, born on June 28, 1971, is a prominent businessman and investor known for his diverse array of ventures. Musk holds pivotal roles across several groundbreaking companies: he serves as the founder, chairman, CEO, and CTO of SpaceX; he is the angel investor, CEO, product architect, and former chairman of Tesla, Inc.; he also holds positions as owner, executive chairman, and CTO of X Corp.; founder of the Boring Company and xAI; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of the Musk Foundation. With a net worth estimated at $178 billion as of April 2024, Musk stands among the wealthiest individuals globally. Born in Pretoria, South Africa, Musk relocated to Canada at age 18, later obtaining Canadian citizenship. He pursued studies at Queen's University in Canada after briefly attending the University of Pretoria. Eventually, Musk transferred to the University of Pennsylvania, earning bachelor's degrees in economics and physics. Musk's entrepreneurial journey began with the co-founding of Zip2, an online city guide software company, which was acquired by Compaq in 1999. Following this success, he co-founded X.com, a direct bank, which later merged with Confinity to form PayPal. eBay acquired PayPal in 2002 for $1.5 billion. Utilizing proceeds from the sale of PayPal, Musk founded SpaceX in 2002, embarking on a mission to revolutionize spaceflight services. He also became an early investor in Tesla Motors, Inc. (later Tesla, Inc.) in 2004, assuming leadership roles within the company, including chairman and CEO. Musk's visionary pursuits expanded to include SolarCity, a solar-energy company acquired by Tesla in 2016, and ventures such as the hyperloop high-speed transportation system proposal, OpenAI, Neuralink, and the Boring Company. Despite his notable successes, Musk's endeavors have attracted criticism, particularly regarding controversial statements and actions, including COVID-19 misinformation and Twitter's management under his ownership. In March 2023, Musk founded xAI, further solidifying his influence in the realm of artificial intelligence. His multifaceted career continues to shape industries and spark debate, positioning him as a polarizing yet undeniably influential figure in the global business landscape.
